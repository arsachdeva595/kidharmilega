ODOP Product

Scented and Glutonious Rice

Cachar's alluvial soil and monsoon climate create ideal conditions for cultivating scented glutinous rice varieties like Joha and Chokuwa rice. The district has a 200+ year agricultural heritage in rice cultivation with established farmer networks and ideal pH levels for this specialty crop. Local expertise and community farming practices make large-scale quality production feasible.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Cachar.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Cachar rice clusters in Silchar and Hailakandi; connect with Cachar District Agriculture Office and visit 3-4 established rice mills like Brahmaputra Rice Mill. Attend agricultural exhibitions at Assam Agricultural University in Jorhat. Study competitor brands like Basmati from Bijnor and understand Northeast premium positioning.
2
Source Raw Materials
Partner with farmer cooperatives in Lala Assam or Hailakandi blocks; negotiate bulk contracts at ₹3,500-4,200 per quintal for raw glutinous rice. First batch: 50 quintals (₹175,000-210,000). Establish relationships with seed suppliers in Cachar and negotiate 3-6 month payment terms.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent milling capacity at existing Silchar rice mills (₹15,000-25,000/month) or partner with Cachar Rice Millers Association members for custom milling. Procure packaging (1kg vacuum-sealed bags with desiccant) from Assam-based vendors (₹40/unit for 1000 units). Start with 5,000 kg monthly production; scale to 15,000 kg by month 4.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam at udyamregistration.gov.in (free, 10 minutes); apply for IEC from DGFT Guwahati office (₹5,000, 15 days). Trademark brand name with IP office (₹10,000-15,000). Get FSSAI Food Business License Category 3 (₹2,000 for small business). Obtain Assam Export-Import Certificate from Cachar Chamber of Commerce.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Seller Central and Flipkart (10-12% commission); list on BigBasket and Instamart for bulk reach. Create WhatsApp Business catalog and contact 50 premium restaurants in Guwahati, Assam. Email outreach to 20 online specialty food platforms (Naturallys, Native, FarmEatz). Target 10 export inquiries via Global Trade Point and TradeKey by month 3.
6
Scale
Achieve ₹8-10L revenue by month 6; reinvest 40% into expanding farming partnerships and cold storage setup. Launch 2-3 product variants (mixed grain packs, organic certification track). Hire dedicated export manager; target ₹50L annual exports by month 12. Partner with 5-7 regional distributors across Northeast; establish brand presence at 50+ retail outlets.
