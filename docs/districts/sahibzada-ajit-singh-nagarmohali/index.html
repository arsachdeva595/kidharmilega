Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Sahibzada Ajit Singh Nagar/Mohali.

1
Learn / Research
Visit MSIE Mohali industrial estate and Baltej Singh Nagar clusters to understand production units; attend quarterly NSIC trade fairs in Chandigarh; connect with Punjab Industrial Association; study HS codes for engineering goods exports via ICCWC portal
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from Tiruppur steel suppliers (₹15-20L for first batch), Brass Valley (Jamnagar) for non-ferrous metals, and local suppliers in Mohali. Join cooperative procurement groups to reduce first-batch costs by 15-20%.
3
Produce / Partner
Lease 2000-5000 sqft space in MSIE Mohali at ₹25-40/sqft/month; partner with established local job workers for initial orders; invest in CNC machines (₹20-30L for 3-4 units); hire 5-8 skilled machinists from local polytechnics
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (micro/small enterprise) via udyamregistration.gov.in; apply for IEC code via DGFT online portal; register GST; obtain factory license from Mohali district administration; trademark brand name via IPR website
5
First Sale
List on IndiaStack, TradeKey, and Global Sources for B2B buyers; approach automotive supply chain vendors in Manesar (Haryana) and Pune; bid for tenders via GeM portal; contact trading houses like EEPC India members; cold-call OEMs in auto, pump, and machinery sectors
6
Scale
Expand product range to precision components; invest in quality certifications (ISO 9001, AS9100); build export capability via ECGC credit insurance; hire business development manager; establish dealer network across 3-4 states; consider backward integration into raw material processing
