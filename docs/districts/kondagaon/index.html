ODOP Product

Bastar Craft

Kondagaon is the historic heartland of Bastar tribal artisans with generations of expertise in bell metal casting and brass work. The region's unique cultural heritage, access to skilled tribal craftspeople, and established supply chains for raw materials make it the ideal hub for authentic Bastar craft production. Local artisan communities have preserved traditional techniques passed down through centuries, enabling world-class quality and cultural authenticity.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Kondagaon.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kondagaon artisan clusters in Dantewada area and Bastar Craft Village; attend Kondagaon Craft Mela (February); connect with SCCRTC (State Chhattisgarh Crafts Resource & Training Centre) and local artisan cooperatives; study GI-certified Bastar craft production standards
2
Source Raw Materials
Source bell metal and brass from licensed suppliers in Raipur or Delhi (first batch ₹75,000-150,000); source traditional clay and wood from local Kondagaon suppliers; establish direct relationships with 2-3 master artisans for consistent quality and pricing
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with registered artisan groups in Kondagaon (like Bastar Crafts Artisan Association); establish production agreement covering design, output targets, quality standards, and payment terms; plan for 4-8 week lead times; implement inventory management system for finished products
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME classification); apply for IEC code from nearest customs office; trademark brand name with IP India; obtain BIS certification for metal products; apply for GI usage authorization from Ministry of Commerce; create brand identity with tribal art motifs and heritage storytelling
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Handmade, Flipkart Artisan, Etsy; approach interior design stores and heritage hotel chains in metros; list on Craftsvilla and iShake; create Instagram content showcasing artisan stories and behind-the-scenes production; send product samples to lifestyle bloggers and home décor influencers; target corporate gifting platforms like GiftKart
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand product line with 8-10 SKUs; hire dedicated export coordinator; approach international distributors in Europe and North America; establish pop-up stores in metros during festive seasons; build B2B relationships with 5-star hotel chains and luxury home furnishing retailers; implement feedback loop with artisans for design innovations
