Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Chikkballapur.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Chikkaballapur ESDM cluster near Hoskote and Kempapura industrial areas; study existing units like Garuda Circuits, Microelectronics Park; attend CII-ITC meetings on electronics manufacturing; enroll in NASSCOM-backed ESDM certification courses
2
Source Raw Materials
Source PCBs from domestic suppliers (PCB Park, Bangalore) and international catalogs (Digi-Key, Mouser); connectors from Phoenix Contact India (Bangalore); semiconductors from Arrow Electronics; first batch material cost: ₹15-25L depending on volume and complexity
3
Produce / Partner
Establish 2,000-3,000 sqft facility with SMT (Surface Mount Technology) lines or partner with existing contract manufacturers in Chikkaballapur (e.g., Garuda Electronics); invest in pick-and-place machines, reflow ovens, AOI (Automated Optical Inspection) equipment; hire skilled technicians from local electronics schools
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam (MSME classification); obtain IEC code from DGFT Bangalore for export; get ISO 9001:2015 and IEC 61000 certifications; brand as tech-forward ESDM partner; register design patents if proprietary
5
First Sale
Launch B2B outreach via LinkedIn targeting automotive & industrial buyers; register on Global Sources and Alibaba; attend trade shows (India Electronics Week, IESA events); partner with Bangalore-based system integrators and distributors; secure first pilot orders (₹10-20L) from local Tier-1 suppliers
6
Scale
Expand production capacity by adding second SMT line; develop 2-3 flagship products/modules for repeat orders; establish quality partnerships with major OEMs; invest in R&D for IoT/5G-ready components; explore FDI through startup schemes; target 30-40% YoY revenue growth
