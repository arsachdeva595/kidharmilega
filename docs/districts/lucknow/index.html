Lucknow Chikan Craft & Lucknow Zardozi

Lucknow has a 400+ year legacy of chikankari and zardozi craftsmanship dating back to the Awadhi courts. The district houses over 3,500 skilled artisans and embroidery clusters primarily in Aminabad, Chowk, and Hazratganj areas. The established supply chain, generational knowledge transfer, and cultural ecosystem make Lucknow the undisputed epicenter for these embroidery styles.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Lucknow.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Aminabad Chikankari cluster and Lucknow Chikankari Association; spend 2-3 weeks apprenticing with master craftspeople. Attend Lucknow Textile Exhibition (annual). Connect with LUCCI (Lucknow Chamber of Commerce) and FDDI Noida for design trends.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source premium cotton fabrics (₹200-400/meter) from Kanpur textile suppliers; import Zari thread from Surat wholesalers (₹50-150 per spool). First batch order: ₹80,000-150,000 for 50-100 pieces. Partner with local dye houses in Lucknow for custom color matching.
3
Produce / Partner
Join established embroidery cooperatives in Lucknow (Lucknow Chikankari Sansthan, Nagar Nigam registered units) or directly partner with 3-5 master artisans on piece-rate basis (₹500-2,500 per item depending on complexity). Minimum initial artisan network: 10-15 skilled hands.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Ministry of MSME portal); obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Lucknow; file GI-protected product tag usage approval with IP Office. Secure FSSAI if adding home-use product line. Register trademark with IP Office (Delhi) — budget ₹8,000-15,000.
5
First Sale
List on Etsy, Amazon Handmade, Craftsvilla with premium positioning; approach luxury wedding planners and bridal boutiques in Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore; email outreach to 200+ export houses; launch Instagram with heritage storytelling content; attend trade fairs like India International Trade Fair (IITF) Delhi and Heimtextil Frankfurt.
6
Scale
After 6 months, hire a dedicated design head; establish direct retail presence in 2-3 metro cities (Delhi, Mumbai); launch B2B portal for export houses; apply for SFURTI cluster development subsidy; invest in digital marketing and influencer partnerships; explore institutional contracts with luxury hotel chains.
