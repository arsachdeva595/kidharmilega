Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Senapati.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Senapati's established kiwi farms in Maram and Oinam villages. Connect with Senapati Kiwi Farmers Association. Study Himachal Pradesh kiwi models (Solan district) for best practices. Allocate 4-6 weeks for field immersion and yield-cost analysis.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified kiwi saplings from ICAR-affiliated nurseries in Himachal Pradesh (Palampur) or Uttarakhand (Nainital). Budget ₹20-25/sapling × 1000 saplings = ₹20,000-25,000 first batch. Local suppliers in Imphal available for secondary materials.
3
Produce / Partner
Acquire 1-2 hectares land in Senapati with slope >15°. Install drip irrigation and trellising in months 1-2. Plant saplings in July-August pre-monsoon. Partner with 2-3 experienced local farmers for first 3 years via revenue-share model (70:30).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME (online via msme.gov.in, ~0 cost). Obtain IEC code from DGFT (Imphal office, ₹100). Register trademark for brand name at IP India (₹4,500-9,000). Obtain Organic certification from IFOAM-accredited body (INDOCERT, Bangalore) — ₹25,000-40,000 for 2-year cycle.
5
First Sale
Launch on BigBasket and JioMart's farm-direct categories (0% commission for first 3 months). Approach premium chains (Auro Organic, Nature's Basket). Partner with juice manufacturers in Delhi-NCR. Contact APEDA for export market linkage. Start with 500kg/month local supply.
6
Scale
Post-breakeven, expand to 5-10 hectares via farmer producer organization (FPO) model. Invest in cold storage facility (₹20-30L) in Imphal. Launch kiwi-based value-added products (juice concentrate, dried chips). Build B2B relationships with 10+ regional wholesalers and 5+ national exporters.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram Reels showing farm-to-table journey (kiwi picking, packing, delivery) targeting #HealthyLiving community. Partner with 5-10 micro-influencers (50K-200K followers) in health/wellness space. Budget ₹30,000/month for 6 months.
  • PR campaign in agriculture and food tech media (Agropedia, Farm2Food Magazine) highlighting Senapati as 'India's Kiwi Capital'. Target mentions in NDTV Good Earth and Indian Express Kochi editions. Monthly press releases.
  • Direct B2B outreach to 50+ premium organic stores, juice manufacturers, and export companies via email and WhatsApp with sample shipments (2kg packs). Offer 10% discount for first 5-ton bulk orders. Attend AgriCare Summit (Delhi) and Goafresh (Bangalore) annual expos.
Government Schemes

Free money you didn't know about

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L grant (35% subsidy for SC/ST/Women, 25% general) for agricultural processing and horticulture enterprises, repayment 5-6 years.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹2Cr cluster development grant for agriculture clusters; Senapati kiwi can form eligible cluster with 20+ farmers.
🏚
Manipur State Horticulture Mission
₹2-5L subsidy per hectare for high-value crop cultivation including kiwi; apply via District Horticulture Office, Senapati.
🏚
Manipur ATMA Scheme (Agricultural Technology Management Agency)
free training, soil testing, and market linkage support for fruit farmers; registration via district agriculture office.
Registration required: Required — udyamregistration.gov.in
