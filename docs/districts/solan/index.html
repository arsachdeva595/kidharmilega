ODOP Product

Mushrooms

Solan is home to the Directorate of Mushroom Research (DMR), India's premier mushroom research institute, providing technical support and certified spawn to farmers. The district's cool alpine climate (1500-2200m altitude), high humidity, and established infrastructure make it ideal for commercial mushroom farming. Over 500 active mushroom farms and 40+ years of cultivation expertise create a mature ecosystem.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Solan.

1
Learn / Research
Visit DMR Solan headquarters and connect with 3-4 established mushroom farmers in Solan town cluster. Attend DMR's 15-day mushroom cultivation training program (₹5000). Join Solan Mushroom Farmers Association and spend 2 weeks shadowing a successful farm.
2
Source Raw Materials
Procure certified spawn from DMR spawn production unit (₹40-60/packet) or HIMFED. Source agricultural waste from local mills around Solan and Baddi. First batch cost: ₹30000-50000 for 500-1000 kg capacity farming unit.
3
Produce / Partner
Either set up own controlled environment structure (polyhouse/shed) costing ₹2-5L, or partner with existing farmers under contract model. Engage DMR's extension service (free) for strain selection and waste processing. Start with oyster mushrooms (easiest), then button/shiitake varieties.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, 10 min), obtain IEC code from nearest APEDA office in Chandigarh. Register organic certification through NPOP via local certifier (₹15000-25000). Create brand name, design packaging with nutritional info (GI-style visual). Obtain FSSAI license (₹5000-10000).
5
First Sale
List on Amazon Fresh and Blink in Chandigarh/Mohali with 5kg weekly supply commitment. Approach 10 restaurants in Chandigarh/Panchkula directly with samples. Partner with 1-2 local vegetable distributors supplying Delhi NCR markets. Launch Instagram account showcasing farm-to-table story. Secure first B2B contract worth ₹50000/month.
6
Scale
Increase capacity to 5000kg/month through additional sheds. Develop value-added product line (dried mushrooms, powder). Launch own e-commerce store using Shopify. Secure export orders through APEDA members. Hire 2-3 staff and systematize operations. Target ₹25-30L annual revenue by month 12.

  • Run Google Shopping ads targeting 'organic mushrooms near me' and 'button mushrooms online' in metros. Retarget website visitors with 20% discount on repeat purchases. Allocate ₹30K/month for paid search.
