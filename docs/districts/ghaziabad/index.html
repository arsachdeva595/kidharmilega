Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Ghaziabad.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Ghaziabad's industrial clusters at Mandi Road, Hapur Road, and SIDC area; meet 10-15 established engineering goods manufacturers; understand supply chains for raw materials sourcing from Jaipur and Bhiwadi steel markets; attend quarterly industry meetings at Ghaziabad Chamber of Commerce
2
Source Raw Materials
Source mild steel and cast iron from Jaipur Steel Market or JSPL suppliers at ₹45-55/kg; electrical components from Chandni Chowk Delhi wholesalers; fasteners from Faridabad industrial zone; initial 10-ton batch investment approximately ₹8-12 lakhs depending on product category
3
Produce / Partner
Identify 2-3 small-scale manufacturing units in Mandi Road cluster offering contract manufacturing services; negotiate production terms at ₹15-25/unit labor cost; alternatively, acquire basic machinery (CNC turning machine ₹15-20L, drilling press ₹5-8L) and hire 3-5 skilled technicians from local ITI institutes
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam on msme.gov.in as small manufacturing unit; apply for IEC code from local DGFT office (Ghaziabad); obtain ISO 9001 certification (₹80-150K) for B2B credibility; register brand name under Ministry of Commerce; file GST registration within 30 days
5
First Sale
List on IndiaMART and TradeKey with technical specifications and certifications; contact 20-30 local OEM procurement managers directly via LinkedIn and email; participate in Engineering Goods India expo; join FIEO for export promotion; engage C-level contacts through Ghaziabad industrial associations
6
Scale
Achieve ₹2-5 Cr annual revenue by month 18; invest in ISO certifications (9001, 14001, 45001); set up in-house testing lab; build direct relationships with 5-10 anchor clients; explore new product categories within engineering goods; establish captive manufacturing unit if margins support it
