Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Kallakurichi.

1
Learn / Research
Visit established rice mills in Kallakurichi (Chinnasalem taluk cluster) and Ariyalur; attend Tamil Nadu Agricultural Department workshops on premium rice varieties; study export buyer preferences through APEDA database and trade fairs in Chennai.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source paddy from farmer cooperatives in Kallakurichi district (₹1,200-1,500 per quintal); secure initial 50 quintals (₹6-7.5L) from local agricultural mandis; establish farmer contracts for consistent supply.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established rice millers in Chinnasalem (₹3-4L for custom milling of 50 quintals); OR invest in small-scale huller and polisher machines (₹25-40L) for in-house processing; ensure proper drying, storage, and quality control facilities.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Micro/Small Enterprise) with local office; apply for IEC code through DGFT; obtain FSSAI license for food processing; create brand identity with packaging design; register trademark with IP office.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh, BigBasket, and Jio Mart with ₹3-5L initial inventory; approach local premium grocery stores and restaurants in Chennai, Bangalore; use WhatsApp Business for B2B orders; create Instagram/Facebook content showcasing farm-to-table story.
6
Scale
Secure PMEGP subsidy (₹20-25L) for capacity expansion; develop 2-3 premium rice varieties with distinct positioning; build direct B2B relationships with restaurant chains; explore APEDA-supported export programs to Middle East; scale D2C with subscription box model.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Create YouTube farm-to-table documentary series showing Kallakurichi paddy cultivation, harvesting, and milling process; share on YouTube, Instagram Reels to build brand authority and appeal to health-conscious urban consumers.
  • Partner with 10-15 premium restaurants in Chennai and Bangalore offering exclusive 'Kallakurichi House Rice' at premium pricing; leverage restaurant menus and chef testimonials in social media campaigns.
  • Run Google Shopping ads targeting keywords like 'premium basmati rice,' 'organic Kallakurichi rice,' and 'export-quality rice online' with 20-30% discount on first orders; track conversion through UTM codes.
Government Schemes

Free money you didn't know about

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (25% for general, 35% for SC/ST) for food processing businesses with job creation.
🏛
APEDA Export Promotion Scheme
5-10% subsidy on export promotion and participation in international trade fairs for agricultural products.
🏚
Tamil Nadu Chief Minister's Comprehensive Health Insurance Scheme
supports MSME food processors with subsidized health and crop insurance coverage.
🏚
Tamil Nadu Startup Fund for Food Processing
grants up to ₹10L for registered food processing startups with government support for manufacturing infrastructure.
