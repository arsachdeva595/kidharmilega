Pan Meethi and Spices processing

Nagaur has a 500+ year legacy of spice processing and Pan Meethi manufacturing, with ideal agro-climatic conditions for growing and processing aromatic herbs and spices. The district's established artisan networks, local expertise in traditional blending techniques, and proximity to major spice-growing regions make it the natural choice for quality production and bulk supply.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Nagaur.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Nagaur spice market clusters in Asan Road and Mandi area; connect with 5-10 established producers; attend Food Processing exhibitions in Delhi/Mumbai; research GI applications for Pan Meethi to understand competitive positioning
2
Source Raw Materials
Source dried herbs, spices from local Nagaur wholesalers (₹2-3 lakhs for initial 100kg batch); procure betel leaves from Madhya Pradesh/Maharashtra suppliers (₹40-60 per kg); contact Nagaur artisans for traditional recipe blends; negotiate bulk rates for 500kg+ orders
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with licensed food processors in Nagaur MIDC or SET UP a small 500 sq ft processing unit (requires FSSAI license); hire 3-4 skilled blenders from local community; establish quality control protocols; invest in basic packaging machinery (pouch sealing, labeling) or outsource initially
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam (MSME) scheme within 2 weeks; apply for IEC code from nearest port authority; obtain FSSAI Food License (₹5,000-12,000); register trademark with IP office (brand name); create BIS-compliant packaging with nutritional info
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon, Flipkart, JioMart with 50+ units; reach out to 20 local kirana distribution networks in Nagaur; pitch to premium grocery chains (Nature's Basket, Auro); create WhatsApp Business catalog for B2B bulk buyers; send samples to 10 export trading houses in Delhi/Mumbai
6
Scale
After month 6, expand product line (add flavored variants); secure distributor partnerships in 3-5 states; develop export packaging to enter Middle East markets; invest ₹50-75L in automated processing to increase capacity 3x; launch influencer marketing on Instagram targeting health/wellness niche
