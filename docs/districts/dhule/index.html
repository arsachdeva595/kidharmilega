ODOP Product

De-Oiled Cake

Dhule is the second-largest cotton-producing district in Maharashtra with a century-old cotton ginning and oil extraction infrastructure. The region's established cotton seed crushing mills, favorable agro-climatic conditions, and proximity to major textile and pharmaceutical hubs in Gujarat and Madhya Pradesh make it ideal for processing de-oiled cake into value-added biochemical products. Existing supply chains and technical expertise reduce startup friction.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Dhule.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Dhule's cotton oil extraction cluster in Dhule city and Nardana; connect with Dhule District Industries Center (DIC); study existing processors like Bhadra Oil Mills; understand API grades required by pharmaceutical buyers via pharma clusters in Aurangabad and Gujarat
2
Source Raw Materials
Procure cotton seeds from Dhule Agricultural Produce Market (APM) at ₹4,500-5,500 per quintal; establish direct contracts with 2-3 cotton ginning mills (initial batch: 50-100 tons = ₹22-27 lakh); identify premium seed suppliers in Aurangabad and Parbhani districts
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing APEDA-registered cotton oil mills in Dhule for initial contract crushing (processing cost ₹400-600 per ton); alternatively, lease space in Dhule Industrial Area and install small-scale solvent extraction unit (₹15-20 lakh capital); hire technical staff from local cotton oil processing community
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME Portal) as food processing unit; obtain IEC from nearest customs office (Jalgaon or Nagpur); apply for ISO 22000 and ISO 9001 certification (₹2-3 lakh, 4-6 weeks); register under food safety authority with FSSAI license for human-grade applications
5
First Sale
Contact pharmaceutical enzyme manufacturers via IndiaMART B2B and Trade India; approach livestock feed manufacturers through PigBiZaff and AnimalNutrition.asia networks; connect with export distributors via APEDA-registered exporter databases; pitch samples to biopesticide companies in Jalgaon and Wardha via local chambers of commerce
6
Scale
Achieve 500+ TPM capacity by month 8; establish direct procurement contracts with 5+ cotton oil mills; launch ISO 2000 certified grades for pharmaceutical applications (20% margin uplift); develop regional distribution network across Maharashtra, Gujarat, Telangana; explore futures contracts via National Commodity Exchange (NCDEX) for price stability

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Create technical whitepapers on de-oiled cake protein content (35-40%) and amino acid profiles; distribute via pharmaceutical and enzyme industry LinkedIn groups and trade publications like Pharma & Biotech Today
  • Sponsor booth at India Pharma Expo (January) and Feed India Expo (annual); conduct on-site quality demonstrations showcasing oil content (%<8) and protein assay results
  • Launch YouTube technical channel explaining extraction methods, biochemical uses (enzyme production, biopesticide formulation), and quality standards; target AgriTech channels and procurement managers
