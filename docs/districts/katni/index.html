ODOP Product

Cut Stone

Katni district sits on rich geological deposits of high-quality sandstone and limestone in its rocky hills, ideal for extraction and cutting. The district has a 300+ year tradition of stone cutting and masonry, with established quarries and skilled artisan clusters. Proximity to major markets like Indore, Jabalpur, and Delhi makes logistics efficient for builders and architects.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Katni.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Katni stone clusters in Badrinath, Narpat, and Dhubela villages; meet 5-10 quarry owners and cutting workshops; attend Katni Stone Cutting Association meetings; study price lists on Indore builder platforms and international architecture suppliers.
2
Source Raw Materials
Contact quarry owners in Katni tehsil for bulk sandstone block contracts (₹25,000-40,000 per truck, 10-15 tons); negotiate quarterly supply of raw blocks; arrange transport to your cutting yard in Katni town or Indore. First batch budget: ₹3-5 lakhs for 20-25 tons of raw blocks.
3
Produce / Partner
Set up a cutting workshop (500-1000 sqft) in Katni's industrial zone with water access; install stone cutting machines (gang saw, multi-blade cutter) from local suppliers or Jaipur; hire 3-4 skilled stone cutters from local artisan clusters; begin with standard cuts before moving to custom carving.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) immediately; obtain IEC code from nearest Customs office in Jabalpur; register with Katni District Chamber of Commerce; create brand identity (logo, business cards) emphasizing heritage and sustainability; file GST as manufacturing unit; apply for state quality certification.
5
First Sale
List on IndiaMART and TradeKey as 'Katni Stone Supplier'; approach 20-30 architects via LinkedIn and portfolio outreach; partner with interior design studios in Mumbai, Bangalore, Delhi; cold-call real estate developers and luxury home builders; create lookbook with stone samples and send to export importers in EU, US, Australia.
6
Scale
After 6 months, open a showroom in Indore or Mumbai to showcase finished pieces; hire a logistics manager to streamline exports; develop custom carving capability (add sculptors); launch B2B WhatsApp/Telegram group for contractors; attend international trade shows (Surfaces India, BuildX, Stone and Tile Expo UK); build referral partnerships with 5-10 top architects.
