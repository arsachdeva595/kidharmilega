ODOP Product

Queen Pineapple and Pineapple Based Products

Dhalai's tropical climate, well-drained laterite soil, and consistent rainfall create ideal conditions for Queen Pineapple cultivation. The region has a 50-year cultivation tradition with established farming networks, and the GI status validates its superior quality and uniqueness compared to other Indian pineapple regions.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Dhalai.

1
Learn / Research
Visit pineapple clusters in Dhalai district (Kamalpur, Khowai blocks), connect with Tripura Horticulture Department, meet established growers at Agartala Agricultural Produce Market; study cold chain logistics and processing standards for Northeast exports
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fruit directly from 3-5 farmer partners in Dhalai at ₹15-25 per kg during peak season (March-June); first batch of 5 tons costs ₹75,000-125,000; establish collection centers in Khowai town for aggregation
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing small processing units in Agartala (Assam-type juice making units available at ₹2-8L rental), or set up basic processing facility with cold storage (₹15-25L capital); hire local women's SHGs for value-added product preparation
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam as food processing MSE; obtain FSSAI food business license (₹2000-5000); apply for IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Agartala; file GI-protected product trademark with 'Dhalai Queen' positioning; comply with APEDA export norms
5
First Sale
Launch on JioMart, Blinkit, LocalOkali within Tripura/Assam first; approach modern retail chains (Spencer's, Nature's Basket); contact export aggregators (Agrotech Foods, Northeast Organic); build direct B2B with juice manufacturers and restaurants via WhatsApp/email outreach
6
Scale
Expand processing capacity to 20-30 tons/season after 6 months; develop 3-4 SKUs (juice, jam, canned); register with state cooperative society for bulk government procurement; pursue APEDA organic certification; establish export partnerships with 2-3 countries; hire dedicated supply chain manager

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Create Instagram Reels and YouTube shorts showcasing farm-to-table journey from Dhalai orchards to kitchen; partner with regional food bloggers and nutrition influencers (50K-500K followers) for organic reach
  • Run targeted Facebook ads to urban metros (Delhi, Bangalore, Mumbai) highlighting GI certification and health benefits (Vitamin C, bromelain); use WhatsApp broadcasts for B2B restaurant/retailer outreach with bulk pricing
  • Establish presence at Agartala weekly farmer markets and premium food expos; create sampling booths in malls (Spencer's, Woodland); distribute physical samples at gyms and health centers emphasizing tropical superfood narrative
