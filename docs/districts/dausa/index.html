ODOP Product

Stone Product

Dausa has naturally abundant high-quality sandstone and limestone deposits with centuries-old stone extraction and carving tradition. The district's skilled artisans and established stone clusters make it the ideal sourcing point for architectural, decorative, and heritage stone products. Proximity to marble and granite corridors plus rail connectivity to metros provides strong logistics advantage.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Dausa.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Dausa stone clusters in Bassi, Lalsot, and Dausa town; meet 3-5 established quarry operators and artisans; attend annual Rajasthan Mining & Stone Expo; research competitor pricing on Indiamart and TradeKey
2
Source Raw Materials
Contact quarry associations in Dausa (Dausa Stone Merchants Association); negotiate for 50-100 tons first batch at ₹80,000-₹150,000 per ton depending on grade and finish; arrange transport via contract haulers (₹15,000-₹25,000 per truck)
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established artisan groups or small workshops in Bassi and Lalsot for custom carving (5-15% premium over raw block cost); or invest in basic cutting/polishing equipment (₹8-₹15L) if scaling production; hire 5-10 skilled workers on contract
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME portal) within 2 weeks; apply for IEC code via DGFT portal (1-2 weeks); trademark brand name via IP India; get GST registration (18% applicable); certify stone quality via Indian Bureau of Mines or buy third-party international certifications (ISO 9001) for export credibility
5
First Sale
List on Indiamart, Tradeindia, Alibaba with high-quality stone product photos; reach out directly to 200+ architects via LinkedIn and email with portfolio; contact heritage boards (ASI) for tender opportunities; approach 10-15 real estate developers in Delhi-NCR, Mumbai, Bangalore with samples and quotes
6
Scale
After 6 months revenue, invest in branded showroom in Delhi or Mumbai; hire dedicated export sales person; apply for APEDA/EPC benefits for export growth; diversify into premium decorative tiles and cladding; establish relationships with 3-5 large construction companies for repeat orders
