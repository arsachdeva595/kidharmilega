Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Nawada.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Nawada's weaver clusters in Nalanda Road and Tanti Bazaar areas; spend 2-3 weeks learning loom types, yarn quality, and design documentation from master weavers; understand traditional patterns like Tasar weaves and cotton dhoti designs.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source cotton yarn (20s-40s count) from Ludhiana spinners at ₹180-220/kg; silk yarn from Karnataka suppliers at ₹400-500/kg; natural dyes from Indigo Planters (Bihar) or Vardhman Chemicals; first 100m batch budget: ₹25,000-35,000.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with registered weaver SHGs in Nawada (contact NIELIT Nawada or District Handicrafts Office); negotiate per-piece rates (₹80-150 per meter for basic cotton, ₹200-300 for silk); start with 5-10 looms producing 50m/week.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSE portal) within 48 hours for ₹0; apply for IEC code via DGFT within 2-3 weeks for export eligibility; trademark brand name via IP India (₹4,500-9,000); get Geographical Indication certification application started with District Administration.
5
First Sale
Launch Instagram shop and basic Shopify store with product photography (₹15,000 for professional shoot); list 20-30 SKUs on Etsy with SEO-optimized descriptions; reach out to 50 boutiques via email with wholesale price sheets (₹200-300 per meter); target 5-8 initial orders in month 1.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to 20-25 looms; approach larger retailers (FabIndia-like networks); launch recurring subscription box (₹2,999/month for curated fabrics); apply for SFURTI grant to establish a design center; build export pipeline with agents in US and EU.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram Reels showcasing loom-to-garment journey featuring real weavers; use hashtags #HandmadeInBihar #SustainableFashion #ArtisanTextiles; post 4x weekly with behind-the-scenes content targeting 25-45 year old women.
  • Partner with 10-15 eco-conscious fashion bloggers and micro-influencers (50K-500K followers) for unboxing and styling content; offer 30% commission on sales via affiliate links.
  • Run Google Shopping ads targeting 'handwoven sarees', 'organic cotton fabric', and 'sustainable textiles' with ₹500-800/day budget; focus on USA, UK, Germany keywords with 20-25% margins.
