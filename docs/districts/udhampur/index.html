Garlic

Udhampur's cool mountain climate, altitude of 600-900m, and fertile alluvial soils create optimal conditions for premium garlic cultivation. The region's established farmer networks and agricultural tradition span decades, with sustainable practices ensuring superior bulb quality and natural disease resistance ideal for both domestic and export markets.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Udhampur.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Udhampur's agricultural extension office and meet cluster farmers near Ramnagar town; study garlic farming practices at Punjab Agricultural University (PAU) Ludhiana; research garlic varieties grown locally (White Agrifound, Yamuna Safed); connect with exporters at Amritsar Spice Market.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source seed cloves from certified farmers in Udhampur block (₹40,000-60,000 per quintal); buy vermicompost from local composting units (₹3,000-5,000 per quintal). First-batch cost for 1-2 acres: ₹150,000-250,000. Connect with Udhampur Horticulture Department for subsidized inputs.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 5-10 registered farmers via pre-harvest contracts; set up small processing unit (₹300,000-600,000) for garlic drying, peeling, and value-addition near Udhampur town. Use cold storage facilities in Udhampur or lease space (₹2,000-4,000/month).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSE online registration) + apply for IEC from nearest Export Promotion Council office in Jammu. Brand name registration at IP office. Get FSSAI registration for processed products. ISO 9001/14001 certification for export eligibility.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon, Flipkart, BigBasket under premium organic garlic category; approach regional restaurant chains via WhatsApp bulk catalogs; list on Jumbish, Edible, and NuttyGritty e-commerce; register with APEDA and contact export houses in Bangalore and Gujarat.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to 5-10 acres farming area; set up dedicated processing unit with drying tunnels (₹800,000); hire packaging and logistics team; apply for ISO certifications for European exports; launch 'Udhampur Himalayan Garlic' brand across tier-2 city modern trade.
