Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps specific to Varanasi and Banarasi Silk.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Madanpura weaver cluster — talk to 10 weavers before spending anything
2
Source Raw Materials
Source silk yarn from Sualkuchi Assam or Kanchipuram — budget ₹15-20K for first batch
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 master weavers for first 6 months — pay per piece
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME (free) + create Instagram page + get logo designed (₹2-5K)
5
First Sale
List on Etsy and Amazon Handmade — post 3 reels showing the weaving process
6
Scale
Apply for UP ODOP Margin Money Scheme (up to ₹10L) after first sale
