Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Bemetara.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bemetara agricultural extension office and connect with Raipur Food Processing Cluster (Mandir Hasaud area). Study successful papaya processors in nearby Rajnandgaon and Durg districts. Attend CFTRI Mysore short course on fruit preservation.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source papayas directly from Bemetara farmers (₹8-12/kg, seasonal peaks June-September). Partner with 2-3 farmer groups for consistency. First batch requirement: 500kg papayas = ₹6,000-8,000. Procure packaging from Raipur suppliers (sterilized jars, labels) at ₹50,000-70,000 for 5,000 units.
3
Produce / Partner
Start with co-processing facility in Raipur Food Park (available on rental basis, ₹15,000-25,000/month) or partner with existing FMCG units in Chhattisgarh. Hire food technician (₹15,000/month) to ensure FDA compliance. Produce first batch of 2,000 units with 40% margin.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) on udyamregistration.gov.in. Obtain FSSAI registration (₹5,000-10,000, mandatory). Apply for IEC code via DGFT portal. Register brand name with copyright office. Get GST registration (18% applicable on processed food).
5
First Sale
List on Amazon Pantry, Flipkart Fresh, JioMart Wholesale with ₹50,000-100,000 initial marketing budget. Approach modern retail buyers (Big Bazaar, More stores) in Raipur and Bilaspur with samples. Pitch to regional distributors like Rajesh Distributors (Raipur). Create Instagram and YouTube channels showcasing farm-to-bottle journey.
6
Scale
After 6 months with 500+ monthly units sold, expand product line (dried papaya, papaya leather, juice concentrate). Open second production batch to 5,000 units/month. Partner with regional FMCG for bulk manufacturing. Target ₹1.5-2Cr revenue by month 18 through Tier-2 city distribution.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Partner with micro-influencers (50K-500K followers) in Tier-2 cities posting organic/health content. Provide free samples and create unboxing/taste-test reels on Instagram/YouTube Shorts. Target ₹1,000-2,000 per influencer monthly.
  • Run Google Shopping ads targeting 'organic papaya jam' and 'natural fruit pickle' keywords in Hindi-speaking regions. Allocate ₹15,000/month for 6 months. Focus on Raipur, Indore, Nagpur metros with 25-30% conversion target.
  • Sponsor local health/wellness events in Raipur and Bilaspur. Partner with yoga centers and health clubs for in-store sampling and 15% discount codes. Build email list for repeat purchases. Create WhatsApp business channel for product updates.
