ODOP Product

Groundnuts

Mungeli's black soil and semi-arid climate create ideal growing conditions for groundnuts with enhanced flavour profiles. The district has a 40+ year tradition of groundnut cultivation with established supply chains, skilled farming communities, and consistent yields of 15-18 quintal/hectare. Proximity to major agricultural markets in MP and Chhattisgarh ensures reliable raw material sourcing and distribution networks.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Mungeli.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Mungeli's Jagatpur and Chhuikhadan agricultural clusters; interview 5+ established groundnut farmers and traders; study Chhattisgarh Rajya Beejan Nigam (seed supplier) and local mandis like Mungeli APMC for pricing and supply trends
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw groundnuts from Mungeli APMC or direct farmer cooperatives like Chhattisgarh Krishi Vikas Nyaya; budget ₹8,000-12,000 per quintal (40-50 quintals for first batch = ₹3.2-6 lakhs); negotiate 2-3% storage facility access
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established groundnut processing units in Raipur or Bilaspur (drying, roasting, sorting); or invest in compact roasting & packaging machinery (₹4-8 lakhs); employ 2-4 skilled workers trained in quality control
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam (MSME portal, free); apply for IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Raipur; obtain FSSAI Food Business License (₹2,000-5,000); register trademark on iPASSI (₹4,500); use packaging with nutritional labels and GI claim disclaimer
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon, Flipkart, JioMart with ₹50-150 per pack pricing; reach out to 20+ regional FMCG distributors via direct contact; list on B2B platforms like IndiaMART and TradeKey; approach 50+ restaurants/cafes for bulk supply trials
6
Scale
Expand product line (spreads, flour, oil); secure 2-3 large institutional buyers (hotels, corporate canteens); invest in export certification (APEDA); build direct-to-consumer via Instagram & WhatsApp; target 30-40% YoY revenue growth through seasonal campaigns

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Partner with 20-30 health & wellness Instagram accounts (50K-500K followers) in Delhi, Bangalore, Mumbai for product seeding and reels; budget ₹1.5-3 lakhs per quarter
  • Run Google Shopping & Facebook/Instagram ads targeting 'healthy snacks,' 'protein rich foods,' 'organic groundnuts' with ₹2-5 lakhs monthly budget; focus on 28-45 age demographic in Tier 1 cities
  • Launch referral program offering ₹100 discount per successful friend referral on D2C website; combine with WhatsApp marketing to existing customers to drive repeat orders at 35-40% lower CAC
