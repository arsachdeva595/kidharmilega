ODOP Product

Pineapple

Ri-Bhoi's high rainfall (above 200 cm annually), acidic volcanic soil, and humid subtropical climate create ideal conditions for pineapple cultivation. Small-scale farmers have perfected traditional intercropping methods that enhance flavor. The district's elevation and microclimate reduce pest pressure, making organic certification easier and commanding premium prices in national and export markets.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Ri-Bhoi.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Ri-Bhoi's main production cluster in Umiam and Nongpoh blocks; interview 10-15 established farmers; attend Meghalaya Agricultural Department's training camps (contact: Ri-Bhoi District Agriculture Office, Shillong); study pricing on APEDA export databases.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified pineapple saplings from Meghalaya State Horticulture Department nurseries (₹3-5 per sapling for 1,000 units = ₹5,000-8,000). Procure compost locally from neighboring villages (₹200/quintal, need 50 quintals = ₹10,000). First-batch setup: ₹50,000-80,000 for 0.5 acres with irrigation lines.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 5-10 small farmer cooperatives in Umiam block via formal cultivation agreements; provide inputs, training, and buy-back guarantees. Register as Primary Producer Organization (PPO) with SFURTI to enable bulk aggregation. Set up a small collection and grading center in Nongpoh (₹4-6 lakhs for equipment and structure).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (proprietorship/partnership) at udyamregistration.gov.in; apply for IEC code via APEDA portal for export eligibility. Create brand identity: design logo emphasizing 'Meghalaya Organic' or 'Ri-Bhoi Heritage'; register on FSSAI ROCS portal; get organic certification (NPOP) via certification body in Assam (3-4 months, ₹15,000-25,000).
5
First Sale
Launch on Blinkit and Zepto via Nongpoh/Shillong micro-fulfillment centers with ₹2-3/piece margin; partner with BigBasket's Preferreds program (apply directly). Use WhatsApp Business and Instagram for bulk B2B inquiry capture. Contact 20 food processing units in Assam (Dibrugarh, Guwahati) with samples and MOQ quotes.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to 2-3 acres and hire a logistics partner (ALLCARGO, Delhivery) for metro cities. Build bulk orders with 3-4 juice brands targeting ₹2-3 Cr annual revenue. Explore FPO (Farmer Producer Organization) model to aggregate 100+ farmers and unlock government cold chain subsidies. Launch direct export to Bangladesh via BIMSTEC trade corridor by month 10-12.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Partner with micro-influencers (50K-200K followers) in Delhi/Bangalore food wellness niches for organic pineapple recipe content; offer 10-15% affiliate commission on Blinkit/Zepto links (₹3,000-5,000/month budget).
  • Run B2B WhatsApp campaigns targeting juice brands and restaurant groups in NE India; share phytosanitary certificates, brix scores, and bulk pricing; aim for 5-10 enterprise contracts per quarter.
  • Sponsor 'Organic & Local' social media campaigns on Instagram Reels highlighting Ri-Bhoi farming practices and farm worker stories (₹1-2 lakhs/quarter); target health-conscious urban mothers aged 28-45.
