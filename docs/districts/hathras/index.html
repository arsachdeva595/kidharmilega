ODOP Product

Hing (Asafoetida)

Hathras has centuries-old expertise in hing sourcing and processing from ferula plants grown in arid regions. The district's climate, water resources, and established supply chains from Afghanistan and Iran make it the natural aggregation center for India's best asafoetida. Local traders have built deep relationships with international suppliers and domestic spice merchants.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Hathras.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Hathras city spice market cluster (near Hathras Railway Station area) and connect with established hing traders like those in Hathras Spice Association. Spend 2-3 weeks understanding sourcing from Iran/Afghanistan via Kandahar imports, quality grading (A/B/C), and pricing dynamics (₹8,000-₹15,000/kg wholesale range).
2
Source Raw Materials
Source 100kg initial batch from established Hathras wholesalers (Budget: ₹12-18 lakhs for premium grade). Contact traders near Hathras Railway Station or work with HATHRAS SPICE MERCHANTS cooperative. Negotiate payment terms and GI certification proof.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with local Hathras processors for repackaging, grading, and standardization rather than starting raw ferula processing (requires complex equipment). Connect with HATHRAS DISTRICT COOPERATIVE SPICE FEDERATION for certified processing units.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Micro/Small Enterprise) online within 2 weeks. Apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) if planning exports. Get FSSAI license (Food Safety certification) — critical for retail sales. Register GI protected product name as per Hathras Hing GI specifications with trade ministry.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Pantry and Flipkart Grocery with 50kg initial stock (₹15-18/gram retail). Simultaneously contact spice wholesalers in Mumbai's Bhagirath Palace and Delhi's Khari Baoli. Reach out to Ayurvedic brands like Baidyanath and Patanjali for B2B bulk orders.
6
Scale
After 6 months and ₹50L+ revenue, expand to premium export packaging and ship to UAE markets via Spice Board's Export Promotion scheme. Build direct relationships with 3-4 Middle Eastern distributors. Launch DTC brand variant at ₹399/10g for Amazon Fresh and online wellness platforms.
