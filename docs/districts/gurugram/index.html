Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Gurugram.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Gurugram's textile clusters in Sector 37, Badshahpur industrial area, and Manesar; meet 5-10 existing apparel manufacturers; attend India Fashion Week events; study competitor pricing at retail outlets in DLF Cyberhub and Ambience Mall.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source cotton and polyester yarn from Tiruppur mills (₹80-120/kg) or Delhi yarn traders; connect with dye suppliers in Okhla, Delhi (₹150-300/kg); negotiate first batch order of 500-1000 kg, budget ₹8-12 lakhs for materials.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing tailoring units in Badshahpur or Manesar for initial 100-500 units; negotiate per-unit production rates of ₹80-150 for basic apparel; alternatively, lease space in Sector 37 industrial complex for ₹30-50k/month and hire 5-10 skilled tailors at ₹12-18k/month.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Ministry of MSME) for tax benefits; obtain IEC code from nearest customs office in Gurugram; register trademark at IP India for brand name; apply for FSSAI if adding textile dyeing; open current account with ICICI or HDFC for B2B payments.
5
First Sale
Launch on Shopify or WooCommerce for D2C; list on Flipkart Seller and Amazon Launchpad for mass reach; reach out to 20 fashion boutiques in Gurugram, Delhi, Bangalore with sample collections; contact wholesale platforms like Goodbuy or Makers Village; offer 15% early-adopter discounts.
6
Scale
After ₹10L revenue, hire dedicated marketing and sales team; expand product range to 50+ SKUs; secure 2-3 anchor B2B clients (corporate uniform contracts worth ₹50L+); explore overseas distributors in US/UK through TradeKey; implement ERP software for inventory; move to 2000-3000 sq ft facility in Sector 37.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram influencer partnership with micro-influencers (50k-500k followers) in fashion niche; create 5-10 reels showcasing manufacturing process and product quality; target cost ₹5k-15k per influencer for 3-4 posts.
  • LinkedIn B2B campaign targeting fashion retailers, procurement managers at brands; promote bulk order discounts and customization capabilities; allocate ₹30k/month for targeted ads.
  • Participate in India Fashion Week and National Textile Fair expos in Delhi-NCR; set up booth with product samples; allocate ₹2-3 lakhs for booth setup and promotional materials to generate 50+ B2B leads.
