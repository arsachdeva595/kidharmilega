ODOP Product

Stone Carving

Jajpur has a centuries-old tradition of stone carving rooted in its proximity to Jagannath temple culture and historical temple construction. The district's abundant local stone quarries and hereditary artisan communities make it a natural hub for high-quality sculptural work. The region's deep connection to mythological narratives and classical Odia architecture ensures authentic, culturally resonant designs.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Jajpur.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Jajpur artisan clusters in Korei and Bari blocks; spend 1 week observing production at established workshops; connect with Jajpur Stone Carvers Association; attend Odisha Handicraft Expo to understand market dynamics and competitor positioning
2
Source Raw Materials
Source sandstone and limestone from Jajpur quarries near Korei (₹2,000-3,000 per ton); purchase carving tools from Cuttack handicraft tool suppliers (₹30,000-50,000 for starter kit); negotiate first batch of 5-10 pieces with local artisans at ₹15,000-25,000 per piece
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 established artisans in Jajpur via revenue-sharing model (50-60% to artisan, 40-50% margin for you); start with smaller pieces (decorative wall panels, small idols) requiring 15-30 days production; scale to larger architectural pieces after first 6 months of quality validation
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME in Jajpur with artisan partnership documentation; obtain IEC code for export capability; file trademark for brand name with IP Office; create Heritage Certification document linking to Jajpur's ODOP status; GST registration as Handicraft producer
5
First Sale
Launch Instagram/Facebook with high-quality artisan photography and time-lapse carving videos; list on Myntra Luxe, Artifact Heritage, and Etsy with ₹25,000-150,000 price points; reach out to 50 architects/interior designers via LinkedIn with custom quote requests; partner with 3-5 heritage hotels in Odisha for exclusive collections
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to 5-8 artisan partnerships; develop 3-4 signature product lines (temple doors, wall panels, sculptures); launch B2B portal for bulk orders; establish studio in Jajpur town center for direct retail; apply for export subsidy and participate in international trade shows (Frankfurt, NY)

  • Partner with 10-15 interior design influencers to feature custom stone pieces in high-end home makeover projects; offer 20-30% commission per verified sale through unique referral links
