ODOP Product

Kosa

Janjagir-Champa has a rich tradition of sericulture and silk weaving dating back centuries, with established silk cocoon farming in the region and skilled artisan communities perfecting Kosa weaving techniques. The district's climate and agro-ecology are ideal for mulberry cultivation and silk production, supporting a dense network of weavers and dyers.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Janjagir-champa.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Janjagir town weaver clusters and Tamnar block silk hubs; spend 2-3 weeks with established Kosa weavers; learn from Chhattisgarh Handloom Board office in Raipur; benchmark pricing at Jaypore, FabIndia, Fab City platforms
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw silk cocoons from Durg/Rajnandgaon district sericulture farmers (₹400-500/kg); natural dyes from Naya Raipur chemical suppliers; first batch of 50-100 meters fabric costs ₹40,000-60,000 via established artisan partnerships
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3-5 established Kosa weaver families in Janjagir town (0755-2234XXX Handloom Board); formalize as raw material supplier with advance payment model; schedule 1-month production cycles; quality-check at each stage
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam at udyamregistration.gov.in as textiles MSME; obtain IEC code from DGFT online portal; trademark brand name at ipindia.gov.in; apply for Handloom Mark certification from DC Handloom Ministry; set up GST registration
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy, Amazon Artisan, Jaypore, and own Shopify store with professional product photography; approach ethnic wear retailers in Delhi Khan Market, Mumbai Bandra; pitch to wedding planners via WeddingZilla; list on Made-in-India export platforms; send samples to 10 international boutiques
6
Scale
After first 6 months, expand artisan network to 8-10 weavers; introduce 3-4 new design variations; hire part-time marketer for Instagram/Pinterest content; apply for SFURTI cluster development grant; explore B2B bulk orders with hotel chains and corporate clients; set up Delhi/Mumbai showroom visit facility
