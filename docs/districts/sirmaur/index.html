ODOP Product

Packaging (Corrugated Boxes)

Sirmaur has strategic proximity to major distribution hubs in Punjab and Haryana, making it ideal for corrugated box manufacturing serving pan-India logistics networks. The district's emerging industrial infrastructure in Nahan and lower land costs compared to metros enable competitive manufacturing. Railway connectivity to major cities facilitates bulk shipment of finished corrugated boxes.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Sirmaur.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Nahan Industrial Estate and Paonta Sahib industrial clusters to study existing corrugated box units; connect with HP MSME Department; attend FIPB (Fibre Industry Promotion Board) workshops; analyze supply chain logistics to Delhi, Chandigarh, and Mumbai hubs.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source recycled cardboard and kraft paper from waste paper aggregators in Punjab (Jalandhar, Ludhiana) and Delhi NCR at ₹25-35/kg; purchase corrugating machinery from suppliers in Ahmedabad or Chennai (initial batch raw material cost: ₹12-15 lakhs for 10-ton monthly capacity setup).
3
Produce / Partner
Establish manufacturing unit in Nahan Industrial Estate with 2000-3000 sq ft space; invest in corrugating machine (₹18-22 lakhs), printing unit (₹8-10 lakhs), and cutting/folding equipment (₹5-7 lakhs); hire 8-10 skilled workers from local vocational institutes.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME on udyamregistration.gov.in; obtain IEC code from DGFT for export; register trademark with IP India; get BIS certification for corrugated boxes (IS 4046); obtain GST registration and pollution control clearance from HP Pollution Control Board.
5
First Sale
Launch B2B outreach to Flipkart, Amazon, Bata, ITC, and regional FMCG companies through direct sales team; list on platforms like IndiaMART and TradeKey; partner with logistics providers (Delhivery, Bluedart) for supply chain visibility; attend PLEXPACK exhibition (Mumbai) and PACK-PRINT India (Delhi).
6
Scale
Expand capacity to 50-ton monthly production by month 8; open regional distribution centers in Bangalore and Chennai; develop branded D2C e-commerce storefront; invest in automated printing technology; establish partnerships with 3-5 large FMCG brands for long-term contracts.
