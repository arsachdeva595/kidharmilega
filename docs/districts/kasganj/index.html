ODOP Product

Zari Zardozi

Kasganj has a 400+ year legacy of Zari Zardozi craftsmanship, with a concentrated cluster of skilled artisans trained in traditional techniques. The district's proximity to Delhi and Agra markets, combined with access to specialized materials through established suppliers, makes it the natural hub for this heritage craft. Generations of artisan families have perfected the art, creating a unique ecosystem of skill transfer and quality maintenance.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Kasganj.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kasganj's main embroidery clusters in Kotwali and Lachcha Bazar areas; spend 2-3 weeks shadowing master artisans; attend textile trade fairs in Delhi (Pragati Maidan) and Agra; learn costing, material sourcing, and market pricing from established traders.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source zari from authorized suppliers in Kasganj bazaar (₹80-120/spool) and Delhi's Chandni Chowk (bulk discounts at ₹60-90/spool); procure fabrics from Surat textile wholesalers; initial batch of 500 meters fabric + 200 spools zari + notions ≈ ₹80,000-120,000.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 master artisans in Kasganj on piecework basis (₹500-2,000 per garment depending on complexity); establish a 500 sq ft workshop for quality control and finishing; or commission production through established cooperatives like Kasganj Zari Zardozi Artisan Association.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME within 5 days online; apply for IEC code through DGFT portal (₹0 cost, online); register brand name with trademark office; obtain GST registration (18% on embroidered items); get ISO 9001 certification for export credibility (₹30,000-50,000).
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy, Instagram Shop, and Amazon Handmade targeting NRI and international wedding market; create a WhatsApp Business catalog for bulk orders; approach 15-20 bridal boutiques in Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore with portfolio samples; participate in wedding expos (Indian Wedding Expos across metros).
6
Scale
Secure second batch of artisans; launch custom design consultation service at ₹10,000-25,000 per project; establish B2B wholesale pricing for bulk orders; export 20-30% of production; partner with 2-3 fashion designers for capsule collections; open a pickup point in Delhi for faster urban reach.

Marketing Ideas
