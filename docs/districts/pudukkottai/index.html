Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Pudukkottai.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Pudukkottai MSME clusters around Avinashi Road and Natarajanpalayam; connect with existing manufacturers like Mahindra First Choice facilities; attend IMTEX trade shows in Bangalore; study IS 8000/ISO 9001 certification requirements.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from Steel Trading Complex in Chennai (₹15-20L for first batch); establish accounts with approved suppliers like Tata Steel, JSW Steel; negotiate 30-45 day payment terms; minimum first-batch cost ₹12-18L for 500kg steel stock.
3
Produce / Partner
Establish 2000-3000 sqft facility with CNC machines (lathe, milling, drilling), quality testing equipment; hire 3-5 experienced CNC operators (₹15-20K/month); partner with established local job-work providers initially; obtain machinery on lease/EMI basis.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME with district DPIIT office; apply for IEC code at Customs; obtain ISO 9001:2015 certification (₹80-120K); register trademark at IP Office; get GST registration; display manufacturing certifications prominently.
5
First Sale
Create LinkedIn manufacturing profile targeting procurement officers; join IndiaMART and TradeKey for B2B visibility; attend Autocar, Aero India, and DefExpo trade shows; send cold samples to Tier-1 automotive suppliers in Tamil Nadu; build relationships with 2-3 anchor customers before scaling.
6
Scale
Achieve ISO/TS 16949 automotive certification; expand machine capacity to ₹50L investment; hire quality engineer and sales team; establish vendor partnerships with 10+ OEM customers; explore aerospace/defense contracts through DPSUs; invest in ERP for production tracking.
