Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Chengalpattu.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Chengalpattu SIPCOT industrial area and Krishnagiri automotive cluster (30km away) to understand manufacturing standards, supplier networks, and quality benchmarks. Connect with SIAM (Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers) Tamil Nadu chapter and visit nearby component suppliers in Padi and Talegaon industrial areas.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from Bangalore steel markets (80km away) and Chennai-based suppliers. Initial raw material batch for small-scale production: ₹15-20 lakhs. Key suppliers: JSW Steel, Jindal Steel, or local distributors at SIPCOT; fasteners from Bengaluru fastener hub.
3
Produce / Partner
Start with job-work arrangement with existing manufacturers in Chengalpattu SIPCOT cluster (capital-efficient model), or establish small 5,000-10,000 sq ft facility with CNC machines, lathe machines, and quality testing equipment. Hire skilled technicians from local ITI colleges (Institute of Technology, Chengalpattu) with 2-3 years experience.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME online portal, obtain IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) from nearest DGFT office in Chennai. Get ISO 9001:2015 and TS 16949 certifications (automotive quality standard) within first 9 months. Establish brand through LinkedIn B2B profile and industry tradeshows.
5
First Sale
Approach automotive tier-1 suppliers and OEMs in Chennai-Sriperumbudur corridor through direct sales calls and samples. Register on TradeKey.com and Global Sources for export leads. Participate in Auto Expo and TAI (Tamil Nadu Automobile Manufacturers Association) events. Join SIAM directory.
6
Scale
After 6 months, add capacity with second shift operations. Invest in advanced quality control (CMM machines). Establish relationships with 3-4 OEM accounts to ensure steady orders. Explore ISO certifications (TS 16949, IATF), obtain ISO 14001 for environmental compliance, and launch specialized product line (EV components) within 18 months.
