ODOP Product

Khasi Mandarin (Orange)

East Khasi Hills possesses ideal agro-climatic conditions with high rainfall (7500mm annually), cool misty hills, and nutrient-rich soil that naturally produces Khasi mandarins with superior taste and aroma. The region's 4000+ year cultivation heritage and indigenous farming knowledge make it the only authentic source of this citrus variety globally.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for East Khasi Hills.

1
Learn / Research
Visit East Khasi Hills citrus cluster around Shillong-Cherrapunji zone; connect with 200+ active Khasi mandarin farmers via Meghalaya Horticulture Mission; attend ICAR-IIHR citrus training workshops; study GI certification database at Office of GI Registrar, Kolkata
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified mandarin saplings from ICAR Research Station, Shillong (₹15-20 per sapling); bulk organic inputs from Meghalaya State Organic Certification Board; first-batch establishment cost for 1-acre orchard: ₹4-5 lakhs including land preparation, saplings, irrigation setup
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established growers holding GI-certified orchards (average 2-5 acres each); negotiate contract farming with minimum 40-50% yield guarantee; establish cold chain linkage through Meghalaya Cold Chain Infrastructure network; implement GAP standards certified by APEDA
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam portal as Micro/Small Enterprise; obtain IEC from nearest APEDA-designated port (Kolkata or Cochin); get GI usage license from East Khasi Hills Mandarin Growers Association; register brand with trade mark office; obtain organic certification from MMRCSL (Meghalaya Medicinal and Aromatic Plants Board)
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh, BigBasket Premium, Jio Mart Organic with minimum 500kg monthly commitment; pitch to Nykaa Clean Beauty (premium segment); activate B2B via IndiaTradeSupply.com and TradeKey; approach 5-star hotel chains and Michelin restaurants in metros; list on Farmer-to-Consumer platforms (FreshToHome, LocalOrganic)
6
Scale
Expand to adjacent West Khasi Hills and Jaintia Hills for volume increase; invest in processing unit for juice/concentrate (₹20-30L for 5-ton capacity); establish cold storage facility (₹50-75L for 100-ton capacity); build export partnerships with fruit agents in UK and Singapore; launch 'Meghalaya Mandarin' B2B bulk export program by month 12; diversify into hybrid products (mandarin-ginger juice, candy)

Government Schemes

Free money you didn't know about

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Program)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy for micro-enterprises in agro-processing with 25% margin money; applicable for value-added products like juice/concentrate
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹10 crore cluster development grant for East Khasi Hills mandarin cluster; covers infrastructure, technology, branding
🏚
Meghalaya Horticulture Development Program
₹50,000-₹1,00,000 subsidy per acre for citrus plantation; zero-interest loans up to ₹5 lakh for farmers
🏚
Meghalaya Export Promotion Scheme
5% subsidy on FOB export value; ₹2 lakh grant for first-time agro-exporters; support for APEDA certification and packaging upgrades
