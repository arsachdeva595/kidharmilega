Agriculture Implements

Hisar is located in the agrarian belt of Haryana with strong agricultural tradition and demand for implements. The district has established manufacturing clusters with skilled labor, easy access to raw materials like steel and iron, and proximity to major agricultural markets across Haryana, Punjab, and Rajasthan.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Hisar.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Hisar's established implement clusters in Industrial Area A and B; connect with 5-10 existing manufacturers; attend IITM Hisar's agricultural technology workshops; study competition in nearby Rohtak and Karnal districts
2
Source Raw Materials
Source steel bars and iron sheets from Ludhiana steel merchants or Hisar local suppliers (₹15-20L for first batch); aluminum from Belgaum or local traders; wooden handles from Himachal Pradesh suppliers; first 500-unit raw material batch costs ₹18-22L
3
Produce / Partner
Lease 2000-3000 sq.ft. industrial shed in Hisar Industrial Area (₹8-12L per annum); hire 8-12 skilled fabricators from existing implement makers; start with contract manufacturing model before owning equipment; invest ₹12-15L in basic machinery (cutting, welding, drilling units)
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME within 48 hours on udyamregistration.gov.in; apply for IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Chandigarh; create brand at Registrar of Companies; obtain ISO 9001 certification (₹2-3L); file for trademark with IPO
5
First Sale
Launch on AgriTech platforms (Tractors Junction, Agri2connect); create WhatsApp catalog for dealer networks; approach regional agricultural cooperatives (Haryana Cooperative); list on Amazon Business & Flipkart B2B; contact state agricultural departments for G2B contracts
6
Scale
After ₹1Cr revenue, invest in CNC machinery and capacity expansion; develop 2-3 specialized product lines based on market feedback; establish distribution centers in Punjab, Rajasthan; pursue export certification (ISO, CE marking); hire dedicated export sales team targeting Asian markets

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Partner with agricultural input dealers across North India for co-branded promotions; offer 2-month financing through NBFC partners to reduce buyer friction
  • Create YouTube channel demonstrating implement usage; sponsor farm shows in Hisar, Rohtak, and Jind; feature farmer testimonials and yield improvements
  • Run WhatsApp group marketing targeting block-level agricultural officers and cooperative managers in 50+ blocks; offer bulk discounts and customization
