Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Almora.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Almora town market (Mall Road, Lala Bazar) and meet 3-5 established Bal Mithai makers (Amar Sweets, Halwai ki Gali artisans). Attend Nanda Devi Mela in Sept-Oct to observe seasonal demand. Connect with Almora Bakers Association and visit Uttarakhand Food Processing Directory.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source khoya from Ranikhet Dairy Cooperative or local milk producers (₹400-500/kg). Procure ghee from Himalayan Organics or certified dairies (₹600-800/kg). Buy sugar, maida, almonds from Almora wholesale market. First batch of 50kg costs ₹15,000-20,000 for raw materials.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing Bal Mithai artisans in Almora (contract manufacturing model) or set up small commercial kitchen in Almora town (₹300k-500k renovation). Obtain FSSAI registration as micro-unit (₹5k-10k). Start with 50-100kg batches; scale to 500kg/week by month 4.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) registration online (free). Apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) from DGFT for export potential. Design packaging with heritage narrative and regional imagery. Get FSSAI license and conduct quality testing via CSIR-IMTECH Chandigarh (₹5k-8k).
5
First Sale
Launch on JioMart, Amazon Fresh, Blinkit with ₹2,000-5,000 initial inventory. Pitch to 10 specialty sweet chains and premium confectioneries in Delhi-NCR. Create Instagram/WhatsApp catalog highlighting heritage story. Partner with 2-3 niche gift platforms (Arpit, Giftxoxo) for corporate gifting segment.
6
Scale
By month 7, expand to 5-6 e-commerce platforms and establish direct retail in 15 stores. Set up branded website with subscription boxes. Pursue GI certification to command premium pricing. Hire second artisan for production and open Almora-based showroom. Build export channel via specialty distributors in USA/UK.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Create short-form video content on Instagram/YouTube Shorts showing traditional Bal Mithai-making process in Almora kitchens; partner with 5-10 micro-influencers (100k-500k followers) in food/lifestyle niche for UGC seeding.
  • Launch seasonal email campaigns tied to Diwali, Holi, Nanda Devi Mela targeting NRI diaspora via curated lists; offer 15-20% early-bird discounts and free shipping on gifts above ₹1500.
  • Conduct product sampling at 10 high-end specialty stores in Delhi, Bangalore, Mumbai and corporate offices (HR teams) with story cards explaining Almora heritage and artisan details; target repeat B2B orders.
