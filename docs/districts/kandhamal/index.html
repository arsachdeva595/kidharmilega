Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Kandhamal.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kandhamal district clusters in Phiringia and Raikia blocks; connect with Kandhamal Turmeric Farmers Cooperative Society; study GI certification standards from Agricultural & Processed Food Products Export Development Authority (APEDA) guidelines; benchmark competitor brands on Amazon, Flipkart, and organic portals
2
Source Raw Materials
Source directly from certified organic farmers in Kandhamal villages (Turturi, Phulbani area) at ₹6,500-7,500/quintal; negotiate bulk contracts for 10-20 quintals first batch (₹65-150L); verify organic certification and GI tag compliance; establish supply agreements with 3-4 farmer groups for consistent quality
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing farmer cooperative societies or establish contract farming agreements; invest in basic processing unit (cleaning, drying, polishing) at Phulbani town cost ₹40-80L; or outsource processing to certified APEDA-registered units in Odisha; maintain organic chain of custody documentation
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) classification online; apply for IEC code from nearest DGFT office (Bhubaneswar); obtain Organic Certification from APEDA/NPOP-accredited agency (₹15-30L for first 3 years); trademark brand name at IP office; get GI tag usage rights from Kandhamal Turmeric Growers Association
5
First Sale
Launch on Flipkart, Amazon, Grofers with ₹2-5K investment; establish presence on Bhavin.com and other organic B2B platforms; reach out to Ayurveda Today, Forest Essentials distribution; create Instagram/YouTube content on organic farming story; participate in India Organic Farmers Fair; approach bulk buyers through APEDA export directory
6
Scale
Expand to value-added products (turmeric face packs, supplement capsules); establish brand presence at 500+ modern trade outlets through distributor partnerships; build export contracts with EU/US importers (target 40% export revenue by Year 2); develop farmer cooperative model to increase sourcing volume to 100+ quintals annually; invest in modern processing/packaging unit (₹200L+) after 12 months positive cash flow
