ODOP Product

Milk Product

Bhiwani has a strong pastoral heritage with extensive cattle breeding and established milk collection infrastructure. The district's cool climate, abundant green pastures, and proximity to major dairy hubs like Hisar and Delhi make it ideal for high-quality milk production. Local cooperative networks and traditional dairy knowledge provide ready access to skilled artisans and supply chains.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Bhiwani.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bhiwani dairy clusters around Tosham and Dadri blocks; interview 5-10 cooperative managers at Bhiwani Dairy Union; study milk collection centers and cold storage infrastructure in Hisar (30 km away).
2
Source Raw Materials
Source milk from local cooperative societies (₹35-42/liter) or partner with 2-3 dairy farmers with 20-50 cattle. First batch: ₹50,000-75,000 for 1,500 liters/day collection setup.
3
Produce / Partner
Establish partnership with existing milk producers or rent a 500 sq ft unit in Bhiwani town; invest in pasteurization equipment (₹2-5 Lakh). Train with NDRI Karnal or join Bhiwani Dairy's producer group for technical support.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, 15 mins); obtain FSSAI registration (₹5,000-8,000); apply for IEC code; trademark brand name at IPO (₹10,000); comply with Haryana Dairy Regulations.
5
First Sale
Launch on JioMart, Amazon Fresh, and BigBasket with 5-10 SKUs; partner with local hotels and sweet shops in Bhiwani, Hisar, and Delhi NCR; approach organized retail chains (Mother Dairy, Amul franchise model).
6
Scale
Establish own collection points across 3 tehsils; invest in branded packaging and cold chain vehicles (₹15-20L); expand to 5-7 products; seek B2B contracts with institutional kitchens and food manufacturers; explore APEDA export registration.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Influencer partnerships with wellness YouTubers and Instagram fitness accounts (50K+ followers) for organic reach; create 15-20 second reels showing farm-to-table journey in Hindi.
  • B2B WhatsApp campaigns targeting 500+ hotels, restaurants, and sweet shops in NCR with bulk pricing and free samples; use JioMart marketing credits.
  • Local radio ads on Fever FM Hisar and partnerships with NGOs promoting organic/natural food to create word-of-mouth in Bhiwani township and nearby cities.
