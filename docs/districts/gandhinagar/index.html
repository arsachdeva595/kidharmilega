Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Gandhinagar.

1
Learn / Research
Visit GSITC (Gujarat Software and Information Technology Corporation) in Gandhinagar, connect with IT associations, attend TechSparks Gandhinagar events, study case studies from local companies like Infosys and Kpit, and analyze fintech hubs in Ahmedabad.
2
Source Raw Materials
Access cloud platforms (AWS, Google Cloud, Azure) costing ₹50,000-2,00,000/month, hire developers from Gandhinagar's talent pool (₹5-15 Lakhs initial team setup), license financial APIs (NEFT, RTGS integrations) costing ₹1-5 Lakhs annually.
3
Produce / Partner
Establish office in Electronic City or Sector 35-37, Gandhinagar; partner with co-working spaces like Workafella or NASSCOM members; hire core team of 3-5 developers and finance experts; use agile methodology for product development.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam as Software Services or Financial Services, obtain IEC code for exports, register with NASSCOM, get ISO 27001 for security compliance, trademark brand with IP office, comply with RBI guidelines if fintech-related.
5
First Sale
List on B2B platforms (LinkedIn, Crunchbase, ProductHunt), leverage NASSCOM network for enterprise sales, approach banks and NBFCs directly, pitch to angel networks in Gandhinagar, use content marketing on Medium and personal tech blogs.
6
Scale
Expand team to 15-25 developers by month 6-9, establish offshore delivery centers, pursue ISO and SOC 2 certifications, seek Series A funding from Ahmedabad/Mumbai-based VCs, expand geographic footprint with regional offices.
