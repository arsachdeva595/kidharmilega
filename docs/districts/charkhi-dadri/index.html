Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Charkhi Dadri.

1
Learn / Research
Visit existing crusher manufacturers in Charkhi Dadri (Gohpur and Israna clusters) and Kota, Rajasthan. Interview 10+ mining operators about equipment needs, pain points, and specifications. Study equipment catalogs from Metso, Sandvik, and local competitors. Attend mining and construction equipment expos like India Mining Summit.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source cast iron and steel plates from Ludhiana or Chhattisgarh suppliers (₹25-40L for initial batch). Source motors, bearings, and gearboxes from Bangalore, Mumbai, or Delhi industrial suppliers. Establish credit terms with 2-3 suppliers. First batch cost: ₹40-50L for prototype and initial 2-3 units.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established foundries in Haryana (Rohtak, Hisar) for casting heavy components. Set up assembly workshop in Charkhi Dadri with 2,000-3,000 sq ft space. Hire 8-12 skilled engineers and fabricators familiar with heavy equipment. Begin with contract manufacturing model before setting up own full production.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSE) classification online. Obtain IEC code from DGFT for export readiness. Get BIS certification for equipment quality (mandatory for Indian market). Register trademark with IP India. Obtain pollution and mining-related clearances from state authorities.
5
First Sale
Approach mining companies through direct B2B outreach and site visits in NCR, Punjab, and Rajasthan. List on IndiaMART, TradeKey, and Global Sources for visibility. Attend mining expos like India Mining Summit and National Stone Mining Conference. Partner with mining equipment dealers and distributors in tier-2 cities.
6
Scale
Post first 6 months, establish regional service centers in Jaipur and Ludhiana for spare parts and maintenance. Build export partnerships with Nepal, Bangladesh distributors. Invest in R&D for advanced screening and automation features. Expand product line to include wet grinding mills and advanced vibrating screens.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • LinkedIn B2B strategy: Create technical content about crusher efficiency, ROI case studies from mining operators, and equipment comparisons. Target mining company procurement heads and quarry owners with product demos and webinars.
  • Trade show dominance: Sponsor or exhibit at India Mining Summit, National Stone Mining Conference, and construction expos. Conduct live equipment demos and distribute technical brochures. Collect qualified leads for 6-month follow-up.
  • Localized partnerships: Collaborate with mining associations in Rajasthan and Punjab to gain credibility. Offer demo units to operators on 30-day trials. Build case studies and testimonials from satisfied mining companies for digital and print marketing.
