Local farming cooperative networks ensure raw material availability year-round at competitive prices.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Amreli.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Amreli's main processing cluster around Amreli city and Damnagar town. Spend 3-5 days meeting 10-15 processors at the Amreli Peanut Producers Association facility. Study Jolly Industries, Samrat Industries models. Attend Gujarat Chambers of Commerce webinars on export regulations. Budget ₹15,000 for research travel.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw peanuts directly from cooperative societies in Amreli (Amreli District Cooperative Association) or from existing processors. First batch cost: ₹3-4 lakhs for 5-10 MT raw peanuts (₹35-45/kg for good-quality blanched peanuts). Establish 3-month credit terms with 2-3 suppliers to minimize upfront cash.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with SFURTI cluster members or micro-processors in Amreli for toll manufacturing initially (50% cost savings vs. own unit). Identify 2-3 co-packers certified ISO 22000, FSSAI-compliant within 15 km of Amreli city. Negotiate ₹12-18 per kg for roasting, flavoring, packaging. Plan for 500 kg first batch.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (₹0 cost). Apply for FSSAI registration (₹3,000-5,000, 1-2 weeks). File IEC with nearest DGFT office in Ahmedabad (₹500, 2-3 weeks). Register trademark with IP India (₹4,500-9,000, 2-3 months). Secure ISO 22000 certification through Bureau of Indian Standards (₹20,000-40,000).
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon and Flipkart (seller fee ₹20-25k each). Create Instagram & TikTok branded accounts with 3-5 UGC-style product videos. Reach out to 50 premium gyms and wellness centers in Ahmedabad, Baroda with free samples. Pitch to 5 specialty e-grocers (BigBasket, Grofers) with bulk pricing. Aim for 100 units sold in month 1.
6
Scale
After 6 months, add 2-3 new SKUs (peanut butter, flavored mixes, premium gift packs). Secure distributorship in 3-5 western Indian cities via local distributors. Apply for PMEGP expansion grant for new processing equipment. Launch B2B export portal targeting US/EU importers. Build B2B supply chain to 10+ institutional clients.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Partner with 10 micro-influencers (50k-200k followers) in health & fitness niche on Instagram; send free monthly samples and negotiate 3-4 reels per month at ₹2,000-5,000 per influencer. Track promo code conversions.
  • Run Google Shopping ads targeting high-intent keywords ('organic peanut butter India', 'roasted peanuts online') with 15% discount code; allocate ₹30k/month, target ₹2 ROAS in month 2-3.
  • Create documentary-style YouTube videos (2-3 mins) showing Amreli peanut farming and processing; upload weekly; promote via Facebook carousel ads targeting 25-50 year old affluent urban women. Budget ₹10k/month.
Government Schemes

Free money you didn't know about

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 Lakhs loan with 25% subsidy for manufacturing units in non-metro areas; process through SIDBI.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹10 Lakhs per unit for cluster development; Amreli has existing clusters; apply through Ministry of MSME.
🏚
Gujarat Enterprise Development Scheme (GEDS)
5% interest subsidy on MSME loans up to ₹1 Cr; apply via District Industries Centre, Amreli.
🏚
Agricultural Infrastructure Development Cess Fund (AIDF)
grants for post-harvest infrastructure and processing equipment for agro-product entrepreneurs in Gujarat.
