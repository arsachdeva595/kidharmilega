ODOP Product

Gold Jewellery

Eluru has a 300+ year heritage of skilled goldsmithing passed down through generations of artisan families. The district's proximity to major temple towns and cultural centers in Andhra Pradesh creates constant demand for traditional festival jewelry. Local artisans possess rare expertise in filigree, granulation, and traditional motif work that cannot be easily replicated elsewhere.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Eluru.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Eluru's main jewelry clusters around Venkateswara Temple Street and Suryarao Pet; spend 2-3 weeks understanding artisan networks, design trends, and pricing structures. Connect with Eluru Chamber of Commerce and local goldsmith associations.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw gold from hallmarked suppliers in Eluru (₹8-12L for initial 250-500g stock) and semi-precious stones from Vijayawada or Hyderabad wholesale markets (₹2-5L for starter collection). Establish credit terms with 2-3 primary suppliers.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3-5 established artisans in Eluru through revenue-sharing models (typically 40-50% of margin to artisan). Start with 5-10 specific designs focusing on bridal and festival categories. Invest in quality control through hallmarking compliance.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam for MSME benefits; obtain IEC code from local customs authority for export eligibility. Secure BIS hallmarking certification. Create brand identity emphasizing 'Eluru Heritage' provenance. Obtain trademark for brand name.
5
First Sale
Launch on Meena Bazaar, Craftsvilla, and own Shopify store with high-resolution photography of pieces. Execute Instagram/Facebook ads targeting wedding keywords (cost ₹5-10L for first 3 months). Approach wedding planners and marriage halls in metros for partnerships.
6
Scale
Expand artisan network to 10-15 craftspeople; develop seasonal collections ahead of major festivals. Open pop-up stores in Bangalore, Hyderabad, Mumbai during wedding season. Build B2B relationships with luxury hotels and wedding retailers. Launch 'Design Your Own' customization service.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram Reels showing artisan craftsmanship process and close-ups of intricate work; partner with wedding influencers (100K-500K followers) for seasonal campaigns targeting engaged couples.
  • YouTube documentary-style content on 'Eluru's Goldsmith Families' and heritage storytelling to build emotional connection; target 25-45 year old female audience in metros.
  • Google Shopping ads during October-March wedding season with high-intent keywords like 'bridal gold jewelry' and 'temple jewelry'; allocate ₹50K-100K monthly budget.
