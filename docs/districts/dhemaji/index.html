ODOP Product

Red Rice

Dhemaji's fertile alluvial plains, abundant water resources from the Brahmaputra river system, and distinct seasonal patterns create ideal conditions for red rice cultivation. The district has a long-standing agricultural tradition and the soil composition naturally produces rice with higher iron content, making it naturally suited for premium red rice production.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Dhemaji.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Dhemaji district's major farming clusters in Lakhimpur and Gogamukh areas; interview 10+ red rice farmers; study market trends on BigBasket, Amazon Fresh, and organic food aggregators; attend agricultural expos in Guwahati and Assam Agricultural University seminars
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified red rice seeds from Assam Agricultural University or local seed banks; connect with cooperative societies in Dhemaji; initial batch of 5-10 tonnes costs ₹2-3 lakhs; identify mills in Lakhimpur for processing and packaging
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 5-10 small farmers via contract farming model or lease farmland (5-10 acres costs ₹5-15 lakhs annually); hire local milling and processing units on revenue-sharing basis; establish quality control with lab testing for iron content and purity
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam for agriculture-based business; obtain IEC code for exports; register brand with Trademark Office in Delhi; get FSSAI Food License; obtain organic certification from NPOP or Indian organic certifying bodies; setup GIS tag application if meeting criteria
5
First Sale
Launch on JioMart, BigBasket, Amazon Fresh, and own Shopify store; partner with regional health chains like Health & Glow; reach out to 50+ organic retailers in metro cities; create partnerships with corporate wellness programs; list on Organic Brands India and similar B2B platforms
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand product line with flour and snacks; establish distribution in 5+ metro cities; hire food consultant for export compliance; invest in influencer marketing with health/nutrition creators; launch subscription model for recurring revenue; scale farmland to 25-30 acres through partnership expansion
