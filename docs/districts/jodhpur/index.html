ODOP Product

Wooden Furniture Products

Jodhpur has a 300+ year legacy of skilled artisans specializing in wooden furniture carving. The district's access to quality hardwood forests, established supply chains, and a large concentrated cluster of furniture craftsmen in localities like Umaid Nagar and Pal Road make it uniquely positioned. The region's cultural heritage and tourism draw from palaces and forts create consistent domestic and international demand.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Jodhpur.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Pal Road furniture cluster and Umaid Nagar in Jodhpur (2-3 day immersion). Spend 2-4 weeks learning from 3-4 established artisan families on carving techniques, wood selection, and pricing. Contact JCCI (Jodhpur Chamber of Commerce & Industry) for cluster insights and supplier lists.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source Sheesham and Mango wood from timber merchants near Osian Road (₹60-90/kg). Buy carving tools from Sadar Bazaar workshops. Upholstery fabrics from Govind Textiles, Jodhpur (₹200-600/meter). First batch of 3-5 statement pieces: ₹1.5-2.5L including labor and materials.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 established artisan families in Pal Road cluster on revenue-share (60% artisan, 40% you) or fixed orders. Alternatively, set up small workshop (400-500 sqft) in Pal Road industrial area with 2-3 trained carvers. Build quality control checklist. Timeline: 30-60 days to first finished piece.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, 10 min). Apply for IEC code from DGFT office, Jodhpur. Register brand name and logo with IP India. Get GST registration (mandatory for export). Obtain hallmarking/authenticity certificate from local handicraft authority. Create Instagram and website with high-quality product photography.
5
First Sale
Launch on Instagram Reels and Pinterest targeting interior designers and luxury home keywords. List on Etsy and Artisan Box (slow initial 2-3 months, then 5-10 orders/month). Approach 5-10 high-end interior designers directly with portfolio. Participate in Rajasthan's annual Jodhpur Heritage Festival (October) for brand visibility. Target tier-1 city affluent audiences via Facebook/Instagram ads (₹10-15K/week budget).
6
Scale
After ₹5-10L revenue: hire 2-3 more carvers and supervisory staff. Move to 1000 sqft dedicated workshop. Launch WhatsApp business catalog for repeat corporate orders. Build B2B relationships with 10-15 interior design firms and hotel procurement teams. Export 20-30% of production via direct B2B to US/UK (contact Rajasthan Export Council for trade missions). Introduce 3-4 new design lines annually based on customer feedback.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Create 15-20 short Reels on Instagram/YouTube Shorts showing real artisans carving intricate patterns, wood selection process, and before-after transformations. Tag interior designers and use hashtags #JodhpurFurniture #ArtisanMade #IndianHeritage. Budget: ₹0 (user-generated content).
  • Partner with 5-10 micro-influencers (20-100K followers) in interior design, luxury lifestyle, and Indian heritage niches for product collaborations and reviews. Send sample pieces; negotiate 15-20% commission on referred sales. Expected ROI: 3-5x in 3 months.
  • Launch Google Shopping and Pinterest ads targeting 'Indian wooden furniture', 'carved wooden chest', 'Jodhpur furniture' keywords. Allocate ₹15-20K/month. A/B test landing pages with lifestyle imagery vs. artisan storytelling. Target US, UK, Canada (high-intent, high-value buyers).
