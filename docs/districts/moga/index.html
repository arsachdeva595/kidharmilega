The district's proximity to agricultural heartland of Punjab ensures consistent demand and deep understanding of farmer needs. Strong supplier networks for raw materials and existing manufacturing infrastructure make it ideal for scaling agricultural equipment production.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Moga.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Moga's established implement manufacturing clusters around Nihal Singh Wala and Moga city; interview 10+ existing manufacturers to understand supply chains, visit MSME cluster centers, and attend Punjab Agricultural Department events to understand farmer procurement patterns
2
Source Raw Materials
Source steel and iron from Ludhiana steel merchants (₹40-50L for first 3-month batch); source bearings and fasteners from Jalandhar industrial suppliers; establish credit terms with 2-3 material suppliers to manage cash flow initially
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing Moga-based contract manufacturers or rent workshop space (₹15-25K/month) and hire 5-8 skilled workers from local metalworking community; start with 1-2 product lines (ploughs, cultivators) before expanding
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam within 10 days online; apply for IEC code from nearest DGFT office for export readiness; register trademark with IP office; obtain BIS certification for agricultural implements; get ISO 9001 certification for quality management
5
First Sale
Launch sales through JoinMandi and AgroStar platforms for direct farmer connections; contact 50+ cooperative societies with product samples; register with APMC mandis in Moga and neighboring districts; approach government procurement portals (GeM) for agriculture department tenders
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand product range to 5-6 implement types; establish dealership network in 3-4 neighboring states; begin export outreach through APEDA; invest in light automation for repeated components; hire quality control staff and implement testing procedures

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Create YouTube channel demonstrating implement usage on actual farms with farmer testimonials; partner with agricultural extension officers for product placement on demonstration plots
  • Sponsor agricultural fairs and farmer melas in Punjab, Haryana, and Uttar Pradesh with product displays and free farm consultations to build direct farmer relationships
  • Run WhatsApp group campaigns targeting cooperative society managers and agricultural input dealers with product specifications, pricing, and bulk discount offers
