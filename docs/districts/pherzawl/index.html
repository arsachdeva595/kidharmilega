Ginger

Pherzawl's cool climate, well-drained volcanic soil, and altitude of 1500+ meters create ideal conditions for growing aromatic, high-oleoresin ginger. The district's traditional farming practices and organic cultivation methods produce ginger with superior flavor and health compounds compared to plains varieties.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Pherzawl.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Pherzawl's primary ginger cultivation areas near Sairang and Saiha; connect with local farming cooperatives and District Agricultural Office; study cultivation practices at Manipur Agricultural University, Imphal; benchmark prices at Imphal Spice Market and Thabal Bazar wholesale clusters
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified ginger seed stock from ICAR-Indian Institute of Spice Research, Kozhikode or local farmers in Pherzawl (₹30,000-50,000 for 1 acre seed stock); negotiate with local agricultural suppliers for farmyard manure and organic inputs; first-batch cost approximately ₹80,000-120,000 per acre for cultivation
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 5-10 registered organic ginger farmers in Pherzawl district through cooperative arrangements; establish small processing unit for drying/powdering (₹200,000-300,000 setup); hire local agricultural labor and train on post-harvest handling; consider SFURTI cluster membership for technical support
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSMEs) online at udyamregistration.gov.in; obtain IEC from DGFT for export eligibility; acquire organic certification from APEDA-recognized certifier (₹40,000-60,000); register brand name with local trademark office; obtain FSSAI license (₹500-5,000) for processed products
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh, BigBasket, and Organic marketplace as vendor; list on B2B platforms (TraceLink, IndiaMART); contact bulk exporters through Export Promotion Council for Spices; establish direct relationships with Ayurvedic companies and health supplement manufacturers; create WhatsApp Business channel for local wholesale inquiries
6
Scale
Expand cultivation acreage to 5-10 acres after proving profitability; develop value-added product line (ginger extract, candies, tea blends); build export contracts with 2-3 international buyers; establish aggregation center in Imphal for collection and processing; apply for PM Vishwakarma certification for premium positioning
