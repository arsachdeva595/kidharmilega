Studded gold jewllery

Hyderabad has a 400-year-old jewellery-making tradition rooted in the Nizam's court, with established artisan clusters in Laad Bazaar and Charminar. The city has access to world-class diamond polishing units, gemstone suppliers, and skilled craftsmen trained in intricate studded work. Its proximity to major financial hubs and international markets makes it ideal for scaling studded jewellery exports.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Hyderabad.

1
Learn / Research
Spend 2-3 weeks in Hyderabad's Laad Bazaar and Charminar cluster; meet 10+ master jewellers; visit Sri Venkateshwara Silks and Central Gold Exchange; attend All India Gems & Jewellery Business Forum webinars; study competitor designs on Tanishq, Malabar Gold.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw gold from certified hallmarking centers in Hyderabad (Abids, Begum Bazaar); gemstones from Jaipur wholesale markets or directly from Hyderabad's Zari Lane suppliers; first batch (₹2-5L) requires 500g gold + ₹1.5L in mixed gemstones; negotiate artisan piece rates at ₹8-15k per complex studded piece.
3
Produce / Partner
Identify and partner with 2-3 master artisans in Laad Bazaar or Charminar; sign MOU with design specifications, quality standards, lead times; start with 20-30 custom pieces in batch 1; oversee hallmarking at BIS-approved centers in Hyderabad (Begum Bazaar, Abids).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME certificate online (takes 10 mins); apply for IEC code from DGFT via online portal (₹0, 7 days); register trademark on IP India portal (₹9,000, 8-10 months); obtain BIS hallmarking certification; create GST registration (if turnover >20L).
5
First Sale
Launch Instagram shop with high-quality 360° product photography; list on Meesho, Flipkart, Amazon with 5-8 key studded designs; approach 5-10 high-end multi-brand jewellery stores in Bandra, Delhi, Bangalore for wholesale; run Pinterest ads targeting 'studded gold jewellery' + 'Indian bridal jewellery'; attend JCK Mumbai or Rajasthan Gems Expo for B2B buyers.
6
Scale
After 6 months, hire 1 designer and 1 business development executive; expand to 50+ active designs; open own retail pop-up in Hyderabad mall; establish wholesale distribution in 3-5 major metros; launch WhatsApp Business for VIP customization; pursue exports to UAE/US via direct distributor partnerships or online B2B platforms.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram Reel series 'From Artisan to You' — showcase 30-60 sec videos of master craftsmen studding gemstones; tag jewellery influencers; use #HydyWeddings #StuddedGoldJewellery; aim for 100K+ views in 3 months.
  • Collaborate with 5-7 wedding planners in Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi; offer 15-20% wholesale margins; ensure featured placement in their mood boards and client lookbooks; track referral sales monthly.
  • Run Google Shopping Ads for high-intent keywords ('studded gold bangle', 'diamond studded ring India', 'bridal studded jewellery'); target metros; set ROAS target of 3:1; retarget website visitors with lookalike audiences on Facebook.

Free money you didn't know about

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy for manufacturing units; process via District Industries Centre, Hyderabad.
🏛
PM Vishwakarma
up to ₹15L credit at 5% interest for registered artisans and craftspeople; direct enrollment for master jewellers.
🏚
TS-iPASS (Telangana Industrial Project Approval and Self Certification System)
single-window clearance for manufacturing units; subsidy on land lease for MSME jewellery units.
🏚
Telangana Craftsmen Credit Link Scheme
easy credit access for registered artisans and jewellery micro-enterprises at subsidized rates.
