Papaya Based Product

Raipur district has ideal tropical climate and soil conditions for year-round papaya cultivation with low production costs. The district has established food processing infrastructure, proximity to major markets via road/rail, and government focus on food processing through dedicated parks. Low farm labor costs and existing supply chains make papaya processing economically viable at scale.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Raipur.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Raipur Food Park (AIBP) and Naya Raipur agro-processing clusters. Attend Chhattisgarh Food Processing Industry Meet. Interview 3-4 existing papaya processors in Raipur. Study FSSAI regulations for processed fruit products and packaging norms. Benchmark pricing at local APMC wholesale market and retail outlets.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fresh papaya directly from farms in Arang and Abhanpur blocks (₹4-6 per kg). Identify sugar suppliers near Raipur (₹35-40 per kg). Source packaging from Durg industrial area. First batch of 500 kg jam requires ₹25,000-35,000 raw material cost. Build relationships with 2-3 reliable farmer groups for consistent supply.
3
Produce / Partner
Either rent processing space in Raipur Food Park (₹15,000-25,000/month) or partner with existing FSSAI-certified small processors as contract manufacturers initially. Invest in basic equipment: cooking vessels, pulper, filling machine, labeling machine (₹2-4 lakhs). Start with pilot batches of 200-300 units. Train 2-3 workers on hygiene and processing standards.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (takes 10 mins, no cost). Apply for IEC code from Raipur DGFT office for export readiness. Get FSSAI registration (₹5,000-8,000 for micro unit). Design brand logo and packaging (₹10,000-20,000). Register trademark with IP India (₹10,000). Complete GST registration online.
5
First Sale
Launch D2C via Amazon FBA and Flipkart Seller (list 3-5 SKUs with ₹250-400 per unit pricing). Create Instagram business profile with product photography and farmer stories. Approach 5-10 modern retail outlets in Raipur (Spencer's, Reliance Fresh) with wholesale offers at ₹180-220 per unit. Pitch to online B2B platforms like Udaan and IndiaMART targeting bulk buyers. Target food service channels via LinkedIn outreach to 50+ hotel chains.
6
Scale
After 6 months of consistent sales, expand SKU range (papaya leather, papaya candy, papaya juice concentrate). Invest in branded packaging and premium line targeting e-commerce. Scale production capacity by 40-50% through additional equipment. Build export relationships through APEDA registration and trade fairs. Develop proprietary enzyme-based health positioning for premiumization. Target distribution in 50+ retail stores across Chhattisgarh and neighboring states.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Partner with 10-15 micro-influencers in health/wellness space in India (10K-50K followers) for organic reach and gifting strategy. Cost: ₹500-2,000 per influencer. Focus on Instagram Reels showing farm-to-jar journey.
  • Run Google Shopping ads targeting keywords like 'best papaya jam India' with ₹500/day budget. Retarget website visitors with 20% discount offers. Expected CAC: ₹150-250 per order on ₹350+ AOV.
  • Host tasting events at 5-6 premium cafes and bakeries in Raipur (partner locations). Offer commission of 15-20% on sell-through. Distribute 500 sample sachets monthly at premium pricing outlets and gym chains.
