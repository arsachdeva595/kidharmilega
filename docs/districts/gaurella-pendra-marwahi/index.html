Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Gaurella Pendra Marwahi.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Gaurella Pendra Marwahi district APMC markets (Gaurella, Pendra) to understand local groundnut grades, pricing dynamics, and seasonal variations. Connect with 3-5 established groundnut traders and 2 processing units in Durg district (nearest processing hub) to learn quality standards and processing costs. Attend CIMMYT or ICAR-sponsored groundnut farming workshops in Chhattisgarh.
2
Source Raw Materials
Directly contract 10-20 smallholder farmers in Gaurella block during sowing season (May-June) for Virginia variety groundnuts (better oil content). Negotiate at ₹4500-5200/quintal (wholesale rates). Alternatively, purchase 50-100 quintals from Durg APMC during Oct-Nov peak harvest at ₹4200-4800/quintal. First-batch cost: ₹3-5 lakhs for 100 quintals.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with certified processing units in Durg (20km away) for shelling, roasting, and grinding—costs ₹800-1200/quintal for full processing. Or invest ₹15-25 lakhs in a small processing unit (1-2 ton/day capacity) with FSSAI approval. Source machinery from suppliers in Bilaspur or through SFURTI cluster facilities.
4
Brand & Register
Register as Udyam MSME (online via udyamregistration.gov.in), obtain IEC from local DGFT office in Raipur (₹500-1000 processing fee). Get FSSAI food safety license (₹2000-5000 for small business). Design brand identity and register trademark with IP office. Obtain WTO Certificate of Origin for exports from District Commerce office.
5
First Sale
Launch on Flipkart and Amazon with 500-1000 units via seller app (5-7 days processing). Price premium groundnut products at ₹280-350/500g (D2C) vs ₹120-150/500g (wholesale). Contact BigBasket, Blinkit category managers directly with 20-unit sample boxes. Simultaneously pitch 5-10 regional FMCG traders (Chhattisgarh and MP regions) with ₹1.5-2L minimum first orders.
6
Scale
After first 6 months, expand to pan-India B2B food manufacturers, negotiate co-packing arrangements for peanut butter brands. Launch export shipments via trading houses like NEPL or Gujarat-based agri-exporters. Introduce 2-3 SKU variants (roasted-salted, unsalted, flavored). Build direct WhatsApp/Telegram channel for recurring B2B orders. Target ₹15-20L monthly revenue by month 12.
