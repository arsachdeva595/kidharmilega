ODOP Product

Siddipet Gollabama

Siddipet has a 300+ year legacy of handloom weaving with generations of artisans skilled in traditional saree making. The district's access to quality cotton and silk threads, combined with its established weaver communities and cooperative structures, makes it the ideal hub for authentic Gollabama production. Government support through handloom clusters and SFURTI initiatives further strengthen the ecosystem.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Siddipet.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Siddipet handloom cluster (Sarangapur village area) and spend 2-3 weeks learning from master weavers. Connect with Siddipet Weavers Cooperative Society and attend their training sessions. Study existing players like Siddipet Sarees and handlooms.com marketplace listings.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source cotton yarn (60s count) from Telangana Spinning Mills near Hyderabad at ₹180-220/kg. Purchase silk thread from Pochampalli or Telangana suppliers at ₹400-500/kg. First batch of 10 sarees requires ₹40,000-50,000. Build relationships with local dyers for natural color options.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3-5 Master weavers in Siddipet cluster through Siddipet Weavers Cooperative (contact: DC Handloom, Siddipet). Negotiate piece-rate of ₹1,200-1,800 per saree. Set quality benchmarks based on GI standards. Plan for 15-20 day turnaround per batch of 20 sarees.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam at udyamregistration.gov.in (take 10 mins, free). Obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Hyderabad. File for trademark at ipindia.gov.in with name and logo. Create GST registration. Apply for Handloom Mark certification from DC Handloom, Telangana.
5
First Sale
Launch on Meesho and Flipkart Sarees category with 5 initial designs. Create Instagram content showing weaver stories and behind-the-scenes production. Build email list on own website (WordPress + Shopify). Reach out to 50 ethnic boutiques in Bangalore, Chennai, Delhi with wholesale pricing. Join Craftroots and similar handloom aggregator platforms.
6
Scale
After ₹10L revenue, hire a sales manager for B2B expansion. Expand product line to Dupattas and Dress materials using same looms. Launch Pinterest advertising targeting 'traditional sarees' and 'Indian heritage textiles'. Open pop-up stores in Bangalore and Mumbai during festive seasons. Explore corporate gifting partnerships with CSR teams.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram Reels series 'Meet the Weaver' featuring 60-second profiles of artisans in Siddipet with their saree creation process, posted 3x weekly to build emotional connection.
  • Pinterest strategy targeting 'traditional Indian sarees', 'handloom textiles', 'sustainable fashion' with rich pins linking to blog posts on saree history, care, and styling tips.
  • Email nurture campaign for wedding season targeting brides 4-6 months before wedding with curated Gollabama collections, styling guides, and exclusive trousseau discounts.
