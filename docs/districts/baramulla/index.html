Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Baramulla.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Baramulla apple clusters in Aharbal, Kachdooma, and Pattan villages. Spend 2-3 weeks with established growers. Study production cycles at Kashmir Horticulture Department (contact: 01942-258621). Review export compliance via APEDA regulations.
2
Source Raw Materials
Purchase quality saplings from Kashmir Horticultural Research Institute (SKUAST-K), Shalimar or licensed nurseries in Baramulla. First-batch cost for 5 acres (approx. 500-600 saplings) + accessories: ₹3.5-4.5 lakhs. Organic fertilizers from local suppliers in Baramulla town.
3
Produce / Partner
Either acquire/lease 2-5 acres of certified land in Baramulla district OR partner with established farmers through contract farming agreements (40-60% margin split). Engage Kashmir Horticulture Department for soil testing and pest management training. Set up cold storage facility (10-50 MT capacity) for post-harvest management.
4
Brand & Register
Register as Udyam (micro/small enterprise) on udyamregistration.gov.in. Obtain IEC code from DGFT (Directorate General of Foreign Trade) for export eligibility. Register FSSAI license for any value-added product processing. Create brand identity emphasizing 'Himalayan Premium' and 'Baramulla Origin' with GI certification pending application.
5
First Sale
List on BigBasket, Jio Mart, and Amazon Fresh for D2C channel targeting tier-1 cities. Establish contracts with wholesale fruit traders in Delhi Azadpur Mandi and Mumbai APMC. Register with APEDA-approved export houses in Srinagar for international sales. Direct B2B outreach to premium supermarket chains (Bungalow, Nature's Basket, Metro).
6
Scale
After 6 months of successful sales, expand production to 8-10 acres and build owned cold storage (50+ MT). Launch value-added product line (juice, dried apples) to capture higher margins. Establish exclusive partnership with 1-2 export traders for consistent 50+ MT annual shipments. Create premium subscription boxes on Cult.fit or Unbox Loyalty.
