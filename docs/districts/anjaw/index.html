ODOP Product

Large Cardamom

Anjaw's high-altitude terrain, pristine forests, and cool climate provide ideal conditions for large cardamom cultivation with natural pest resistance. The district's geographic isolation and indigenous farming practices ensure organic, chemical-free production. Local tribal communities possess generations of cultivation expertise passed down through traditional methods.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Anjaw.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Anjaw district; connect with local farmer collectives in Hawai and Chowkham villages; study cardamom drying and grading processes at regional agricultural extension centers; attend spice trade meets in Kochi or Delhi
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fresh large cardamom directly from Anjaw farmers at ₹800-1200 per kg (first batch: 500 kg = ₹4-6 lakh); establish relationships with 3-5 verified farmers; arrange cold storage at ₹1.5-2 lakh per month
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with local farmer cooperatives or lease processing units in Anjaw; invest ₹5-8 lakh in drying, sorting, and packaging equipment; train local labor on international quality standards (ISO 22000 compliance); aim for 200-300 kg monthly processed output
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free online); obtain IEC code from DGFT (₹500-1000); get organic certification from NPOP agency (₹30-50k, 6-month process); trademark brand name; obtain FSSAI registration (₹2000-5000)
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon & Flipkart with ₹2-3k MOP; contact spice wholesalers in Kochi, Nizamuddin (Delhi), and Sangli; pitch to ayurvedic brands like Dabur, Patanjali; use LinkedIn for B2B export inquiries; build WhatsApp business catalog
6
Scale
Expand to 2-3 ton monthly processing by month 9; establish export certifications (ISO, APEDA); hire export consultant to enter USA/EU markets; develop co-branded products with premium tea/coffee brands; create subscription boxes for niche markets

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram/YouTube series: 'From Anjaw to Your Kitchen' — behind-the-scenes drying, grading, and packaging videos highlighting organic farming; tag food influencers and chef accounts for reach
  • LinkedIn B2B outreach: Direct messaging to spice importers, food manufacturers, and specialty retailers with product samples and bulk pricing; attend virtual trade fairs (Annapoorna Expo, Spice Board events)
  • Google Shopping & Amazon Ads: Geo-targeted ads to premium spice buyers in metros; focus keywords: 'organic large cardamom,' 'Arunachal Pradesh cardamom,' 'premium black cardamom'; offer free shipping above ₹999
