ODOP Product

Lac

Khunti district has a rich tradition of lac crafting passed down through generations, with abundant kusum and palas trees that host lac insects naturally. The district's skilled artisan community and ideal climate conditions make it the epicenter of India's lac industry, producing high-quality products with unique designs.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Khunti.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Khunti's primary lac craft clusters in Arki, Murhu, and Khunti town. Spend 2-3 weeks learning from established artisans; visit Khunti Handicraft Cooperative Society. Document production techniques, market prices, and buyer preferences.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source refined lac resin from Jharkhand Lac & Shellac Board suppliers in Ranchi (₹400-500/kg). Procure wood blanks and metal components from local suppliers in Khunti bazaar. Budget ₹30,000-50,000 for first batch of raw materials covering 500-800 bangles.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established artisan groups under Khunti Handicraft Society or recruit trained craftspeople. Set up workshop space (500 sq ft) in Khunti town (rent ₹3,000-5,000/month). Start with 100-200 pieces/month; scale production as demand grows.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online via udyamregistration.gov.in — free). Apply for IEC code via DGFT portal for export eligibility. Register brand name with Trade Mark Registry. Obtain FSSAI if including food-grade lac products. Get Handicraft Certification from DC Handicraft ministry.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Handmade, Etsy, and Flipkart Handcrafted with 20-30 hero products. Create Instagram account showcasing artisan stories and process videos. Reach out to international fair-trade platforms (Global Goods Partner, Serrv). Pitch to 5-10 corporate gift consultants for bulk orders.
6
Scale
After 6 months, hire 3-5 additional artisans to increase capacity to 500+ pieces/month. Establish WhatsApp B2B channel for wholesale inquiries. Participate in IGTC trade shows and craft exhibitions. Launch co-branded collections with interior design studios and online marketplaces.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram Reels strategy: 15-30 second videos of artisans crafting bangles in real-time, shot in natural light at Khunti workshops. Target hashtags #LacCraft #JharkhandHeritage #ArtisanMade. Aim for 100K views/month by month 3.
  • Partner with 10-15 lifestyle influencers (micro-influencers, 50K-500K followers) in home décor and sustainable fashion. Send curated gift boxes; request unboxing videos and honest reviews. Target 3-4 partnerships per month.
  • Launch 'Artisan Stories' email newsletter (via Substack/ConvertKit) featuring weekly profiles of craftspeople in Khunti, production insights, and exclusive 10% discount codes. Build subscriber base to 5,000 by month 6.
