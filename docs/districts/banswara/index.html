Marble Products

Banswara sits on rich marble deposits with centuries-old artisan expertise in cutting, polishing, and finishing. The region's geological advantage combined with skilled labor makes it a natural hub for premium marble production. Proximity to Gujarat and Madhya Pradesh quarries ensures steady raw material supply.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Banswara.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Banswara marble belt (Sajjangarh-Betla cluster) and meet 3-4 established marble units; spend 2-3 days understanding quarry sourcing, polishing workflows, and current export channels. Connect with Banswara Chamber of Commerce for cluster insights.
2
Source Raw Materials
Partner with Banswara marble quarry cooperatives for first 10-ton batch (₹1.2-1.8L per ton raw). Identify local polishing compounds from Jaipur suppliers (₹2-3L for 3-month stock). Budget ₹15-22L for raw materials + tools for first production run.
3
Produce / Partner
Lease 2,000-3,000 sq.ft workshop in Banswara marble cluster (₹2-4L/year rent). Hire 4-6 skilled polishers (₹12-18K/month) and 1 supervisor. Start with 2-3 marble cutting/polishing machines (₹8-12L total). Alternatively, contract-manufacture with established Banswara artisans for first 6 months.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME within 2 weeks online. Apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) from DGFT for export capability. Create brand identity (logo, packaging) reflecting 'Banswara Heritage' angle. Register trademark with IP India. Obtain GST registration.
5
First Sale
List on NirmanHub, IndiaMart, and TradeKey for B2B. Create Instagram/Pinterest with high-res marble finish photography for D2C. Contact 5-10 interior design studios in Mumbai, Bangalore, Delhi to pitch custom orders. Reach out to 2-3 export trade houses in Jaipur/Mumbai for distribution partnerships.
6
Scale
After 6 months, establish direct relationships with 5-7 major real estate developers. Hire export coordinator, expand to 8-10 skilled artisans. Launch 'Made in Banswara' branded showroom in Mumbai/Bangalore. Invest in CNC polishing machines to increase output 3x. Target ₹3-5 Cr revenue by month 18.
