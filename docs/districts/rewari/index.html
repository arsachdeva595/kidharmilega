Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Rewari.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Rewari Industrial Estate and Bawal SIDCUL industrial area to study existing units (Jyoti CNC, Sanghvi Movers, Bharat Forge suppliers). Connect with Rewari Chamber of Commerce and spend 2-3 weeks shadowing 3-4 precision engineering units. Analyze product catalogs from 10 competitors on IndiaMART and Global Trade platforms.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from Ludhiana steel stockists (₹15-20L for 50-ton initial batch), bearing suppliers in Gurugram (₹3-5L), and electrical components from Noida industrial zones (₹2-3L). Budget ₹25-30L for first 3-month raw material inventory. Negotiate 30-45 day credit terms with established suppliers.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing Rewari micro-units for sub-assembly or lease 1500-2000 sqft semi-automated workshop in Bawal SIDCUL (₹3-4L annually). Invest in 3-4 CNC machines (₹8-12L), one milling machine (₹4-6L), and hand tools. Hire 8-12 skilled technicians from local polytechnics and vocational training centers.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam-MSME within 10 days online (free). Obtain IEC code from DGFT Faridabad office (₹500, 5 days). Apply for ISO 9001:2015 certification (₹30-40K, 8-10 weeks). Create branded packaging with QR codes linking to product specs. Register trademark with IP Office (₹5000).
5
First Sale
Post product catalogs on IndiaMART Premium (₹20K/year), Alibaba (₹5K/month), and TradingIndia.com. Contact 30-40 automotive OEMs via LinkedIn directly with samples and COAs. Attend India Auto Parts Summit (Delhi) and Subcontract India Expo (Bangalore). Build email list of procurement officers from automobile suppliers and pitch fortnightly.
6
Scale
After 6 months of ₹20-30L monthly revenue, invest in 2 additional CNC machines and hire 5 more technicians. Open a showroom in Delhi-Gurgaon automotive cluster (₹2-3L setup) to service OEM clients directly. Pursue ISO 45001 and TS 16949 certifications. Establish a supply agreement with 2-3 Tier-1 auto suppliers for recurring orders.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Create YouTube channel showcasing CNC machining process, tolerances achieved, and customer testimonials from OEMs. Upload 2 videos weekly. Target 'precision engineering' and 'automotive components' searchers with Google Ads (₹500/day budget).
  • Partner with 5 automotive engineering colleges (DTU, BITS Pilani extensions, NIT Kurukshetra) for placement tie-ups and internships. Sponsor workshops on advanced manufacturing. Hire 2-3 interns per batch and convert 50% to full-time engineers.
  • Sponsor booth at India Auto Parts Summit (Delhi), Subcontract India, and SME Expo. Distribute samples and offer ₹500 incentives for OEM meetings. Follow up all leads within 48 hours with competitive quotes.
Government Schemes

Free money you didn't know about

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L grant for registered MSME in manufacturing, 25% subsidy for general category, 35% for SC/ST/women founders.
🏛
MUDRA Loan Scheme
up to ₹10L unsecured loan for manufacturing units, no collateral required, 7-8% interest, 5-year repayment.
🏚
Haryana Enterprise Promotion Scheme (HEPS)
Capital investment subsidy up to ₹20L, 10% subsidy on plant and machinery for MSME in manufacturing.
🏚
Haryana MSME Export Promotion Scheme
3% refund on export turnover for first 3 years, priority for participation in international trade fairs.
