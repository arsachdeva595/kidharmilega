West Delhi's proximity to Delhi's affluent residential and commercial hubs creates strong local demand for premium furniture. The district has established clusters of skilled artisans and woodworkers, supported by accessible raw material markets and logistics infrastructure. Historical prevalence of furniture manufacturing communities ensures availability of trained labor and established supply chains.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for West.

1
Learn / Research
Visit furniture clusters in Kirti Nagar, Motia Khan, and Mehrauli in West Delhi; spend 2-3 weeks understanding production methods, costing, and supplier networks. Visit Furniture Mall in Kirti Nagar and meet 5-10 established manufacturers to understand market dynamics.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from timber dealers in Kirti Nagar (plywood ₹300-500/sheet, solid wood ₹80-200/kg) and fabric suppliers in Mehrauli. Budget ₹3-5 lakhs for first batch of 20-30 piece designs. Establish accounts with 3-4 reliable suppliers for consistent quality.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established woodworking workshops in Kirti Nagar on contract basis initially (₹15,000-25,000/month workspace rental). Alternatively, invest ₹20-40 lakhs to set up own production unit with basic machinery (saw, sander, drill). Hire 4-6 skilled carpenters and 2-3 upholsterers.
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam (Ministry of MSME) as manufacturing enterprise. Apply for IEC code from DGFT for export capability. Register trademark for brand name (₹5,000-10,000). Open GST account and obtain pollution certificate for manufacturing unit.
5
First Sale
Launch on Flipkart Home, Amazon Home, and Pepperfry with 8-10 hero SKUs. Create Instagram account with professional product photography and target Delhi-NCR audience. Partner with 2-3 local interior designers for first 10 orders. Engage in Furniture Fairs at NITA (Dwarka) and IIC (Delhi).
6
Scale
After ₹10 lakh revenue, expand product range to 50+ SKUs. Open showroom in commercial area (Mehrauli or Saket). Hire dedicated sales and customer service team. Establish partnerships with corporate procurement teams and hospitality chains. Scale manufacturing to 2 shifts, targeting ₹50-100 lakh annual turnover by month 18.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Partner with 10-15 micro-influencers (10K-50K followers) in interior design and home decor on Instagram for product placements and home tour collaborations.
  • Run targeted Google Shopping and Facebook Ads for keyword searches like 'wooden furniture Delhi' and 'custom sofa NCR' with 15-20% discount on first purchase.
  • Create YouTube channel showcasing furniture production process, design tips, and customer testimonials; optimize for 'how to choose furniture' and related long-tail keywords.
