Tourism (Medical & Religious)

South Delhi hosts India's most visited religious sites (Lotus Temple, Akshardham, Jama Masjid, Nizamuddin Dargah) alongside top-tier medical institutions (AIIMS, Apollo, Fortis), creating a unique convergence of spiritual healing and advanced healthcare. The district's infrastructure, connectivity, and high international visitor footfall make it ideal for integrated medical-religious tourism packages.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for South.

1
Learn / Research
Conduct 20 interviews with medical tourism operators at AIIMS-Delhi, Apollo Sarita Vihar, and Fortis. Visit Nizamuddin Dargah, Lotus Temple, and Jama Masjid to understand visitor flow. Join Delhi Tourism Board webinars. Study competitor packages on platforms like Practo, Vaidya, and MedTourism.
2
Source Raw Materials
Partner directly with 2-3 hospitals (AIIMS, Apollo, Max) for medical consultation tie-ups. Establish relationships with 5-7 verified hotels in Saket, Chhatarpur, Mehrauli. Connect with licensed guides at major temples/dargahs. Initial partnership cost: ₹5-10 lakhs for contracts and advance payments.
3
Produce / Partner
Register as a travel/medical tourism service provider under Udyam. Partner with existing hospitals' international patient departments rather than building own facilities. Hire 2-3 multilingual coordinators (Hindi, English, Arabic speakers preferred). Create templated care packages and documentation.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Service provider category), obtain IEC code for international marketing. Register under GST (6% applicable). Create brand identity with clinic/temple imagery. Register on Ministry of Tourism's medical tourism portal. Obtain IATA credentials if offering travel bookings.
5
First Sale
Launch on Google My Business, TripAdvisor, and Practo with 15+ verified reviews. Create WhatsApp Business account for direct inquiries. Partner with 3-4 medical tourism aggregators (MedTourism, India Medical Tourism). Run targeted LinkedIn ads to Middle Eastern healthcare seekers. Attend 2-3 international medical tourism expos (IMTJ, Arab Health).
6
Scale
Build proprietary mobile app with package customization. Establish sub-offices in 2-3 key source countries (Dubai, London, USA). Partner with 2-3 additional hospital chains. Launch corporate wellness packages. Create content hub (blog, YouTube) with 50+ SEO-optimized articles. Expand to Tier 2 cities' religious + medical packages.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Partner with 5 international medical tourism blogs to publish guest articles about South Delhi's medical-religious fusion. Offer commission (5-8%) on bookings through referral links. Target high-intent keywords like 'cardiac surgery India cost' and 'spiritual healing pilgrimage India'.
  • Create 10-minute video testimonials of 3-4 satisfied medical tourists (with permission) and run YouTube/Facebook ads targeting USA, UK, and Middle East audiences. Budget: ₹2-5 lakhs/month for 6 months.
  • Host quarterly webinars (free) on topics like 'Why Indians Choose Delhi for Heart Surgery' and 'Spiritual Wellness Packages' on LinkedIn. Capture 200+ qualified leads per session and follow up via email/WhatsApp.
