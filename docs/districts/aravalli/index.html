Agro Processing

Aravalli district benefits from rich agricultural diversity, favorable monsoon patterns, and proximity to major Gujarat markets. The district's farming communities have generations of expertise in pulses, grains, and oilseeds—ideal raw materials for agro-processing ventures. Government focus on ODOP and rural entrepreneurship makes this an optimal location for scaling small-scale processors.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Aravalli.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Modasa and Meghraj taluka farming clusters; study ODOP network at Aravalli District Industries office; attend FSSAI training workshops at Ahmedabad; connect with existing agro-processors in Banaskantha and Sabarkantha for process benchmarking.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source directly from Aravalli farmer cooperatives (Moong Belt around Modasa) and Banaskantha pulses markets; first-batch procurement of 10 MT mixed pulses/grains approximately ₹3-5 lakhs; establish relationships with local APMC mandis.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established mini-dal mills in Modasa or lease 1500-2000 sqft FSSAI-compliant facility in Aravalli GIDC; hire trained processors from local vocational institutes; implement IMS (inventory management system) from day one; consider contract manufacturing with certified FSSAI units initially.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (msme.gov.in) within 2 weeks; obtain FSSAI registration (₹5000-12000, 30 days); apply IEC code via ECGC portal; register brand name with IPv4 (₹5000-8000); design packaging with QR codes linking to sustainability story.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Handmade and Flipkart Sellers within 60 days; pitch to regional chains (Big Basket, Blinkit, Jio Mart); create WhatsApp Business catalog targeting local B2B buyers; approach HoReCa sector through Zomato for Business; attend Gulfood and Anuga food expos for export leads.
6
Scale
Expand product range to 8-10 SKUs; invest in semi-automated processing (₹15-20L) by month 12; establish certified cold storage (₹20L); hire quality/compliance manager; pursue ISO 22000 certification; develop contract-farming model with 50+ local farmers; explore franchise model for regional expansion.
