Sugarcane based product (Ex. Jaggery, Molasses)

Kabirdham has a long tradition of sugarcane cultivation with favorable agro-climatic conditions for high-yield crops. The district's proximity to sugarcane farms, established processing units, and skilled artisans make it naturally suited for jaggery and molasses production. Local farmers have generations of expertise in sugarcane harvesting and traditional processing methods.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Kabirdham.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kabirdham's jaggery clusters in Kawardha and Bemetara blocks; interview 3-5 established jaggery producers; study production at Chhattisgarh Sugarcane Development Board; understand seasonal supply dynamics (Nov-March peak).
2
Source Raw Materials
Source sugarcane directly from local farmers in Kabirdham district or partner with sugarcane agricultural societies; first batch of 500 kg sugarcane costs ₹3,000-₹4,500; negotiate with farm clusters for bulk pricing; identify 2-3 reliable suppliers before committing.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing jaggery co-operatives like Kabirdham Sugarcane Farmers Cooperative; lease production unit space in Kawardha industrial area (₹5,000-₹8,000/month); hire skilled boiler operators and cookers with 5+ years experience; produce in batches of 50-100 kg initially.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam with Manufacturing classification under Food Processing; apply for IEC code through DGFT portal; obtain Food Safety License (FSSAI) from district food authority; register trademark for brand name with IP office; get organic certification from certification bodies if targeting premium segment.
5
First Sale
List on Amazon, Flipkart, and BigBasket with minimum order of 250g packs at ₹80-120 per unit; approach local sweet shops and bakeries in Raipur and nearby cities with wholesale pricing; set up WhatsApp business catalog; partner with regional distributors covering Central India; sell at farmers markets in Kawardha during peak season.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand product range to flavored jaggery (cardamom, ginger), packaged molasses, and jaggery powder; build B2B relationships with 10+ food companies; apply for GI tagging to increase premium positioning; launch subscription model on D2C channels; establish retail presence in 20+ modern trade stores across Chhattisgarh and neighboring states.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Create Instagram Reels showing jaggery production process from cane to finished product; feature local farmers and artisans; target health-conscious millennial audience with hashtags #NaturalSweetener #TraditionalJaggery; collaborate with 5-10 micro-influencers in wellness space.
  • Partner with regional sweet shop chains (Balaji Mithai, Sharma Sweets) in Raipur for in-store promotions and sampling; offer bulk discounts for monthly orders; create co-branded packaging emphasizing authenticity.
  • Run Google Shopping campaigns targeting keywords like 'natural jaggery,' 'organic gur,' 'molasses substitute'; focus on Tier 2-3 cities in Central India; retarget website visitors on Facebook/Instagram with product benefits and testimonials.
