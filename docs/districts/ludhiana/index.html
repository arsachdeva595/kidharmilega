Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Ludhiana.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Ludhiana's Humbran Industrial Area and Focal Point to understand cluster dynamics; attend meetings at Ludhiana Chamber of Commerce; spend 2-3 weeks shadowing established manufacturers to understand production cycles, lead times, and quality standards
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fabric from suppliers in Ludhiana's textile market (Ram Nagar area) — 1000 meters of cotton-polyester blend costs ₹80K-120K; partner with 2-3 suppliers for bulk discounts; negotiate 30-45 day payment terms; first batch production: ₹200K-400K for 500 pieces mixed designs
3
Produce / Partner
Identify contract manufacturers in Humbran or Focal Point clusters offering full-service production (cutting, stitching, finishing); negotiate per-piece rates ₹120-250 depending on complexity; start with 500-1000 unit trial batches; establish quality control checkpoints at cutting, stitching, and finishing stages
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam under Ministry of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises; obtain IEC code from Directorate General of Foreign Trade for export capability; file trademark with IP India; get GST registration (18% on apparel); setup basic branding with Canva or hire local designer for logo and label design
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon and Flipkart seller accounts (list 50-100 SKUs); create WhatsApp Business account for B2B inquiries from retailers and distributors; reach out to 50+ national retail chains with product samples; post daily content on Instagram and TikTok showcasing designs and behind-the-scenes manufacturing; contact 20-30 corporate procurement offices for uniform and gifting orders
6
Scale
After 6 months of ₹50L+ monthly sales, invest in backward integration by leasing sewing machines and hiring 20-30 workers; expand product range from basics to seasonal collections; establish own logistics with regional distributors; launch branded retail pop-ups in tier-1 malls; build B2B export channel via trade portals (Global Sources, Alibaba); hire digital marketing specialist for paid ads on Google and social platforms

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram Reels campaign featuring 15-second user styling videos; partner with 20-30 micro-influencers (50K-500K followers) in fashion/lifestyle niches; offer ₹500-1000 per video; cost: ₹1-2L for month-long campaign; expect 5-8% conversion to sales
  • LinkedIn B2B outreach targeting procurement managers at top 100 Indian corporates; cold email campaign emphasizing bulk discounts and customization; offer 2-3% discount for first 500-unit orders; cost: minimal; expected ROI 10-15% conversion
  • Facebook and Google Shopping ads targeting 25-45 year old women and men in metros and tier-2 cities; ₹3-5 per click average; weekly budget ₹10-15K; retarget website visitors with 20% discount codes; expect 2-3% ROAS in months 2-3
Government Schemes

Free money you didn't know about

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (35% for SC/ST/women) on machinery and working capital for manufacturing units
🏛
Technology Upgradation Fund Scheme (TUFS)
up to 25-50% subsidy on acquisition of computerised knitting/cutting machines for apparel manufacturing
🏚
Punjab Industrial Development Scheme 2022
5-7% subsidy on plant and machinery for textile manufacturing units in notified clusters
🏚
Punjab Startup Policy
₹5L seed grant and incubation support for apparel-tech startups; startup registration at Punjab State Industrial Development Authority (PSIDA)
