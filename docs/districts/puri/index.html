ODOP Product

Orissa Pattachitra

Puri has been the epicenter of Pattachitra art for over 500 years, with hereditary artisan communities like the Chitrakaras preserving the craft through generations. The district's deep connection to Hindu mythology and pilgrimage tourism creates natural demand and cultural authenticity. Access to natural pigment sources and established artisan networks in villages like Raghurajpur make production sustainable.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Puri.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Raghurajpur village near Puri (40 km from city) — the Pattachitra cluster with 200+ artisan families. Spend 2-3 weeks understanding techniques, cost structure, and market. Connect with Orissa Chitrakala Parishad (OCP) in Bhubaneswar for formal training and network access.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source natural pigments from traditional suppliers in Puri bazaar (₹8,000-12,000 for starter kit) and animal hair brushes from artisans in Raghurajpur (₹3,000-5,000). Buy cotton cloth in bulk from Cuttack textile market (₹50-100 per meter). First batch materials: ₹25,000-35,000.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3-5 experienced Chitrakara artisans from Raghurajpur cluster through direct contact or OCP referral. Start with 20-30 paintings monthly (6-12 months of artwork). Negotiate piece rates (₹2,500-8,000 per painting based on size/complexity) and ensure fair trade practices.
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam (online, free). Obtain IEC code from DGFT for export capability. Register brand name with IP Appellate Board. Get GI tag certification letter from DC Handicraft, Puri. Create business name like '[Your Name] Pattachitra Works' to leverage both heritage and personal authenticity.
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy (15% commission) targeting global art collectors — list 15-20 pieces with high-resolution photography. Simultaneously approach 5-10 luxury hotels and resorts in Puri for bulk orders. Create Instagram (@pattachitra_studio) with process videos. Reach out to Flipkart Handlooms curator for placement.
6
Scale
After ₹15-20L revenue: Hire 2-3 dedicated artisans full-time. Expand to larger formats and collaborations with interior designers. Launch customization service. Approach exporters for bulk international orders. Open small studio-cum-retail space in Puri main tourist corridor. Partner with luxury brands for limited edition collections.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram Reels & TikTok showing real artisans painting live, 60-second process videos with storytelling — focus on heritage tourism influencers and art collectors.
  • Partner with 10-15 heritage hotels (ITC Grand Odisha, etc.) for in-room art displays and referral commissions — offer 40% wholesale discount.
  • Create 'Art Lover's Subscription Box' on Udaan and Flipkart Handlooms — curated monthly pieces (₹5,000-8,000/box) sold via D2C email list and WhatsApp groups.
