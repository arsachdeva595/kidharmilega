Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Kottayam.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kottayam Rubber Board office and Kottayam Rubber Manufacturers Association (KRMA). Study 3-4 existing units in Kummanam or Vaikom clusters. Attend quarterly rubber industry meets organized by Indian Rubber Manufacturers Research Association (IRMRA). Expected timeline: 4-6 weeks.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw latex from registered dealers in Kottayam (Kummanam cooperative processing units) — ₹45-55/kg. First batch: 5 MT = ₹2.5-2.75L. Accelerators and vulcanizing agents from Cochin Port suppliers. Total raw material for first 100 days: ₹8-12L.
3
Produce / Partner
Establish 500-1000 sqft processing unit with vulcanizing presses, molds, and curing oven (₹6-10L capital). Partner with established Kottayam manufacturers for contract manufacturing (30-40% cost reduction). Hire 8-12 skilled workers from local Kummanam labor pool. Obtain ISO 9001:2015 certification within 3 months.
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam (Ministry of MSME). Obtain IEC code from nearest Customs office for export readiness. File for brand trademark with IP office. Get ISO 6134 (Rubber glove standards) certification. All within 6-8 weeks, ₹1-1.5L total cost.
5
First Sale
Launch B2B sales via IndiaMART Premium account targeting automotive OEMs and construction companies. Pitch to government hospitals and healthcare procurement portals (e-Procurement). Cold outreach to 50+ food processing units in Tamil Nadu and Karnataka. Target 5-10 anchor contracts in first 90 days.
6
Scale
Expand production capacity by 200-300% after first ₹1.5Cr annual revenue. Set up dedicated export cell for FDA-approved glove products (healthcare). Build distributor network in 5-6 states. Launch D2C branded glove product line via Amazon and specialized healthcare platforms after ₹3Cr revenue milestone.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • LinkedIn B2B campaign targeting procurement managers at Tier-1 auto component suppliers. Monthly case study posts showing product certifications and customer testimonials. Expected lead generation: 10-15 qualified inquiries/month.
  • Sponsorship of Kerala Rubber Board annual conference and trade shows (ICCW, IEEMA). Direct booth presence at Mumbai, Bangalore industrial expos. Budget: ₹5-8L/year for 3-4 major events.
  • Healthcare partnership marketing: Free product samples to 100+ government hospitals in Kerala with certified test reports. Obtain hospital bulk purchase orders for gloves. Target ₹20-30L first-year healthcare segment revenue.
