Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Gaya.

1
Learn / Research
Visit the Gaya garment cluster near Wazirganj and Kotwali areas; meet 5-10 existing garment manufacturers to understand production costs, lead times, and quality standards. Connect with SSIDC Gaya office for cluster data.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source cotton and polyester blends from Ahmedabad textile wholesalers (contact Calico Group or Rajpal Group) or local Patna suppliers. First batch cost: ₹2-3 lakhs for 500 units mixing. Order minimum: 1000+ units per SKU.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing Gaya garment factories (e.g., units in Kotwali industrial area) on contract manufacturing basis at ₹60-120 per piece depending on complexity. Alternatively, establish in-house unit with 10-15 sewing machines, 2 pressers, 1 cutting table; hire experienced supervisor from existing factories.
4
Brand & Register
Register as Udyam (Micro/Small enterprise) via udyamregistration.gov.in; obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office for export-readiness; trademark brand name with IP office (₹10-15K). Register with local GSTRO in Gaya for GST compliance.
5
First Sale
Launch D2C store on Shopify or Wix; list on Flipkart and Amazon via seller central; pitch B2B bulk orders via LinkedIn and direct outreach to retail chains (Reliance, Chhota Bazaar); attend India Export Summit in Delhi or Chennai to find export buyers.
6
Scale
After first 500K revenue, expand product line to include sarees, formal wear, kids' clothing; invest in automated cutting tables; establish quality certifications (OEKO-TEX, BCI); explore government export subsidies via MEIS; build brand presence via Instagram influencer collabs with fashion micro-influencers.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Create YouTube channel showcasing 'Factory to Wardrobe' behind-the-scenes production videos; target 25-45 year old B2B buyers and fashion enthusiasts; aim for 1000 subscribers in 3 months to build credibility.
  • Partner with 5-7 regional fashion micro-influencers (10-50K followers) in Bihar and Jharkhand for product seeding and discount code campaigns; track ROI via affiliate links on Instagram and TikTok.
  • Run Google Shopping ads targeting 'wholesale garments supplier' and 'custom uniform manufacturer' keywords; allocate ₹15-20K/month to capture B2B search intent in tier-2 cities.
