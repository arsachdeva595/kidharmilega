Machineries - Agriculture & Dairy

Palwal benefits from Haryana's robust agricultural economy, proximity to Delhi NCR market, established industrial clusters in Hodal and Faridabad adjacent areas, and a skilled workforce trained in mechanical engineering. The district's location on major highway corridors enables efficient logistics for heavy machinery export across India and neighboring states.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Palwal.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Hodal industrial cluster (30km from Palwal), meet 5-7 established machinery manufacturers, study Haryana Agricultural Department tender specifications, analyze competitor machinery at Jind and Hisar agricultural fairs, benchmark with TAFE and Mahindra tractor specs
2
Source Raw Materials
Source cast iron from Rohtak foundries (₹25-30L for 5-ton initial batch), electrical components from Faridabad suppliers, hydraulic systems from Bangalore OEMs via distributors, ball bearings from Mumbai/Kolkata suppliers — total first batch ₹40-50L
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 contract manufacturers in Hodal industrial cluster for assembly and fabrication, or establish 5000 sq ft workshop in Palwal MSME cluster with basic machinery (CNC, welding, grinding), hire 8-10 skilled workers from adjoining industrial areas
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam as Small Manufacturing Enterprise, obtain IEC code from Faridabad DGFT office, get ISO 9001 certification (₹2-3L), register brand with IP office, obtain agricultural machinery safety certificate from test lab, GST registration as goods manufacturer
5
First Sale
Launch on Agribusiness.in and Farmland.com platforms, approach state agricultural cooperative societies with product demonstrations, sell through established agricultural equipment dealers in Palwal, Faridabad, Jind districts, participate in Haryana Agriculture Expo and KrishiMela fairs, secure 2-3 government tenders through GeM portal
6
Scale
Establish company-owned distribution centers in Jind and Hisar (₹10L each), build buyer financing partnerships with NBFC serving agriculture sector, expand product line to 8-10 variants, develop regional dealer network with 15-20 partners, invest in digital marketing targeting farm cooperatives via social media and WhatsApp groups

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Create YouTube channel with 10-15 minute demos of machinery in real farm settings, partnering with popular farmers and cooperatives in Haryana for authentic testimonials
  • Sponsor and participate actively in Haryana's KrishiMela, Jind, and Agri-Expo shows with live machinery demonstrations and discounts for cooperative group purchases
  • Build direct WhatsApp broadcast network targeting 500+ agricultural cooperative societies and dairy farm operators with product updates, seasonal offers, and financing options
