Milk Processing (Desi Ghee) and Milk Products

Auraiya has a strong tradition of dairy farming with indigenous cattle breeds (Gir, Sahiwal) known for high milk fat content, ideal for premium ghee production. The district's cool climate and abundant green pastures support high-quality milk production. Local milk cooperatives and established cold chain infrastructure make it naturally suited for dairy product manufacturing.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Auraiya.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Auraiya's dairy clusters in Kanpur Road area and connect with Auraiya Cooperative Milk Union. Study production at Mathura (similar ODOP) dairy units. Interview 5-10 existing desi ghee producers to understand milk procurement rates (currently ₹28-32/liter) and production yield (20L milk = ~1kg ghee).
2
Source Raw Materials
Source milk from Auraiya Milk Cooperative (₹28-32 per liter, minimum 50L daily) or direct farmers in Mungaoli and Dibiypur villages. First batch: ₹50,000 for 100L milk + ₹15,000 for containers/equipment. Negotiate payment terms with cooperative for first 30 days.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing ghee producers in Kanpur Road industrial area for initial production under contract manufacturing (₹5-7 per kg commission). Alternatively, set up basic kitchen in compliance with FSSAI guidelines: pressure cooker + clarification vessel + storage containers. Hire 2-3 skilled workers from local dairy sector.
4
Brand & Register
Register under UDYAM scheme (online, free). Obtain FSSAI registration (₹5,000-8,000, 2-4 weeks). Register with IEC for exports. Establish brand identity: get custom labels printed (₹2-4 per unit for 1000+). Register trademark with IP India (₹4,000-6,000).
5
First Sale
List on Blinkit, BigBasket, Jiomart (commission 12-18%) targeting tier-2 cities first. Launch Instagram/Facebook B2C with farmer-story content. Approach premium supermarkets (Spencer, Reliance Fresh) in Agra/Lucknow for shelf placement. Connect with ayurvedic shops in Varanasi and Mathura through WhatsApp business outreach.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand product line (paneer, khoa). Increase milk sourcing to 300L daily through multiple farmer networks. Open wholesale accounts with regional distributors in Bihar and Madhya Pradesh. Set up contract manufacturing in larger capacity unit. Launch export via DirectShip or B2B platforms to UK/US markets.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Partner with Ayurveda wellness influencers on Instagram/YouTube to demonstrate ghee's health benefits (lactose-free, gut health). Target channels: @DrAnandDugar, @VedavatiAyurveda. Budget: ₹5-10K per influencer per month.
  • Create WhatsApp-based farmer-to-consumer storytelling campaign: share weekly photos/videos from Auraiya dairy farms. Use template: 'Meet Farmer Rajesh's 15 Gir cows — their milk becomes YOUR ghee.' Drive WhatsApp group sales with ₹500 first-order discount.
  • Cold-call premium hotel/restaurant chains in NCR region (Olive, Haandi, luxury resorts) with samples and bulk pricing. Position as 'authentic Auraiya ghee' for high-end Indian cuisine. Target 5-10 hotel accounts at ₹20,000/month each.
