ODOP Product

Pineapple

Thoubal's tropical climate, high rainfall (1900mm annually), and nutrient-rich alluvial soil create ideal conditions for pineapple cultivation. The district has over 2,000 hectares under pineapple farming with strong farmer networks, making it a natural production hub. Proximity to Imphal market and established cold-chain infrastructure support large-scale commercial operations.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Thoubal.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Thoubal's established pineapple clusters in Moirang and Lilong villages; interview 5-10 farmers through Manipur Horticulture Society; study 3-month crop cycle data from ICAR-NEH Research Center, Imphal.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source virus-free pineapple saplings from Manipur State Horticulture Department nurseries (₹2-3 per sapling); bulk order 5,000-10,000 saplings for 0.5-1 acre trial; negotiate with fertilizer suppliers in Imphal for bulk rates (₹40,000-60,000 for 6-month supply).
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing farm groups in Thoubal under FPO (Farmer Producer Organization) model or lease 0.5+ acres locally; hire local farm labor (₹300-400/day); implement drip irrigation and organic pest management; plan 18-month maturation cycle.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Agriculture) under MSME via e-portal; obtain FPO registration via Ministry of Agriculture if forming producer collective; apply for IEC code from APEDA for export eligibility; register brand name with Trademark office; secure FSSAI registration (₹12,000) for value-added products.
5
First Sale
List on BigBasket, Jio Mart, and local WhatsApp groups targeting hotels/restaurants in Imphal within first 2 months; partner with 2-3 wholesale fruit merchants; launch Instagram account with farm-to-table visuals; supply to local supermarkets in Imphal (Hotel Manipur area, Khwairamband Bazar).
6
Scale
After first harvest, invest ₹5-10L in cold-chain facility (small cool room); develop 2-3 value-added SKUs (juice, jam); hire export agent to target Bangladesh/Bhutan; expand to 2-3 acres; join state ODOP cluster programs for subsidy on equipment.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Partner with 3-5 premium restaurants in Imphal for farm-direct supply; create Instagram Reels showing harvest and farm life; offer first-time bulk buyer discounts (10-15%).
  • Join Manipur ODOP portal and leverage government promotional events; exhibit at State Agricultural Fairs in Imphal (annual June-July events) to connect with traders.
  • Create WhatsApp broadcast list targeting 200+ hotels, restaurants, and fruit wholesalers; send weekly availability and pricing updates with farm photos.
