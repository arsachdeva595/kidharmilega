The region's skilled artisan workforce, established supply chains for cotton yarn, and deep expertise in design innovation make it the undisputed hub for premium terry towel production in India.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Solapur.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Solapur's Textile Park and Vikarabad weaving cluster (30km from city center). Shadow 2-3 established manufacturers for 2 weeks. Attend Mumbai Home Furnishings Fair (IHGF) for market exposure. Interview 10+ retailers at Solapur's Latur Road wholesale market.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source cotton yarn from STDC (Solapur Textile Development Center) and registered suppliers in Solapur — budget ₹2-3L for first batch (5000-10000 meters). Dyes from Ahmedabad or local Solapur chemical dealers. First-batch material cost: ₹4-5L for 10,000 pieces.
3
Produce / Partner
Register as artisan partner with SFURTI clusters in Solapur or set up 5-10 power looms (₹15-20L investment). Alternatively, outsource weaving to 3-5 registered weavers in Vikarabad cluster while handling design, quality, and finishing in-house.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free online, 5 mins). Apply for IEC code from DGFT Delhi (₹500, 3 days). Trademark brand name with IP India (₹4500, 18 months approval). Get GTIN from GS1 India (₹10,000 for startup pack).
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Handmade and Flipkart Originals (free listing, 25% commission). Pitch 10 premium hotel chains (Taj, ITC, Oberoi) with samples and volume pricing. Contact Tanishq, Fabindia, and Ethnicity retailers for wholesale partnerships. Run Instagram/Facebook ads targeting affluent homemakers (budget ₹500/day, 5% conversion target).
6
Scale
After 6 months, apply for PM Vishwakarma grant for skill development. Expand loom capacity by 20-30%. Build 5-10 export relationships in USA/EU via IndiaTradeSupport portal. Launch B2C subscription (monthly curated towel boxes). Establish your own retail presence in Mumbai and Bangalore metro cities.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Partner with lifestyle influencers (50k-500k followers) on Instagram. Send 10-15 gifting boxes with brand story cards. Target hashtags #luxurytowels #sustainablehome #indiancrafts. Budget: ₹2-3L monthly for 5-7 partnerships.
  • Create YouTube educational content: 'How Solapur Towels Are Woven' (4-5 min videos). SEO-optimize for 'best terry towels India,' 'sustainable home textiles,' 'GI certified towels.' Convert viewers via links to Shopify store.
  • Launch LinkedIn B2B outreach campaign targeting hospitality procurement managers and corporate gifting heads. Offer 10-15% bulk discounts for first-time orders above 500 pieces. Budget: ₹50-75K for sales tool (Apollo.io/LinkedIn Sales Navigator).
