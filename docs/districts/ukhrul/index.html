ODOP Product

Kachai Lemon

Ukhrul's high-altitude terrain (800-1500m), cool tropical climate, and nutrient-rich volcanic soil create perfect conditions for Kachai lemon cultivation with superior acidity and aroma. The fruit thrives in the region's unique microclimatic conditions, which cannot be replicated elsewhere, giving it distinct organoleptic properties recognized globally.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Ukhrul.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Ukhrul district farm clusters near Kasom and Ashalong villages; meet 5-10 established lemon farmers; study soil composition and seasonal harvest calendars at State Agricultural Department, Imphal; contact Manipur Citrus Development Board for GI compliance requirements.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified disease-free saplings from Manipur Horticulture Department or ICAR-IISR Lucknow; negotiate bulk rate ₹80-150 per sapling; initial 0.5-acre plantation requires 150-200 saplings (₹15,000-30,000), plus farmyard manure ₹5,000, irrigation setup ₹40,000-60,000.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3-5 established Ukhrul lemon farmers for contract farming model; provide saplings and technical support; harvest sharing agreement (70-30 split typical); alternatively, lease 1-2 acres ready plantation land from District Horticulture Society for ₹5,000-8,000/acre annually.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Ministry MSME portal) as Agri-based manufacturing unit; apply for IEC code via DGFT online portal; register GI-authorized brand with District Commissioner's Certification Office; get organic certification (if pursuing) from NPOP-recognized body like INDOCERT (₹15,000-25,000); design packaging with GI logo, Manipur origin mark, and nutritional claims.
5
First Sale
Launch on JioMart, BigBasket, and Amazon Fresh targeting premium organic segment; reach out to specialty grocers like Naturals, The Whole Truth, Urban Platter; partner with Akaram by Shivoham and similar wellness brands; export via Agrotech portal and Trade India; establish direct B2B with juice companies (Tropicana supplier contacts) and food processors.
6
Scale
After first harvest (12-14 months), expand acreage to 2-3 acres; launch value-added products (juice concentrate, pickle, oil); build cold storage facility (₹50,000-80,000); apply for SFURTI cluster benefit to subsidize processing unit; target institutional buyers (schools, hospitals); establish agro-export FIEO membership for international logistics.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram/YouTube content: Behind-the-scenes farm videos and organic cultivation stories; partner with health & wellness micro-influencers (100K-500K followers) for product unboxing and nutritional benefits; hashtag #KachaiLemonStory to build community.
  • Email marketing to premium organic food retailers and FMCG supply chains; monthly newsletter with harvest updates, GI certification stories, and B2B volume discounts; target category buyers at Nature's Basket, Whole Foods India analogs.
  • Trade show presence at Natural Products Expo India, Organic Farming Expo (Bangalore, Delhi); conduct lemon juice tasting booth; distribute GI certification brochures; generate B2B supplier leads and distributor partnerships.

PM-AAGRI SCHEME
up to ₹50,000 subsidy for farm mechanization and horticulture infrastructure including irrigation systems and cold storage.

PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 Lakh loan (80% subsidy for SC/ST/women) for agri-processing and export setup.

Manipur State Horticulture Mission
₹40-60% subsidy on saplings, fertilizers, and farm equipment for fruit cultivation across Ukhrul district.
🏚

customs duty exemption and logistics subsidy (10-15% of export value) for GI-tagged products from recognized clusters.
