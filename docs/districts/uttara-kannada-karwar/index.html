ODOP Product

Spices

Uttara Kannada's tropical climate, high rainfall, and nutrient-rich soil from the Western Ghats foothills create ideal conditions for growing premium quality spices like cardamom, black pepper, cinnamon, and red chillies. The district's proximity to major ports like Karwar enables direct export access. Historical spice cultivation heritage and farming communities provide ready supply chains and expertise.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Uttara Kannada (Karwar).

1
Learn / Research
Visit Karwar town and meet spice traders in the wholesale market near Karwar Port; attend sessions at ICAR-Krishi Vigyan Kendra Uttara Kannada; network with established APEDA-registered exporters in the district to understand quality standards, export protocols, and seasonal supply patterns.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source directly from cooperative societies like Uttara Kannada Spice Growers Cooperative or purchase from established dealers in Karwar wholesale market. First batch (500 kg mixed spices): ₹150,000-250,000 depending on variety and organic certification. Build relationships with 3-5 farmer suppliers to ensure consistent quality.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with certified processing units in Karwar town for grading, cleaning, and packaging (contract processing cost: ₹8-12 per kg). Alternatively, set up small processing facility (₹1-2 Cr capex) with moisture control, cold storage, and packaging lines. Start with contract manufacturing to reduce capex.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) and apply for IEC code via DGFT portal (required for exports). Obtain FSSAI food license and organic certification (NPOP) if targeting premium segment (₹50,000-150,000). Register trademark on brand name (₹5,000-10,000). Set up GST registration (18% on spices).
5
First Sale
List on Amazon, Flipkart, and BigBasket with 150+ unit SKUs first; parallel approach: reach out to 20-30 regional organic stores and specialty food shops in Bangalore, Pune, Goa. Email spice traders and food companies with samples. Use APEDA's trade database to identify direct export buyers. Target Karwar port's exporter networks for B2B sales.
6
Scale
After 6 months, increase farmer partnerships to 10+, launch 3-4 new spice blend SKUs, secure ISO 22000 certification for larger B2B contracts, negotiate shelf space in 50+ modern retail stores. Explore Amazon Global Selling or Shopify for international DTC sales. Invest ₹50L in branded packaging and marketing to build consumer recognition.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram & Pinterest content strategy: post 3x/week farm-to-table videos from Karwar farmers, reels on spice benefits, traditional recipes; partner with 5-10 food bloggers for unboxing reviews and recipe collaborations; target #IndianSpices #OrgnicFood communities.
  • LinkedIn B2B outreach: create a company page, post export certifications and quality reports monthly, direct outreach to 100+ food companies, FMCG brands, and food service chains with case studies of supply quality and compliance.
  • Offline sampling strategy: set up stalls at 3-4 farmer markets and food expos in Bangalore, Pune, Goa annually; sponsor local food festivals in Karwar to build brand credibility; distribute branded samples to 50+ specialty/organic stores with on-shelf demonstrations.
Government Schemes

Free money you didn't know about

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 Lakh loan for manufacturing unit with 35% subsidy for general category, enabling spice processing setup.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
supports spice cluster infrastructure, common facilities, and marketing; applicable for Uttara Kannada spice hub.
🏚
Raitha Bandhu Scheme (Karnataka)
₹50,000 cash assistance for farmers every season; can provide supply-side support and farmer partnership subsidies.
🏚
Karnataka Agriculture & Food Export Promotion Policy
subsidy on export certifications, logistics, and participation in international trade fairs; eligible for spice exporters.
