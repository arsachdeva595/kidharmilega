ODOP Product

Tomato

Satna's northeastern Madhya Pradesh location provides alluvial soil with optimal pH balance and mineral content for premium tomato cultivation. The district's water table, seasonal rainfall patterns (1000-1200mm), and temperature fluctuations (15-38°C) create ideal ripening conditions. Proximity to major demand centers Delhi, Mumbai, and Indore ensures rapid market access.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Satna.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Satna agricultural mandi (main wholesale hub near Satna city center), connect with 3-5 established tomato farmers in Maihar and Satna blocks, study crop cycles at Satna Agricultural Science Centre, review best practices at ATMA (Agricultural Technology Management Agency) office.
2
Source Raw Materials
Procure seeds from IIHR Bangalore distributor in Jabalpur (₹8,000-12,000 per kg for hybrid seeds), source organic inputs from Bhopal-based suppliers (Krishitech, Agritech) at ₹1.2-1.8L per acre for full season; negotiate bulk rates with local farmers' input societies; initial batch land requirement 0.5-2 acres at ₹2-3L per acre annual lease.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 5-10 progressive farmers in Satna district under contract farming model (supply seeds + inputs, buy entire yield at pre-agreed rates); establish collection center at Maihar for aggregation; implement soil testing (₹500-800 per sample) and drip irrigation (₹15,000-25,000 per acre); train farmers on organic certification protocols via APEDA-listed certifiers.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam for agricultural enterprise; obtain IEC code for exports from nearest APEDA office (Indore, 120km away); get Organic Certification (ECOCERT, APEDA-listed body, 6-8 weeks, ₹15,000-30,000); register brand name with IP office; obtain FSSAI license if adding value-added products (₹5,000).
5
First Sale
Launch on BigBasket, Blinkit, Jiomart direct farmer partnerships within month 2; negotiate with 2-3 regional hotel chains (Bhopal, Indore) for regular supply contracts; list on organic marketplaces (Crunchy Tales, Farms2Kitchen) with 40% gross margin; activate WhatsApp Business and Instagram for D2C pre-orders from organic communities in Delhi/Bangalore.
6
Scale
Expand aggregation network to 20-25 farmers by month 9; establish cold storage unit (₹40-60L capex) in Satna city for year-round supply; develop tomato concentrate/powder line (₹50L setup) for B2B food manufacturers; target Tier 2 city retail chains; pursue GI registration application (if quality standards met) for premium positioning.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram Reels showing farm-to-table journey; partner with 10 micro-influencers in organic/sustainable living space (50K-500K followers) for farmer spotlight content + unboxing videos; target followers of Big Basket, Crunchy Tales accounts.
  • WhatsApp group-based community building: create locality-wise groups in Bangalore, Delhi NCR, Pune; send weekly crop updates + recipes; offer 15% early-subscriber discount; track customer retention via group engagement.
  • B2B email outreach to 200+ restaurants/hotels across Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Uttar Pradesh highlighting consistency, organic certification, and competitive pricing (₹12-16/kg wholesale); sponsor 2-3 local farm fairs in Satna annually.
