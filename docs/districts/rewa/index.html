Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Rewa.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Rewa Bamboo Cluster (Govardhan Nagar area) and meet established bamboo artisan groups; connect with 3-4 master craftspeople; study market at Gandhi Nagar handicraft market in Delhi and Jaipur; analyze competitor pricing on Etsy and Amazon Handmade for similar products.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source processed bamboo strips from established suppliers in Rewa district (₹15,000-25,000 for 100 kg first batch); establish relationships with 2-3 local dye makers for natural finishes; negotiate 30-day payment terms; estimated first raw material investment: ₹45,000-60,000.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 5-8 skilled artisans in Rewa through cooperative groups or direct wage-based agreements; establish a small 800-1000 sq ft workshop in Rewa town for quality control and finishing; begin with 2-3 core product lines (baskets, mats, small furniture pieces); produce 200-300 units monthly in first 3 months.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Micro/Small Enterprise) within 15 days; apply for IEC code through DGFT portal (₹500-1000, 2-3 weeks); register brand name with IP India; obtain GST registration (mandatory for exports); create product specification sheets and quality certifications for export compliance.
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy with 15-20 product listings (target initial ₹3-5 lakh monthly sales); simultaneously list on Amazon Handmade and own Shopify store; pitch to 20-30 interior design studios via LinkedIn; approach 10-15 eco-resort and boutique hotel chains for bulk orders; participate in Rewa District ODOP trade fairs.
6
Scale
After stabilizing ₹5-8 lakh monthly revenue, expand artisan network to 15-20 craftspeople; introduce 5-6 new product lines; establish export partnerships with European wholesalers through trade missions; invest in branded packaging and sustainability certifications (Fair Trade, FSC); target ₹25-30 lakh monthly revenue by month 12.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram Reels strategy: post 3x weekly artisan-in-action videos showing bamboo crafting process; use hashtags #BambooArtisanRewa #SustainableLiving #HandmadeInMP; target sustainability micro-influencers with 50k-200k followers for collaboration (₹2,000-5,000 per post).
  • LinkedIn B2B outreach: create 10-12 case studies of corporate clients using bamboo products for office décor; directly message procurement teams at 100+ companies; position as sustainable office furnishing solution; aim for ₹2-5 lakh corporate orders.
  • Email marketing funnel: build list of 2,000+ eco-conscious home décor enthusiasts via Pinterest pins and blog content (SEO-optimized for 'bamboo furniture India'); monthly newsletter highlighting new designs, artisan stories, and sustainability impact; target 8-12% conversion rate.
