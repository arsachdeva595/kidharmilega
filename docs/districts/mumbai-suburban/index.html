Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Mumbai Suburban.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Zaveri Bazaar, Bhuleshwar jewellery cluster, and BKC jewellery parks in Mumbai to understand supplier networks, pricing, and design trends. Meet 5+ established jewellers and gemologists. Enrol in FCI (Gem & Jewellery Council of India) online gemology certification course.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source bullion from Mumbai's IBJA (Indian Bullion Jewellers Association) members and MMTC-PAMP. First batch cost: ₹25-50 lakh for 5-10 kg gold and 2-5 carats diamonds. Contact suppliers in Zaveri Bazaar, Fort area, and online via IBJA directory.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with hallmarked artisans in Bhuleshwar or BKC clusters (₹10-20K per piece labour cost). Alternatively, lease mini manufacturing unit (₹1-3L monthly) in registered jewellery parks. Ensure BIS hallmarking certification for all gold and silver pieces.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, ₹0 cost). Obtain IEC code from DGFT for export. Get BIS hallmark registration. Register brand with IP office. Open FSSAI account if selling online. Obtain state Shops & Establishments license.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon, Flipkart, and Meesho with ₹15-20K monthly ad spend. Create Instagram and Pinterest accounts (jewelry-focused UGC content). Partner with 3-5 local high-end jewellery retailers. Participate in wedding expos and luxury fairs in Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore.
6
Scale
Expand product SKUs after first 3 months. Launch influencer collaboration (luxury lifestyle creators). Apply for SEIS/CLCS export incentives. Open showroom in commercial area (BKC, Navi Mumbai). Hire 2-3 design apprentices. Build B2B bulk order pipeline with 5+ corporate clients.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Create Instagram Reels showing live jewellery-making process from Bhuleshwar artisans to build authenticity. Use hashtags #MumbaiJewellery #HallmarkedGold. Target 25-45F audience with ₹5K weekly ad spend.
  • Partner with 10 micro-influencers (50K-200K followers) in luxury/wedding niche for unboxing and styling content. Offer 10-15% commission per sale. Focus on Pinterest and Instagram Shopping.
  • Launch referral program offering ₹5K-10K discount vouchers for customer referrals. Run SMS campaigns to B2B retailers in Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore about bulk orders. Sponsor 2-3 destination wedding expos annually.
Government Schemes

Free money you didn't know about

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
provides loans up to ₹3L at 0% interest for artisans and small jewellery makers engaged in gem-cutting and design
🏛
PMEGP (PM Employment Generation Programme)
grants up to ₹25L for manufacturing enterprises in gems and jewellery sector with subsidy of 25-35%
🏚
Maharashtra Gems & Jewellery Policy
provides 10-year tax holidays and 50% subsidy on rent in registered jewellery parks for manufacturing units
🏚
SGSY (State Subsidy Scheme for Artisans)
provides ₹2-5L concessional loans to registered craftspersons engaged in jewellery manufacturing
Registration required: Required — udyamregistration.gov.in
