Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Munger.

1
Learn / Research
Visit lemongrass cultivation clusters in Munger (Jamalpur, Asarganj taluks); connect with Munger District Agriculture Office; study essential oil processing at CSIR-IIIM Lucknow's lemongrass research outputs; shadow 2-3 existing essential oil producers in Varanasi or Kannauj.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source lemongrass saplings from Munger District Agricultural Department nurseries or Krishi Vigyan Kendra (cost ₹15,000-25,000 for 1-acre starter lot); purchase distillation equipment (300L capacity) from Kannauj machinery suppliers (₹80,000-120,000); order glass bottles and labels in bulk from Firozabad or online (₹20,000 for 5,000 units).
3
Produce / Partner
Establish distillation unit on 0.5-1 acre leased/owned land in Jamalpur or Asarganj block; partner with established essential oil distillers in Kannauj (Uttar Pradesh) for toll processing initially (₹400-600 per kg of oil); hire 2-3 seasonal workers during harvest; target 50-100 kg essential oil monthly in Year 1.
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam (MSME) within 15 days of startup; apply for IEC code via DGFT portal (₹0, online); register trademark with IP India (₹3,500-5,000); obtain Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) license for value-added products (₹5,000-8,000); check Munger District administration for any lemongrass cultivation certifications.
5
First Sale
Launch D2C on Amazon, Flipkart, and Nykaa Beauty with ₹5 minimum markup; secure B2B contracts through LinkedIn outreach to cosmetic and Ayurvedic companies; pitch to wellness retailers via industry directories (Beauty & Wellness Association); list on IndiaTradeSupplies and Global Sources for export leads; send samples to 10-15 wellness brands monthly.
6
Scale
Expand cultivation to 2-3 acres by month 8-10; develop value-added products (lemongrass tea, dried herb kits, diffuser blends) to boost margins; establish contract farming partnerships with 10-15 local farmers to secure raw material; target exports to Southeast Asia via trade shows (Cosmex India, Interorganic); build B2B subscription model with 20-30 regular wholesale customers.
