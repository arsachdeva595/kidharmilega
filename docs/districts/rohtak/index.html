Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Rohtak.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Rohtak's existing fastener clusters around Rail Nagar and Sector 7-8. Study competitor offerings at Rohtak Tool & Machine Centre. Connect with HSIIDC (Haryana State Industrial Infrastructure Development Corporation) for market data. Attend industry trade shows at Delhi Pragati Maidan for market sizing.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from Rohtak Local Steel Merchants Association (₹8-12L for first 500-1000 units) or directly from JSW Steel/Tata Steel stockists in Sonepat (50km away). Establish accounts with component suppliers in Dharuhera and Bahadurgarh industrial areas. Typical first batch cost: ₹10-15L for mixed fastener inventory.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing machining units in Rohtak's Industrial Area for contract manufacturing or invest in 2-3 CNC machines and hiring skilled operators (₹8-12L equipment cost). Alternatively, purchase semi-finished components and operate as a trading + light manufacturing hybrid model starting with ₹5-8L investment.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam on udyamregistration.gov.in as manufacturing unit. Apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) through DGFT portal for export capability. Obtain ISO 9001:2015 certification (₹1-2L) for B2B credibility. Register brand name with IP India and get GST registration under MSME category.
5
First Sale
Launch on IndiaMART and TradeKey with bulk product listings and bulk discounts. Cold-call to top 50 OEMs in Delhi-NCR region (automotive, appliances, machinery manufacturers). Attend National Manufacturing Conclave and industry expos. Use LinkedIn B2B outreach targeting procurement managers. Partner with 5-10 distributors across NCR offering 15-20% margin.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand product range to specialized fasteners (aerospace-grade, stainless, customized). Open a showroom in Rohtak Main Market. Invest in automated packaging and quality testing equipment. Develop export channels through APEDA (Agricultural & Processed Food Products Export Development Authority) and FIEO (Federation of Indian Export Organisations). Hire technical sales team to penetrate automotive OEM supply chains.
