Ceramic Tiles & Sanitary Goods

East Delhi houses over 800+ ceramic tile manufacturing units concentrated in industrial clusters like Okhla, Kondli, and Patparganj, with established supply chains, skilled labor, and proximity to raw material suppliers. The district has decades of ceramic manufacturing expertise, enabling rapid scaling and access to experienced workforce. Strong backward linkage with distributors across North India makes it ideal for tile and sanitary goods production.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for East.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Okhla Industrial Area Phase 1-3 and Kondli ceramic clusters; spend 2 weeks meeting 10-15 established manufacturers (Somani Tiles, Nitco, Kajaria); understand production processes, cost structures, and distributor networks; attend ceramic tile expos at Pragati Maidan.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw clay from Rajasthan (Jaisalmer/Bikaner region) via traders; partner with local suppliers in Delhi NCR for feldspar and silica; contact: Rajasthan Clay Suppliers Association. First batch raw material cost: ₹8-12 lakhs for 10,000 sq ft tile production capacity.
3
Produce / Partner
Lease 2,000-5,000 sq ft manufacturing unit in Okhla/Kondli (₹12-18 per sq ft monthly); purchase second-hand kiln and pressing equipment (₹40-80 lakhs); hire experienced tile production supervisor and 8-10 skilled workers; start with contract manufacturing arrangement with established brands before launching own brand.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME immediately; obtain IEC code from DGFT for exports; apply FSSAI if including sanitary goods; secure BIS certification for tiles (IS 15622-1:2017); trademark brand name with IP office; company registration (OPC/Private Ltd recommended for scale).
5
First Sale
Approach 50+ authorized tile distributors in Delhi NCR through industry associations (IAEME); list on Amazon Business and Flipkart for SMEs; create WhatsApp catalog with product images for direct contractor outreach; target 5-10 real estate projects via project site visits; offer 2-3% volume discounts for bulk orders.
6
Scale
After 6 months profitability, add sanitaryware production line; expand to 3-4 state distributor networks (UP, Punjab, Haryana); invest in digital showroom/website; apply for PMEGP/SFURTI subsidies to reduce capex; hire sales team for retail partnerships; explore export to Bangladesh/Nepal via trade corridors.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Create YouTube channel (TileMastery DIY) with 2-3 minute videos on tile installation trends, durability tests, and design inspirations; target 100K subscribers in 12 months; repurpose content on Instagram Reels for younger architects.
  • Partner with 10-15 interior designers in Delhi NCR to showcase products in their portfolios; offer 15% designer discount; generate word-of-mouth referrals from high-end residential projects.
  • Run Google Shopping ads targeting keywords 'ceramic tiles near me' and 'sanitary ware suppliers Delhi' with budget ₹2-3 lakhs monthly; optimize for contractor and B2B queries.
Government Schemes

Free money you didn't know about

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy for manufacturing MSMEs with 35% margin for SC/ST/women entrepreneurs.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
cluster development support, common facility centers, and working capital assistance up to ₹50L for ceramic clusters.
🏚
Delhi Industrial Policy 2021-2031
15% capital subsidy (capped ₹1Cr) for manufacturing units in notified industrial areas; additional GST refund benefits.
🏚
Delhi MSME Udyam Plus Scheme
free brand registration, digital marketing subsidy (₹50K), and export promotion grants up to ₹5 lakhs.
