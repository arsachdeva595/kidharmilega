ODOP Product

Naga Mircha

Peren district's unique high-altitude climate, volcanic soil, and monsoon patterns create ideal conditions for cultivating Naga Mircha with its signature extreme heat and distinct flavor profile. The district has generations of farming expertise in chilli cultivation and established export networks that command premium prices in global spice and hot sauce markets.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Peren.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Peren district's established chilli clusters in Longjem and Seithekema villages; connect with existing farmer cooperatives under Peren ODOP office; attend Nagaland Agricultural Department workshops on GI certification maintenance and export standards.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source premium Naga Mircha seeds from certified nurseries in Peren (₹15,000-25,000 for first-season seedlings covering 0.5 acres); establish direct farmer partnerships for bulk dried chilli procurement at ₹300-400 per kg; arrange drying and packaging from local vendors in Peren town.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3-5 smallholder farmers in Peren district under contract farming (guaranteed buyback at ₹350/kg); set up a small drying and processing unit (2,000-5,000 sqft) in Peren town with basic solar/mechanical dryers; hire 2-3 staff for sorting, drying, and packaging operations.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) for ₹0 online; apply for IEC code from DGFT office in Kohima (₹5,000-10,000 processing); obtain Spice Board certification for export-grade chilli; register brand name with USPTO/WIPO for global protection; apply Nagaland Food Safety authority certification.
5
First Sale
Launch via Spice exporters in Nagaland (Guwahati-based traders); list on Alibaba and Global Sources for B2B buyers; establish Amazon Global and etsy presence for D2C sales; pitch to specialty food brands (Schwartz, Simply Organic); attend food trade fairs in London, Las Vegas, and Singapore.
6
Scale
Expand farmer network to 15-20 suppliers by month 8; launch proprietary hot sauce line with regional branding; open direct Amazon FBA warehouses in USA/UK; partner with 5+ e-commerce platforms in tier-1 Indian cities; invest in ISO 22000 certification for premium positioning.
