Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for South-West.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Moti Nagar and Sector 8-12 dairy clusters in South-West Delhi; interview 5-7 existing milk processors at Mother Dairy nodes and local dairies; study ₹2-10L monthly operations at Dairy Cooperative plants
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw milk from Delhi Cooperative Milk Federation (DCMSF) procurement centers at ₹28-32/liter for 500L daily (₹14-16K daily cost); procure culture and additives from Sigma-Aldrich distributor in Okhla; first batch budget: ₹50,000 for 500L capacity trial
3
Produce / Partner
Either: (a) register as small dairy unit with FSSAI and lease 800-1200 sq ft space in Sector 8 Dwarka with 5-ton refrigeration setup (₹25-35L), OR (b) partner with existing licensed dairy producers in Moti Nagar offering contract production at 8-12% margin on final product cost
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, 10 min process); obtain FSSAI license (₹5000, 30 days); apply for Trademark for brand name (₹4500); open CURRENT account with HDFC/ICICI for B2B invoicing; print labels compliant with FSSAI nutrition panel format
5
First Sale
Launch with 50 units across 10-12 neighborhood Blinkit/Zepto nodes (20% commission); pitch to 8-10 sweet shops in Rajiv Chowk/Sector 9 with 35% wholesale margin; secure 1-2 restaurant partnerships for dessert supply (mohur-based pricing); sell 20-30 units daily in first month targeting ₹25-30K weekly revenue
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to Greater Noida dairy corridor and Gurgaon metro proximity zones; hire 2-3 delivery staff; establish WhatsApp-based subscription model (20 units/month per customer at ₹8-10/unit premium); scale to 2000+ daily units by month 12 with additional 20L daily capacity

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram Reels showing live yogurt-making process from raw milk to finished product; target 25-40 demographic with 'Farm-to-Table' storytelling; use hashtag #DelhiDairyStories; 3-4 reels/week at 20% engagement cost
  • Partner with 15-20 lifestyle YouTubers in Delhi NCR for unboxing and taste-test videos; 5-min reviews of Lassi/Rabri with family reactions; ₹10-15K per video for 50K+ subscriber channels
  • Run Google Local Services Ads targeting 'milk delivery Delhi' and 'fresh yogurt near me'; budget ₹500/day for first 90 days; geo-target South and West Delhi ZIP codes with 5-star FSSAI certification emphasis
