ODOP Product

Wooden Crafts

Basti has a deep-rooted tradition of wooden craft heritage spanning generations, with abundant access to quality Sheesham wood from local forests and nearby regions. The district's skilled artisan community possesses specialized knowledge in intricate carving and joinery techniques passed down through families, making it naturally suited for premium wooden product manufacturing.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Basti.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Basti's primary artisan clusters in Kotwali and Sadar areas; connect with 3-4 established craftsmen; study market rates at Delhi's High Road Furniture Market and Mumbai's Chor Bazaar; understand lead times and quality grading.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw Sheesham wood from local timber yards in Basti (cost: ₹80K-150K for first 5-ton batch); identify 2-3 trusted suppliers; maintain 30-day inventory cycle; negotiate direct mill prices for bulk orders.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3-5 established artisans in Basti through revenue-sharing model (12-18% of product value); establish quality checkpoints; define product specs; plan for 45-60 day production cycle; consider small workshop setup with 2-3 salaried craftsmen.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Ministry of MSME); apply for IEC code from DGFT; file trademark for brand name; obtain Handicraft Certification from DC Handicraft; setup GST registration; create brand identity with documentation of heritage craftsmanship story.
5
First Sale
Launch on Flipkart Handmade and Amazon Artisan; build Instagram/Pinterest presence with product photography; reach out to 20+ interior design studios in Delhi/NCR; pitch to luxury furniture retailers; use WhatsApp Business for B2B inquiries; launch minimal website with product catalog.
6
Scale
Achieve ₹15-20L monthly revenue by month 6; expand product line to seasonal collections; hire dedicated sales/marketing person; establish relationships with 5-7 large furniture retailers; explore bulk exports to 2-3 countries; scale production through additional artisan partnerships.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram Reels & TikTok: Create 15-second videos of artisans hand-carving wood, sanding, and finishing; use #BastiWoodCraft #SheeshamMagic #ArtisanLife to reach DIY and design enthusiasts; target 2-3 posts weekly.
  • LinkedIn B2B outreach: Connect with interior design firms, hospitality procurement managers, and corporate buyers; share case studies of custom installations; aim for 10-15 qualified B2B leads monthly.
  • Pinterest SEO strategy: Pin high-quality product images with keywords like 'handcrafted wooden furniture', 'traditional Indian wood carving', 'Sheesham wood decor'; link to product pages; target DIY and home décor boards.
