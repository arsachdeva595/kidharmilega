ODOP Product

Wooden Toys and Handicraft

Sehore has a strong heritage of woodcraft tradition with skilled artisans practicing for generations. The district's access to quality timber from nearby forests and established craft clusters in villages like Ralamandal and Badi make it ideal for wooden toy production. The existing artisan community and cultural continuity provide both raw talent and market understanding.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Sehore.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Ralamandal and Badi villages in Sehore to study existing clusters; interview 5-10 master craftsmen; visit Sehore Government Museum; study toy designs at Madhya Pradesh Handicraft Board office in Bhopal.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source timber from authorized wood merchants in Bhopal's timber market (Arera Colony area); establish relationships with artisans in Ralamandal; first batch (500 toys): ₹80,000-120,000 including wood, dyes, tools.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing artisan groups in Sehore or register under PM Vishwakarma to formalize production; set up small workshop (300-500 sq ft) near artisan clusters; establish quality control process with design templates.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (micro/small enterprise); apply for IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Bhopal; trademark brand name with IP Office; obtain Handloom Mark certificate from DC Handicraft Ministry; register GST.
5
First Sale
Launch on Flipkart Handlooms, Amazon Craft, Etsy with high-quality photography; pitch to gift retailers via B2B platforms like TradeKey; engage Instagram/Pinterest with artisan story content; approach boutique hotels and premium toy chains directly.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand product SKUs (animals, vehicles, games); hire 2-3 additional artisans; apply for SFURTI cluster development grant; establish export contracts through APEDA; launch own DTC brand website; participate in international trade fairs (Frankfurt, Singapore).

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram Reels showing 60-second artisan carving videos; post 3x weekly with behind-the-scenes content; use hashtags #SehoreWooden #ArtisanToys #EcoPlaytoys to reach eco-conscious parents globally.
  • Partner with 5 eco-parenting blogs/YouTube channels for product reviews and gifting; offer 20% affiliate commission; target audiences interested in Montessori and sustainable toys.
  • Create 'Adopt an Artisan' gifting campaign; each purchase shows customer the artisan's name and story; launch during peak gifting seasons (Diwali, Christmas, Mother's Day) on Facebook with lookalike audiences.
