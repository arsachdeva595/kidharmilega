ODOP Product

Marine Foods

Ramanathapuram's 200+ km coastline and strategic location on the Bay of Bengal make it ideal for marine food production. The district has established cold chain infrastructure, direct access to fishing grounds, and a skilled workforce with generations of marine processing expertise. Proximity to major ports like Vizhinjam and established seafood export routes provide natural competitive advantage.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Ramnad (Ramanathapuram).

1
Learn / Research
Visit Ramanathapuram fishing harbor and auction centers at dawn; network with established exporters in Nagercoil and Tuticorin seafood clusters; study APEDA export standards and competitor offerings on IndiaSeafood.com
2
Source Raw Materials
Contract with licensed fisheries associations in Ramanathapuram for regular supply (₹3-5L for first month's raw material); establish relationship with 2-3 boats/fishing cooperatives; source packaging from Chennai suppliers (₹50K-100K initial investment)
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing seafood processing units in Ramanathapuram town for co-processing (₹30K-50K monthly rental); alternatively, set up small 500 sq ft facility with freezing and sorting equipment (₹15L capital); hire local processing staff with existing experience
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, free); Apply for IEC code from Ramanathapuram customs office; Get FSSAI food business license (₹2000-5000); Obtain Marine Products Export Development Authority (MPEDA) registration; Trademark brand name with IP office (₹10K-15K)
5
First Sale
List on IndiaSeafood.com and FreshtoHome; Contact HoReCa aggregators (Swiggy Instamart, BigBasket); Approach restaurant procurement teams directly; Create WhatsApp B2B catalog; Apply for APEDA export certification and contact 5-10 international importers with samples
6
Scale
Achieve HACCP and ISO 22000 certification by month 6-8; Launch branded D2C website and Instagram commerce; Expand product range to value-added items; Target institutional contracts (hotels, corporate cafeterias); Set up second processing unit; Explore EU registrations for higher-premium markets
