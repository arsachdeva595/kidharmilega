ODOP Product

Marine

Diu is strategically positioned as a major fishing port with access to Arabian Sea's rich marine resources. The district's long coastline, established fishing infrastructure, and centuries-old maritime tradition make it ideal for both seafood production and marine-based handicrafts. Local artisans have developed specialized skills in shell and pearl jewelry making.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Diu.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Diu fishing harbor, meet with fish traders at Diu Fish Landing Centre, study existing seafood exporters in Jampore area. Connect with Diu District Fisheries Department for market data and supplier networks. Attend IIFT seafood trade shows.
2
Source Raw Materials
Partner with registered fish vendors at Diu Port or Gogla Harbor for daily catch supply (₹50,000-100,000 initial inventory). Source shells from local collectors in coastal villages around Diu (₹20,000-30,000 first batch). Negotiate cold storage space at Diu Fish Processing Facility.
3
Produce / Partner
For seafood: establish partnership with existing processing units or set up small cold storage + cleaning facility. For handicrafts: engage local artisans in Diu's shell craft clusters through District Artisan Federation. Obtain FSSAI registration for food products before production begins.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME online (udyamregistration.gov.in). Obtain IEC code from local customs authority at Diu. Apply for FSSAI license (₹2,000-5,000) and Apeda registration for exports. Design branding highlighting Diu's heritage and sustainability credentials.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh, Grofers, and BigBasket for fresh seafood. Create Instagram shop for marine handicrafts targeting domestic tourists and international buyers. List on Etsy and World Market for shell jewelry. Partner with 5-star hotels in Diu, Goa for direct supply. Contact export agents in Mumbai for bulk B2B sales.
6
Scale
Expand product range to value-added items (smoked fish, fish curry paste). Build own brand e-commerce website with cold chain logistics. Establish QR-based traceability for export compliance. Open retail outlet in Diu's Lighthouse area for tourist footfall. Scale to frozen and processed variants for broader geographic reach.

How to get customers

  • Instagram Reels showcasing daily catch from Diu harbor and artisans hand-crafting shell jewelry — tag travel influencers and food bloggers visiting Diu.
  • Partner with OYO and Airbnb to place branded shell handicrafts and seafood hampers in tourist accommodations — include QR code linking to online store.
  • Launch WhatsApp Business catalog for fresh seafood pre-orders targeting restaurants in Gujarat and Goa — offer bulk discounts and weekly delivery schedules.
