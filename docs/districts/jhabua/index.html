Kadaknath Chicken

Jhabua district's indigenous Kadaknath breed thrives in the region's tribal belt with ideal climatic conditions and traditional rearing practices spanning centuries. The local community's expertise in poultry farming and natural free-range grazing environment creates unmatched meat quality and authenticity. GI certification ensures premium positioning and protected market access.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Jhabua.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Jhabua district poultry clusters near Thandla and Meghnagar blocks; interact with established Kadaknath rearers through Jhabua District Cooperative; study GI certification requirements from Madhya Pradesh Tribal Authority; benchmark premium pricing at specialty markets in Indore (₹500-700/kg vs ₹250-300/kg regular chicken).
2
Source Raw Materials
Source Kadaknath chicks from registered hatcheries in Jhabua (Govt. Animal Husbandry Department); contact Indore-based feed suppliers for organic/conventional mix; first batch cost for 500 birds: ₹75,000-100,000 (chicks ₹35-40/bird, 45 days feed ₹30-35/bird).
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing Kadaknath farmers in Jhabua for contract rearing (revenue sharing 60:40 model) or establish dedicated poultry shed with open-housing system (₹8-10 per bird capacity); hire trained farm manager from local agricultural polytechnics; implement NABARD-approved animal husbandry practices.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Agriculture/Poultry classification) online at udyamregistration.gov.in; obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Indore for export eligibility; apply for GI tag usage certification from Jhabua District Administration; register FSSAI food business license (₹500 for proprietor, ₹3000-5000 for facility audit); design packaging with GI mark, nutrition facts, and traceability QR code.
5
First Sale
Launch on premium D2C platforms (Licious, FreshCo, LocalCircles) within 6-week turnaround; pitch to Michelin-listed and 5-star restaurants in Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore with sample distribution (₹1-2L for initial supply); list on 1mg Ayurveda and Jiva Health for health-conscious segment; create Instagram/YouTube content on nutrition benefits and GI protection story.
6
Scale
Expand to 5,000 birds production by month 9 using 2-3 contract farmers; establish cold chain and processing unit (FSSAI approved) in Indore for value-added products (marinated packs, meal kits); secure wholesale contracts with QSR chains (Mainland China, Smoke House Deli); initiate export shipments to Singapore/Malaysia via Indore port; build B2B SaaS ordering for restaurant chains.

up to ₹25L grant for poultry farming with 25% subsidy for general category and higher for SC/ST, applicable to established poultry units in Jhabua.

MUDRA Yojana
₹10L collateral-free loan for poultry business under Agriculture sector; repayment flexible, processing via regional banks in Indore.

50% subsidy on poultry shed construction and equipment for GI-protected breeds; processed through Jhabua District Animal Husbandry office.

10% incentive on export value for GI-certified products; support for cold chain and processing facility setup in food parks.
