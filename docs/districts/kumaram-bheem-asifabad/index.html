Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Kumaram Bheem Asifabad.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kumaram Bheem district headquarters and meet cooperative societies like Telangana Millet Farmers Producer Company; study cultivation practices in Adilabad region millet clusters; connect with existing exporters through APEDA network; attend millet summits organized by SFAC
2
Source Raw Materials
Source directly from KMBL district farmers or cooperative societies at ₹35-42/kg for first batch of 10-15 tons (₹3.5-6.3L); establish contract with 3-5 farmer groups for consistent supply; negotiate better rates for bulk orders above 5 tons monthly
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing SFURTI-supported millet processing clusters in Adilabad/Karimnagar for cleaning, sorting, and packaging; alternatively, set up small processing unit with cost of ₹15-25L including machinery (destoner, grader, packaging line) or outsource to co-packers at ₹2-3/kg
4
Brand & Register
Register as Udyam under agriculture processing; obtain IEC code from nearest customs office; get FSSAI license (₹5,000-50,000 depending on capacity); brand registration under your chosen name; obtain organic certification if sourcing from organic farms (₹20,000-40,000 first year)
5
First Sale
Launch on BigBasket, Jaggery, Amazon Fresh, and Bhatinda mandi platforms with ₹500 wholesale packs; run Instagram Reels on health benefits with micro-influencers; create WhatsApp B2B groups targeting bakeries and restaurants; initial target: 500-1000 units/month through 2-3 channels
6
Scale
Expand to 5+ e-commerce platforms; launch own website with subscription model for recurring revenue; develop value-added products (millet granola, millet noodles); establish direct export partnerships through APEDA; achieve ₹50-80L annual revenue by month 18

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram & YouTube content strategy: Post 3x weekly health benefit shorts featuring nutritionists endorsing millet's low-GI properties; partner with 5-10 nano-influencers in fitness/wellness niche for organic reach
  • B2B LinkedIn campaign targeting food manufacturers, bakeries, and corporate wellness programs with case studies on millet usage; offer sample packs and bulk pricing incentives
  • Local district association partnerships: Sponsor millet-awareness drives through APEDA, partner with district agriculture office for promotion at farmer markets, create farm-stay agri-tourism experiences
