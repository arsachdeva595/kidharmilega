ODOP Product

Rice based products

Peddapalli has a strong agricultural base with abundant rice cultivation in the Krishna and Godavari river basins, providing consistent raw material supply. The district has generations of traditional knowledge in rice processing and transformation into value-added products. Local artisan networks and proximity to major southern markets create ideal conditions for scaling rice-based food businesses.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Peddapalli.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Peddapalli city and surrounding villages (Tandur, Luxettipet) to study existing rice processing units. Meet 3-4 established producers to understand seasonal variation, yield per 100kg rice, and local cost structures. Research Karimnagar district's similar operations for benchmarking. Spend 2 weeks understanding moisture levels, storage, and shelf-life management.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source milling-grade rice directly from farmers around Peddapalli (₹2,400-2,800 per 100kg). Establish relationships with 2-3 local rice traders in Luxettipet mandi. For first 500kg batch, allocate ₹1,25,000 for raw materials, ₹50,000 for jaggery/sesame/coconut from wholesale markets in Hyderabad (wholesale only). Secure quality certification by testing samples.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 1-2 micro food processing units already FSSAI-certified in Peddapalli city (contact through district MSME office) for initial contract manufacturing. Cost: ₹8-15 per kg for puffed rice production. Alternatively, set up small in-house facility with 100kg/day capacity roaster and milling equipment. Begin with puffed rice and rice cakes (simpler products) before attempting complex desserts.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME online portal) within 48 hours. Apply for IEC code from DGFT (IEC Centre, Hyderabad) for export readiness. Obtain FSSAI license (non-hazardous category, ₹4,000-6,000). Register trademark with IP India for brand name. Contact Telangana MSME Development Corporation for branding support. Create basic packaging design reflecting Telangana motifs.
5
First Sale
Launch on Blinkit and Zepto (₹5,000 listing fee per SKU) in Hyderabad first, then expand to Tier-2 cities. Simultaneously, reach 50 retail stores in Peddapalli and Karimnagar via direct sales (field team). Create Instagram business account with 2 posts/week (product stories, recipes, behind-the-scenes). Offer 15% introductory discount to first 100 online customers. Target food bloggers in Hyderabad for reviews.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to 2 new SKUs (rice vermicelli, rice flour based products). Increase wholesale network to 200+ retail points across Telangana. Apply for PM Vishwakarma loan for equipment upgrade (₹3L additional capacity). Establish own e-commerce website integrated with Shopify. Begin negotiations with regional supermarket chains (Spencer's, More, Reliance Fresh). Target ₹50L revenue by month 12.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • YouTube series 'From Farm to Crunch' (8-10 min episodes) showing rice-to-product journey from local farms via Peddapalli. Target food YouTube channels with 50K-500K subscribers for sponsored collaboration. Budget: ₹50K-1L per 3-month cycle.
  • Partner with 5-10 regional Instagram/Facebook food influencers (10K-100K followers) in Hyderabad, Bangalore for unboxing and recipe content. Provide ₹2,000-5,000 hamper per influencer. Measure through unique discount codes per influencer.
  • Create WhatsApp community group 'Peddapalli Rice Lovers' with 500+ members; share recipes, wellness content, and exclusive pre-order offers. Monthly contests with ₹500 gift vouchers. Drive group via Blinkit/Zepto links.

Free money you didn't know about

PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L credit + 35% subsidy for manufacturing enterprises; eligible for food processing with Udyam registration
🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹3L collateral-free loan + ₹15K toolkit subsidy for artisans in food processing; specifically supports traditional rice processors

Telangana MSME Development Scheme
₹1L subsidy for branding, packaging, and quality certification for registered food enterprises

10-year stamp duty exemption and 50% property tax waiver for food processing units in rural districts including Peddapalli
