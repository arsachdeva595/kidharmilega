ODOP Product

Millets

Malkangiri's semi-arid terrain, poor soil quality, and erratic rainfall create ideal conditions for millet cultivation without intensive chemical inputs. The district's tribal farming communities have 500+ years of millet-growing knowledge, and the crop's drought resilience provides climate-adaptive livelihood security. Government push toward nutri-cereals positions Malkangiri as a natural hub for organic, sustainable millet production.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Malkangiri.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Malkangiri Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK) and Raipur Agricultural University for crop protocols. Study farmer clusters in Korkonda and Kalimela blocks. Spend 2-3 days with organic farmers in Dhulikhandi. Research NITI Aayog's nutri-cereals roadmap and recent market reports from Technopak.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified millet seeds from Malkangiri District Cooperative, ICAR-ICRISAT Hyderabad (₹1,500-2,000/quintal). For first batch, contract 2-3 farmers for 5-10 quintals (₹7,500-20,000). Buy food-grade HDPE pouches from Bhubaneswar suppliers (₹12,000-15,000 for 10,000 units). Total first-batch cost: ₹25,000-50,000.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 5-8 certified organic farmers in Korkonda block via written procurement agreements. Arrange drying facilities at village level or partner with existing Odisha Millet Processing clusters. Use hired labour for cleaning/grading (₹500-800/day). Consider outsourcing to SFURTI-supported millet processing units in Odisha for value-added products.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam certificate via udyamregistration.gov.in (free, 5 min online). Apply for IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Bhubaneswar (₹1,000 processing fee). Trademark brand name via ipindia.gov.in (₹4,500). Get organic certification from NPOP-accredited body (₹8,000-15,000). Register FSSAI license for food processing (₹100-500).
5
First Sale
Launch on Nature's Basket, Blinkit, and Jio Mart (approach vendor managers with samples). Create Instagram/Facebook presence with farm-to-table storytelling and farmer testimonials. Pitch to corporate B2B buyers via LinkedIn. Approach local organic stores in Bangalore, Delhi, Mumbai. List on Amazon Fresh. Target export enquiries via TradeKey and Global Sources. First target: 500 units/month at ₹120-150/kg D2C price.
6
Scale
Month 6+: Introduce 3-4 value-added SKUs (millet snacks, instant upma mix). Expand farmer base to 15-20 farmers for 50+ quintal annual supply. Hire dedicated supply chain and digital marketing manager. Pursue B2B contracts with HUL, ITC, Nestlé for co-branded nutri-products. Apply for PMEGP expansion grant. Target 5x revenue by month 18.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • YouTube channel 'Malkangiri Farmer Stories' — 2-min bio-docs of partner farmers, millet health benefits, traditional recipes. Target 50K subs in 6 months via YouTube Shorts.
  • Partner with 20+ Instagram wellness influencers (50K-200K follower micro-influencers) for unboxing + recipe content. Offer 20% affiliate commission. Budget: ₹2-3 lakhs/quarter.
  • Sponsor 5-6 organic/health expos in metros (Delhi, Bangalore, Mumbai) in year 1. Set up sampling booth, collect 2,000+ email leads per event. Follow up via email marketing.
