ODOP Product

Honey

South West Khasi Hills' dense forests and diverse flora create ideal conditions for wild and semi-managed beekeeping. The region's clean air, minimal industrial pollution, and endemic wildflower species produce honey with unique organoleptic properties. Local tribal communities have centuries of beekeeping tradition, ensuring authentic production knowledge.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for South West Khasi Hills.

1
Learn / Research
Visit established honey clusters in Jaintia Hills and Ri-Bhoi district; connect with Meghalaya Beekeepers Association; study honey grading standards at APEDA New Delhi; analyze competitor pricing on JustHoney, Honey and Beyond platforms; visit ICAR-ATARI Umiam research station.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source 10-20 healthy bee colonies from registered apiaries in Cherrapunji or Sohra regions; negotiate ₹3,000-5,000 per colony; establish relationships with local beekeepers for future supply; initial batch cost ₹80,000-150,000 for setup and first extraction.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 5-10 traditional beekeepers in South West Khasi Hills under contract farming model; provide modern extraction equipment (centrifuge ₹60,000-80,000); implement quality control testing; aim for 500-1,000 kg first production (6-8 months); register facility for FSSAI compliance.
4
Brand & Register
Register as proprietorship/partnership under Udyam (free online); apply for FSSAI license (₹5,000-10,000); obtain IEC code from DGFT Mumbai; register trademark with IPO (₹4,500-9,000); design packaging with nutrition facts and GI-aspiration messaging; certify organic if possible (₹20,000-30,000).
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh and Flipkart's Super Stores with aggressive first-month pricing; create WhatsApp business catalog for local B2B; approach 20-30 premium organic retailers in Shillong, Delhi, Bangalore; submit samples to 5 food magazines for reviews; run Facebook/Instagram micro-campaigns targeting health-conscious demographics.
6
Scale
After 6 months, develop honey blends (turmeric honey, ginger honey); launch value-added products; establish export channel via APEDA-registered exporters; open direct farm-store in Shillong; build B2B corporate gifting program; pursue GI registration for marketing advantage.
