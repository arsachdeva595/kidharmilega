Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Tapi.

1
Learn / Research
Visit rice clusters in Dharmaj, Surat and processing units in Tapi taluka; attend rice trade shows at Erode Rice Traders Association; study competitor products on Amazon India and export certifications required for target countries.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source premium long-grain paddy directly from farmers in Navapur, Uchchhal talukas during harvest (Oct-Dec) at ₹2,200-2,400/quintal; establish relationships with 3-4 farmer groups; budget ₹4-6 lakhs for first 50-ton batch with milling and processing.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established rice mills in Tapi district (e.g., around Vyara taluka) for milling, grading, and bagging; or invest in small-scale processing unit (15-25 TPD capacity) with FSSAI-approved facility setup in Navapur industrial area.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online via udyamregistration.gov.in), obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Surat, register FSSAI license (₹5,000-15,000), trademark brand name via IP India, and get ISO 22000 certification (₹30,000-50,000) for export readiness.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon FBA with minimum 500 units SKU; list on BigBasket and Blinkit with direct vendor partnership; target first 100 retail accounts in Gujarat via direct sales; reach out to 10-15 HORECA chains in Surat-Vapi belt with samples and bulk pricing.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to 5-7 SKUs (flavored rice, rice mixes); secure APEDA certification and begin container exports to USA/UK; establish D2C subscription model; open 20+ modern retail touchpoints across Gujarat and Maharashtra.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram & YouTube Reels showcasing farm-to-table journey from Tapi farmers; partner with food/health influencers (500K+ followers) for recipe content and organic reach.
  • Run geo-targeted Google Shopping Ads in tier-1 cities (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore) with focus on keywords 'premium basmati rice' and 'healthy rice India'; allocate ₹2-3 lakhs/month budget.
  • B2B partnerships via LinkedIn outreach to restaurant chains and institutional bulk buyers; host tasting events at 3-4 premium hotels in Surat quarterly; offer 15% bulk discounts for repeat orders.
