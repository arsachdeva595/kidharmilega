Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Gurdaspur.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Gurdaspur foundry cluster (Batala, Gurdaspur city centers) — meet 5-10 existing foundry owners through Gurdaspur Chamber of Commerce; study ISO 8501, ISO 9001, automotive casting standards; analyze competitor catalogs on IndiaStack, TradingKey.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source cast iron and steel scrap from authorized dealers in Ludhiana and Amritsar (₹30-40 per kg bulk); procure bentonite from Rajasthan suppliers; negotiate credit terms with material suppliers — first batch of 10 tons costs ₹4-6 lakhs.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established foundries in Gurdaspur cluster for first 6-12 months under revenue-sharing model; hire 1-2 metallurgists and 4-6 skilled workers; invest in testing equipment (hardness tester, tensile tester) worth ₹8-12 lakhs.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME portal), obtain IEC code from DGFT, certify ISO 9001 and ISO 8501 (₹3-5 lakhs), register trademark through IP India, get fire safety and pollution control approvals from Punjab Pollution Control Board.
5
First Sale
Launch on Udyam marketplace and Indiamart with product catalog; cold-call automotive tier-1 suppliers (Bharat Forge, Sona Comstar contacts); exhibit at IMTEX Bangalore and Autotech India; join Gurdaspur Foundry Association and attend quarterly B2B meets.
6
Scale
Achieve ₹5 Cr annual revenue by month 18-24; invest in CNC machining centers for value addition; pursue export certifications (CE, ABS approval); build direct relationships with 3-5 international distributors; consider capacity expansion to 500+ tons annual production.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Create YouTube technical channel (10-15 videos) showing casting process, quality checks, international certifications — target automotive procurement managers with LinkedIn ads (₹2-3L annual budget).
  • Sponsor Gurdaspur Foundry Association events and industry conferences; distribute glossy catalogs with ISO certificates, metallurgical test reports, and customer testimonials at IMTEX, Autotech India.
  • Partner with trade portals (GlobalTrade, TradeKey, Alibaba) offering bulk discount for export orders; invest ₹5-8L in first year for premium listings and verified seller badges.
