Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Thrissur.

1
Learn / Research
Visit coconut processing clusters in Aluva and Kunnamkulam in Thrissur; connect with Kerala Coconut Development Board and VHAI (Vegetable and Handlooms Export Association); attend monthly trade meets at Cochin Port Trust
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fresh coconuts from KPCL (Kerala Plantation Corporation Limited) approved farmers or local cooperative societies in Thrissur district; approximately ₹25,000-₹35,000 for first 1000 coconuts; establish relationship with 2-3 farmer groups for consistent supply
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established small-scale processing units in Aluva industrial area or invest in cold-press equipment (₹8-₹15 lakhs); hire trained operators from local technical institutes like ICAR-CTCRI; start with outsourced production to test market viability
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam (micro-enterprise, ₹0 cost); obtain Food Safety License from FSSAI (₹5,000-₹10,000, 7-10 days); apply for IEC code from DGFT office in Kochi; register trademark with IP India; get ISO 22000 certification for food safety
5
First Sale
List on Amazon Fresh, BigBasket, Blinkit with ₹2-3 lakh investment; approach organic retailers like Organic Brands, Nature's Basket; contact food export agencies in Cochin; pitch to established coconut oil brands as white-label supplier; launch Instagram & Facebook with recipe & health content
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand product range to coconut milk powder and desiccated coconut; establish export partnerships with UK and Germany distributors; negotiate bulk orders with cosmetic brands (body oils, hair care); open D2C website with subscription model; collaborate with Ayurvedic & wellness influencers

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Partner with 10-15 micro-influencers (50K-200K followers) in wellness & organic lifestyle on Instagram; provide free product + commission on sales; target audience aged 25-45 with health consciousness
  • Run Google Shopping Ads targeting high-intent keywords like 'virgin coconut oil India organic' and 'desiccated coconut bulk buy' with 15% discount for first 500 customers
  • Sponsor Kerala Food Festival booths in metros (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore) twice yearly; offer product sampling and direct-to-consumer sales; build email list for recurring customers
