Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Manendragarh- Chirmiri- Bharatpur.

1
Learn / Research
Visit tomato farming clusters in Manendragarh and Chirmiri blocks; study production methods at local farmer cooperative offices; analyze processing at Durg-based food processing units; connect with Chhattisgarh Rajya Beej Nigam for seed and agronomy data
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fresh tomatoes directly from farmer networks in Manendragarh-Chirmiri belt (₹1.5-2.5 per kg seasonal rate); establish relationships with 3-4 farmer groups; first batch (5-10 quintals) costs approximately ₹75,000-₹1,50,000 depending on season
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 established farmers for contract farming; rent 500 sq ft space in Manendragarh town for basic processing (washing, cutting, drying); outsource paste manufacturing to SFURTI-supported tomato processing clusters in Chhattisgarh or start with solar dehydration units
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME classification); obtain IEC code from Raipur customs; acquire FSSAI license (₹5,000-₹15,000); design packaging with Chhattisgarh ODOP labeling; register trademark for brand name at IP office
5
First Sale
Launch on Bhagirath (Chhattisgarh e-commerce), Jio Mart, Big Basket; establish direct B2B relationships with 15-20 local restaurants and 5-8 food manufacturers; participate in Manendragarh district haat bazaars and farmer markets; build WhatsApp-based orders from nearby towns
6
Scale
Expand farmer network to 10+ suppliers; establish processing unit with paste/powder production capacity of 500 kg/day; sell through major retail chains (DMart, Spencer's); develop 2-3 SKUs (paste, sauce, powder); enter institutional procurement (schools, colleges, NGOs)

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Create farm-to-table storytelling content on Instagram and YouTube showing harvesting, processing, and family recipes using the tomatoes; partner with 5-8 food bloggers from Chhattisgarh and Central India
  • Sponsor local agricultural fairs, farm expos, and farmer meetings in Raipur, Durg, and Manendragarh; distribute free samples and recipe cards at these venues to build brand awareness
  • Launch referral program offering 10% discount for restaurant and B2B customers who refer new bulk buyers; create WhatsApp broadcast group for seasonal offers and product updates to 500+ local customers
