ODOP Product

Furniture

Maharajganj has a centuries-old tradition of wood-based handicrafts, with concentrated artisan clusters in towns like Jalalpur and Koraon. The region's proximity to timber suppliers in Nepal and availability of skilled labor has made it a natural hub for handcrafted furniture. Strong local demand from residential and commercial sectors across North India provides immediate market access.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Maharajganj.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Jalalpur and Koraon furniture clusters in Maharajganj; spend 2-3 weeks with 3-4 master craftsmen to understand wood types, design process, and market trends. Connect with Maharajganj Handcrafts Association. Research competitor pricing on Etsy, Alibaba, and local Mumbai furniture markets.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source Sheesham and Sal wood from timber dealers in Gorakhpur (50 km away) or directly from Nepal border suppliers. Initial batch: ₹150,000-₹250,000 for 50-80 pieces (mix of chairs, tables, small items). Negotiate 15-20% discount for bulk orders. Establish credit relationship with 2-3 suppliers.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 established artisan groups in Jalalpur (3-5 craftsmen per group) on contract basis; provide designs and materials, pay ₹300-500 per day per artisan. Start with 20-30 pieces monthly. Alternatively, establish micro-workshop with 2-3 trained carpenters (capital: ₹600,000-800,000 for tools, workspace, materials storage).
4
Brand & Register
Register as Proprietorship or Partnership under Udyam (MSME), apply for IEC (Importer Exporter Code) if targeting exports. Create brand identity: logo, website on Shopify/WordPress (₹15,000-30,000 annually). Register trademark with IP office (₹5,000-10,000). Print catalogs with photography (₹20,000).
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy (global reach, 5% commission), Amazon seller account (18-25% commission), and WhatsApp catalog with 50-100 high-quality product images. Reach out to 20-30 local interior designers, hotels, and corporate offices with product samples and pricing sheets. Create Instagram profile with behind-the-scenes artisan content (3-4 posts/week). Run Google Search Ads targeting 'handcrafted furniture' keywords (₹500-1,000/month budget).
6
Scale
After 6 months, hire a dedicated salesperson to manage B2B relationships (salary ₹12,000-15,000/month). Expand product range by introducing new designs (beds, cupboards, decorative items). Start batch exporting to 2-3 countries via freight forwarders; target 10-15% of revenue from exports by month 12. Invest ₹100,000-150,000 in professional product photography and video for digital marketing. Partner with 1-2 logistics companies for faster delivery to pan-India customers.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Partner with 10-15 interior designers across Delhi, Lucknow, and Varanasi; offer 20% wholesale discount + co-branded Instagram content featuring their projects. Target: 5-8 bulk orders per month at ₹50,000-100,000 each.
  • Create YouTube channel documenting entire artisan process (wood selection to finishing); publish 2 videos/month highlighting craftsmanship. Target: 10,000+ subscribers in 6 months, leading to 15-20% D2C traffic boost.
  • Run Pinterest Ads targeting 'handcrafted wooden furniture' and 'eco-friendly home decor' with rich product pins linking to Etsy shop. Monthly budget: ₹5,000-8,000. Target: 500+ monthly shop visits.
