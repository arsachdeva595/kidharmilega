The district's agricultural biodiversity and existing herbal processing clusters provide ready access to raw materials and skilled labor for manufacturing.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Shivamogga (Shimoga).

1
Learn / Research
Visit Shivamogga's herbal clusters in Hosanagar taluk and connect with IIIM (Indian Institute of Integrative Medicine) partnerships; study existing players like Himalaya Herbal and Patanjali's supply chains; attend Bio-India expo or Pharmexcil trade shows
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from Shivamogga's organic farmers network (₹8-12L for first 6-month raw material batch) or from Hosanagar spice and herb market; verify APEDA certification for export-quality supplies
3
Produce / Partner
Establish GMP-certified manufacturing unit (₹35-50L investment) or partner with existing FSSAI/GMP-certified co-manufacturers in Bangalore's pharma corridor; employ Ayurvedic formulation expert as consultant
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSE status online; obtain FSSAI license (₹5,000-8,000); apply for DCGI registration for Ayurvedic products; file trademark with IPO; obtain IEC code from DGFT for export readiness
5
First Sale
Launch flagship 3-5 SKU range on Nykaa, Amazon Ayurveda, Ajio, and Facebook/Instagram; partner with 10-15 wellness clinics in Bangalore, Pune, Mumbai for pilot B2B sales; engage wellness influencers (30K-100K followers) for authentic reviews
6
Scale
Expand product line to 15+ SKUs based on sales data; establish corporate wellness partnerships with 5-10 companies; initiate export via Pharmexcil/APEDA networks; open pop-up stores in metro wellness hubs; launch subscription box model for recurring revenue

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Partner with 15-20 Ayurvedic practitioners and wellness coaches on Instagram/YouTube (reel-based content on seasonal detox, stress-relief rituals) using micro-influencer affiliate model (15-20% commission)
  • Launch 'Forests of Shimoga' content series on LinkedIn/TikTok featuring raw material sourcing stories, farmer partnerships, and herbal extraction processes to build brand credibility and B2B trust
  • Create corporate wellness sampling campaigns targeting HR departments in Bangalore tech companies; offer bulk discounts + free wellness workshops to drive repeat B2B orders
