ODOP Product

Rice

Dhamtari's alluvial plains and moderate climate create ideal conditions for rice cultivation with natural sweetness and aroma. The region receives consistent monsoon rainfall (1,200-1,400 mm annually) and has rich black soil with high organic content, enabling high-quality rice production. Strategic location in Chhattisgarh's rice belt ensures reliable supply and farming expertise across multiple rice varieties.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Dhamtari.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Dhamtari grain markets (Rice Mandi, Kurud Agri Market) and meet 5-10 progressive farmers. Study milling units in Dhamtari city. Connect with Chhattisgarh State Farmers' Federation to understand production cycles, pricing, and certification requirements.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source rice directly from 10-15 Dhamtari farmers at ₹18-22 per kg (harvest season). Partner with 1-2 local rice mills for cleaning/polishing at ₹1.5-2 per kg. First batch of 10 MT costs ~₹2.2-2.5 Lakhs. Arrange storage via cold storage facility in Dhamtari or Rajnandgaon.
3
Produce / Partner
Register with 3-4 rice mills in Dhamtari (Kurud has 12+ mills) for milling, grading, and packaging. Negotiate bulk rates for 10+ MT monthly orders. Source BIS-approved polypropylene bags (500g-10kg) from suppliers in Raipur. Consider forming FMCG partnership model with cooperatives like Dhamtari District Cooperative Marketing Federation.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, 1-2 days at udyamregistration.gov.in). Apply for IEC from local DGFT office in Raipur. Register brand name with IP India. Obtain Food Safety License (FSSAI) from Dhamtari Health Department (₹2,000-5,000). Get BIS certification for rice grading (IS 1121). Allow 30-45 days for full registration.
5
First Sale
Launch D2C on JioMart, Flipkart, BigBasket with ₹5-8/kg margin markup. Approach 20-30 premium grocery retailers (Nature's Basket, local organic stores) in Raipur, Bilaspur. Email 15-20 bulk buyers (hotels, schools). Set up Amazon Seller account, list at ₹45-55 per kg for 500g packs. Target first sale within 45 days via retailer partnerships.
6
Scale
Expand supply to 30-50 MT monthly by month 7-8. Add 2-3 complementary products (pulses, wheat, millet). Establish export channel via customs broker in Kolkata or Chennai. Launch branded B2B subscription model. Expand to 5-7 neighboring states via regional distributors. Target ₹1-1.5 Cr revenue by year 2.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram Reels showing farm-to-table journey: farmer harvesting → milling → packaging. Tag food influencers in Raipur, Bilaspur. Post 2x weekly, cost ₹5K/month for influencer partnerships.
  • Collaborate with 5-10 wellness cafes and organic stores in Raipur for in-store demos. Offer 20% introductory discount. Generate 50-100 repeat customers per store. Cost: ₹30K/month for co-marketing.
  • Email B2B campaign targeting 500 restaurants, hotels, institutional caterers. Case study: how Dhamtari rice improves dish taste. Lead with sample pack + competitive per-kg pricing. Target 5-10 B2B accounts, each ordering 500-1000kg/month.
