Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Uttarkashi.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Uttarkashi's agricultural clusters in Rudraprayag and Chopta areas; engage with 8-10 established red rice farming cooperatives; study GI-tagged product standards at District Agriculture Office, Uttarkashi; research 3-5 successful red rice brands (e.g., Aastha Organics, Uttarkashi Red Rice Co.).
2
Source Raw Materials
Connect with registered red rice farmers through Uttarkashi Agricultural Produce Marketing Committee (APMC); source first batch (500 kg minimum) at ₹80-100 per kg raw rice; packaging materials from Delhi/Panipat suppliers at ₹8-12 per unit for 500g packs. First batch total: ₹70,000-80,000.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 certified milling units near Uttarkashi town (Bhatwari or Uttarkashi main market) for processing and packaging; arrange FSSAI-certified packaging facility (can subcontract with existing Uttarkashi food processors); maintain organic certification chain for GI compliance.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Micro/Small classification); apply for IEC code from APEDA (Agricultural & Processed Food Products Export Development Authority); obtain FSSAI license (₹2,000-5,000); file GI usage registration with local ODOP cell; design packaging highlighting GI certification and nutritional benefits.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon, Flipkart, BigBasket (health/organic category) with focus on SEO keywords 'Uttarkashi red rice + health benefits'; create Instagram/YouTube content around Himalayan sourcing story and wellness benefits; reach out to 20-30 organic/natural food retailers in metro cities (NCR, Mumbai, Bangalore); list on Jio Mart organic section.
6
Scale
Expand product line (red rice flour, roasted snacks); build direct-to-consumer email list and retention program (target 30% repeat customers by month 12); establish wholesale partnerships with 50+ retail chains; explore APEDA export schemes; launch subscription boxes; open brand experience center in Dehradun (gateway market).

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram Reels series — '72-hour farm-to-table journey' showing Uttarkashi farmers harvesting, milling, and packaging; partner with 5-7 micro-influencers (50K-200K followers) in wellness/health niches for authentic product reviews.
  • Content marketing via blog posts and long-form YouTube videos on '10 Health Benefits of Red Rice' + 'Why Himalayan Red Rice Beats Asian Varieties' targeting organic food searchers; aim for top-3 Google rankings for 'buy Uttarkashi red rice online'.
  • B2B outreach — cold email 100+ health food retailers, wellness cafes, and corporate wellness programs in NCR with sample packs and wholesale pricing; attend 3-4 natural/organic trade shows annually (India Organic Expo, Natural Products Expo).
