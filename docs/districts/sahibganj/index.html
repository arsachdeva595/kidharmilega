ODOP Product

Jute

Sahibganj's fertile Gangetic plains and riverside location provide ideal agro-climatic conditions for premium jute cultivation. The district has established jute processing infrastructure, skilled artisan communities, and proximity to jute mills in Bengal, making it a natural production and trading hub for jute handicrafts.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Sahibganj.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sahibganj jute clusters around Rajmahal and Tilka Manjhi areas; study the Balurghat-Sahibganj jute belt in West Bengal; attend Kolkata Jute Expo; interview 10+ established jute artisan groups and traders
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw jute fiber from Sahibganj Agricultural Produce Market or West Bengal Jute Mills Association; negotiate first batch (500 kg) at ₹45-55/kg = ₹22,500-27,500; connect with local retting facilities along Ganges tributaries
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with registered artisan SHGs in Sahibganj (e.g., Sahibganj Jute Weavers Cooperative); establish wage structure at ₹200-300/day; start with 50-100 artisans; invest in basic quality control workspace in Sahibganj town
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME under handicraft category within 48 hours online; apply for IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Ranchi; register brand name with IP India; get Handicraft Mark certification from Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Saheli, Flipkart Samarth, and IndiaArtCollective within 30 days; contact 50+ hotel and restaurant chains in Delhi-NCR with direct samples; list on Global Artisans Platform; pitch to Fab India and Ananda retail networks
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand artisan network to 300+ workers; launch B2B SaaS order management system; open direct sales office in Kolkata; establish export compliance team for 10+ country shipping; hire quality manager and production controller
