Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Raigad.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sassoon Dock (Mumbai, 40km away), Ratnagiri fish market, and Malad Fishing Cooperative to understand catch cycles, pricing, and trader networks. Attend Maharashtra Fisheries Department workshops at District Fisheries Office, Raigad.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fresh catch directly from 5-7 registered fishing cooperatives in Alibaug, Murud, and Shrivardhan (first batch ₹1.5-2.5L for 500kg mixed stock). Establish credit relationship with 2-3 ice suppliers (₹30K/month cold storage rental).
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing fish processors in Raigad via Khadi Udyog or NAFED clusters; or set up own cold-chain facility (FSSAI Grade B license) with ₹20-25L investment in flash-freezing and vacuum-packing equipment in Alibaug or Pen.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME, ≤₹1Cr turnover), obtain IEC code via DGFT for export, get FSSAI license (₹10K, 3-4 weeks), obtain Fishing License from District Authority. Brand as '_____ Raigad Seafood' with coastal/sustainability messaging.
5
First Sale
Launch on Blinkit/Zepto with ₹2L minimum order guarantee; list on B2B platforms (Udaan, IndiaStack); pitch to 20+ 4-5 star hotels via direct sales. Use WhatsApp Business for B2B and D2C orders; target Mumbai suburbs (Powai, Bandra, Andheri) with delivery in 24 hours.
6
Scale
Build own cold-storage depot in Navi Mumbai (₹40-50L); hire sales team for institutional clients; expand to 8-10 coastal villages for direct catch procurement; launch 2-3 value-added SKUs (fish fillets, prawns) after 6 months; secure export contract with Belgium/Netherlands importers.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Run Instagram Reels showing live fishing at sunrise in Alibaug, behind-the-scenes cold-chain processing, and chef partnerships with Mumbai restaurants (target food bloggers, 50K-500K followers).
  • Partner with 5-star hotel chefs to create co-branded 'Raigad Special' menu items; feature in hotel newsletters and award submissions (e.g., Michelin Guide prep).
  • Launch WhatsApp catalog with daily catch updates and pricing; offer first 50 customers 25% off via referral codes; run monthly 'Know Your Fisherman' stories featuring boat captains from Raigad.
