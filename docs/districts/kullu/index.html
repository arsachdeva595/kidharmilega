ODOP Product

Kullu Shawl

Kullu shawls have been woven in the Kullu valley for centuries using wool from local sheep. The distinctive geometric patterns and natural wool make them uniquely suited to the cold mountain climate and growing global demand for sustainable fashion.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps specific to Kullu and Kullu Shawl.

1
Learn / Research
Stay in Kullu valley for 2 months — understand the weaving communities
2
Source Raw Materials
Source local Kullu wool from shepherds directly — very accessible
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with weaver families in Naggar and Raison villages
4
Brand & Register
Build Instagram around the mountain lifestyle and sustainable story
5
First Sale
Sell at Dilli Haat and Kullu Dussehra fair (massive footfall)
6
Scale
Export to sustainable fashion buyers on Etsy and Faire
