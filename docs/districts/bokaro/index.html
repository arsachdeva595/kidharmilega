Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Bokaro.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bokaro Handicrafts Cooperative Society clusters in Bokaro city and Chas block. Study designs at Jamshedpur's furniture showrooms. Interact with 5-7 established woodcraft producers to understand material sourcing, pricing, and market dynamics.
2
Source Raw Materials
Partner with timber suppliers in Bokaro Forest Division and registered mills in Tatanagar (adjacent Jamshedpur). First batch raw material cost: ₹40,000-60,000 for 20-30 mid-range pieces. Build relationships with 2-3 suppliers for steady access to Sal and Sheesham wood.
3
Produce / Partner
Connect with 8-12 registered artisans through Bokaro District Handicrafts Association. Begin with 60% outsourced production, 40% in-house quality control workshop. Invest ₹2-3 lakhs for basic workshop setup (hand tools, finishing station, storage). Train artisans in your design specifications within 2-3 weeks.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam within 7 days (udyamregistration.gov.in). Apply for IEC code via DGFT portal for export readiness. Trademark your brand name with IP India. Get Handicraft Artisan certificate from District Handicrafts Office. ISO 9001 certification optional but valuable for premium positioning.
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy, IndianArtCo, and Craftroots within month 1. Set up Instagram and Pinterest with high-quality product photography. Reach out to 20 interior design studios in Ranchi and Jamshedpur with custom quotes. Partner with 2-3 premium home décor boutiques in metro cities for consignment sales.
6
Scale
After 6 months, hire 2-3 full-time product designers. Expand to corporate gifting partnerships with IT companies in Bangalore/Pune. Launch B2B portal for bulk orders. Establish retail presence in 5-7 metro cities. Invest in automated finishing equipment (₹5-8 lakhs) to increase production capacity 3x.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Partner with 10-15 Instagram micro-influencers (50K-200K followers) in home décor niche for monthly product features and stories. Budget: ₹8-12K per influencer per month.
  • Launch YouTube channel with 2 videos/week showing artisan-at-work, design process, and sustainability practices. Target keywords: 'handmade wooden furniture India', 'eco-friendly home décor'. Aim for 5K subscribers by month 6.
  • Create email nurture sequence for interior designers and architects via LinkedIn outreach. Offer 15% B2B discount + free custom design consultation for first 50 qualified leads.
