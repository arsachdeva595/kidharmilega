ODOP Product

Cashew Nut

South Tripura's tropical climate, abundant rainfall, and fertile alluvial soil create ideal conditions for high-yield, large-kernel cashew cultivation. The region's 150+ years of cashew farming expertise combined with GI certification ensures consistent quality and premium market positioning. Sustainable farming practices and low chemical inputs align with global organic demand trends.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for South Tripura.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sonamura and Udaipur cashew clusters in South Tripura; meet 5-10 established cashew processors; attend APEDA trade shows; study GI certification standards; connect with Ministry of Agriculture extension centers
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw cashew from South Tripura Farmer Producer Organization (FPO) or direct farms near Sonamura; first batch of 5 tons (~₹15-20L) available Aug-Nov harvest; negotiate 8-12% discount for bulk orders
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with certified processing units in Udaipur industrial cluster (wet cashew processing outsourced at ₹8-12/nut) OR establish own small roastery (₹20-30L capex for 2-ton/day capacity); ensure FSSAI/Agmark certification within 2 months
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) within 1 week online; obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office (Agartala); file GI label usage with Controller of Patents (₹50K); design packaging with GI logo; register trademark with IPO (₹4-5K)
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon/Flipkart with 500g premium pack at ₹450-550; create Instagram/YouTube content on farming story and GI certification; pitch B2B buyers via IndiaMART at ₹280-320/kg wholesale; send samples to 20 premium restaurant chains in Tier-1 cities
6
Scale
Expand to 15-20 ton monthly production by 6-month mark; establish direct export contracts via APEDA (target 50+ shipments/year); develop 3-4 new variants (salted, roasted, flavored); build farmer network of 200+ suppliers; hire export-import consultant for RCEP/bilateral trade opportunities
