ODOP Product

Sandstone products

Karauli district sits atop rich deposits of high-quality red and buff sandstone, ideal for carving fine details. The region has a 5-century-old tradition of stone carving passed down through generations of artisan families, with clusters around Karauli town and Hindaun. Access to Agra and Delhi markets, combined with a skilled labor pool, makes this the natural hub for premium sandstone products.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Karauli.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Karauli town and Hindaun block; spend 1 week with established artisan families (contact through District Industries Centre Karauli) to understand raw material quality, carving techniques, and local pricing. Attend the annual Karauli Sandstone Artisan Meet (usually Feb-Mar). Study competitor portfolios on Etsy, Wayfair, and 1stDibs to understand international price points.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source sandstone blocks directly from quarries around Karauli town (₹15,000-40,000 per ton depending on quality/color) or through established stone suppliers like Karauli Stone Enterprises. For first batch: ₹2-3L for 5-8 tons of mixed quality sandstone, plus ₹1L for hand tools and chisels. Negotiate payment terms with suppliers for 15-30 days net.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3-5 established artisans in Karauli/Hindaun clusters on commission-per-piece basis (₹500-2,000 per small piece, ₹5,000-30,000 for larger sculptures). Alternatively, invest in a 500-800 sq ft workshop with hand-carving stations. Register as a Manufacturing MSE under Udyam and establish a formal artisan employment or subcontracting agreement.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (udyamregistration.gov.in) and apply for IEC code (through Customs) for export eligibility. File for trademark with brand name at IPO. Create a 'Heritage of Karauli' positioning with storytelling around artisan bios. Obtain state certificate of origin from Rajasthan MSME directorate. Apply for FSSAI if including any food-contact items.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Handmade and Etsy within 3 months (10-15 SKUs, ₹3,000-50,000 price range). Approach luxury interior designers via LinkedIn and email outreach with portfolio PDF. Pitch to 5-star hotels, resorts, and event venues in Delhi-NCR and Jaipur. Create Instagram content featuring artisan-at-work videos and customer home installations. Offer ₹5,000-10,000 launch discount on first 20 orders.
6
Scale
After 6 months, if achieving ₹5-8L/month revenue, expand to Flipkart, 1stDibs, and Wayfair. Hire a dedicated export sales person to target US/UK distributors and architects. Establish a showroom in Jaipur (₹1.5-2L rent + setup) or Delhi NCR. Launch a heritage tour experience (artisan workshop visits + product purchases) as a premium revenue line. Scale artisan network to 10-15 skilled carvers with formal SKU standardization.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Create a 'Meet the Artisan' LinkedIn and Instagram series showcasing 1 featured artisan per month with carving time-lapse videos, personal story, and a 15% discount code for followers — drives brand loyalty and social proof among affluent B2C and B2B audiences.
  • Partner with 5-10 luxury interior design influencers (50K-200K Instagram followers) for styled home photoshoots featuring Karauli pieces; offer 30% commission on resulting sales — reach high-intent affluent homeowners.
  • Run a targeted Google Shopping campaign for 'handcrafted sandstone garden statues', 'carved stone home decor' with 8-12% ROAS target; focus on US, UK, AUS; test Conversion API on Shopify for pixel optimization.

PM Vishwakarma
