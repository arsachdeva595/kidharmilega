Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Begusarai.

1
Learn / Research
Visit chilli markets in Begusarai town and Barauni during harvest season; meet 5+ established chilli traders at the Begusarai Agricultural Produce Market; study varieties (Bhut Jolokia, Bird's Eye, Byadgi style) and pricing at local mandis
2
Source Raw Materials
Source seeds from Bihar Rajya Seed Corporation or local farmer cooperatives; first-batch procurement of 500kg dried chillies costs ₹75,000-₹150,000 depending on variety; partner with 3-5 farmers in Matihani or Bahua blocks for consistent supply
3
Produce / Partner
Either lease 2-5 acres for cultivation (₹20,000/acre annually) or partner with existing farmer groups through contract farming; invest in basic drying infrastructure (bamboo beds, shade structures) costing ₹150,000-₹300,000 for small-scale operation
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) online at udyamregistration.gov.in within 2 days; apply for IEC code via DGFT portal for export eligibility; register brand name with State Trademark Office; obtain FSSAI license for packaged products (₹10,000-₹20,000)
5
First Sale
List on Alibaba, TradeKey, and Global Sources for B2B exports; partner with 2-3 local spice mills through direct meetings; use WhatsApp Business to reach 50+ HORECA buyers; start with 100kg orders on Amazon Business and Udaan platforms
6
Scale
After 6 months, invest ₹500,000-₹800,000 in semi-mechanized processing unit; build relationships with 3+ export houses for bulk orders; launch branded retail line on Flipkart, Blinkit, and local kirana networks; expand to adjacent districts for sourcing
