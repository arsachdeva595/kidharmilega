Chemical Products

Valsad is strategically positioned in Gujarat's chemical corridor with established petrochemical infrastructure, proximity to major ports (Hazira, Dahej), skilled chemical workforce, and existing clusters of chemistry-based industries. The district benefits from strong backward linkages in raw material supply chains and forward integration with end-user industries across pharmaceuticals, textiles, and manufacturing.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Valsad.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Valsad's established chemical clusters around Hazira and Dahej industrial areas; meet 3-5 existing chemical manufacturers to understand production processes, regulatory requirements, and market demand. Study Valsad Chemexcil directory and attend CHEMTECH/ICHEMF exhibitions for market insights.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from Hazira petrochemical suppliers (GAIL, Reliance downstream suppliers) and chemical traders in Vapi, Mumbai. Budget ₹15-25 lakh for first batch of specialty chemicals including base compounds, additives, catalysts. Negotiate credit terms with established suppliers.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 2000-3000 sqft FMEG space in Vapi-Valsad industrial zone (₹8-12/sqft monthly); install batch reactors, distillation units, quality testing lab. Hire experienced chemical process engineers (₹4-6L annually) and technicians. Start with contract manufacturing partnerships with larger firms before scaling independent production.
4
Brand & Register
Register as manufacturing enterprise under Udyam (MSME classification); obtain IEC code from nearest Customs office (Surat/Hazira); secure FSSAI registration if producing food-grade chemicals; file for ISO 9001/14001 certifications. Name: ChemTech/ValChem/SpecialtyIndia. Set up basic website with product specs and technical data sheets.
5
First Sale
Launch B2B outreach to pharma companies in Vapi-Valsad corridor and Ahmedabad; register on IndiaMART, Alibaba, TradeKey for export inquiries. Approach chemical distributors in Mumbai, Delhi, Chennai. Attend CPhI India and ChemTech exhibitions in Mumbai/Hyderabad. Secure first contracts with established distributors or small pharma manufacturers.
6
Scale
After 6 months, secure bank financing against purchase orders (via MUDRA); invest in automated production lines and quality lab upgrades; hire sales team for regional expansion across India; establish export network through chemical trading companies; develop 2-3 proprietary formulations for differentiation.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Create detailed technical content (whitepapers, case studies, TDS sheets) and distribute via LinkedIn to pharma/textile procurement teams; build credibility through industry publications
  • Partner with 3-5 chemical distributors as exclusive regional suppliers and co-market through their networks; offer volume discounts and flexible payment terms
  • Attend 2-3 annual B2B trade shows (CPhI India, ChemTech Expo) with product samples and technical team; network with procurement heads from large industrial houses
