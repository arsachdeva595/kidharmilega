Granite Slabs

Warangal Urban lies in the Deccan Plateau with abundant granite quarries and natural stone deposits, coupled with a skilled workforce trained in stone cutting and polishing. The district's proximity to major metros (Hyderabad, Bangalore) and existing infrastructure for logistics make it ideal for large-scale granite slab production and export.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Warangal Urban (Hanumakonda).

1
Learn / Research
Visit Warangal's established granite clusters around Hanumakonda and Kazipet; meet at least 5 active manufacturers to understand quarry sourcing, machinery costs, labor models; join Warangal Chamber of Commerce; attend stone expo at Bangalore or Hyderabad to benchmark market pricing and export channels.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw granite blocks from licensed quarries in Warangal district (₹8-12 per sq.ft for rough blocks); secure contracts with 2-3 quarry suppliers for consistent supply; negotiate first batch of 500-1000 sq.ft for ₹15-20 lakhs; establish payment terms with quarries (30-45 days).
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing stone processing units in Hanumakonda for initial production (lease model: ₹1-2L monthly for machinery access + labor) or invest in own diamond saw machinery (₹25-40L); hire experienced cutters and polishers from local labor pool; aim for 200-300 sq.ft daily output initially.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam as MSME (manufacturing unit, online at udyamregistration.gov.in); obtain IEC code from nearest APEDA office (Hyderabad) for export eligibility; register brand name with IP; obtain ISO 9001 certification (₹2-3L) and HSN codes for GST compliance; get mill certificate from Telangana geology department.
5
First Sale
List on Indiamart, TradeKey, and Global Sources for B2B buyers; contact 20 local architects and real estate developers with price quotes and samples; reach out to interior design studios in Hyderabad and Bangalore; join platforms like Alibaba for international inquiry; attend trade shows like India Build Summit; use WhatsApp for direct outreach to construction firms.
6
Scale
After ₹50L revenue milestone, invest in 2nd production line to increase capacity; build direct relationships with 3-5 large architecture firms for repeat orders; hire a dedicated export manager to chase FIEO and APEDA schemes; explore Telangana IIFC clusters for shared facility expansion; launch website with project portfolio and testimonials.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Create Instagram/LinkedIn portfolio account showcasing before-after project transformations using Warangal granite in luxury homes and commercial spaces; partner with 3 micro-influencers from design/architecture space for sponsored posts (₹10-20K per month).
  • Sponsor architecture student competitions and design contests in Hyderabad/Bangalore; offer free sample slabs to winning designers; capture leads through post-event follow-up; build long-term relationships with emerging design studios.
  • Run Google Ads and YouTube campaigns targeting keywords like 'premium granite slabs India export', 'granite countertops wholesale', 'stone supplier architects'; allocate ₹30-50K/month initially to capture high-intent B2B searches.
