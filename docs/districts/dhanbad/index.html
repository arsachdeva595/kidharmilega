Dhanbad is the epicenter of India's coal mining industry with reserves exceeding 3 billion tonnes. The district has 70+ operational coal mines, established mining infrastructure, skilled workforce, and proximity to major power plants and industrial consumers. Centuries of mining expertise make it the ideal location for coal extraction and value-added coal products.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Dhanbad.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Dhanbad mining clusters (Bhelatand, Nirsa, Jharia coalfield) to understand extraction, quality standards, and buyer networks. Study Coal India Limited's operations and connect with mining contractors and coal merchants in Dhanbad town market.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from established coal mines through mining contractors or acquire mining leases (requires ₹2-5 Cr upfront). Connect with Dhanbad coal merchants for wholesale stock. First-batch sourcing: ₹5-10 Cr for 1000-2000 tonnes quality coal.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing coal mines for direct extraction or establish a coal processing unit (washing, crushing, sizing). Hire mining engineers and production managers. Invest in processing equipment (washery, screens, crushers) costing ₹3-8 Cr.
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam (MSME portal), obtain IEC code from DGFT for exports, secure mining lease/permission from Jharkhand Mineral Development Corporation, apply for GST registration (5%), and obtain ISO 9001 certification for quality. Brand as regional coal processor/trader.
5
First Sale
Pitch directly to power plants and steel mills with samples and quality reports. Register on National Coal Index platform, join coal trading exchanges (ICCX), and contact industrial buyers via B2B portals like IndiaStack and Tradeindia. Attend mining expos in Delhi and Chennai.
6
Scale
Expand processing capacity, diversify into value-added products (briquettes, activated carbon), establish export agreements with Japanese and Korean importers, invest in logistics fleet, and acquire additional mining leases in Dhanbad-Asansol region.
