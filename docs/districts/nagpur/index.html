Nagpur Orange

Nagpur's unique climate with monsoon rains, black soil, and moderate temperatures create ideal conditions for mandarin cultivation. The district has over 50,000 hectares under orange cultivation with established farmer networks, cold chain infrastructure, and decades of horticultural expertise making it the global benchmark for mandarin quality.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Nagpur.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Nagpur's Sitabuldi Market and Sadar Bazaar to understand orange grading, pricing, and buyer networks. Connect with farmers in Umred, Katol, and Narkhed tehsils. Attend Nagpur Agricultural Produce Market Committee (APMC) auctions to learn real-time pricing dynamics.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fresh oranges directly from Nagpur APMC (minimum lot: 10-15 tons for wholesale). First batch cost: ₹5-8 lakhs for 15 tons depending on season. Establish relationships with Grade-A growers in Sitabuldi cluster for consistent supply and quality certification.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with Nagpur-based cold storage facilities (Prag Cold Storage, Nagpur Fruit Processing cluster) for processing and value addition. Set up minimal processing: grading, sorting, waxing facility (₹20-30 lakhs). Or collaborate with existing APMC-registered processors for contract manufacturing of juices and concentrates.
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam (Agri-based), apply for IEC code from DGFT for export eligibility. Obtain Nagpur Orange GI certification through APMC authentication. Get food safety certification (FSSAI) if processing. Register trademark with brand name at IP India (cost: ₹10,000-15,000).
5
First Sale
List on Jio Mart, Big Basket, Nature's Basket with GI tag. Approach premium hotel chains (Taj, ITC) for bulk supply contracts. Use FreshIndaba and IndiaTradeHub platforms for export buyer connections. Launch Instagram/Facebook with harvest season updates and farm stories to build D2C audience.
6
Scale
After 6 months, add 2-3 value-added SKUs (juice, jam). Establish direct export partnerships through APEDA-registered exporters. Open kiosk in 5-10 premium retail locations in Mumbai, Pune, Bangalore. Launch subscription boxes for tier-1 cities. Pursue organic certification (₹2-3 lakhs) to command 30-40% premium.
