Dehydrated Vegetables (Onion)

Bhavnagar's fertile agricultural plains and cool coastal climate create ideal conditions for onion cultivation with consistent quality. The district has established infrastructure for food processing, skilled labor force, and proximity to major ports (Bhavnagar Port) enabling efficient export operations. Historical expertise in agricultural processing and existing networks of dehydration units position it uniquely for scaled production.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Bhavnagar.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bhavnagar's established dehydrated vegetable clusters around Botad and Mahemdabad talukas; interact with 5-10 existing processors to understand production capacity, yield rates (typically 8:1 fresh to dehydrated ratio), and market pricing. Study the Bhavnagar Agricultural Produce Market for seasonal onion availability and pricing trends.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fresh onions directly from Bhavnagar mandis (Botad Mandi, Mahemdabad) during harvest season (Oct-Dec) at ₹1,500-2,500/quintal. First batch: 50 quintals fresh onions = ₹75,000-125,000 investment. Identify 3-4 reliable suppliers for year-round consistency.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing FSSAI-certified dehydration units in Bhavnagar (e.g., processing clusters near Bhavnagar city) on toll-processing basis initially (₹3-5/kg processing cost) or acquire small-scale dehydrators (cabinet or continuous dryer, ₹2-4L). Target 5-10 MT monthly production by month 3.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, free, 30 mins); obtain IEC Code from nearest DGFT office (Ahmedabad, processed in 7-10 days); register FSSAI license with local authority (₹5,000-8,000); trademark brand name at IP India; set up GST (18% on dehydrated vegetables under 0701).
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Business, Flipkart Wholesale, and IndiaTradeKey simultaneously. Approach regional spice companies (Everest, MDH suppliers) via direct B2B networking. List on TradeKey and Global Sources for international buyers. Attend Gulfood or SIAL food expos for direct export contracts.
6
Scale
After 6 months, secure bulk export orders via APEDA; establish company-owned dehydration facility (₹25-40L investment); hire dedicated export manager; expand to 50+ MT monthly; develop private-label products for major retailers; explore value-addition (onion powder, minced onion blends).
