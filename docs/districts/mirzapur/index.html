ODOP Product

Carpets

Mirzapur has a 400+ year legacy of carpet weaving with concentrated artisan communities possessing hereditary skills in the craft. The district's proximity to raw material suppliers and established export infrastructure make it the carpet capital of India. Local weavers have perfected techniques for wool, silk, and cotton blending that command premium prices globally.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Mirzapur.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Mirzapur carpet clusters in Naktpur and Suhagpur areas; spend 2-4 weeks learning weaving techniques directly from established weavers; connect with Mirzapur Carpet Exporters Association; study GI certification requirements and export logistics.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw wool (₹150-250/kg) and silk (₹400-600/kg) from suppliers on Mirzapur Road; natural dyes from local markets or ISKCON-affiliated suppliers; initial batch of 5-10 carpets (6x4 ft) requires ₹80K-120K investment; establish credit terms with 3-4 primary suppliers.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 established weaver groups or individual master weavers through cooperative model (25-30% commission basis); provide design specifications and quality benchmarks; establish production timeline of 45-60 days per carpet; implement quality control through sampling and inspection.
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam (MSME) for tax and subsidy eligibility; apply for IEC code from Directorate General of Foreign Trade; file GI-certified product label with state textile ministry; register trademark with IP India; set up GST registration; create brand identity with heritage narrative.
5
First Sale
List on Flipkart, Amazon Home & Furniture with premium positioning; create Instagram shop showcasing artisan stories and design process; contact 50+ interior design studios via B2B outreach; establish partnerships with 3-5 luxury hotel chains; launch D2C website with payment gateway; join TradeKey and Global Sources for export inquiries.
6
Scale
After first 6 months and ₹15-20L revenue, expand to 8-12 weaver partners; develop 3-4 signature collections per season; hire export-focused sales representative; attend International Trade Fairs (Heimtextil Frankfurt, Canton Fair); establish 2-3 overseas distribution partners; invest in digital marketing targeting interior design professionals.
