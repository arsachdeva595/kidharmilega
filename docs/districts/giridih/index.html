Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Giridih.

1
Learn / Research
Visit established steel mills in Giridih district (Dalmia Group, Ispat Industries, others near Giridih town) and Bokaro Steel Plant. Attend construction material expos in Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore. Study IS 1786 standards for TMT bars and connect with JSMS (Jharkhand Small & Micro Enterprises) clusters
2
Source Raw Materials
Source iron ore from Gua, Tilbe, and Giridih mining zones (₹800-1200/MT). Coal from Eastern Coalfields Limited contracts. Scrap steel from Kolkata and local recycling units. First batch requirement: ~500 MT raw material (~₹40-50 Lakhs investment)
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing mini-steel mills in Giridih (Mahagiri area has 15+ operational units) or lease induction furnace + rolling mill setup. Alternatively, co-produce under contract with established mills and brand under own label. Production capacity: 100-200 MT/month for starter scale
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) with DPIIT, apply for IEC from APEDA for exports. Get IS 1786 certification from BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards). Register trademark with 'Giridih' heritage positioning. Open ICICI/HDFC business account, obtain GST registration
5
First Sale
Launch B2B storefront on IndiaMART and TradeKey. Create LinkedIn profile targeting procurement managers. Contact top 50 real estate firms directly. Register with government e-procurement portals (GeM, CPSE portals). Attend construction expos in Pune and Delhi. Offer samples to 10 local contractors
6
Scale
After 6 months, establish branch office in Mumbai or Bangalore for faster market reach. Hire dedicated B2B sales team. Secure bank credit lines (SIDBI/PFC) for ₹1-2 Cr working capital. Expand to specialized products (seismic-resistant TMT, stainless steel variant). Partner with logistics providers for pan-India delivery within 72 hours

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Launch YouTube channel 'Steel Engineering Insights' with tutorials on TMT bar selection for builders—target construction managers and engineers; post weekly 5-min videos on quality, testing, durability
  • Sponsor construction expos and exhibitions in Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore with product demonstrations and sample offerings; create demo videos showing tensile strength and corrosion resistance
  • Establish partnerships with 5-10 top architectural and engineering firms; provide case studies of structures built with Giridih TMT bars; publish in construction magazines and B2B journals
