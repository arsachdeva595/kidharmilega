Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Bhadrak.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bhadrak's key rice clusters at Basudevpur, Chandbali, and Tihidi; meet 5-10 established rice merchants and farmers at Bhadrak Grain Market; understand local varieties like Swarna, BR, and MTU; attend agricultural fairs at Bhadrak town.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source paddy directly from farmer groups in Basudevpur taluk (₹2,500-3,200/quintal); contact rice mill operators like Bhadrak Rice Mills Association; first batch of 10 tons costs ₹3-4 lakhs; establish relationships with 2-3 reliable suppliers.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing rice mills in Bhadrak town (custom milling at ₹8-12 per kg); or invest in small-scale huller and polisher unit (₹25-40 lakhs); hire experienced quality inspectors; obtain ISO 22000 certification within 3 months.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam on udyamregistration.gov.in; apply for IEC code via DGFT portal; register trademark at Kolkata IP office; get food processing license from Bhadrak District Administration; GST registration as manufacturer.
5
First Sale
List on BigBasket, Blinkit, Grofers, and Amazon Fresh; approach institutional buyers (schools, hospitals) in Bhadrak and Balasore; sell through WhatsApp to local retailers; partner with 5-star hotels in Bhubaneswar; attend food expos in Mumbai and Delhi.
6
Scale
Expand to 100+ tons monthly production by month 8; hire dedicated export manager; get APEDA certification; partner with 10+ distributors across India; launch 2-3 premium variants; invest in food testing lab for quality assurance.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram reels showing farm-to-table journey from Basudevpur rice fields; partner with 10 food bloggers for authentic kitchen demos; run carousel ads targeting health-conscious 25-45 age group.
  • WhatsApp marketing to B2B buyers (hotels, caterers) with bulk pricing and custom packaging options; create verified WhatsApp Business catalog.
  • Email campaigns to institutional buyers (school canteens, hospital kitchens) emphasizing nutrition and quality certifications; attend 3 trade shows annually in Mumbai, Hyderabad, and Delhi.
