Textiles - Home Made Ups

Karur has a 400-year legacy of textile weaving with established supply chains, skilled artisan communities, and proximity to cotton-producing regions. The district's reputation for quality home textiles, combined with existing infrastructure and cooperative societies, makes it ideal for scaling home furnishing businesses.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Karur.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Karur textile cluster in Erode Road area and Manmadhai cooperative society to understand production processes, visit Karur Textiles Association office, meet 3-5 established home textile manufacturers, attend annual Karur Textile Fair.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source premium cotton yarn from Karur Yarn Market (₹80-120/kg) or Coimbatore mills, dyes from Dyestuff manufacturers in Erode (₹500-1500/kg), embroidery threads from local suppliers; first batch of 5000 meters fabric costs approximately ₹2-3 lakhs including yarn and processing.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established weaving clusters in Karur like Chettinadu weavers or register as a textile manufacturing unit, hire 3-4 experienced weavers and finishers, invest in basic loom machinery or contract production with cooperative societies like Karur Weavers Cooperative Union.
4
Brand & Register
Register as MSME under Udyam portal, obtain IEC code from DGFT office in Coimbatore, register GST as textile manufacturer, trademark your brand name with IP office, obtain ISO 9001 certification to boost export credibility.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon, Flipkart, Etsy with 50-100 SKUs, pitch to home decor retailers in Mumbai and Bangalore, create Instagram/Pinterest presence showcasing designs and heritage story, approach luxury hotels and corporate bulk buyers directly, list on global B2B platforms like Global Sources.
6
Scale
Establish second production unit or expand loom capacity, hire dedicated export sales team, achieve ISO 14001 environmental certification, launch 2-3 new seasonal collections annually, expand to European B2B platforms, build own DTC brand website with 10-15% monthly growth targeting.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Create Instagram Reels and TikTok videos showing behind-the-scenes weaving process, artisan interviews, and design inspiration; use hashtags #KarurTextiles #HandweavedHome to reach design-conscious audiences.
  • Partner with interior designers and home decor influencers (50K-500K followers) for sponsored content and affiliate commissions; target influencers in home decor and sustainable living niches.
  • Launch seasonal email campaigns to luxury hotel chains, Airbnb hosts, and corporate gifting managers highlighting bulk order discounts and customization options; segment by order volume and margin potential.
