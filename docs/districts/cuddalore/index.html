Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Cuddalore.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Cuddalore district's primary cashew clusters in Srimukundapuram and Vriddhachalam taluks; meet 5+ established cashew farmers and processors; study at Tamil Nadu Agricultural University's cashew research station; attend Cochin Port's cashew auction market briefings
2
Source Raw Materials
Contact cashew procurement centers in Cuddalore town; source raw cashews from licensed FPOs like Cuddalore Cashew Growers Cooperative; first batch cost ₹3-4 lakhs for 10 tons; establish 45-day payment terms with suppliers
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with SFURTI-registered cashew processing units in Vriddhachalam (100+ registered); invest in IQF freezing or vacuum roasting equipment (₹15-30L); alternatively, outsource to existing processors taking 12-15% commission per ton while you manage sales and branding
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) immediately; apply for IEC code at Chennai DGFT office; obtain Spices Board certification and food license from Cuddalore District Health Authority; file brand trademark at Delhi IP Office; get FSSAI registration (₹12,000 one-time)
5
First Sale
Launch on BigBasket and Amazon Fresh with ₹5-8L first inventory (MOQ 2-5 tons); build direct B2B relationships with export houses via Cochin Spice Board; create Instagram and LinkedIn profiles showcasing farm origin story; secure first corporate bulk order (₹10-15L) from Chennai-based wellness companies within 60 days
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to organic and fair-trade certifications (₹3-5L investment); establish 2-3 regional warehouses in Chennai, Bangalore, Hyderabad; launch private label partnerships with 5+ premium retailers; target ₹3-5 Cr annual revenue by month 18 through export market penetration and D2C channels
