Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Sheopur.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sheopur district's agricultural ministry office (Sheopur Sadar block) and connect with local guava farmer associations. Study the Sheopur mandi auction system, visit Gwalior Agriculture Department for crop health data, and shadow 2-3 established guava farmers for 2 weeks.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source sapling and organic inputs from Madhya Pradesh State Agriculture Department's nurseries (₹30,000-50,000 for initial seedlings for 1 acre). Contact local seed suppliers in Sheopur town for bulk pesticides and fertilizers. First batch sourcing cost: ₹2-3L for 2 acres of production.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing guava farmers in Sheopur through revenue-sharing model (60:40 or 70:30 split) to avoid heavy capex. Alternatively, lease 2-3 acres in villages around Sheopur town (₹40,000-60,000 annually) and hire local farm labor at ₹200-300/day. Set up basic drip irrigation (₹1.5L per acre).
4
Brand & Register
Register as Udyam (MSME) within 1 week online at udyamregistration.gov.in. Apply for IEC code via DGFT portal for export potential. Get organic certification from IFOAM or APEDA if targeting premium segment (₹50,000-1.5L). Register brand name at trademark office (₹5,000-10,000).
5
First Sale
Launch on JioMart, BigBasket, and Blinkit with bulk guava supply after getting FSSAI registration. Approach Gwalior and Indore juice manufacturers for B2B contracts. Use WhatsApp groups and local Facebook pages to sell to nearby urban centers (Gwalior, Indore). Contact Mahabaleshwar-style export houses for initial export trials.
6
Scale
After 6 months, set up a small processing unit (juice/paste production) with ₹15-25L investment to increase margins. Expand to 5-10 acres of farming partnerships. Build relationships with 2-3 export logistics companies. Launch branded D2C website and Instagram marketing targeting health-conscious urban consumers pan-India.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Create Instagram Reels showing farm-to-table journey from Sheopur orchards, partner with micro-influencers (10K-50K followers) in wellness/health niche to drive D2C sales.
  • Run WhatsApp Business campaigns in Gwalior, Indore, and Bhopal urban groups offering weekly fresh guava subscriptions at ₹399/kg with free home delivery.
  • Sponsor local farmer markets and weekly fairs in tier-2 cities (Gwalior, Indore) with sampling booths and QR code links to BigBasket/JioMart.
