Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Surat.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Diamond Bourse, Ashok Marg, Surat (world's largest diamond trading center) and Varachha jewelry cluster. Spend 2 weeks learning gem grading from Gem & Jewellery Export Promotion Council (GJEPC). Join Surat Diamond Association for market intelligence. Cost: ₹20,000-50,000 for courses.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source rough diamonds from licensed traders in Diamond Bourse (minimum 5 carats). Establish accounts with established suppliers in Varachha (gold, silver, gemstones). First batch procurement: ₹10-20 lakhs minimum for mixed inventory. Negotiate 30-45 day credit terms with established traders.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 master craftsmen in Varachha or Rander clusters via GJEPC-registered workshop networks. Start with 10-20 artisans on commission basis (8-15% per piece). Alternatively, lease production space in Surat jewelry clusters (₹1-5 lakhs/month) and hire trained cutters and polishers.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (micro/small) at udyamregistration.gov.in. Apply for IEC (Import-Export Code) at www.dgft.gov.in. Register with GJEPC for export benefits. Register trademark with IP India (₹4,500 per class). Obtain hallmark certification from BIS-recognized centers.
5
First Sale
Launch on Meesho (B2B marketplace for SMEs), 1mg.com jewelry section, and own Shopify store. Create Instagram/Facebook content showcasing design process and artisan stories. Approach wedding planners and high-end boutiques in Tier 1 cities. Attend IIJW (India International Jewelry Week) in Mumbai. First month target: ₹5-10 lakhs via B2B channels.
6
Scale
Expand product range (men's, contemporary, bridal lines). Open show-room in Bombay/Delhi jewelry hubs. Scale to 50-100 artisans. Build export partnerships in UAE/USA via GJEPC. Invest in digital marketing (Facebook/Google ads) targeting overseas Indians. Aim for ₹5 Cr revenue by Year 2.

How to get customers

  • Partner with wedding planning influencers on Instagram/YouTube for bridal jewelry showcase reels (₹50K-1L per influencer) targeting engaged couples in metro cities
  • Run Google Shopping ads targeting 'diamond jewelry near me' and 'certified gemstones' keywords, focusing on export quality claims and GIA certification (₹2-5L monthly budget)
  • Create TikTok/YouTube Shorts showing 30-second artisan craftsmanship videos (diamond cutting, polishing, setting) to humanize brand and attract Gen Z buyers
