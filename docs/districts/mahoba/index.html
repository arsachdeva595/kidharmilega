Gaura Stone Craft

Mahoba has a centuries-old tradition of stone craftsmanship rooted in the region's architectural heritage. The availability of high-quality sandstone deposits and a skilled artisan base with generational knowledge makes it the natural hub for Gaura stone craft production and innovation.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Mahoba.

1
Learn / Research
Visit the Mahoba stone craft clusters in Charkhari and Jaitpur blocks; spend 5-7 days observing artisan workshops, understand carving techniques, and network with master craftspeople. Connect with Mahoba Handicraft Association for market insights.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw Gaura sandstone blocks from Mahoba quarries (₹8,000-15,000/block); order basic hand tools (chisels, mallets) from Jaipur tool suppliers (₹25,000-40,000 for starter set); negotiate with 2-3 trusted suppliers for consistent quality.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3-5 established artisans in Mahoba on piece-rate contracts (₹2,000-8,000/piece depending on complexity); ensure contracts specify quality benchmarks and timelines; consider co-working arrangements to manage inventory and quality control.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam as micro/small enterprise; apply for IEC for export capability; register brand name with DPIIT; obtain GI authentication certificate from DC Handicraft office; create heritage-focused brand story around artisan collaboration.
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy with 15-20 high-quality product photos and detailed heritage narratives; reach out to 50 interior designers via LinkedIn with custom quotations; list on Amazon Handmade and Indianbeads; pitch to 5-10 luxury hotels and resorts for décor contracts.
6
Scale
After 6 months, hire a design team to create seasonal collections; establish showrooms in Delhi/Bangalore; scale to 8-10 artisan partnerships; launch B2B export program targeting 3-4 new countries per year; develop online consultation service for custom commissions.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Create 8-10 minute documentary-style reels on Instagram/YouTube featuring artisan crafting process, stone selection, and finished pieces; share behind-the-scenes stories to build emotional connection with luxury home décor enthusiasts.
  • Partner with 10-15 premium interior designers in Delhi/Mumbai for co-branded lookbook campaigns; offer them 15% margin on referrals; feature their completed projects on your website and social channels.
  • Launch targeted Google Ads and Pinterest campaigns targeting keywords like 'handcrafted stone sculpture', 'luxury home décor', 'heritage art'; retarget website visitors with 20% first-order discounts to drive conversions.
