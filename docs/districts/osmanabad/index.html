Local infrastructure includes pulses mandis at Latur and direct access to national markets via NH-44.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Osmanabad.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Latur mandi and Parli Vaijnath pulse trading zones to understand grading, pricing cycles, and buyer networks. Connect with Osmanabad District Cooperative Federation and attend pulse farmer meets. Study APEDA export requirements and inspect existing pulses processing units in Parbhani district.
2
Source Raw Materials
Engage directly with registered seed suppliers in Latur (Vijay Seeds, Namdhari Seeds). For first 10-ton batch: ₹2.5-3.5L for certified seeds + ₹1.5-2L for land preparation. Contact NARD (National Agricultural Robotics Division) or local farmer unions for bulk supplier discounts.
3
Produce / Partner
Either lease 10-50 acres from local farmers or partner with existing farmer cooperatives in Parli, Latur talukas. Engage Krishi Vigyan Kendra Latur for sustainable farming guidance. Start with contract farming model—farmers grow on your specifications, you handle processing and sales.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (basic info + GSTIN), obtain IEC code from DGFT office in Aurangabad (2-3 weeks). File FSSAI registration if adding value-added products. Register brand name at Intellectual Property Appellate Board. Obtain ISO 22000 certification for food safety (₹40-60K).
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh, BigBasket under ODOP category. Create B2B accounts on TradeKey, Global Sources for export inquiries. Supply initial batches to regional FMCG distributors (Britannia, ITC Aashirvaad local partners). Pitch to Government e-Marketplace (GeM) for institutional tenders.
6
Scale
Invest in mechanized processing unit (dehulling, grading) to increase margins from 20% to 35%. Hire export manager to secure international contracts. Establish 2-3 farmer producer groups under FPO model for consistent supply. Add packaged private-label products for retail chains.

How to get customers

  • Run Instagram/YouTube content series 'Farmer Stories from Osmanabad' showing sustainable practices—target urban organic consumers via Instagram Ads (₹10K/month budget)
  • Partner with 3-4 health/wellness influencers (50K-100K followers) for unboxing and recipe videos on pulses—₹50-75K per influencer quarterly campaign
  • Create B2B WhatsApp broadcast group for dal millers and exporters sharing weekly market rates, availability updates, bulk discounts—0 cost, high conversion
