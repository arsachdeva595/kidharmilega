Granite (Black Galaxy)

Prakasam district sits atop one of India's richest granite deposits with naturally occurring Black Galaxy stone. The established quarrying infrastructure in Chimakurthy and Ongole clusters, combined with proximity to major ports (Visakhapatnam, Chennai) and 40+ years of mining expertise, makes it ideal for granite processing and export.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Prakasam.

1
Learn / Research
Visit the Chimakurthy granite cluster (40+ km from Ongole) and meet 5-10 established quarry owners. Study mining licenses from Andhra Pradesh Mines & Geology Department. Attend India Stone Congress or Stone India Exhibition (annual). Connect with APCEA (Andhra Pradesh Cement Manufacturers Association) for supplier networks.
2
Source Raw Materials
Secure mining lease in Chimakurthy or partner with existing quarry operators (40-50% revenue share typical). Budget ₹5-10 Cr for quarrying rights and machinery. First batch: 50-100 tons of raw blocks = ₹15-25 L depending on quality and quarry partner agreements.
3
Produce / Partner
Invest in diamond wire saw cutting units and multi-blade polishing machines (₹20-30 L per unit). Hire experienced stone masons from Chimakurthy (wages ₹300-500/day). Partner with existing finishing units in Ongole town to avoid capex, offering 15-20% commission per finished slab.
4
Brand & Register
Register business as MSME under Udyam (msme.gov.in) within 2 hours online. Obtain IEC from DGFT office in Vijayawada or Hyderabad. Apply for BIS certification (IS 2556) for granite quality. Register trade mark with IP Office (Delhi). Get mining compliance from state mines department.
5
First Sale
List finished slabs on Alibaba.com, Global Sources, and TradeKey with high-resolution images and certifications. Reach out to 100+ architects on Houzz and ArchiPalace platforms. Contact 20 bulk importers in US/UK via email campaigns. Use WhatsApp business for B2B inquiries from local builders. Partner with 3-5 tile/construction material retailers in major Indian metros for offline placement.
6
Scale
After ₹1 Cr in revenue, invest in in-house polishing/finishing units to improve margins from 20% to 35%. Expand quarrying capacity by acquiring adjacent leases or partnering with 2 more quarries. Launch D2C website with interior design consultation (online). Hire export compliance officer and expand to 8-10 export markets. Consider backward integration into diamond wire manufacturing.
