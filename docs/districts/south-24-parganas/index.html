ODOP Product

Leather Goods

South 24 Parganas has a centuries-old tradition of leather working with established artisan communities in areas like Basirhat and surrounding regions. The district's proximity to Kolkata provides access to raw materials, markets, and skilled labor trained in traditional leather techniques. The existing ecosystem of leather tanneries and artisan clusters makes it a natural hub for leather goods production.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for South 24 Parganas.

1
Learn / Research
Visit leather clusters in Basirhat and adjacent areas in South 24 Parganas; connect with SIDBI-supported artisan groups; study tanning methods at local leather associations; visit Kolkata leather markets to understand pricing and demand
2
Source Raw Materials
Source leather from established tanneries in Bantala (Kolkata) or Basirhat leather suppliers; procure metal hardware from Mumbai or Kolkata suppliers; first batch of 50-100 units requires ₹150,000-300,000 depending on product mix and leather quality
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 established artisan groups in Basirhat through DC Handicraft registered clusters; start with contract manufacturing; invest ₹200,000-400,000 in basic tools, dye vats, and finishing workspace; hire 2-3 trained artisans on freelance basis initially
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, free); apply for IEC code from local DGFT office; trademark brand name at IP Office; register as GST regular taxable entity; obtain animal hide sourcing certificates; create brand identity around traditional South Bengali craftsmanship
5
First Sale
Launch Instagram and Pinterest accounts showcasing artisan stories and product close-ups; list on Amazon Handmade India and Flipkart Artisan; approach lifestyle boutiques in Kolkata and major metros; start with 500-piece initial order fulfillment; use Ungodly Goods or Artisan Collective platforms
6
Scale
Expand artisan partnerships to 8-10 clusters; invest in retail presence in Kolkata and Delhi; develop 15-20 SKUs by month 6; launch wholesale distributor program; apply for export certifications; target ₹1.5-2 Cr revenue by end of Year 2
