Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Mayiladuthurai.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Mayiladuthurai's farming clusters in Kattumannarkoil and Tirukkoshtyur; interact with established rice mill owners at Mayiladuthurai APMC; attend NAFED rice trade shows; study export regulations via APEDA website.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified paddy seeds from Tamil Nadu Seeds Corporation (Tirukkoshtyur); negotiate bulk procurement at APMC market; initial 50-ton batch costs approximately ₹1,00,000-₹1,50,000 including processing.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established rice mills in Mayiladuthurai (capacity 10-50 tons/day) through toll milling at ₹2-3 per kg; alternatively, lease/purchase small husking and milling equipment for ₹3-8 lakh.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME portal); apply for IEC code via ECIC; obtain Food Safety License from FSSAI; trademark brand name via IP India; get phytosanitary certificate for exports.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh, BigBasket, Flipkart Wholesale; register on Indiamart for B2B; contact APEDA-listed export houses; approach organic food retailers; build WhatsApp-based bulk ordering system.
6
Scale
Expand to 5-7 varieties; invest in packaging automation; establish cold storage facility; build direct relationships with 20-30 restaurants/hotels; initiate organic certification for premium segment; explore contract farming models.
