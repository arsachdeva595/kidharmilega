Groundnuts/Peanuts (Peanut Based Products)

Junagadh has ideal agro-climatic conditions with well-drained sandy loam soils perfect for peanut cultivation, producing 200,000+ MT annually. The district has established infrastructure, skilled labor, and proximity to major food processing hubs like Ahmedabad and Surat, enabling efficient processing and distribution.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Junagadh.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Junagadh Agricultural Department and APEDA office; conduct market research at Ahmedabad's Food Park and Surat's processing clusters; interview 5-7 established producers in Junagadh taluka; study competitor products on Amazon, BigBasket.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw peanuts directly from Junagadh farmers (₹45-55/kg wholesale) or from NAFED depot in Junagadh; negotiate contracts for 5-10 MT initial batch (₹250,000-500,000); identify reliable oil suppliers and packaging material vendors in Ahmedabad.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing MSME food processors in Junagadh industrial estate for co-packing (₹8-12/kg processing cost) OR set up small production unit (roasting machine ₹200,000, grinding machine ₹150,000, packaging ₹100,000); hire 2-3 skilled workers from local labor market.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME online; apply for IEC code from APEDA; get FSSAI food license (₹2,000-5,000); design brand identity emphasizing 'Junagadh-origin' and nutritional benefits; trademark brand name with Indian IP office.
5
First Sale
Launch on BigBasket, Blinkit, and Amazon Fresh (takes 2-4 weeks); set up WhatsApp Business catalog for local B2B; reach out to 20-30 local kirana stores with samples and ₹15,000 initial order; pitch to schools and offices as bulk supplier; attend Ahmedabad food trade shows (GFTIE).
6
Scale
After 6 months: expand product range (peanut butter, chikli variants); secure FMCG distributor partnerships in Gujarat and nearby states; apply for PMEGP/SFURTI subsidy for equipment upgrade; develop export certification (ISO 22000, organic); launch Instagram/YouTube content marketing featuring Junagadh's farming story.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Partner with fitness influencers (Instagram, YouTube) for product seeding and reviews highlighting protein content; create 15-30 second Reels showing farm-to-table journey; target #HealthySnacking and #FitnessFood audience.
  • Launch email campaign to B2B buyers (bakeries, food manufacturers, corporate wellness programs); offer first-time buyer discount (10-15%); create product specification sheets and nutritional data.
  • Run Google Shopping Ads targeting 'peanut butter price', 'healthy snacks online'; sponsor local food festivals and Junagadh AgriExpo; partner with nutrition blogs and health websites for sponsored content and backlinks.
Government Schemes

Free money you didn't know about

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy (25% for general, 35% for SC/ST) for manufacturing unit setup
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹40 lakh for food processing clusters and artisan collectives in Junagadh
🏚
Gujarat Agro-Industries Corporation (GAIC) Subsidy
25-30% capital subsidy for food processing enterprises; technical support and market linkages
🏚
Gujarat Food Processing Policy 2021
GST refund up to 5% and electricity subsidy for registered food processors; export promotion grants
Registration required: Required — udyamregistration.gov.in
