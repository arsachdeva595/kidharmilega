ODOP Product

Jaggery

Ayodhya's agricultural landscape in the Indo-Gangetic plains yields premium sugarcane varieties with optimal juice concentration and lower bagasse content. The district's traditional knowledge of jaggery production, combined with abundant sugarcane cultivation in surrounding areas (Sultanpur, Raebareli proximity), creates a natural production ecosystem. Historical artisan communities in Ayodhya have perfected low-temperature crystallization techniques that preserve nutrient density and flavor complexity.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Ayodhya (Faizabad).

1
Learn / Research
Visit Ayodhya jaggery clusters in Bikapur and Sultanpur taluks to study production methods from established artisans. Attend 3-day hands-on workshops with master jaggery makers. Research sugarcane varieties at Dr. Rajendra Prasad Central Agricultural University, Pusa. Cost: ₹8,000-15,000.
2
Source Raw Materials
Procure premium sugarcane from Sultanpur district cooperative societies (₹2,500-3,200 per quintal for juice). Connect with local sugarcane farmers via Ayodhya Agricultural Produce Market. First batch (500 kg sugarcane yielding ~120 kg jaggery): ₹35,000-45,000. Establish direct farmer contracts for seasonal supply Dec-May.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 established jaggery artisans in Ayodhya town for co-production under your brand (revenue-share model: 40% to artisans). Alternatively, lease a 200 sq ft production unit near sugarcane market with traditional kadhais setup. Investment: ₹80,000-200,000 for rental, equipment, licenses. Target 50 kg/day production initially.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME portal) as food processor; apply for FSSAI license (₹5,000-8,000 for small scale); obtain IEC code for export potential. Trademark your brand name at IP office (₹3,000-5,000). Design heritage-inspired packaging emphasizing Ayodhya heritage and handcrafted authenticity. Get BIS certification for jaggery if targeting premium segments.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Brand Registry and Flipkart seller panel with 'Handcrafted Ayodhya Jaggery' positioning; price 500g packs at ₹280-350 (wholesale: ₹180-200). Simultaneously pitch to 15-20 premium sweet shops in Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore; target ₹500-800/carton wholesale. Create Instagram content around artisan stories and production heritage. Partner with 2-3 organic online retailers (Natureland, BigBasket organic). Target ₹50,000 first month revenue.
6
Scale
After 6 months profitability, expand product line (jaggery powder, cubes, organic variant). Scale D2C via influencer partnerships with wellness/Ayurveda creators. Build B2B relationships with 50+ sweet shops across 5 states. Initiate exports to UK retailers via distributor partnerships. Invest in semi-automatic production to increase capacity to 200 kg/day. Target ₹25-30 Lakhs annual revenue by month 18.
