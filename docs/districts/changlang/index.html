Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Changlang.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Changlang arecanut clusters in Miao and Namsai blocks; connect with District Agricultural Officer and local farmer cooperative CAFSCO; study Mumbai's Crawford Market and Delhi's Bhagirath Palace for retail pricing and demand patterns.
2
Source Raw Materials
Purchase raw arecanut from registered farmers via Changlang Agricultural Produce Cooperative Federation (CAPCF) at ₹120-150/kg; first batch of 2,000 kg costs approximately ₹2.4-3 Lakh; also source from established traders like Sai Nath Enterprises in Changlang town.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established processing units in Miao (drying, sorting, grading); or invest in basic solar drying racks (₹50K-1.5L) and hire local labor; engage with Changlang District Horticulture Mission for technical support on post-harvest management.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online portal), apply for IEC code via DGFT Kolkata office (for exports), register brand name with IP Registry, obtain FSSAI license for food processing if value-adding, get Arunachal Pradesh Horticulture Department certification.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh, JioMart, Blinkit with ₹50K initial listing cost; approach wholesale suppliers like Spice Route Traders and Venkateshwara Agencies (Mumbai); contact 20-30 paan shops in Kolkata, Mumbai via direct outreach; list on TradeKey and Global Sources for export inquiries.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to 5,000 kg monthly production; establish own processing unit (₹15-20L investment); hire sales representatives for major metros; launch branded consumer packging (₹3-5L); apply for APEDA (Agricultural Products Export Development Authority) support for export certification and market access to Gulf countries.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram/YouTube Reels showing farm-to-table journey from Changlang terraces; partner with paan influencers in Mumbai/Kolkata for authentic testimonials; target hashtags #BetelLover #NortheastProducts.
  • B2B LinkedIn campaign targeting wholesale suppliers, food distributors, and export houses; webinar series on arecanut quality standards and health benefits with certified agronomists.
  • Sponsorship of regional trade shows like India International Trade Fair (New Delhi) and Chennai Export Summit; direct booth presence targeting distributors and bulk buyers.
