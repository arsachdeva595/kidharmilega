Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Ambala.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Ambala City Industrial Estate and Ambala Cantonment clusters to meet established manufacturers like Radical Scientific Instruments and Advance Laboratory. Attend Indian Science Congress or Lab India Expo. Study ISO 9001 and ISO 13485 certifications required for medical equipment segment.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source borosilicate glass from Hindustan National Glass & Industries (HNGIL), Bangalore; optical glass from Schott India, Mumbai; stainless steel from Jindal Steel; electronic components from Mumbai electronics wholesale market. First-batch raw material cost: ₹8-12 lakhs for 500-unit initial production run.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing Ambala manufacturers for contract manufacturing or lease space in Ambala City Industrial Estate (₹50-80k monthly). Recruit precision fitters and glass technicians from local polytechnics. Implement quality control using NABL-accredited testing labs in Ambala like NIFFT or private labs.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME immediately for subsidy eligibility. Obtain IEC from DGFT for export. Apply for ISO 9001:2015 certification (₹1.5-2.5 lakhs, 3-4 months). Register trademark with IP India. Create manufacturer registration on NSIC portal.
5
First Sale
List on IndiaMart and TradeKey for B2B inquiries. Register with IndiaStack for government procurements. Contact 50+ AICTE-approved labs directly with product catalogs. Attend Lab India Mumbai (February), ArabLab Dubai, Analytica Munich for export leads. Use LinkedIn for institutional outreach with technical specifications.
6
Scale
After 6 months, hire dedicated export manager and establish relationships with distributors in USA (Carolina Scientific), Germany (VWR), UK (Jencons-PLC). Invest in R&D for specialized products like precision microscopes or electronic measurement equipment. Expand to 5-6 product categories and hire 20-30 skilled workers.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Create YouTube technical channel demonstrating equipment specifications and lab setup cases for educational institutions; target IIT, DU, and medical colleges with dedicated sales outreach.
  • Partner with science fair organizers in India and Southeast Asia for exhibition booths; sponsor school science competitions to build brand awareness among next-generation buyers.
  • Publish technical white papers on precision measurement and lab efficiency in journals like Current Science and share on ResearchGate; establish thought leadership in scientific community.
