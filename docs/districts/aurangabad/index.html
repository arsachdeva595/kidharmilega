Aurangabad's semi-arid climate, fertile Gangetic plains soil, and well-developed agricultural infrastructure make it ideal for lemongrass cultivation. The district's proximity to major spice hubs and established agri-export networks provide direct market access. Local farmer cooperatives and existing herbal cultivation expertise create a ready ecosystem for scaling lemongrass production.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Aurangabad.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Aurangabad's established lemongrass farms in Daudnagar and Rohtas blocks; connect with Bihar Agricultural Department's office in Aurangabad; study market at Spice Park, Phulwari Sharif near Patna; join local farming cooperatives like Aurangabad Krishi Utpad Samiti
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified lemongrass seedlings from ICAR-IISR Lucknow or Bihar Horticulture Department nurseries (₹15,000-25,000 for 1 acre startup); procure organic fertilizers from local agro-input stores in Aurangabad city; first-batch raw material cost approximately ₹30,000-40,000 for 0.5 acres
3
Produce / Partner
Lease 0.5-2 acres cultivated land in Daudnagar block; partner with established farmers through contract farming model; install basic drip irrigation system (₹50,000-80,000); hire 2-3 seasonal laborers during harvest; set up simple drying rack facility or partner with existing spice drying units in Aurangabad
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam portal as micro/small enterprise; obtain IEC code from Aurangabad Custom House; apply for Organic Certification from APEDA or certified agency; register brand with IP Office; obtain FSSAI license for processed products (tea, powder); create GST registration in Aurangabad
5
First Sale
List on Amazon Pantry, Flipkart, Blink, and JioMart for D2C reach; approach local Ayurvedic brands like Patanjali, Dabur, Himalaya for bulk B2B contracts; distribute through Kerala-based spice exporters already operating from Aurangabad; create Instagram/YouTube content showcasing farm-to-cup story; participate in Aurangabad Agricultural Trade Fair annually
6
Scale
Expand to 2-5 acres by month 6-9; invest in small-scale processing unit for drying and powder packaging; establish contract farming network with 10-15 local farmers; launch export registrations and certifications (ISO, Organic EU); develop premium branded variants for online channels; build B2B relationships with 3-5 major FMCG/wellness brands
