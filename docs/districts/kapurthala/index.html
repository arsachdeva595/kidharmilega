Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Kapurthala.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kapurthala Auto Parts Cluster (Sultanpur Lodhi area) and meet 10+ established manufacturers; study OEM specifications at SIAM (Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers) office in Delhi; attend Auto Expo or ACMA conferences; connect with 5 active suppliers on LinkedIn in Kapurthala.
2
Source Raw Materials
Contact raw material suppliers in Kapurthala Industrial Area and Ahmedabad steel market; order first batch of cast iron and steel forgings (₹8-15L); source precision tools from Bangalore machine tool dealers; establish credit line with 2-3 suppliers for recurring needs.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing CNC machining units in Kapurthala cluster on job-work basis initially (avoid owning machinery); or lease 500 sq ft space in Kapurthala Industrial Area and install 2-3 used CNC machines (₹25-30L); hire 5-8 skilled technicians from local ITI Kapurthala.
4
Brand & Register
Register as MSME Udyam (udyamregistration.gov.in) within 1 week; obtain IEC (Import-Export Code) from DGFT portal for export readiness; register company with MCA; apply for ISO 9001:2015 certification (₹2-3L) essential for OEM sales; create basic website showcasing technical capabilities.
5
First Sale
Contact purchasing departments of Sonalika, TAFE, Mahindra in Punjab directly with samples and certifications; attend Kapurthala Chamber of Commerce events; list on IndiaStack B2B platform and TradieKey; reach aftermarket dealers through local Kapurthala automobile associations; attend ACMA (Automobile Component Manufacturers Association) trade fairs.
6
Scale
After 6 months, invest in own CNC capability (₹40-50L); hire technical sales engineer; open satellite office near major OEM clusters (Bajaj in Aurangabad, TAFE in Madurai); apply for Export Promotion Financing from banks; consider ISO TS 16949 for international tier-1 tier-2 opportunities; expand product range to allied components.
