Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Medchal-Malkajgiri.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Medchal-Malkajgiri pharma industrial clusters (HITEC City extension, Gajwel industrial area) and meet 5-10 existing manufacturers; attend Indian Pharmaceutical Conference; study WHO-GMP, FDC compliance requirements from CDSCO; analyze top 20 pharma products in your segment from IQVIA database
2
Source Raw Materials
Source APIs from established suppliers in Hyderabad (FIRL, Farmhall, Laurus Labs supply chain) or IIFC Hyderabad; bulk purchase 500kg-5MT APIs at ₹8-15L; source excipients from Colorcon India, Signet Chemical; negotiate credit terms (15-30 days) with minimum first-batch cost ₹25-35L for formulation development
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing WHO-GMP certified contract manufacturers in Medchal (Aurobindo, Hetero Labs extensions) for pilot batch production; alternatively, acquire small existing FSSAI/CDSCO-approved manufacturing unit in the district; invest in quality control lab (HPLC, dissolution apparatus) costing ₹15-20L
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam on udyamregistration.gov.in within 1 week; obtain CDSCO Drug Manufacturing License (Form 17) — timeline 60-90 days; register IEC for export from DGFT; file brand trademark with IP India for pharma segment; obtain FSSAI license; set up statutory compliance for Drugs and Cosmetics Act 1940
5
First Sale
Launch on 1mg, PharmEasy, Apollo Pharmacy marketplace within 6 months of CDSCO approval; approach 50-100 wholesale distributors in Hyderabad/Telangana; secure institutional contracts with government hospitals via TNEB, APIDC tenders; build LinkedIn B2B presence for CDMO contracts; contact international distributors for export-ready formulations
6
Scale
After 500L annual revenue, invest in automation (blister packaging, labeling lines ₹30-50L); expand product portfolio to 8-12 SKUs; establish second manufacturing shift; apply for WHO-GMP certification; enter regulated markets (USFDA, EMA) with dossier submission (₹3-5Cr capex); build distribution in 3-4 South Indian states
