ODOP Product

Moradabad Metal Craft

Moradabad has a 300+ year legacy of metal craftsmanship with an estimated 50,000+ artisans and established supply chains for brass, copper, and silver. The district accounts for ~40% of India's brassware exports and hosts dedicated metal craft clusters in Chandi Bazar and Loha Mandi. Proximity to Delhi markets and established export infrastructure make it ideal for scaling metal craft businesses.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Moradabad.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Chandi Bazar and Loha Mandi in Moradabad; attend India International Trade Fair (IITF) Delhi; meet established exporters like Anil Exports or Moradabad Brassware Association; study bestselling designs on Amazon, Etsy, and Wayfair; understand HS codes 7326, 7615, 7418 for export classification
2
Source Raw Materials
Source brass ingots and copper sheets from local suppliers in Loha Mandi (₹60-80 per kg for small batches); silver from Delhi bullion markets; first batch of 50 units (mixed designs) costs ₹150,000-300,000; negotiate 15-20% bulk discounts after 3 months
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established artisan clusters in Chandi Bazar (contact Moradabad Artisan Network); or hire 2-3 skilled craftsmen (₹15,000-20,000/month); start with 10-15 designs; setup small workshop (500-1000 sq ft) with basic tools; quality-check in-house or use local auditing services (₹500-1000 per batch)
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam via udyamregistration.gov.in (ITC-HS classification: 7326/7615); apply for IEC from DGFT portal (₹0 online); trademark brand name at ipindia.gov.in (₹4,500-9,000); obtain ISI mark for specific products (if exporting); register GST as manufacturer (18% applicable)
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon.in, Flipkart, and own Shopify store (target ₹2-5L sales in 3 months); reach out to 20+ home décor retailers via LinkedIn/calls; list on IndiaStack and Global Sources for B2B export leads; negotiate 30-40% retailer margin; use WhatsApp catalog for corporate gifting leads; participate in Delhi Trade Fair 2024-2025
6
Scale
After 6 months, hire 2 more artisans and a quality manager; expand product range to 30+ SKUs; secure export orders via trade shows and B2B platforms (target ₹30-50L exports annually); open warehouse in Delhi NCR or establish drop-shipping with 3PL partner; invest in Instagram/YouTube content marketing for D2C; explore jewellery-grade designs for premium segment (5x margins)
