ODOP Product

Granite Products

Dungarpur is naturally endowed with vast deposits of high-quality granite and marble reserves, making it one of Rajasthan's premier stone extraction regions. The district has generations of skilled artisans trained in traditional stone cutting, polishing, and design work. Proximity to major quarries combined with established supply chains to processing units makes it ideal for scaling granite tile and slab production.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Dungarpur.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Dungarpur district's granite clusters near Sagwara and Aspur; spend 1-2 weeks studying local quarry operations, processing units, and artisan workshops. Connect with Dungarpur Chamber of Commerce and MSME cluster associations. Study competitor pricing on Urban Clap, Houzz, and international B2B platforms.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw granite blocks from Dungarpur quarries (₹80-150 per sqft rough stone). Establish relationships with 2-3 quarry owners near Sagwara. First batch: 5 tons of raw blocks (~₹4-6 lakhs). Source polishing equipment and diamond tools from Jaipur industrial suppliers (ICCI network). Total first-batch cost: ₹8-12 lakhs.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing processing units in Dungarpur town (Sagwara cluster has 50+ units). Negotiate shared facility space (₹15-30K monthly) or co-production agreement (5-8% commission on output). Alternatively, lease a small 500 sqft unit with 2-3 polishing machines (₹50-80K monthly rent + ₹15-20L equipment investment). Hire 2-3 skilled cutters and 2 polishers from local artisan networks.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME within 10 days (udyamregistration.gov.in). Apply for IEC code from DGFT via ICEGATE portal (₹0 cost, 5-7 days). Trademark brand name with IP India (₹4500 + legal fees). Register GST (18% manufacturing, 5% retail). Create brand identity: logo, website (Wix/Shopify ~₹200/month), product catalogue with high-res stone photography.
5
First Sale
Launch WhatsApp Business catalog targeting architects via Instagram ads (₹5K initial budget). List on Flipkart B2B and TradeKey for export inquiries. Contact 20-30 builders/contractors in Rajasthan via direct calls using local business directories. Join ITPO's India International Trade Fair (IITF) as exhibitor (~₹2-3L booth cost). Create Pinterest boards and YouTube channel showcasing installation before-afters.
6
Scale
After month 6: Invest in CNC finishing machines (₹20-30L) to add precision edge-cutting. Hire business development manager for export markets. Apply for PMEGP subsidy if not availed earlier. Establish showroom in Jaipur's granite hub (Ajmeri Gate area, ₹80-120K monthly). Build B2B relationships with 3-4 major trading houses for Middle East exports. Target ₹1.5-2 Cr annual revenue by month 18.
