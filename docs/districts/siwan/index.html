ODOP Product

Mentha

Siwan's semi-temperate climate, alluvial soil from the Ganges plains, and established agricultural traditions make it ideal for mentha cultivation. The district's existing infrastructure for spice and herb processing, combined with proximity to major pharma hubs in North India, creates a natural advantage for mentha production and value-added processing.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Siwan.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Siwan's primary mentha-growing clusters around Marhaura and Bisfi blocks; connect with existing farmers and processors at Siwan Agricultural Produce Market Committee (APMC); study distillation techniques at nearby herb processing units in Samastipur district.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source mentha saplings from certified nurseries in Lucknow (Uttar Pradesh) or local farmers in Siwan (₹40,000-60,000 for 1 acre seeding); purchase processing equipment (basic still distillery ₹300,000-500,000) from suppliers in Kannauj, UP; organic fertilizers from local agricultural input dealers.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 5-10 mentha farmers in Siwan on contract farming basis (supply seeds, buy output at premium); establish a small processing unit (1,000-5,000 sq ft) for drying and distillation; hire trained distillery operators from existing mentha oil units in UP border areas.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam under MSME (classification: 20115 — spice and aromatic plants processing); obtain IEC code from DGFT for export eligibility; apply for organic certification if targeting premium markets (FSSAI, APEDA); trademark brand name with IP office; get APEDA registration for agricultural exports.
5
First Sale
List on IndiaMART as supplier (mentha oil, dried leaves); approach pharmaceutical buyers via CHEMEXCIL portal; register with Jaipur-based export trading houses specializing in essential oils; use Alibaba for direct B2B international sales; contact organic retailers (Big Basket Organic, Nature's Basket) for branded mentha products.
6
Scale
Expand contract farming network to 50+ farmers; invest in advanced CO2 extraction technology (₹15-20L) for premium menthol; develop branded retail products (teas, oils, balms) for D2C via Shopify/Amazon; establish food-grade certification for cosmetic-grade mentha; explore government subsidy for cold-storage and processing unit expansion.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Partner with LinkedIn-based B2B content on menthol's pharmaceutical benefits; target CPOs at top 500 pharma companies with case studies on Siwan mentha quality and cost-advantage.
  • Create Instagram content showcasing farm-to-bottle mentha distillation process; partner with Ayurveda/wellness micro-influencers (10K-100K followers) in India for mentha oil reviews and DIY mentha tea recipes.
  • Attend SIAL India (food ingredients expo), Natural & Organic Products World, and Global Pharma India exhibitions; setup booth with mentha oil samples and export pricing; collect buyer leads for quarterly follow-up.
