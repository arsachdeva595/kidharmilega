ODOP Product

Millets

Davangere's semi-arid climate, well-drained black soil, and 700mm annual rainfall create ideal conditions for ragi, jowar, and finger millet cultivation. The district has 40+ years of millet farming heritage with established farmer networks and proximity to major processing hubs in Bangalore (140km), enabling efficient value-addition and distribution.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Davangere.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Davangere's millet farming clusters in Honnali and Jagalur taluks; interview 10+ established millet processors at Davangere Agricultural Produce Market (DAPM); analyze competitor brands like Conscious Food and True Elements on Amazon/Flipkart; spend ₹15,000 on market research.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source 5 MT ragi and 3 MT jowar from Davangere District Cooperative Marketing Society or direct farmers in Harihara taluk at ₹45-55/kg; negotiate first-batch cost ₹450,000-550,000; establish 3-month supply agreements with minimum quality certifications (FSSAI, organic if targeting premium).
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with SFURTI-registered processing units in Davangere (Sai Processing Unit or Agro Value Added center) for cleaning, drying, and packaging; or invest ₹1.5M-2.5M in own processing equipment (cleaner, grinder, packing machine); target 500kg/day initial capacity.
4
Brand & Register
Register FSSAI license (₹10,000-15,000 online via Davangere District Commissioner office); obtain Udyam registration (free, 10 mins online); apply for IEC code at Bangalore Export Promotion Zone or online via ICEGATE (₹500); register trademark with IP office (₹9,000-12,000); design packaging with nutritional info and sustainability messaging.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon FBA (10% commission) and Flipkart Seller (8% commission) with ₹500g and 1kg SKUs priced at ₹220-280 per 500g; approach 50+ modern retail chains via Davangere-Bangalore corridor (Naturals, Healthkart outlets); send samples to 20 health blogs for reviews; run ₹50,000 Google Shopping Ads campaign targeting 'millet health benefits' keywords; initial target: 100 units/week through combined channels.
6
Scale
After 6 months, add 3-4 value-added SKUs (millet flour blends, instant millet bowls, millet granola); expand distribution to 5 major cities via food wholesalers; establish export partnerships with APEDA-registered traders; invest ₹300,000 in influencer marketing via fitness/wellness creators; target ₹3Cr revenue run-rate by month 18.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Partner with 15-20 fitness micro-influencers (50K-500K followers) on Instagram to create millet recipe reels and pre/post-workout nutrition content; offer 10% affiliate commission on sales; budget ₹150,000 over 3 months.
  • Launch SEO-optimized blog content on own website targeting keywords like 'ragi health benefits,' 'jowar nutrition,' 'millet recipes for diabetics'; publish 2 articles/week; aim for organic traffic of 500+ visitors/week within 4 months with ₹20,000 content creation budget.
  • Execute sample distribution at 10-12 premium gyms and wellness centers in Bangalore, Pune, and Hyderabad (₹80,000 budget); include recipe cards and 20% first-order discount codes; convert 8-10% of samplers into repeat customers.
Government Schemes

Free money you didn't know about

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (25% for general category) for processing and value addition in agriculture.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹10L cluster-based support for food processing units with technical and marketing assistance.
🏚
Karnataka State Agricultural Prices Commission KAPC Subsidy
partial cost support for certified organic millet producers; ₹5,000-10,000 per acre benefit.
🏚
Karnataka Export Promotion Scheme
5% cashback on export FOB value for registered food exporters; ₹2-5L annual benefit for growing exporters.
