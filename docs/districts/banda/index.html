ODOP Product

Shazar Stone Craft

Banda has been a historic center of stone carving excellence for over 500 years, with abundant access to premium quality local stone deposits and a concentrated cluster of skilled hereditary artisans. The district's geological advantage combined with deep cultural knowledge of stone craft techniques makes it the natural epicenter for Shazar stone products.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Banda.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Banda town (50km from Jhansi rail junction) and spend 5-7 days in the traditional stone carving cluster around Nayaganj and Atarra villages. Meet 3-5 established artisan families, observe production techniques, and document the supply chain. Connect with DC Handicraft Banda office for cluster data and current artisan networks.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source premium marble and soapstone blocks from quarries in Rajasthan (Makrana/Kishangarh area, ₹8,000-15,000 per cubic foot) or local Banda limestone suppliers. Negotiate first bulk order of 2-3 tons (₹1.5-2.5L). Buy traditional carving tools from local suppliers in Banda bazaar or Jhansi (₹30,000-50,000 for starter set). Establish relationship with 2-3 primary artisan partners in Banda for production.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 established Banda artisan families through formal craft agreements; avoid employing directly initially. Provide raw stone blocks and design sketches; artisans handle production. Monthly production capacity: 8-15 mid-sized pieces (sculptures, vases, wall panels) or 30-50 smaller jewelry/decor items per artisan partnership. Set up basic workshop space (200-300 sq ft rented studio in Banda town at ₹5,000-8,000/month) for quality checks, finishing, and packaging.
4
Brand & Register
Register business under Udyam portal (MSME classification) within 48 hours. Apply for IEC (Import-Export Code) from DGFT for international sales. Obtain GI certification holder status from DC Handicraft Banda (leverage existing GI tag for marketing). Create brand identity: logo, tagline, packaging design reflecting Banda's heritage. Trademark brand name with IP office. Get ISO 9001 or craft-specific certification for export compliance.
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy (global reach, 6-8% fees), Amazon Handmade India (domestic + export), and Flipkart Artisan (domestic premium). Simultaneously, pitch to 5-10 high-end interior design firms in Delhi/Mumbai with portfolio images. Create Instagram/YouTube presence showcasing artisan process and finished pieces (focus on craftsmanship narrative). Reach out to boutique hotel chains and luxury hospitality brands in Rajasthan/Delhi with bulk order proposals. Target first month revenue: ₹2-3L from 4-6 high-ticket sales.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand artisan partnerships from 3 to 8-10 families across Banda district to increase production. Hire a dedicated sales manager to handle B2B inquiries and corporate contracts. Launch private website with e-commerce capability (₹50,000-80,000 development cost). Explore international wholesale accounts via trade shows (IGTF Mumbai, India International Trade Fair Delhi). Establish quality control team. Consider opening small flagship showroom in premium Delhi/Bengaluru location. Target year-2 revenue: ₹50-75L through mixed B2B/B2C/Export channels.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram Reels & TikTok series: 15-30 second videos showing raw stone transformation into finished art piece, emphasizing skill and heritage. Post 3x weekly. Target hashtags: #BandaStoneArt, #IndianHandicraft, #ArtisanLife. Aim for 50K+ followers within 6 months.
  • LinkedIn B2B campaign targeting Corporate Offices, Hospitality Chains, Architects: 'Art That Elevates Spaces' campaign with case studies of installations in 5-star hotels. Monthly thought leadership posts on craftsmanship-driven sustainability. Direct outreach to 100+ decision-makers.
  • YouTube documentary-style channel: 5-10 minute mini-documentaries featuring individual artisan stories, production process, historical context of Banda stone craft. One feature per fortnight. Embed shopping links. Target 10K subscribers for conversion to sales.
