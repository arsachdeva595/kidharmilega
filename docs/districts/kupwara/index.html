ODOP Product

Walnut

Kupwara's cool alpine climate, well-drained soil, and 600-1800m elevation create ideal conditions for walnut cultivation. The district has a 200+ year tradition of walnut farming integrated with apple cultivation. Local expertise in harvesting and processing ensures consistent quality that commands premium prices in domestic and export markets.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Kupwara.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kupwara district walnut markets in Handwara and Kalaroos during Sept-Oct harvest season. Connect with District Horticulture Department and local farmer cooperative unions like Kupwara Walnut Growers Association. Study pricing at Srinagar's fruit markets and analyze export logistics via Kashmir Agro Products Export Association.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source directly from Kupwara farming clusters (Handwara, Kalaroos, Lolab valleys) or through farmer cooperatives. First batch cost: ₹8-12 per kg for raw walnuts in bulk (50-100 kg minimum). Budget ₹4-6 lakh for initial 500-750 kg inventory including drying, grading, and packaging.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with SFURTI-supported walnut processing units in Srinagar or establish small-scale processing facility with hulling and drying equipment (₹15-25 lakh capex). Alternatively, work with existing J&K walnut exporters on contract basis for first 6 months to validate model.
4
Brand & Register
Register business under Udyam (MSME) portal, obtain IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) from DGFT Srinagar office. Get FSSAI registration for food processing (₹2000-5000). Design brand identity emphasizing 'Kupwara origin' and obtain trademark. Certifications: Organic (if applicable), GMP, Spice Board if processing.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon, Flipkart, Jio Mart with 250gm-1kg premium packs at ₹450-650. Parallel B2B outreach to organic retail chains (Nature's Basket, BigBasket), health food stores via LinkedIn. Create Instagram/YouTube content on walnut health benefits and Kupwara heritage. Partner with 2-3 export agents for Middle East orders.
6
Scale
After ₹20L+ revenue, introduce walnut butter and roasted variants. Expand distribution to 50+ pin codes via logistics partnerships. Secure Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana funding for processing facility upgrade. Target 10-15 major B2B institutional clients (corporate gifting, nutraceutical brands).

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts showing walnut harvesting in Kupwara orchards, health benefits, and recipe usage targeting 25-40 affluent urban audience. Partner with micro-influencers in wellness niche (50K-500K followers).
  • B2B LinkedIn campaign targeting nutritionists, gym chains, corporate wellness programs with bulk pricing and health reports. Use webinars on heart health and brain function benefits.
  • Amazon A+ content with lifestyle images, certification badges, customer testimonials, and comparison with competitor walnuts. Run PPC campaigns targeting 'dry fruits online' and 'organic walnuts' keywords at ₹15-20 per click.
