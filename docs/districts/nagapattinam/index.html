ODOP Product

Marine food

Nagapattinam's strategic location on the Coromandel Coast with direct access to the Bay of Bengal provides consistent, high-quality seafood sourcing. The district has established fishing infrastructure, skilled artisanal fishermen, and decades of marine export experience. Cool climate and proximity to major ports make it ideal for processing, cold-storage, and rapid export of perishable marine products.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Nagapattinam.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Nagapattinam fishing harbor (Nagapattinam Port), meet fishermen co-operatives at Nagapattinam Fishing Cooperative Society, study cold-storage facilities at East Coast Road. Attend Tamil Nadu Fisheries Department trade fairs. Cost: ₹5,000-10,000 for 5-day research trip.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source directly from Nagapattinam fishing auctions (morning 4-7 AM) or registered fish suppliers like Nagapattinam Seafood Exporters Association members. Initial batch cost for mixed seafood (100 kg): ₹40,000-60,000. Establish credit terms with 2-3 consistent suppliers.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with certified processing units under FSSAI at Nagapattinam Industrial Estate or Vedaranyam nearby. Alternatively, rent cold-storage space (₹3,000-5,000/month for 500 kg capacity) and hire trained handlers. Ensure IPC (Aquaculture Practices Certificate) compliance.
4
Brand & Register
Register UDYAM (micro or small enterprise on udyamregistration.gov.in). Apply for IEC (Import-Export Code) via ICEGATE portal. Obtain FSSAI Food Business License (₹100-500 based on turnover). Trademark brand name with IP India. Get BIS certification if exporting.
5
First Sale
Launch on Blinkit and Swiggy Instamart for urban D2C. Create WhatsApp Business catalog targeting restaurant owners via local business groups. Pitch to Reliance Fresh, BigBasket wholesale. Contact seafood exporters via LinkedIn and attend Seafood Exporters Association of India (SEAI) meetings in Chennai.
6
Scale
Post ₹10L revenue, open 2nd cold-storage unit in Vedaranyam (30 km away). Build export partnerships with 2-3 international buyers via TradeKey or Global Sources. Launch value-added products (frozen breaded prawns, marinated crabs). Hire supply chain manager. Expand to Tier-2 cities via modern retail partnerships.

  • WhatsApp bulk messaging to 500+ restaurant owners in Chennai, Bangalore offering bulk wholesale rates with 5% early-adopter discount. Partner with restaurant aggregators like Dineout.
