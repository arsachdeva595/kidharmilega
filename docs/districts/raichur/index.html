Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Raichur.

1
Learn / Research
Visit established pharma clusters in Raichur (near Lingasugur industrial area) and Bangalore; connect with Raichur Chamber of Commerce; study GMP requirements from PCI (Pharmaceutical Standards Commission of India); attend CPSE pharma seminars.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source APIs from Gujarat (Ahmedabad, Vadodara) or Hyderabad suppliers; excipients from Mumbai or Bangalore vendors; packaging from Bangalore/Pune manufacturers. First batch raw material cost: ₹15-25 lakhs depending on product type.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing GMP-certified contract manufacturers in Raichur or Bangalore initially; establish in-house facility only after securing initial orders; engage with FSSAI/DCGI-approved manufacturers for formulation development.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) status immediately; obtain IEC code from customs; acquire FSSAI registration for formulations; obtain manufacturing license from Karnataka State Drugs Authority; file for WHO-GMP certification; register trademark with IPO.
5
First Sale
Approach wholesale distribution networks through pharma distributors in Raichur and neighboring districts; list on B2B platforms (Pharmazone, Tradeindia); connect with hospital procurement departments; engage with government health schemes (PMJAY, state health programs).
6
Scale
Expand product portfolio to high-demand therapeutic categories; pursue ISO/WHO-GMP certifications for international markets; establish export network with regulatory compliance for target countries; invest in R&D for differentiated formulations; develop institutional relationships with large hospital chains.
