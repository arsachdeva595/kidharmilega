Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Khairthal-Tijara.

1
Learn / Research
Visit the Khairthal-Tijara automotive cluster; meet existing manufacturers in Tijara Industrial Area; attend auto component exhibitions like SIAE (Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers) events in Delhi/Gurgaon; study ISO/TS 16949 quality standards; connect with SIAM member companies.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from Jabalpur steel suppliers and Delhi-NCR aluminum foundries; typical first-batch cost ₹8-12 lakhs for 500-unit trial production; negotiate bulk discounts with suppliers like SAIL, Tata Steel; verify material certifications for automotive-grade specs.
3
Produce / Partner
Establish manufacturing unit in Tijara Industrial Area (land lease ₹50-100 per sq ft); procure CNC machining and casting equipment (₹30-50L for entry-level setup); hire experienced production engineers from existing cluster units; implement lean manufacturing practices; obtain ISO 9001 certification within 6 months.
4
Brand & Register
Register on Udyam portal as medium enterprise; obtain IEC code from DGFT for export eligibility; register business as Pvt Ltd for B2B credibility; obtain ISO/TS 16949 certification (mandatory for OEM supply); trademark brand name with IP office.
5
First Sale
Contact tier-1 suppliers (Bosch, Delphi, ZF) through industry directories; list on ThomasNet and Global Sources for international leads; attend Auto Expo and SIAE trade shows; pitch to fleet operators through LinkedIn B2B outreach; secure first PO from regional OEM partners targeting ₹50L+ annual orders.
6
Scale
Achieve 60% capacity utilization within 6 months; reinvest 40% of profits into advanced machinery (automation CNC, testing labs); hire quality assurance team; expand product line to 8-10 component variants; establish quality partnerships with international certification bodies; target 3-5 new OEM contracts by month 12.
