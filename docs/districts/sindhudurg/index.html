Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Sindhudurg.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Vengurla and nearby Malvan towns (15km from Sindhudurg district HQ). Meet 10-15 established processors at VCCPL (Vengurla Cashew Cluster Processing Limited), visit auction yards, and study GI compliance standards. Attend Maharashtra State Warehouse and Logistics Corporation meetings.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw cashew nuts from Kerala suppliers (Kannur district) at ₹450-550 per kg wholesale. Negotiate first-batch order of 5000kg (₹25-27L). Packaging materials from Pune-based vendors (cartons ₹2-4 per unit). Total first-batch investment: ₹28-32L including processing charges.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing VCCPL-registered processor units on revenue-sharing (60-70% to processor, 30-40% margin for you) OR lease 500-1000 sq.ft. space in Vengurla industrial area (₹30-50K/month) and hire 4-6 skilled sorters/packers (₹8-12K monthly each). Processing timeline: 15-20 days per batch.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (proprietorship/MSME), obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Panaji (20km away). Apply for GI-tagged product certificate through district office. Register brand name with Trademark Registry (Delhi). Get FSSAI food safety license (₹2000-5000). Labeling must display GI mark, origin district, processing date, and allergen info.
5
First Sale
List on Amazon, Flipkart, BigBasket, and Blinkit with premium positioning (₹450-600 per 250g). Approach 50-100 local retail chains in Maharashtra (Modern Bazaar, Paunar). Direct B2B emails to top 30 US/EU importers via TradeKey. Send samples (5kg) to 10 corporate gifting companies (Giftxoxo, Arpit Group) and top 20 5-star hotels in Mumbai/Pune.
6
Scale
After 6 months: increase production to 20,000 kg/month, hire brand manager, launch Instagram/YouTube with farm-to-table content. Approach Patanjali, ITC, Taj Hotels for bulk contracts. Apply for SFURTI cluster grant for machinery upgrades. Expand to roasted/flavored variants. Aim for ₹3-5Cr revenue by month 18.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • YouTube channel: 'From Vengurla to Table' — 2-min videos showing sorting, roasting, packaging process. Target health/gifting keywords. Cost: ₹50K/month for 2 videos. Expected reach: 50K-100K monthly views by month 6.
  • Instagram Reels + TikTok: User-generated content from customers unboxing premium packs, lifestyle imagery. Partner with 20 micro-influencers (50K-200K followers) in Mumbai/Bangalore for gifting season. Budget: ₹3-5L/season.
  • B2B LinkedIn outreach: Targeted campaigns to US/EU import companies, hotel procurement teams, corporate gifting managers. Case study: 'How Vengurla GI Cashews Became UAE's #1 Premium Snack.' Cost: ₹1-2L/quarter.
Government Schemes

Free money you didn't know about

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (35% for general, 10% interest on loan) for food processing MSME in rural Sindhudurg
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹10L for cluster-level infrastructure, machinery, training in Vengurla cashew processing zone
🏚

15% subsidy on capex for export-oriented cashew units, priority water/electricity allotment
🏚

Interest subsidy (2-3%) on MUDRA loans up to ₹10L for food processing entrepreneurs in Sindhudurg district
