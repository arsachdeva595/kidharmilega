ODOP Product

Oil - Agar

North Tripura's tropical climate and rich forest ecosystem naturally sustain thriving agar tree plantations across thousands of hectares. The district has developed deep expertise in traditional agarwood distillation, with multi-generational distillers perfecting extraction techniques. Local communities have established established supply chains and market networks spanning Middle East, Europe, and Southeast Asia.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for North Tripura.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Agartala and Khumulwng (North Tripura's agar cluster) to meet 15-20 established distilleries; interview producers at Udaipur agarwood market; study buyer networks at Medziphema (Nagaland) agar trading hub; spend 2-3 weeks understanding grading and distillation methods.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source Grade-A agar wood chips from North Tripura Forest Department certified suppliers or local farmers in Khumulwng at ₹8,000-12,000 per kg; negotiate 50kg starter batch (₹4-6L); establish direct contracts with 3-4 plantation owners; ensure FSC certification documentation.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing Agartala-based micro-distilleries (rent their equipment 4-6 hours/day at ₹2,000-5,000/batch) or invest in a 5-liter copper still setup (₹150K-300K); learn traditional steam distillation over 2-3 months through apprenticeship; target 10-15ml output per kg of wood (highest quality grades).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME Classification); apply for IEC code from DGFT Agartala; file trademark for brand name at Kolkata IP Office; obtain Food Safety License from North Tripura FSSAI; get Export Certificate from Tripura Spices & Aromatic Board; create GMP-certified packaging labels.
5
First Sale
Launch on Nykaa Luxe and Hyphen Beauty (luxury agar oil category) with ₹5,000+ per 5ml positioning; build WhatsApp B2B catalog targeting UAE fragrance traders via LinkedIn; email outreach to 200+ French and Japanese fragrance houses; partner with 2-3 luxury gift retailers in Delhi/Mumbai; create Instagram content showcasing distillation process (@AgarStories format).
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to 5-10kg monthly production; build direct relationships with 10+ wholesale buyers in Middle East; launch signature blends (pure agar + floral notes at ₹8,000/5ml); apply for SFURTI cluster development grant to upgrade distillery; develop eco-certified 'wild-harvest' positioning for sustainability premium.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Create Instagram Reels (weekly) showcasing live distillation process, wood grading, and aroma testing; partner with 5-10 fragrance influencers (10K-100K followers) in luxury niche for unboxing and testing content; target hashtags #OudLovers #FragranceCommunity.
  • Build B2B LinkedIn strategy: send personalized case studies to 50+ fragrance houses monthly showing batch consistency, extraction yields, and certifications; join fragrance trade groups (Fragbits, InternationalFragranceAssociation); sponsor fragrance webinars.
  • Partner with 3-5 luxury gift retailers (Fabindia, Good Earth, Artisans' Collective) for in-store placement; create sampling program for high-end perfumeries in Delhi/Mumbai/Bangalore; sponsor luxury lifestyle events (Art, Design, Wellness festivals).
Government Schemes

Free money you didn't know about

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (25% for general, 35% for SC/ST) for distillery setup and working capital.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹10-50L for cluster development, equipment upgrades, and collective branding in North Tripura agar zone.
🏚
Tripura Agro-Processing Policy
5% interest subsidy on MUDRA/bank loans up to ₹50L for food processing units including agar oil distilleries.
🏚
Tripura Export Promotion Scheme
5-10% export incentive on FOB value for agriculture/food products; logistics subsidy for shipments to international ports.
