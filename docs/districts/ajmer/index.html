Granite and Marble Products

Ajmer district has rich granite deposits with unique color variations (pink, brown, yellow) and exceptional hardness making it ideal for premium construction. The region has established quarrying infrastructure, skilled workforce, and proximity to major markets in Delhi-NCR and Gujarat. Historical expertise in stone extraction and processing has created a competitive advantage in quality and cost-efficiency.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Ajmer.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Ajmer Marble and Granite Association in Ajmer city; study quarries around Khimsar and Deshpoke areas; attend annual Stone and Ceramic India Expo in Bangalore; connect with 20+ established suppliers in Khimsar cluster.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source granite blocks from Ajmer quarries (₹3-5 lakh per truckload for initial 5 truck orders); establish supplier relationships with quarry owners near Khimsar; negotiate 30-45 day credit terms; budget ₹15-20 lakh for first batch raw materials.
3
Produce / Partner
Lease or purchase 2-3 acres near Khimsar industrial area (₹5-8 lakh per acre annually); invest in CNC cutting machines (₹20-40 lakh), polishing units (₹10-15 lakh); hire 8-12 skilled cutters and polishers from local workforce; establish partnerships with 3-4 local master artisans.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam portal for MSME recognition; obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Jaipur; register business under GST with Ajmer tax authority; register trademark with IP Office; get stone testing certification from relevant lab.
5
First Sale
List on B2B platforms (IndiaMART, TradeKey) with detailed product photos and certifications; contact 20-30 top real estate developers directly via LinkedIn; approach 15-20 architects in Delhi-NCR, Mumbai, Bangalore; participate in Construction and Building Materials Trade Fairs; establish WhatsApp business account with product catalog.
6
Scale
Establish second processing unit with higher automation; hire export-focused business development manager; build relationships with 5-7 international distributors; invest in digital marketing (Google Ads for 'marble suppliers India'); expand product range to include specialty finishes; achieve ₹5-10 Cr annual revenue by month 24.
