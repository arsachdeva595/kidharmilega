Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Tenkasi.

1
Learn / Research
Visit established coir pith manufacturers in Tenkasi district (Nagercoil-Tirunelveli clusters) to understand production processes, quality standards, and market dynamics. Connect with Tamil Nadu Coir Board and local SIDCO coir parks.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source coconut husks from local coconut processors in Tenkasi, Nagercoil, and surrounding areas at ₹2000-3000 per ton. Establish relationships with 5-10 local suppliers for consistent raw material flow. First batch: ₹80,000-120,000 for 30-40 tons processing capacity.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing coir processing units in SIDCO parks or establish small processing facility (1000-2000 sq ft) with basic equipment: husk shredders, drying beds, hydraulic press, sieving screens. Alternatively, contract with established coir pith manufacturers for white-label production initially.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME through udyamregistration.gov.in (Handicraft classification). Obtain IEC from ICER portal for exports. Secure BIS certification (IS 17064 for coir pith) essential for exports. Register business name and GST within Tenkasi district.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Business, IndiaMART, and TradeKey for B2B sales. Create DTC presence via Shopify/WooCommerce targeting gardening enthusiasts. Contact nursery associations in Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, and Telangana for bulk orders. Reach out to European distributors through GlobalGAP certification pathway.
6
Scale
After ₹10L revenue, invest in ISO 9001 & organic certification for premium positioning. Expand to Southeast Asia via Singapore distributors. Develop value-added products (pre-hydrated, nutrient-enriched blocks). Hire 2-3 skilled staff and establish 3000-5000 sq ft production facility with better machinery.
