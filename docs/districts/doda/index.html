Natural Oils (Lavender)

Doda's elevation (1,500-2,500m), cool climate, and mineral-rich soil create ideal conditions for lavender cultivation with superior essential oil yield. The region's pristine environment and minimal pesticide use result in organic-grade lavender oil that commands premium prices in international wellness markets. Local farming communities have generations of experience in high-altitude crop cultivation.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Doda.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Doda district and connect with existing lavender cultivators in Marwah and Ganote villages; study cultivation practices at local agricultural extension centers; analyze competitors in Himachal's Lavender Valley near Solan; join aromatherapy trade forums like IAHS (Indian Aromatherapy & Herbal Society)
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fresh lavender flowers from Doda farmers (June-August harvest season) at ₹80-120 per kg; identify distillation equipment suppliers in Delhi/Bangalore (₹2-5L for small setup); budget ₹8-12L for first 500kg batch processing; establish farmer partnerships for year-round dried lavender supply
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing essential oil distillers in Doda or set up micro-distillery with 50L capacity steam distiller; engage with NABARD-backed agricultural producer organizations for supply chain support; obtain certification from APEDA for export quality; start with contract manufacturing at established Himachal-based units while building own capacity
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) enterprise online; obtain IEC code from DGFT for export eligibility; apply for Spice Board/APEDA recognition for organic certification; register brand name and logo at CGPDTM; get GMP or ISO 22000 certification from third-party labs in Delhi/Bangalore for premium positioning
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Handmade India and Flipkart AYUSH category with premium positioning; partner with wellness e-commerce platforms (UrbanMeisters, Pluckk, Vitros); approach 50+ organic beauty retailers and spa chains directly; create B2B profile on TradeKey and Global Sources for bulk export inquiries
6
Scale
Establish 5-10 farmer partnerships in Doda for sustainable supply; develop 3-5 derivative products (lavender soap, pillow spray, bath oil blends); hire local distillery staff and implement quality lab testing; target 15+ international distributors in UK, EU, Australia; apply for export incentives under MEIS/RODTEP schemes
