ODOP Product

Orange - (Tamenglong Mandarin)

Tamenglong's elevated terrain, rich volcanic soil, and cool hill climate create ideal conditions for high-quality mandarin cultivation. The district's 2,000+ hectares of established orange orchards and 50+ years of farming heritage make it uniquely positioned. Local farmers have perfected micro-climate management and organic practices that produce superior fruit quality and natural sweetness.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Tamenglong.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Tamenglong district office and connect with 20+ farmers in Hangrum, Nungba villages; study Manipur Agriculture Department's orange cluster data; visit Langthabal Mandi in Imphal to understand wholesale pricing and buyer behavior; attend APEDA webinars on citrus export standards
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified orange saplings from ICAR-NRC for Citrus (Nagpur) or Manipur Horticulture Department (₹8-12 per sapling, ₹50,000-80,000 for 5,000-8,000 saplings for 2-3 hectares); negotiate bulk rates with local organic fertilizer suppliers in Imphal (₹8,000-12,000 per MT)
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 5-10 established farmers in Tamenglong via cooperative model or contract farming; invest in shared cold storage facility (₹20-30L for 100-ton capacity); implement GI-compliant post-harvest handling per Tamenglong Orange Production Manual; hire local farm managers at ₹12,000-18,000/month
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam as agricultural business; obtain IEC code from nearest customs office (Imphal); register brand name with IP office; obtain FPO (Farmer Producer Organization) status for export eligibility; file GI sub-license application with Controller of GI at Chennai
5
First Sale
Launch on JioMart Fresh and BigBasket with ₹2-3L minimum order commitments; create Instagram + YouTube content around orchard practices and GI heritage; list on Tasty Harvest and AgroStar B2B platforms; approach Fruit & Vegetable Importers' Association in Singapore; partner with Manipur Horticulture Export Board for APEDA-linked buyers
6
Scale
Expand orchard area to 10-15 hectares via government land leasing schemes; build dedicated cold chain logistics (₹40-50L capex); launch regional online brand presence (Flipkart Fresh, local players); explore value-added processing unit for juice/concentrate; target FMCG distribution in Tier 2 cities by month 18
