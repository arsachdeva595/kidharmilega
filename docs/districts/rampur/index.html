ODOP Product

Applique Work along with Patch Work,Zari Patch work, and Mentha

Rampur has a 400+ year legacy of fine textile embroidery inherited from Mughal court traditions, with established artisan communities in Rampur city and surrounding villages. The district's climate supports mentha cultivation, creating a dual-product ecosystem unique in India. Proximity to Delhi and established export networks make it ideal for scaling traditional crafts.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Rampur.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Rampur city appliqué clusters in Sadar Bazaar and Milak areas; meet 3-5 master artisans; study production techniques at local craft centers. Spend 2-3 days observing mentha cultivation in villages like Aonla and Syan. Cost: ₹5,000-10,000.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source base fabrics from Rampur textile wholesalers (₹80-150/meter for cotton) and Chandpol Delhi for zari threads (₹200-400/spool). Mentha seeds from Kannauj cooperative suppliers (₹500/kg). First batch of 50 cushion covers + 5L mentha oil: ₹60,000-80,000.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3-5 established artisans in Rampur city through local craft associations (Rampur Handicraft Cooperative, contact DC Handicraft office). Provide design briefs, fabric, and threads; pay per piece (₹400-800 per cushion). For mentha oil, lease 0.5-1 acre in Aonla village and contract with local distillation units (₹30,000-50,000/season).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online at udyamregistration.gov.in) under Handicraft; obtain IEC from nearest DGFT office for export eligibility. Apply for GI-tagged artisan certificate through DC Handicraft Rampur. Create brand identity with Rampur heritage narrative; file trademark at IP office.
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy (global audience for handcrafts), Amazon Handmade, and your WordPress e-commerce site. Target 50-100 first sales via Instagram/Pinterest content showcasing artisan stories. Approach 10-15 boutique interior design studios in Delhi-NCR for B2B placement. Reach pharmaceutical distributors for mentha oil (contact Kannauj mentha industry associations).
6
Scale
After ₹15-20L revenue: hire 10-15 artisans, open small production unit (500 sq ft) in Rampur, expand online presence to Shopify + LinkedIn B2B. Partner with 2-3 export agents for USA/EU markets. Launch corporate gifting division and conduct 4-6 artisan workshops annually for experiential tourism revenue.
