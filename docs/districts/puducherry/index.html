Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Puducherry.

1
Learn / Research
Research Puducherry's existing pharma clusters near Thattanchavady and Kalapet industrial areas. Visit 3-4 operational pharma units to understand GMP compliance, regulatory requirements (WHO-GMP, ISO certifications). Connect with Puducherry Industries Department and Confederation of Indian Industry (CII) Puducherry chapter for industry insights. Study FSSAI, DCGI, and state pharmaceutical licensing processes.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source APIs and excipients from Gujarat API suppliers (Ahmedabad, Vadodara clusters) or direct importers in Mumbai. First batch cost for contract manufacturing setup: ₹15-25L for raw materials. Identify local suppliers for packaging materials (bottles, blister packs) near Puducherry port. Establish accounts with 2-3 certified vendors; negotiate payment terms for 30-45 days credit.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing CDMO (Contract Development and Manufacturing Organization) units in Puducherry like those near Thattanchavady, or establish own facility with GMP-certified manufacturing space (2000-5000 sq ft). Hire experienced QA/QC personnel and production supervisors. Install quality testing equipment (HPLC, dissolution apparatus). Apply for manufacturing license from Puducherry Directorate of Health & Family Welfare.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) online immediately. Obtain IEC code from DGFT for export eligibility. Register brand name with IP office. Apply for WHO-GMP or ISO 13485 certification (3-6 months process, ₹10-15L cost). Obtain Drugs License from state authorities. Set up quality certifications: ISO 9001, ISO 14001, OHSAS 18001. Create product compliance documentation and SOPs.
5
First Sale
Launch on B2B platforms: Tradeindia, IndiaMART (pharmaceutical section). Connect directly with hospital procurement departments and pharmaceutical distributors through LinkedIn and trade calls. Participate in pharmaceutical trade shows: Indian Pharmaceutical Congress, CPhI India (Mumbai). Offer samples to 50-100 target pharma companies. Negotiate first bulk orders of ₹10-30L. Use email and WhatsApp for follow-ups with hospital chains.
6
Scale
After reaching ₹5Cr revenue, expand production capacity by 40-50%. Develop 2-3 proprietary formulations or specialty chemicals. Apply for export certifications (FDA, EMA if targeting those markets). Establish distribution partnerships in 5-6 states. Launch direct D2C nutraceutical brand leveraging manufacturing expertise. Hire business development team focused on export markets.
