Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for West Godavari District.

1
Learn / Research
Visit seafood processing clusters at Kakinada Port and Machilipatnam; attend meetings with AP Fisheries Department; study export certifications (HACCP, FSSAI, IEC requirements) and observe 3-5 operational units near Yanam
2
Source Raw Materials
Partner with local fishing communities and FPOs in Kakinada, Machilipatnam, or Narasapuram for raw supply; budget ₹5-8L for first batch of 10-15 tons fresh prawns; negotiate monthly supply contracts at market rates (₹150-250/kg)
3
Produce / Partner
Lease or co-process at existing HACCP-certified units in Kakinada Industrial Estate (₹2-4L/month); hire experienced cleaning, cooking, and freezing staff; establish quality control with trained HACCP auditors; start with 2-5 ton daily capacity
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Manufacturing) online; apply for IEC code via dgft.gov.in; obtain FSSAI food safety license; register with APEDA for export benefits; create brand identity and register trademark; obtain AP Pollution Control Board clearance
5
First Sale
Launch on IndiaMART and TradeKey for B2B buyers; pitch to HoReCa clients via direct sales; contact seafood exporters in Vishakhapatnam; list on BigBasket and Amazon Fresh for D2C; attend Seafood Expo Global for international buyers
6
Scale
Secure Grade A or Grade B certification; increase processing capacity to 10+ tons/day; develop 3-4 value-added SKUs; build distribution partnerships with major retailers; establish cold storage facilities; apply for export subsidies through APEDA
