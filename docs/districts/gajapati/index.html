ODOP Product

Cashew Kernel

Gajapati district has ideal agro-climatic conditions with consistent rainfall and well-drained laterite soils suited for cashew cultivation. The district has over 45,000 hectares under cashew farming with established supply chains and traditional processing expertise passed through generations. Proximity to port infrastructure at Visakhapatnam (Andhra Pradesh) facilitates seamless export of processed kernels globally.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Gajapati.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Gajapati district cashew clusters in Odisha (Gosani, Kukudpur, Nendaliput areas) and spend 2-3 weeks understanding processing techniques. Connect with Gajapati Cashew Processors Association and visit processing units. Study quality grading standards (W240, W320, W450) and food safety certifications (FSSAI, ISO 22000). Budget: ₹30,000-50,000 for travel and research.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw cashew nuts (RCN) from local farmers' cooperatives in Gajapati or established RCN traders in Anacardium markets. Initial batch of 1,000 kg RCN costs ₹4-5 lakhs depending on quality grade. Partner with Gajapati Agricultural Cooperative Society or work with local mandi traders for consistent supply arrangements.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing FSSAI-certified cashew processing units in Gajapati (Paralakhemundi or Gossani clusters) under job-work model initially—no capital investment needed. Alternatively, set up small processing unit (roasting, grading, packing) with ₹20-25 lakhs investment in equipment (roaster, sorter, packaging machine). Hire 8-10 trained workers familiar with cashew processing standards.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) classification within 2 weeks on udyamregistration.gov.in. Apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) from DGFT if exporting. Register with FSSAI (Food License) costing ₹3,000-5,000. File trademark for brand name (₹10,000-15,000). Obtain required licenses from Gajapati District Administration for food processing.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon, Flipkart, Blinkit, Swiggy Instamart with MOQ of 50-100 units per SKU. Target wholesale customers through Udaan and Globalbuy B2B platforms. Reach out to 100+ retailers in 5 major cities (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai) with samples and 40% distributor margins. Create Instagram and Facebook pages showcasing farm-to-table journey. Initial sales target: ₹5-10 lakhs in Month 1-2.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand distribution to 500+ retail points across 10+ cities. Develop premium sub-brands (salted, roasted spiced, chocolate-coated variants). Export to 3-5 countries via food exporters or direct B2B channels—target ₹5 lakhs/month export revenue by Month 12. Build institutional sales with corporate gifting and bulk orders. Invest in co-packing partnerships to increase production to 50+ tons/month.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Partner with fitness micro-influencers (10K-100K followers) on Instagram and YouTube demonstrating cashew kernels as protein-rich post-workout snacks. Create Reels showing farm-to-table processing journey from Gajapati. Budget: ₹2-3 lakhs/month for 20-30 influencer collaborations.
  • Run targeted Google Shopping and Facebook Ads in metros (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore) targeting health-conscious professionals aged 25-45 with keywords like 'premium cashew kernels,' 'organic dry fruits,' 'protein snacks.' Budget: ₹1.5-2 lakhs/month for sustained ROAS of 3-4x.
  • Execute experiential sampling campaigns in 50+ high-end supermarkets (Blinkit stores, Big Bazaar premium sections) across metros and Tier-2 cities. Partner with 5-10 premium coffee chains (Starbucks, Café Coffee Day) to include cashew kernels in gift hampers. Budget: ₹3-4 lakhs/quarter.
