ODOP Product

Dharmavaram Silk Sarees

Ananthapuram has a centuries-old tradition of silk weaving concentrated in the Dharmavaram cluster. The region's skilled artisan base, access to quality silk yarn from Karnataka, and established supply chains for zari and dyes make it the natural epicenter for this craft. The GI status strengthens market authenticity and premium positioning.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Ananthpuram (Anantapur).

1
Learn / Research
Visit Dharmavaram cluster in Ananthapuram (80km from city); spend 3-5 days with master weavers and traders at Sri Sai Silk House, Dharmavaram Silk Cooperative Society; attend Ananthapuram District Handloom Cluster meetings; analyze competitor sarees and pricing on local markets and online.
2
Source Raw Materials
Partner with Dharmavaram Silk Cooperative (bulk silk yarn supply, ₹50,000-100,000 first batch) or individual suppliers like Geetha Silk Mills; procure zari from Kolhapur or Surat suppliers (₹20,000-40,000); sample weaving cost ₹2,000-5,000 per saree with local artisans; negotiate a 3-month payment cycle.
3
Produce / Partner
Establish direct tie-ups with 3-5 master weavers in Dharmavaram for initial 50-100 saree batches; visit looms weekly; provide designs and quality specs; consider renting a small workspace (₹5,000/month) in Ananthapuram city for finishing, packaging, and photography; hire 1 quality inspector.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) online at udyamregistration.gov.in; apply for IEC code via DGFT portal; trademark brand name with IP Office; obtain GI usage certificate from Handloom Ministry or District Collector; GST registration (mandatory for ₹40L+ turnover).
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Handmade, Flipkart Sarees, Meesho, and own Shopify store; create Instagram/Pinterest content (50+ high-quality saree photos); collaborate with 10-15 ethnic wear micro-retailers in tier-2 cities; pitch to wedding exhibition organizers; send sample sarees to fashion bloggers and bridal consultants; run Instagram Ads targeting wedding season (May-Oct, Feb-Mar) with ₹500/day budget.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand design portfolio (20+ new designs); hire 2-3 in-house finishing staff; open a showroom pop-up in Bangalore/Hyderabad tier-1 mall; launch B2B wholesale tier (₹50K minimum orders); partner with 5-10 online boutiques; run Google Shopping and YouTube ads; explore corporate gifting and bulk custom orders.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Partner with 20 wedding bloggers/Instagram influencers (10K-100K followers) in tier-2 cities for UGC saree styling posts; offer ₹3,000-5,000 gift per influencer; track code and affiliate links.
  • Run Pinterest campaign targeting 'silk sarees wedding', 'traditional sarees', 'ethnic wear'; use Rich Pins; invest ₹1,000/month; aim for 5% CTR to website.
  • Host virtual Zoom sessions with NRI customer groups monthly; showcasing saree draping, weaver stories, GI certification; offer ₹500 discount codes for attendees.
