Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Jhansi.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Jhansi's toy cluster in Sadar Bazar and Govind Nagar; meet 3-5 established soft toy manufacturers; attend Delhi Toy Fair (Jan); study competitor pricing on Flipkart & Amazon; research import duties for export markets.
2
Source Raw Materials
Contact textile suppliers in Jhansi (Sadar Bazar) for polycloth & nylax; order from Surat-based fiber/stuffing wholesalers; first batch of 500-1000 units: ₹60,000-80,000; establish 30-day payment terms with suppliers.
3
Produce / Partner
Either set up basic in-house production (sewing machines, cutting tables in 500 sq ft space) or partner with 2-3 existing Jhansi artisan groups for order-based manufacturing; start with outsourced production to minimize capex; hire quality auditor part-time.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME online (₹0); obtain IEC code from Directorate General of Foreign Trade (₹0); trademark brand name via IP India (₹9,000-15,000); get ISO 9001 certification (₹30,000); register on GeM portal for B2B orders.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Seller Central & Flipkart with 10-15 SKUs; approach Delhi toy wholesalers (Sadar Bazar, Nai Sarak) with bulk discounts; pitch to corporate gifting agencies via LinkedIn; list on Meesho for reseller network; approach kindergarten & play schools directly.
6
Scale
After 6 months: introduce licensed character designs; hire sales rep for B2B; launch own D2C website; expand to 2-3 new metros via local distributors; apply for PM Vishwakarma or SFURTI cluster support for production upgrades.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram Reels & TikTok: unboxing videos, kid testimonials, behind-the-scenes making process; partner with 10 micro-influencers (100K followers) in parenting/toys niche for UGC content.
  • B2B email campaigns to 500+ toy retailers & gift shops with bulk pricing, seasonal promotions, and MOQ details; attend National Toy Fair, Delhi Toy Fair, and regional trade shows with product booth.
  • WhatsApp Business API for direct seller outreach to retailers; run Google Shopping ads targeting 'soft toys for kids' in top 10 metros with 20% discount on first order.
