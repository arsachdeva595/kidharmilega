ODOP Product

Stone carved products

Kurnool district is naturally endowed with abundant granite, limestone, and sandstone quarries. The region has a 400+ year-old artisan community skilled in stone carving, with established supply chains and local expertise. The district's proximity to raw material sources and low operational costs make it ideal for scaling stone craft production.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Kurnool.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kurnool stone carving cluster near Oravakallu and Koilkunta villages; meet 5-10 established artisans; study products at local cooperative marts; attend Hyderabad craft expos; research competitor pricing on Etsy and 1stDibs.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw stone blocks from Kurnool Granite Association quarries (₹2-5 per kg bulk); negotiate first batch of 500kg mixed blocks for ₹8,000-12,000; establish accounts with local tool suppliers (chisels ₹200-500 each); budget ₹40,000-50,000 for initial materials and tools.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3-5 certified artisans in Kurnool via local cooperative networks; establish a small workshop (500 sq ft) near artisan clusters for ₹5,000-8,000/month; create design templates and quality standards; start with 20-30 pieces/month and scale to 100+ by month 6.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, free); apply for IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Vijayawada; trademark brand name with IP India; create GST registration (12% applicable); develop brand identity emphasizing heritage and handcrafted authenticity.
5
First Sale
Launch Etsy shop with 30+ product photos (invest ₹15,000 in professional photography); list on Amazon Handmade India; create Instagram account with artisan backstory content; reach out to 50 interior designers via LinkedIn; send samples to 10 luxury hotel chains; use WhatsApp Business for B2B inquiries.
6
Scale
After 6 months, hire a logistics partner for export packaging; expand product range (fountains, planters, tiles); establish B2B partnerships with 5-10 online platforms; invest in custom mold-making for repeat designs; open showroom in Hyderabad or Bangalore; apply for export subsidies and RFID labeling support.

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Create 15-second Instagram Reels showing artisan hands carving stone in real-time; post 3x weekly with hashtags #KurnoolCrafts #StoneCarving #HandmadeIndia; collaborate with 5 interior design micro-influencers (50k-200k followers) for product features.
  • Launch email campaign targeting 1,000 architects and interior designers quarterly; share design inspiration, artisan stories, and exclusive pre-orders; offer 10% early-bird discounts; track open rates and refine messaging.
  • Sponsor booth at 2 major Indian craft fairs annually (Amar Jyoti Crafts Fair in New Delhi, Hyderabad Craft Week); offer live carving demonstrations; collect 500+ qualified leads; follow up with personalized product catalogs.
