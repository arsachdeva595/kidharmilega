ODOP Product

Turmeric

Tseminyu district's fertile volcanic hill soils, temperate climate with optimal monsoon patterns, and altitude between 900-1500m create ideal conditions for turmeric cultivation with superior curcumin levels. The region's traditional agricultural practices and minimal chemical intervention preserve the spice's natural potency and aroma, making it distinctly prized in both domestic and international markets.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Tseminyu.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Tseminyu turmeric farms around Zonheboto and Satakha villages; connect with Nagaland Agricultural Department and local farming co-operatives; study curcumin testing labs in Nagpur and Cochin; research existing exporters like Everest Spices and MDH in Delhi
2
Source Raw Materials
Source seed rhizomes directly from Tseminyu farmers (₹3-5 lakhs for 5-10 acres first batch); establish relationships with 3-4 local farmers; minimum first-year procurement: 8-12 tons fresh turmeric; negotiate price: ₹4500-6500/quintal depending on curcumin level
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing turmeric processing units in Tseminyu or hire local processors for drying, polishing, grinding; or set up basic processing unit (drying beds, polishing machine, grinder) within 3 months; train 4-6 local workers in quality control and food safety standards
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, ₹0 cost); apply for IEC code (Directorate General of Foreign Trade, Kolkata office, ₹0 cost); get FSSAI license for food processing (₹10000-20000); obtain organic certification from APEDA-accredited certifier (₹40000-80000); trademark brand name with IP office (₹25000-35000)
5
First Sale
List on Amazon Fresh & Spice Focused channels (10-12% commission), Udaan B2B platform (5-8% commission), direct outreach to 20-30 ayurvedic companies via LinkedIn; launch minimal D2C website (Shopify, ₹2000/month); participate in Aroma India Expo (Delhi, Feb-Mar) and Spice Fairs in Cochin; approach premium retailers like Nature's Basket and Organic stores in metros
6
Scale
After 6 months, invest ₹15-25 lakhs in automated processing unit; build brand presence through food bloggers and health influencers on Instagram (5000-10000 follower accounts); expand to packaged turmeric capsules and milk powder blends; establish export linkages via APEDA registered agents; explore FMCG partnership for distribution in 200+ retail stores across North India
