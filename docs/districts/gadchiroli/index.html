The region's emphasis on chemical-free farming and indigenous varieties like Kolam rice (with GI potential) positions it as a premium organic rice hub.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Gadchiroli.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Gadchiroli's farming clusters in Bhamragad and Mulchera talukas; connect with farmer producer organizations (FPOs) and state agricultural department; understand Kolam rice cultivation cycles and organic certification requirements (NPOP/PGS standards)
2
Source Raw Materials
Source paddy from farmer collectives in Gadchiroli (₹2000-2500/quintal); partner with 5-10 farmers for guaranteed supply; initial batch requirement: 50-100 quintals (₹10-25L); identify FSSAI-approved milling partners in Nagpur or Amravati
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing FSSAI-certified rice mills in Gadchiroli (contact District Agricultural Officer for approved list); negotiate custom milling rates (₹150-250/quintal); ensure organic certification audit by NPOP-accredited bodies (₹40-80K annually); start with 20-30 quintal batches
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) via udyamregistration.gov.in within 2 days; apply for IEC (Import-Export Code) via icegateway.gov.in; obtain FSSAI Food License (₹5-10K, 2 weeks); register brand name with TRADEMARK office (₹4500); design packaging emphasizing organic, Kolam variety, and local origin
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh and BigBasket with minimum 50-unit inventory (₹8-12L); approach local organic chains like Organic Brands, Kathaaro, and health stores in Nagpur/Pune; reach out to 20-30 export agents via APEDA for international orders; activate Instagram/Facebook with farmer-to-table storytelling
6
Scale
Increase farmer network to 30-50 producers by month 6; introduce 2-3 value-added SKUs (rice flour, organic upma mix); expand to corporate bulk contracts (offices, wellness programs); apply for PMEGP subsidy for processing unit expansion; establish direct-to-consumer subscription box model by month 8-10

Marketing Ideas

How to get customers

  • Instagram Reels series: 'Farm to Table' showing farmer profiles, organic certification process, and recipe ideas; target 25-45-year-old health-conscious women in metro cities with ₹2-5K monthly spend on premium food
  • Partner with 10-15 micro-influencers (wellness, organic lifestyle accounts) in Mumbai, Bangalore, Delhi for unboxing and recipe content; offer 20% commission on affiliate sales; budget ₹8-12L/quarter
  • Participate in organic food festivals and APEDA export fairs in Bangalore, Delhi, Mumbai; sponsor 2-3 wellness community events in premium localities; run sampler packs at ₹199 via BigBasket to drive trial
