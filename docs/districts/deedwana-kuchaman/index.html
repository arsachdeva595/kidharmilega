Marble & Granite Products

Deedwana-Kuchaman is home to the legendary Makrana marble quarries with over 300 years of mining heritage. The region's geological formations, skilled artisans, and established supply chains make it the world's premier marble hub. Proximity to major markets and existing infrastructure reduce production barriers.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Deedwana-Kuchaman.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Makrana marble cluster (40km from Kishangarh) and Deedwana-Kuchaman district mining areas; attend India Stone Mart (Jaipur); interview 5 established marble traders; study GI certification benefits from DPIIT portal
2
Source Raw Materials
Source marble blocks directly from Makrana quarries (₹2-5L per truckload, 10-15 ton capacity); establish relationship with 2-3 quarry owners; negotiate long-term supply agreements; first batch: ₹10-15L for 2-3 truck loads of prime quality white Makrana marble
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing cutting & polishing units in Makrana cluster (avoid high CAPEX); lease workshop space (₹50K-1L/month) or negotiate profit-sharing with artisan collectives; hire master craftsmen from local labor pool; install basic machinery (saw, polisher) for ₹20-30L if going solo
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME portal); apply for IEC code via SIMS portal for export eligibility; register brand name at IP India; obtain GI certification authentication letter from DPIIT; get ISO 9001 certification (₹2-5L); display GI tag prominently on all marketing
5
First Sale
Launch on Houzz (architect marketplace) and ArchiPro; contact 20 interior design studios in metros via WhatsApp + portfolio PDFs; bid on govt tender projects via GeM portal; create Instagram account showcasing project installations; partner with 3-4 luxury real estate developers for referrals; attend Design Hub expos in Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore
6
Scale
After 6 months, hire a sales executive for B2B architect outreach; launch premium D2C website with AR visualization; expand into adjacent products (granite, sandstone); establish satellite showrooms in Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore; secure ₹1-2Cr credit line for bulk orders; apply for SFURTI cluster development subsidy
