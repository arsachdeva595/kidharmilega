Rim and Axle and Iron Work

Shamli has an established ecosystem of iron workers, foundries, and metal fabrication units with skilled artisans trained in rim and axle manufacturing. The district's proximity to Delhi-NCR and industrial clusters provides ready access to vehicle manufacturers, tractor companies, and export markets. Historical expertise in metallurgy and metalworking makes it naturally suited for precision axle and rim production.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to start

From zero to first sale — concrete steps for Shamli.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Shamli's industrial clusters in Bhera and Garh Mukteshwar; meet 5-10 established rim and axle manufacturers; study production processes at foundries like JSW Steel suppliers; attend SIAM (Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers) workshops.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from Delhi Steel Market (Lajpat Rai Market) or Nagpur-based suppliers; negotiate first-batch steel coils (5-10 MT) at ₹45,000-55,000/MT; establish credit terms with 45-60 day payment windows; initial raw material cost ₹3-5 lakhs.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing artisans in Shamli through DC Handicraft office referrals; lease a 3,000 sq ft workshop in Bhera industrial area (₹8,000-12,000/month); invest in CNC lathe (₹15-20L), grinding machine (₹2-3L), and testing equipment; hire 4-6 skilled workers at ₹12,000-18,000/month.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam as MSE (Micro/Small Enterprise); obtain IEC code from DGFT Noida; get ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 16001 certifications (₹50,000-1L); file trademark with IP Office Delhi; register GST and obtain BIS certification for automotive components.
5
First Sale
List on IndiaStack B2B platform, Alibaba.com, and Global Trade Platform; contact JCB, Mahindra & Mahindra, TAFE tractor dealers directly with samples; register with automobile spare parts distributors in Delhi, Gurgaon, Bangalore; approach Bosch and Delphi supplier networks; send cold emails to top 50 tractor OEMs with product specs.
6
Scale
Achieve ISO/TS 16949 automotive certification by month 8; expand team to 15-20 workers; upgrade machinery to 2-3 additional CNC units; establish QA lab; explore contract manufacturing agreements with Tier-1 auto suppliers; target ₹2-3 Cr revenue by month 18-24; build export shipment capability to 5+ countries.
