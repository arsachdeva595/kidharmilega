Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bhadradri Kothagudem ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bhadradri Kothagudem district agricultural office in Kothagudem town and connect with 5-10 established chilli farmers in Thirumalkapur and Luxettipet panchayats. Interview them on yield (15-20 tons/hectare), costs (₹80K-120K per hectare), and buyer networks. Study Telangana State Agricultural Department reports on chilli productivity.
2
Source Raw Materials
Identify 2-5 acre plots in Thirumalkapur taluk (cost ₹30-50 lakh for 2 acres) or work with farmer co-operatives via NABARD-linked groups. Source hybrid/desi chilli seeds (₹2000-3000/kg) from Tamil Nadu seed vendors or IISR Kozhikode. First harvest cost: ₹1.5-2 lakh for 2 acres including labour, irrigation, and inputs.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 15-20 smallholder farmers in Thirumalkapur cluster via farmer producer organizations (FPOs) under SFURTI. Establish a small processing unit (drying, grading, packaging) in Kothagudem town with ₹50-75 lakh investment. Hire 5-8 workers and train in quality grading per APEDA standards.
4
Brand & Register
Register as a producer company or sole proprietor under Udyam MSME (online, free). Apply for IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Hyderabad (₹0 fee, 3-5 days). Get APEDA export registration (₹2000, mandatory for chilli exports). File for FSSAI food safety license (₹5000-10000). Create brand identity with Telangana tourism imagery.
5
First Sale
List on Spice export platforms (Global Sources, Alibaba, TradeKey) and connect with 3-5 bulk importers via email outreach. Target restaurants and food manufacturers via LinkedIn B2B. Approach IndiaStackTrade and state-run SPICEs office in Hyderabad. Minimum first batch: 5-10 tons, expected sale at ₹150-200/kg wholesale.
6
Scale
After first 6 months of ₹50-75 lakh revenue, expand to 5-10 acre farming network, add value-added product line (chilli powder), certify for organic/GlobalGAP to command premium (₹250-350/kg). Target 3-5 new export markets. Set up cold storage in Kothagudem.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh grant + ₹5 lakh subsidy for agri-processing unit setup
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹40 lakh for chilli cluster development, packaging, branding, and training
🏚
Telangana AgriTech Promotion Scheme
₹10-15 lakh subsidy for farm automation and drying equipment for registered FPOs
🏚
Telangana EXPORT-IMPORT Incentive Scheme
5% cashback on export invoices for agricultural products with IEC registration
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
