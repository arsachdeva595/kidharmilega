Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Khagaria ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Khagaria district artisan clusters around Parbatta and Mirza villages; spend 2-3 weeks learning from master craftsmen; attend SFURTI program workshops in bamboo craft hubs; study market at Delhi Craft Bazaar and Fab India stores (₹10K-15K for travel and learning).
2
Source Raw Materials
Source bamboo culms directly from Khagaria forest produce traders (₹8-12 per culm) and cane from authorized suppliers in Munger district; negotiate first batch of 500 units at ₹25,000-35,000; build relationship with 2-3 artisan groups for consistent supply.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with registered artisan SHGs in Khagaria (verified through SFURTI database) or directly hire 3-5 trained artisans at ₹250-350/day; establish small production space (200 sq ft) with drying area; begin with 100-150 units/month; document video of production process for marketing.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) classification online (free); apply for IEC code via DGFT portal (₹500-1000); file trademark for brand name with IP India (₹4500-9000); get FSSAI registration if adding food-contact items; obtain GST registration (18% applicable).
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy with 50+ high-quality images (₹0 listing fee, 6.5% commission); simultaneously list on Amazon Handmade (2% commission after verification); approach 10-15 boutique home décor stores in Patna, Delhi, Bangalore with bulk discounts; start Instagram organic content with #BambooArtKhagaria (post 3x/week); email 50 eco-tourism companies for gifting partnerships.
6
Scale
After ₹5L revenue, hire dedicated inventory manager; expand artisan network to 15-20 craftspeople; launch own branded D2C website with Shopify (₹15K/month); explore B2B partnerships with hotel chains and corporate bulk orders; apply for PMEGP expansion grant (₹15-25L subsidy); participate in Chennai Craft Fair and Indian Handicraft Expo.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L grant (35% subsidy) for bamboo craft micro-enterprises in rural Bihar
🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹15K advance + ₹10K interest subsidy + free skill training for registered traditional craftspeople and artisan partnerships
🏚
Bihar Bamboo Mission
₹5L subsidy for bamboo processing units and artisan skill development; prioritizes Khagaria district clusters
🏚
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹20L infrastructure grant for bamboo craft clusters; direct support in Khagaria artisan hubs
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
