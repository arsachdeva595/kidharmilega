Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Vikarabad ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Tandur mining cluster (30 km from Vikarabad town) and walk 2-3 active quarries — talk to mine managers about reserve quality, mining cost per MT, and regulatory process (free, 1 day). Call 5 processors already operating in the district (numbers from local Industries office) and ask about their supplier cost, output quality, and buyer contracts. Attend the Vikarabad District Industries Centre (DIC) monthly haat and network with existing Fuller's Earth business owners — no registration fee.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw bentonite/palygorskite lumps from Tandur quarries (₹1,200-1,600/MT for 99% purity). Get 10 MT as first batch (cost ₹12-16K). Verify purity via free assay at CGCRI (Central Glass and Ceramic Research Institute) lab in Hyderabad, 90 km away. Build relationships with 2-3 quarry suppliers to avoid supply shock.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 2,000-3,000 sq ft processing unit in Vikarabad MSME cluster or Tandur industrial area (₹15-25K/month). Invest in ball mill (₹1.5-3L) and screening equipment (₹1-2L). Alternatively, partner with existing processors to handle granule/powder production on contract basis (saves ₹3-5L capex, splits profit 60-40). Target first production run of 5-10 MT/month by month 3.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME online (free, 15 min). Apply for IEC code with nearest DGFT office for export readiness (₹0 cost, 7-10 days). Explore GI registration for Vikarabad Fuller's Earth (₹5K filing, 12-18 month approval) to add premium positioning. Get ISI or FSSAI certification if targeting cosmetics (₹20-30K, 30 days). Open a business bank account (₹0, 5 days).
5
First Sale
First sale: Contact 5 existing cosmetic manufacturers in Hyderabad via referral from DIC and offer free 5 kg sample. Pitch ₹2-5L trial order (500 kg granules at ₹3,500-4,500/MT). List on Amazon/Flipkart as private seller — start with 10 kg clay mask packs at ₹250-350 each. Expected first-month revenue: ₹3-8L from B2B + ₹20-40K from D2C.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue, hire 1 sales exec (₹20K/month) to manage B2B accounts and 1 packaging/QC person (₹15K/month). Set up export logistics via freight forwarder in Hyderabad (Rajendra Container or similar). Attend India Ceramics Expo (Jan) and international trade fairs in Germany/Dubai to open export channels. Invest ₹1.5-2L in food-grade bulk packaging and compliance. Target ₹30-50L/month by month 18-24.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25-35% subsidy up to ₹25L loan, applicable for manufacturing. Interest rate 6-7% from banks.
🏛
MUDRA Yojana
collateral-free loans up to ₹10L at 7-10% interest for small processing units.
🏚
Telangana Industrial Investment Assistance Scheme (TIIAS)
20% subsidy on plant & machinery cost for MSME manufacturers, up to ₹50L cap.
🏚
Telangana ODOP Export Support Scheme
5% incentive on FOB value of exports, requires IEC and Udyam registration.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
