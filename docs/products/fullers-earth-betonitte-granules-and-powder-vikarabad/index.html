Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Vikarabad ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Vikarabad mining clusters (Tandur taluk area) and connect with existing Fuller's Earth producers like those near Tandur. Meet 2-3 established units to understand mining leases, production capacity, quality grading, and pricing. Attend MSME and mining industry webinars; research cosmetic industry standards (ISO 9001, BIS certification requirements).
2
Source Raw Materials
Partner with or lease access from existing miners in Vikarabad; negotiate raw ore at ₹2,000-3,500/MT. For first batch (10 MT), allocate ₹25-35 lakhs including grinding, drying, and packaging. Source packaging from Hyderabad suppliers (50km away); typical costs ₹8-12/kg for branded pouches.
3
Produce / Partner
Secure a 2,000-3,000 sq ft facility with grinding mills, drying units, and sieving equipment. Partner with local contract manufacturers in Vikarabad who handle grinding/processing (cost ₹500-800/MT). Alternatively, obtain a mining lease (2-5 hectares) and invest in in-house equipment (₹15-20L capex). Ensure BIS certification (IS 2918:1992) for quality standards.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) within 48 hours online; apply for IEC code from nearest DGFT office (Hyderabad). Obtain BIS license and ISO 9001 certification (₹3-5L, 3-4 months). Register brand name and design trademark with IP office. Get GST registration (Composite scheme if turnover <₹1.5Cr). Obtain mining lease or purchase agreement if applicable.
5
First Sale
Launch on B2B platforms: Alibaba.com, Global Sources; contact cosmetic manufacturers via LinkedIn and industry directories. Simultaneously, build D2C presence on Amazon, Flipkart, Nykaa with 'Pure Vikarabad Fuller's Earth' positioning. Partner with 5-10 local beauty retailers and Ayurvedic shops for immediate offline sales. Engage cosmetic formulators and contract manufacturers with sample offers.
6
Scale
After 6 months and ₹1Cr+ revenue, invest in private label manufacturing capability; launch 2-3 branded cosmetic products (face masks, scrubs). Establish export partnerships with distributors in UAE, UK, and USA. Increase production capacity by 50% via additional equipment or facility expansion. Build influencer partnerships and run targeted Instagram/Facebook campaigns for D2C segment.
