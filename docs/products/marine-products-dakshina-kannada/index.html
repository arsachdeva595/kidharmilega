Dakshina Kannada's 90 km Arabian Sea coastline provides year-round access to 5,000+ tonnes daily fish catch from Mangaluru and Udupi fishing harbors, the nation's second-largest fishing cluster after Kerala. The district hosts 200+ FSMS-certified processing units concentrated in Mangaluru (Bayar, Pandeshwar, Surathkal areas) and Udupi, with established cold storage at -20°C capacity of 10,000+ MT. Direct export corridors run via Mangaluru Port (110 km), air-freight via Bangalore (250 km), and road links to major cities; wholesale buyers include EU importers, US distributors, and domestic retail chains.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Dakshina Kannada ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Mangaluru fish market (Bayar or Pandeshwar areas) on a Saturday morning — call 3-4 exporters (listed on APEDA portal), attend a monthly CFIA (Canned Food Industry Association) meeting at Mangaluru Chamber (free entry). Spend ₹0, takes 4-6 hours, you'll learn pricing, supplier networks, and current export rates.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from Mangaluru fishing harbor primary agents (Bayar Jetty area) or established processors like Suramar, SeaFresh — first 10 MT batch costs ₹8-12 L depending on variety (frozen shrimp ₹80-120/kg, frozen fish ₹40-60/kg). Negotiate 15-30 day credit terms; vet suppliers via FSSAI certificate and APEDA registration.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 2,000 sqft FSMS-certified unit in Surathkal or Bayar cluster (₹25-40K/month), hire 8-10 skilled laborers (₹12-15K/month each), procure -20°C cold storage units (₹15-25L capex), packaging machines (₹3-5L). Partner with an existing processor for co-packing if capital-constrained — they charge 10-15% processing fee. Total capex: ₹25-40L for in-house, ₹0 for co-packing (pay-per-kg model).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (free, 15 mins) with PAN/Aadhaar. Apply for FSSAI license (₹5,000, 7-10 days). If exporting, register with APEDA (₹1,500, 5 days) and get IEC from customs (₹500, 3 days). Skip GI registration (no GI status for this product). Total cost: ₹7,000, 15 days.
5
First Sale
First wholesale order: contact 3-5 hotel chains' procurement teams directly via LinkedIn/email with FSMS certificate + price list. Typical first order: 500 kg frozen shrimp at ₹80/kg = ₹40K revenue. List on BigBasket Seller Central or Amazon Seller Central (2-3% margin loss but 500+ kg/month possible). Expect ₹50-80K first-month wholesale revenue.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 export-logistics manager (₹25-30K), add second -20°C cold storage unit (₹15L more capex), expand to APEDA export agents (5+ new export partners), attend 2-3 seafood trade shows (Seafood Expo Asia in Bangkok, India Seafood Show in Mumbai). Build compliance margin: 6 months of FSSAI audit prep, third-party HACCP certification (₹50-75K), and ISO 22000 (₹1-2L). Hire QA staff (₹18-22K).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25% subsidy on plant and machinery up to ₹25L, applicable for MSME registration
🏛
MUDRA Loan
up to ₹10L at 8.5% interest, 5-year tenure, no collateral for businesses under ₹10L turnover
🏚
Karnataka ODOP Scheme
One-time subsidy up to ₹10L for unit setup, priority lending via banks, free skill training via KSDP
🏚
Karnataka Export Promotion Scheme
5% reimbursement on shipping/logistics costs for exports, max ₹5L/FY, applies to APEDA-registered units
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
