Deep-rooted fishing community expertise spanning generations ensures consistent quality and sustainable catch.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Dakshina Kannada ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Mangaluru Fish Market (daily auctions, 5-6 AM) and Malpe Fishing Harbour. Meet 5-10 fisher cooperative groups. Understand catch calendar (monsoon closure June-Aug), pricing cycles, and quality grading standards. Join KMFDC (Karnataka Marine Fisheries Development Corporation) training.
2
Source Raw Materials
Register with Mangaluru Port Authority and local fishing co-operatives for direct catch access. First 5-ton batch: ₹2-3 lakhs (prawns ₹400-500/kg, mackerel ₹80-120/kg). Secure cold-storage space at ₹10-15K/month (50-ton capacity). Partner with local ice suppliers (₹500-800 per ton).
3
Produce / Partner
Tie up with 3-5 registered fishermen/cooperatives for consistent supply. Establish in-house processing unit (cleaning, grading, packaging) or partner with existing seafood processors in Mangaluru (Hoige Doigé area has 50+ processors). Invest in Udyam-registered cold-storage with temperature monitoring (-18°C to -25°C).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSE classification). Obtain IEC code from nearest ICEGATE centre in Bengaluru or Mangaluru Port. Get FSSAI license for processing/trading. Implement HACCP/ISO 22000 for export-quality standards. Register trademark with IP India. Create GST registration under food trading.
5
First Sale
Start with B2B: approach 20-30 restaurants/hotels in Bengaluru/Udupi via LinkedIn and WhatsApp with sample boxes (cost ₹5K-10K). List on Swiggy Instamart, Blinkit, JioMart Fresh (2-3 weeks approval). Partner with seafood wholesalers at Bangalore City Market. Direct exports via APEDA-registered agents to Japan, Thailand.
6
Scale
After ₹25L revenue (4-6 months), add value-added lines: smoked fish, fish pickle, dried shrimp. Open brand presence on Flipkart, Amazon Fresh. Export 30-40% revenue by month 12. Establish own cold-chain trucks. Expand to sister products (fish oil, fish meal) targeting aqua-feed industry.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (PM Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L loan for cold-storage, processing units with 35% subsidy for general category.
🏛
MUDRA Yojana (PMMY)
₹10L unsecured loan for inventory and equipment under Tarun subcategory.
🏚
Karnataka Marine Fisheries Subsidy Scheme
50% subsidy on cold-storage infrastructure and ice-making units for registered fishing co-operatives.
🏚
Karnataka Export Promotion Scheme
10% of FOB value rebate on seafood exports to priority markets (SAARC, ASEAN, Africa).
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
