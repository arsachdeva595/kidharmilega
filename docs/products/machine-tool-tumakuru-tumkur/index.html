Existing industrial infrastructure across 9 Industrial Areas and 7 Industrial Estates has attracted ₹456.47 billion cumulative investment, positioning Tumakuru as the nodal hub for machine tool production linking to Bengaluru automotive and aerospace buyers.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Tumakuru (Tumkur) ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Integrated Machine Tool Park (IMTP) site in Vasanthanarasapura or National Investment and Manufacturing Zone (NIMZ) office in Tumakuru to see layout, existing tenants, plot sizes, and rental terms — free site walk. Call 3-4 machine tool manufacturers already in IMTP cluster (contact via District Industries Centre, Tumakuru) to understand their setup costs, supplier network, and first-year revenue. Attend Tumakuru District MSME Cluster meet (monthly, contact DIC) — zero cost, meet other founders. Register free on Udyam portal and check existing machine tool units in district.
2
Source Raw Materials
Identify precision casting suppliers in Belagavi (90km away, 2-3 days lead time) or Chennai (500km) for cast iron bodies; mild steel and alloy steel from Jindal or Tata in Bengaluru (100km, 1-2 days). Fasteners and bearings from Bengaluru suppliers like Bossard India or local agents in Tumakuru Industrial Area (₹50-80L first-batch procurement for 5-10 machine tools). CNC controllers and electrical components from Bangalore Electronics City or Hyderabad suppliers. First prototype lathe or milling machine will cost ₹15-25L in components; expect 30-40 days lead time for custom casting.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 2000-3000 sqft unit in one of Tumakuru's 9 Industrial Areas (Sira, Kunigal, Koratagere) at ₹20-40 per sqft/month or 542-acre IMTP when open (expected 2024-25, preferred for MSME subsidy eligibility). Hire or partner with experienced CNC programmer (₹25-40K/month) and 2-3 skilled machinists (₹15-25K/month each). Setup cost: ₹25-40L for tooling, fixtures, testing equipment, safety setup; 3-6 months ramp-up to first saleable unit. Alternatively, start with job work/sub-contracting for larger firms (₹5-8L setup, faster cash flow).
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam (free, 10 minutes online). Apply for ISO 9001 certification (₹3-5L, 3-4 months turnaround — critical for B2B buyers and exports). If targeting exports, obtain IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) from DGFT via IndiaStack (₹5K, 5-7 days). Consider GI registration for 'Tumakuru Machine Tools' (₹50-70K legal cost, 18-24 months — optional but differentiates). File for MSME subsidy under PMEGP or One District One Product scheme (₹25L max subsidy, apply via DIC).
5
First Sale
First sale: approach Bengaluru auto component makers (Sona Comstar, Motherson, Lumax) or aerospace suppliers (HAL, Aerojet) via district export association or direct referral. List on IndiaMART and Udaan B2B — first inquiries arrive in 2-3 weeks, expect ₹8-12L first order (2-3 machines) with 30-60 day payment terms. Attend IMTEX Bengaluru (biennial, next Jan 2025) to find buyers and partners; booth cost ₹2-4L. First-month revenue if sale closes: ₹8-15L (40% of contract value upfront).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: expand to 5000 sqft facility, hire production supervisor (₹50K/month), add 2-3 more machinists. Invest in 2-3 CNC machines to reduce sub-contracting and improve margin from 25% to 35%. Start export compliance: appoint freight forwarder, get third-party lab testing (ISO certification updated), file tax returns for GST (15% rate on machines, careful audit). Exhibit at IMTEX, Pragati Maidan Delhi, and Hannover EMO (export-focused, ₹10-15L per show). Partner with trade bodies — Confederation of Indian Industry (CII), SIAM, All India Association of Industries (AIAI) — for buyer networks and subsidy information.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)
up to ₹25L subsidy (35% of project cost, max equipment cost ₹40L) for manufacturing units; apply via MSME-DI, 3-4 month approval
🏛
MUDRA Loan Scheme
up to ₹10L collateral-free for working capital, 7-10% interest, via SBI or other banks; 2-week approval
🏚
Karnataka One District One Product (ODOP) Scheme
25% subsidy for MSME units in IMTP cluster, free land lease for 5 years, ₹10-15L direct cash benefit; apply via DIC Tumakuru
🏚
Karnataka MSME Cluster Development Scheme
₹2-5L grant for first purchase of pollution control, testing equipment, tooling; apply via KIA (Karnataka Industries Association)
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
