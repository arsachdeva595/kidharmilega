Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Khawzawl ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Khawzawl's farming clusters near Aizawl and study cultivation practices with local farmers; connect with Mizoram State Agriculture Department for soil testing, variety selection, and best practices documentation.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source pineapple suckers from established Khawzawl growers or certified nurseries in Aizawl; first-batch cost for 0.5 hectares: ₹2-4 lakh (suckers + soil prep + initial fertilizer).
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 established Khawzawl pineapple farmers for contract farming or lease agricultural land locally; implement organic farming with drip irrigation to reduce costs and improve quality.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free online via udyamregistration.gov.in); apply for IEC code via Indian Customs; register FPO (Farmer Producer Organization) for bulk export eligibility; create brand name reflecting 'Khawzawl' origin.
5
First Sale
Initial sales via APMC Aizawl market and Mizoram State Agriculture Department channels; launch on BigBasket, Blinkit, Zepto within 3 months; approach juice manufacturers (ITC, Tropicana, local brands) with samples.
6
Scale
Scale to 2-3 hectares by month 12; establish cold storage facility in Aizawl (₹30-50 lakh); partner with 10-15 farmers for contract farming; target 3-4 export countries with certification (organic, GlobalGAP).
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidized loan for agribusiness with 35% subsidy for SC/ST applicants
🏛
ATMA (Agricultural Technology Management Agency)
free technical training, soil testing, and market linkage support for pineapple farmers
🏚
Mizoram Horticulture Mission
subsidies up to 50% on drip irrigation, cold storage, and farm equipment for fruit cultivation
🏚
Mizoram State Agricultural Department Export Promotion Scheme
support for FPO registration, export certification, and market access to SAARC and ASEAN countries
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
