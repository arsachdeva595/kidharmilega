Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Khawzawl ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call 3 pineapple suppliers in Khawzawl town market (Khawzawl Main Haat). Visit one active farm near Zokhawthar village. Register free on Udyam website. Attend weekly farmers market in Khawzawl to understand seasonal pricing — all free, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source directly from Khawzawl pineapple belt (Zokhawthar, Teikhang, Saitual sub-clusters). Contract 2-3 farmers for consistent weekly supply: expect ₹12-18 per kg depending on season. First batch of 1000 kg costs ₹15,000-18,000. Farmers in Khawzawl Pineapple Cluster sell 70% of state production.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (Zero capex): Partner with existing packing/processing unit at Khawzawl Food Business Incubator (FBU) on revenue-share basis. Option B (Capex): Rent 1000 sq ft unit near Khawzawl town for ₹8,000-12,000/month, install basic washing and grading equipment (₹1.5-2.5L). Start with manual labor (2 workers @ ₹8,000/month each). Expect daily throughput of 300-500 kg after 4 weeks ramp-up.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (15 mins online, free). Apply for GI registration support under One District One Product scheme (₹50,000 subsidy, 90-day process). Obtain FSSAI registration for processed products (₹12,000-18,000). If exporting: apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) via Customs portal (free). Total compliance cost: ₹60,000-70,000 over 4 months.
5
First Sale
First sale target: Jio Mart Kirana direct procurement (contact Jio Mart local vendor manager in Aizawl). First order likely 50-100 kg. List on Amazon Fresh Produce (takes 2 weeks approval). First-month revenue estimate: ₹40,000-80,000 at wholesale + ₹15,000-25,000 from local retail. Use Mizoram State Agricultural Marketing Board (MISAMB) to connect with institutional buyers.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire supply chain manager (₹20,000/month), formalize farmer contracts (3-4 contract farmers), expand to 3 varieties (Kew, Mauritius, MD2). Exhibit at India International Fruit & Vegetable Fair (held annually, register ₹5,000). Partner with 2 exporters already shipping to Middle East markets to piggyback. Engage logistics partner for cold-chain supply to metros (₹40,000-60,000/month).
