Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Washim ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Washim agricultural market (Mandi) on Tuesday or Friday to understand soybean quality, pricing, and seasonal availability. Call 3-4 soybean oil mills already operating in Washim taluk (ask at Mandi office for contact details). Register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) with basic business plan — costs ₹0, takes 15 minutes online. Attend the weekly agri-input haat in Washim town center to meet current soybean processors and understand their unit economics.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw soybean from Washim Mandi (main wholesale market) or directly from farmers in Washim and Malegaon tehsils at ₹4500-5200 per quintal depending on season. First batch: 5 quintals costs ₹22,500-26,000. Visit Mandi directly on market days (Tuesday/Friday) between 6-10 AM. Alternative: link with cooperative societies like Washim District Cooperative Cotton Union for bulk, stable pricing.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A — Rent 500-800 sqft space in existing MSME cluster or near Washim town at ₹3000-5000/month, install basic oil extraction (cold press) and milk processing equipment (cost ₹3.5-6L). Option B — Partner with existing small oil mill owners in Washim for contract processing at 8-12% of output as fee. Start with oil extraction (higher margin 28-38%), then scale to soy milk if demand grows. Basic FSSAI license and food handling training required upfront — ₹15,000-25,000, 4-6 weeks approval.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (free, 15 minutes) with your unit's details and address. Apply for FSSAI State License (₹3000-5000, 30-45 days) at Washim District Food Authority office. Get GST registration (₹0, online, 3-5 days) once business starts. If exporting, apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) at DGFT office in Aurangabad (50 km away, ₹0, 5-7 days processing). GI registration not applicable — no GI status for Washim soya products.
5
First Sale
First sales: Contact 5-10 local retailers in Washim town and nearby Akola town directly with sample (cost ₹500 samples). List on BigBasket seller portal (5% commission) and local Jio Mart for D2C — first order typical 50-100 units. Approach 2-3 animal feed manufacturers in Maharashtra (Nagpur, Aurangabad regions) with soybean meal samples — typical first institutional order 500-1000 kg. Expected first-month revenue: ₹25,000-45,000 from mixed channels (retail + online + B2B pilot).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (~1500-2000 kg production): hire 1 processing operator (₹12,000/month) and 1 logistics/sales person (₹10,000/month). Shift to larger 1200-1500 sqft facility (₹6000-8000/month). Apply for PMEGP subsidy (35% capital subsidy up to ₹35L) to scale processing equipment. Exhibit at India Food Forum (Mumbai), Nutrex (Delhi), and regional agri-expos in Nagpur to access institutional buyers. Set up WhatsApp Business catalog and Instagram shop linking to Meesho or Flipkart Shopsy for broader D2C reach.
