Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Paschim Bardhaman ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Durgapur industrial cluster and DCCPP (Durgapur Ceramic & Clay Products Park) to study 15-20 existing refractory manufacturers. Interview owners about clay sourcing (₹3000-5000/ton), kiln fuel costs, quality standards (ISO 9001), and steel mill supply relationships. Spend 2-3 weeks in Asansol-Durgapur understanding buyer requirements.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source refractory clay from local suppliers in Paschim Bardhaman (₹40-50 lakhs for initial 100 tons). Partner with established clay mines near Durgapur. Order alumina and silica additives from Gujarat suppliers (IndiaMART, TradeKey). First batch production cost: ₹8-10 lakhs for 5000 standard bricks.
3
Produce / Partner
Invest in high-temperature kiln (₹10-15 lakhs used kiln available), molding equipment (₹3-5 lakhs), and drying facilities. Partner with existing local kiln operators for outsourced production initially. Alternatively, collaborate with DCCPP members or FICCI-affiliated ceramic manufacturers for contract production while building own capacity.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) online in 5 minutes. Apply for IEC code from DGFT for export eligibility. Obtain ISO 9001:2015 certification (₹1.5-2 lakhs) mandatory for steel mill buyers. Register brand name under trade mark (Delhi/Kolkata). Get pollution control approval from WBPCB for kilns.
5
First Sale
Build B2B relationships through LinkedIn targeting procurement managers at Tata Steel Jamshedpur, JSW Steel, Durgapur Steel Plant. Create spec sheet with test certificates. Join industry portals: IndiaMART, TradeKey, AliBaba. Cold-call 50 foundries and steel furnace operators. Attend India Cer Expo and industrial ceramics trade shows. Offer sample batches at ₹8/unit to pilot with 2-3 customers.
6
Scale
After 6 months and ₹50L revenue, invest in second kiln (₹12L) to double capacity. Build direct relationship with top 5 steel mills through technical reps. Establish export sales to ASEAN via Dubai trading houses (5-10% margin). Develop specialized products (chrome-magnesite, alumina-silicate) for premium furnace applications (30-40% margin). Hire quality control manager and sales executive to handle B2B relationships.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakhs subsidy for manufacturing unit with 25-35% subsidy on fixed asset
🏛
MUDRA Loan Scheme
up to ₹50 lakhs unsecured working capital loan at 8-10% interest for MSME registered units
🏚
West Bengal Subsidy on Interest for Industrial Loan
3-4% interest subsidy on industrial loans up to ₹1 crore for manufacturing in non-metro districts
🏚
West Bengal Industrial Development Policy (2022)
Investment subsidy of 15% on fixed assets for manufacturing units in backward areas with minimum ₹50 lakh investment
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
