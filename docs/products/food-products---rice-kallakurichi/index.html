Kallakurichi district, carved from Villupuram, spans 3,529.67 sq km across 7 taluks with 562 revenue villages producing paddy as primary crop due to black and alluvial soil fertility. The district is 140 km southwest of Chennai and 220 km northwest of Bangalore, positioning it between two major consumption hubs with strong road connectivity via NH44 and NH66. Raw material (paddy) is sourced within 15-25 km radius from farming communities, enabling cost-effective milling and processing with minimal supply chain friction.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kallakurichi ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit OM MURUGA AGRO FOODS (GST: 33BCFPA2348B2ZE) directly in Kallakurichi town to understand their operations (free). Call 3-4 paddy suppliers in Kallakurichi and Chengam taluks to understand seasonal pricing and minimum order quantities. Register for free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) to check competitor MSMEs in your area.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source paddy from farming cooperatives in Chengam (₹6,500-7,500 per quintal, 10-20 quintal minimum), Kallakurichi market haat, and individual farmers via Krishi Vigyan Kendra network. First batch: 50 quintals (5 MT) paddy = ₹3.25-3.75L. Expect 65-70% milled rice yield (3.25-3.5 MT rice output).
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 1,500-2,000 sq ft milling unit in Kallakurichi town at ₹12,000-18,000/month (shared or standalone). Partner with existing rice mill for first 3-6 months on contract milling basis (₹40-60 per quintal milling charge = ₹2,000-3,000 per batch). Setup: ₹2-3L for custom-built shed + ₹3-4L for small dehusking+drying setup, or ₹6-8L for 2-3 TPD (tonnes per day) own milling unit. Start with contract milling at ₹5-6L investment.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME (free, 1 week online). Apply for GI tag through Ministry of Food Processing (cost: ₹10,000-15,000, timeline: 8-10 months; benefit: price premium 15-25%). Obtain FSSAI license (₹5,000-12,000, 2-3 weeks). If exporting: apply for IEC from customs (₹0-2,000, 1 week). Design packaging with QR code linking to origin story (₹20,000-50,000 for first 10,000 units).
5
First Sale
First sale: approach Amazon Vendor Central for Fresh category (₹50,000-1L stock deposit required, 15% commission). First order size: 500-1,000 kg (₹40,000-75,000 value). Parallel: approach DMart supplier portal (direct negotiation, 30-day payment). Expected first month: ₹1-2L revenue if 2-3 orders secured.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 packing supervisor + 2-3 packers (₹12,000-15,000/month total). Add 1,000+ sq ft cold storage (₹4-6L capex). Apply for PMEGP subsidy (₹10-25L, 25% subsidy available) for second milling unit expansion. Exhibit at SIAL India (food trade show) or Taste of India (D2C platform). Target: 4-5 wholesale buyers + 2-3 D2C channels by month 12.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
₹25 Lakh max subsidy at 25% for new units, max project cost ₹10-50L, applicable for food processing MSMEs
🏛
MUDRA Shishu/Kishore Loan
₹50L unsecured loan at 8-10% interest for MSME working capital and equipment purchase, processing time 7-10 days
🏚
Tamil Nadu Chief Minister's Comprehensive Health Insurance Scheme
health coverage for workers at ₹0 premium to employer for units with <50 employees
🏚
Tamil Nadu Food Processing Cluster Scheme
free plot allocation + 5-year tax holiday for units setting up in notified food processing clusters (apply via Tamil Nadu Industries Directorate)

100% export-worthy. India exports 30.3% of world's rice. Kallakurichi rice already sought by NRI market (USA, UK, Canada, Malaysia, Singapore). You need: IEC code (₹0-2,000, 1 week), FSSAI certificate, packaging with HS code labeling, and shipping via sea freight (₹80-120 per 50kg bag to US/UK). First shipment: 500-1,000 bags (25-50 MT). Rice is commodity, demand never dies. Worst case: you sell unmilled paddy to existing mills at ₹600-750/quintal, lose ₹10-20K processing margin per batch. Exit option: sell milling machine as secondhand at 60-70% recovery (₹3-4L back). Paddy remains saleable year-round. Unlike fashion/electronics, rice has guaranteed end-market. Risk is margin compression, not demand destruction.
