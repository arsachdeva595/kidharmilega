Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Coimbatore ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Coimbatore's Koundampalayam and Lakshmi Mill Road industrial clusters, meet 5-10 established pump manufacturers, attend IMTEX or industry expos to understand design standards, material specs, and export requirements. Study competitors on TradeKey and Alibaba.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source cast iron and steel from local suppliers in Coimbatore (Mettupalayam Road); copper wire from Karnataka; bearings from Bangalore suppliers. First batch of 100 pumps @ ₹2-3L for raw materials. Build relationships with 3-4 primary vendors for bulk discounts.
3
Produce / Partner
Register with local foundries and machine shops in Coimbatore for casting, machining, and assembly (no large capex needed initially). Partner with 1-2 established pump makers for outsourced production or semi-CKD assembly model. Get ISO 9001 certification early.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (micro-enterprise), obtain IEC code from nearest bank for export. File for trademark on brand name with IP India. Obtain ISI/BIS certification for motor pumps (mandatory for domestic sales). Get GST registration and TAN.
5
First Sale
List on IndiaStack B2B, Flipkart Business, Amazon Business for domestic market. Use TradeKey, Global Sources, Alibaba for export leads. Partner with 2-3 agricultural equipment distributors in Tamil Nadu and neighboring states. Attend agri-expo events. Target cooperatives and government irrigation tenders.
6
Scale
After 6 months, establish your own small assembly unit in Coimbatore with 10-15 workers. Build export partnerships with 5+ international distributors. Develop 2-3 specialized pump variants for niche markets. Invest in digital marketing and participation in international trade shows.
