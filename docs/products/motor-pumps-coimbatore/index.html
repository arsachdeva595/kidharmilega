Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Coimbatore ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call 3 Arasur cluster manufacturers (get numbers from Coimbatore MSME Facilitation Centre, Sathy Road); ask them: raw material cost breakdown, production timeline per unit, which buyer segments pay fastest. Visit Arasur industrial area (Saturday morning) — see 2-3 operating units in action. Register free Udyam at udyamregistration.gov.in. Cost: ₹0. Timeline: 1 week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Contact Tiruppur copper sheet suppliers (500+ registered) — request quotes for 100 kg copper coils. Tiruppur to Coimbatore: 30 km. Casting iron foundries in Coimbatore itself (Arasur, Saravanampatti zones). First batch raw material cost for 50 units of 1 hp pump: ₹1.2-1.5L. Lead time: 7-10 days after payment.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 2,000-3,000 sqft unit in Arasur cluster (₹15,000-25,000/month); buy motor winding machine (₹2-3L, second-hand ₹80-120K), assembly bench, testing equipment. OR partner with existing Arasur manufacturers for job work (you supply materials, they assemble at ₹1,200-1,800 per unit). Small-batch option: ₹12-18L setup over 2 months; partnership option: ₹2-4L for first 100 units.
4
Brand & Register
Udyam registration (free, 15 mins online). Apply for MSME certification if turnover <₹50Cr. GI registration NOT applicable (motor pumps are manufactured goods, not handicrafts). IEC registration from nearest DGFT office if export planned (₹500, 5 days). NSIC/SIDBI partnership for working capital loans (8-12% interest, ₹5-25L, 2 weeks approval).
5
First Sale
First orders via IndiaMART Premium (₹2,999/month subscription) + local plumbing dealers in Coimbatore city. Call dealers in Avinashi Road (pump retail hub), show 1-2 sample units. First sale target: 5-10 units in month 1 = ₹35,000-70,000 revenue. Expected first-month profit after costs: ₹5,000-12,000.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 2-3 assembly workers (₹300-400/day each), 1 QC person, 1 sales executive. Exhibit at Tamil Nadu Water Works Association trade shows (Mar-Apr), All India Manufacturers Association (AIMA) expos. Launch branded website with price calculator. Tie up with 5-7 state irrigation departments for annual supply contracts (₹50-100L each).
