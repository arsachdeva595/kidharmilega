Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Balaghat ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Chinnaur village cluster in Balaghat (40km from Balaghat city) to meet 50+ farmer collective groups; connect with Balaghat Mandi (wholesale grain market) traders; study GI certification holders like Chinahul Premium Rice — understand their sourcing, milling, and branding approach
2
Source Raw Materials
Source 10-20 tons seed stock directly from registered Chinnaur farmers in Lamta block (₹30-35 per kg); partner with 2-3 farmers on contract farming basis; negotiate first batch milling at local certified mills (₹2-3 per kg milling cost); budget ₹8-12L for first 20-ton procurement cycle
3
Produce / Partner
Either co-invest in contract farming with farmer groups (35-40% of output) or establish partnership with existing rice mill in Balaghat town with quality certification (ISO 22000); ensure GI-compliant processing; conduct soil testing and harvest planning with Indira Gandhi Krishi Vishvavidyalaya advisory
4
Brand & Register
Register business as FPO (Farmer Producer Organization) or private company; obtain Udyam registration (MSME portal); apply for IEC code via DGFT for export readiness; register brand name with IP office; obtain health and food safety certification (FSSAI); leverage existing GI status in branding — no new GI application needed
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh, Blinkit, BigBasket specialty sections with premium positioning (₹80-120 per kg retail); approach 20-30 specialty multi-cuisine restaurants in Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi with B2B pricing (₹45-55 per kg); set up WhatsApp Business for bulk orders; partner with 1-2 export traders in Indore or Mumbai for international sales; create Instagram content around terroir and GI heritage
6
Scale
After 6 months, establish own 500-ton rice processing unit near Balaghat (₹25-30L capex) to improve margins; expand to 5-7 e-commerce platforms; hire dedicated export consultant; develop 2-3 value-added products (brown rice, rice bran oil); build farm-to-table restaurant partnerships in tier-1 cities; apply for PMEGP subsidy for processing unit expansion
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy for food processing unit with 35% capital subsidy for general category
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
cluster development support for rice processing and agricultural value-added products
🏚
Mukhyamantri Kisan Kalyan Yojana (MP)
income support and crop insurance for rice farmers in identified districts like Balaghat
🏚
Madhya Pradesh Food Processing Policy 2017
capital subsidy up to ₹2Cr for food processing units with employment creation focus
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
