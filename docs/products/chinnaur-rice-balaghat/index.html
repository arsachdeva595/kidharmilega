Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Balaghat ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call 3 rice farmers in Balaghat town (ask for names at Balaghat Agricultural Produce Market Committee); visit one paddy field during harvest season (Oct-Nov); attend Balaghat Kisan Haat on market day — zero cost, get price lists and yield data firsthand this month
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw Chinnaur paddy from Balaghat tehsil (primary cluster around Balaghat town and Lalbarra village); first batch: 5 tonnes paddy at ₹22-25/kg = ₹110,000-125,000; connect via BALAGHAT APMC or farmer unions — 1-2 week lead time
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 800-1,200 sq ft rice milling unit in Balaghat town or near Katangi (₹8,000-12,000/month) OR partner with existing miller taking 8-10% processing fee; equipment needed: cleaning machine (₹60,000), grading machine (₹80,000), packaging (₹15,000) — total ₹250-400K if renting with basic gear; miller route = ₹40-50K initial cost
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 5 min online); apply for GI logo usage from GI registry Chennai via district administration (₹5,000-8,000, 2-3 weeks); get FSSAI registration (₹8,000, 1 week); if exporting: apply for IEC from DGFT online (₹500, 1 week) — total ₹15,000-18,000
5
First Sale
Launch on Bhat.io and Amazon Fresh with 500kg stock (costs ₹80-90K for packaging/delivery setup); first order expected 50-100kg at ₹180/kg = ₹9,000-18,000 revenue in week 1; month 1 target: ₹2-3L in retail + ₹1-1.5L in wholesale (10-15kg to local restaurants) = ₹3-4.5L first month
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 supply chain coordinator (₹20K/month) to manage farmer sourcing; add B2B export sales rep (₹25K/month); move to 2,000 sq ft facility (₹18,000/month); add vacuum packing machine (₹150K); launch on 2-3 regional e-grocers; exhibit at IITF Delhi (Dec, 2L cost) to access hotel and export buyers — scale to ₹20-25L by month 8

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
One District One Product (ODOP) scheme
50% subsidy on branding, packaging, certification (up to ₹4L) for registered units; apply via Ministry of Agriculture nodal officer
🏛
PMEGP (PM Employment Generation Programme)
25-35% subsidy on equipment (max ₹25L loan, ₹6.25L subsidy for general/SC) for rice milling setup; apply via nearest KVIC/SIDBI
🏚
Madhya Pradesh ODOP Support Fund
₹2L soft loan for registered producers at 2% interest over 7 years; apply via District Industries Centre (DIC) Balaghat
🏚
MP Krishi Vikas Nidhi
₹50,000 one-time subsidy for farmers/producer groups adopting certified crop varieties; available through district agricultural office
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
