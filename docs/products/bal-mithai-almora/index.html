Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Almora ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Almora town market on Saturday — talk to 3 existing Bal Mithai sellers (ask for shop addresses at any sweet shop), call Almora ODOP nodal officer at district industries office (free consultation), attend Almora Haat on 1st-3rd of month. Zero cost, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source khoya from Phalsima dairy co-op (15 km from Almora town) — ₹350-400/kg, bulk order minimum 10 kg. Pure ghee from Ranikhet dairy union — ₹600-700/kg. Sugar and sugar balls from Haldwani wholesale market (45 km, one-hour drive) — ₹45-55/kg. First batch of 20 kg Bal Mithai costs ₹8,000-10,000 raw material.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 300 sq ft kitchen unit in Almora food market or homestay commercial kitchen setup (₹5,000-8,000/month) OR partner with 3-4 existing artisans on profit-sharing (15-20% of sales). Hand-making batch size is 5 kg per 4-6 hours. To reach 50 units/day, work with 2-3 artisans or invest ₹200,000 for semi-automated khoya-roasting setup.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam at udyamregistration.gov.in (free, 10 minutes) as food processing micro-business. File GI use certificate with Ministry of Food Processing Delhi (₹2,000 filing fee, 6-8 week process). If exporting, apply for IEC at custom's portal using Aadhar (₹0 cost, 1-2 weeks). Total cost ₹2,000, timeline 8 weeks.
5
First Sale
Upload product to Amazon/Flipkart seller portal (15% commission) with GI tag prominently displayed. First order: start with 10 units on Instagram DM to Kumaoni diaspora groups, expect ₹1,500-2,000 in first week. Use first-month revenue (₹8,000-15,000 from 50 units) to buy cardboard boxes (₹1.5/unit) and thermal packaging.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month (280 units/day), hire 2 full-time artisans (₹15,000/month each) and 1 packer (₹8,000/month). Set up dedicated 1200 sq ft rented unit in Haldwani industrial area (₹20,000/month). Apply for PMEGP subsidy (₹10L equipment grant, 35% subsidy) to buy batch-production mixer. Exhibit at Udyam Aavahan fairs, approach Patanjali and Big Basket for B2B contracts, open Instagram shop with Reels on Almora heritage angle.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
₹10-25 lakh equipment subsidy, 35% subsidy for general category, 10% applicant contribution
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹3 lakh cluster support per unit, working capital and machinery, applicable if forming cluster of 5+ producers
🏚
Uttarakhand One District One Product (ODOP) Scheme
50% subsidy on packaging, branding, and quality certification up to ₹1.5 lakh
🏚
Uttarakhand Self Employment Scheme
₹1 lakh revolving fund, ₹50,000 grant for women entrepreneurs, 8% interest subsidy on MUDRA loans up to ₹10 lakh
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
