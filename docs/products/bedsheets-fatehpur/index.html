Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Fatehpur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Fatehpur's established weaving clusters in Auraiya Road and Naubasta area. Study competitors on Amazon and Flipkart selling bedsheets at ₹500-3000 range. Connect with ATMA (Amended Technology Management Association) office in Fatehpur for artisan networks and production insights. Attend fabric expos at Delhi Pragati Maidan or Mumbai ICCW.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source 100% cotton yarn (20-40s count) from cotton mills in Uttar Pradesh such as those in Kanpur and Varanasi at ₹200-300/kg. Purchase synthetic dyes from chemical suppliers in Ahmedabad or Surat. First batch of 500 bedsheets (2.5 lakh meters fabric) will cost approximately ₹8-12 lakhs including dyeing and finishing.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 5-10 power-loom units in Fatehpur's weaving clusters (contact through district MSME office) or establish your own 2-4 loom unit. Engage local artisans for design work and quality checks. Alternatively, work with established manufacturers on contract basis to reduce capital expenditure.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam through udyamregistration.gov.in (free, takes 2 hours). Obtain IEC code from DGFT Lucknow office for export eligibility. Register trademark with IP Office Delhi. Obtain textile certification (BIS ISI mark) for quality assurance. Create GSTIN through GST portal.
5
First Sale
Launch Amazon and Flipkart seller accounts with minimum 100 SKUs across sizes and designs. Create Instagram and Pinterest accounts showcasing designs with lifestyle photography. Start with ₹5000-10000 monthly Facebook/Instagram ads targeting home décor enthusiasts. Build email list through website and offer first-time buyer discount of 15%. Approach 50-100 retail stores in tier-2 cities with wholesale pricing (30% margin).
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand product line to include pillow covers, duvet covers, and cushion covers. Launch premium luxury line priced at ₹5000+ per bedsheet. Establish partnerships with 3-5 major departmental chains (Shoppers Stop, Home Town, Lifestyle). Increase export presence by attending trade shows in UAE, UK, and USA. Hire 2-3 full-time designers to create seasonal collections. Scale production to 5000+ bedsheets/month.
Government Schemes

PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy for manufacturing unit with 25% mandatory personal contribution
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹30 lakh for cluster development with tooling, design, and marketing support
🏚
UP ODOP Scheme
direct procurement support, exhibition participation funds, and brand building assistance for ODOP products
🏚
UP Vishwakarma Incentive Scheme
₹10000-25000 per artisan for skill upgradation and tool provision
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
