Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Auraiya ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Ajitmal or Auraiya block dairy cluster (free visit, arrange via District Dairy Office), call 3-4 established ghee makers, ask production cost and milk sourcing. Call Auraiya Cooperative Milk Union for raw material rates. Register free Udyam account online (takes 15 mins). This week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source milk from Ajitmal, Bhagyanagar, or Sahar blocks (₹140-160/liter for A2 breeds), negotiate 100L daily minimum. Identify 2 backup suppliers. First batch 100L costs ₹15000. Traditional ghee-makers in Auraiya town can guide sourcing.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 500-800 sq ft unit in Auraiya town or near Ajitmal block for ₹5000-8000/month, OR partner with existing artisan maker (60-70% revenue split). Install stainless steel boiler (₹80000), cooling tank (₹60000), packaging station (₹40000). Total: ₹250000-350000 for independent unit, ₹0 for artisan partnership.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 15 mins). Obtain FSSAI license (₹8000-12000, 20 days). Apply for GI registration via ODOP scheme (₹0 govt cost, process through District Industries Centre). Get IEC code for exports (₹0, online at APEDA). Register brand name at State IP office (₹8000-10000). Total 3-4 months.
5
First Sale
First sale: contact nearest modern retail store manager directly with 5L sample ghee for testing. Expect first order 20-30 units if product quality matches. Or list on Blinkit seller portal (upload in 2 days after FSSAI approval), first 10-15 online orders within 1 week. Expected first-month revenue ₹40000-60000 from mixed channels.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 2 production staff (₹8000 each), 1 logistics person (₹10000), invest ₹200000 in second boiler and packaging line. Apply for SFURTI subsidy to cover 50% of this expansion. Exhibit at Agrotech India (annual, Delhi), Global Food Ingredients India (Mumbai) to get bulk orders. Add paneer and khoa products to expand per-customer revenue.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakhs loan at 0% for 7 years, 35% subsidy for SC/ST/women founders
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹75 lakhs cluster subsidy, 90% capex support for modern equipment when 5+ units collaborate
🏚
UP ODOP Scheme
zero-interest working capital loan ₹10-50 lakhs for 3 years, 50% subsidy on IEC and export packaging costs for ODOP products
🏚
UP Dairy Development Scheme
₹5 lakhs subsidy for milk processing units with FSSAI approval, 40% capex support for cooling and storage equipment
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
