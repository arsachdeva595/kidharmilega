Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Alipurduar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Alipurduar furniture clusters near Falakata and Madarihat; meet existing artisans at District Handicraft Development Council office; study furniture production at Siliguri furniture hub (50km away) to understand wholesale channels.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source Sal and Teak wood from local timber merchants in Alipurduar and Siliguri; buy hardware from Siliguri industrial market; first batch ₹2-3L for 10-15 pieces of mixed furniture.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 local woodworking artisans; set up a small 1500 sq ft workshop in Alipurduar town; establish production workflow for tables, chairs, and storage units with 2-3 month lead time.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam on udyamregistration.gov.in (1 day); apply for IEC code via DGFT portal; register brand name on Indian Intellectual Property Office; create FSSAI/BIS certifications if applicable; GST registration.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Handmade and Meesho with 5-8 hero SKUs; create Instagram business profile with workshop and product photography; reach out to interior designers in Kolkata via LinkedIn; partner with 2-3 furniture retailers in Siliguri and Kolkata.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand product range (outdoor furniture, modular designs); hire 2-3 additional artisans; launch B2B corporate gifting program; apply for SFURTI cluster development grant; explore export to SAARC countries via trade bodies.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L loan at 5% interest for manufacturing units with margin money assistance.
🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
₹15K toolkits and ₹5L collateral-free loans for artisans; direct support for traditional craftspeople.
🏚
West Bengal Handicraft Development Corporation (WBHDC) subsidy
10-15% capex subsidy for registered handicraft units.
🏚
West Bengal MSME Cluster Development Scheme
grant support for cluster formation and common infrastructure for furniture artisans.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
