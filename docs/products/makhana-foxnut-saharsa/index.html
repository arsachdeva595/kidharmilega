Makhana (Foxnut) ki
opportunity, Saharsa mein.

Saharsa district sits in northern Bihar's Mithila region, surrounded by extensive wetlands ideal for Euryale ferox cultivation at yields of 2.5–3 tonnes of seed/hectare (yielding ~1 tonne of popped makhana). The 9-cluster makhana belt includes Darbhanga, Mithila, Madhubani, Purnia, Katihar, Supaul, Araria, Sitamarhi, and Saharsa, collectively managing over 25,000 metric tonnes in annual exports to the US, UK, Canada, Australia, UAE, China, South Korea, Thailand, and Europe. With 1,100 exporters and a projected industry growth rate of 9.7% CAGR through 2032, the region controls the global makhana supply chain.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Saharsa ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Darbhanga or Mithila cluster makhana wholesale market on a weekday; call 3 active makhana exporters from the Saharsa ODOP list (available on saharsa.nic.in); attend a local haat in Saharsa town to see raw seed pricing and quality grades. Cost: ₹0. Timeline: 1 week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw makhana seeds directly from Darbhanga wholesale market (2 hours from Saharsa) or Saharsa's own wetland farmers; expect ₹40–60/kg for Grade A seeds; first batch of 50 kg costs ₹2,500–3,500. Suppliers clustered in Darbhanga Mandi and Saharsa town; negotiate 15-day payment terms.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 500 sq ft food-grade kitchen unit in Saharsa town (₹8,000–12,000/month) or partner with 2–3 local artisans on a fixed ₹15–20/kg processing fee; buy a roasting drum (₹40,000–60,000), packaging machine (₹25,000–40,000), and basic grading sieve (₹8,000). Total production setup: ₹75,000–1,50,000 if renting space + equipment; ₹3,00,000–5,00,000 if buying own 1,000 sq ft unit.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME online (free, 30 mins); apply for GI certification under Makhana Saharsa (filing fee ₹4,500, already registered in 2008 — leverage this); register as food business under FSSAI (₹500–2,000, 5 days). If exporting: get IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) from DGFT office in Patna (₹0, online, 1 week). Total formal cost: ₹5,000–10,000.
5
First Sale
First sale via Amazon Seller Central or Flipkart Seller: list 500g pack at ₹299–349; expect 20–30 units in month 1 (₹6,000–10,000 revenue). Simultaneously, approach 2–3 specialty health stores in Patna, Delhi NCR via WhatsApp with samples; target first bulk order of 10 kg to a corporate gifting buyer (₹12,000–15,000 revenue). Month 1 realistic target: ₹18,000–25,000 gross revenue.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 2 full-time processors and 1 sales/ops person (₹25,000–30,000/month total); scale to 1,500 sq ft rented kitchen; apply for PMEGP subsidy (₹10L–25L, 35% subsidy) to upgrade equipment; exhibit at India International Food Fair (Delhi, Oct) and Export Promotion Council trade shows; build email list of 500+ B2B buyers; hire a part-time export consultant (₹15,000/month) for US/UK market entry.
