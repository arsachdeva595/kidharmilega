Minor Forest Produce - Mahua ki
opportunity, Korba mein.

Korba district sits at the heart of India's mahua belt, with dense sal and mixed deciduous forests providing year-round supply of Madhuca longifolia flowers. The Jagdalpur (Bastar) and Ambikapur clusters on the Chhattisgarh-Jharkhand border create a 3-state supplier ecosystem; by May 2018, 26,000 tonnes of mahua flowers had arrived at APMCs with Chhattisgarh contributing 55 percent. Ranchi (Jharkhand), 280 km north, is India's largest mahua price-setter market, and Korba's forest density combined with tribal producer networks ensures direct access to raw materials at wholesale prices.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Korba ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Jagdalpur APMC or Ambikapur mandi (call ahead) to watch mahua trade, speak to 3-4 primary producers about flower quality and availability. Free. Call Korba District APMC (+91-7652-XXX APMC office) or attend a local haat in Lara or Gevra block where mahua is sold. Spend ₹0, spend 2 days.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source dried mahua flowers from Ambikapur or Jagdalpur primary traders at ₹120-160/kg in bulk (25-50 kg first order). Cost: ₹3,000-8,000 for trial batch. For higher-grade flowers meant for distillation, connect with Ranchi wholesalers via phone (0651-XXXX codes) and negotiate direct supply; lead time 7-10 days post-payment.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (₹35-60L): Rent a 1,000-1,500 sqft unit in Raigad or Lara industrial zone; install basic fermentation tanks, distillation setup (if spirits), packaging machines. Hire 2-3 artisans trained in food/spirit production. Option B (₹5-12L): Partner with existing distilleries in Ambikapur or Ranchi for contract production; you handle sourcing, branding, and sales. Option B faster and lower-risk.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online at udyamregistration.gov.in, 15 min). File for Chhattisgarh MSME registration (₹500). If spirits: obtain excise license from Korba District Excise Office (₹20,000-50,000 deposit, 30 days). If export: apply for IEC via FIUEMS (₹0, online, 5 days). GI registration not applicable for mahua yet; skip for now.
5
First Sale
List on Amazon, Flipkart, and own Shopify store by month 2. Target first sale via Instagram influencer partnership (micro-influencers in wellness niche) by week 6. First order: 100-200 units of 500ml bottled craft spirit or mahua oil (500ml bottles). Expected first-month revenue: ₹80K-2.2L depending on channel.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month, hire a part-time supply chain manager (₹25K/month) and a customer service person (₹18K/month). Apply for SFURTI scheme (₹10L subsidy for cluster-based units if located in designated zones). Exhibit at IITF (Delhi, Nov) and specialty food expos (cost: ₹80-120K booth fee). Build B2B relationships with 3-4 large cosmetic or FMCG brands; each contract could be ₹15-40L annually.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L loan at 5% subsidy for new MSME units; apply via KVIC or bank.
🏛
MUDRA Scheme
Shishu/Kishor loans up to ₹50L at low interest (5-7%) without collateral; ideal for scaling after first 6 months.
🏚
Chhattisgarh State Agriculture Department MFP Support Scheme
₹5L subsidy for processing units focused on minor forest produce; direct application to District Agriculture Office.
🏚
Chhattisgarh Industrial Promotion Board MSME Incentive
10% GST refund on equipment purchased for food/cosmetic processing units; valid for 3 years.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
