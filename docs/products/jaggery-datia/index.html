Traditional knowledge passed through generations ensures consistent quality and authentic flavor profiles.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Datia ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Datia's jaggery clusters in Seondha, Lateri, and Aharua villages. Spend 1-2 weeks observing traditional production. Connect with Datia Jaggery Producers Association. Study seasonal cycles (September-March peak season). Cost: ₹5,000-10,000 travel and accommodation.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source sugarcane directly from farmers near Seondha or through cooperative procurement. Identify 2-3 local jaggery makers willing to supply product for initial distribution. First batch: 5 MT (₹150,000-200,000). Establish relationships with 3-4 limestone suppliers in district.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established small jaggery makers in Datia (bulk of startups begin with co-production). Negotiate production terms (15-25% margin for artisans). Alternatively, lease 500 sq.ft. space near sugarcane farms and hire 2-3 experienced workers. Start with 2-3 MT/month capacity. Equipment cost: ₹400,000-800,000 (open pan, storage, packaging).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam via udyamregistration.gov.in (free, <2 weeks). Apply for IEC code via DGFT portal (₹0, import-export eligibility). Get FSSAI license (₹3,000-5,000, 7-10 days). Register brand name with IP India if planning premium positioning. Obtain organic certification from NPOP-accredited bodies (₹20,000-40,000, 3-6 months).
5
First Sale
Week 1-2: Approach 20-30 wholesale buyers in Indore, Gwalior, and Bhopal with samples. Week 2-4: List on Amazon FBA, Flipkart, BigBasket with ₹15/kg wholesale cost, ₹45-60/kg retail pricing. Launch WhatsApp business channel targeting Ayurveda centers and yoga studios (free). Negotiate with local organic stores for placement (20-25% margin). Offer B2B bulk orders on IndiaMART.
6
Scale
Month 6-9: Expand to 5-8 MT/month production. Add value-added line (jaggery powder, fortified blocks). Secure 3-5 institutional contracts with sweet manufacturers. Launch branded e-commerce store with subscription model (₹3,000-5,000 web setup). Hire logistics partner for pan-India delivery. Explore Fair Trade or organic export certification (₹50,000-100,000) to access premium international markets.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy for food processing (25% for gen, 35% SC/ST), loan: ₹75L
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹25L for cluster development, training, and common processing facilities
🏚
Madhya Pradesh Food Processing Policy 2020
₹10L startup subsidy + 5% working capital for food processing micro enterprises, 3-year incentive period
🏚
MP MUDRA Yojana
collateral-free loans up to ₹10L at 8% interest for food processing businesses, Ministry of MSME aligned
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
