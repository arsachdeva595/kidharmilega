Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Poonch ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Poonch district agricultural office and connect with existing peanut farmer clusters in Mendhar, Surankote, and Abbaspur blocks. Study 2-3 established peanut farms to understand yield patterns, seasonal requirements, and local pest management. Attend Directorate of Agriculture Kashmir training workshops on high-altitude legume cultivation.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified peanut seeds from National Seed Corporation depot in Srinagar (₹8,000-12,000 per 50kg). Source organic manure from local composting units in Poonch at ₹150-200 per quintal. First-batch cost for 0.5 hectare cultivation: ₹55,000-75,000 (seeds, manure, labor). Connect with district agriculture department for subsidized inputs through PM-KISAN scheme.
3
Produce / Partner
Establish contract farming arrangement with 10-15 small farmers in Poonch or cultivate 1-2 hectares directly. Invest in basic processing unit (drying, roasting, grading equipment) costing ₹200,000-400,000. Partner with nearby food processing clusters in Jammu (30km away) for value-added product development. Install small-scale oil extraction unit if targeting peanut oil market.
4
Brand & Register
Register as Udyam (online via udyamregistration.gov.in) in agricultural processing category. Obtain Food Safety and Standards Authority (FSSAI) license (₹5,000-15,000) for processed peanut products. Apply for IEC code via DGFT portal (required for exports). Register trademark for brand name with IP Office. Obtain GI certification eligibility document from district commerce department (optional but valuable for premium positioning).
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh, BigBasket, and Flipkart (commission 15-25%) with premium 'Himalayan Peanuts' positioning. Approach local retail chains (Reliance Fresh, Nature's Basket) for direct shelf placement. Create Instagram and Facebook pages with farm stories and health benefits content. Start B2B sales to snack manufacturers in Jammu food corridor via direct outreach. Partner with health food online stores (Urban Platter, NaturalFacts) for marketplace presence.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to 3-5 hectares of cultivation or formalize farmer partnerships with contract agreements. Launch 2-3 value-added SKUs (flavored roasted, peanut butter, oil) based on initial customer feedback. Establish export channel through APEDA-registered agro-export company targeting UK and EU markets (demand for organic Indian peanuts is strong). Implement cold storage facility (₹100,000-150,000) for off-season sales. Build B2B relationships with 5-10 food companies and secure bulk supply contracts.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh loan subsidy for new agricultural processing enterprise with 35% subsidy for general category
🏛
PM-KISAN Samman Nidhi
₹6,000 annual payment to registered farmers for input costs; applicable if cultivating own land
🏚
Jammu & Kashmir New Agri-Export Policy 2021
provides marketing support and subsidy of 15-25% on agro-exports, ideal for targeting international markets
🏚
Jammu & Kashmir Horticultural Department Scheme for Crop Diversification
grants for farmers transitioning to high-value crops like peanuts, up to ₹50,000 per hectare
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Marketing

Ye marketing ideas try karna mat bhulna

  • Create short-form video content on Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts showcasing farm-to-table journey from Poonch fields to packaging, emphasizing altitude and sustainability. Use hashtags #HimalayanNuts #PoonchFarming #HealthySnacking. Partner with micro-influencers (50K-200K followers) in health and nutrition space for product seeding.
  • Launch email marketing campaign targeting health-conscious B2B buyers (snack companies, gyms, nutritionists) with seasonal harvest updates and bulk pricing. Create monthly newsletter highlighting nutritional benefits and recipes. Use Mailchimp or similar tool with 30% discount for first bulk order.
  • Participate in national agricultural expos (Krishi Jagran Mela, AgriTech Summit) and regional trade fairs in Jammu to showcase products and build direct B2B connections. Conduct product sampling and distribute brochures at premium food retail chains and health stores across Kashmir valley and Jammu.
