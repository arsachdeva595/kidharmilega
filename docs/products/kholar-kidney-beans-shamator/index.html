Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Shamator ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Shamator district's agricultural offices and connect with local farming collectives; study kidney bean cultivation practices at Nagaland Agricultural Department (Kohima). Interview 5-10 existing Kholar farmers to understand yields, costs, and supply chains. Research competitive pricing on BigBasket, Amazon Fresh, and organic food platforms.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source premium kidney bean seeds from Nagaland Agricultural Board or certified organic seed suppliers in Northeast India. Establish direct relationships with 2-3 farmer groups in Shamator for first 5-10 quintal batch at ₹4,500-5,500 per quintal (₹2.25-5.5L for initial stock). Negotiate long-term contracts at volume discounts.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing farmer cooperative societies in Shamator (e.g., Shamator Farmers' Collective) for aggregation and light processing. Set up small cleaning, grading, and packaging unit (1,000-2,000 sq ft) in Kohima or district town. Invest in food-grade storage and packaging machinery (₹4-8L). Alternatively, contract manufacturing with certified FSSAI-approved agro-processors in Nagaland.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam on udyamregistration.gov.in (free, online). Apply for IEC code via DGFT portal (₹500). Obtain FSSAI license (₹500-2,000 for small business). Register brand name with IP India. Get organic certification from NABL-accredited body (₹20-40K). Ensure packaging complies with FSS Act labeling requirements.
5
First Sale
Launch D2C via Shopify or WooCommerce website. Register on BigBasket, Blinkit, Amazon Fresh (seller panels). Approach organic food distributors and premium grocery chains in Bangalore, Delhi, Mumbai. Create LinkedIn B2B profile and contact food processors, restaurants. Partner with regional e-commerce platforms like Nagaland Online. Run Instagram/Facebook campaigns targeting health-conscious urban consumers (₹5-10K/month budget).
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to 3-4 new states via distributor partnerships. Launch value-added products (Kholar flour, roasted snacks). Pursue GI certification to strengthen premium positioning. Invest in automated packaging (₹15-25L) to increase production capacity 3x. Explore B2B partnerships with food manufacturers, meal kit companies. Establish export channels with trade bodies like APEDA for ASEAN and US markets.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (25-35% of project cost) for food processing/agriculture-based business in Shamator district.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹25L grant for agro-processing clusters; applicable if partnering with farmer groups in Shamator.
🏚
Nagaland State Agriculture & Horticulture Mission
subsidized seeds, organic certification support, and export incentives for high-value crops like kidney beans.
🏚
Nagaland MSME Promotion Scheme
5% interest subsidy on MUDRA loans (up to ₹10L) for food processing units in rural districts like Shamator.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
