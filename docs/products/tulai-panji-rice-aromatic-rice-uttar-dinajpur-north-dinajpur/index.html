Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Uttar Dinajpur (North Dinajpur) ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Uttar Dinajpur's Kaliaganj and Hemtabad blocks (major cultivation areas). Connect with Uttar Dinajpur Producers Company Ltd and local farmer cooperatives. Study market at Kolkata's New Market and specialty rice retailers. Attend agricultural fairs at Raiganj.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source paddy from certified Tulai Panji farmers in Kaliaganj block (₹35-40/kg for quality seed stock). First batch: 2-3 tons paddy = ₹75,000-₹90,000. Establish direct farmer contracts for consistent supply year-round.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with MILLING: Contact rice mills in Raiganj and Islampur (provide dehulling, grading, sorting). OR START SMALL: Lease 5,000 sq ft space in Raiganj, install small huller and polisher (₹12-18L). Hire 4-5 workers. Use traditional sun-drying to preserve aroma.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, free). Apply for IEC from DGFT office in Kolkata. File GI-protected usage license with West Bengal DPIIT. Obtain FSSAI license for food processing (₹5,000-₹8,000). Trademark brand name with IP cell in Delhi.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon/Flipkart as 'Tulai Panji Premium Rice' (₹200-300/500g). Create Instagram/YouTube content on heritage/aroma/recipes. Pitch to premium hotels in Kolkata, Delhi, Bangalore (HQ email outreach). Join online B2B platforms (IndiaTradeKey, TradeKey). Partner with specialty gifting brands.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand SKUs (organic, certified, smaller premium packs for gifting). Negotiate export contracts via trade bodies (APEDA). Build direct relationships with 2-3 NRI import houses. Launch subscription model for high-value customers. Set up dedicated e-commerce website for brand control.
