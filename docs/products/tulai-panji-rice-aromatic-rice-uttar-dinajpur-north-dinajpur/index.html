North Dinajpur also has 500+ small-scale rice mills and processors. Primary buyers are Bengali diaspora across India, institutional clients in Delhi-NCR, and export markets like Bangladesh (40% of regional sales) and Nepal (15%).

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Uttar Dinajpur (North Dinajpur) ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call 3 rice millers in Raiganj (contact via Uttar Dinajpur Chamber of Commerce), visit Kaliaganj gram panchayat office to understand farmer networks, attend Friday haat in Raiganj town to see raw grain pricing. Zero cost, doable in 1 visit.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw Tulai Panji paddy from farmer cooperatives in Kaliaganj block (Kaliaganj Krishi Unnayan Kendra) at ₹28-32/kg paddy. First batch: 5-10 MT costs ₹1.4-3.2L. Processing (husking + drying + sorting) done by established mills in Raiganj industrial cluster at ₹3-4/kg. Net first-batch cost (rice): ₹1.8-2.4L for 5 MT usable stock.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Partner with existing co-registered miller (Raiganj MSME cluster has 20+ licensed units) — ₹15-20k/month rental + 10% of milling margin. Option B: Rent 500 sq.ft. space in Raiganj industrial estate (₹6-8k/month) and contract milling. Avoid owning equipment initially. Total operational cost: ₹2-3L for 3 months before first sale.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME (free, 15 mins online via udyamregistration.gov.in). Apply for GI registration certificate copy from Ministry of Agriculture (already granted 2017 — obtain certified copy from Geographical Indications Registry, Chennai, cost ₹500-1000, 2 weeks). Apply for IEC code from DGFT (₹500, 1 week) if exporting. Total: ₹1500-2000, 3 weeks.
5
First Sale
First sale: List on Amazon Fresh (250 units min, 500g pack) or directly approach Bengal Sweet Stores in Kolkata (10-15 stores, 50kg trial order). Expected first order: 50-100kg at ₹120/kg wholesale = ₹6-12k gross. Expected first-month revenue (3-4 orders): ₹25-35k.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 packaging + quality checker (₹12-15k/month), invest ₹50k in vacuum sealing equipment, open direct account with 2-3 large retail chains (ITC Aashirvaad, Modern Bazaar). Apply for PMEGP subsidy (₹50L max, 25% subsidy for SC/ST/women, 15% general) or SFURTI cluster scheme (₹15-25L fund for group units). Exhibit at Kolkata Food & Hospitality Expo (Jan/Feb) and Mumbai Food Expo (Mar).

Government Schemes

up to ₹50L loan with 25-35% subsidy for SC/ST/women entrepreneurs, covers equipment and working capital
🏛
MUDRA Loan Scheme
up to ₹10L unsecured loan at 7-10% interest for processing and packaging equipment, no collateral

10% interest subvention on loans up to ₹10L for agri-businesses, reimbursement after 6 months operation

10% capital subsidy up to ₹30L for food processing units in Tier 2/3 towns
