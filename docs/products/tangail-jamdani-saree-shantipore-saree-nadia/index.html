Nadia district (3,927 sq km, 51.67 lakh population) in North Bengal is home to three active handloom clusters: Phulia (30km south of Krishnanagar), Tangail (district center), and Santiniketan (40km south). Raw materials — silk yarns, muslin, and gold thread — are sourced from suppliers in Kolkata (90km) and Tamil Nadu (1,200km by rail). Kolkata port connects to 40+ international destinations; domestic buyers concentrated in Delhi, Mumbai, Hyderabad. Annual market stands at ₹50 crore with 15,000+ artisans producing 2,000+ sarees monthly.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Nadia ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Tangail cluster (district center) and spend 1-2 days at Phulia haat on Friday morning — talk to 5-10 weavers, ask current saree prices, turnaround times, raw material costs. Call Biren Kumar Basak's workshop (enquire via Nadia DC Handloom office). Join ODOP WhatsApp group for Nadia sarees. Cost: ₹0, travel only.
2
Source Raw Materials
Contact artisans in Phulia or Tangail cluster directly — source 5-10 sample sarees at ₹8K-15K per piece depending on design complexity. Visit 2-3 silk yarn wholesalers on Rabindra Sarani, Kolkata (90km) to lock raw material supply at ₹2K-4K per kg. Total first-batch cost: ₹80K-120K for 10 sarees.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Partner with 3-5 artisans in Phulia/Tangail cluster — give them yarn, design, timeline (30-60 days per saree), pay ₹8K-12K per piece. Option B: Lease 500 sq ft weaving unit in cluster (₹3K-5K monthly) and hire 2-3 senior weavers. Cost range: ₹50K-200K monthly for 5-10 sarees depending on design.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (free, 10 mins). Apply for GI registration support via DC Handloom Nadia (₹0 direct cost, ministry covers). Get business PAN from local income tax office (₹0). If exporting: apply for IEC from DGFT Kolkata office (₹5K-10K, 7 days), open EEFC bank account (₹0), register with RCMC. Timeline: 30-45 days total.
5
First Sale
List 10-15 sample sarees on Instagram shop (₹0 startup) with WhatsApp order link. Simultaneously approach 2-3 boutiques in Kolkata, Delhi (Chandni Chowk) — arrange visit, show samples. Target first order: 5 sarees at ₹15K-25K each = ₹75K-125K. Ship via Shiprocket or BlueDart. Expected first-month revenue: ₹1-2L if 2-3 orders land.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 1 business manager (₹20K/month), 1 quality checker (₹12K/month), set up e-commerce on Shopify (₹3K/month). Attend 2-3 wedding expos yearly (Delhi, Mumbai — ₹50K-100K booth cost). Partner with 1-2 export agents for US/UK. Launch custom order service (₹100K+ per saree). Collaborate with influencers in wedding niche for content (5-10 pieces at ₹12K each for content).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹15,000 initial credit + ₹1.5L working capital loan at 0% interest for first year, 5% after. Artisans under scheme.
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25% subsidy on equipment cost up to ₹50L for manufacturing, 35% for SC/ST. Process: Apply through District Handloom Office.
🏚
West Bengal Handloom Weaver Welfare Board
₹5,000 annual assistance to registered weavers, free health insurance
🏚
West Bengal One District One Product (ODOP) Scheme
Free GI registration support, export promotion subsidy (10-15% on FOB value), training programs via DC Handloom
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
