Cotton Yarn ki
opportunity, Mansa mein.

Mansa district (2,174 sq km, population 7.7L) is located in Punjab's prime cotton-growing region and serves as a distribution hub for yarn moving to fabric mills across northern India, Gujarat, and export markets. Raw cotton is sourced locally or from nearby Bathinda and Rajasthan within 100-200 km radius. Existing spinning mills cluster around Mansa town and nearby Malout tehsil, creating shared supplier networks and buyer relationships. The district connects to buyers in Ludhiana textile hub (120 km), Panipat (250 km), and Surat (800 km) via NH21 and rail.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Mansa ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call 3 spinning mills in Mansa town and Malout — ask about their sourcing, staffing, and buyer relationships. Visit Mansa Cotton Market (local haat) on Tuesday/Friday to watch raw cotton trading. Register free Udyam account online (udyamregistration.gov.in). Zero cost, takes 1 week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw cotton from Mansa-Bathinda cotton traders (₹45-55 per kg for decent quality); chemical suppliers in Ludhiana (dyes ₹200-500/kg, sizing ₹80-120/kg). First batch: 2-5 MT cotton = ₹9-25L. Negotiable credit terms if you reference an existing mill.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (own unit): Rent a 2,000-3,000 sq ft unit in Mansa MSME cluster (₹8-15K/month), install 1-2 spinning frames (₹18-35L each = ₹36-70L total). Hire 5-8 skilled spinners (₹12-18K/month each). Option B (partnership): Partner with an existing mill on revenue-share (you handle buying, they spin). Start with option B if capital is ₹25L; option A needs ₹50L+.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 30 min online). Get GST registration (₹0, online, 5 days). Get BIS certification for cotton yarn if bulk-selling (₹30-50K, 2-3 months). Apply for IEC code (Importer Exporter Code) if exporting (₹0-5K, 10 days via customs.gov.in). Consider GI registration if your mill cluster wants collective branding (₹1-2L, 12-18 months, ministry may subsidy 50%).
5
First Sale
First sale: Contact 2-3 fabric mills in Ludhiana via phone (get numbers from local traders) — offer samples. First order likely ₹2-5L (500-1,000 kg at ₹120-150/kg net). List on TradeKey.com and Global Sources simultaneously — expect first export inquiry in 4-6 weeks. First-month revenue if you land one mill order = ₹2-5L.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: Hire a sales manager (₹25-35K/month) to manage mill relationships and exports. Invest in quality testing lab (₹8-12L) to offer certified yarn grades. Attend Intl Textile Trade Fairs (Texfair India, IGTF Bangalore) twice yearly. Apply for PMEGP or SFURTI subsidy (₹10-25L grant) to expand to 2-3 spinning frames. Target ₹30-50L/month by month 18-24.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
₹10-25L subsidy (25% to general, 35% to SC/ST) for setup cost, payable within 5 years
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹15-50L cluster-level subsidy for 10+ mills to jointly upgrade machinery and branding
🏚
Punjab Technology Upgradation Scheme
15% subsidy on new spinning machinery if mill is in Punjab, applicable to modernization
🏚
Punjab Export Promotion Scheme
5% rebate on export duties and 10% subsidy on international logistics for textiles exported from Punjab
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
