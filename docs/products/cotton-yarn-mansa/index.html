Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Mansa ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit existing cotton mills in Mansa city and Malout region; connect with Punjab Cotton Mills Association; attend India Expo Centre textile trade shows; study competitor yarn counts (20s, 30s, 40s) and quality standards
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw cotton from Punjab Agricultural Department or traders in Bathinda; establish relationship with suppliers in Mansa cotton market; first batch requirement: 50,000-100,000 kg cotton at ₹5,500-6,500/quintal = ₹27.5-65 lakhs
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing cotton mills in Mansa (Surjit Cotton Mills, Lakshmi Spinning Mills) for toll manufacturing initially; alternatively invest in small spinning unit with 1,000-5,000 spindle capacity; hire trained mill operators from local textile workforce
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME (micro if <₹25L investment); obtain IEC code from DGFT for exports; get Textile Committee registration; register trademark with IP India; obtain ISO 9001 certification for quality assurance
5
First Sale
Join TradeKey and Global Sources for B2B export leads; list on Alibaba and Made-in-India; approach textile mills through direct sales with samples; participate in India International Yarn Expo (IIYE); leverage industry associations for referrals
6
Scale
Expand spindle capacity from 1,000 to 10,000+ spindles; invest in quality testing labs (AFIS, tensile testing); develop value-added products (organic cotton yarn, blended yarns); establish distribution network across India; target European and American textile brands

PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh loan with 25% subsidy for manufacturing units
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
grants up to ₹40 lakh for textile clusters and common facilities
🏚
Punjab Industrial Development Scheme
capital subsidy up to 15% and interest subsidy for textile manufacturing
🏚
Punjab Export Promotion Scheme
incentives and support for textile exports and FTA compliance
