Dharmapuri is located on Tamil Nadu's eastern plateau, 165 km from Chennai, with direct access to steel mills, casting foundries, and electronics suppliers within a 300 km radius. The district hosts hundreds of small and medium auto component units certified to IATF 16949 and ISO 14001, many clustered near industrial estates. Major OEM plants in southern Tamil Nadu (Sriperumbudur, Ranipet) source components from Dharmapuri units; exports to Europe, ASEAN, and Middle East already represent 35-40% of shipments from the region.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Dharampuri ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Dharmapuri auto cluster in town or Harur MSME hub (free; call District Industries Centre at 04342-246000 to get cluster contact). Call 3-4 small unit owners on WhatsApp/phone (find contacts via Tamil Nadu MSME directory). Attend monthly auto component trade meet at Harur or Chennai auto hub; check SIAM (Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers) events calendar.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source steel sheets from Jai Bhim Steel (Harur) or JSW Steel dealers; casting blanks from local foundries in Harur or Hosur (90 km away); electronics from component suppliers in Bangalore/Hosur industrial corridor. First batch 500-1000 units: ₹4-6L for raw materials including 20% wastage buffer. Lead time 15-20 days.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 1000-2000 sq ft unit in Harur MSME Industrial Estate or Dharmapuri town industrial park at ₹8-12K/month. Minimum tooling (dies, fixtures): ₹8-15L if buying used CNC/lathe machines. OR partnership with artisan unit in existing cluster: 20-30% revenue share on first 6 months, ₹0 upfront capex. Manual machining + semi-automated assembly setup can start at ₹15-20L total, producing 50-100 units/day.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online in 15 min). Open IEC code with DGFT (₹0 fee, 3-5 days processing). ISO 9001 and IATF 16949 certification costs ₹2-5L but NOT mandatory in year 1 — get them once landing orders. Consider GI registration support via Tamil Nadu MSME directorate if making specialty components (₹1-2L subsidy available).
5
First Sale
First sale via IndiaMART or Tradeindia to local auto parts dealers; typical first order 100-200 units for ₹3-8L. List 5-10 SKUs with 2-3 high-res photos and detailed specs (dimensions, material, applications). Target first month: ₹2-5L gross revenue from 3-4 orders. Second month: approach Tier 2 suppliers directly with sample units and ISO docs.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 1 quality checker + 1 shop supervisor (₹8-12K each). Apply for PMEGP subsidy or SFURTI cluster grant (₹5-10L subsidy available). Attend SIAM auto expos and Auto Cluster meet, Bangalore (Feb-Mar). Invest in basic CNC machine (₹15-25L) to reduce dependency on job work. Register for PLI scheme if producing EV-specific components (6% of market growth).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
Production Linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme
4-6% incentive on incremental sales for automotive components; specifically encourages EV-related component production
🏛
PMEGP (Pradhan Mantri Employment Generation Programme)
₹25L subsidy (max) at 5% interest for manufacturing MSME; 10-15% subsidy on equipment
🏚
Tamil Nadu MSME Development Policy
₹5-10L cluster development grants; ₹2-5L for ISO/IATF certification support for auto component makers
🏚
Guidance Industrial Estate Support
₹3-8L for factory shed rental subsidy in notified MSME clusters for first 3 years
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

₹15-30L minimum: ₹1-1.5L for factory rent (3 months advance), ₹8-15L for basic machines (lathe, drill, grinder — used OK), ₹4-6L for raw material first batch, ₹1-2L for tools/fixtures, ₹1L for Udyam+IEC+testing. Can start smaller (₹10-12L) if renting time on existing machines.
Ideal: 2-3 years in auto component production or mechanical background. NOT mandatory if you hire a skilled shop supervisor (₹10-12K/month) with 10+ years experience. First 3 months: you learn processes, second month onwards: supervisor runs daily ops.
PMEGP: ₹25L subsidy (max). SFURTI: ₹5-10L for cluster development. PLI Scheme: 4-6% incentive on EV component sales. Tamil Nadu gives ₹2-5L for ISO/IATF certs. PM Vishwakarma (if artisan): ₹5L term loan at 8% for tool+setup.
IndiaMART, TradeIndia listings (₹5-10K/month fee) bring local garage orders. Harur MSME association can connect to 50+ Tier 2 suppliers. Cold call Bosch/Continental vendors in Bangalore/Hosur. Attend SIAM trade meets in Chennai. Expect first order in 20-30 days.
Bilkul. Auto components are India's second-largest export item (USD 21.2B/year, FY24). Get IEC code (₹0, 3-5 days). Get IATF 16949 cert (₹2-5L, 2-3 months). Use Global Sources / Alibaba B2B to find ASEAN/Europe buyers. FOB pricing 35-50% margin. First export order: 2-4 months after certification.
Real risk: weak quality = no repeat orders. Mitigation: hire quality manager by month 2. Second risk: OEM order dependency — one customer cancels, income drops 40%. Counter: build 3-5 small customer base in parallel. Exit: sell machines and molds to another unit (depreciated 30-40% in 18 months, but recoverable). Best case: scale to ₹50L annual and sell unit to larger supplier at 2-3x revenue multiple.
