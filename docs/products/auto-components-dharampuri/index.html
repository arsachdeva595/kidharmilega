Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Dharampuri ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Dharmapuri SIPCOT industrial area and established manufacturers like Amalgamated Engineering, Sona Comstar facilities. Attend SIAM (Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers) automotive component conferences. Study ISO/TS 16949 certification requirements for automotive suppliers.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source cast iron and aluminum from Coimbatore foundries (₹15-20L for initial batch). Source fasteners and bearings from Chennai-based component suppliers. Establish relationships with local SIPCOT vendors. First batch raw material cost approximately ₹25-30L.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established Dharmapuri manufacturers for contract manufacturing initially. Set up facility with CNC machining, precision grinding, and inspection equipment. Alternatively, establish 5,000-10,000 sq ft manufacturing unit with 20-30 skilled workers in SIPCOT industrial park.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam for MSME classification. Obtain IEC code from DGFT for export capability. Apply for ISO/TS 16949:2016 or equivalent quality certification. Register business name with TNATA (Tamil Nadu Small Industries Association). Develop technical datasheets and quality certifications.
5
First Sale
Approach auto OEMs in Sriperumbudur, Chennai automotive cluster directly with samples and certifications. List on B2B platforms like TradeKey, GlobalSources, and AutoZone B2B. Partner with established automotive distributors in major cities. Participate in SIAM automotive supply chain exhibitions. Target aftermarket sellers on Flipkart, Amazon Business.
6
Scale
Achieve ISO/TS 16949 certification to unlock Tier-1 OEM contracts worth ₹5-10Cr annually. Expand product line to high-margin items like electronic control modules and advanced suspension components. Set up second facility in other automotive clusters. Establish export operations to South-East Asia and Europe.
