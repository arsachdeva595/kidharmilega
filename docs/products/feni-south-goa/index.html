Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — South Goa ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Feni production clusters in Ponda, Sattari, and Quepem (South Goa) to meet established distillers and master artisans. Spend 2-4 weeks understanding fermentation science, aging processes, and local regulations. Connect with Goa Cashew Association and Goa Food Processing Association for industry insights. Study competitors like Diageo-owned brands and local family producers.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source cashew apples and coconut palms from local farmers in Ponda and surrounding villages (cost: ₹2-4L for first 1000 liters of quality toddy). Establish contracts with 3-4 suppliers to ensure consistent supply year-round. First-batch production cost: ₹8-12L including raw materials, yeast cultures, and bottling.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with licensed existing Feni distillers in South Goa for contract manufacturing initially (reduces capex to ₹5-10L for bottling/labeling facility). Alternatively, obtain Feni distillery license from Goa Alcohol Beverages Export Council (GAPEC) and set up small production unit with traditional copper stills. Hire trained fermentation technicians from established producers.
4
Brand & Register
Register as Udyam (MSME) and obtain IEC from DGFT for exports. Apply for Feni producer license with South Goa excise department. Create brand identity emphasizing GI protection and heritage. Obtain FSSAI food safety license and alcohol manufacturing license (IMFL under Goa Alcohol Act). Trademark brand name. Cost: ₹50-75K.
5
First Sale
Launch on Whisper App and The Alcohol Co. (premium spirits platforms) with professional photography and 200-500ml bottle SKUs. Approach 20-30 premium bars and restaurants in Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore directly with samples. Create Instagram/YouTube content showcasing traditional distillation process. List on MasterCard's Priceless platform and Sotheby's wine marketplace. Target international distributors via LinkedIn and spirits trade shows.
6
Scale
After 6 months, establish distribution in 3-4 metros through specialized alcohol distributors (5-7% margin). Launch limited-edition aged variants (3yr, 5yr vintage). Export to 2-3 countries via global spirits distributors (research DTC shipping logistics). Partner with 2-3 luxury hotels for exclusive house spirits. Build community via Feni tasting events and whiskey club partnerships. Aim for ₹2-5Cr revenue by Year 2.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L loan at subsidized rates for manufacturing unit setup; particularly useful for distillery infrastructure (stills, bottling lines).
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
grants up to ₹10L for artisan cluster development; applicable since Feni is traditional product with GI status.
🏚
Goa Agri-Export Policy (2022)
incentives including 5% subsidy on export freight and marketing support for food & beverage exports; direct support for Feni exporters.
🏚
Goa Tourism & Heritage Product Grant
support for traditional products marketed as heritage; up to ₹5L for branding, packaging, and promotional activities for GI-certified products like Feni.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
