Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — South Goa ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sattari taluk feni cluster (Pilar-Pernem road) or call Goa ODOP SPOC at Industries Officer-I, Directorate of Industries, Trade & Commerce, Panaji — attend free ODOP orientation. Zero cost, 1-2 days.
2
Source Raw Materials
Contact licensed suppliers in Sattari or Pernem taluks for raw cashew apples (Aug-Dec) at ₹8-12 per kg and coconut palms year-round. First batch: 500 kg fruit = ₹4,000-6,000. Supplier names available via Goa Cashew Export Association or District Horticulture Office.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Partner with existing licensed artisan producer for 30-40% revenue share (lowest capex). Option B: Rent 800-1200 sqft unit in industrial areas near Vasco or Margao (₹8-12k/month), buy fermentation vats (₹80k), setup storage (₹1.5-2L). 2-4 week fermentation cycle per batch.
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam (free, 30 mins online). File GI usage application with Goa Industries Dept (₹5k, 2-3 months). Obtain FSSAI license (₹10k, 3-4 weeks). If exporting: IEC registration via DGFT portal (₹500, 1 week) and customs clearance at Mormugao. Total compliance cost: ₹50-80k over 4-5 months.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Wines & Spirits (3-4 week approval) with 6-12 bottle first order at ₹900/bottle (60% margin). Parallel: pitch to 3-5 premium bars in Goa at ₹800/bottle (40% margin) with 20-30 bottle opening order. Expected first month: ₹40-60k revenue from 50-80 bottles.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 1 operations manager (₹25k/month), scale to 3-4 simultaneous batches, apply for PMEGP subsidy for capex expansion, exhibit at India International Spirits Trade Show and Pro Pack India. Approach 2-3 EU importers via Wines & Spirits specialist brokers. Build influencer partnerships with mixology accounts on Instagram and YouTube.
