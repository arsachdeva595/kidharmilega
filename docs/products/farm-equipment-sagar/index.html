Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sagar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sagar equipment cluster in Rehli town (30km from Sagar city center) — meet 3-4 existing manufacturers, ask about raw material suppliers, visit the district ODOP nodal office near Collector's office to understand govt support. Call State Agricultural Department procurement desk in Bhopal — ask what equipment they procure. Free, doable this week via local auto-rickshaw.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source hydraulic cylinders, electric motors, and forgings from Indore industrial area (120km, 3-4 hour drive) — first batch of 20 units costs ₹3.5-4.2L. Alternative: source raw steel from Jabalpur scrap dealers (90km). Start with one supplier relationship; negotiate 30-day payment terms.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 1,200-1,500 sqft workshop in Rehli (₹4,000-6,000/month) or partner with existing artisan who owns machinery (revenue share 60:40). Basic setup: welding station, lathe, testing jig, assembly table. Initial 2-3 months: produce 8-12 units, cost ₹50-70K per unit including labor.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (₹0, free) within 1 week. Apply for ODOP GI registration support via District Industries Center Sagar (₹5,000-15,000 govt subsidy covers 80% of GI filing cost, 3-4 month process). Get IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) from nearest DGFT office in Bhopal for export readiness (₹0, online, 2 days). Formal branding: ₹20-40K for logo, website, business cards.
5
First Sale
First sale: contact State Agricultural Department procurement portal or attend Krishi Vigyan Kendra fairs in MP cities. Post product on Agritech platforms (BigsIsBetter, FarmERP). First order size typically 5-8 units. Expected first-month revenue ₹1.5-2.2L (4-5 units at ₹35-45K each wholesale). Direct farmer contact via agricultural society WhatsApp groups — negotiate ₹50-65K per unit with 50% advance.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: shift to 3,500 sqft space, hire 2 semi-skilled machinists (₹12-15K/month each), 1 QA person. Attend AIATSE (All India Agricultural Technologists and Scientists Expo) in Delhi, Krishi Dharma expo Indore. Build export pipeline: contact Nepal farm machinery dealers via email, Bangladesh agricultural ministries. Invest ₹50K in exportable product certification (CE/ISO equivalent).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Program)
25% subsidy on machinery and equipment up to ₹25L total project cost for SC/ST/Women founders, 35% for backward regions
🏛
MUDRA Scheme
collateral-free loan up to ₹10L at 8-9% interest for manufacturing micro-enterprises, 7-year repayment
🏚
Madhya Pradesh One District One Product (ODOP) Scheme
50% subsidy on brand registration, export fair visits, packaging; additional 20% credit subsidy for SC/ST beneficiaries
🏚
MP SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹5-25L cluster development grant for artisan groups, includes skill training and common facility centers
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
