Churu district has a centuries-old tradition of woodworking with access to high-quality timber sources including teak, rosewood, and mango wood. The region's skilled artisans have preserved traditional carving techniques passed down through generations, making it an ideal hub for authentic wooden product manufacturing. The district's proximity to timber markets in North India and established craftsmanship networks provide a competitive advantage.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Churu ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit the Churu wood artisan clusters in Taranagar and Ratangarh towns to understand existing product lines, pricing, and artisan networks. Connect with the District Industries Centre (DIC) Churu and MSME Development Institute. Study competitor products on Etsy and Woodcraft India platforms.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source seasoned teak and rosewood from timber merchants in Bikaner (90 km away) or established suppliers in Churu town. First batch budget: ₹2-3 lakhs for 5-6 quintals of mixed wood. Build relationships with local sawmill operators for bulk pricing discounts.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with master artisans in Taranagar who specialize in traditional carving techniques. Start with 3-4 skilled artisans producing 15-20 pieces monthly. Alternatively, rent a 2000-3000 sqft workshop space in Churu city (₹5000-8000/month) and hire trained carpenters. Invest in basic hand tools and one pneumatic carving machine (₹2-3 lakhs).
4
Brand & Register
Register MSME Udyam online for GST and government benefits. Obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Rajasthan for export eligibility. Create brand identity with Hindi-Marwari cultural elements. Get ISO 9001 certification for quality assurance to boost export credibility.
5
First Sale
List 10-15 hero products on Amazon, Flipkart, and Etsy with professional photography. Launch Instagram and Pinterest accounts showcasing artisan stories and behind-the-scenes carving videos. Pitch custom products to interior design firms and 5-star hotel chains in Delhi-NCR. Join B2B platforms like IndiaBizClub and Global Sources.
6
Scale
After ₹20L revenue, expand product range to include kitchen accessories and toys. Build direct relationships with 2-3 bulk corporate buyers for annual contracts. Open a showroom in Delhi or Jaipur for B2B clients. Hire a dedicated export manager to manage international orders and documentation.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh government subsidy (35% for SC/ST/women) for manufacturing setup
🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
₹15,000 toolkits and interest-free credit up to ₹3 lakh for registered artisans and craftspeople
🏚
Rajasthan Udyam Vikas Yojana
10-15% capital subsidy on plant and machinery investment for MSME units in manufacturing sector
🏚
Rajasthan Industrial Investment Policy
VAT/SGST exemption for 5 years on wooden products exports from notified districts
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Marketing

Ye marketing ideas try karna mat bhulna

  • Create 15-30 second Reels on Instagram showing real artisans carving intricate wooden pieces, emphasizing heritage and sustainability. Use hashtags like #IndianCrafts #SustainableDesign #TarangarArts to reach eco-conscious buyers.
  • Partner with 10-15 interior design influencers on Instagram (50K-500K followers) for product collaborations and home tour features. Offer 20-30% discount in exchange for authentic product reviews and shoots.
  • Launch a corporate gifting campaign targeting logistics, IT, and pharma companies in NCR. Create catalog PDFs for bulk orders (minimum 50 pieces) with custom engraving options. Use LinkedIn for B2B outreach and webinars on sustainable corporate gifting.
