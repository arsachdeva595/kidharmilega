Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — West Garo Hills ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit cashew clusters in Tura (West Garo Hills district HQ) and connect with existing farmers; study processing units in Shillong and Assam; visit Cashew Research Station Puttur in Karnataka online for knowledge; join APEDA's cashew exporter networks
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw cashew nuts directly from local farmers in Tura, Gasuapara, and Baghmara blocks during Oct-Feb harvest; negotiate bulk rates of ₹180-220/kg for first batch of 2-5 MT; establish relationships with 3-4 farmer cooperatives for consistent supply
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established cashew processing units in Guwahati (2-3 hrs away) for initial roasting, shelling, and grading or invest ₹12-18L in semi-automated processing setup; hire 4-6 trained workers; aim for first 500kg batch within 60 days
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Micro/Small) within 48 hrs online via udyamregistration.gov.in; apply for IEC code via APEDA within 5-7 days; trademark brand name via Indian IP office; obtain FSSAI registration for ₹5000-12000; get GradeA organic certification if targeting premium segment
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon, Flipkart, Blink, and Jiomart with 300-500 product listings; partner with regional premium organic retailers (Naturals, Health & Glow); contact hotel groups in Shillong/Guwahati with bulk samples; reach out to NRI gift boxers via LinkedIn; list on IndiaStack for B2B wholesale
6
Scale
After month 6, expand product line (cashew butter, candied cashews, flavored variants); open WhatsApp Business account with 500+ direct customers; negotiate co-packing agreements with larger processors; target 2-3 export contracts worth ₹50L+ with Vietnam/Indonesia; launch subscription boxes; build influencer partnerships with food bloggers
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy for micro-enterprise setup with 25% margin money contribution
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
grants for agricultural clusters and processing units, up to ₹25L for cashew processing infrastructure
🏚
Meghalaya State Agricultural Processing Policy
10-15% capital subsidy for agri-processing units and export-oriented businesses in rural districts
🏚
Meghalaya Industries Investment Promotion Policy 2012
5-10 year tax holidays and infrastructure support for agricultural entrepreneurs in West Garo Hills
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
