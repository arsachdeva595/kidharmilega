Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Belagavi ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call 3-4 foundry owners in Belgaum central zone (Old Belgaum area has 40+ operating units); visit Belgaum MSME cluster office or contact Belgaum Chamber of Commerce to get introducer letters. Attend monthly vendor meets at local automobile parts cluster — attend free, listen for 2 hours, get real cost structure.
2
Source Raw Materials
Iron ore and coal sourced from Ballari district suppliers (120km away) at ₹8-12/kg; scrap metal from Belgaum recycling yards (Belagaum Hosamandi); silica sand from Kanakangi (60km). First-batch cost for 5 tonnes of mixed castings: ₹75,000-1,20,000 including raw material + fuel.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 2000-3000 sq ft foundry unit in Belgaum MSME industrial park at ₹15-25k/month (shared) or ₹35-50k (dedicated) with power connection. Hire a foundry supervisor (₹12-18k/month) and 4-5 furnace workers (₹8-12k each). Induction furnace + moulding equipment + sand recycler: ₹18-35L. Operating cost per tonne: ₹8-12k (fuel, labour, materials).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (free, 15 minutes). Apply for PM Vishwakarma scheme for ₹3L subsidy if eligible. For export, apply for IEC from DGFT Bangalore office (₹0, 7-10 days). GI registration application through Belagaum Chamber (₹15-25k, 2-3 years — defer for first 2 years). Obtain BIS mark optional initially (₹50k+ but not mandatory for wholesale).
5
First Sale
First sale: contact 3 automotive Tier 2 suppliers in Belgaum district with sample castings; typical first order 500-1000 kg at ₹45/kg = ₹22,500-45,000. Post 10-15 verified samples on IndiaMART with ₹48-55/kg price; expect 2-3 first inquiries within week. First-month target: 2-3 tonnes = ₹1,20,000 revenue at 30% gross margin = ₹36,000 profit.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month (200+ tonnes), hire 2 additional supervisors and 8-10 more workers. Invest ₹15-20L in second furnace. Get ISO 9001 certification (₹1-1.5L, brings buyer trust). Exhibit at Indian Automobile Parts Manufacturers Association shows in Chennai and Pune (₹3-5L annually). Engage export trading house as partner; target 30% of output to export by month 18.

Haan, easily. Foundry castings are high-margin exports (₹60-80/kg vs ₹45-55 domestic). Main buyers: USA (automotive suppliers), Germany (machinery), UK (industrial equipment). Process: Get IEC (free, 10 days from DGFT Bangalore), join export trading house or find direct buyer via Alibaba, ship via Mangalore port. Exit: Foundry unit sells at 60% of setup cost (land + equipment liquid). Worse case: Sell machinery (₹8-12L recovery from ₹25-35L investment), lease building month-to-month.
