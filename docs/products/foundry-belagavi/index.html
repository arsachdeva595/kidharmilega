Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Belagavi ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Belagavi's Londa industrial cluster (40km from city) to study 15-20 operating foundries; attend quarterly meetings at Belagavi Foundrymen Association; benchmark at Belgaum Industrial Association. Spend 2-3 weeks understanding production cycles, quality certifications (ISO 8501, ISO 6954), and cost structures.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source scrap iron from Belgaum scrap yards (₹25-28/kg, ₹5-8L for first 250MT batch); bentonite from local suppliers in Londa (₹3-4/kg); silica sand from nearby quarries in Belgaum-Bailhongal belt (₹1-2/kg). First-batch raw material cost: ₹12-18L for 100MT production capacity.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established foundries in Londa cluster via job work arrangement (60% commission) for first 12 months; simultaneously establish small 20-30MT capacity unit with used cupola furnace (₹8-12L), moulding equipment (₹3-5L), and ancillary machinery (₹2-3L). Employ 8-10 skilled moldmakers and operators from local cluster.
4
Brand & Register
Register as MSME under Udyam portal; obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Belgaum for export eligibility; secure ISO 9001:2015 certification (₹2-3L, 6 months); obtain BIS license if targeting automotive tier-1 suppliers; create foundry-specific branding emphasizing precision, heritage, and quality certifications.
5
First Sale
Target first contracts via RFQ responses on TorqueBidding, Areo, and LinkedIn B2B outreach to automotive Tier-1 suppliers; participate in Pragati Maidan engineering expos (₹3-5L booth cost); approach regional industrial chambers in Pune-Aurangabad corridor; leverage cluster reputation for quality certifications and on-time delivery.
6
Scale
After 6 months profitability, expand to 50-100MT capacity; add CNC machining capability (₹15-20L investment); target export markets via APEDA registration; establish relationships with logistics partners for bulk shipments; recruit quality engineers and invest in testing equipment (UTM, spectrometer) for premium segment castings.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (35% of project cost) for micro-enterprises in manufacturing; ideal for first-time foundry setup.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
cluster-based support up to ₹5Cr for Belagavi foundry cluster modernization; applies if joining cluster development initiatives.
🏚
Karnataka Industrial Policy 2020-2028
15-25% capital subsidy for manufacturing units in designated industrial areas; eligibility for Belagavi's notified industrial zones.
🏚
Karnataka State MSME Promotion Scheme
5% interest subvention on term loans up to ₹1Cr from nationalized banks; applicable for foundry units registered under Udyam.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Marketing
