South Salamara-Mankachar has developed a strong tradition of manual cashew processing with local expertise in hand-picking and roasting techniques. The district's agricultural infrastructure and proximity to raw cashew suppliers in coastal states makes it economically viable for processing and value-addition. Lower labour costs compared to southern cashew hubs enable competitive pricing while maintaining quality.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — South Salamara- Mankachar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Assam State Food Processing Cluster in Guwahati (Jorabat area). Connect with existing cashew processors in Barpeta and Goalpara districts. Research competitive brands like Jabardast Foods, Assam Organic. Study cashew sourcing from Gujarat and Karnataka suppliers—typical rates ₹300-400/kg raw nuts.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw cashew nuts directly from Andhra Pradesh (Tirupati) or Karnataka (Udupi) suppliers at ₹300-350/kg for initial 1000 kg batch (₹3-3.5L cost). Establish relationships with 2-3 suppliers to manage supply continuity. First-batch packaging (500g tins, pouches) costs ₹15-20/unit for 500 units.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing cashew processing artisans in South Salamara or Barpeta (contact district KVIC office). Alternatively, set up small roasting unit (₹1.5-2L investment for roaster, grader, packaging machines). Start with contract manufacturing—40-50% of production cost. Invest in food safety certification (FSSAI) initially.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) online portal (free, 10 mins). Apply for IEC code from nearest DGFT office (Guwahati) for export capability. File trademark for brand name with IP India. Get FSSAI license (₹8000-12000 for small business). Register GST with 5% tax slab for packaged food.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Pantry and Flipkart Direct (registration free, 25-30% commission). Set up WhatsApp Business for B2B enquiries. Approach 20-30 premium retail chains (Reliance Smart, Big Bazaar, Naturals stores) with samples. List on local Assam e-commerce platform (Kiranakart). Offer 100-unit starter packs to corporate offices in Guwahati, Delhi NCR at ₹550-700/500g.
6
Scale
After 6 months: launch 3-4 flavour variants (masala, honey-roasted, dark chocolate). Expand distribution to 100+ retail outlets via 2-3 distributors. Target exports to Bangladesh (₹50-80L annual potential) via APEDA tie-ups. Invest in influencer marketing on Instagram/YouTube with regional food bloggers. Introduce B2B subscription model for corporate pantries.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh government subsidy (35% for general, 45% for SC/ST) for new food processing units
🏛
APEDA export assistance scheme
up to 50% subsidy for branding, packaging, and international quality certifications for cashew exports
🏚
Assam AAPPC (Assam Agro & Food Processing and Promotion)
₹5-15 lakh subsidy for setting up food processing units in ODOP clusters
🏚
Assam Udyam Plus Scheme
interest subsidy of 6% for MSMEs in food processing sector for loans up to ₹50 lakh from SLBC approved banks
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
