South Salamara-Mankachar, headquartered in Hatsimigari in western Assam, is a Tier 3 district with 350,000 population and established cashew cultivation suited to tropical climate. Raw cashew nuts are grown locally on 13-14 meter tropical trees, with dwarf cultivars (6 meters) available for higher-yield farming. The district has officially designated cashew processing as ODOP, ensuring buyer connect via Ministry of Food Processing channels and access to PM MFPEU subsidy schemes for unit setup.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — South Salamara- Mankachar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Hatsimigari weekly cashew market on Tuesday; call 3 local raw cashew suppliers to get pricing (₹100-130/kg baseline); register free Udyam on udyamregistration.gov.in; attend monthly ODOP buyer meet at district food processing office (contact via Ministry website)
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw cashew nuts from local growers in Hatsimigari and nearby villages; expect ₹100-130/kg for bulk (50-100 kg batches); first-batch cost ₹7,500-13,000 for 75-100 kg; suppliers active in Hatsimigari main market, within 5km radius
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 500-800 sq ft unit in Hatsimigari or partner with 5-8 local home-based artisans (₹5,000-15,000/month per unit). Buy roasting drums (₹25,000-40,000), salt, packaging materials (₹20,000 initial stock). Manual processing: hand-picking, roasting, cooling, salting, packing — 3-4 days per 50kg batch. Partner model saves ₹80,000-150,000 upfront capex.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 1 day online); apply for PM MFPEU subsidy (₹10L max grant, 35% subsidy on equipment) via district food processing office; get FSSAI registration (₹5,000-8,000, 10-15 days); if exporting, apply for IEC (₹0 online via eicgov.in, 3-5 days); pursue GI registration later (₹50,000-80,000 cost, 18-24 months timeline) once brand scales
5
First Sale
First sales: list on Amazon FBA (₹500/month storage + 15% commission) or sell direct via WhatsApp to 50 office/retail contacts in Guwahati; first order: 5-10kg via Amazon = ₹2,500-5,000 revenue; target ₹15,000-25,000 first-month D2C revenue via word-of-mouth + local haat stalls
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 2-3 full-time staff (₹8,000-12,000/month each), move to 1200 sq ft dedicated unit, buy second roasting drum, apply for PM Vishwakarma loan (₹5L at 5% interest), approach regional distributors for 500kg+ monthly contracts, exhibit at North-East Food Tech expo (Guwahati, annual), launch 3 SKUs (salted, cashew barfi, cashew curry mix)

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
Pradhan Mantri Micro Food Processing Enterprises Upgrade Scheme (PM MFPEU)
35% grant on equipment and infrastructure, max ₹10 lakhs, applicants must have Udyam registration
🏛
One District One Product (ODOP)
dedicated buyer connect, export market linkage, branding support via Ministry of Food Processing, zero direct subsidy but channel priority
🏚
Assam State Food Processing Policy
25% subsidy on cold storage and food safety equipment for registered MSME units in cashew and spices
🏚
Assam MSME Loan Guarantee Scheme
collateral-free working capital loans up to ₹25 lakhs at 7% interest for registered food processors
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
