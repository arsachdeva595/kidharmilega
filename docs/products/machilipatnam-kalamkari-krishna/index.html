Machilipatnam Kalamkari ki
opportunity, Krishna mein.

Pedana and Machilipatnam towns in coastal Krishna district (13 km apart) are the only two production centers for Machilipatnam Kalamkari in India, with 150-200 active artisan families. Raw materials — vegetable dyes, cotton cloth, and wooden blocks — are sourced locally from Krishna region suppliers and within 50 km radius. Buyer proximity includes Vizag port (120 km) for exports, Delhi/Bangalore interior design markets (900-1000 km), and direct international reach to USA, UK, Germany.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Krishna ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Pedana town (13 km from Machilipatnam) this month — call Krishna District Handicrafts Federation, walk Pedana main market, speak to 3-5 artisans about process and costs. Cost: zero. Register free on Udyam portal from your phone.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source cotton cloth from Machilipatnam cloth wholesalers (Rythu Bazar area), vegetable dyes from local plants supplier or order from Jaipur Indigo merchants. First 10-piece batch of sarees: ₹8,000-12,000 material cost. Partner directly with Pedana artisans (₹800-1,500 per saree labor) or buy finished pieces at ₹3,500-5,500.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 300-400 sq ft work unit in Pedana or Machilipatnam (₹3,000-5,000/month) OR start with artisan partnership (zero rent, 40-50% revenue share). Initial setup: ₹1.5-2.5L (wooden blocks, dye vats, work tables, tools). Begin with 20-30 pieces/month using artisan partnership.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 10 mins online). File GI claim with Ministry of Handloom (support from state DC Handloom office, ₹5,000-10,000 legal cost, 4-6 month process). Get IEC from customs if exporting (₹2,000-5,000, 5-7 days). Register trademark for brand name (₹4,000-8,000).
5
First Sale
First sale: List on Etsy or create Shopify store (₹3,000-5,000 setup). Target 2-3 pieces/week first month on D2C (₹15,000-25,000 revenue). Simultaneously pitch to 10-15 heritage decor retailers via WhatsApp with catalog — expect first wholesale order ₹20,000-40,000 within month 2.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 2-3 full-time design coordinators, rent 800 sq ft dedicated workspace (₹8,000-12,000), partner with 8-10 artisans formally, apply for PMEGP subsidy (₹10L maximum for equipment), build export relationship with 2-3 international distributors. Exhibit at India Mart, ICCW trade shows, Art+Living fairs.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹15,000 toolkit subsidy + ₹2L collateral-free loan for artisans and small producers in handloom sector
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹10L subsidy for manufacturing unit setup, 25% subsidy for general category, 35% for SC/ST
🏚
Andhra Pradesh Artisan Welfare Board
₹5,000-10,000 direct subsidy for traditional craft producers registered with DC Handloom
🏚
AP Handloom Advancement Society (APHAS)
interest-free term loans up to ₹5L for product development, 2-year grace period
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
