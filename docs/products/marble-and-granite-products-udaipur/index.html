Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Udaipur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Udaipur marble cluster (Bhata road, Udaipur city) — walk 10-15 carving units, talk to 3-4 artisans about your idea and their work rates. Call 2-3 raw material suppliers from Makrana limestone lists (Google 'Makrana marble suppliers Udaipur'). Attend Udaipur Chamber of Commerce haat (monthly, free) or follow ODOP WhatsApp groups. Spend ₹0, take 3-5 days.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw material from Makrana marble wholesalers in Udaipur city (Bhata road area) or direct from Makrana quarries (180 km, ₹8-12K per ton delivered). First order: 1-2 tons marble blocks = ₹20-30K. Sandstone from local suppliers (cheaper, ₹2-3K per ton). Granite from Bengaluru wholesalers (₹4-6K per ton). Secure supplier credit terms: 50% advance, 50% on delivery.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 500-600 sq ft stone carving workshop in Bhata road cluster for ₹8-12K/month (utilities, power included). Alternatively, partner with 1-2 skilled artisans (5-8 people per team, ₹15-25K/month wages) in exchange for 40% revenue share. Invest ₹80-150K in carving tools (chisels, grinders, saws, polishing equipment). First batch of 5-10 pieces takes 20-30 days. Production cost per medium-sized piece: ₹8-15K (including artisan labor, material, overhead).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 10 minutes). Apply for MSME registration in Rajasthan. If exporting, apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) from DGFT website (₹0, 2 weeks). GI registration not applicable (no GI status for Udaipur marble). Open a business bank account (₹500). No formal trademark needed initially. Cost: ₹5-10K for local MSME registration, statutory compliance.
5
First Sale
First sale: Post 3-5 high-quality WhatsApp photos (product + detail shots) to Instagram/Facebook; target interior designers and hospitality accounts. Simultaneously call 5-10 hotel procurement teams or 3-4 design studios in nearby Ahmedabad and Delhi. First orders typically ₹80-200K (5-15 pieces). Use Shopify or WooCommerce for online store (₹200-500/month). Expected first-month revenue if 2 orders land: ₹150-300K gross; net after production costs: ₹80-150K.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue, hire 1 full-time sales/export manager (₹25-35K/month) and expand workshop to 1200 sq ft. Partner with 3-4 artisan teams instead of 1-2. Invest ₹2-3L in catalog photography and video (for export buyers). Attend India Expo Center Delhi Art & Design Fair (Feb-Mar) and Rajasthan Craft Fair. Join ODOP export consortium for bulk freight discounts. Explore SFURTI subsidy for cluster upgrade. Target 15-20% month-on-month growth via repeated B2B orders.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 L subsidy for manufacturing, 35% of project cost for SC/ST/women, 25% for general category
🏛
MUDRA Loan Scheme
up to ₹10 L unsecured working capital for MSME at 6-8% interest; Shishu loans up to ₹50K instant approval
🏚
Rajasthan MSME Incentive Scheme 2022
5% subsidy on capital investment up to ₹50L for manufacturing units in clusters; electricity duty exemption for 5 years
🏚
Rajasthan One District One Product scheme
free Udyam registration, priority for SFURTI cluster grants, export facilitation support via RAJAMB (Rajasthan Agro-based Industries Marketing Board) for stone products
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
