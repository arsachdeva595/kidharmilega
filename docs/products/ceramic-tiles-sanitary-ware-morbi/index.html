Ceramic Tiles & Sanitary Ware ki
opportunity, Morbi mein.

Morbi district (4,872 sq km, population 960,329) is located in coastal Gujarat with direct access to kaolin and clay deposits that feed the 40+ year ceramic ecosystem. The Morbi ceramic cluster operates as a contract manufacturing hub with contract manufacturers and allied suppliers concentrated in a 15-25 km radius, enabling just-in-time raw material procurement and 2-5 day delivery to Indian construction sites. Global ceramics market stands at USD 248.89 billion (2023) with tiles representing over 48% by application, and Asia-Pacific commanding 40% share — Morbi producers supply directly to UAE, Saudi Arabia, UK, and US markets via established export channels.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Morbi ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Morbi ceramic cluster this week: walk through the 15-25 km manufacturing zone (start near Regency Ceramics facilities), spend 1-2 hours talking to 3-4 existing tile manufacturers (owners will speak freely), call 2-3 raw material suppliers listed on Morbi Chamber of Commerce to understand current kaolin/clay prices. Attend Morbi MSME cluster meet (check morbi.nic.in for dates) — all free, zero cost.
2
Source Raw Materials
Raw material: contact kaolin/clay suppliers in Morbi industrial area (suppliers compete heavily, rates typically ₹1800-2400 per ton for bulk); get first-batch quote for 50 tons = ₹90K-120K. Tiles equipment: existing manufacturers in Morbi rent tile presses and kilns on hourly basis (₹800-1200/hour for press work) — negotiate a partnership for first 10,000 sq. mt. at ₹180-220 per sq. mt. (includes labor, kiln, glaze).
3
Produce / Partner
Three routes: (A) Rent a 2000-3000 sq ft shed in Morbi industrial estate (₹2.5-4L per month, includes utilities), buy a 20-ton capacity batch mixer (₹18-22L), hire 8-10 skilled workers (₹8-12L per month wages + PF); total first 3 months ₹60-85L. (B) Partner with existing Morbi manufacturer on contract manufacturing (your brand, their facility) — you supply design/glaze/QC, they handle pressing/firing; upfront ₹0, per-unit cost ₹220-280 per sq. mt., MOQ 5,000 sq. mt. (B-route recommended for first 6-12 months). (C) Buy a turnkey plant setup (press+kiln+dryer) for ₹2.5-3.5Cr — only if raising outside capital.
4
Brand & Register
Register business as proprietorship on Udyam Portal (free, 5 minutes); Register design+trademark on eBiz IP office (₹2K-5K, 30 days); If exporting: apply for IEC from nearest DGFT office (Rajkot is closest, 50 km away) — IEC costs ₹0 and takes 5 working days online. GI registration for 'Morbi Ceramic Tiles' is not applicable (district-level GI, not product-specific). Obtain ISO 13006 certification (optional but increases B2B credibility, costs ₹15-25K, takes 45 days).
5
First Sale
First sales route: Post 3-4 product photos + specifications on IndiaMART + Alibaba (free); Target first 10-15 orders from small contractors via WhatsApp inquiries from cluster. First order: expect 500-1000 sq. mt. from a contractor, value ₹1.1-2.8L at ₹220-280/sq. mt. wholesale; Delivery in 5-7 days via Morbi's established logistics (road freight to South/North India, ₹25-35/sq. mt.). Month 2-3: target 5-8L revenue from 20-30 contractor and distributor orders. Month 4-6: Scale to ₹25-40L monthly through 2-3 distributor tie-ups covering a region (North/South/East).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (= ~45,000 sq. mt./month = feasible with contract manufacturing): Hire a sales manager (₹50K/month) and one operations coordinator (₹35K/month). Exhibit at CERATEC (Morbi bi-annual ceramics expo) and BuildTech Expo (national construction show) — costs ₹2-3L per show but generates ₹20-50L in orders. Expand product line (add cool roof tiles, antimicrobial tiles, 60cm x 120cm and 80cm x 120cm sizes) to increase margins by 8-12%. Establish 2-3 regional distributors in Tamil Nadu, Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh (each capable of ₹20-30L/month offtake). Build basic export logistics: partner with Morbi-based export house or freight forwarder, target UAE/Saudi Arabia market for FOB sales (higher margin at 35-50%).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (25% for general, 35% for SC/ST/Women) for manufacturing unit; processing via District MSME office, Morbi.
🏛
MUDRA Loan
up to ₹10L unsecured loan at 8-10% interest for working capital and equipment; zero collateral, 5-year tenure; available via SBI, ICICI Bank, regional banks in Morbi.
🏚
Gujarat MSME Cluster Development Scheme
up to ₹50L grant for joint facility (shared kiln, testing lab, design studio) if you form a cluster group with 5+ tile makers; apply via District Industries Centre.
🏚
Gujarat Export Promotion Scheme
2-5% subsidy on export FOB value for first 3 years if exporting; IEC required; apply via GSGPC (Gujarat State Granite and Stone Products Council equivalent for ceramics).
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Seedhe sawaal, seedhe jawab

₹85 lakh se ₹3 crore lagega. Contract manufacturing route se ₹85L-1Cr mein start kar sakte ho (design, QC, marketing, sales ke liye). Apna plant lagane mein ₹2.5-3.5 crore chalega. PMEGP se ₹25L subsidy mil sakta hai.
Haan, tile manufacturing ya construction sector mein 3-5 saal ka experience zaroori hai. Ya fir Morbi mein 2-3 mahine ka hands-on training karni padegi. Koi bhi local manufacturer aapko on-the-job training de sakta hai.
PMEGP se ₹25L subsidy (25-35% depending on category); MUDRA Loan ₹10L tak interest ke saath; Gujarat MSME Cluster scheme se ₹50L grant agar 5+ makers ka group banao; Export subsidy 2-5% agar videshi market mein jao.
Pehla 3 mahine local Morbi ke contractors se milega — sab WhatsApp par available hain. Phir IndiaMART, Alibaba, Flipkart B2B par listings se inquiries aayengi. Distributor network 6-9 mahine mein banegi (South, North, East regions).
Bilkul. Global ceramics market ₹248B hai, tiles mein 48% share. Morbi se directly UAE, Saudi Arabia, UK, US ko exports hota hai. IEC registration free hai (5 days), phir freight forwarder ke through UAE/Saudi ko ₹2800-3200/sq mt FOB price pe supply kar sakte ho.
Biggest risk: demand drop during real estate slowdown. Iska matlab 6-8 mahine bandi. Second risk: labor shortage (migrant workers). Third: equipment breakdown (kiln failure ₹50L+ repair). Exit option: apna brand bandh kar do, factory rental aur equipment apne manufacturing partners ko bech do — ₹40-60L mein recoup kar paoge.
