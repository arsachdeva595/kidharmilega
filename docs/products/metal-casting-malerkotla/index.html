opportunity, Malerkotla mein.

Malerkotla district in central Punjab has 400+ casting foundries clustered around the town center and extends 15km into adjacent areas, with raw material suppliers (scrap yards, ingot dealers) within 2-5km radius. The district connects via NH-44 to Delhi-Chandigarh corridor and has rail access via Malerkotla Junction, enabling quick shipping to OEM hubs in Haryana and UP. Local supply chains for molds, cores, and finishing services reduce lead times to 7-10 days vs 21+ days for greenfield locations.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Malerkotla ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call 3-5 existing foundry owners in Malerkotla town (get numbers from District Industries Centre or malerkotla.nic.in) and visit their units to observe operations and costs — zero cost. Attend one monthly MSME cluster meet at Malerkotla Chamber of Commerce. Register free Udyam account online (udyamregistration.gov.in).
2
Source Raw Materials
Scrap cast iron and aluminum sourcing: visit Lohian Khas scrap market 8km from Malerkotla town (open 6am-11am daily) for inbound material; first batch of 5 tonnes costs ₹1.2-1.5L depending on grade. Ingot suppliers in Malerkotla town (Ashok Metals, Sharma Alloys) supply virgin aluminum/zinc at ₹280-320/kg. Establish credit line with 2-3 suppliers.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 3,000-5,000 sq ft shed in Malerkotla MSME cluster (₹8-12/sq ft/month = ₹24-60k/month) OR partner with existing small foundry on revenue-share (₹15-25k/month). Setup cost: furnace (₹12-25L used, ₹40-60L new), mold materials (₹2-5L), core equipment (₹1-3L), hand tools (₹50-80k). Total capex ₹15-50L depending on new vs used equipment. Hire 1-2 skilled molders (₹15-20k/month each), 1 fitter (₹12k/month).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 15 min online). File for IEC with nearest APEDA office in Chandigarh (₹0, takes 5-7 days). Explore GI registration for Malerkotla Metal Casting (₹10-15k filing + 12-18 month process via Ministry of Commerce). Set up basic website with product spec sheets and ISO 9001 certification (₹40-60k one-time).
5
First Sale
List 5-10 sample products on IndiaMART as 'Verified Supplier' (₹250/month premium). Approach 10 local automotive suppliers with sample castings and quotes. Target first order: 50-200 units at ₹200-500 per piece = ₹10-80k first month revenue. Negotiate 15-30 day payment terms.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue, hire 1 sales/export manager (₹25-35k/month), 2-3 additional operators (₹12-15k each), 1 QC inspector (₹18k). Apply for PMEGP subsidy (₹25L loan, 35% subsidy) to buy new furnace capacity. Exhibit at Hannover MetalCasting Fair (biennial) and Export Promotion Council shows. Certify ISO 9001, AS9100 (aerospace buyers pay 15-20% premium).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
₹25L loan at 0% interest for 5 years, 35% subsidy (₹8.75L max), credit guarantee via CGIMS
🏛
MUDRA Loan Scheme
₹10L loan for working capital/equipment, simple 5-min approval, no collateral needed, 2-year moratorium option
🏚
Punjab MSME Development Scheme
25% subsidy on plant & machinery capex (max ₹50L), applicable for first-time units in notified clusters
🏚
Punjab Export Promotion Scheme
10% subsidy on export turnover for first ₹1Cr, valid for 3 years
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
