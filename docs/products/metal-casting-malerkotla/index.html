The district benefits from proximity to major industrial hubs in Ludhiana and Jalandhar, skilled artisan workforce, and reliable supply chains for raw materials including scrap iron, brass, and aluminum ingots.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Malerkotla ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Malerkotla foundry cluster (Old City area near Ahmedgarh road) — meet 5-10 existing casting unit owners; attend Punjab MSME Federation workshops; study competitor operations in Ludhiana foundry belt (30km away); understand ISO 9001/8001 certification requirements.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source cast iron scrap from Ludhiana scrap yards (₹25-30/kg), brass ingots from Delhi metal wholesalers (₹450-500/kg), sand from Malerkotla/nearby quarries (₹3-5 per kg). First batch raw material cost: ₹15-20 lakhs for 10-15 MT trial production.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing Malerkotla foundries as production partner first (reduces capex to ₹5-8L); or lease existing small foundry unit (₹2-3L/month) with molding pit capacity 5-10 MT/month. Hire experienced coremaker (₹15-18k/month) and foundry supervisor (₹20-25k/month).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME (online, 10 minutes, free); apply for IEC code from local DGFT office (Ludhiana, 2 weeks); obtain pollution clearance from Punjab PPCB; register under shop/factory act; trademark brand name with IP office; ISO 9001 certification (₹1.5-2L, 4-6 weeks).
5
First Sale
List on TradingIndia.com, IndiaMART (B2B portal), and GlobalTrade.com; contact 50 pump OEM makers in Punjab/Haryana directly; reach out to PHDCCI (Punjab chamber) trade delegations; participate in biannual IITF exhibitions; email export houses in Ludhiana/Jalandhar with product catalog + ISO cert.
6
Scale
After 6 months: invest ₹20-30L to build owned foundry unit (5-10 MT/month capacity); hire second shift supervisor; certify additional quality standards (ISO 8501, ASTM); develop export relationships with 3-5 international distributors; expand product range to include 3-4 new casting types based on market demand.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (25-35% grant) for new foundry units; interest subvention 6% for 5 years.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
cluster development, training, tech upgradation grants for Malerkotla foundry cluster; up to ₹1Cr per project.
🏚
Punjab Start-Up Scheme
₹10L direct grant + ₹10L concessional loan for manufacturing MSME; land lease subsidy 50% for 10 years.
🏚
Punjab Export Promotion Scheme
reimbursement of 50% freight costs for first 3 export shipments; ISO certification cost reimbursement up to ₹1.5L.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
