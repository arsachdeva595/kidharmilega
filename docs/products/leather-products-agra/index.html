Agra district (4,027 km²) in western Uttar Pradesh is home to 500+ tanneries concentrated in the Agra leather cluster, fed by raw hides from cattle zones across UP. The district sits 240km from Delhi and 450km from Mumbai, providing direct access to national export corridors and international shipping via air and sea. Global footwear brands now source from Agra as a viable alternative to China, with established supply chains to Dubai, Germany, and North America.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Agra ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Agra leather cluster (Kling Bazaar, Sadar Bazaar tannery areas) this weekend — walk 3-4 tanneries, talk to masters about raw material costs, order timeline, MOQ. Call 2-3 leather exporters listed on Council for Leather Exports directory. Register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in). Cost: ₹0, time: 6 hours.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source hides from Agra Mandi (wholesale livestock market) or dedicated tanneries in Nai Sarai area. First batch: 50-100 hides at ₹1200-1800/hide (raw), or buy finished tanned leather at ₹800-1500 per sq. ft. depending on grade. Supplier zone: Nai Sarai, Kling Bazaar, Sadar Bazaar. First-batch cost (100 sq. ft. finished leather): ₹80K-150K.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (Asset-light): Partner with 3-5 artisans in Agra cluster on per-unit commission (₹100-300/pair or item). Option B (Small unit): Rent 800 sq. ft. workshop in Agra leather industrial area at ₹8-12K/month, hire 2-3 cutters + 1 supervisor (₹8-12K/month each). Buy cutting + stitching tools: ₹80-120K. Monthly overhead at 5-unit production: ₹25-30K. Breakeven: 8-10 orders/month at wholesale price.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 15 min). Apply for GI certification through Ministry ODOP program (₹5K-10K filing + 3-4 months approval) — confirms 'Agra leather' authenticity. If exporting: Get IEC (Import-Export Code) from DGFT via Udyam portal (free, 5 days). Open RCMC (Rajiv Gandhi Chamber of Commerce Membership) for export benefits (₹3K, 1 week). Trademark brand name at IP Office (₹4.5K, 3 months).
5
First Sale
First sale strategy: Create Udyam profile immediately, get verified seller badge. List on Flipkart Seller Hub and Amazon Seller Central (zero upfront cost, 0.5% referral + FBF charges). First order: contact 5 boutique leather stores in Delhi/Mumbai, offer 30% trade discount, MOQ 5 units = ₹25-30K revenue (₹5-6K per piece wholesale). Target: first order week 3-4, monthly revenue ₹80-120K by month 3.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire full-time operations manager (₹18K/month), open branded showroom in Agra (₹30-40K/month rent), exhibit at Leather & Lifestyle Fair Noida, IITF Delhi (₹80-120K booth cost). Expand SKUs (bags, belts alongside shoes/wallets). Build export network: join Council for Leather Exports membership (₹15K/year), attend Global Leather Summit, pitch to German, UK, Canadian importers. Delegate D2C to social media manager + performance marketer (₹15-20K/month total). Target: ₹30L annual revenue, 8-10 employees.

₹10 L unsecured loan at 8-12% interest for working capital; no collateral needed
