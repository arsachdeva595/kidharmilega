Moonj Grass product ki
opportunity, Udham Singh Nagar mein.

Udham Singh Nagar sits at the Uttarakhand-Uttar Pradesh border where Moonj grass grows naturally in October-November harvest seasons, feeding a 50-artisan base concentrated in Deshi Deoria, Bhatni, Sonughat, Behra Dabar, and Purvanchal. These clusters are within 20-40 km of each other, enabling centralized procurement of raw grass and dyes while maintaining distributed artisan production. Delhi-NCR (270 km) and Lucknow (200 km) provide immediate B2C and B2B markets; international buyers access the supply chain via Panipat and Saharanpur existing trade corridors.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Udham Singh Nagar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Behra Dabar village, meet the 20-artisan group in Kiran's courtyard (contact via Jagriti Enterprise Center, Purvanchal), attend the weekly Haridwar craft haat on Thursday to see current Moonj product sales prices and buyer types. Record wholesale and retail rates for durries, baskets, and mats. Zero cost, doable within 3 days.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw Moonj grass directly from Purvanchal villages (Oct-Nov harvest) at ₹2-4 per kg; contact Deshi Deoria and Bhatni village councils for bulk supplier names. Order 50 kg first batch (₹250-300) to test drying and knot-making process. Powdered dyes from Panipat wholesale market (₹80-150 per kg, 8-10 kg starter pack = ₹800-1200). Total raw material for 100-piece first production: ₹3000-5000.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Partner directly with Behra Dabar artisan group for bulk orders under your brand (you supply dyes and bulk grass, they produce at ₹50-80/piece labor cost). Option B: Rent 300-sqft shared space in Panipat or Saharanpur craft cluster at ₹8000-12000/month, hire 4-5 learners, invest in 2-3 coiling frames (₹15000-25000). With artisan partnership route: ₹0 rent, ₹0 machine cost, ₹8000-12000/month material cost for 80-120 pieces. Standalone unit: ₹35000-45000 first-month setup including workspace, tools, and material.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 1 day). Apply for GI registration as your brand's sourcing story under DC Handicraft Uttarakhand (₹5000 application, 6-8 month approval process — prioritize after first ₹5L revenue). Obtain PAN (free) and GST (₹0, 7-day online). If exporting: apply for IEC via DGFT online (₹0, 2-3 weeks). Total cost: ₹5000-8000, timeline 2-4 weeks for core registrations.
5
First Sale
Launch Instagram Shop with 20-30 curated products (durries ₹450-800, baskets ₹600-1200, lampshades ₹500-900). Post 3x/week reels showing artisan hands, dyeing process, product styling. Target first 20 orders (₹12000-18000 revenue) from family, community, and Instagram organic reach in weeks 1-4. Simultaneously pitch 5-8 lifestyle boutiques in Delhi-NCR for wholesale (50-piece orders, 40% discount). Expected first-month revenue: ₹18000-28000.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire a 1-person packing and dispatch assistant (₹12000/month), upgrade to dedicated 600-sqft unit (₹18000/month rent), establish standing orders with 3-4 resort chains and corporate gifting agencies. Exhibit at IHGF Delhi Pragati Maidan (Feb) and Bhaarat Adhivasī Vikas Sammelan shows (cost ₹35000-50000 booth). Recruit second artisan group from Sonughat to handle order overflow. Add 4-5 new product lines (woven pouches, bottle covers, ceremonial torans) by month 14.
