Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sikar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sikar city's furniture cluster (Fort Road area and Bari Basti) and meet 3-4 active workshop owners — talk to them about raw material costs, artisan wages, and buyer channels. Call 2 timber suppliers in Jaipur (Ajmeri Gate market) for current teak and mango wood rates. Register on MSME Udyam portal (free, 20 mins online). Zero cost, doable within 1 week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source timber from Jaipur's wholesale timber yards (Ajmeri Gate, Bapu Nagar) — first batch of premium teak (5-10 cubic meters) costs ₹1.5-2.5L depending on grade and quality. Partner with an existing local workshop owner for sample production (rent their unit space for ₹8,000-12,000/month) while you secure orders.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent a 1,200 sq ft workshop in Sikar's furniture cluster for ₹12,000-15,000/month; hire 3-4 skilled carpenters at ₹12,000-18,000/month each; buy basic tools (chisels, sanders, saws, finishes) for ₹80,000-1,20,000. Option B: Partner with an existing artisan workshop on revenue-share (40-60% split) — zero rent, lower initial risk. Total setup for Option A: ₹6-10L over 6 months.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME online (free, 2 days). Apply for GI registration through Sikar District Industries Centre if creating heritage brand (₹15,000-25,000 cost, 6-8 month process, non-mandatory for sales but helps premium positioning). If exporting, obtain IEC code from DGFT online portal (₹0, 10 days). Get Rajasthan State Handicraft Certificate from DC Office (₹2,000, 15 days) to unlock state subsidies.
5
First Sale
First sale: List 5 products on Etsy, Instagram Shop, and Wayfair (upload photos, pricing, dimensions). Target first order from interior designer networks via LinkedIn outreach — typical order size ₹60,000-1,50,000. Post before/after carving videos on Instagram Reels and TikTok weekly. Expected first-month revenue if you get 2-3 custom orders: ₹1,20,000-2,00,000 gross (after material costs: ₹50,000-80,000 net).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire a designer (₹25,000/month) to manage Etsy + Instagram content and quote management. Attend India Pavilion at Las Vegas market trade show (January, ₹3-5L investment) to source bulk export orders. Participate in Jaipur Furniture Fair (annual event) to pitch to B2B retailers and interior designers. Invest in CNC machines (₹8-12L) to increase production from 10 to 25 units/month. Partner with a logistics company (DHL, FedEx) for export shipments. Hire 2 more carpenters and 1 finishing expert.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 Lakhs subsidy (25% of project cost) for entrepreneurs in handicraft; eligible for Sikar district artisans.
🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
up to ₹3L advance + ₹15,000 annual toolkit grant for traditional woodworkers; 5% interest subsidy on business loans up to ₹5L.
🏚
Rajasthan Craft Development Scheme
₹50,000-2L subsidy for artisans and small workshops; training support and market linkage; apply via District Industries Centre.
🏚
Rajasthan ODOP Export Promotion
10-15% subsidy on trade fair participation, export quality certification support, and logistics cost sharing for products manufactured in Sikar district.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

100% exportable. US, UK, Germany, Netherlands, Australia are top markets. Process: Get IEC code (10 days, free), photograph products, join Alibaba or Global Sources, price at ₹(local cost × 2) for FOB export pricing, ship via freight forwarder (Jaipur has many).
