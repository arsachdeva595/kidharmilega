Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sonitpur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call Nath Brothers (GST 18AGWPN1762E1Z0) in Rangapara or visit Baghel Agro Industries this month. Ask about their paddy procurement, milling process, and wholesale channels. Attend a local Guwahati grain market haat (Tuesday/Friday). Register on Udyam (free, online) to get a business number.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source paddy directly from paddy farmers in Sonitpur (Rangapara, Gohpur, Titabar blocks) at ₹20-24/kg. First batch: 5 MT paddy = ₹1-1.2L. Establish relationship with 3-4 farmer suppliers to ensure year-round availability.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a milling unit in Rangapara or nearby with soaking, steaming, and drying infrastructure (₹15-25K/month). Alternatively, partner with Nath Brothers or Baghel Agro for toll processing at ₹2-3/kg margin. Initial production: 1-2 MT/week parboiled rice.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online at udyamregistration.gov.in). Apply for IEC code at DGFT if exporting (₹0, 5 days online). Obtain rice purity/quality certification from local food lab (₹3-5K). Skip GI registration initially—cost ₹2-4L and takes 18+ months; not mandatory.
5
First Sale
First sale: contact 2-3 wholesale distributors in Guwahati directly (list from Guwahati wholesale rice market). Offer trial lot of 500 kg parboiled rice at ₹48/kg. Expect first order ₹25-35K within 2 weeks. Simultaneously list on Amazon Seller Central and Flipkart (₹10K registration + inventory ₹50K) for D2C traction.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire a dedicated sales person for wholesale accounts (₹12-15K salary), invest in packaging design and branding (₹2-3L), apply for PMEGP or SFURTI subsidy for unit expansion (₹25L subsidy), exhibit at Guwahati Food & Hospitality Fair (Sept-Oct) annually. Scale to 10-15 MT/month production by month 18.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy (25% for general, 35% for SC/ST) for setting up rice processing unit
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹50 lakh cluster development grant if partnering with 10+ units in Sonitpur; covers equipment, branding, common facility center
🏚
Assam One District One Product (ODOP) scheme
direct procurement support, Udyam registration fee waiver, participation in state trade fairs (₹0-5K booth subsidy)
🏚
Assam Agricultural Machinery Scheme
50% subsidy on parboiling and drying equipment (max ₹2.5L) for farmers/processors registering as FPOs or primary agricultural units
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
