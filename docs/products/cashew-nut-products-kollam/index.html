Cashew Nut Products ki
opportunity, Kollam mein.

Kollam has a 150+ year legacy in cashew processing with over 500 registered factories, established supply chains, and skilled workforce. The district's geographic advantage as a port city ensures raw cashew import efficiency, while its cluster infrastructure enables cost-effective production and quality control. Local expertise in value-added products like cashew candy and cashew oil extraction provides competitive differentiation.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kollam ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kollam Cashew Cluster in Asramam and Chavara; study 10+ operational units (capacity 50-500 MT/year); interact with KCDC (Kollam Cashew Development Council); benchmark export quality standards; budget 7-10 days, ₹15,000
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw cashew from authorized importers in Kollam Port (Cochin Port backup); established suppliers: African Cashew Traders, Kerala Cashew Importers; first batch 5 MT costs ₹22-28 lakhs; secondary materials from local suppliers in Kollam
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with semi-automated unit in Kollam (lease or buy 2,000-5,000 sq ft facility costing ₹8-15L/year); hire 8-12 skilled processors (₹12-15K/month); or contract-manufacture via KCDC-registered units charging ₹8-12/kg processing fee; initial production 20-30 MT/month
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, 15 mins); apply IEC from DGFT Cochin office (₹5,000, 7 days); trademark brand at IP office (₹4,500-8,000, 18-24 months); obtain FSSAI license (food manufacturing) costing ₹8,000-12,000; export certification (ISO 22000, organic if applicable)
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon, Flipkart, Blinkit (vendor onboarding ₹50K each); direct B2B outreach to 100+ exporters via email/LinkedIn; partner with 20-30 premium retail chains (hotels, gift shops) in metro cities; export via customs agents in Cochin; initial sales target ₹5-8L/month
6
Scale
Expand to 50-75 MT/month by month 6; add 2-3 value-added SKUs; build B2B export operations (50% of revenue); open own retail corner in 5-8 metros; hire sales team for institutional sales; invest in marketing budget (₹2-5L/month) for brand building
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (25-35% of project cost) for manufacturing unit in Kollam; food processing eligible
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹50L cluster development support; Kollam Cashew Cluster pre-identified for common facility centres
🏚
Kerala Food Processing Industry Policy (2018)
5% interest subsidy, land cost reimbursement up to ₹5L for units; VKAY scheme for branding support up to ₹10L
🏚
KINFRA (Kerala Industrial Infrastructure Development Corporation)
subsidized industrial space, common facilities; KINFRA Food Park in Kasaragod/Kollam region offers 50% capex subsidy
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
