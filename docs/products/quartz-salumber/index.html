Salumber is strategically located in southern Rajasthan with abundant quartz-rich mineral deposits in its geological formations. The district has decades of mining expertise, established supply chains to major stone processors in Kishangarh and Jaipur, and proximity to National Highway 76 enabling cost-effective logistics to ports and manufacturing hubs.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Salumber ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Salumber mining clusters (near Ahar and Pur villages) and meet 3-5 established quartz operators; study Kishangarh engineered stone buyers at the Kishangarh Stone Fair (Jan/Feb); research international quartz pricing on ICEX and Global Trade Atlas; attend Indian Bureau of Mines webinars on mining licenses.
2
Source Raw Materials
Identify mining leasehold opportunity via Rajasthan Mines & Minerals Department (lease cost ₹2-8L annually); or source raw quartz ore from existing operators in Salumber taluk at ₹800-1200/tonne for initial 100 tonnes trial batch costing ₹1-1.2L plus ₹30L for basic beneficiation unit setup.
3
Produce / Partner
Lease or build a small beneficiation unit (100-200 tpa capacity) near Salumber town with crushing, washing, and grading machines; partner with Kishangarh stone processors for contract processing initially; source equipment from Indian mineral processing vendors in Jaipur or Udaipur (Kasganj machines typical at ₹15-25L for pilot scale).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Ministry of MSME portal, instant online), apply for IEC via Rajasthan Chambers of Commerce; register brand name with Ministry of Corporate Affairs; obtain Mining License from Rajasthan Dept of Industries; get environmental clearance and water board permission; ISO 9001 or relevant mineral standard certification.
5
First Sale
Launch on IndiaMART and TradeKey targeting engineered stone exporters with bulk quotations; contact Kishangarh cluster buyers directly with samples; participate in ICCF (India Ceramics & Tiles Fair) and international mineral expos; initiate email outreach to glass manufacturers in Firozabad, UP and Belgaum, KN; list on GlobalTrade.net for export inquiries.
6
Scale
After ₹50L revenue, invest in automated beneficiation unit (₹40-60L) to increase capacity 3x and reduce per-unit cost by 15-20%; establish direct contracts with top 5 engineered stone exporters for annual offtake; explore premium ultra-pure quartz verticals (semiconductor, solar); build regional warehouses in Jaipur and Ahmedabad for faster fulfillment; target ISO certifications for specialized grades.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (25% for general) for manufacturing unit setup in Rajasthan
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
cluster development support for Salumber mining community with infrastructure and capacity-building grants up to ₹4 Cr per cluster
🏚
Rajasthan Mineral Beneficiation Policy 2016
5-year tax exemption and royalty rebate (25%) for value-added mineral processing units
🏚
Rajasthan Mining Dependent Development Fund
concessional credit and equipment leasing for mineral entrepreneurs at 3-4% interest rates via State Bank of Bikaner & Jaipur
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
