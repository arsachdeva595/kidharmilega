Aromatic Rice ki
opportunity, Charaideo mein.

Charaideo district sits in the Brahmaputra valley of central Assam, 100km east of Guwahati, with ideal monsoon patterns and alluvial soil for chinigura aromatic rice cultivation. The district has a base of 500+ farmer families growing the variety, concentrated in Sonari, Chiring, and Boginadi tea blocks. Key buyer markets are Guwahati hotels (30km+), Kolkata restaurants (700km via NH-37), Bangladesh wholesale markets (200km via Nagaland borders), and emerging export channels to Singapore and UAE.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Charaideo ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call 5 chinigura farmers in Sonari block (ask for contacts at Charaideo agriculture office), visit one paddy field during harvest season, attend the weekly grain traders haat at Dibrugarh market on Tuesday mornings — all free, doable this month. Register for Udyam at udyamregistration.gov.in (15 min, Aadhar + PAN).
2
Source Raw Materials
Source paddy directly from farmer co-ops in Sonari and Chiring blocks — typical first-batch cost ₹45-50 per kg green paddy (650 kg/tonne) = ₹29,250-32,500 per tonne milled rice. Bulk prices drop 15-20% at 10+ tonne orders. Supplier area: within 30km of Dibrugarh town.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Lease a rice mill unit in Dibrugarh MSME cluster (₹1.5L-2L/month, 2000 sq ft, includes hulling + drying equipment). Option B: Partner with a local rice miller (handles production for 8-12% milling margin). Initial inventory: 10 tonnes = ₹325K-375K paddy cost + ₹50K-75K drying/milling + ₹40K packaging = ₹415K-500K first batch.
4
Brand & Register
1. Register Udyam (free, 15 min) with milling unit address. 2. Apply for GI registration at Geographical Indications Registry, Kolkata (₹15K filing fee, 18-24 month approval if approved) — coordinate via Assam Food Industries Federation. 3. File IEC with DGFT Guwahati office (₹0, online via icegate.indiaport.gov.in, 3-5 days for export). 4. FSSAI registration for packaged food (₹5K, 10 days).
5
First Sale
First sale: Upload product on Udaan (free listing) with 500kg test batch, ₹155/kg wholesale pricing. Target first order from a Guwahati 3-star hotel via your regional distributor — typical order ₹75K-125K (500-800kg). Expected first month revenue: ₹2.5L-3.5L if 2-3 hotel contracts + 1-2 online orders.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 1 supply-chain manager (₹25K/month) to lock farmer contracts, hire 1 export agent (commission 4-6%) to handle Bangladesh/Nepal shipments, exhibit at AAFED (Assam Agriculture & Food Products Exhibition), apply for PMEGP subsidy (₹10L, 25% capital subsidy) for mill expansion to 30+ tonnes/day capacity.

Profit margins are 18-28% wholesale so quality consistency matters.
PMEGP: ₹10L subsidy (25%) for milling unit. MUDRA: ₹10L loan at 7.5-8%. Assam Export Subsidy: 15% on export shipping + certification. GI registration: ₹15K filing (strategic — approval takes 18-24 months but locks premium pricing). SFURTI: ₹5L-20L grants for cluster infrastructure (shared drying, testing lab).
Direct: Call 20 fine dining restaurants in Guwahati (get list from Hotel Association) with ₹50/kg sample offer — 5-10 will order. Platform: List on Udaan (free), expect 1-2 orders in first 2 weeks at ₹155/kg wholesale. Distributor route: Meet grain wholesalers at Dibrugarh market — they buy 2-5 tonnes and resell to hotels across NE.
Haan. Bangladesh buys 18 lakh tonnes annually — bulk buyers pay ₹120-140/kg FOB. Nepal + Bhutan import via Indian traders. Singapore, UAE take premium packaged rice (₹200-250/kg FOB, 40% margin). Process: Get IEC (free, 5 days), FSSAI certificate (₹5K, 10 days), then approach export agents in Kolkata or Guwahati — they handle shipping + documentation for 4-6% commission.
Honest: Market crashed 70% 2020-21 post-pandemic, so demand risk exists. Downside scenario: If hotel orders dry up, you have 3 exits — (1) Sell raw rice as commodity to larger millers at ₹90-100/kg (break-even if costs ₹95/kg), (2) Shift to organic positioning and target premium D2C at ₹300/kg (requires rebranding, +3 months), (3) Partner with an export agent to move inventory to Bangladesh at ₹120/kg (no margin but clears stock). Mitigation: Lock 2-year contracts with 3-5 anchor hotels before scaling beyond 10 tonnes/month.
