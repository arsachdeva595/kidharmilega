Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Azamgarh ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit the Azamgarh pottery cluster (Tikait Nagar, Dargah Road area) to learn from master potters; spend 2-3 weeks apprenticing with established craftspeople; visit the National Institute of Design (Ahmedabad) pottery section; connect with Azamgarh Handicraft Association members.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source clay locally from Azamgarh clay suppliers (₹3,000-5,000 per ton); purchase mineral glazes from Delhi ceramic suppliers like Ceram India or Kamal Impex (₹15,000-25,000 for initial stock); first batch raw material cost approximately ₹40,000-60,000 for 200-300 pieces.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 established artisan families in Tikait Nagar (profit-sharing: 40-45% to artisans, 55-60% to you); alternatively, set up a small kiln facility (₹300K-500K investment) and hire trained potters on contract basis; ensure kiln temperature control at 1,200-1,300°C for authentic black glaze development.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online at udyamregistration.gov.in); apply for IEC code (Directorate General of Foreign Trade website) for export capability; register brand with Intellectual Property Cell; obtain GI-tagged product certificate from Azamgarh District Administration; file MSME recognition under DC Handicraft ministry.
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy with 100+ high-quality photos (hire professional photographer, ₹10K-15K); create Instagram business account with behind-the-scenes artisan stories; pitch to premium home décor retailers (Fab India, Good Earth, local luxury boutiques) with wholesale pricing at 40-45% margin; list on own Shopify store with blog content on GI heritage; approach corporate gifting platforms (Giftcart, Incredibox).
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand product line (dinnerware, planters, art installations); establish showrooms in Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore (₹50L-100L per location); hire digital marketing specialist for SEO and Pinterest strategy; explore B2B partnerships with 5-star hotels and Airbnb experience providers; launch subscription box for home décor enthusiasts; apply for government export subsidies under SFURTI scheme.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (35% for general, 45% for SC/ST/women) for business setup including machinery and working capital.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹50L cluster grant for infrastructure, tool kits, and common facility centers in Azamgarh pottery cluster.
🏚
Uttar Pradesh Handloom & Handicraft Development Scheme
25-30% capital subsidy for artisan-linked enterprises, skill development allowance, and export promotion support.
🏚
UP MSME Growth Fund
subsidized credit up to ₹1Cr at 2% reduced interest rate for registered handicraft micro-enterprises.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
