Nizamabad in Azamgarh district (Uttar Pradesh, population 2.89M) is 250km from Delhi and 150km from Lucknow, with two rail routes (NH-56 connectivity) enabling rapid distribution to North India and export hubs. The cluster of 200+ artisans uses locally-mined smooth black clay and vegetable-based finishing solutions, keeping raw material costs low and environmental footprint minimal. High global demand for black pottery's distinct lustrous finish and silver-engraved motifs has created steady export pipelines to USA, UK, Germany, and UAE.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Azamgarh ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Nizamabad Black Pottery Cluster in person (30km from Azamgarh town); call 2–3 local artisan groups; attend Saturday market at Azamgarh Haat or check ODOP CFC (Common Facility Centre) hours online. Zero cost, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Contact artisans in Nizamabad directly or access SFURTI Cluster vendor list via DC Handicraft office. First batch of 100 pieces (mixed vases, tea-pots, decorative figurines): ₹8,000–₹12,000. Raw material (clay, vegetable solution, mercury) sourced from local suppliers in Azamgarh town, 30–40km from Nizamabad.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent a 300–500 sq.ft. unit in Nizamabad ODOP CFC at ₹2,000–₹4,000/month with kiln access. Option B: Contract-manufacture with 3–5 established artisans (₹1,000–₹2,000 per artisan per month + per-piece commission). Production cost per piece: ₹30–₹50 (all-in). Monthly production capacity: 500–1,200 units depending on complexity.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) online free at udyamregistration.gov.in (same day); file GI certification application with DC Handicraft (₹5,000–₹10,000, 3–6 months); apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) at DGFT portal (₹500, 5 days). Total time: 2–3 months, cost: ₹10,000–₹15,000.
5
First Sale
First sale: list on Etsy (₹150–₹300 per piece, 3–6% fees) or approach 2 Delhi interior design studios directly with samples. Minimum first order: 50–100 units from wholesale buyers or 200–300 INR revenue from first 5 Etsy sales. Expected first month: ₹15,000–₹30,000 gross revenue if selling wholesale; ₹8,000–₹12,000 if D2C only.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 2–3 dedicated production coordinators (₹8,000–₹12,000/month each), secure 1,500–2,000 monthly unit orders, add export logistics partner, exhibit at Delhi Home & Gift Fair (Jan/Nov), and register with 2–3 international importers. Transition from artisan partnerships to in-house small production unit (₹2–₹4L rent + 2–3 salaried artisans).
