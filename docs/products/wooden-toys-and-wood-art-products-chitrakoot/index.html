Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Chitrakoot ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call Chitrakoot DC Handicraft office (0-cost) to get names of 3-4 active toy makers; visit their workshops in Chitrakoot town center or Maidagin area on Friday; attend weekly wooden craft haat at Chitrakoot Bazaar to see live pricing and customer types. Record: unit production rate, raw material source, worker count, and waste patterns.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source wood from Satna timber merchants (92 km away) — Dudhi Sundari ₹8-12/kg, Koraiya ₹6-10/kg. Lac from local suppliers in Chitrakoot or order via IndiaMART. First batch cost for simple dolls/blocks (500 units): ₹18K-22K. Raw material area: Satna Timber Market on Rewa Road.
3
Produce / Partner
Start with artisan partnership model — rent a 400 sqft shed (₹2K-4K/month) in craft cluster area, hire 2-3 trained carvers at ₹200-300/day. Alternatively, partner with 1-2 established carvers on piece-rate (₹15-25 per unit) for first 6 months to derisk. 8 HP power requirement (₹3K-5K monthly for electricity and tools). Total monthly opex: ₹35K-45K.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam within 1 day (online, free). Apply for GI certification support via DC Handicraft (₹0-5K application cost, Ministry bears rest). Obtain IEC from DGFT Lucknow office online (₹0 application, 7-10 days processing) if targeting export. Branding via local trademark registration (₹1000-2000).
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Handmade, Flipkart Artisan, and Etsy simultaneously (₹0 listing cost). First sale target: 20 units via Etsy to US/Europe buyers at $5-12/unit (₹400-1000). Expected first month: ₹15K-25K revenue. Reach out to 5 Delhi-based toy importers on IndiaMART with ₹50K minimum order offer.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue, hire production manager (₹8K/month) and sales/export coordinator (₹6K/month). Invest in semi-automatic sanding machine (₹40K-60K) to reduce labor time. Apply for PMEGP expansion subsidy (30% capex subsidy). Exhibit at Handicraft India Expo (Delhi, Jan-Feb) and Nuremburg Toy Fair (Germany, Jan) for bulk export orders.
