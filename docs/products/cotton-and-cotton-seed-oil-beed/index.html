Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Beed ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Beed cotton markets (Parli Vaijnath, Gevrai mandis) and meet cotton ginning unit operators; study processing at established oil mills near Aurangabad; attend Maharashtra State Agricultural Department workshops on oil extraction standards
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw cotton from Beed district cooperative societies or direct farmers at ₹5,500-6,500/quintal; first batch of 50 quintals requires ₹3-3.5L; partner with local cotton gins for seed separation (₹200-300/quintal processing fee)
3
Produce / Partner
Set up mini cottonseed oil extraction unit with expeller or solvent extraction equipment (capacity 5-10 TPD) near Parli or Ashti; cost ₹8-12L for equipment; alternatively, outsource processing to established mills at ₹400-600/quintal margin; obtain FSSAI registration for food processing
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (micro/small enterprise), obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office for exports, register brand with FSSAI for edible oil category, secure ISO 9001 and ISO 22000 certifications (₹40,000-60,000); open dedicated PAN/GST account for food processing operations
5
First Sale
Launch on Indiamart and Tradeindia for B2B bulk orders; approach FMCG distributors in Maharashtra, Gujarat, Karnataka; create Amazon Business/Flipkart wholesale listings; directly pitch to food service companies and institutional buyers; list on Krishibid for farm-direct positioning
6
Scale
After 6 months, invest in own processing unit if outsourcing; expand geographic footprint to Vidarbha and Central India via distributor networks; develop private label offerings for retail FMCG brands; pursue ISO and organic certifications to access premium export markets; build agro-contract farming network for steady raw material supply
