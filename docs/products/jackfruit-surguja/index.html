Surguja district in northern Chhattisgarh sits along the Chhota Nagpur plateau with humid subtropical conditions suited to jackfruit. The district has direct road access via NH-6 and NH-39 to Mumbai, Kolkata, and port cities; within 500km radius are major food processing hubs in Madhya Pradesh and Chhattisgarh. Proximity to Sri Lanka (sea route 600km) and Bangladesh (land route 1200km) makes this an ideal export gateway for Asian markets consuming 45% of global jackfruit supply.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Surguja ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Surguja district fruit market (Raigarh or Ambikapur mandi) on a Tuesday or Friday, speak to 3-4 jackfruit orchardists and traders. Call Surguja ATMA (Agricultural Technology Management Agency) office at Ambikapur for climate/yield data — free. Register Aadhaar + Udyam (single form, free, 10 mins online). No money required, do this within 7 days.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source jackfruit from established growers in Surguja's Mainpur, Udaipur, and Ramanujganj blocks (8-10 ton/week available May-Sept). First batch: ₹25,000 for 500kg mixed varieties (Cheena and Golden Pillow). Visit local APMC mandi or contact Surguja Horticulture Department for grower networks. Negotiate direct harvest access to secure year-round small quantities.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent 1000 sq ft processing unit in Ambikapur or Raigarh (₹8,000-12,000/month) with basic drying, pulping, and packaging equipment (₹3-4L initial). Option B: Partner with 2-3 women's SHGs already processing jackfruit (share 15-20% revenue, zero capex). Start with freeze-drying or sun-drying for chips (low-cost entry). Total operational cost: ₹1.5-2L first 3 months (raw material + labor + packaging).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 15 mins). Apply for GI tag via Surguja District Industries Center (₹5,000-10,000 application fee, 6-month process). Obtain FSSAI registration (₹5,000-8,000, 2-3 weeks). If exporting: Get IEC code from Customs via DGFT (free, online, 24 hours). File trademark for brand name (₹4,500, 8-10 month approval). Total compliance cost: ₹25,000-35,000 over 4-5 months.
5
First Sale
First sale: List on Blink or Big Basket (3-5% commission) with 50kg jackfruit chips at ₹400/pack (500gm). Expected first month: 100-200 packs = ₹40,000-80,000 gross revenue. Parallel: Contact Britannia snack R&D (email via LinkedIn) with 5kg sample — B2B order potential is 2-5 ton/month at ₹100/kg = ₹2-5L/month after 2-3 months.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: Upgrade to 2500 sq ft processing unit (₹18,000/month rent), hire 2-3 production staff (₹6,000/month each), invest in IQF (Individually Quick Frozen) equipment (₹8-10L) to unlock 40% margin premium. Target 10-15 ton/month capacity by month 12. Exhibit at IITF Delhi (Nov), Anaheim Fancy Food Show (Jan) to unlock Australia/USA markets. Build export logistics via Star Agri or Cargill Fresh (freight forwarding partners in Ambikapur).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
up to ₹15,000 subsidy for artisans processing jackfruit, plus 5% loan guarantee from banks participating under the scheme.
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25-35% subsidy on equipment and machinery, up to ₹25 lakh for processing units in ODOP categories.
🏚
Chhattisgarh SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹7 lakh to ₹15 lakh subsidy for food clusters in Surguja, common facility centers for drying/packaging.
🏚
CG Mahila Kosh Yojana
subsidized loans at 3% interest for women-led jackfruit processing SHGs, up to ₹5 lakh per group.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
