Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kishanganj ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit successful dragon fruit farms in Aurangabad (Maharashtra) and Jalgaon districts; connect with ICAR research centers in Bihar; interview fruit wholesalers at Kissan Mandi in Patna; study climate data from Kishanganj meteorological office.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source healthy dragon fruit cuttings from ICAR-RCER Patna or established nurseries in Maharashtra (₹50-100 per cutting); bulk drip irrigation systems from Jain Irrigation dealers in Patna; organic fertilizer from local agricultural co-operatives; first 1-acre setup approximately ₹3-4 lakhs.
3
Produce / Partner
Start with 0.5-1 acre demonstration plot; partner with 2-3 experienced farmers in Kishanganj for contract growing; implement drip irrigation and vertical trellising systems; engage ATMA (Agricultural Technology Management Agency) for technical support and soil testing.
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam (MSME) for farm-based enterprise; obtain FPO (Farmer Producer Organisation) membership; apply for IEC code for exports; register food business license if processing fruits; create brand identity emphasizing 'Kishanganj Premium Dragon Fruit' with sustainability narrative.
5
First Sale
Launch on Blinkit and JioMart with competitive pricing (₹200-300/kg retail); partner with Metro, DMart, and Spencer's for shelf space; connect with juice chains (Jamba Juice, local smoothie shops) in Delhi-NCR; list on Lybrate and Amazon Fresh; establish WhatsApp-based bulk ordering with corporate offices.
6
Scale
Expand to 5-10 acres by month 12; develop cold storage facility in Kishanganj; create processed product line (freeze-dried powder, dragon fruit leather); establish direct export relationships with 2-3 Southeast Asian importers; organize farm tours to attract agri-tourism revenue; secure contract farming with 15-20 farmers in district.
