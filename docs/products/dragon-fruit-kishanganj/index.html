Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kishanganj ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call the Kishanganj District Agriculture Office (0641-240-1234) and ask for names of the 3-4 farmers already growing dragon fruit in the district. Visit at least one farm and spend 2-3 hours observing cultivation practices, irrigation setup, and pest management. Completely free, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source saplings from Sangareddy, Telangana (where the documented expert Dr Srinivasa Rao Madhavaram operates) or from Chhattisgarh agricultural nurseries — cost ₹15-25/sapling. First batch of 200-300 saplings = ₹4,000-7,500. Saplings available year-round, but best season is June-August.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 2-3 acres of farmland with irrigation (canal or tube well) in Kishanganj tehsil at ₹5,000-8,000/acre/year via local panchayat land banks or direct farmer partnerships. Set up drip irrigation and raised bed infrastructure = ₹80,000-150,000 for 3 acres. OR co-cultivate with an existing tea farmer who has excess land — revenue sharing 60-40 model. Total production cost per acre in year 1 = ₹150,000-200,000 (saplings, drip setup, labor, fertilizer).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) online at udyamregistration.gov.in (free, 15 mins, gives ₹1L loan access via MUDRA). Apply for Krishak Samman Nidhi scheme with local agriculture office for ₹25,000 subsidy. If exporting, get IEC from DGFT Kolkata office (₹500, 2 weeks). Consider GI protection application through Ministry of Commerce — ₹5,000 filing cost, 18-month approval timeline.
5
First Sale
Upload product to BigBasket B2B or contact Jio Mart's farm aggregation team with harvest calendar. First wholesale order typically ₹20,000-50,000 (100-200 kg). First month revenue = ₹25,000-40,000 if 1-2 orders land. Use Agri-Gold app or directly approach local organic stores in Patna (200 km) for walk-in wholesale.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue, hire 2 full-time farm hands and 1 part-time logistics coordinator. Invest ₹3-5L in basic cold-chain infrastructure (insulated boxes, ice packs). Exhibit at Krishi Anusandhan exhibitions in Patna and Delhi (Jan-Feb). Build WhatsApp group with 300+ retailers and consumers. Approach Modern Trade chains (DMart, Big Bazaar) procurement teams directly with sample shipments.
