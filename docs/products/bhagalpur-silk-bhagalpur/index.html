Bhagalpur Silk ki
opportunity, Bhagalpur mein.

Bhagalpur district in northern Bihar sits on the Ganges River with direct rail and road connectivity via NH-31, positioning it as India's second-largest silk cluster with 25,000 looms and 100,000 people engaged in cocoon sericulture, yarn spinning, and weaving. Raw cocoons arrive from local mulberry farms and neighboring districts; the Maskan Bari and Puraini Weaver Centres form the production spine. Buyers span West Asia (primary), Europe, US, Japan, and domestic home furnishing/fashion brands concentrated in Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bhagalpur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Maskan Bari or Puraini Weaver Centre clusters this month (free, 2-hour trip from city center). Call 3 registered cooperative societies listed on BHAGALPUR.BIHAR.GOV.IN ODOP page; ask weaver contact rates, lead times, cocoon prices. Attend weekly haat at Maskan Bari to meet direct weavers. Register free Udyam account at udyamregistration.gov.in (15 min online).
2
Source Raw Materials
Contact Puraini Weavers' Centre office (phone via district administration) for artisan rate-sheets; expect ₹20/meter tusser silk weaving labor. Source raw cocoons from Bihar Sericulture Department supplier list or local traders in Bhagalpur town (Chowk Bazaar area); first batch: 100 kg cocoons = ~₹15,000-18,000, yields ~1,500-2,000 meters finished silk. Arrange silk dyeing at one of 8-10 dyeing units in Narsarai, 15 km away.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (asset-light): Partner with 2-3 registered weavers on retainer (₹25,000-40,000/month each for 50-100 looms output). Option B (capital): Rent 500-1,000 sq ft loom unit in Maskan Bari industrial cluster (₹15,000-25,000/month), hire 8-12 part-time weavers at ₹20/meter piecemeal. Subsidized modern looms available through Bihar Handloom Department scheme (40% subsidy on ₹1-1.5L per loom). Monthly raw material + labor: ₹2-3L for 4,000-6,000 meters production.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 15 min). Apply for GI certification support under DC Handloom ministry (process: file application with district administration, ₹5,000-10,000 legal fee, 6-8 month approval, already awarded 2009 so use existing tag). For export: IEC registration at local DGFT office (₹5,000, 10 days) + open EEFC bank account (free at SBI/HDFC). File for PMEGP if setup includes looms (₹25L subsidy max, 25% founder contribution).
5
First Sale
First sale: Join IndiaArtizans.com or 1stDibs (free listing, 15% commission). Post 10-12 product shots (cushion covers, stoles, dupattas, sarees) via pre-order model on Instagram (tag @bhagalpursilk, DM buyers directly). Expected first order: 20-30 meters from boutique fashion brand or interior designer (₹800-1,200/meter wholesale = ₹16,000-36,000). Target first-month revenue: ₹80,000-1,20,000 (5-6 small orders).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: Hire 1 full-time production manager (₹20,000/month) to coordinate weavers. Partner with 1-2 additional cooperatives for parallel production runs. Invest ₹2-3L in branded packaging (custom boxes, tissue, hang tags). Exhibit at India International Handloom Expo (Delhi, Bangalore annually), Home and Décor Expo, or Surajkund Mela (Jan-Feb). Pursue wholesale contracts with large home chains (FabIndia, Westside Home). Set up dedicated export desk; target 2-3 containers/year to Germany, UK, US within 18 months.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (PM Employment Generation Programme)
Up to ₹25 lakhs subsidy for handloom unit setup; 25% founder contribution required; processed via district Khadi Board
🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹15,000 tooling grant + 5% interest subsidy on ₹1L loans for registered artisans; implemented via SIDBI
🏚
Bihar Handloom Park Scheme
40% subsidy on modern looms (₹1-1.5L per loom); land lease at ₹5/sq ft in Maskan Bari cluster; application via District Handicraft Office
🏚
Weavers' Loan Waiver Scheme (Bihar)
Eligible for outstanding artisan loans up to ₹50,000 waived if registered with cooperative society
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
