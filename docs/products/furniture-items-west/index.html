Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — West ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kirti Nagar furniture cluster (West Delhi) on a weekday morning — walk 2-3 showrooms, talk to 2-3 shop owners about their sourcing, production, and margins. Call 2 timber suppliers in Himachal (get numbers from shop owners). Attend Delhi Furniture Fair (usually Feb-Mar) or browse ODOP official page for district contacts — all free, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Timber from Kangra Valley (Himachal Pradesh) — ₹40-60/sqft for premium wood; metal fittings from Rohtak/Bahadurgarh (Haryana) — ₹5-15k per ton; Plastic/polymer from Delhi NCR suppliers. First batch of 20-30 units requires ₹1.5-2.5L raw material spend. Kirti Nagar has timber wholesalers within 2-5 km radius.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 800-1200 sqft workshop unit in Kirti Nagar, Amar Colony, or Mundka (₹10-18k/month) with basic CNC/carpentry tools. Alternatively, hire 4-6 skilled carpenters on contract basis (₹300-500/day each) and set up in a 400 sqft rented space (₹6-10k/month). Setup cost for tools: ₹3-8L. Labour cost per unit: ₹2-5k depending on complexity.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 10 mins — get UID). Apply for MSME Udyam PMEGP subsidy (if eligible, 25% subsidy up to ₹25L). Obtain BIS certification for furniture (₹20-40k, 4-6 weeks). If targeting export: Apply for IEC (₹0, online, 7 days) and register with APEDA (₹0). Optional: Apply for GI registration if planning district-wide collective brand (₹4-8L legal costs, 2-3 years).
5
First Sale
First sale: List 3-5 sofa/table designs on Udaan, Pepperfry within 2-3 weeks of production. Approach 2-3 local retailers in Amar Colony/Kirti Nagar simultaneously — offer wholesale discount of 20-25%. First order realistically ₹50-200k from retail buyer or ₹100-400k from platform (10-15 units). Expected first-month revenue: ₹2-5L if executing well.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: Hire production manager (₹25-40k/month), hire 2-3 additional carpenters, move to 1500-2000 sqft workshop. Invest in CNC machine (₹8-15L) to reduce per-unit cost by 30-40%. Apply for SFURTI cluster development grant (₹50L available for group of 10+ units). Exhibit at India Furniture Fair, Heimtextil (Germany), showcase on LinkedIn. Explore bulk B2B contracts with real estate developers and hotel chains.

Seedhe sawaal, seedhe jawab

₹3-15 lakh se shuru kar sakte ho. Minimum: ₹3L (small workshop, basic tools, first batch raw material). Better: ₹8-10L (1000 sqft space, CNC machine, 2 carpenters, 3 months inventory). Maximum: ₹15L (full setup with 4-5 workers, advertising, website).
Haan, carpentry ya production background zaroor chahiye. Agar nahi hai toh 3-6 mahine kisi manufacturer ke saath intern/work karke seekh lo. Design jaankari optional hai — you can hire a designer (₹10-20k/project).
PMEGP: 25% subsidy up to ₹25L. MUDRA: ₹10L loan at 8-10%. Delhi MSME subsidy: 25% on machinery up to ₹5L. SFURTI cluster grant: ₹50L if you form group of 10 units. PM Vishwakarma: ₹15k direct benefit if registered artisan. Udyam registration: free, 10 mins online.
Start local: 2-3 retailers in Kirti Nagar/Amar Colony ko wholesale offer do (20-25% discount). Saath saath Udaan, Pepperfry, Urban Ladder par list karo — 7-15 din mein first D2C order aayega. Real estate developers ko directly approach karo — ek project se ₹5-20L order mil sakta hai.
Bilkul, high demand hai UAE, Saudi Arabia, UK, USA mein. Process: IEC registration (free, online), Udyam registration, APEDA membership. First order ₹2-5L minimum. Shipping cost 5-8% of FOB value. Margins 40-55% FOB. Lion/Alibaba/direct B2B distributors best channels hain.
Real risk: over-production, bad market timing, design mismatch. Exit options: (1) Pivot to B2B bulk orders (schools, offices) — safer demand. (2) White-label manufacturing for established brands — lower risk than own brand. (3) Sell equipment, return to carpentry work. Safest: start with wholesale only, not D2C, until you have 3+ repeat customers.
