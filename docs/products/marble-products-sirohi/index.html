Marble Products ki
opportunity, Sirohi mein.

Sirohi district has abundant high-quality marble reserves and a centuries-old tradition of marble carving. The region's skilled artisan community, passed down through generations, combined with access to raw marble quarries, makes it naturally suited for premium marble product manufacturing. The district's cultural heritage and established craft networks provide a strong foundation for scaling marble-based businesses.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sirohi ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sirohi district marble clusters in Abu Road and Pindwara towns; spend 1 week learning from established artisans at workshops; study competitor offerings on Etsy and Amazon; benchmark pricing across ₹5K-₹100K product range; estimated research budget: ₹50K
2
Source Raw Materials
Source marble blocks from Sirohi quarries (contact District Industries Centre for supplier list); negotiate first batch of 2-3 tonnes at ₹15K-25K per tonne; procure carving tools from Udaipur handicraft suppliers; first-batch raw material cost: ₹200K-400K
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 established artisans in Abu Road on revenue-share basis (40-50% artisan cut); establish small workshop (500-1000 sqft) for finishing, quality-check, and packaging; or work as inventory partner with existing cooperative units in Sirohi
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) online at udyamregistration.gov.in; obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Jaipur for export eligibility; register brand name with State Industries Department; apply for Rajasthan government ODOP benefit certification
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy (15% fee, global audience) and Amazon.in with 8-12 product SKUs; build Instagram shop with artisan storytelling (reels showing carving process); approach interior designers in Delhi/Mumbai with bulk order samples; list on Alibaba for B2B export inquiries; initial monthly sales target: ₹3-5L
6
Scale
Hire 2-3 additional skilled artisans by month 6; expand product range to 25+ SKUs; invest in professional photography/videography for marketing; establish branded retail presence on luxury e-commerce (Rare Earth by Sheetal Matai, The Artistry); target corporate gifting and hospitality sector with bulk orders; aim for ₹20-30L monthly revenue by month 12

PM Vishwakarma
₹15K skill development grant + ₹1L collateral-free loan for registered artisans and craftspeople

Rajasthan ODOP Support Scheme
₹10L working capital support + brand development grant for identified ODOP products
🏚
Rajasthan Handicraft Promotion Scheme
50% subsidy on machinery purchase and 20% on export logistics for registered handicraft units
