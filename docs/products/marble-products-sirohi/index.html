Sirohi district in southwestern Rajasthan straddles limestone-rich Aravalli foothills, giving direct quarry access within 30-40 km. The district hosts 180+ registered marble units and 2000+ skilled carvers concentrated in Sirohi town and Abu Road tehsil. Established export channels reach Middle East (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Oman) and Southeast Asia (Thailand, Vietnam, Malaysia) via Mumbai and Delhi ports, with shipping times of 15-25 days.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sirohi ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sirohi marble cluster (Sirohi town main bazaar) on a Tuesday or Thursday when artisans are working. Spend 3 hours calling 5-6 established marble unit owners via Rajasthan ODOP contact list and ask them about raw material costs, lead times, and current export demand. Register free Udyam account online (takes 20 mins). Cost: ₹0, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw marble blanks from 3-4 quarries in Abu Road tehsil (35 km south) or prefabricated blocks from Sirohi main market. First batch of 50 units (decorative items, vases, coasters mix) costs ₹40,000-80,000. Compare 3 suppliers before committing. Check for white and black marble quality first — these account for 60% of sales.
3
Produce / Partner
Start with artisan partnership model: rent a 400-500 sq ft workshop in Sirohi industrial area (₹6,000-10,000/month) or collaborate with existing carvers on revenue-share (25-30% commission on retail price). Initial investment: tools (chisels, grinding wheels, polishing compounds) = ₹30,000-50,000. Production capacity: 30-40 pieces/month with 2-3 skilled carvers.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 2 days). Apply for One District One Product scheme support via Rajasthan Handicraft Ministry (₹50,000-2L grant for tooling and branding). Get IEC code from customs if targeting exports (₹0, takes 1 week online via DGFT portal). Optional: GI registration takes 18-24 months but boosts export premium by 15-25%.
5
First Sale
First sale: list 10-15 items on Etsy or Artifactly with studio photography (₹8,000-15,000 for pro shoot). Target first orders through WhatsApp to local interior designers and architects (contact via Instagram DMs). First-month revenue expectation: ₹30,000-80,000 if 3-5 orders materialized. Aim for 2-3 medium wholesale orders (₹2-5L each) from Metro design firms simultaneously.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 2 additional skilled carvers (₹15,000-20,000/month each), invest ₹3-5L in a kiln and finishing equipment, secure a 1,000 sq ft showroom-cum-workshop in Sirohi town. Exhibit at IHGF (Indian Handicrafts & Gift Fair) Delhi and GCC Jeddah trade shows. Build a small export team to handle documentation (shipping, invoices, compliance) — outsource to a customs broker (₹5,000-10,000 per shipment). Consider private label partnerships with hospitality chains (contract manufacturing).
