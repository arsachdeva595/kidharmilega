Patna, Bihar's capital, sits on the Ganges River corridor with direct rail and NH connectivity to major electrical equipment clusters in Pune, Bangalore, and East Coast ports. The district has a concentrated artisan base of brass workers with multi-generational expertise in rod, wire, and strip production. Raw material sourcing is streamlined via national copper-zinc suppliers and port linkages from Paradip and Haldia, placing delivered costs competitively against Jamnagar and other Western brass hubs.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Patna ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Patna's brass artisan cluster in Gulzarbagh or Kankarbagh (free) — observe production techniques, connect with 3-4 master craftsmen, call local brass wire suppliers (Jain Traders, Sharma Metals) for current copper-zinc pricing. Register for free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in). Zero cost; do this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw copper and zinc from Jain Enterprises (Patna) or national suppliers via IndiaMART — expect ₹90-120/kg for copper, ₹180-220/kg for zinc. First batch of 100 kg costs ₹18-25L depending on purity and market timing. Lead time: 5-7 days.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent a 1500-2000 sq ft workshop in Gulzarbagh industrial area (₹8-12K/month) + invest in basic furnaces, drawing machines, quality tools (₹80-120L). OR Option B: Partner with established artisan on revenue-share (20-30% commission on output). Both start with 20-30 kg daily capacity, scale to 100+ kg/day within 6 months.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME (free, 15 mins online). Obtain IEC code from Director General of Foreign Trade (DGFT) for exports (₹5K, 10 days). Apply for PM Vishwakarma if partnering with traditional artisans (50% subsidy on tools up to ₹15K). File GI registration application with Geographical Indications Registry (₹10-15K, 18-24 month process) to strengthen brand positioning.
5
First Sale
First sale target: B2B. Contact 10 electrical equipment manufacturers in Delhi NCR via LinkedIn or trade portals; send samples of brass rods/wires with COA. Expect first order of 500-1000 kg at ₹5-8L revenue. Timeline: 3-4 weeks from first contact to purchase order. Parallel: List on TradeKey, GlobalSources for export inquiries.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire a quality control officer (₹20-25K/month), invest in semi-automated drawing machines (₹40-50L), expand to 2-3 production shifts. Exhibit at India International Trade Fair (IITF), Engineering Export Promotion Council (EEPC) tradeshows (₹2-3L booth cost). Build WhatsApp bulk order groups with top 20 customers.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹15,000 tool/equipment subsidy for artisans; 5% annual interest subvention on loans up to ₹125,000; free skill certification.
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25-35% subsidy on setup cost (max ₹25L), interest-free term loan for 5 years for Patna-registered MSMEs.
🏚
Bihar State ODOP Scheme
15% subsidy on machinery purchase; zero-interest working capital loans up to ₹10L; free participation in state-level exhibitions.
🏚
Bihar Handicraft Promotion Scheme
₹2L subsidy for cluster-based artisans; free design consultation; export support fund covering 50% of export certification costs.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
