Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Chhatarpur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call 5 furniture makers in Chhatarpur town (get numbers from ODOP district office or district.chhatarpur.nic.in). Visit one workshop on a Tuesday or Wednesday to see raw material sourcing, unit economics, and lead times. Attend Chhatarpur District Industries Centre office meeting (free) to understand tender process and ODOP support. This costs ₹0 but takes full day; do this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source wood from Chhindwara or Seoni timber yards (50-80 km away, quality-grade sal/teak costs ₹4,500-6,500/cubic meter). First batch 2-3 cubic meters = ₹12-18K for raw material to test designs. Connect via existing cluster suppliers or reach out to Chhatarpur ODOP cell for preferred vendor list. Confirm 15-day delivery from timber seller.
3
Produce / Partner
Start via artisan partnership (no capital): hire 2-3 skilled carpenters (₹300-400/day each, 6-month contract), rent 1000-1500 sq ft workshop in Chhatarpur town (₹4,000-8,000/month). Or: invest ₹3-5L to set up your own unit with hand tools + 1-2 power saws. Artisan route = ₹0 setup; owned workshop = ₹3-5L. First product delivery in 30-40 days; expect 10-15 units/month in Year 1.
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam MSME (free, 5 days, online). Apply to ODOP cell for certification (₹2,000-5,000 processing, 20 days, includes Chhatarpur ODOP sticker/backing). If export target: apply for IEC (Importer Exporter Code) via Customs within 10 days of Udyam, free. Open business bank account (₹0). Skip GI registration unless scaling to ₹5Cr+ (GI application costs ₹10-15K and takes 18-24 months).
5
First Sale
First sale: target local school tender (search Chhatarpur District Education department tender portal online). First order realistic: 15-20 units (6-8 school chairs, 4-5 study desks) = ₹40-60K order value, 25% margin = ₹10-15K profit. Simultaneously list 5 products on Amazon/Flipkart (free listing). Expected first month revenue: ₹30-60K (mix of 1-2 institutional orders + 3-5 D2C orders). Timeline to first sale: 45-60 days.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 full-time sales manager (₹15-20K/month) to handle tenders and B2B calls. Invest ₹1-2L in larger workshop (2500 sq ft), add 3-4 permanent carpenters. Register for export compliance (freight forwarder contact, shipping documentation). Exhibit at IITF Delhi (₹1.5-2.5L booth cost) and UP/CG trade fairs. Partner with 2-3 institutional dealers in Delhi, Indore to handle wholesale pipeline. Hire 1 content person (₹10K/month) for Instagram/YouTube furniture videos.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
up to ₹15,000 as toolkit subsidy for artisans; 5% interest rate on ₹1-5L loans for registered wood craftspeople
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25% subsidy (up to ₹10L) for general entrepreneurs, 35% for SC/ST, for workshop setup and machinery
🏚
Madhya Pradesh ODOP Interest Subsidy
5% interest subvention on loans up to ₹10L for 3 years for registered ODOP artisans and small businesses
🏚
MP Kaushal Vikas Scheme
apprenticeship stipends of ₹5,000-8,000/month for skilled training in wood furniture making (12-month courses)
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
