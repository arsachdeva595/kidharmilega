Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — East Garo Hills ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit established pineapple clusters in East Garo Hills (Ampati, Williamnagar areas); connect with District Agriculture Office & KVK East Garo Hills; attend APEDA export training workshops in Shillong; study Jain Irrigation drip farming models for hilly terrain
2
Source Raw Materials
Source elite pineapple suckers from ICAR-ATARI (Assam) or certified nurseries in Meghalaya; procure 2000-3000 suckers for 0.5-1 acre pilot at ₹6-10 per sucker; organic compost from local farmers (₹2000-3000/acre); total first-batch cost ₹30,000-50,000
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with FPOs like Meghalaya Horticulture Producers Co-operative or individual farmers in Williamnagar block; implement drip irrigation on slopes; maintain 2m×1.5m spacing; harvest 12-16 months post-planting; train local farmers on hybrid varieties (Kew, Red Spanish, Mauritius)
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam via udyamregistration.gov.in as sole proprietor; apply for IEC from nearest DGFT office (Shillong); obtain FSSAI license for processed products (₹5000-10000); trademark brand name via IP India; register GST under agricultural produce exempt initially
5
First Sale
List on Jio Mart, Blinkit, Amazon Fresh with SKU photos; approach Ratnadeep, Nature's Basket in Bangalore/Pune; sell surplus to organized juice brands (Minute Maid, Tropicana bottling units); direct B2B to hotels via Hypercity, Reliance Fresh; create Instagram account with farm-to-table story content
6
Scale
Expand cultivation to 5-10 acres by month 12; establish post-harvest facility with ripening chambers and packing line (₹50-80L investment); partner with food processor to launch frozen/canned products; apply for APEDA export promotion incentives; diversify into value-added products (juices, pickles, jams)
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L credit-linked subsidy for agro-processing units in NE region with 25% subsidy for general category
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹25L for cluster development of food processing in Meghalaya; focus on common facility centers
🏚
Meghalaya Agricultural Mechanization Scheme
50% subsidy on drip irrigation and farming machinery for horticulture crops; contact Department of Agriculture, Meghalaya
🏚
State Horticulture Mission (Meghalaya)
free training, subsidized saplings (30-50% cost), and technical support for commercial pineapple cultivation in East Garo Hills
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
