Industrial clusters here already produce engineering goods, making it ideal for scaling steel furniture production.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — North 24 Parganas ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Barrackpur and Naihati industrial clusters; meet existing steel furniture manufacturers at Barrackpur Industrial Exposition ground; study market at Kolkata furniture hubs (Salt Lake, Topsia); analyze competitors on IndiaMART and Alibaba.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source mild steel coils from Tata Steel vendors in Jamshedpur (₹25-30L for 10 MT first batch); fasteners from Kolkata's Baghbazar wholesale market; powder coating from local suppliers in Naihati. Total first-batch raw material cost: ₹35-40L.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing job-work units in Barrackpur (₹2-3L monthly capacity rent) or invest in 2,000 sq ft workshop with CNC cutting, welding, and powder coating setup. Hire 4-6 skilled welders and 2 supervisors. Start with small batch production (50-100 units/month) and scale.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Ministry MSME portal), obtain IEC from nearest DGFT office in Kolkata, register GST, and acquire ISO 9001 certification. File trademark for brand name with IP India (₹5,000-10,000).
5
First Sale
Launch on Flipkart B2B, IndiaMART, and TradeKey for B2B; create Instagram/Facebook catalog showcasing designs; partner with 2-3 furniture dealers in Kolkata for offline distribution; directly pitch to corporate procurement teams via LinkedIn.
6
Scale
After 6 months, invest in CNC bending/cutting machinery (₹50-75L); expand team to 15-20; develop modular design range; apply for export subsidies; establish showroom in Kolkata's High Street; explore bulk contracts with hotel/hospitality chains.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy for manufacturing units in non-metro North 24 Parganas.
🏛
MUDRA Scheme
up to ₹10L collateral-free loan for working capital and machinery.
🏚
West Bengal Industrial Infrastructure Development Corporation (WIIDC)
subsidized land/shed allotment in Barrackpur industrial park with 5-10 year lease benefits.
🏚
West Bengal Chief Minister's Employment Generation Scheme
wage subsidy for first 100 employees hired in manufacturing sector.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
