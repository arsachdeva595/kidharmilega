Buyer markets are pan-India schools and institutions; success story Maskeen Overseas supplies competitively across the country.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — North 24 Parganas ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Barasat Steel Furniture Cluster (Barasat town, near bus stand) — walk 3-4 existing units, ask production cost per bench, talk to Maskeen Overseas if accessible. Call 3 stainless steel suppliers in Howrah (Kadam Ganj metal market) and ask raw material pricing. Free. Doable this week. Also check current GeM registration and school tender websites to see live demand.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source stainless steel from Howrah Kadam Ganj market (₹450-550/kg) or direct from CST Steel Kolkata; wood from West Bengal timber yards in Barrackpore (₹400-600 per cu ft). First batch cost for 10 units (benches + chairs): ₹80-120K. All suppliers concentrated in Howrah and Barrackpore — 20-30 km from Barasat.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 2000 sq ft workshop in Barasat MSME cluster or Bidhannagar industrial area (₹8-12K/month). Start with 2-3 trained artisans (₹12-15K/month each). Buy welding equipment, hand tools (₹1.5-2.5L one-time). Alternatively, partner with 1-2 existing artisans and subcontract production (no rent cost, ₹5-8K per unit production cost). Scale to in-house by month 4-6 when revenue flows.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME online (free, 10 mins, udyamregistration.gov.in). Open business bank account (ICICI, HDFC offer free MSME accounts). If exporting, apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) via DGFT portal (₹0 cost, 2 weeks processing) and GST registration (18% on furniture). No GI registration required for this product.
5
First Sale
Post product photos + specs on GeM (free listing, 30-40% of institutional buyers search here). Reach out to 5-10 school furniture distributors in Delhi/Mumbai via LinkedIn and email (first order typically ₹5-15L, 20-30 units). Push Instagram content: before-after school setups, durability videos. Target first sale month 2-3. Expected revenue first month: ₹2-5L from 1-2 orders.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 1 sales person (₹15-20K) and 2 additional artisans; invest ₹3-4L in hydraulic press for faster production. Exhibit at India Furniture Expo (Delhi, May), Delhi School Expo. Open WhatsApp catalog channel for distributor orders. Approach corporate offices for office seating custom orders (higher margin). Explore Bangladesh/Nepal export via Kolkata freight forwarders. By month 12, aim for ₹20-30L/month revenue.
