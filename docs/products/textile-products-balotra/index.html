Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Balotra ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Balotra textile clusters in Sumerpur and city center; connect with master printers at Textile Association of Balotra; spend 1 week shadowing 2-3 established printers; analyze 5 competitor products on Etsy and Amazon.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source 100% cotton base fabric from Pali or Jodhpur suppliers (₹150-200 per meter for bulk orders); natural dyes from local suppliers or Surat chemical market (₹8,000-15,000 first batch); wooden blocks from local artisans (₹2,000-5,000 per block set); aim for ₹80,000-120,000 first-batch material cost.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 1-2 established block printers in Sumerpur (negotiate 40-50% of retail price for production); or hire 2-3 skilled dyers and 1 block printer directly (monthly cost ₹15,000-25,000); start with 500 meters monthly production, scale to 2,000 by month 4.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam at udyamregistration.gov.in (free, 15 mins); apply for IEC code through DGFT portal (₹0 cost); register brand name and logo with IP India; obtain textile quality certifications (GOTS or Oeko-Tex optional but valuable for exports).
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy (12% fee) and Amazon Handmade (15% fee) with 15-20 initial SKUs; cold-email 30 home décor brands and fashion retailers with samples; list on Airtrade and TradeKey for B2B export leads; initiate Instagram content showing process and heritage story.
6
Scale
Hire dedicated export manager to pursue EU/US distributors after first ₹20L revenue; expand product line to apparel and furnishings; establish brand presence at Delhi Fair, IHGF exhibitions; build artisan collective model to increase production capacity to 10,000 meters/month.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy for textile micro-enterprises, covers 35% of project cost.
🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
up to ₹3 lakh collateral-free loan plus ₹15,000 toolkit grant for traditional craftspeople and artisans in textiles.
🏚
Rajasthan Textile Policy 2020
5% VAT exemption, power subsidy of ₹2/unit for textile manufacturing units, export promotion rebate up to 5%.
🏚
Rajasthan MSME Udyam Grant Scheme
₹1 lakh one-time subsidy for registered MSME textile units, plus facilitation for government tenders.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
