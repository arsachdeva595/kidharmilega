Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Dahod ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit corn farming clusters around Dahod taluka (Kukshi, Fatehpur villages), meet 3-5 established corn millers near Ujjain Road, attend Gujarat Agro-Industries Association meetings, benchmark product quality against competitors in Indore and Bhopal markets
2
Source Raw Materials
Source seed corn from Gujarat State Seed Corporation (GSSC) or Nuziveedu Seeds; negotiate with Dahod farmer cooperatives for ₹18-22 per kg bulk rates; first batch cost (5MT) approximately ₹1.2-1.5L including processing; establish contracts with 10-15 local farmers
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing FMCG-certified mills in Dahod (Jhalod Co-operative Mill) or set up modular milling unit (corn grinder, roaster, packaging machine); hire experienced miller and food safety supervisor; obtain FSSAI license (₹5,000-8,000); conduct trial batches for quality and shelf-life testing
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam under food processing category, apply for IEC code via ICEGATE for export readiness, trademark brand name with IP office, obtain FSSAI food safety certificate and FPO registration, design packaging with nutrition labels in 2-3 Indian languages
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh, Jiomart, and Blinkit with pre-launch influencer seeding in Gujarat tier-2 cities; approach 15-20 local kirana chains and Patanjali stockists with wholesale discounts (10-15%); partner with 2-3 food distribution platforms (Khatabook, NinjaCart); conduct sampling activations in Indore, Bhopal, and Dahod city markets
6
Scale
Expand SKU range (roasted corn, corn poha, corn flour variants); establish distribution in 3-4 neighboring states (Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Rajasthan); apply for Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samridhi Yojana subsidy; build farmer producer organization for supply stability; invest in automated packaging to increase capacity 3x by month 12
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Pradhan Mantri Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy for food processing unit (35% for general, higher for SC/ST)
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹5-15L cluster development grant for Dahod corn processing cluster
🏚
Gujarat Food Processing Policy 2021
25% capital subsidy on processing equipment, 5-year property tax exemption
🏚
Mukhya Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (MMKSY)
subsidized water access for contract farming arrangements
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
