Corn Products ki
opportunity, Dahod mein.

Dahod district (3,642 sq km) is 140 km south of Indore and 160 km east of Vadodara, with rain-fed maize cultivation across 880+ sq km of farmland. The district's 74% tribal population (primarily Bhil communities) has 400+ years of millet and maize farming heritage, controlling 60,000+ small landholdings of 1-3 hectares each. Buyer markets are Vadodara, Indore, and Surat (combined 8M people) plus export corridors to Mundra and Pipavav ports (320-380 km away).

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Dahod ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Zero-cost: Visit Dahod mandi on Monday/Thursday at 6 AM; call 3 maize suppliers in Fatehpur-Taloda tehsil (names from district agriculture office); attend Dahod Krishi Haat (monthly, free entry); check Ministry ODOP data and talk to 2 existing corn processors in Dahod town. Takes 3-4 days total.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from Fatehpur-Taloda-Devgarh maize belt (80 km network). First 10 tonnes batch: ₹4-5L. Contact 5-6 farmer producer groups (FPGs) under NAFED. Order via agriculture mandis in Fatehpur or Taloda; transport via hired truck ₹8,000-12,000 per 10 tonne load.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 1,500 sq ft unit in Dahod Industrial Estate or Fatehpur artisan zones (₹8,000-15,000/month). Partner with 2-3 local grain processors (dal mills converting to corn) on revenue-share model OR buy hammer mill + dryer setup (₹4-8L). Start with contract processing for 6 months (₹0 capex risk). 10 tonnes/day processing capacity = ₹1.2-1.5L monthly throughput at 20-30% margin.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 10 minutes online). Apply for GI registration through Ministry of Food Processing (₹5,000-15,000, 8-12 months, but not mandatory initially). If exporting: Register IEC with DGFT Vadodara office (₹500, 15 days). Get FSSAI license (₹2,000-5,000, 30 days). Test grain at NDRI Karnal (₹3,000) for export certification.
5
First Sale
Day 1: List on JioMart Seller, Blinkit Direct, Amazon Seller Central (free). Day 7: Cold-call bakeries in Indore (Babaria, Rau) and Vadodara (Alkapuri industrial area) with 5 kg free sample + quote. First order size: 100-200 kg to local restaurants/cafes at ₹55/kg. Expected first-month revenue: ₹1.8-2.5L from 5-6 institutional buyers.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: Hire 1 QC person + 1 sales person (₹15,000/month each). Participate in Annapoorna Organic Expo (Delhi, January), GulfFood (Dubai, February). Approach modern trade chains (Spencer's, Aditya Birla); apply for PMEGP subsidy (₹25-30L) to upgrade to automated line. Set up second unit in Pitol for proximity to Indore market (saves ₹3,000/month transport).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
₹25-27L subsidy cap (35% of project cost for general, 50% for SC/ST); applicants get 5% bank loan interest subsidy for 5 years
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹50L cluster development grant for 10-15 existing units + ₹2-3L per unit for equipment; Gujarat allocation: ₹8-10 Cr annually across food clusters
🏚
Gujarat ODOP Scheme
Export promotion subsidy of 5% on FOB value for first ₹5 Cr exports; processing equipment subsidy 25-40% under GVCEPC
🏚
Mukhyamantri Krishak Sanjay Yojana
Free crop insurance + ₹10,000/hectare subsidy on maize farming for tribal farmers; benefits farmers in your supply base
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
