Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Gumla ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit ragi farming clusters in Gumla (villages around Raidih, Gurru, and Dumri blocks). Meet 3-5 established ragi farmers and FPCs like Gumla Krishi Vikas Samiti. Study local varieties and yields. Attend agricultural fairs at Gumla town and connect with district agriculture office.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source organic ragi directly from farmer cooperatives in Gumla at ₹3,500-4,200/quintal. For first batch (5-10 quintals), budget ₹20,000-40,000. Partner with FPCs (Farmer Producer Companies) registered under SFURTI for bulk supply. Visit grain mandis in Gumla town (Lingra Mandi).
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with certified ragi millers in Gumla or nearby Simdega district for cleaning, grading, and milling (₹500-800 per quintal). Get organic certification (FSSAI + NPOP) through Jharkhand Organic Mission. Start with co-packing or small-scale own production; invest in basic packaging equipment (sealer, labeling machine) for ₹1.5-3 lakhs.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) using online portal (free). Apply for IEC code via DGFT portal if exporting. Obtain FSSAI license (₹10,000-20,000). Trademark brand name with IP office. Get organic certification (₹15,000-30,000) from NPOP-accredited bodies like AFSSAI or ONYX.
5
First Sale
Launch on Blinkit, BigBasket, Amazon Fresh with minimum order 500 units (20kg boxes at ₹400-500/kg). Simultaneously pitch to organic retailers (Naturals, Health+) with local B2B demos in Ranchi. Run Instagram + Facebook ads targeting 'organic millets' with 5-10% discount launch offer. Contact health influencers for seeding.
6
Scale
After 6 months, launch 3-5 value-added SKUs (ragi malt, instant ragi mix, ragi cookies). Secure B2B contracts with 2-3 health food brands for private labeling. Apply for SFURTI subsidy (₹10-15 lakhs) to scale milling capacity. Establish direct export through food aggregators or B2B export marketplaces (GlobalTrade, Indo Global).
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Pradhan Mantri Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L loan at 0% interest for first 5 years for women/SC/ST entrepreneurs in food processing.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹10-15L subsidy for millet processing clusters in Gumla.
🏚
Jharkhand Organic Mission
50% subsidy on organic certification costs and ₹2,000/hectare for organic farming promotion.
🏚
Jharkhand ODOP Scheme
marketing support, brand registration grants, and trade fair participation subsidies for identified ODOP products.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
