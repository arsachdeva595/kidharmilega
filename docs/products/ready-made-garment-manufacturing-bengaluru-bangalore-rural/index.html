Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bengaluru (Bangalore) Rural ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Doddaballapur textile market on a Tuesday or Thursday, walk through active garment units (ask locals for directions), call 3-4 manufacturers cold and ask about their daily output, raw material costs, and export experience. Simultaneously register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) with your basic details. Zero cost, doable in 4 days.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source cotton and polyester blends from suppliers in Bengaluru textile market (Gandhinagar area) or Tiruppur, Tamil Nadu. First batch of 500 units requires ₹2.5-4L for fabric, buttons, zippers, thread. Identify 2-3 reliable trim suppliers within Bengaluru rural itself — Hoskote and Whitefield have emerging supplier networks. Get 3 price quotes, negotiate payment terms (30-day typical for ₹3L+ orders).
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 2,500-3,500 sq ft unit in Doddaballapur MSME cluster or Hoskote industrial area at ₹80-120 per sq ft annually (₹2-4L annually). Hire experienced production head (₹25-35K/month) and 8-12 skilled tailors (₹12-18K each). Buy basic machinery: 4-6 industrial sewing machines (₹40K each), 1 pressing unit (₹80K), 1 cutting table with tools (₹50K). Total capex ₹8-12L. Alternatively, partner with 3-4 artisans in Doddaballapur working from home units — lower risk, slower growth.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online 15 mins). Apply for IEC code from Directorate General of Foreign Trade (dgft.gov.in) — ₹500-1,000, 5-7 days. File GST registration (free) — mandatory for >₹20L turnover. GI registration not applicable but not required for export. Cost: ₹2,000 total, timeline 2-3 weeks. Get trademark for brand name (₹4,500, 6 months) from IPO if planning D2C push.
5
First Sale
First sale: List 20-30 SKUs on Flipkart Seller Central (free registration, 1-2% commission) starting with best-sellers (t-shirts, trousers, basic shirts). First order realistically 50-100 units at ₹300-400 wholesale margin per piece = ₹15K-40K first month. Simultaneously pitch to local department stores (get introductions through local merchant associations). Expect first genuine ₹1L+ order within 4-6 weeks if quality is consistent.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue, hire dedicated export/wholesale sales person (₹20K/month), add 4-6 more tailors, upgrade to computerized embroidery/pattern-cutting if designs demand it (₹3-5L). Exhibit at ITME Society shows (Chennai, annual), IGATEX (Mumbai) to connect bulk buyers. Build WhatsApp catalog system for repeat wholesale orders. Partner with 1-2 freight forwarders for consistent export shipments (negotiate better rates at volume). Move to 5,000 sq ft unit by month 8-10 to hit ₹20L/month.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (25% for SC/ST/women, 15% others) on machinery and working capital for MSMEs in textile manufacturing, apply through District Industries Center
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹25L grant for clusters, common facility centers, and artisan groups in textile sector, managed by Ministry of Textiles
🏚
Karnataka Textile Policy 2021
50% capital subsidy up to ₹50L for new garment manufacturing units, processing through Karnataka State Textile Development Corporation
🏚
Karnataka MSME Cluster Development Scheme
₹3-10L grants for cluster infrastructure (cutting centers, design labs, testing facilities) if you partner with 10+ garment units
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
