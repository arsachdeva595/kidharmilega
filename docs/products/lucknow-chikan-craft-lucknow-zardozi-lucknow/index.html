Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Lucknow ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Aminabad Chikankari cluster (15 min from Lucknow city center) on a Tuesday morning. Walk Gajrulawli Lane, observe 3-4 open-front artisan workshops, talk to at least 2 workshop owners about their production cost and timeline (free, 2 hours). Call Lucknow Small Industries Development Corporation (LSIDC) helpline to request free ODOP information packet.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from existing artisan workshops in Gajrulawli or Aminabad Lane. First batch: 10 pieces (mix of sarees/kurtis) costs ₹40,000-80,000 with 20-30 day lead time. Negotiate directly with workshop owners for better rates on repeat orders. Supplier base in Aliganj sells zari and thread wholesale.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent a 300-400 sqft workspace in Aminabad industrial area (₹8,000-15,000/month), hire 2-3 trained artisans, invest ₹3-5L in initial inventory. Option B: Partner with 2-3 existing artisan units on commission basis (15-20% commission, zero space cost). Both models work; commission is lower risk entry.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, 15 min, free). Apply for GI certification support via ODOP scheme (₹0-50,000 govt support). If exporting, apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) via DGFT portal (₹0, 7 days). Register trademark with IP office (₹5,000-10,000, 6 months). Total: ₹5,000-60,000, 2-3 months.
5
First Sale
First sale: post 5-10 product photos on Instagram with geo-tag #LucknowChikan. Target bridal wear groups on Facebook (5-10 groups, post twice weekly). Aim for first order ₹25,000-50,000 within 6-8 weeks via DM. Expected first-month revenue: ₹30,000-80,000 (2-3 pieces). Ship via Delhivery/BlueDart (₹300-800 per saree).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire one full-time sales person (₹15,000/month) + one QC/packing person (₹12,000/month). Exhibit at 2-3 weddings/festive expos annually (₹30,000-100,000 per stall). Launch WhatsApp Business API for customer management. Explore Amazon Handmade and Flipkart Saree categories. Build email list, send monthly lookbook to 2,000+ customers.

₹1.5 lakh to ₹5 lakh depending on model. Agar artisan partnership lo toh ₹1.5-2.5L se shuru kar sakte ho (inventory + registration). Agar apna workshop khologe toh ₹4-5L lagega (rent, machines, initial stock). Loan le sakte ho PM Vishwakarma se ₹3 lakh tak collateral-free.
Embroidery ka direct experience zaroori nahi, lekin textile business samajhna padega. 2-3 mahine cluster mein ghum-ghum ke artisans se seekh lo, kya defects hote hain, kya quality markers hain. Ek achche supervisor/quality check person ko hire kar lo jo already Chikankari janta ho.
ODOP scheme: ₹50 hazar tak GI certification aur export promotion mein. UP state scheme: ₹2-5 lakh new unit setup par. PM Vishwakarma: ₹3 lakh loan at 5% interest. Udyam registration aur IEC export ke liye free hain. ITC ki market access scheme bhi available hai.
Instagram par post karo high-quality photos, wedding groups mein join karo (Facebook). Wedding expo mein stall lagao — Lucknow mein har mahine 5-10 bridal shows hote hain. Direct B2B: wedding planners ko call karo, bulk order de sakte hain ₹50-100k. Etsy/Amazon Handmade par upload karo, 4-6 hafton mein pehla order aa jayega.
Bilkul. Gulf countries (UAE, Saudi Arabia) ko ₹100 crore ka saman jaata hai har saal. Europe ko ₹70 crore. IEC le lo, GST registration zaroor karo, freight forwarder dhundho (Lucknow mein alag hain). Pehla shipment ₹2-5 lakh ka ho sakta hai, 30-40 days lead time. Margin 45-55% FOB mein milta hai.
Risk: Chikankari ka demand toh achha hai, par export market abhi disrupted hai (shipments band hue hain, ₹2 crore goods unsold pade hain). Domestic market zyada stable hai — wedding season mein demand sure hai, lekin off-season mein bechna mushkil hota hai. Exit: inventory sell kar sakte ho discount deke ya artisans ko commission-based kaam de kar losses handle kar sakte ho. Total downside: initial inventory loss aur 2-3 mahine runway kharch.
