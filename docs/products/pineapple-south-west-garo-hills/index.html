Pineapple ki
opportunity, South West Garo Hills mein.

South West Garo Hills district spans 1662 sq km of hilly terrain with elevation 300-1000m, creating natural pineapple-growing microclimate with monsoon rainfall supporting year-round cultivation. Ampati is the administrative hub where District Horticulture Officer Smt. D. Nongkhlaw coordinates farming support. Primary production clusters exist in Ampati, Dadengre, and Betasing tehsils, supplying regional markets in Shillong (100 km), Guwahati (200 km), and Bangladesh border towns, with wholesale movement through Guwahati fruit market and Shillong produce mandis.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — South West Garo Hills ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Ampati haat on market day (Wednesday/Saturday), meet 3-4 pineapple farmers, ask yield, costs, and buyer networks. Call District Horticulture Officer Smt. D. Nongkhlaw at Ampati office (toll-free: 1800-114-558) for free soil testing and variety recommendations. Zero cost, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source quality suckers/seedlings from Meghalaya Horticulture Mission nurseries in Ampati or Shillong (₹8-12 per plant). Purchase in batches of 500-1000 plants (₹5,000-10,000). Organic compost from local sources or ICAR-recommended vermicompost suppliers in nearby Assam (₹300-400/bag).
3
Produce / Partner
Lease 0.5-1 hectare hilly land in Ampati/Dadengre area (₹1-2L per year) or partner with existing farmers on revenue-share. Establish beds, irrigation (drip system ₹20-30K for 0.5 ha). Hire 2-3 farm hands seasonally (₹200-300/day). First harvest at 18-20 months, annual maintenance ₹50-80K per hectare.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online on udyamregistration.gov.in, 15 min). Open bank account for subsidy claims. If processing: apply for PMFME scheme subsidy (up to 35% of equipment cost, max ₹10L) via District MSME or Block office. Export: apply for IEC code at nearest DGFT office in Shillong (₹500, 2-3 weeks). GI registration not required for ODOP pineapple currently.
5
First Sale
First sale: contact Guwahati Fruit Market wholesale buyers (minimum order 100 kg, ₹25-35/kg wholesale). List on JioMart/Blinkit with Shillong address (30-40/unit retail). Attend Ampati weekly haat to sell 20-30 kg direct to consumers (₹60-80/kg). Expected first-month revenue ₹15-25K from mixed channels.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 2-3 permanent farm staff, invest in cold storage unit (₹80-150K), tie-up with 1-2 institutional buyers (hotels, food processors). Apply for PMFME subsidy to add processing line (juice, chips). Attend Agri Startup India expo in Delhi (April) and North East regional horticulture shows to meet bulk buyers. Expand land to 2-3 ha by year 2-3.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

up to 35% subsidy on equipment + 10% for working capital, max ₹10L per unit
🏛
Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)
₹6000/year direct cash transfer to farmer account (if landholding ≤2 ha)

Meghalaya State Agricultural Department Horticulture Subsidy
50% subsidy on drip irrigation systems for hill cultivation

Meghalaya MSME Development Fund
concessional loans up to ₹20L at 7% interest for agricultural processing units

Farming experience madad dega but pineapple ki cultivation asaan hai — 18-20 mahine mein first harvest. District Horticulture Officer Smt. D. Nongkhlaw free training deti hai. Local farmers se directly sikh sakte ho Ampati mein.
PMFME Scheme (processing mein 35% subsidy), PM-KISAN (₹6000 saal mein), Meghalaya horticulture subsidy irrigation par (50%), aur cheap loans ₹20L tak State MSME fund se. Toll-free helpline 1800-114-558 pe call kar sakte ho information ke liye.
Guwahati Fruit Market mein 100+ wholesale buyers hain — directly jaao aur bolo. Ampati haat par har hafta retail customers aate hain. JioMart/Blinkit pe list kar ke online orders pakdo. Hotels aur restaurants Shillong/Guwahati mein B2B supply de sakte ho.
Haan, Bangladesh aur Nepal ko ja sakta hai — Dawki/Shella border checkpoints nearby hain. IEC code lelo Shillong DGFT se (₹500), aur Kolkata exporters ke saath tie-up kar. Greenhouse grown pineapple quality zyada demand hai outside India. Returns better hote hain but logistics and certification zra mehnga.
Pineapple cultivation risk low hai — demand stable hai Assam/Meghalaya/Bangladesh mein. Worst case, wholesale price fall mein ₹20-25 per kg milta hai (cost recovery). Can pivot to processing (juice, chips) agar fresh sale flop ho. Land ka alternative use — other horticulture crops ya dairy. But 3 saal mein orchards establish ho jaate hain, so initial loss recovery difficult.
