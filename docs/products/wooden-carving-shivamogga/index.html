Buyers span South India (Bangalore 250 km, Chennai 500 km) and international markets via ports at Mangaluru (200 km).

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Shivamogga ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Shivamogga city's furniture cluster (Kote Basti, Dhananjaya Nagar areas) — call 5-6 established carvers like Sai Furniture Art (reference from DC Handicraft records), ask about raw material costs, monthly production, and buyer channels. Attend weekly Saturday haat at Shivamogga vegetable market where some artisans network. Register free Udyam account online. Zero cost, doable within 3 days.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source seasoned timber from timber merchants in Thirthahalli (30 km from city) or Shivamogga Forest Development Corporation depot — typical cost ₹50-80 per cubic foot. First batch for 1 medium sofa set costs ₹8,000-₹12,000 in raw material. Supplier area: Thirthahalli timber market, established 60+ dealers.
3
Produce / Partner
Two paths: (a) rent 1,000-1,500 sq ft workshop in Kote Basti/Dhananjaya Nagar (₹8,000-₹15,000/month) + buy hand-carving tools, chisels, sanders (₹40,000-₹60,000) + hire 2-3 artisans at ₹12,000-₹18,000/month each = ₹2,50,000-₹4,50,000 startup. (b) Partner with 3-4 existing artisans as subcontractors (zero rent, they carve from home) = ₹60,000-₹1,50,000 startup. Path (b) is founder-friendly for first 6 months.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online in 15 minutes). Apply for Pradhan Mantri Micro Units Development & Refinement Scheme (PMEGP) subsidy ₹5-10L (20% subsidy on capital, 3% interest subvention) via nearest KVIC office in Shivamogga. Get GST registration (₹0, online). If exporting, apply for IEC code via DGFT portal (₹0). Timeline: 2 weeks Udyam + 4 weeks PMEGP approval + 1 week IEC.
5
First Sale
List 3-5 carved sofa designs on Amazon Handmade with ₹45,000-₹75,000 pricing. First order typically ₹1-2 sofa sets (~₹50,000-₹1,20,000 gross). Parallel: Email 30 interior designers in Bangalore/Hyderabad with portfolio + pricing. Expect first month ₹1.5-₹3L revenue if you get 2-3 orders. Use WhatsApp Business + Instagram reels showing carving process.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire a full-time sales person (₹25,000/month) + expand workshop to 2,000 sq ft (₹25,000/month) + recruit 6-8 artisans + apply for SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries) cluster development grant (₹up to ₹40L over 5 years). Exhibit at Delhi Furniture Show (Oct), Mumbai Trade Fair (Feb), and Bangalore Design Week (annual) to access B2B buyers. Build export network via Bangalore-based freight forwarders (CIF cost +15-20%).
