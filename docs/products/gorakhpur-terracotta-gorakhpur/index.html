Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Gorakhpur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Aurangabad village in Bhathat block, Gorakhpur (free; 25 km from Gorakhpur city). Meet 3-5 terracotta artisans in their workshops. Ask about clay sourcing from local ponds, kiln capacity, time per piece, and current buyer channels. Call Gorakhpur ODOP nodal officer (available on district website) to ask about artisan partnerships and subsidy eligibility. Attend Gorakhpur Haats (weekly markets) to observe retail price points. Zero cost, all doable within 1 week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source clay directly from Aurangabad village artisans or from local suppliers near Kabis ponds in Bhathat. First batch: order 100 kg of raw clay @ ₹300-500/bag (₹3-5K total). Visit local potters' associations in Aurangabad to negotiate bulk rates. Supplier contact: Aurangabad village terracotta clusters—ask local block office for list. Transport: hire local cart, ₹500-1000 per trip.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (Asset-light): Partner with 2-3 existing artisans in Aurangabad on commission (20-25% of wholesale price you earn). No factory cost. Option B (Own production): Rent small workshop space in Aurangabad (₹5-10K/month, 300 sqft). Buy or lease a small kiln (₹80-150K one-time or ₹2K/month rental). Hire 1-2 experienced artisans @ ₹8-12K/month each. Monthly production cost: ₹15-25K for 200-300 pieces. Time to first saleable batch: 4-6 weeks.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) online — free, 15 min. Apply for GI certification support via ODOP scheme (₹5-10K subsidy, 4-6 month process) through district handicraft office. If exporting: apply for IEC (Importer Exporter Code) via GemsandJewellery portal (free, 10 days). Register trademark for brand name (₹9-15K, 6-9 months). Get ISO 9001 if targeting bulk export (₹30-50K).
5
First Sale
First sale: list 50 pieces on Etsy (₹1500 setup + listing fees). Post 15-20 hero product photos on Instagram. Join Amazon Handmade India (non-gated category for handicrafts—₹15% commission). Reach out to 10 gift export companies in Delhi via email with product catalog and 10-piece sample order (₹5K cost). Target first month: 5-8 orders, ₹25-40K revenue from D2C + 1 wholesale enquiry.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 dedicated photographer + content creator (₹12K/month). Add 2-3 more trained artisans (₹18-24K/month payroll). Apply for PMEGP subsidy to fund brand advertising (up to ₹5L grant). Exhibit at Delhi Craft Bazaar, Jaipur Furniture & Handicraft Fair, Kolkata International Handicraft Fair (₹30-50K booth cost). Set up corporate gifting vertical by contacting 50+ HR procurement teams. Build WhatsApp broadcast list (500+ past customers) for new launches.
