Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bhilwara ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bhilwara's textile clusters in Mega Denim Park and Sangaria industrial area; spend 1 week understanding mill operations, dyeing processes, and embroidery techniques; connect with Bhilwara Textile Association and attend IITF or Intertextile Delhi showcases
2
Source Raw Materials
Source denim fabric from established mills in Bhilwara (Jindal Poly Films, Vardhman Mills) at ₹120-200/meter; procure embroidery threads and beads from Jaipur suppliers (estimated ₹50K for 500kg initial batch); negotiate bulk rates for first 1000 units targeting ₹25-35 per garment production cost
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 established tailoring units in Bhilwara city employing 20-50 workers each; alternatively, lease 1500-2000 sqft space in Sangaria industrial area for ₹8-12K/month and hire 15-20 skilled stitchers; ensure quality control through weekly inspections and sampling
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Ministry of MSME) for GST benefits; apply for IEC code from DGFT (Rajasthan office) within 2 weeks; file trademark with IP office; set up basic brand identity (logo, packaging design) via Fiverr or local design studios costing ₹15-25K
5
First Sale
Launch on Flipkart and Amazon as 3P seller with 100-150 SKUs by week 8; simultaneously approach 50-75 multi-brand retail outlets (Westside, Lifestyle) with samples; attend IITF or regional textile expos; start Instagram-focused D2C campaign targeting 5K followers in 3 months with influencer collaborations
6
Scale
After ₹50L+ revenue, establish exclusive retail counters in 5-7 metro cities; expand product line (jackets, shirts, accessories); hire dedicated export manager to pursue EU and US wholesale accounts; invest in automated embroidery units (₹50-80L) to reduce production costs by 15-20%
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy for MSMEs in textiles with job creation mandate
🏛
Production Linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme for Textiles
4-6% incentive on incremental sales for apparel manufacturers with ₹100Cr+ turnover potential
🏚
Rajasthan Textile Policy 2019-2029
5-10% subsidy on capital investment and 5% interest subvention on working capital loans
🏚
Rajasthan MSME Promotion Scheme
₹50K-100K grant for brand registration and ₹25K subsidy for design development and certification
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
