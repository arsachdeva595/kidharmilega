Pashmina ki
opportunity, Leh mein.

Leh's high-altitude geography (11,000+ ft) provides the perfect climate for raising Changthangi goats whose wool produces the world's finest Pashmina. The district has a 500+ year tradition of Pashmina weaving with artisans skilled in hand-spinning and hand-looming techniques. The region's cultural heritage and government support through GI certification make it the epicenter of authentic Pashmina production.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Leh ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Leh's Pashmina clusters in Khalsa and Changspa Road; meet master weavers at Leh Handloom Society; study GI certificate requirements and export norms; attend ICCW (Indian Chamber of Commerce & Welfare) workshops; visit Ladakh Craft Centre showroom
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw Changthangi wool directly from Leh Wool Corporation or herder cooperatives around Leh (₹8,000-12,000/kg for raw wool); negotiate with dyers near Leh city center; establish relationships with silk thread suppliers in Chandigarh or Delhi; first batch: ₹200,000-400,000 for 50-100 shawls
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3-5 skilled artisans/weaver families registered with DC Handloom Leh; provide wool + dye supplies on consignment; set quality standards aligned with GI specifications; consider co-working with Leh Handloom Cooperative Society for economies of scale
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME online (msme.udyamregistration.gov.in); apply for IEC code via DGFT portal; certify GI compliance with DC Handloom Leh; file trademark via IPO Delhi; obtain ISO 9001 certification for quality; register brand with Ladakh Chamber of Commerce
5
First Sale
Launch D2C website with high-resolution imagery and heritage storytelling; list on Luxepolis, Nykaa Luxe, and Ajio Premium; pitch to luxury boutiques in Delhi (Mehboob Studio, Delhi boutiques), Mumbai (Colaba, Bandra), and Bangalore; engage lifestyle influencers (50K-500K followers) for reels; launch WhatsApp Business for direct inquiries
6
Scale
After 6 months: expand artisan network to 15-20 weavers; launch seasonal collections tied to festivals; establish pop-up stores in metros during wedding season; develop B2B corporate gifting program with minimum orders of 50-100 units; apply for export incentives (RODTEP, RCMC); partner with luxury travel agencies for tourist sales
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma
up to ₹15,000 as advance for raw materials, interest-free loans up to ₹3 Lakh, free upskilling for artisans
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 Lakh subsidy for handloom enterprises, 25% subsidy for general category in Leh
🏚
Ladakh Handicrafts Development Scheme
50% subsidy on looms, raw material support, and export incentives for GI-certified products
🏚
Skill Development Program by UT Ladakh
free training for weavers and designers, certified courses in Pashmina production and finishing
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
