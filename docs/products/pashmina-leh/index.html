The district has approximately 800-1200 active artisans concentrated in Leh Main Market and surrounding villages, with raw pashmina sourced locally from herding communities. Suitable For

First-gen founder with ₹5-15L savings, appreciation for craft, and 12-18 month runway. Ideal for someone who wants to build a D2C brand or export business. Requires hands-on involvement in quality control and customer relationships.

Not for: Founders seeking quick turnover (<6 months), those uncomfortable with high raw material costs (₹8-12K per raw shawl), or those lacking patience for craftsmanship-focused production. Skip if you need volume manufacturing over quality.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Leh ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Leh Main Market (Main Bazaar) on any weekday and speak with 5-6 existing pashmina retailers — ask about supplier names, current pricing, customer queries, and pain points. Call the DC Handloom office in Leh at the published number to request introduction to 2-3 artisan cooperatives. Register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in). Zero cost, 4-5 hours total.
2
Source Raw Materials
Contact pashmina suppliers in Leh Main Market area or Changspa Road (artisan concentration zone). First batch cost: ₹2-4L for 30-50 raw pashmina shawls unfinished. Negotiate with 2-3 suppliers for best quality-to-price ratio. Raw material sourcing takes 2-3 weeks lead time.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent a 500 sq ft unit in Leh Main Market cluster (₹8-12K/month). Hire 2-3 skilled artisans on contract (₹15-20K/month each) or Partner directly with artisans in villages (commission-based 25-35% of finished product value). Total setup: ₹3.5-5L including unit deposit, basic tools, and 2-month raw material stock.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (₹0, 10 minutes online). File GI claim documentation through DC Handloom office (₹5-10K admin cost, 4-6 weeks). Apply for IEC code if exporting (₹1,000, via Customs or trade portal). Register trademark for brand name (₹5,000-₹8,000, 45-60 days). Total regulatory cost: ₹11-23K, 2-3 months total timeline.
5
First Sale
First sale target: Etsy or Instagram Shop in week 4-6. List 10-15 products with professional photography (₹5-8K for shoot). First order likely ₹8-15K D2C or ₹1-2L B2B wholesale. Expected first month revenue: ₹1-3L if mixing D2C + wholesale. Realistic target: 8-12 D2C orders + 1 corporate order by month 2.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire a full-time Operations Manager (₹25-30K), a Sales/Marketing person (₹20-25K), and 1-2 additional artisans. Establish direct relationships with 3-5 international buyers via trade shows (Heimtex, Premiere Vision). Open a second unit for finishing/packaging (₹5L capex). Apply for PMEGP subsidy for expansion (30-35% subsidy up to ₹10L).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
₹15,000 tool kit assistance and ₹3 lakh collateral-free loan at 0-5% interest for traditional artisans, plus ₹500/month stipend for 18 months
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
35% subsidy up to ₹10 lakhs for manufacturing, 25% for service sector, available to SC/ST/women with higher subsidies
🏚
Ladakh Tourism and Trade Promotion Scheme
25% subsidy on export promotion activities, trade show participation up to ₹5L per annum
🏚
Jammu & Ladakh Handicraft Development Scheme
₹2L interest-free term loan for artisans and manufacturers, ₹1L grant for GI-tagged product marketing
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
