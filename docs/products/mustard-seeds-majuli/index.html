Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Majuli ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call Majuli Agricultural Produce Marketing Committee office in Garamur — ask them to connect you with 3 active mustard seed suppliers this week. Visit 2-3 seed merchants at Majuli's weekly haats (Wednesday/Saturday). Register free on Udyam portal using Aadhaar. Zero cost, 3-4 days of work.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from local Majuli farming co-operatives in Garamur and Kamalabari areas. First batch: 500 kg costs ₹25,000-30,000 (₹50-60/kg wholesale rate). Negotiate storage at supplier's warehouse for 2-3 weeks free to test cleaning and grading quality.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing seed cleaning and sorting units in Garamur (renting costs ₹2,000-3,500/day) rather than buying machinery initially. First batch processing: ₹8,000-12,000 for cleaning, grading, packaging in 500g pouches. Or hire 2-3 artisans on ₹150-200/day wages for hand-sorting and packing at your rented 200 sq ft space (₹3,000-4,500/month rent).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 15 minutes). Apply for ARUNDHATI GOLD SCHEME for subsidy on processing equipment. File for GI registration support through District Agriculture Department (₹25,000-40,000 legal costs, 8-12 month process). If exporting: get IEC code from DGFT (free online), register with APEDA. Total regulatory cost: ₹50,000-80,000, timeline 4-6 months.
5
First Sale
List on Flipkart Seller Central (500g pack at ₹220-280) and Amazon (KYC required, 2% referral fee). First order target: 50 packs via organic search + local Facebook group promotion. Expected first-month revenue: ₹8,000-15,000 at 40-50% margins. Alternatively, contact 5 regional spice distributors (Kolkata, Guwahati, Shillong) with sample — negotiate ₹40,000 first order.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 logistics coordinator, rent 500 sq ft storage in Garamur, partner with cold chain distributor for North-East network. Apply for MLAADS and MPLADS funds for processing unit expansion. Exhibit at Assam Agrotech Expo (annual), India Food Forum. Launch regional e-commerce partnerships (local Assam platforms). Target: ₹30-40L annual revenue by month 18.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
up to ₹15,000 subsidy for individual agricultural processors, interest-free loans up to ₹2L
🏛
MUDRA Shishu Loan Scheme
unsecured loan up to ₹50,000 at 0% collateral, 8-10% interest rate
🏚
ARUNDHATI GOLD SCHEME
subsidy on food processing equipment for registered micro-enterprises (20-50% subsidy, max ₹5L)
🏚
MLAADS (MLA Area Development Scheme) and MPLADS
local MP can allocate ₹1-2L annually for infrastructure in your manufacturing cluster
