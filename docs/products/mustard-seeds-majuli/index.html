Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Majuli ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Majuli Agricultural Produce Market in Garamur and Majuli Agricultural Department office to understand yield data, pricing trends, and farmer networks. Meet 5-10 established mustard farmers near Kamalabari and Dakhin Pathar. Connect with spice merchants at Assam State Agricultural Marketing Board (ASAMB) in Guwahati to understand buyer requirements and quality standards.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source seeds from local Majuli farmers or certified seed producers in Assam (approximately ₹60,000-80,000 for 10 quintals first batch). Establish relationships with 3-4 reliable farmers in Kamalabari block. Budget additional ₹15,000-20,000 for basic storage, cleaning, and packaging equipment. Negotiate contract rates directly with farmers during harvest season (October-November).
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 8-12 smallholder farmers in Majuli through contract farming agreements (20-30 kg per farmer minimum). Invest in basic processing — cleaning machines (₹100,000-150,000), grading equipment, and storage containers. Alternatively, tie up with existing farmer producer organizations (FPOs) in Majuli for aggregation and quality control. Start with 5-10 quintals per batch.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam via udyamregistration.gov.in (free, 10 minutes). Obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Guwahati for export capabilities. Get FSSAI license (₹5,000-10,000) for food business. Trademark your brand name via ipindia.gov.in (₹9,000-15,000). Register as Agri Producer Organization if scaling to 50+ farmers.
5
First Sale
Launch on Bijnor Mustard cluster wholesale portals and Assam Agricultural Marketing Board's online portal. Create seller accounts on BigBasket B2B, IndiaStack (B2B agriculture), and Krishify. Contact 20-30 spice wholesalers in Darbhanga (Bihar) and Bijnor (UP) mustard hubs directly. Launch D2C via Shopify + Instagram + WhatsApp catalog. Attend Indiamart and Global Sources food expos in 2-3 months.
6
Scale
After 100+ quintals sold, invest ₹3-5L in semi-automated processing plant for oil extraction and paste production. Expand farmer partnerships to 30-50 across Majuli. Build relationships with 5-10 export agents for Bangladesh, Nepal, Myanmar markets. Launch premium organic variant (₹200-250/kg) to target health-conscious D2C buyers. Develop bulk supply contracts with Haldiram's, MDH spice companies, and Ayurvedic manufacturers.

₹2-5 lakh support for value-added agricultural products, crop diversification in Majuli

₹5-10 lakh grant for establishing farmer collectives for bulk production and marketing
