Tamenglong district at 1,290 m elevation with 40+ percent land under orange cultivation produces Citrus reticulata with 10-14 segments, 233g average weight, and 45.75% juice content (TSS 11.93 °Bx) — making it premium-grade fruit. Raw materials (micro-irrigation, shade nets, mulch film) are sourced from Imphal suppliers 50-70 km away with farmer collectives providing direct supply networks. Harvest November-February drives seasonal demand peak; buyer markets include Delhi-NCR, Bangalore, Singapore, Bangkok, and Dhaka within 1,500 km reach.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Tamenglong ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call 3 farmer groups in Tamenglong town (contact via Tamenglong Horticulture Department, free); visit Imphal main orange market (Modern bazaar) to track pricing and buyer types; attend next Tamenglong Orange Festival or check www.tamenglong.nic.in for farming groups — zero cost, doable in 1 week
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fresh fruit directly from farmer collectives or lease 1-3 acre existing orchard in Tamenglong taluk (₹40K-₹80K/year lease). First batch sourcing: 5-10 tonnes contact Tamenglong Horticulture Department or cooperative unions in district — ₹2-3L for 5 tonne trial batch at ₹40-45/kg farm rate.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (asset-light): Partner with 2-3 farmer collectives for aggregation, grading, packing (₹50-60/tonne processing fee). Option B (asset-heavy): Rent 500-800 sqft cold storage + packing unit in Tamenglong town (₹15K-20K/month) with ₹3-5L initial infra (coolers, grading table, packing line). Start with Option A (₹0 capex, revenue-share model), scale to B at ₹20L/month turnover.
4
Brand & Register
Month 1: Register Udyam online (free, 5 mins) at udyamregistration.gov.in. Month 2: Apply for GI usage certificate via Ministry of Agriculture (₹5K-15K fee, 30-45 days) at agricoop.nic.in. Month 3: If exporting, get IEC from DGFT (₹0 online application) for fruit export compliance. Get APEDA registration (₹0) for export certification.
5
First Sale
First sale: Approach Imphal-based Modern Trade buyers (Spencer's, Jio Mart) with 500 kg sample shipment. Simultaneously list on BigBasket/Blinkit vendor portal (free listing, 15% commission). First order likely 1-2 tonnes at ₹50-55/kg wholesale. Expected first-month revenue: ₹2.5-3.5L if securing 2-3 retail partners + 500kg D2C orders.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 1 supply chain coordinator (₹18K/month), invest ₹50-80L in dedicated cold chain + branded packaging, add 2-3 more export markets via APEDA-listed agents, attend food trade shows (Gulfood Dubai, Asian Fruit Logistica Singapore). Scale to ₹50L+/month by adding juice processing or dried orange chips (value-add = 60-70% margins).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
MIDH (Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture)
50% subsidy on micro-irrigation and shade net installation (₹3-5L per hectare capacity building, applicable if expanding cultivation)
🏛
PMKSY (Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana)
75-90% subsidy on drip/micro-irrigation systems for orchards, processing ₹2-8L per hectare
🏚
Manipur Organic Farming Mission
₹5,000-₹15,000 per hectare subsidy for organic certification and equipment (relevant since Tamenglong uses organic-only production)
🏚
Manipur Export Promotion Scheme
10-15% subsidy on export shipping, packaging, and market entry for GI-tagged products
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
