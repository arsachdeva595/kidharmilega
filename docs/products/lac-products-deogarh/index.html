Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Deogarh ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Deogarh's lac clusters in Mahadev Nagar and Kusundpur areas; attend Deogarh Haat artisan markets; connect with Deogarh Lac Cooperative Society; study products at cluster centers and interview 5-10 established makers about material sourcing, design trends, and pricing strategies.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source lac resin from established suppliers in Deogarh (₹800-1200/kg) and Ranchi; procure wooden bases from local carpenters (₹2000-5000 per 50 pcs); obtain natural dyes from suppliers in Kolkata (₹150-300/color). First batch of 500 units = ₹40000-60000 including packaging.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3-5 master artisans through local cooperative unions; establish a small production unit with basic tools (polishing wheels, molds, brushes) costing ₹150000; train a team of 2-3 artisans; establish quality control checkpoints for design consistency and finish quality.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam classification as micro/small manufacturing; apply for IEC code from nearest APEDA office in Ranchi; design a brand identity reflecting Deogarh heritage; register trademark with IP office; obtain Hallmark certification if applicable; apply for Artisan ID with Ministry of MSME.
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy and Amazon Handmade with high-quality product photography and heritage storytelling; create Instagram business account featuring artisan stories and behind-the-scenes production; reach out to 20 wedding planners in metro cities via LinkedIn; approach home décor retailers in Bangalore, Delhi, Mumbai with catalog; list on GiftingTree and Indian Heritage platforms.
6
Scale
After 6 months, onboard 2-3 additional artisan groups; expand product line to include seasonal collections; apply for SFURTI cluster development grant; establish B2B relationships with 5-10 international distributors; hire a digital marketing specialist; open pop-up stores in metro cities during peak seasons; launch a D2C subscription box for home décor enthusiasts.
