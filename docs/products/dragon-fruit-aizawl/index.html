Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Aizawl ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Long Yi Sheng Fruit Industry (53-hectare plantation in Aizawl) or local farmer cluster; call Aizawl Agriculture Horticulture Department (0389-2314546) for cultivar and climate data; attend weekly Aizawl District Farmers Market (Thursdays) to meet active growers. Zero cost, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified dragon fruit saplings (Red, Yellow, White flesh varieties) from regional nurseries in Aizawl or from Sikkim Agricultural Department-listed suppliers. First batch of 500 saplings: ₹25,000-35,000. Lead time: 2-3 weeks.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 2-5 hectares in Aizawl rural belt at ₹30,000-50,000/hectare/year; install drip irrigation (₹3-5L per hectare) and fencing (₹2-3L per hectare). Partner with local agronomists for first-year setup. Total initial land + infra: ₹8-15L for 2 hectares. Production kicks in month 18-24.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME (free, online, 1 week) at udyamregistration.gov.in. For export: apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) at dgft.gov.in, ₹0 cost, 3-5 days processing. Open Export Promotion Capital Goods (EPCG) account if scaling exports. GI registration for dragon fruit is not yet applicable but monitor Ministry ODOP updates.
5
First Sale
First sale: contact Aizawl-based export agents or directly pitch to Jio Mart B2B (jiomart.com/wholesale) with minimum first order of 2-5 tons. Expected first-month revenue: ₹2-4L (50-100 kg/day at wholesale rates). Use FedEx Cold Chain or regional logistics (Aizawl-Delhi 48-hour delivery).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 2-3 farm laborers (₹15K each/month), invest in cold storage unit (₹20-30L), onboard 1 sales/export coordinator. Exhibit at Aizawl Agricultural Fair (Jan-Feb), apply for SFURTI or PM Krishi Sinchayee Yojana for irrigation subsidy (30-50% cost). Target Vietnam/China exports via existing trade channels.

Seedhe sawaal, seedhe jawab

₹5-8 lakh minimum for 1-2 hectares (land, irrigation, saplings, initial 12-month expenses). ₹20-30 lakh for 5 hectares + cold storage. Can start smaller with farmer partnership on leased land.
Farming background helps but not mandatory — Aizawl district's 89% literacy + success cases like Long Yi Sheng prove that dedicated learners succeed. Mandatory: 6-month intensive training from local agronomists (free via government horticulture dept).
PM Krishi Sinchayee (50% irrigation subsidy), PMFME (₹10L processing subsidy), Mizoram Horticulture Mission (free training), and potential ₹2L subsidy under state schemes. Apply via Udyam registration (free).
Three routes: (1) Jio Mart B2B wholesale at jiomart.com/wholesale (first order ₹1-2L); (2) Local Aizawl hotels, restaurants, retail shops (walk-in sales, ₹50K/week); (3) Export agents already in Aizawl (connect via Chamber of Commerce).
Bilkul — Aizawl already exports to China and Vietnam. Apply for IEC (free) at DGFT. Main buyers: China (bulk, ₹60-75/kg FOB), Vietnam (premium, ₹75-90/kg FOB). Standard containers: 40-ton shipments. Certifications needed: organic (if claiming), APEDA registration.
Biggest risk: crop failure in first 2 years (disease, climate). Mitigation: partner with established grower initially (revenue-share model), not full land investment. Exit option: transition to pulp/juice processing (lower risk, ₹30-50L market in Northeast). Break-even is 18-24 months, so realistic runway critical.
