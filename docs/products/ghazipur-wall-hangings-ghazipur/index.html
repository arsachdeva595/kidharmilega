opportunity, Ghazipur mein.

Ghazipur district (2199 km², 2.74M population) straddles the Ganges with jute mills and cotton suppliers in nearby Mau and eastern UP districts. The five named clusters—Paharpur Kala Gazipur, Rasulpur, Kolwar, Dhuvarjun, Saidpur—are concentrated within a 30 km radius and employ handloom weavers trained in jute-cotton blending. Varanasi, 40 km away, hosts 3M+ annual tourists; Delhi NCR (400 km) and Mumbai (1100 km) are reachable via NH-119 and rail, enabling wholesale buyer access within 1–3 days.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Ghazipur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Rasulpur cluster on any Wednesday (haat day) or call Nisha Craft Vikas Samiti (+91 XXXXXXXXXX via Ghazipur DC office) to meet artisans Kaiser Jahan Ahamad or Mohammad Jane Alam. Attend a live weaving session at Paharpur Kala Gazipur. Register free Udyam account online. Zero cost, 1 week turnaround.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source jute from Mau Jute Mills (Mau district, 35 km away) at ₹25–35/kg; cotton from local suppliers in Ghazipur town; colored yarns from Surat textile wholesalers. First batch: 20 pieces = ₹35–50K raw materials. Negotiate artisan rates: ₹500–1200 per piece labor depending on complexity.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent 400 sq ft workspace in Rasulpur cluster (₹8–15K/month) and hire 2–3 artisans on piece-rate basis (no fixed salary, pay per unit). Option B: Form partnership with existing weaver group and order 50–100 pieces/month on consignment (20% upfront, 80% on delivery). Initial cost: ₹80–150K for space + 1-month raw material stock.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 30 min). Apply for GI-tagged label through Nisha Craft Vikas Samiti (₹5K, 2 months). File IEC (Importer Exporter Code) if exporting (₹0, online via DGFT portal, 5 days). Open FSSAI if gifting food items (not needed here). Total compliance cost: ₹5K, timeline 8 weeks.
5
First Sale
First sale: List on Etsy (free; 6.5% commission) and pitch 5 interior design studios in Varanasi within week 1. Target first order: 10 pieces to a design studio at ₹30K gross. Expected first-month revenue: ₹40–60K gross (2–3 local orders + 1–2 online orders).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 logistics coordinator, contract with 3–5 artisan groups (not 1–2), move to 800 sq ft dedicated unit, invest ₹2–3L in photography/website. Exhibit at Home India Expo (Delhi, Jan–Feb), Craft Mela (Lucknow), and Varanasi tourism trade shows. Scale to 150–200 pieces/month. Hire designer for 3–4 seasonal collections.
