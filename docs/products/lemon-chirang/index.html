Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Chirang ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Panbari village on a Friday (weekly haat day), speak directly with 3-5 Kazi Nemu farmers, call the Chirang District Agriculture Office (+91-3668-245XXX area code) to confirm current yields and buyer contact lists, register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) — zero cost, doable within 2 weeks.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source saplings from established Panbari farmers (₹20-30 per sapling, first order 100-500 plants = ₹2,000-15,000) or lease farmland in Panbari cluster at ₹3,000-5,000/bigha/year. Negotiate directly with farmer collectives in Panbari village; Chirang District Agriculture Office has certified grower lists.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Lease 2-3 bighas (1-1.5 acres) in Panbari cluster, hire 1-2 farm laborers at ₹6,000/month each, invest ₹100,000-150,000 in saplings and setup (irrigation, fencing, mulch). Option B: Partnership model — contract with existing 5-10 farmers to supply fresh Kazi Nemu at harvest, handle only aggregation and grading (₹50,000 initial investment). First harvest in 10-12 months.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 15 mins online). Apply for GI certification support via Pradhan Mantri Suksham Khadya Udhyog Unnayan Yojana (free registration, 2-3 months processing). Obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Guwahati (₹0, online application) if planning exports. State FSSAI food safety certification (₹2,000-5,000, 2 weeks if for farm produce only). Total ₹2,000-5,000, 4-6 weeks.
5
First Sale
First sale: Contact 2-3 Spitalfields-linked UK importers via email (sourced from Chirang export success stories) OR list on Freshworks, LocalCircles with ₹50-100/kg pricing. First institutional buyer likely 500-1,000 kg order. First month realistic revenue ₹25,000-50,000 from 500-1,000 kg sold at ₹50-100/kg.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (100-150 metric tons annually): hire 1 procurement manager to coordinate farmer network, 1 logistics coordinator for cold chain, 1 marketing person for D2C. Exhibit at Agrotech India (annual, Bangalore), India Food Forum (Mumbai). Acquire ISO 22000, organic certification (₹20,000-40,000) to unlock premium pricing. Expand to 10-15 farmer partnerships, invest ₹5-10L in temperature-controlled storage facility in Chirang.
