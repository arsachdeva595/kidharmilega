Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Chirang ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Chirang district lemon clusters around Jagdpur and Nongstoin blocks; connect with Chirang Horticulture Department and 20-30 established lemon farmer cooperatives; attend Assam Agri-Hort expo to understand pricing and quality benchmarks.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source saplings from Assam Agricultural University nursery (Jorhat) or certified nurseries in Chirang; procurement cost for 500 saplings with 5-year yield projection: ₹40,000-60,000; fertilizers and pesticides from local agro-dealers in Chirang town.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 5-10 contract farmers in Chirang through written agreements; establish collection center in Chirang town; invest in basic sorting, grading, and packaging unit (₹3-5L); or lease 2-3 hectares and hire local labor at ₹300-400/day.
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam (MSME) as agricultural business; apply for IEC code from Guwahati Custom House for export eligibility; obtain GI Certification compliance certificate from Assam Agricultural Department; register brand with IP office and get FSSAI approval for processed products.
5
First Sale
Initial sales: supply 5-10 tons monthly to Guwahati APMC market and Kolkata Central Market; list on BigBasket, Blinkit, and JioMart for D2C; approach juice manufacturers in Gujarat and Punjab with samples; launch WhatsApp bulk ordering channel targeting restaurants in Assam.
6
Scale
After 6 months, add value-added products (lemon squash, dehydrated lemon); build cold storage facility (₹15-20L); export 20-30% production via trade houses; expand farming network to 50+ contract farmers; establish brand presence in 5-10 Indian cities via modern retail and horeca channels.
