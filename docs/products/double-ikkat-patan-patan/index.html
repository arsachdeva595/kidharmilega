The Double Ikkat weaving cluster is concentrated in Patan town with approximately 40-50 active weaver families practicing the technique. Buyers span luxury Indian boutiques in Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, plus significant export demand from US, UK, and Japan markets where Indian heritage textiles command 50-70% premiums.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Patan ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Zero-cost: Visit Patan town weavers cluster (call 0900-2200 hrs, ask for 'Patola bunkar samaj'), attend Patan Haat (weekly village market), call DC Handloom office Patan for current weaver directory — do this before spending any money.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from established weaver families in Patan town center. Budget ₹1.8-2.2L per saree + ₹30-50K per dupatta. First batch: commission 2-3 pieces. Timeline: 3-6 months lead time. Supplier area: Patan (central town, walkable cluster) and Mehsana tehsil.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner model: Commission finished pieces from existing weavers rather than setting up loom rental. Cost: ₹1.8-2.2L per saree (materials + artisan labor). If you want in-house: rent 600-800 sq ft workspace in Patan town (₹5-8K/month), lease or buy 1-2 looms (₹80-120K each), hire 1 trained weaver + 1 assist (₹15K/month salary). Total in-house setup: ₹4-6L over 6 months.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 2 days). Apply for GI mark under existing Patan Patola registration (no additional cost, link via DC Handloom). If exporting: Apply for IEC code (₹0, Directorate of Foreign Trade, online). Quality certification: BIS/Handloom Mark (₹8-15K, 4 weeks). Total formal setup: ₹10-20K, timeline 4-6 weeks.
5
First Sale
Launch Instagram shop with 8-10 product photos of finished pieces. First sale channel: Instagram DMs + WhatsApp to metro boutique owners (personal outreach). Expected first order: 1 saree from boutique or 1 from direct customer. First-month revenue: ₹1.8-2.2L if 1 sale lands. Platform: Instagram + own WhatsApp group for repeat customers.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month (approx 5-6 sarees + dupattas): Hire 1 full-time Instagram/sales manager (₹20K/month). Start paid Instagram ads (₹1-2K/day budget). Exhibit at India International Trade Fair Delhi, Ahmedabad Textile Expo. Build wholesale relationships: 5-10 boutiques across metros. Commission 8-10 pieces/month instead of 2-3. Explore B2B museum/hotel gifting.
