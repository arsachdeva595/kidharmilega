opportunity, Lahaul & Spiti mein.

Lahaul & Spiti sits at 10,000-13,000 ft altitude where wild seabuckthorn thrives pesticide-free. Rangrik village cluster includes 70 women collectors earning ₹8,000/season per person from berry sales at ₹63/kg to Spiti Sea-buckthorn Society. The district's collections have grown nearly 30x in 20 years, with dried leaves fetching ₹300/kg; closest buyer markets are Kullu, Manali, and Delhi (4-6 hours by road via NH5).

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Lahaul & Spiti ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call Spiti Sea-buckthorn Society (contact via HP ODOP website); visit Rangrik village in August during berry collection to watch harvesting; attend Kullu haat or Manali health food market to observe competitor pricing. Zero cost, takes 1-2 weeks.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source berries directly from Rangrik village collectors via Spiti Sea-buckthorn Society: ₹63/kg in Aug-Sep; order minimum 50-100 kg first batch (₹3,150-6,300). Dried leaves from same collectors at ₹300/kg. Build direct relationship with 5-10 women collectors to secure volume.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a small 300-400 sq ft processing unit in Spiti Valley or Keylong (₹5,000-8,000/month) with basic equipment: juicer, oil extractor, drying rack, bottles, labels (₹80,000-120,000 one-time). OR partner with existing 1 unit in Spiti Valley to process on commission (10-15% of output). Production ratio: 10 kg berries = 6-7 litres concentrate.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 15 mins online); apply for ODOP scheme subsidy once registered; get FSSAI license (₹10,000-15,000, 2-3 weeks); if exporting, apply for IEC (₹1,000-2,000) and APEDA registration (free). Total compliance cost: ₹15,000-20,000.
5
First Sale
List on Amazon Fresh, Meesho, PatanjaliAyurveda e-commerce portal first (no listing fee, 30% commission). First order: target 20-50 units via influencer seeding to wellness accounts. Expected first-month revenue: ₹30,000-50,000 (50-70 units at ₹500-800 per bottle).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month, hire 2-3 processing staff (₹200-300/day) and 1 packaging+shipping person. Exhibit at India Organic Expo, Natural Products Expo (Feb-Mar in Delhi). Apply for PM Vishwakarma loan (₹1L at 0% for first ₹3L). Start cold outreach to 50+ Ayurvedic chains and health food stores. Partner with 2-3 wellness e-commerce platforms (HealthKart, CurevFit store).
