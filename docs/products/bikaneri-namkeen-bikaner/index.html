opportunity, Bikaner mein.

Bikaner district in northwest Rajasthan has 100+ years of namkeen production heritage, with raw materials (gram flour, peanuts, spices) sourced within 150km from local mills and border-region farms. The old city Kote Gate area and industrial zones around Bikaner City house 50+ registered namkeen units ranging from family workshops to mid-scale processors. Buyer markets span Delhi Metro (direct wholesale), Mumbai FMCG distribution, and international shipping via land routes to nearest ports (Mundra 450km, Jaipur airport 330km) and Air Cargo facilities.

✓ Verified Success Story
Bikanervala Foods

Founded in 1905 by Shri Lal Chand Agarwal as Bikaner Namkeen Bhandar in Kote Gate, old city. Moved to Delhi in 1950 and expanded to Chandni Chowk's Paranthe Wali Gali. Now a ₹1,300 crore company with presence in 100+ cities and exports to 7 countries. Shyam Sundar Aggarwal joined at age 16 in 1968 and scaled product line to 15+ SKUs by early 1970s.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bikaner ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit old Bikaner city Kote Gate area on a Tuesday and walk 3-4 existing namkeen shops (Agarwal Namkeen Bhandar, local producers). Call Bikaner Chamber of Commerce (+91-151-2544321) and ask for list of 5 gram flour mills. Register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) — takes 20 minutes. Zero cost, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source gram flour from local mills in Bikaner city (₹45-55/kg), peanuts from Sriganganagar wholesale market (₹80-100/kg), spice blends from local Bikaner masala suppliers (₹150-200/kg). First 100kg batch costs ₹8,000-12,000. Supplier area: Bikaner City industrial zone near Laxmi Nagar.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Partner with existing artisan in Bikaner (20-30 units making namkeen) — provide raw materials + ₹500-1000/batch for production. Option B: Rent 500-800 sq.ft. unit in Bikaner industrial area (₹5,000-8,000/month), buy one 10kg batch fryer + packaging equipment (₹80,000-150,000), hire 2-3 workers. Start with 20kg/day capacity. Total cost: ₹4-6L for own unit over 6 months.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 20 mins). Apply for FSSAI license via food.fssai.gov.in (₹2,000-5,000, 15 days). Since GI is already awarded to Bikaneri Namkeen, register your brand name with state IP cell (₹1,000, 30 days). If planning export: Get IEC from DGFT portal (free with UDYAM + PAN), apply for GI certified product label (Rajasthan State ODOP office covers this). Total: ₹5,000-8,000 and 60 days.
5
First Sale
First sale: Create listing on Amazon/Flipkart (takes 7 days), start with 50-100 units at ₹300/pack (500g). Launch via Instagram/WhatsApp to 50 local contacts (friends, family, office groups) — aim for 30-50 units first month. Parallel: Pitch 3-5 local grocery stores (100 units/month minimum order). Expected first-month revenue (blended): ₹25,000-40,000.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: Hire dedicated sales manager for wholesale channel, expand to 2-3 more cities (Delhi, Mumbai) via regional distributors, apply for PMEGP subsidy for expansion (₹50L loan, 35% subsidy cap). Set up co-packing with existing larger unit to handle 50kg/day without hiring staff. Add 2-3 new SKU variants (Masala Mix, Chana Dal, Aloo Bhujia). Exhibit at ITC NeGD Food Trade shows, apply for SFURTI cluster scheme for shared cold storage.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP
₹50L collateral-free loan with 35% subsidy (women 40%) for MSMEs, 7-10% annual interest, repay over 84 months
🏛
MUDRA
Shishu/Kishor/Tarun schemes up to ₹10L for working capital, near 0% subsidy but quick disbursal, 5-7% interest
🏚
Rajasthan ODOP Subsidy
eligible units get 20-30% subsidy on machinery, processing equipment, and cold chain infra; apply via district ODOP cell
🏚
Rajasthan State Food Processing Policy
10% CGST refund for first 3 years, accelerated land allotment in industrial zones, apply via Industries Department
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Frequently Asked Questions

Seedhe sawaal, seedhe jawab

₹3-5 lakh se shuru kar sakte ho — ₹80,000 machinery (fryer), ₹1 lakh packing, ₹50,000 raw material, ₹1.5 lakh rent 3 months, ₹50,000 licensing-FSSAI. Artisan partnership se sasta ho sakta hai (₹1.5-2 lakh). Delhi/Mumbai se poora business chalate hue, ₹8-10 lakh chahiye 6 mahine ke liye.
Bilkul nahi, lekin food safety aur quality ek samajhna zaroori hai. FSSAI ka basic training online kar lo (₹0, YouTube pe videos hain). Aur Bikaner ke existing makers se 1-2 mahine ka apprenticeship lo — wo sikhayenge gram flour quality, spice blend ratio, deep fry temperatures. Agar tu organized rehega, seekh jayega 2-3 mahine mein.
Central PMEGP se ₹50 lakh tak loan mill sakta hai 35% subsidy ke saath. Rajasthan ODOP scheme se 20-30% subsidy machinery pe. MUDRA se ₹10 lakh, interest sirf 5-7%. State aur central dono se apply kar sakte ho — kum se kum 1 to pass hona chahiye. Aur GI tag toh Bikaner ke namkeen ke paas pehle se hai, toh export bhi jaldi process ho jayega.
Flipkart-Amazon pe list karke 2-3 haftey mein 50-100 units bik jayenge. Lokaal — apne area ke 5-10 kirana stores mein rakh de, 10-20% margin de unhe. Whatsapp groups (neighborhood, office friends) me bhej — first 100 units itsey nikl jayenge. Wholesale ke liye Bikaner Chamber of Commerce se contact lo, unhe local grocers aur retailers ke names denge. First order 200-500 units, ₹25,000-40,000 revenue expected pehle mahine.
Bilkul. Bikaneri Namkeen ka GI tag pehle se USA, UK, Canada, UAE, Singapore jaisa countries mein demand hai. Lekin export karte samay FSSAI certificate, IEC number, aur 12-month shelf-life certification zaroori hoga. Packing bhi international standards mein hona chahiye. Export se margin 42-58% FOB hota hai — jo wholesale se zyada ho. 50-100kg se shuru kar, 6-8 weeks lagenge shipment mein pohunchne mein.
Honest answer — namkeen ek commodity hai, margins thin hain (28-38% wholesale). Agar quality drop hua ya inventory rote reh gaya, nuksaan ho sakta hai. Lekin risk mitigate karne ke liye: (1) first 2-3 mahine small batch banao, (2) artisan partnership karke own inventory risk kam karo, (3) wholesale + D2C mix rakh taki inventory fast move ho. Worst case, artisans ko khud supply de sakte ho — toh loss zyada nahi hoga. Market toh established hai, bas execution aur quality theek rakhna zaroori hai.
