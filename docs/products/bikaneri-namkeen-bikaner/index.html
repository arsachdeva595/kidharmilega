The namkeen cluster spans Old City, Ratangarh, and Jaipur Road with 500+ active production units, ranging from single-room operations to ₹50+ Cr companies. Buyers are spread across Delhi-NCR, Mumbai, Gulf countries, and increasingly online platforms, with established logistics networks to all major ports and distribution hubs.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bikaner ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Ratangarh namkeen cluster (5 km from Bikaner city) or Old City Bikaner market — walk through 20-30 production units, speak to 3-4 owners about raw material costs, production timeline, and buyer channels. Call BIFFL (Bikaner Food & Flavours Ltd) or state ODOP nodal officer. Register free Udyam account online. This costs zero and takes 3 days.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from Suryagarh Mandi (Bikaner main grain market), Ratangarh wholesale cluster, or Jaipur-based spice suppliers like Shayam Spice Trading. First batch cost ₹40-60K for 100 kg mixed namkeen production. Chickpea flour at ₹35-45/kg, peanuts ₹60-80/kg, spices ₹80-150/kg. Supplier areas: Ratangarh, Mandi Road, Jaipur Road.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 400-500 sq ft unit in Ratangarh MSME cluster or Old City (₹6,000-12,000/month) or partner with existing small unit for co-production (10-15% of output cost). Buy used fryer, mixing drums, sieving machine, packing station (₹2-4L) or hire at ₹1,500/day. Start with batch production of 30-50 kg daily, manually packed. Timeline: 2-3 weeks to operationalize.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 15 minutes online). Apply for GI-backed certification through Bikaner District Industries Centre (DIC) — free, 30-45 days. Get FSSAI license Category 3 (₹2,500, 7 days). Apply for IEC code with Customs if exporting (₹500, online). Get trademark registration for brand name (₹10-15K, 8-10 months). All registrations doable by Month 2.
5
First Sale
First sale: List on Amazon Seller Central or Flipkart with 500g packs. First order via wholesale — contact 10 local retailers in Bikaner, offer ₹2,000-3,000 trial orders. Join Blinkit, JioMart vendor network (3-5 days onboarding). Expected first month revenue ₹30-50K from 150-200 kg sales mix (wholesale 60%, D2C 40%).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 2-3 production staff (₹8,000/month each), 1 sales coordinator (₹10,000), 1 packaging specialist (₹8,000). Invest in semi-automatic equipment (₹5-8L more). Apply PMEGP subsidy for expansion. Participate in IITF Delhi, GiftExpo Mumbai, Rajasthan Vyapar Mela. Approach wholesalers in Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore. Explore exports via APEDA and trade fairs.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to 35% subsidy for capital equipment (max ₹10L subsidy) for manufacturing units employing 10+ people
🏛
MUDRA Loan
up to ₹10L unsecured loan from banks for food processing MSME; zero collateral, 7-8% interest rate
🏚
Rajasthan ODOP Scheme
subsidy on GI certification, trademark registration, export promotion, and FSSAI license cost (up to 50% reimbursement)
🏚
Rajasthan MSME Cluster Development Scheme
30% subsidy on machinery cost for units in notified clusters like Ratangarh
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
