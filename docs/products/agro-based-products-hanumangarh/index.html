Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Hanumangarh ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Hanumangarh's agricultural mandi in Sangarpur and Bhadra; connect with farmer cooperative groups like Hanumangarh District Cooperative Union; attend Indian Institute of Rice Research webinars on premium variety cultivation; benchmark with top exporters like Dunar Foods and Tirupati Export Group based in the district
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified seeds from ICAR research centers in Ludhiana (₹2-3L for 10 quintal batch); secure quality cotton from local farmer networks; first-batch raw material cost approximately ₹8-12 lakhs for 50 metric tons of rice production; establish direct relationships with 10-15 farmers through local agricultural societies
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing rice mills in Sangarpur industrial area (hourly rates ₹800-1200 per ton); contract with cotton ginning units in Hanumangarh town; alternatively obtain FSSAI license and start small-scale processing unit (₹20-30L capex); employ 8-12 skilled workers for quality control and packaging
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (micro/small enterprise classification); obtain IEC code from DGFT for export eligibility; acquire FSSAI license for packaged foods (₹10000-25000); apply for Agmark certification for quality assurance; register brand at Rajasthan State Industries Department; get ISO 22000 certification for food safety (₹50000-100000)
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Business and IndiaMART for B2B wholesale; activate Flipkart and BigBasket for D2C retail; partner with premium chains like Nature's Basket and DMart; attend Agrotech India and Expopack exhibitions; create WhatsApp Business profile with 50+ bulk buyers; approach exporters like Daawat, India Gate, and regional APEDA-registered exporters
6
Scale
Build direct-to-farmer relationships in 3-4 surrounding districts (Bikaner, Sri Ganganagar); introduce 2-3 new value-added products (organic rice, flavored rice blends); hire export manager by month 9-10; target 30-40% revenue from exports by year 2; establish own cold storage facility (₹50L capex); join Hanumangarh Agricultural Exporters Association for collective marketing
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh loan subsidy for agro-processing startups with 15-25% government subsidy component
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
grants up to ₹40 lakh for modernizing agro-clusters and collective processing units in Hanumangarh
🏚
Rajasthan Agricultural Export Policy 2022
5-10% subsidy on export value for APEDA-registered agro exporters from Hanumangarh
🏚
Rajasthan Kisan Kalyan Yojana
₹2000-5000 per farmer for adopting certified seeds and modern techniques; creates supply-chain partnerships with agro-entrepreneurs
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
