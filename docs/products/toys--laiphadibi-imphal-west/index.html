Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Imphal West ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Imphal West artisan clusters in Laiphadighat and Singjamei areas; connect with existing Laiphadibi makers; study toy designs at Manipur State Museum; analyze competitor pricing on Etsy and global craft platforms
2
Source Raw Materials
Source dyed cotton and jute from local Imphal markets (Ghari Bazaar); procure natural dyes from herbal suppliers in Ukhrul; initial batch (100 toys) costs ₹15,000-25,000 through established artisan networks
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3-5 skilled artisans in Imphal West through local craft associations; establish mini-workshop space (400 sq ft, ₹8,000-12,000/month rent); use Manipur State Handloom Board for artisan verification and quality standards
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Micro-Enterprise) online; apply for IEC code through DGFT portal; register trademark with IP India; create storytelling brand identity linking to Manipuri folklore; obtain handicraft certificate from DC Handicraft office
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy and Amazon Handmade with high-quality photography and cultural narratives; reach out to IndiaArtisan.com and Craftroots; approach gift retailers in Delhi (Dilli Haat), Mumbai, and Bangalore; partner with 1-2 cultural tourism operators in Imphal
6
Scale
Expand artisan network to 15-20 makers; develop signature collections around seasonal folklore themes; pursue GI tag registration to increase brand value; establish showroom in Imphal or online flagship store; explore B2B partnerships with luxury hotel chains and corporate gifting agencies
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Yojana
up to ₹15,000 direct cash transfer + toolkit support + credit linkage for artisans and small craft businesses
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy (35% for general category) for setting up handicraft unit with artisan employment
🏚
Manipur State Handicraft Development Board Support
duty exemptions on raw material imports, marketing assistance, and participation subsidies in national craft melas
🏚
Manipur MSME Promotion Scheme
₹2-5 lakh working capital subsidy + 5% interest subvention for registered handicraft businesses in Imphal West
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
