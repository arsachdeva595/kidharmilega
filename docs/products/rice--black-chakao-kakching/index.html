Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kakching ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kakching district agricultural office and connect with farmer collectives in Kakching town; study GI certification specifics via Manipur Organic Mission. Attend APEDA export seminars in Delhi/Kolkata. Research competitor pricing on Organic Brands, Conscious Food, and BigBasket premium sections.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source seeds directly from Kakching District Seed Bank or certified farmer groups (contact district Agricultural Department); expect ₹3-5L for first 10-ton batch from established local suppliers with GI-certified credentials.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with registered farmer producer organizations (FPOs) in Kakching through DC-AAFAGR scheme; or lease 2-3 hectares and hire local farmers via revenue-sharing model. Establish milling and packaging unit in Kakching town (₹20-30L investment).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Ministry MSME portal), obtain IEC code (via nearest DGFT office in Imphal), apply for Organic Certification (NPOP/Jaivik Bharat), secure GI usage license from Controller of GI (ensure product sourced from Kakching only). Trademark brand name via IP India.
5
First Sale
Launch D2C on Meesho, Amazon Accelerator (Premium Organic tier), and BigBasket sellerplus; contact organic distributors like Organic Brands, Conscious Food; pitch to premium hotel chains in Delhi/Bangalore via LinkedIn B2B outreach; exhibit at Annapoorna exhibition in Delhi.
6
Scale
After ₹1Cr+ revenue milestone, invest in co-packing facilities in Manipur to serve bulk FMCG orders; establish export subsidiary to tap Japan/Korea health-food importers; develop value-added products (rice flour, bran) to increase margins; expand into 5-10 premium cities via modern retail partnerships.
