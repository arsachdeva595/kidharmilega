Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Giridih ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call Lall Steels (Mahtodih, Tundi Road — publicly available) to understand production flow, raw material pricing, and local buyer networks. Visit Giridih district main market and Mahtodih cluster on a Saturday to observe active TMT bar trade. Register on Udyam portal (free, 15 minutes online) and check Ministry ODOP database for current schemes. Zero cost.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source mild steel rounds/ingots from JSPL suppliers in Raigarh (Chhattisgarh) or JSW partners in Odisha — first batch 20-30 MT costs ₹18-22L at ₹58-65/kg. Arrange transport via local freight (₹2.5-3.5/kg for 250km). Supplier area: Mahtodih has 3-4 established scrap dealers and foundry input suppliers.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 2000-2500 sq.ft unit in Mahtodih industrial cluster (₹8-12K/month, 2-3 year lease typical). Install bar cutting machines (4kW, 40mm capacity) — ₹35-45L for basic semi-automatic setup. Partner with local artisans for initial batches (share model) or hire 2-3 semi-skilled operators at ₹8-12K/month each. Full setup: ₹90-110L capital + ₹5L working capital = ₹95-115L initial.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, 48 hours, free). Apply for Udyam MSME benefits immediately. Obtain IS:1786 certification from BIS office Ranchi (₹15-20K, 4-6 weeks). If targeting export: file IEC application via DGFT Kolkata portal (₹5K, 3 weeks). File for GI registration claim via MSME (Ministry) — Giridih Steel currently has no GI tag; costs ₹60-80K but builds brand defensibility.
5
First Sale
First sale: cold-contact PWD tenders (publish monthly on state portal), post on IndiaMART/TradeKey under 'TMT bars Jharkhand', pitch to 5-10 local material suppliers (₹5-10L typical first orders). Expected first month: 8-12 MT = ₹5-7L gross revenue. Payment terms: 30% advance, 70% against delivery.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month (35-50 MT/month), hire an operations manager (₹25-30K/month), shift to dual production lines (₹80-100L capex), register for GI status, apply for SFURTI cluster subsidy, attend India International Steel Congress (ISC) in Delhi/Kolkata for bulk buyer contact. Target 5-8 institutional repeat buyers by month 8.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L bank loan with 35% subsidy for manufacturing, repay over 5 years
🏛
MUDRA Loan (Business Development Bank)
unsecured ₹10L at 8.5% interest, 5-year tenure, for working capital and asset purchase
🏚
Jharkhand MSME Cluster Development Scheme
₹20-50L grant for common facility centers (smelting, testing labs), 40% subsidy for machinery
🏚
Jharkhand GI Registration Support
₹4-6L grant for collective GI application by district producers (30% subsidy available)
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
