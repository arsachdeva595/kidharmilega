Handicraft Items ki
opportunity, Kachch mein.

Kachch district in north Gujarat borders Rajasthan and the Arabian Sea, providing access to cotton, wool, and wood raw materials from regional suppliers. The 744 handicraft clusters are concentrated around Bhuj (textile hub), Mundra (pottery and metalware), and smaller towns, with 212,000 registered artisans producing woodware, embroidered goods, pottery, and zari items. Buyer access spans Mumbai wholesale markets (Dadar textile market, Navi Mumbai SEZ), Delhi retail (Nida House, handicraft exports), and direct export corridors via Mundra Port and Ahmedabad Air Cargo.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kachch ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bhuj handicraft cluster or Mundra pottery hub (zero cost) — call 3 existing handicraft unit owners using ODOP contact list, attend monthly Bhuj artisan haat at Gandhi Park. Register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) using Aadhaar. This week, doable.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from Bhuj textile suppliers (Nilam Market, Old City) or Mundra pottery artisans — first batch of 100 embroidered pieces costs ₹25-40K, 50 pottery items cost ₹10-20K. Negotiate 30-day payment terms. Connect via KCCI (Kachch Chamber of Commerce) or local artisan cooperatives.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 5-10 artisans directly (zero rent model) or rent 500 sq ft unit in Bhuj MSME cluster at ₹3-5K/month. Your role: design, quality control, order fulfillment. Artisan partnership model costs ₹80-150K upfront (advance payments + material supply). Unit rental + equipment = ₹150-250K.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 15 min online). Apply for GI certification support via One District One Product Scheme (covers 50% legal fees, ₹10-20K). If exporting: get IEC code (₹500, Ahmedabad DGFT office, 5 days). Brand name, logo, packaging design = ₹15-30K. Total: ₹25-50K.
5
First Sale
First sales via Instagram DM (200 followers = 2-3 orders/month at ₹2-5K each) or WhatsApp direct sales. List 20 products on Etsy (₹0 to start, 6.5% + payment fee). Target first ₹20K revenue in month 2, ₹50K by month 4. Wholesale: cold-call 10 Mumbai retail shops, aim for one ₹10K order in month 2.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 operations manager (₹15-20K/month), hire 1 digital marketer (₹12-18K/month), establish quality control in-house. Exhibit at India International Trade Fair (IITF, Delhi, Sept), Vibrant Gujarat Summit, Mumbai Handicraft Trade Fair. Approach 5-10 export distributors. Invest ₹50K/month in Facebook/Instagram ads targeting USA, UK, Australia.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
up to ₹15,000 interest-free loan for registered artisans, 0% interest for first 2 years, skill certification credit
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
₹10-25L subsidy (25% of project cost for general, 35% for SC/ST) for unit setup, processing timeline 60 days
🏚
Gujarat ODOP Support Scheme
50% subsidy on GI registration, branding, and digital marketing costs (up to ₹5L), payable post-approval
🏚
Gujarat Handicraft Development Scheme
20-30% subsidy on machinery, working capital loan at 5% interest, export promotion grant of ₹50K per first export shipment
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
