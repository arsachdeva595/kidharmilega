Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Suryapet ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit established cement plants in Suryapet (Ultratech, ACC, Cements Suryapet) to understand production process, capacity utilization, and market dynamics. Connect with Telangana State Mineral Development Corporation and Suryapet Chamber of Commerce for regulatory and sourcing insights.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source limestone from quarries in Suryapet district (contact TSMDC), gypsum from Rajasthan mines (~₹800-1200/ton), silica sand from Andhra Pradesh suppliers. First-batch raw material cost for pilot run: ₹2-5 Cr depending on capacity.
3
Produce / Partner
Establish manufacturing facility with wet/dry process technology. Partner with established cement equipment suppliers (ThyssenKrupp, FLS Smith) or contract with existing plants for co-production initially. Consider acquiring non-operational kiln and retrofitting it.
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam (MSME classification — Large Enterprise if investment >₹10 Cr). Obtain IEC code from DGFT for export eligibility. Get IS 269 (OPC) or IS 1489 (PPC) certification. Trademark brand with Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) certification mandatory.
5
First Sale
Directly approach state PWD, NHAI, major contractors via tenders. Use IndiaStack B2B platforms (Udyam registry visibility), engage with trade associations. Establish relationships with Ready-Mix Concrete (RMC) plants and major real estate developers in Telangana, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh.
6
Scale
Increase kiln capacity by 50% after 18 months of operations. Launch specialized cement variants (white cement, fly-ash based) to command 15-25% price premium. Establish regional distribution hubs in Hyderabad, Bangalore, and coastal Andhra Pradesh. Export to Bangladesh and Sri Lanka via Chennai/Visakhapatnam ports.
