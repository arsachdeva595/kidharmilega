Moonj Products ki
opportunity, Sultanpur mein.

Sultanpur district (2,440 km²) sits on Gomti and Yamuna banks where moonj grass grows wild — the raw material costs ₹0 if harvested Oct-Nov. Six villages (Mahewa, Dandi, Badoka, Deshi Deoria, Bhatni, Sonughat) hold 500+ women artisans trained in traditional coiling and sun-drying techniques passed down 60-70 years. Buyer markets include Delhi-NCR (300 km north), institutional chains nationwide, and export buyers in EU/USA who value handmade, eco-friendly storage.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sultanpur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Deshi Deoria or Bhatni village (20 km from Sultanpur town); spend a day with artisans to understand moonj weaving and material cycles — free, doable via local taxi (₹500). Call Jagriti Enterprise Center (search online) to learn about 'Moonj of Purvanchal' group structure.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw moonj grass directly from Oct-Nov harvest in Gomti/Yamuna banks near Sultanpur — cost ₹2,000-5,000 for 50 kg first batch (enough for 40-60 pieces). Alternatively, buy dried-and-peeled moonj from Mahewa or Dandi village suppliers at ₹8-12/kg. Connect via local ODOP coordinator or call Sultanpur DC office.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Partner with artisan group (Moonj of Purvanchal or independent cooperative) — provide designs, pay per piece ₹50-150 depending on complexity. Option B: Rent 300-400 sqft unit in Dandi/Mahewa for ₹2,000-4,000/month, hire 2-3 trained artisans at ₹6,000-10,000/month each. First batch (100 pieces) costs ₹8,000-15,000 in artisan labor + materials.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online 30 min, no cost) using GST/Aadhar. Apply for GI registration as secondary beneficiary under 'Moonj Products of Sultanpur' (application filed for Prayagraj — Sultanpur can file separate). Get IEC code from DGFT (₹0, online, 2 weeks). Open current account + GST registration (₹500, same day via CA).
5
First Sale
First sale: List 20 SKUs on Amazon Handmade (₹5,000 setup via listing expert) or Etsy (₹0). Post 10-15 high-quality product photos (hire photographer ₹3,000-5,000). First order typically ₹8,000-20,000 from D2C within 30 days. Simultaneously cold-call 5-10 lifestyle store owners in Delhi/Lucknow; expect first ₹50K-100K wholesale order within 45 days.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 full-time marketer (₹15K/month), 1 logistics person (₹12K/month), scale artisan group to 50+ workers via subcontracting. Attend Akar Craft Fair (Jaipur) and Global Handicraft Expo (Delhi) twice yearly. Build export pipeline via trade bodies (India Handicrafts). Negotiate bulk rates from logistics (₹50-100/kg to metros, ₹150-300/kg export FOB).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma (Pradhan Mantri Vishwakarma Shram Samman Yojana)
₹15,000 toolkit + ₹50,000 credit + 2-year interest subsidy for registered artisans and first-time entrepreneurs
🏛
PMEGP (Pradhan Mantri Employment Generation Programme)
25-35% subsidy up to ₹10L project cost for manufacturing/trading unit, applicable for women entrepreneurs
🏚
One District One Product (ODOP) Margin Money Scheme
UP — ₹1-5L subsidy on setup cost for registered clusters and cooperatives in handicraft
🏚
Chief Minister Youth Employment Scheme (Uttar Pradesh)
₹20,000 monthly stipend for 2 years if you employ 10+ local youth in moonj production
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
