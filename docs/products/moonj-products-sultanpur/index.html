Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sultanpur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sultanpur's moonj artisan clusters in Amethi block and Khargupur village. Interview 5-10 established weavers about production costs, yield, and supply chains. Study export pricing via crafts.nic.in and GI registry. Attend Indian Handicrafts Trade Fair (IHTF) in Delhi to benchmark competition.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fresh moonj grass directly from Sultanpur farmers (June-September harvest season) at ₹500-800/quintal; buy 2-3 quintals for first batch (₹1,500-2,400). Contact Sultanpur district handicraft cooperative or local SFURTI clusters for pre-negotiated pricing. Keep 30-day working capital buffer.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3-5 established moonj weavers in Sultanpur as contract manufacturers under Udyam MSE framework. Provide designs and quality specs. Alternatively, establish your own small 2-3 loom workshop with initial staff of 2-3 trained weavers. Production cycle: 15-25 days per batch depending on complexity.
4
Brand & Register
Register as Udyam (online, free). Obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office (required for exports). Apply for GI-certified seller status via Ministry of DC Handicraft's portal. Create brand identity emphasizing 'Sultanpur GI-certified' and sustainable moonj heritage. Register trademark with IP India.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Handmade and Flipkart Aashirvaad with 10-15 SKUs (baskets, mats, bags in 3-5 price tiers: ₹500-₹5,000). Run Instagram content showcasing artisan stories and craft process (10-15 posts/month). Pitch to 20-30 premium home décor retailers in Tier 1 cities and GI-certified gift platforms. Approach 5-10 boutique hotels for bulk orders.
6
Scale
After 6 months of 20-30% monthly growth, expand product range to include furniture, wall hangings, and designer collaborations. Open experiential pop-up stores in Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore. Partner with international fair trade organizations (FAIR TRADE USA, WFTO) for export certification. Build B2B relationships with hospitality chains and corporate gifting agencies. Invest in digital marketing targeting EU eco-conscious consumers (Facebook + Google Ads budget: ₹50K-₹100K/month).
