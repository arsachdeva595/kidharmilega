Gaura Stone Craft ki
opportunity, Mahoba mein.

Mahoba district (2030 km²) sits in Bundelkhand region of central Uttar Pradesh, 65 km south of Jhansi on NH-75, with direct access to premium Gaura sandstone quarries in Charkhari and Pahara tehsils. The artisan cluster of 400-500 skilled Gaurahari craftspeople is concentrated in Mahoba town and Charkhari, historically producing temple sculptures, ritual vessels, and decorative pieces. Buyer markets span temple procurement networks across North India, spiritual tourism destinations like Haridwar/Varanasi, and growing exports to Singapore, Malaysia, and UK wellness retailers.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Mahoba ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Mahoba town's Gaura Stone Craft artisan cluster near Charkhari Chowk on any Tuesday (weekly haat day) — talk to 3-4 established artisans about stone sourcing, pricing, production timelines. Call DC Handicraft Mahoba office (05652-220355) for cluster contact details. Free, takes 2 hours. No investment needed.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw Gaura Stone from Charkhari and Pahara quarries (20 km from town) via established suppliers — first batch of 10-15 pieces costs ₹25000-40000 depending on size/carving complexity. Order through local 'Patthar Vyapar' merchants in Charkhari market. Marble sourcing via Jhansi market (60 km), ₹15000-20000 for sample batch. Lead time: 10-14 days.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (Asset-light): Partner with 1-2 established artisans in Mahoba for production on commission (40-45% cost per unit, you handle design/finishing/branding). Option B (Own production): Rent a 300-400 sq ft workshop in Mahoba industrial area for ₹3000-5000/month, invest in hand tools (₹30000), hire 1-2 junior artisans at ₹8000-12000/month. Total: ₹80000-150000 for full setup. Artisan partnership model recommended for first 6-12 months.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME-DP portal, 15 mins online, free). File GI Authorized User Application at IP Office, Delhi with proof of Mahoba origin and artisan credentials (₹5000-8000 legal cost, 6-8 weeks processing). Get Udyam certification letter + DC Handicraft 'Mahoba Gaura Craft' attestation (free from district office). If exporting: apply for IEC from local DGFT office in Jhansi (₹500, 5 working days). Total formal cost: ₹6000-10000, timeline 2 months.
5
First Sale
First sale: Create Instagram shop + Etsy store (free, 1 week setup), list 5-8 hero products (Nandi Set ₹600, Temple Sculpture ₹2500-5000, Coaster Set ₹300). Target yoga/wellness communities via Instagram Reels showing carving process. First realistic order: 3-5 units in Week 2-3 from D2C channels at ₹2000-3000 ASP (60-70% margin). Expected first-month revenue ₹15000-25000 if 5-8 orders. Parallelly approach 2-3 temple supply wholesalers in Varanasi/Lucknow via WhatsApp with product photos.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire a dedicated design/photography person (₹15000/month), partner with 2-3 more artisans, allocate ₹30000/month for Amazon/Etsy ads. Join UGC export networks like APEDA/GEMs portal (free) to access bulk international orders. Exhibit at Mumbai Handloom Expo (Feb), Delhi Craft Bazaar (Oct) — cost ₹25000 per show, but 5-10x order spike typical. Establish wholesale tie-ups with 3-5 national spiritual chains (Akshay Kumar's spiritual goods chains, etc.). Build email list of 500+ repeat customers for seasonal campaigns.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
up to ₹15000 tooling grant + ₹500/month stipend for 18 months if registered as artisan, covers hand tools and working capital
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹15-20 Lakhs cluster development support if registering as 5+ person WSHG, funds workshop upgrades, kiln, tool sharing
🏚
Uttar Pradesh Mahila Shakti Yojana
if women-led, ₹1 Lakh subsidy + ₹25000 loan guarantee under Mukhyamantri Kanya Sumangala Yojana
🏚
UP ODOP Scheme Direct Benefit Transfer
₹10000 per quarter marketing grant if certified MSME registered with district handicraft officer, covers e-commerce setup and trade show exhibits
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
