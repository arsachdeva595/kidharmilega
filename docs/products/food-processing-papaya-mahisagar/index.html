Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Mahisagar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Mahisagar's papaya farming clusters around Virpur and Morbi taluka; connect with Gujarat Agro Industries Corporation (GAIC) and KVIC centers. Study competitor brands like Anand Milk and local processors in Godhra. Attend food processing workshops at Food Processing Industries Training Center, Ahmedabad.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source papaya directly from Mahisagar farmers (available year-round, bulk rate ₹8-12/kg). For first batch of 100kg papaya, approximate raw material cost ₹1,200-1,500. Contact Mahisagar Agricultural Produce Market Committee (APMC) for supplier networks. Bulk sugar, spices from Vadodara wholesale markets.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with registered food processing units in Godhra or Virpur offering co-packing services (₹80-120/unit for small batches). Alternatively, set up small kitchen with basic equipment: pressure cooker, sterilizer, packaging machine (₹4-6 lakh for 500-1000 units/day capacity). Obtain Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) license (₹500-5000) and hire experienced food processor.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (formerly MSME registration, free online). Apply for IEC code from DGFT Ahmedabad for export potential. Trademark brand name with IP Appellate Board (₹4500-9000). Obtain FSSAI import-export certificate (₹10000). Create packaging with allergen labeling, nutritional info, and manufacturing/expiry dates per standards.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon, Blinkit, JioMart with ₹5-10k per product launch fee. Build own Shopify store with content-driven storytelling about Mahisagar papaya heritage. Approach specialty retailers like Nature's Basket, Organic Brands, local premium supermarkets. Partner with food festivals and farmer markets in Gujarat and Mumbai. Engage food bloggers and Instagrammers for product seeding.
6
Scale
After ₹20-30L revenue, expand product line (papaya leather, dried papaya chips, juice concentrate). Secure FSSAI plant certification for larger facility (₹50-80 lakh capex). Penetrate modern trade (Reliance Fresh, BigBasket) with distributor partnerships. Launch 3-5 SKUs with seasonal variants. Explore B2B contracts with food manufacturers and international distributors.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy (35% for general category) for food processing unit setup in Mahisagar.
🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹15,000 direct financial support and low-interest loans (5% interest) for traditional food artisans and processors.
🏚
Gujarat MSME Competitive Advancement Support Scheme
subsidized land lease (50% discount) and power cost rebate (20%) for food processors in ODOP districts.
🏚
Gujarat Food Processing Policy 2019
capital subsidy up to 25% for value-added processing units and export credit guarantee from SIDBI.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
