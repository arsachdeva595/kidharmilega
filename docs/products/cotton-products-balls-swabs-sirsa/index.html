Cotton Products (Balls, Swabs) ki
opportunity, Sirsa mein.

Sirsa is one of Haryana's largest cotton-producing districts with ideal agro-climatic conditions and a well-established supply chain. The region has consistent cotton yield and proximity to major textile hubs in Jaipur and Panipat, making it cost-efficient for cotton ball and swab manufacturing. Local familiarity with cotton processing ensures quality control and raw material availability.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sirsa ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sirsa cotton markets (Dandi Mandi) and connect with cotton ginning units; study competitors in Panipat textile cluster; analyze medical/hygiene product demand via Apollo Pharmacy, Netmeds, and hospital procurement officers
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw cotton lint from Sirsa Cotton Cooperative Federation or local ginning mills at ₹50-60/kg; purchase wooden sticks from local suppliers in Sirsa or Ambala at ₹2-4 per stick; first batch of 500 kg cotton + materials = ₹30,000-40,000
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing small cotton processing units in Sirsa town (available at ₹500-1000/day for contract manufacturing) or invest in semi-automatic cotton ball/swab making machines (₹2-4 lakhs for 50 units/day capacity); train 2-3 workers on quality standards
4
Brand & Register
Register as Udyam MSME (online, free); obtain IEC code from Directorate of Foreign Trade for export readiness; register brand with Ministry of Textiles; secure ISO 13485 certification (medical device standard) to access hospital procurement at cost of ₹80,000-120,000
5
First Sale
Launch on Udyam Marketplace and IndiaStack; approach hospital procurement departments directly with samples; list on Indiamart and Tradeindia; reach out to medical supply distributors in Delhi-NCR region; create WhatsApp catalog for B2B buyers
6
Scale
After 6 months, invest in automated production line (₹12-18 lakhs) to increase capacity 5x; build export partnerships with international medical distributors; develop premium variant (sterile, hypoallergenic) for premium hospital chains; establish quality testing lab (₹5 lakhs)
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakhs loan at subsidized rates for manufacturing units in textile sector
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
grants up to ₹10 lakhs for cluster development and common facility centers in textile manufacturing
🏚
Haryana Startup Policy
startup recognition benefits, tax exemptions, and subsidized office/factory space for registered startups in manufacturing
🏚
Haryana Export Promotion Scheme
reimbursement of 25-50% of export-related costs including certification, testing, and marketing for ODOP products
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
