Totapuri mango production clusters in Pallakad and Ramnagar yield 350-400 metric tons per season from smallholder and commercial orchards. Processed mango products can reach Delhi, Mumbai, and international ports in 48-72 hours via road logistics.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Krishnagiri ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Pallakad or Ramnagar mango clusters (50km from Krishnagiri town) and meet 3-4 growers. Call Zepto's Krishnagiri procurement office to understand their sourcing specs. Register on Udyam (free, online). Attend the Krishnagiri agricultural mandi on a Monday — costs ₹0, takes 4 hours.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw Totapuri mangoes directly from Pallakad/Ramnagar growers at ₹15-22/kg during June-August peak season. First batch cost for 5 metric tons: ₹90,000-₹1,10,000. Negotiate for direct pickup or farm delivery. Confirm variety certification from seller (Totapuri specificity matters for export).
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 500-800 sqft unit in Krishnagiri town or Hosur with power backup (₹12,000-18,000/month). Install: pulping machine (₹80,000), pasteurizer (₹60,000), filling machine (₹40,000), cold storage unit (₹1,20,000). Total unit cost: ₹3,80,000-₹5,60,000. OR partner with 2-3 local women's SHGs who already have basic equipment — cost ₹50,000 coordinator fee + revenue share 12-15%.
4
Brand & Register
Get FSSAI license (₹5000, 30 days). Register Udyam (free, instant online). File for GI registration support via State Ministry (if pursuing GI status — process takes 18-24 months, no cost). Get IEC code from Directorate General of Foreign Trade (₹0, online, 15 days). Get APEDA registration if exporting (₹0, online). Total timeline: 60 days. Total cost: ₹5,000.
5
First Sale
First sale target: pitch 10 D-Mart stores within 50km radius with 50kg pilot order at wholesale margin 30%. Expected first order: 200-300kg across 3-4 stores. Use Jumbotail B2B platform to list and get orders from nearby HoReCa. First-month revenue target: ₹1,80,000-₹2,40,000 from 1000-1200kg sales.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue, hire 1 sales manager (₹20,000) and 1 quality checker (₹15,000). Exhibit at Natural Foods Expo India (Mumbai, November) and India Trade Show (Delhi, January) — booth cost ₹1,20,000. Open a second production line in Hosur (25km away) to handle volume. Apply for PMEGP subsidy (₹10-25L) for equipment upgrade.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Kisan Samman Nidhi
₹6000/year direct income support to raw mango grower partners.
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
₹10-25L subsidy (25% for general category) for processing unit setup with annual turnover target ₹40L.
🏚
Tamil Nadu Food Processing Industries Policy 2021
20-25% subsidy on plant and machinery cost, up to ₹50L.
🏚
Tamil Nadu MSME Cluster Development Scheme
₹5-10L soft loan for cluster-based food processors at 2% interest for 3 years.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
