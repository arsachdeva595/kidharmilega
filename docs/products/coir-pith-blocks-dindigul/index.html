Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Dindigul ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Dindigul's coir processing clusters in Kamarajar Nagar and Kallimalaipatti to observe block production, meet 5-6 established manufacturers, and understand the supply chain dynamics. Research export certifications (APEDA, ISO 9001) and visit agri-input markets in Coimbatore and Chennai.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source coconut husks from coconut processing mills in Dindigul district (₹15,000-25,000 per ton) and establish relationships with 2-3 suppliers for consistent raw material. Budget ₹2-3 lakhs for first batch of 50-100 tons capacity.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing coir processors in Dindigul SIPCOT industrial area for contract manufacturing or set up a small processing unit with shredding, composting, and pressing machinery. Invest in IMS (Industrial Machinery Solutions) or similar processing equipment costing ₹25-40 lakhs for 100-ton monthly capacity.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME, obtain IEC code from DGFT for exports, certify through APEDA for agricultural product exports, and secure organic/eco-certifications (ECOCERT or similar). Create brand identity emphasizing sustainability and nutrient-richness, register as a partnership or private limited entity.
5
First Sale
List on Agritech platforms (Krishi Jagran, BigHaat), approach nurseries via direct B2B outreach, and contact landscaping companies. Create LinkedIn B2B presence and attend agri-expos in Bangalore and Chennai. Initiate export inquiries through TradeFed and APEDA portals.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to 200+ ton monthly capacity, establish regional distribution partnerships in Karnataka and Telangana, develop value-added product lines (compressed blocks, fortified pith), and hire an export manager to grow international sales to 30-40% of revenue within 18 months.

PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakhs grant for manufacturing unit with 25% subsidy for general category entrepreneurs
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹40 lakhs for cluster development, skill training, and branding for coir industry artisans
🏚
Tamil Nadu Startup and Innovation Policy
financial support, mentoring, and access to incubation centers for agri-tech entrepreneurs
🏚
TN Coir Board Support Scheme
subsidized machinery, training, and export promotion for coir product manufacturers in the state
