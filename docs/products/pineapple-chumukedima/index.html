Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Chumukedima ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit established pineapple farms in Chumukedima and Dimapur district; connect with Nagaland State Agricultural Department in Kohima; study regional farmer cooperatives like Chumukedima Farmers' Association; research yield data and climate patterns.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source healthy pineapple crowns and suckers from reputable nurseries in Tamil Nadu (Tiruppur/Kodaikanal region) or Meghalaya; budget ₹15,000-25,000 per acre for planting material; first-batch cost for 0.5 acre = ₹12,000-18,000.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with local Chumukedima farmer cooperatives for land leasing (₹40,000-60,000/acre/year) or buy/lease 0.5-1 acre directly; hire local farm laborers for cultivation; implement drip irrigation system (₹80,000-120,000 for 0.5 acre); production cycle = 18-24 months to first harvest.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME under agriculture; apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) for future exports; trademark brand name with IP India; obtain food safety license (FSSAI) if processing value-added products; register with State Agricultural Department.
5
First Sale
Launch on JioMart, Blinkit, and Amazon Fresh targeting metro cities; partner with regional traders in Assam, Manipur; list on local NE platforms (Nagaland Bazaar, Northeast365); approach bulk buyers like Godrej Agrovet and Britannia for processing contracts; set up farm-gate sales kiosk for tourists.
6
Scale
Expand to 2-3 acres by month 18; develop processing unit for juices/jams (₹5-8L investment); negotiate distribution contracts with modern retail chains; export to SE Asia via established export houses; build agro-tourism experience (farm stays, workshops) to diversify revenue.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP
up to ₹25L subsidy (35% for general, 25% for SC/ST/women) for setting up agriculture processing unit
🏛
Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)
₹6,000 annual income support to registered farmers for 2 hectares
🏚
Nagaland Agricultural Sector Mission (NASM)
subsidy on modern farming equipment and irrigation systems (40-50% cost support)
🏚
Nagaland Agro-Export Policy
incentives for value-addition and export promotion of agricultural products (10-15% export credit support)
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
