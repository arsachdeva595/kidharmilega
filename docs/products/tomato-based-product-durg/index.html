Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Durg ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Durg's farmer markets (Supela Market, Bhilai Nagar Market) and connect with tomato growers; study existing tomato sauce manufacturers in Chhattisgarh like those in Raipur's food processing zones; analyze APEDA export data for tomato products to UAE and Bangladesh
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw tomatoes from local farmers at Durg Agricultural Produce Market Committee (APMC) at ₹8-12/kg; establish contracts for off-season supply; first batch of 1000 kg tomatoes + spices + packaging ≈ ₹40,000-60,000
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with FSSAI-certified contract manufacturers in Durg (Bhilai Food Parks) or establish own facility near APMC; alternatively, collaborate with existing food processors in Chhattisgarh State Food Processing Cluster
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Micro/Small) with local DPIIT office; apply for IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Raipur; get FSSAI Food Business Operator (FBO) license; trademark brand name at Delhi Patent Office; create packaging design with tomato imagery and regional flavor
5
First Sale
List on BigBasket and Blinkit for D2C; pitch to Godrej, Mother Dairy, and regional retailers; join IndiaStack platform for B2B wholesale; contact restaurant clusters in Raipur and Bilaspur; leverage WhatsApp Business for direct retailer outreach
6
Scale
After ₹10L first-year revenue: expand product line (chili sauce, pasta sauce); set up own 500 sq ft production unit; hire quality manager and salesperson; pursue PMEGP subsidy for capacity expansion; explore state fair participation for brand visibility
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy for micro enterprises in food processing, 35% subsidy for SC/ST/OBC
🏛
PM Vishwakarma Yojana
₹15,000 toolkit subsidy + interest-free loans up to ₹3L for food processing artisans
🏚
Chhattisgarh Food Processing Sector Scheme
₹5-10L subsidy for new food processing units with 10+ employment generation
🏚
Chhattisgarh ODOP Promotion Scheme
branding, certification, and export support for registered ODOP entrepreneurs
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
