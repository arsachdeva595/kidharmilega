opportunity, Reasi mein.

Reasi's high-altitude temperate climate, rich biodiversity, and unique soil composition create ideal conditions for growing rare aromatic plants including saffron precursors, lavender, mint species, and medicinal herbs. The district's proximity to Himalayan foothills and traditional knowledge of cultivation passed through generations make it naturally suited for premium aromatic plant production.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Reasi ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Reasi Agricultural Produce Market and connect with existing aromatic plant cultivators; study cultivation practices at Central Institute of Medicinal & Aromatic Plants (CIMAP) regional office in Srinagar; attend organic certification workshops at Kashmir's agriculture extension centers
2
Source Raw Materials
Source seedlings from CIMAP certified nurseries in Kashmir or Himachal Pradesh; procure from local Reasi farmers (₹2-5 lakhs for initial 1-2 acre plantation); minimum first-batch dried plant cost ₹1-2 lakhs depending on variety selection
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 15-20 small farmers across Reasi for contract cultivation; establish small-scale drying and processing unit (₹3-8 lakhs setup); hire experienced herb processors familiar with Himalayan plant varieties; implement organic certification protocols from APEDA for export readiness
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (formerly MSME) with agricultural classification; obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Jammu for export; register with Spices Board and APEDA for herbal/aromatic plant export; obtain organic certification from APEDA-accredited agency (₹40-80K); trademark brand name with IP cell
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Handmade and Flipkart Seller for D2C; contact Himalaya, Dabur, and Patanjali procurement teams directly; list on Indiamart and Global Trade Park for B2B; approach specialty stores in Delhi's Greater Kailash and Mumbai's Bandra wellness clusters; pitch to premium Ayurvedic spas and wellness centers
6
Scale
Expand to 5-8 acres cultivation across multiple villages; invest in essential oil distillation unit (₹15-25 lakhs); establish export partnerships with German and Swiss organic distributors; launch premium private-label products for international wellness brands; open experience center in Srinagar for B2B buyers
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samruddhi Yojana (PM-KISAN)
₹6,000 annual income support for registered farmers
🏛
PM Formalisation of Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PM FME)
up to ₹10 lakh loan for food processing including aromatic plant processing at 2% interest subsidy
🏚
Jammu & Kashmir Horticulture Development Scheme
50% subsidy on seeds, saplings, and cultivation cost for medicinal and aromatic plants
🏚
J&K EXIM Policy Incentive
10% export promotion subsidy on FOB value for agricultural exports including aromatic plants
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
