Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Unakoti ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit herbal cultivation clusters in Unakoti district (especially around Kailashahar and Agartala periphery), connect with TRIPURA MEDICINAL PLANTS BOARD, study existing herbal tea makers via NABH certified facilities in Northeast India, and attend APEDA herbal export seminars
2
Source Raw Materials
Source Crotalaria retusa dried leaves from certified organic herb farmers in Unakoti at ₹600-800/kg; packaging from Kolkata suppliers at ₹2-4/unit; first 100kg batch costs approximately ₹80,000-120,000 with packaging and labor
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with small-scale herbal processing units in Kailashahar or Agartala under NABH guidelines, or establish small processing facility with drying equipment (₹300-500K investment); ensure FSSAI and Ayush Ministry compliance for herbal products
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam as MSME (online at udyamregistration.gov.in), apply for IEC from APEDA, obtain FSSAI registration (₹500-1000), trademark brand name at IPO (₹5000-8000), and get herbal product certification from state Ayush department
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh and Flipkart Health+Wellness with ₹5-10K initial listing cost, create Instagram and YouTube content on medicinal benefits (partner with 5-10 wellness micro-influencers), initiate direct outreach to 20-30 Ayurveda clinics in Guwahati and Tripura, list on Boonbox and Nykaa Naturals
6
Scale
Expand SKU variants (cold-brew sachets, herbal blends), establish distribution in 50+ Ayurveda clinics and wellness centers across Northeast, pursue GI registration for 'Unakoti Tea' (2-year process), launch corporate wellness partnerships, and explore export to Bangladesh and Southeast Asia via APEDA support

₹50K-2L subsidy for herbal product enterprises with FSSAI registration

Tripura MSME Startup Support
₹10L soft loan at 3-5% interest for food processing and agro-based businesses in Unakoti district
