Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Malkangiri ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call 3 village-level millet farmers in Malkangiri town's farmer cooperative (available via Odisha Ministry of Agriculture contact list — free). Visit Odisha One District One Product office at Malkangiri district collector's office to get supplier list, market rates, and cluster mapping. Register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) using Aadhaar. Time: 1 week, cost: ₹0.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from Malkangiri farming villages (Chitrakonda, Kalimela blocks have highest millet acreage). First 1-ton batch costs ₹18,000-22,000 at farm gate. Connect via NABARD millet farmer groups or visit Malkangiri grain mandi on Wed/Sat for direct farmer contact. Supplier area: within 25 km of Malkangiri town.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 1-2 artisan women's groups or rent a 1000 sq ft semi-processed grain unit in Malkangiri town (₹8,000-12,000/month). Cost for cleaning, grading, and packaging first 2 tons: ₹40,000-60,000. Alternatively, outsource processing to Baghel Agro Industries (existing millet processor in district) on job-work basis at ₹4-6/kg processing fee.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 15 min). Apply for GI registration support via Odisha Surplus Scheme at ₹25,000 govt subsidy. Obtain FSSAI license (₹5,000-8,000, 10 days). If exporting, apply for IEC from DGFT (₹0 fee, online). Total cost: ₹30,000-35,000. Timeline: 4-6 weeks.
5
First Sale
First sale: List on Blinkit or Amazon Fresh (order size typically 50-100 kg per order at ₹110-140/kg retail). Alternatively, cold-call 10 wellness centers in Bangalore or Delhi with ₹15,000 introductory case (100 packs). Expected first-month revenue: ₹50,000-80,000 from 500-1000 kg sales across 3-4 channels.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue, hire 1 full-time logistics coordinator (₹15,000/month) and 1 sales/export handler (₹18,000/month). Exhibit at India International Trade Fair (Delhi, Nov) or BioFach (Frankfurt, alternate years) for export visibility. Backward-integrate with 5-8 farmer groups for direct supply agreements, reducing cost by ₹2-3/kg.
