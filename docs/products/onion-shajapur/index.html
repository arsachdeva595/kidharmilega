Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Shajapur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Shajapur Mandi and Ujjain Agriculture Market to understand trading patterns and pricing. Interview 5-10 local farmers and commission agents. Connect with District Agriculture Department office in Shajapur city for soil testing reports and crop calendars. Study onion varieties suited for export (Badshah, N-257, Surat Red).
2
Source Raw Materials
Purchase onion seeds from Gujarat Seed Corporation (Vadodara) or local seed dealers in Shajapur at ₹15,000-25,000 per quintal. Lease 2-5 acres farmland in Shajapur district at ₹12,000-18,000 annually per acre. First-batch seed, fertilizer, and labor cost: ₹2-4 lakhs for 5 acres. Contact: Shajapur District Agriculture Office or local krishi vigyaan kendras.
3
Produce / Partner
Start with direct farming on leased land (4-5 month cycle June-Oct) or partner with organized farmer groups like Shajapur Kisan Vikas Samiti. Engage farm laborers at ₹300-400/day. Install drip irrigation for cost efficiency. Harvest Oct-Nov, grade, and cure for 2-3 weeks in ventilated storage. Engage Agri-warehouse operators in Indore for cold storage.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) at udyamregistration.gov.in for agriculture processing unit. Obtain IEC code from DGFT portal for export eligibility. Register brand name with State Trade and Commercial Department. Get FSSAI license (₹2,000-5,000) if packaging value-added products. Obtain Apeda certification for export.
5
First Sale
Partner with NAFED or MAPFED for wholesale bulk orders. List on BigBasket Business and Amazon Business B2B for institutional bulk sales. Contact 5-10 exporters in Indore (Bhagirath Commodities, Agrotech International) for export contracts. Engage with local restaurants and hospitality groups directly. Launch branded packaging on Amazon Fresh and Flipkart Fresh with min 500kg first inventory.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand from 5 acres to 15-20 acres through contract farming partnerships. Build 500-1000 MT cold storage facility near Indore. Launch value-added product line (dehydrated onion, onion powder) to capture premium margins. Develop B2B SaaS model connecting farmers to exporters. Target ₹50 Cr annual revenue by Year 2 through geographic expansion to Maharashtra and Rajasthan onion markets.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakhs subsidy for agricultural processing ventures with 25% margin
🏛
APEDA Agri-Export Promotion Scheme
5% incentive on FOB value of exports and quality certification support
🏚
Madhya Pradesh Agriculture Infrastructure Development Fund
subsidized cold storage and processing facility setup (40% subsidy up to ₹1 Cr)
🏚
MP Krishi Udyam Yojana
₹10 lakh concessional loans for farm-based enterprises with 5% interest subsidy
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Marketing

Ye marketing ideas try karna mat bhulna

  • Partner with 50+ restaurants in Indore and Ujjain for branded packaging and monthly supply contracts. Create in-restaurant signage highlighting health benefits and origin story. Target fine-dining chains (The Ritz, Radisson).
  • Run Instagram and Facebook campaigns showcasing farm-to-table journey with farmer testimonials and recipe videos. Target home cooks and health-conscious audiences. Budget: ₹50,000/month for content creation and micro-influencer partnerships.
