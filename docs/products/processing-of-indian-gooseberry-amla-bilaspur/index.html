Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bilaspur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bilaspur's major Amla clusters in Ghumarwin and Jhandpur blocks; network with existing processors at Bilaspur Food Processing Hub; study supply chains at Shimla and Solan food parks; attend regional Ayurveda conferences.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fresh Amla from Bilaspur farmers (July-October peak season) at ₹30-50/kg; establish supply agreements with 3-5 farmers; budget ₹3-5 lakhs for 5,000-10,000 kg first batch; store in climate-controlled facilities.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing FSSAI-certified processing units in Bilaspur or Shimla for custom production (₹15-25/kg processing cost); alternatively, set up own facility with machinery (juice extractor, dryer, packaging line) costing ₹20-40 lakhs; hire food technician for quality control.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSE classification); obtain IEC code from DGFT for export eligibility; secure FSSAI license (₹1000-5000); trademark brand name with IP office; display GMP/ISO certifications on packaging.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon, Flipkart, and Ayush.gov.in marketplace within 2 months; target B2B buyers via LinkedIn and IndiaMART with bulk pricing; pitch to Ayurvedic retailers and health supplement chains directly; establish direct wholesale relationships with 5-10 distributors.
6
Scale
Expand product range (Amla candy, juice concentrate, powder); establish contractual supply with 20+ farmers; invest ₹50+ lakhs in own processing facility by month 8-10; pursue organic certification for premium positioning; begin direct exports to 2-3 countries by month 12.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh grant + ₹5 lakh subsidy for food processing micro-enterprises
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
cluster-level infrastructure support and artisan training for food processing
🏚
HP Food Processing Policy 2021
20% subsidy on fixed asset investment for food processing units + 2-year power tariff concession
🏚
State Investment Subsidy Scheme (HPFDC)
15% capital subsidy for micro and small enterprises engaged in food and agro-processing in Himachal Pradesh
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
