Mining & Crushing equipment for Minerals & Stones (Jaws and Screens, etc) ki
opportunity, Charkhi Dadri mein.

Charkhi Dadri, 112.6 km from Delhi and 295 km from Chandigarh, is India's 22nd district (notified 2016) with direct access to limestone, sand, and gravel reserves that fuel Northern India's ₹180 Cr annual mining equipment market. The district's two main mining hubs (Charkhi Dadri and Badhra tehsils) process 400,000+ tons of aggregate annually, creating steady demand for jaw crushers, vibrating screens, cone crushers, and magnetic separators. Buyers include 400+ active mining operations in Haryana, Punjab, and Delhi NCR, plus exporters shipping to Vietnam, Indonesia, Philippines, and UAE.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Charkhi Dadri ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Charkhi Dadri town mining market (near Charkhi Dadri tehsil office) and speak with 3 mining equipment dealers about their supplier network; call 2 active quarry owners from Haryana Mines directory to understand actual pain points with screens and crushers; register free on Udyam portal using Aadhaar. Cost: ₹0, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source manganese steel and rubber components from Faridabad's automotive supplier cluster (Sector 10-14, 45 km away) or directly from Bhiwadi industrial area suppliers. First-batch cost for 1 jaw crusher prototype: ₹12-18L. Polyurethane components from Delhi's Okhla industrial estate suppliers (90 km). Lead time: 3-4 weeks.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 3,000-4,000 sqft fabrication unit in Charkhi Dadri town or Badhra industrial area (₹8,000-12,000/month). Partner with 2-3 local fabrication artisans experienced in heavy equipment welding; typical unit needs 4-6 skilled workers. Initial tooling + equipment: ₹18-25L. Alternatively, start as assembler by outsourcing fabrication to Faridabad shops (higher cost, lower quality control).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (₹0, 3 days). Apply for MSME Certificate of Registration (free). If exporting: apply for IEC from DGFT in New Delhi (₹5,000, 10 days). Optional: pursue one-district-one-product (ODOP) support through Haryana Industries Department for visibility. Brand name registration: ₹1,500-3,000 at trademark office. Total timeline: 30-45 days.
5
First Sale
First sale target: IndiaMART Insta B2B platform (post 5 product variants with clear specs) or direct outreach to 10 mining contractors in Sonipat, Rohtak districts via WhatsApp with ROI calculator showing screen efficiency gains. Expected first order: 1-2 jaw crushers or 3-4 vibrating screens (₹8-20L). First-month revenue realistic: ₹0-15L depending on lead conversion. Use WhatsApp for follow-ups; mining buyers are phone-driven.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 2 full-time sales engineers to handle Delhi NCR and Punjab; exhibit at EXCON (mining machinery fair in Bangalore, Jan-Feb) and MiningIndia trade shows; build export network via mining equipment distributors in Vietnam and Indonesia (contact via TradeKey or Alibaba B2B scouts). Upgrade to 8,000 sqft unit with CNC machines. Add 4-6 more fabrication staff. Invest ₹15-20L in quality certifications (ISO 9001, CE marking for export).
