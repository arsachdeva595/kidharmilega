Panipat lies 90km north of Delhi in Haryana's NCR with direct NH connectivity and 6 airports within 150km radius. The Dindoli, Chawla, and Kharkhoda clusters together house 33,000 small and medium units employing over 1 million workers in blanket, carpet, and upholstery fabric production. Raw materials (yarn, chemicals) are sourced locally or from adjacent regions; finished products reach Delhi retail, Indian hospitality chains, and export buyers in USA, Europe, Middle East via established logistics networks.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Panipat ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Dindoli or Chawla cluster on a weekday morning — walk the lanes, identify 3-4 artisan groups or unit owners, ask them supply costs for 100 blankets and current buyer types. Call Panipat Handloom Association (free). Register on Udyam (free, 15 mins online). This week, zero cost.
2
Source Raw Materials
Meet yarn and chemical suppliers in Chawla or Dindoli — negotiate 50kg starter batch (mix of cotton, acrylic, wool). Budget ₹15,000-25,000 for first raw material batch. Lead time: 5-7 days. Visit 2-3 suppliers to compare quality and price.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent a small loom unit (1-2 looms) in Dindoli or Chawla for ₹8,000-15,000/month with power included. Hire 1-2 contract weavers at ₹12,000-18,000/month. Option B: Partner with 3-5 artisans on piece-rate (₹150-400 per blanket depending on complexity). Setup: looms + dyeing setup (rent) = ₹1,50,000-3,00,000 initial. Production capacity: 50-150 pieces/month depending on design complexity and team size.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 10 mins). Open GSTIN (₹0, online). Obtain IEC code from DGFT Delhi for export (₹0, online, 1 week). Print hang tags and packaging locally (₹5,000). GI registration not applicable (Panipat handlooms have no GI tag). Obtain pollution control clearance from Haryana State Pollution Control Board (₹2,000-5,000, 2-3 weeks).
5
First Sale
Week 1: List 5 products on Amazon Handmade and Etsy with photos in your Dindoli/Chawla unit. Week 2: Email 10 hotel supply wholesalers in Delhi with samples and pricing. Week 3: Attend ITC HandCrafts or similar platform launch. First order: assume ₹8,000-20,000 (wholesale) or ₹15,000-35,000 (export) in month 2-3. Expected first-month revenue: ₹0 (setup); Month 2: ₹20,000-50,000.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire full-time operations manager (₹25,000/month), expand to 5-8 looms, add designs team (freelance ₹10,000/month). Attend India Home Expo, TNA (Textile Association of India) trade fairs. Build WhatsApp catalog for B2B repeat orders. Negotiate credit terms (30 days) with suppliers. Explore export logistics partner in Delhi-Noidafor shipment consolidation. Target: ₹30-50L/month by year 2.

Where to Set Up

Infrastructure ready hai — plot lo aur shuru karo

Dindoli Handloom Cluster
Largest concentration of blanket and carpet looms in Panipat; 8,000+ units in 2-3km radius. Loom rentals available ₹8,000-15,000/month. Yarn, dyes, and chemicals suppliers located within cluster. 24/7 power and water supply.
MSME Craft Cluster
Chawla Industrial Area
Secondary handloom concentration with 5,000+ units specializing in rugs and upholstery fabrics. Better space availability for new units than Dindoli. Plot rentals ₹12,000-20,000/month for 500-1000 sq ft.
MSME Industrial Cluster
Kharkhoda Textile Zone
Emerging cluster with 2,000+ units, focus on shoddy yarn and recycled fabric production. Lower rents (₹6,000-10,000/month) but less established infrastructure for handloom/carpet units.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP
up to ₹25 lakh loan with 35% subsidy for general category, 10% margin money required — apply via KVIC Panipat.
🏛
MUDRA Scheme
up to ₹10 lakh unsecured loan for micro-enterprises at 8-10% interest from SBI/PNB branches in Panipat.
🏚
Haryana Handloom Promotion Council
provides free training, subsidy on raw material (up to 20%), export promotion grants; apply via District Industries Centre Panipat.
🏚
Haryana MSME Subsidy Scheme
15-20% subsidy on plant and machinery for handloom units; max ₹5 lakh per unit, apply within 6 months of business registration.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
