Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Himmatnagar/Sabarkantha ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit established ceramic tile manufacturers in Himmatnagar industrial area (near GIDC); meet 3-4 tile producers to understand production processes, costs, and market rates; attend Gujarat Chamber of Commerce events for industry insights.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source clay and feldspar from suppliers in Khambhat or Mehsana district; source ceramic glazes from Mumbai or Gujarat chemical suppliers; first batch raw material cost approximately ₹8-12 lakhs for 50,000 sq ft monthly capacity.
3
Produce / Partner
Either lease existing tile manufacturing unit in Himmatnagar GIDC (₹2-4 lakhs/month) or partner with existing manufacturers on revenue-share basis; invest in kilns, grinding mills, and pressing machines if starting new facility.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME within 2 weeks on udyamregistration.gov.in; apply for IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Ahmedabad; create brand identity with logo and packaging; register business name under Shops & Establishments Act.
5
First Sale
Launch on Flipkart Business and Amazon Business B2B; create WhatsApp catalog for retailers; list on Indiamart and Tradeindia; reach out to 10-15 tile distributors in major cities (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore); participate in construction trade shows.
6
Scale
After 6 months, add 2-3 new tile designs based on sales data; expand distribution to 15-20 states; invest in digital marketing (Google Ads for 'ceramic tiles near me'); explore export to Bangladesh and Sri Lanka; consider second production line if capacity utilization exceeds 80%.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakhs subsidy for manufacturing units with 25% equity contribution
🏛
MUDRA Loan Scheme
up to ₹10 lakhs unsecured loan for expanding tile manufacturing capacity
🏚
Gujarat Industrial Policy 2020
capital subsidy up to 10% on plant and machinery for manufacturing; VAT refund schemes
🏚
GIDC (Gujarat Industrial Development Corporation)
discounted industrial plots and ready-made sheds in Himmatnagar with power and water access
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
