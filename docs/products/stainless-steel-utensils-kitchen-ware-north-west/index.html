Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — North-West ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Narela Industrial Estate and Bhajanpura cluster; interview 5-10 existing manufacturers; study pricing, production capacity, and supply chain at Wazirpur utensil markets; join Delhi MSME Federation to access cluster data
2
Source Raw Materials
Source stainless steel sheets from Jamshedpur suppliers or local scrap dealers in Narela; negotiate bulk rates (₹80-120 per kg for 304 grade); first batch of 500 units requires ₹2-3L raw material investment; connect via DPIIT vendor registry
3
Produce / Partner
Either lease 2,000-5,000 sq ft space in Narela/Bhajanpura Industrial Estate (₹15-25K/month) and invest in hydraulic presses, polishing units, and assembly stations; OR partner with existing manufacturers for contract manufacturing at 15-20% margin; hire 5-8 skilled workers from local cluster
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam on msme.gov.in (free, 10 mins); apply for IEC code via DGFT (₹0); register trademark with IP India (₹4,500-9,000); register GST (mandatory); obtain ISI certification for cookware (₹30K-50K); register with Delhi Pollution Control Board
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Seller Central and Flipkart Seller Hub with 15-20 SKUs; simultaneously approach bulk buyers via IndiaMART B2B; reach out to 50+ retailers in Delhi/NCR; create Instagram/Facebook shop showcasing product range and testimonials; partner with 2-3 distribution agents in pan-India metros
6
Scale
After first ₹50L revenue, invest in in-house manufacturing setup; hire dedicated export manager and expand to B2B platforms (TradeKey, Global Sources); apply for APEDA certification for export; open own brand showroom in key metros; introduce premium/designer lines; explore online marketplace partnerships (Meesho, Unbox, Bolo Indya)
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (35% for general, 50% for SC/ST/women) for manufacturing units in NCR
🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹15K skill training + ₹1L interest-free loan for artisans in metal craft; applicable for cluster-based workers
🏚
Delhi Enterprise Promotion Scheme (DEPS)
15% rebate on GST for MSME manufacturing units registered in Delhi for first 5 years
🏚
Delhi MSME Fund (Restart)
₹1-5L soft loans at 5% interest for working capital; collateral-free for registered units
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
