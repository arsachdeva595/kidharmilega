Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — North-West ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Narela Industrial Estate or Mundka metal cluster this week — walk into 5 unit workshops, ask owners about production capacity, raw material costs, and buyer channels. Call 2-3 raw material suppliers in Wazirpur and ask live stainless steel prices. Attend the monthly North West Delhi MSME haat or trade fair (free entry). Register on Udyam portal (free, online, 10 minutes).
2
Source Raw Materials
Source stainless steel coils/sheets from Wazirpur market (Krishan Nagar area) at ₹150-200 per kg or directly from authorized distributors of jindal steel, essar, or apollo metals. First batch cost: ₹80000-150000 for 500 kg mixed grades (304, 430 variants). Suppliers offer 15-30 day credit post trust-building.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 1000-1500 sqft unit in Narela Industrial Estate or Mundka cluster at ₹12000-20000/month (includes power backup). Buy basic machines (10-15 yr old secondhand) — hydraulic press (₹60000-80000), buffing wheel (₹15000-25000), packing line (₹20000-30000). Or partner with 3-5 contract manufacturers in the cluster who will produce against 40% advance, 30 days delivery (no fixed cost, scalable). Production cost per unit: ₹400-700 depending on size/complexity.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online). Apply for IEC code via Directorate General of Foreign Trade (₹0, takes 3 days online via eVaahan). File GI registration for 'North West Delhi Stainless Steel Utensils' (₹3000-8000, takes 18-24 months, not mandatory but gives legal protection). Get ISI mark for cookware if targeting premium retail (₹40000-80000 certification, optional but increases wholesale credibility). Total: ₹3000-88000, timeline 1-3 months for operationals.
5
First Sale
Register as seller on Amazon (₹500 monthly subscription) and Meesho (free). Post 5-10 SKUs (1-liter cooking pot, 3-piece set, dinner plate set, spoon set). Offer first 50 units at ₹300 cashback or ₹1499 instead of ₹1999. First order via platform typically 20-50 units. Expected first-month revenue: ₹60000-150000 (depending on ASP and conversions). Parallel: cold-email 10 hotel groups and 5 restaurant chains in Delhi/NCR with bulk pricing, expect 1-2 orders of 100-200 units within 4-6 weeks.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (approx. 300-400 units sold): hire 1 operations manager (₹20000/month), 1 sales/export coordinator (₹18000/month), and subcontract production to 2-3 trusted cluster partners. Register for DGFT export incentive schemes (Remission of Duties/Taxes on Exported Products — RoDTEP, 2-4% benefit). Exhibit at International Trade Fairs (Chennai Coffee Day, Delhi Restaurant Expo, Bright Kitchen Show). Launch private-label line for Flipkart/BigBasket. Apply for SFURTI subsidy (₹10L-25L capital grant) to upgrade machinery and hire skilled labor.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
Up to ₹25L loan at 5% interest subsidy for manufacturing units, 10-year repayment, ₹5L-25L eligible setup cost coverage
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹10L-25L non-repayable subsidy for cluster-based MSMEs to upgrade machinery, tooling, and working capital; 3-4 month application process
🏚
Delhi MSME Turn-Around Scheme
Up to ₹50L subsidy for existing MSMEs to modernize production, focus on export competitiveness; administered by Delhi Directorate of Industries
🏚
Delhi Startup Policy
₹20L one-time subsidy and ₹5L/year recurring support for registered startups in manufacturing, 7-year tax exemption if incorporated as startup
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
