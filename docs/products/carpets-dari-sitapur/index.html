Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sitapur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sitapur Carpet Market (Main Bazar, Sitapur town) on any weekday — walk through 10-15 carpet units, ask about loom rental rates, artisan wages, and wool sourcing. Call Sitapur District Industries Centre (05862-250500) and ask for a list of ODOP-registered carpet manufacturers. Attend Sitapur Craft Haat (held monthly at District Level) to meet 20-30 exporters. Cost: ₹0, travel only, doable in 2 days.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source wool from Bikaner wool merchants (established in Sitapur market) at ₹180-220/kg for medium-grade, or direct from Rajasthan Wool Federation. For first batch (100 sq.m carpets = 800-1,000 kg wool needed), budget ₹1.8-2.2L. Dyes sourced from Agra textile suppliers (150 km away) at ₹60-120/kg. First-batch raw material cost: ₹2.3-2.8L.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent loom space in Sitapur carpet cluster (Nai Basti area) at ₹3,000-8,000/loom/month for 2-4 looms. Hire 1-2 master weavers (₹12,000-18,000/month each) and 2-3 apprentices (₹5,000-8,000/month each). Option B: Partner with 5-10 independent weavers in the cluster, provide raw materials, pay ₹25-40 per sq.m finished carpet. Setup cost (Option A): ₹25,000/month rent + ₹30,000/month wages = ₹55,000/month + ₹2.5L machinery. Option B: ₹0 fixed rent, only per-unit payments.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (udyamregistration.gov.in, free, 15 mins). Register brand name at IP India (₹10,000, 90 days). Apply for GI registration through Ministry of Textiles ODOP support (subsidized 50%, ₹25,000-50,000 total). Obtain IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) from DGFT (free, online). Get ISO 9001 certification if targeting premium export markets (₹30,000-50,000, 60 days). Estimated timeline: 4-6 months, ₹65,000-110,000 total.
5
First Sale
List 10-15 designs on Etsy with 5-8 carpet designs (₹200 photoshoot, ₹500 listing fee). Contact 10 interior design studios in Delhi/Mumbai with samples. Post 3x weekly on Instagram/Pinterest with design process + customer homes. First order likely from Etsy (₹25,000-60,000) or interior designer (₹40,000-80,000). Expected first-month revenue: ₹50,000-1.5L.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire an export coordinator (₹25,000/month), join Sitapur Carpet Exporters Association, attend international trade shows (Delhi Handcraft Week, Mumbai Craft Fair, Heimtextil Frankfurt). Scale to 8-12 looms, bring 10-15 steady weavers, invest ₹4-6L in branded packaging and website. Target: ₹30-50L/month by month 18.
