Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Saitual ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Saitual Agriculture Department and connect with ginger farmer collectives in Aibawk and Sialsuk villages. Study cultivation at Mizoram Agricultural University research plots. Attend Saitual ginger farmers' meetups to understand local yield (15-20 tons/hectare), pest management, and GI certification compliance.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified GI ginger rhizomes from registered farmer cooperatives in Saitual (approx ₹80-120K per ton for quality rhizomes). First batch: 500 kg to 2 tons. Negotiate contracts directly with 5-8 farmers for consistent supply. Budget ₹2-4L for initial rhizome stock and input materials.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 10-15 smallholder farmers on contract farming (30:70 cost-share model) or establish your own 2-3 hectare plot. Set up small drying/processing unit (₹60-80K) in Saitual town. Source dehydration equipment from Chennai spice processors. Begin with fresh ginger aggregation and basic drying; scale to powder/extract after 6 months.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) as agricultural producer; obtain IEC code from Lunglei Regional Export Promotion Council. File GI mark usage application (ginger must source from Saitual district). Legally register as producer company or cooperative for direct GI benefit. Obtain FSSAI license (food processing registration). Brand setup budget: ₹80-150K.
5
First Sale
Secure first bulk order from regional spice traders in Guwahati or Imphal (target 500 kg fresh/100 kg dried). Launch D2C on Amazon, Flipkart, JioMart under Udyam listing. Pitch to Ayurvedic companies (Dabur, Patanjali) for B2B supply contracts. List on 1mg, HealthKart for dried/powdered formats. Attend Gulfood Dubai or trade shows in Mumbai/Bangalore to meet export buyers.
6
Scale
After 6 months, scale to 5-8 hectares through farmer networks. Invest ₹25-40L in commercial processing facility (drying, powdering, packaging). Launch proprietary value-added products (ginger candy, instant ginger tea, ginger extract). Establish direct export partnerships with 3-5 countries via registrars. Aim ₹1.5-2.5 Cr revenue by Year 2 through blended channels.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Program)
up to ₹25L subsidy for setting up ginger processing unit, eligible if registered MSME in backward district.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
grants up to ₹25L for food processing clusters; Saitual ginger qualifies for infrastructure co-investment.
🏚
Mizoram Agriculture Department's Organic Farming Subsidy
up to ₹1L per hectare for certified organic ginger cultivation with 3-year contract farming support.
🏚
Mizoram Export Promotion Fund
5-10% subsidy on export certification (ISO, organic, GI) and international trade fair participation for registered ODOP producers.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
