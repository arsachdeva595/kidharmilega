Kargil district sits at 10,000-11,500 ft elevation in the Indus Valley, with glacial-fed irrigation enabling year-round water access and zero pesticide residue in produce. The two primary clusters—Hardass and Kurbathang—contain 1514 hectares of apricot orchards producing 3,775 MT annually, with 800 MT transported to Kashmir markets and 2,975 MT underutilized domestically. Buyer proximity: 80 km to Srinagar wholesale (Aali Masjid Market), 250 km to Delhi retail networks, and direct sea access via Karachi to Middle East markets.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kargil ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Hardass or Kurbathang cluster (contact Kargil District Agriculture Office: 01985-232223) to meet 5-10 orchard owners and packers. Call 2 apricot traders in Srinagar's Aali Masjid Market (find via local haat whatsapp groups). Register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in). Cost: ₹0, takes 4 days.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fresh apricots (June-July harvest) directly from Hardass/Kurbathang growers at ₹40-60/kg, or dried apricots (available year-round) at ₹250-320/kg wholesale rate. First batch: 500 kg dried = ₹125K-160K. Negotiate with 3-4 suppliers in Kurbathang MOCN area for bulk discounts and quality consistency.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent drying/processing unit near Kurbathang (₹10K-15K/month) with existing equipment. Option B: Partner with established MOCN member packers on consignment (0% upfront, 10-15% commission). Option C: Set up basic drying tunnels (₹300K investment) if processing fresh fruit. Typical first-year cost: ₹250K-600K for rent, initial stock, basic packaging.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 15 mins online). Apply for GI certification use under Ministry of Agriculture at zero cost (GI tag already exists). If exporting: obtain IEC code from APEDA (₹500, 1 week), FSSAI registration (₹5K, 10 days), AGMARK certification (₹3K, 2 weeks). Total compliance cost: ₹8.5K, timeline: 4 weeks.
5
First Sale
First sale: list on Udaan B2B (minimum order 50 kg, ₹5K order, 30% margin). Or contact Metro buyer in Delhi directly via APEDA vendor network (first order: 500 kg, ₹1.6L gross revenue). Or supply to cooperative in Srinagar (Aali Masjid): first order 200 kg, ₹64K. Expected first month: 1-2 orders = ₹50K-150K gross.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (400 kg/month): hire 1 QA person (₹8K/month), 2 packers (₹5K each), 1 delivery coordinator (₹6K). Invest ₹50K in cold storage unit (critical). Target exhibitor slots at Aroma India Expo (Delhi), SIAL India (Mumbai) via APEDA sponsorship. Build brand story: glacier-fed, GI-certified, 75-year family farming background. Launch Instagram content series: orchard-to-table videos, nutri facts, recipe collabs.
