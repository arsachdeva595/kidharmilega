Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Rajanna Sircilla ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Zero cost: (1) Visit Mid Manair Dam cluster office and speak directly with cluster manager — learn current hatchery capacity, feed production bottlenecks, and buyer lead lists. (2) Call Fisheries Rajanna Sircilla district office and request cluster statistics. (3) Attend the nearest Rythu Bazaar (Friday or weekly market) and interview fish traders on price trends, quality demands, export partnerships. (4) Register free on APEDA's exporter database to see real-time export rates.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fingerlings direct from Mid Manair hatcheries (available year-round, ₹3-5 per piece for carp varieties); buy feed raw materials (maize, groundnut cake) from Hyderabad's grain wholesale market Begumpet or Secundrabad, or direct from Nizam Sugar feedstock suppliers — first-batch cost ₹8-12L for 50 tons initial feed stock and 50,000 fingerlings for cage culture pilot.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Partner with existing Mid Manair cage operators for contract farming (zero fixed cost, 40-45% revenue share). Option B: Rent a 10-acre plot with water access in Mid Manair cluster (₹1.5-2L/year) or approach a co-op society for water lease. Hire 8-12 farm workers at ₹8,000-10,000/month each, install cage nets (₹25-30L for 4-6 cages), aeration, and feed hoppers. Total setup: ₹35-45L for a modest 2-3 cage operation producing 50-80 tons/year.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 5 minutes) using PAN + Aadhar. Obtain Fisheries Trade Licence from District Fisheries Officer (₹3,000-5,000, 15 days). File IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) with DGFT via e-Biz portal (₹0, online, 3-5 days) if exporting. Apply for FSSAI registration for value-added products (₹500-1,000, 10 days). Total compliance cost and timeline: ₹5,000-10,000, 30-45 days.
5
First Sale
First sales: Approach Rythu Bazaar officials in Hyderabad with 50 kg fresh fish sample, negotiate placement (10% commission, no upfront fees). Simultaneously, register on Udyam and list on Jio Mart Fresh seller dashboard (₹0 listing, 20% commission). Target first order: 200-300 kg to a local hotel chain or cloud kitchen via direct WhatsApp pitches. Expected first-month revenue: ₹1.5-2.5L if 500 kg/month moves at ₹350/kg wholesale.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (~2.8 tons/month): (1) hire a sales manager and 2-3 logistics coordinators, (2) invest ₹20L in cold chain (3-4 insulated vans, ice production unit), (3) register for HACCP certification (₹8-15L, 60-90 days) to unlock EU exports. Exhibit at Aqua India 2025 (December, Mumbai) and Food & Hospitality India (September, Delhi) to secure bulk institutional and export orders. By ₹20L/month, scale to 6-8 cages and consider feed production unit (₹50L investment).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY)
up to ₹20 Cr subsidy for new aquaculture infrastructure, hatcheries, and processing units in identified clusters; 40% capital subsidy for SC/ST, 25% for others.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹50L-1Cr cluster development funds; Rajanna Sircilla fisheries cluster is eligible for value-chain linkage training and cold-chain subsidies.
🏚
Telangana Fisheries Development Corporation (TFDC) subsidy
₹50,000-5L for new fish farmers; also provides interest-free crop loans up to ₹2L for aquaculture inputs.
🏚
Telangana State MSME Subsidy Scheme
up to 25% of land + building cost (max ₹25L) for new units in designated clusters; SIDBI soft loans at 4-6% interest available via DFC linkage.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
