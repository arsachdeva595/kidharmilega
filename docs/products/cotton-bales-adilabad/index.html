Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Adilabad ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Zero cost: (1) Visit Adilabad grain/cotton market (Haat Bazar, Adilabad town) — talk to 5 cotton traders on prices and varieties (Mallika vs Rashi yields); (2) Call two ginning unit owners in Nirmal taluk (ask locals for referrals) — understand ginning cost (₹800-1200/bale) and 3-month lead times; (3) Attend Adilabad DCCB (District Cooperative) meeting or cotton farmers' association gathering (seasonal Nov-Dec)
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw cotton directly from 40-60 cotton farmers in Adilabad/Nirmal taluks (harvest Nov-Feb). First-batch cost: 10 MT raw cotton = ₹5.3L at ₹5,400/quintal (market rate). Alternatively, buy pre-ginned cotton from Nirmal Ginning Cluster (20+ units) at ₹52,000/bale (360kg). Lead time: 10-14 days.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Lease ginning unit in Nirmal (₹25k-40k/month, 5-10 bale/day capacity); invest in cotton pressing/baling machine (₹8-12L, 50-100 bale/day). Total: ₹12-15L for 3-5 month runway. Option B: Partner with existing ginning unit owner for 60% of margin; your role = sourcing, branding, sales (₹2-3L setup only). Process: raw cotton → ginning → pressing into bales → grading (Mallika/Rashi/Tulasi) → storage in godowns (Adilabad or Parbhani).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 15 min). File IEC (Importer Exporter Code) from DGFT Hyderabad office (₹0, online, 5 days). Apply for GI registration support under Ministry of Textiles (₹20k-50k govt subsidy available, 6-8 month process) — use 'Adilabad White Gold Cotton' branding. Get GST registration (₹0, online). Cost: ₹2k legal fees. Timeline: 3-4 weeks.
5
First Sale
First sale: Contact Tiruppur mills via B2B directories (IndiaMart, TradeKey) or direct outreach; first order = 20 bales (7.2 MT) at ₹52k/bale = ₹10.4L. Ship via TATA Logistics or FedEx to Tiruppur (₹1200/bale freight). Expected first-month revenue: ₹10-12L (2-3 small mill orders) OR ₹2-3L (10-15 D2C orders via Flipkart sellers portal). Payment: 15-30 day terms.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 1 full-time sales officer (₹20k/month) to manage mill contracts; invest ₹4-6L in 40-50 MT godown storage near Mumbai port (cuts freight cost 12-15%, enables FOB export). Exhibit at ITME (International Textile Machinery Exhibition, Dec) in Mumbai or Texfair (July) in Bangalore to attract 10-15 mill buyers. Build 1-2 direct relationships with Vietnam/Bangladesh traders for 1 container/month export (₹3-5L monthly revenue). Hire logistics coordinator for export docs (₹15k/month).
