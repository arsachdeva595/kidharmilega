Hosiery Garments ki
opportunity, Tiruppur mein.

Tiruppur is located 100 km southwest of Chennai in Tamil Nadu's textile belt, with direct access to raw cotton via supply corridors from Andhra Pradesh and Karnataka. The Tirupur cluster contains 8,000+ hosiery manufacturing units concentrated in a 25 km radius, supported by 500+ cotton ginning mills and 2,000+ dyeing/finishing units within 50 km. Proximity to Chennai port (140 km), Bangalore airport (200 km), and NH48 corridor ensure rapid export clearance to 140+ countries—the cluster already captured 21.8% of India's total knitted garment exports in FY19.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Tiruppur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Tirupur textile market on a Tuesday/Thursday morning — start at Avinashi Road garment cluster. Call 3 random hosiery makers (check Tiruppur Exporters' Association directory on their website), ask for a 1-hour walk-through their unit. Free. Next, register on Udyam portal (udhyamregistration.gov.in) using Aadhar. Takes 15 mins. Attend any monthly Tiruppur MSME networking meetup (check SIDCO Tiruppur office WhatsApp groups). Zero cost, massive learning.
2
Source Raw Materials
Contact suppliers in Avinashi Road, Tirupur Central, or Mettupalayam areas directly. First batch: 50-100 units mixed (kids t-shirts, leggings, vests) costs ₹40-60 per unit in cotton. Negotiate payment terms 30 days for first order. Confirm lead time (7-10 days). Visit supplier personally to verify quality — fake factories exist. Budget ₹3-5L for first batch of 500-800 units with 2-3 supplier mix to test fit.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (Low capex): Partner with 2-3 existing manufacturers on job-work basis — you supply design/color spec, they produce for ₹15-25/unit labor. Requires ₹1-2L working capital only. Option B (Scale play): Lease a small unit (500-800 sqft) in Tirupur MSME cluster (Avinashi Road) for ₹8-12K/month, install 3-4 second-hand knitting machines (₹2-3L total), hire 2-3 operators. Total setup ₹8-12L including machines, fixtures, quality gear. Most first-time founders choose Option A.
4
Brand & Register
Day 1: Register Udyam (free, instant online). Day 7: Apply for Udyog Aadhaar (included in Udyam). Month 2: Register IEC code with DGFT office Coimbatore (₹0, online, 3-5 days). Month 2-3: Open bank CURRENT account with Udyam certificate + PAN + property lease. Month 3: Get GST registration online (free, 5-7 days). GI registration not needed unless you plan collective branding via TEA — optional at ₹1-2L if pursuing.
5
First Sale
First D2C sale: List 50 SKUs on Amazon Seller Central (registration ₹3,600/month) by month 2. Upload product photos + size charts. Target first order from 1-2 influencers in kids fashion niche (Instagram, send samples worth ₹2-3K, ask for reel). Expected first month: 40-80 units, ₹15-25K gross revenue from D2C. Parallel: Email 20 multi-brand retailers in Bangalore/Chennai with catalog + moq (minimum order 200 units) — expect first bulk order in month 3, ₹50-80K revenue.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (month 6-8), hire 1 full-time operations manager + 1 export/compliance executive. Invest ₹5L in better quality machines or tie up with larger job-work partners to handle 2-3K units/month. Begin export orders via direct outreach to garment importers in US (apparel groups, e-commerce brands) — leverage Tiruppur Exporters' Association directory. Exhibit at Mumbai or Delhi textile trade shows (₹2-5L booth cost). Establish quality certifications: GOTS (for organic cotton claim) or WRAP if exporting (₹50-100K annually).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh loan at 6% subsidy if you create 2+ jobs, eligible for units in Tiruppur MSME cluster
🏛
MUDRA Loan (Udyam Credit)
up to ₹10 lakh unsecured loan for registered micro-enterprises, zero collateral, 8.5-9% interest, repay in 3-5 years
🏚
Tamil Nadu MSME Subsidy Scheme
25% subsidy on plant/machinery cost up to ₹2 lakh for new units in notified textile clusters (Tiruppur qualifies)
🏚
RoSCTL (Rebate of State and Central Taxes and Levies)
5% rebate on FOB value for apparel exports from Tamil Nadu, applicable to Tiruppur hosiery exporters
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
