Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Tiruppur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Tiruppur Textile Park and ISED (Industrial Sewing Equipment Development) cluster near Avinashi Road. Attend TEXPROCIL workshops and connect with 5-10 established manufacturers through Tamil Nadu Hosiery Manufacturers Association. Study competitor units in Lakshmi Mills and Iyer Nagar areas.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source yarn from Tiruppur-based suppliers (Lalco Polyester, Suvin Cotton) at ₹80-120/kg. Source dyeing and finishing services from STDC (State Textile Development Corporation) or private units. First batch raw material cost: ₹50,000-200,000 for 500-1000 pieces.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with contract manufacturers in Tiruppur's Lakshmi Mills cluster (minimum order 500-1000 pieces) or lease 3,000 sqft space in TIRUPPUR ISED with 10-15 sewing machines for in-house production. Hire 8-12 skilled operators at ₹12,000-18,000/month. Production capacity: 5,000-10,000 pieces/month.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online at udyamregistration.gov.in, instant MSME status). Apply for IEC code from nearest DGFT office (Coimbatore). Register trademark through IP India portal (₹9,000-15,000). Open business bank account and GST registration.
5
First Sale
Launch on Myntra FirstCry Seller program (15% commission), Flipkart SMB, Amazon Accelerator. Post on Instagram (reels on fit, comfort, fabric) and Pinterest. Contact 20 wholesale distributors through industry directories. Send samples to export trading houses listed on DGFT portal.
6
Scale
After 6 months, increase production to 15,000-20,000 pieces/month. Add 3-4 new SKUs (athleisure, thermals). Apply for SFURTI grant for infrastructure upgrade. Build 5+ B2B partnerships with national retailers. Hire design and quality teams. Explore ISO 9001 and GOTS certification.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh grant (25% for SC/ST/women) for manufacturing setup
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹20 lakh per unit for cluster infrastructure and brand building
🏚
Tamil Nadu Textile Cluster Development Scheme
infrastructure support and interest subsidy of 5% on loans up to ₹50 lakh
🏚
Tamil Nadu Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises Promotion Scheme
capital subsidy of 25% for equipment purchase up to ₹10 lakh
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
