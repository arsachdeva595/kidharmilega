Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Tuticorin (Thoothukudi) ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Tuticorin fishing harbor and Fishing Port Authority; connect with 3-4 active fishing cooperatives (Vailhaar, Nettukuppam cooperative); study cold chain logistics at existing seafood exporting units; attend Tamil Nadu Fisheries Department workshops
2
Source Raw Materials
Source directly from Tuticorin Central Fish Market (morning auctions) or registered fishermen cooperatives; first-batch sourcing cost ₹2-3L for 500kg premium catch; establish relationship with 2-3 reliable suppliers offering daily freshness guarantees
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with licensed seafood processing units in Tuticorin (HACCP/FSSAI certified); alternatively, lease a 1000-2000 sqft processing space near the harbor with freezing, de-heading, de-shelling equipment; hire trained processing staff at ₹8000-12000/month per worker
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam as food processing MSE; obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office (Chennai); apply for FSSAI registration (process cost ₹500-2000); register trademark with IP India; get phytosanitary certificates from Fisheries Department for exports
5
First Sale
Launch on Bigbasket, Freshmeat, Licious with 5kg premium boxes at ₹1200-1500; pitch to 10-15 premium restaurants in Chennai/Bangalore for supply contracts; contact 3-5 seafood exporters for bulk supply arrangements; create Instagram/WhatsApp catalog for D2C pre-orders
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand product line to value-added offerings (marinated shrimp, fish fillets); open second processing unit; hire business development manager for B2B export contracts; integrate with Shopify/WooCommerce for omnichannel sales; apply for RCMC certification to directly export to 4-5 countries
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy for food processing units with ₹25L investment
🏛
MUDRA Loan
up to ₹10L working capital at 8-12% interest for seafood processing businesses
🏚
Tamil Nadu Fisheries Department Cold Chain Support Scheme
30% subsidy on refrigeration/freezing equipment for fish processing
🏚
TN Export Promotion Scheme
5% subsidy on export logistics and certification for seafood processors registered with Fisheries Department
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
