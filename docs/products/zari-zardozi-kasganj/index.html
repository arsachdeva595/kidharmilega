Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kasganj ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Old City Zari Bazaar in Kasganj (free, 2 hours) — walk through home workshops, speak to 3-4 established artisans about current orders and payment terms. Call the Kasganj ODOP coordinator at District Commerce Office (free call). Attend the weekly haat at Sadar Bazaar to see current piece prices and buyer types. Register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Contact fabric suppliers in Delhi's Chandni Chowk (Radhika Silk House, Mehra Fabrics) for bulk velvet/silk samples — ₹500-1,500/meter for base fabric. Order 20-30 meters first batch (₹15,000-25,000). Source metallic thread (zari) from Lucknow's Hazratganj or Delhi's Karol Bagh at ₹800-1,200/spool (order 50 spools = ₹40,000-60,000). Sequins and beads from local Kasganj suppliers at ₹100-300/kg.
3
Produce / Partner
Do NOT rent a factory — partner with 2-3 existing home-based artisans who have regular work. Offer them piece-rate contracts (₹200-600 per embroidered piece depending on complexity). Provide fabric + zari, they provide labor + expertise. Cost per piece: ₹400-800 (materials + labor). Alternatively, rent a 300 sq ft room near Old City (₹3,000-5,000/month) if you want to train and manage 3-5 new artisans — add ₹1,500/month per artisan for wages.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 20 min). File for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) at Kasganj Customs Office (₹0, needed for export, 7 days). Apply for One District One Product (ODOP) certification through District Commerce Office — unlocks credit access + exhibition invites (₹0 fee). Skip GI registration for now (costs ₹25,000+ and takes 2+ years) — focus on getting sales first.
5
First Sale
Week 1: List 5-10 finished pieces on Etsy (free, 20% fee per sale) + Instagram Shop (free setup). Target first order from a Delhi bridal designer — reach out to 15-20 shops via WhatsApp with sample photos. Expected first order: 3-5 pieces at ₹2,000-3,500 each = ₹6,000-17,500 revenue. First month realistic: 10-15 pieces at ₹1,500 average net = ₹15,000-22,500. Use ODOP platform linkage events (attend 1 trade show/quarter, free entry for registered ODOP vendors).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month turnover (~80-100 pieces/month): hire a dedicated sales person (₹10,000/month) to cold-call designers + manage repeat orders. Recruit 5-8 artisans on retainer (pay 20% above market rate to lock exclusivity). Attend India International Trade Fair (Delhi, Nov) and Handicrafts Expo (Mumbai, Jan) — ODOP covers stall subsidy. Set up WhatsApp Business catalog + export CRM. Partner with 1-2 export agents in Delhi who service Middle East (they handle all customs/shipping for 8-10% commission on FOB value).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
One District One Product (ODOP)
₹1L to ₹5L collateral-free MUDRA loan, 5% interest subsidy by govt for registered vendors, free exhibition stalls at national trade fairs (value ₹30,000+)
🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
₹15,000 toolkit grant + ₹2L collateral-free loan at 5% interest for artisans in registered clusters (Kasganj qualifies), applies to your hired artisans
🏚
UP Handicraft Cluster Development Scheme
₹50,000 to ₹3L grants for artisan training, quality improvement, and marketing within identified clusters like Kasganj
🏚
UP MSME Promotion Policy 2022
15% rebate on electricity bills for registered handicraft units in notified clusters, valid for 5 years
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
