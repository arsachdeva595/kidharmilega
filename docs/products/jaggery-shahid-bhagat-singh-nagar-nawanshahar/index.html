Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Shahid Bhagat Singh Nagar (Nawanshahar) ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Nawanshahr jaggery clusters around Balachaur and Nangal areas; interview 5-10 established jaggery producers; study traditional methods at Punjab Agricultural University, Ludhiana; analyze market reports from APEDA and Ministry of Food Processing.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source sugarcane from local farmers in Nawanshahr district (₹25-30 per kg); establish relationships with 3-4 sugarcane suppliers; plan first batch of 500 kg requiring ₹12,000-15,000 raw material cost plus ₹8,000-10,000 for packaging and containers.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 traditional jaggery artisans in Balachaur cluster for initial contract manufacturing; invest ₹200,000-300,000 in basic equipment (boiling pans, cooling trays, storage tanks) if setting up independent unit; ensure traditional low-heat boiling methods to preserve nutritional quality.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSE classification); obtain IEC code from DGFT for export; get FSSAI license for food processing; trademark brand name with IP office; apply for Geographical Indication if pursuing collective model with local producers.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon, Flipkart, Blinkit with organic health positioning; approach 20-30 organic retail chains (Nature's Basket, Sattvic Foods); contact food exporters for bulk orders; create Instagram/YouTube content showing traditional production methods for D2C customers; target health bloggers for influencer partnerships.
6
Scale
Expand product line (jaggery powder, jaggery blocks in premium packaging); open retail presence in 3-5 metros; establish direct export partnerships with 2-3 international distributors; invest in cold-storage facility; introduce B2B supply to ice-cream, chocolate, and nutritional supplement manufacturers; scale production to 10-15 MT monthly.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy at 35% for general entrepreneurs in agro-based small business
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹10 lakh per unit for food processing clusters; applicable for Nawanshahr jaggery artisans
🏚
Punjab Agricultural Department Subsidy on Equipment
40-50% subsidy on food processing machinery and cold storage for agri-entrepreneurs
🏚
Punjab State Agro-Industries Development Board Credit Guarantee Scheme
up to ₹50 lakh collateral-free loan for food processing units with 5% government guarantee
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
