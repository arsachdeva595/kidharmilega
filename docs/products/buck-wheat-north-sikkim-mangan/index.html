Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — North Sikkim (Mangan) ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Mangan town and connect with North Sikkim Farmer Producer Organizations (FPO), study existing buckwheat clusters near Lachung and Dzongu. Attend the annual Sikkim Organic Farming Expo. Analyze pricing on Amazon and Flipkart organic sections.
2
Source Raw Materials
Partner with local farmers in Mangan subdivision or Tashiding block for certified organic buckwheat seeds (₹80-120/kg). First batch: 500kg costs ₹40,000-60,000. Source organic fertilizer from SOAO (State Organic Agricultural Organization) approved vendors.
3
Produce / Partner
Start with contract farming via Sikkim Seed Board or establish direct partnerships with 10-15 local farmers. Invest in basic drying and storage facilities (₹200-400k). Consider partnering with SLMF (Sikkim Livelihoods Mission Foundation) for training and quality control.
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam (Ministry of MSME), obtain IEC code from DGFT for exports. Apply for Organic Certification (NPOP) from FSSAI-accredited certifier — costs ₹15-25k, takes 3-4 months. Register brand name with IP office. Set up GST registration.
5
First Sale
Launch via Amazon Fresh (Organic category), Flipkart Supermart, and direct outreach to Nature's Basket, Big Bazaar organic sections. Start with 50-100kg monthly supply. Pitch to GreenJum and Biokart online platforms. Send samples to 20+ foreign importers via TradeKey and Alibaba.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to 2-3 new states via distribution partnerships. Invest in processing (buckwheat flour, noodles production) to increase margins from 40% to 60%. Build B2B relationships with food manufacturers. Launch export shipments to 3-5 countries targeting ₹2-3 Cr revenue by Year 2.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy for manufacturing unit with 10-15% margin subsidy
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
specialized cluster support with ₹50L+ funding for processing units in agricultural clusters
🏚
Sikkim Organic Farming Mission
free certification support, ₹5k/hectare subsidy, and extension services for organic producers
🏚
Sikkim Agri-Export Promotion Scheme
₹10-15 lakh support for export-focused agri-businesses, including cold-chain and packaging equipment
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
