opportunity, Cachar mein.

Cachar district in the Barak Valley produces over 2.8 lakh tonnes of paddy annually on 2.2 lakh hectares of wetland fields, with direct access to Silchar's ₹40+ Cr rice trade hub and rail-road connectivity to Kolkata and Bangladesh borders. The region's puddled field systems and farmer collectives in Silchar, Dhalai, and Lakhimpur tehsils create a ready supply network. Scented glutinous rice varieties are sold locally via Silchar wholesale markets and emerging e-commerce, with 60% of Cachar's rice output consumed as raw paddy or local milling.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Cachar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Silchar Rice Market (open 5-9am daily) and speak to 3-5 mill owners about glutinous rice volumes and pricing. Call Consortium of Producers (contact via Cachar DC office) to understand farmer supply chains. Attend Tuesday Silchar vegetable haat to observe wholesale dynamics. Zero cost, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source pre-soaked seeds and rice seedlings from Cachar Agriculture Department's seed certification center (Silchar) or Dhalai farmer collectives. First 2-3 tonne batch costs ₹2-2.5L (paddy procurement at ₹1800-2200/quintal). Bulk sourcing requires 4-6 week lead time. Dhalai & Lakhimpur tehsils are the main production zones.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent a 2000-sqft milling unit in Silchar's industrial area (₹8-12K/month), purchase husking and grading machinery (₹35-55L). Option B: Partner with existing mill owners for toll milling at 8-12% of output. Employ 3-4 workers for sorting, packing, grading (₹12-18K/month each). Initial 6-month costs: ₹45-75L (mill setup + 4 batch procurement).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (5 minutes, free, online at udyamregistration.gov.in). Apply for GI registration for Cachar Scented Rice variant through Consortium (₹10-15K filing + 8-12 months processing). Obtain Spice Board registration for food safety (₹5K, 2 weeks). Get IEC from DGFT Guwahati office for export (₹0, 15 days). Total formal cost: ₹15-25K.
5
First Sale
First sale: Approach Silchar Rice Mill Association for bulk order (minimum 500kg at ₹120/kg = ₹60K revenue). List on Blinkit/BigBasket (5-7 day approval), start with ₹5-10K first order. Upload on Amazon Fresh specialty category. First month target: ₹1.2-1.8L revenue across 3-4 channels.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 2 sales staff (₹15K/month each), rent 1000-sqft packaging facility (₹10K/month), invest ₹8-12L in branding and social media (₹1-1.5L/month spend). Exhibit at Northeast Food Bazaar (Feb, Guwahati) and GI Haat Bazaar (Delhi, June). Build WhatsApp B2B channel to Delhi hotels and North Indian food retail chains.
