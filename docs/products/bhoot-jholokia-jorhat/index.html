Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Jorhat ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Nakachari or Teok village cluster this week — call Jorhat District Agriculture Office (0376-2344680) for farmer contact list, or attend Jorhat haat on market day to see raw chilli volumes and pricing. Walk through farms to understand soil, climate, yield patterns. Zero cost, 1-2 days required.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw dried Bhoot Jolokia from established farmers in Nakachari (primary) or Teok (secondary) clusters at ₹400-500/kg for 100-200kg initial batch. Negotiate directly with farmer cooperatives listed via Jorhat ATMA (Agricultural Technology Management Agency) or approach middlemen at Jorhat grain market. First-batch cost: ₹50-100K for 100-200kg.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent a 500-800 sq ft unit (₹5-8K/month) in Jorhat town or Teok with grinder, drying racks, packaging machine (₹60-120K one-time for basic setup). Option B: Partner with established chilli processors in Nakachari cluster on contract basis (pay ₹20-30/kg processing fee, keep margins). Recommended: hybrid model — source from farmers, process at rented unit, scale to 200kg/month output in 3 months. Total setup: ₹120-200K for Option A in months 1-2.
4
Brand & Register
Register as sole proprietor on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 15 min) and get 12-digit Udyam ID same day. File GI registration application through Directorate of Industrial Development, Assam (cost ₹5-8K, timeline 18-24 months — apply early as Bhoot Jolokia GI is pending). Get IEC code from DGFT online portal (₹0, valid for lifetime exports). Apply for FSSAI license at ₹10-15K (mandatory for food processing). Timeline: Udyam + IEC + FSSAI = 4-6 weeks.
5
First Sale
Week 1-2: List branded product on Amazon Seller Central and Flipkart Seller (commission 15-25%, but fastest to first sale). Target first order: 50 units at ₹250 retail = ₹12.5K gross revenue, ₹6-8K net margin. Simultaneously approach 5 local restaurants/cloud kitchens in Jorhat town for bulk ₹450/kg orders (negotiate 10kg trial order = ₹4.5K order value). Expected first month: ₹15-25K revenue from 3-5 orders if active on 2-3 channels.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue target: hire 1-2 skilled packagers (₹8-12K/month salary), outsource logistics to local Ekart or Delhivery franchise (pickup at ₹15-20/shipment), set up social media marketing (Instagram Reels on chilli-prep videos, cost ₹0). Exhibit at Heimat International Food Fair (Delhi, Feb-Mar), GulF Food Exhibition (Dubai, Feb) if exporting, or IITF Delhi if domestic-focused. Scale processing to 400-600kg/month = need 1,200 sq ft unit (₹12-15K/month) and 2nd grinder (₹40K). At ₹10L/month, hire a logistics/export manager (₹15-20K/month) and a sales person (₹10-15K/month).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
One District One Product (ODOP) Scheme
provides ₹50-100K subsidy for processing equipment, ₹0 interest credit guarantee via SIDBI up to ₹10L, and free branding/logo support (already provided to Bhoot Jolokia in Jorhat)
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25% subsidy for manufacturing unit setup (max ₹10L subsidy) + 3-year GST exemption for registered applicants; process via District Industries Centre Jorhat
🏚
Assam MSME Upgradation Scheme
provides ₹2-5L grant for quality certification (FSSAI, ISO 22000) and equipment upgradation; applied via Directorate of Industrial Development, Assam
🏚
Assam Export Promotion Policy 2023
5% export subsidy on FOB value for agricultural products, 50% airfreight subsidy for first 12 months of export; apply via Assam Export Promotion Board
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
