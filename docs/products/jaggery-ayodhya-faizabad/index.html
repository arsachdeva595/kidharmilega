Jaggery ki
opportunity, Ayodhya (Faizabad) mein.

Ayodhya district in eastern Uttar Pradesh, 130 km east of Lucknow, sits on the Saryu riverbank with 20% of its 2,540 km² dedicated to sugarcane cultivation, feeding a dense network of sugar mills and refineries. The district has a century-old artisan base in jaggery-making passed through generations, with raw material proximity eliminating supply chain gaps. Buyer markets include Lucknow, Varanasi, and export hubs in Mumbai—all within 300 km by rail/road.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Ayodhya (Faizabad) ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Ayodhya's main sugarcane mandi in city center on a Tuesday-Thursday morning (free). Call 3 jaggery suppliers from Naka Basti cluster and ask: current gur rates, supply terms, quality grades. Register free on e-NAM (enam.gov.in) and browse live jaggery price listings from Ayodhya district. All doable this week, ₹0 cost.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw sugarcane juice from Ayodhya's sugar mills (Govardhan Khand, Sagri area) or nearby refineries. First batch: negotiate 500kg of pure juice at ₹18-22/kg (₹9,000-11,000 per batch). Suppliers typically accept payment after 3 days. Alternative: partner directly with artisans in Naka Basti (central jaggery-making cluster) who will supply pre-made jaggery at ₹95-110/kg (no production cost).
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (rent unit): Lease a 500 sq ft food-certified kitchen in Sagri Food Processing Zone or near Naka Basti mandi for ₹4,000-6,000/month. Equipment cost: ₹1.5-2.5L (copper vessels, cooling frames, thermometer). Hire 2-3 skilled artisans at ₹8,000-12,000/month. Option B (artisan partnership): Partner with existing jaggery maker, provide raw material, take 35-40% margin on finished product (₹0 rent, ₹0 labor cost). Most founders start with Option B.
4
Brand & Register
Month 1: Register Udyam MSME online (udyamregistration.gov.in, ₹0, 15 mins). Month 2: Apply for Agmark certification through UP Food Safety (₹5,000-8,000, 4-6 weeks). Month 3: If exporting, apply for IEC code via APEDA (apeda.gov.in, ₹2,000-3,000, 2 weeks). Get FSSAI food license (₹5,000-10,000, 3-4 weeks). Packaging design: hire graphic designer on Fiverr for ₹3,000-5,000.
5
First Sale
First sale: Approach Blinkit/Zepto local logistics hubs in Ayodhya — they onboard local brands within 2 weeks. First order: negotiate 100 packs of 500g at ₹180/pack wholesale (₹18,000 revenue). Expected first-month revenue: ₹25,000-40,000 from 150-200 retail packs sold. Simultaneously cold-call 10 sweet shops in Lucknow with sample boxes (₹1,500 cost) — expect first wholesale order within 3 weeks.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: Move from artisan partnership to own unit operation (hire 4-5 production staff, cost ₹50,000/month). Launch corporate gifting campaign targeting HR teams (100-unit bulk orders, ₹250/unit). Exhibit at Natural & Organic Products Expo India (Mumbai, ₹30,000-50,000 stall cost) to connect institutional buyers. Apply for APEDA export subsidy schemes (50% subsidy on marketing costs). Hire one sales person at ₹20,000/month for B2B routes (hotels, restaurants, ayurveda companies).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
Pradhan Mantri KISAN SAMPADA Yojana (PM KISAN SAMPADA)
₹50 lakh subsidy for setting up food processing unit with 50% capital cost reimbursement for equipment.
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
₹25 lakh subsidy for manufacturing unit, 25% project cost as grant for SC/ST/women founders.
🏚
UP Food Processing Industry Policy 2017
5-year property tax exemption, 50% sales tax reimbursement for first 5 years of operation.
🏚
Integrated Horticulture Development Mission
Direct linkage subsidy for agro-processing units linked to sugarcane cultivation, up to ₹15 lakh for equipment.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Frequently Asked Questions

Seedhe sawaal, seedhe jawab

Minimum ₹2.5-3 lakh se start kar sakte ho — ₹1 lakh Udyam + license, ₹1 lakh first raw material batch, ₹50,000 packing/branding. Maximum ₹12-15 lakh agar khud ka unit rent karo aur equipment khareedho. Sabse sasta: existing jaggery maker ke saath partner ban jao, bas ₹30,000-50,000 aur 3 mahine ke sales experience chahiye.
Bilkul zaroori nahi. Ayodhya mein 50+ artisans hain jinhe generations se experience hai. Tum sirf quality control, sourcing, aur sales sambhalo. Lekin food hygiene, inventory, aur customer management seekhna padega — 1-2 mahine mein skilled ho jaoge.
Central: PM KISAN SAMPADA se ₹50 lakh subsidy, PMEGP se ₹25 lakh grant mil sakta hai (application ek mahine lagta hai). UP State: Property tax 5 saal exempt, 50% sales tax refund. e-NAM par free listing aur APEDA export subsidy schemes available hain agar ₹50L+ export karo.
Sabse aasan: Blinkit/Zepto local hubs mein approach karo — 2 hafte mein onboard kar denge. Ya phir Lucknow ke 10-15 sweet shops, health stores, yoga centers ko directly call karo samples bhej kar. Pehla order 15-20 days mein aa jayega, ₹8,000-15,000 ka hona chahiye.
Haan, bilkul export hota hai. Nepal, Bangladesh, Mauritius, UAE ko bhejte hain. Process: Agmark certification + IEC code + APEDA registration, total 4-5 hafte lagta hai. ₹120-150/kg FOB price, 45-60% margin milega. Lekin minimum ₹5-10 lakh ka first shipment hona chahiye jisse profitable rahe.
Jaggery har jagah ka staple product hai — customer loss nahi hota agar quality theek rakho. Worst case: Udyam registration cancel karo (2 hafte), artisan partnership se exit kar jao (no loss, bas reputation). Lekin Ayodhya mein competition hai — tum agar D2C brand weak ho, sirf wholesale market mein chale jayega 10-15% margin se. Strong brand banana zaroori hai first 6 mahine mein.
