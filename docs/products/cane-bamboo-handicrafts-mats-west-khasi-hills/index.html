Cane & Bamboo Handicrafts (Mats) ki
opportunity, West Khasi Hills mein.

West Khasi Hills spans 3,911.22 sq km in Northeast India with dense bamboo and cane forests providing year-round raw material. Three established artisan clusters in Mairang, Lyrnai village, and Tyrchang village employ 60-80 traditional weavers trained in generations-old mat-weaving techniques. Proximity to Shillong (90 km) and Northeast urban markets, plus direct access to Assam-Meghalaya trade routes, creates medium-tier export potential to South Asia and Southeast Asia.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — West Khasi Hills ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Contact Mairang Craft Cluster leader this week (phone West Khasi Hills District MSME Office for contact). Visit one artisan unit in Lyrnai village to see production. Call 2-3 cane suppliers in the Shillong-Mairang road area. Register free Udyam account at udyamregistration.gov.in using Aadhaar. Zero cost, doable in 5 days.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw bamboo and cane directly from forest villages around Mairang and Tyrchang (₹8,000-12,000 per 100 kg, seasonal April-September). First batch: ₹30,000-40,000 for materials to produce 50-80 mats. Negotiate direct with Sung Valley clay suppliers for pottery-grade raw material if adding ceramics later.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent 300-400 sq ft workshop in Mairang town (₹5,000-8,000/month) and hire 2-3 trained weavers at ₹8,000-10,000/month each. Option B: Partner with existing artisans in Mairang/Lyrnai as order-based subcontractors (lower fixed cost, 15-20% commission). Initial production cost ₹40,000/month for materials + labour.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online). Apply for SFURTI cluster development grant if joining an organized cluster (₹40L potential cluster subsidy, not individual). Open IEC account at DGFT for export eligibility (₹5,000, 10 days). Explore GI registration for Cane-Bamboo Mats under Ministry MSME support (₹50,000-80,000 lawyer fees, 18-24 months process). File for PM Vishwakarma artisan scheme benefits if employing traditional craftspeople.
5
First Sale
First sale target: Etsy shop launch with 30-50 mat listings (₹200-600 per mat). Or approach 3-5 Shillong interior design studios for bulk order (minimum 25 mats, ₹15,000-25,000 first order). Expected first-month revenue: ₹8,000-15,000 from 10-15 online orders or 1-2 wholesale deals.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire a dedicated sales/export coordinator (₹12,000/month), upgrade to 800-1,000 sq ft production space (₹12,000/month), register on Alibaba and German craft export platforms. Exhibit at North East India craft fairs and IHGF Delhi expo (₹50,000-80,000 booth cost). Establish direct relationship with 2-3 international importers via LinkedIn and trade directories.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹15,000 loan at 0% interest for traditional craftspeople; ₹500 cash gift on enrollment
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹40L cluster-level grant for organized groups of artisans; subsidy up to 90% of infrastructure cost
🏚
Meghalayan Age Support Program
Infrastructure setup grants and mentorship for young artists aged 18-35; Andrasphera Kharmawlong (Mairang ceramicist) received funding for Ceramic Studio setup
🏚
Ateliers
Government-backed workspace and exhibition support for traditional craftspeople in Meghalaya; applicants get subsidized rent and market linkage support

Kaafi milti hai. PM Vishwakarma ₹15,000 loan deta hai 0% interest par agar artisan ho. SFURTI cluster grants ₹40L tak de sakti hai agar organized group banao. Meghalaya ke Ateliers aur Meghalayan Age scheme young founders ko ₹20-50L tak infrastructure subsidy dete hain. Etsy, Amazon Handmade, aur Flipkart Handloom par start karo—international buyers automatically aa jayenge. Local mein Shillong ke 10-15 interior design studios hain jo bulk order dete hain. WhatsApp message se hotel chains (Assam-Meghalaya) ko pitch karo—first order 25-50 mats ka mil sakta hai ₹20-30L mein. LinkedIn export brokers ko search karo—direct Germany aur UK importers khud contact kar rahe hain.
Bilkul ho sakta hai. IEC registration ₹5,000 mein 10 days mein mil jayega. Bangladesh, Nepal, Southeast Asia mein heavy demand hai bamboo mats ke liye. Germany aur UK mein eco-conscious buyers hain—zyada price pay karte hain. FOB price ₹300-500 per mat rakh sakte ho, 40-50% margin milega. Lekin customs, shipping, packaging samajhna padega—ek export broker hire kar lo jo 2-3% commission le.
Sachai yeh hai ki mat-making business mein downside limited hai. Raw materials saste hain, artisans seasonal ho sakte hain to part-time chal sakta hai. Agar online nahi chal raha, local restaurants, cafes, travel agencies ko supply kar sakte ho. Pottery aur glass clusters mein combine karke bundled product bana sakte ho. Worst case, artisans ko return kar do aur inventory shift kar do—capital lock-in zyada nahi hota. Lekin patience aur 10-12 months commitment zaroori hai.
