Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Mungeli ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Mungeli APMC Mandi (Wednesday+Saturday auctions, 6am), call 3-4 groundnut farmers in Pathariya block (via Mungeli district agriculture office 07643-242080), attend Mungeli Krishak Samiti meeting (monthly), register free on Udyam portal — all zero-cost, doable in 1 week
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from Pathariya block farmers or Mungeli APMC directly; ₹2.5-3L for first 10 MT (May-July harvest season, best quality). Suppliers: Mungeli Krishak Samiti, local commission agents in Mungeli town market, direct from Pathariya tehsil farms
3
Produce / Partner
Lease 500-800 sq ft unit in Mungeli Industrial Estate (₹3000-4500/month) or start via contract with existing groundnut processing units (15-20 units operating in Mungeli block). Own setup: solar dryer (₹4-5L), shelling machine (₹80000-120000), roasting drum (₹40000-60000). Partnership route: 20-25% commission to processing unit for first 6 months, zero capex
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam within 10 days (free, online at udyamregistration.gov.in). Apply for GI registration via State GI cell (₹2000-5000 fee, 6-8 month timeline) if planning 'Mungeli Groundnuts' brand. Export: obtain IEC from DGFT (₹2000-3000, 2 weeks), open ECCBS account with exporter's bank for export incentives under ROSCTL scheme
5
First Sale
First sale: list on Udaan (B2B platform, ₹5000 seller fee) targeting canteen suppliers, post on Blinkit vendor portal for Raipur/Durg metro. Secure first order of 500 kg at ₹125/kg = ₹62500 revenue (net profit ₹12500 after margin). Expected first month: 2000-3000 kg across 3-4 buyers = ₹3-4.5L topline
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 1 quality controller (₹18000/month), 1 logistics coordinator. Exhibit at Chhattisgarh Pravasi Mela (annual state trade fair in Raipur, Oct-Nov). Apply for PMEGP subsidy (₹5L-10L for scaling), join state-level export facilitation program. Build brand via Instagram (reels of farm-to-pack), YouTube farmer testimonials, collaborate with nutrition influencers in Hindi/English

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Program)
₹5-10L subsidy + ₹10L collateral-free loan for processing/export unit setup, 35% of project cost as subsidy for SC/ST founders
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
cluster-level grants up to ₹25L for common processing facility if 10+ producers group together, technical support free
🏚
Chhattisgarh Krishi Karman Yojana
₹1L cash incentive for farmers/processors increasing groundnut output by 20% YoY, 2-year validity
🏚
Rajiv Gandhi Kisan Nyay Yojana
₹10000 per acre subsidy for groundnut cultivation area, indirect support for feedstock cost reduction
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Downside: raw material (groundnuts) is liquid, can be sold back to APMC at 5-10% loss if no buyer found. Processing equipment has 40% resale value after 1-2 years. Biggest risk is demand — contract sales first 6 months before scaling inventory. Exit: sell lease to another processor (easy in Mungeli clusters), return to farmer supply model with zero capex. No sunk costs if you avoid owning land/building. Realistic failure mode: low margins (below 15%), not zero sales.
