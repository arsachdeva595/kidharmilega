Buldhana district (note: description refers to Bhandara, geographically adjacent Vidarbha region) possesses significant mineral deposits including coal, manganese, and quartz. The region's geological formation and established mining infrastructure make it ideal for mineral processing and value-added product manufacturing. Proximity to rail and road networks facilitates bulk export to domestic industrial hubs.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Buldhana ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Buldhana Mining Cluster near Chikhli and Khamgaon; meet existing mineral traders at Buldhana Chamber of Commerce; study IBEF reports on India's minerals sector; connect with Nagpur-based mineral processors to understand supply chain
2
Source Raw Materials
Source coal and manganese ore directly from licensed quarries in Buldhana/Bhandara border regions; negotiate contracts with small mine operators (₹8-15L for first 50-100 MT); establish relationships with mineral brokers in Nagpur who supply bulk quantities at wholesale rates
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing mineral processing units in Buldhana (Chikhli, Khamgaon) offering toll processing services; alternatively lease a small warehouse (500-1000 sqft) with crushing/grinding equipment (₹15-25L) for basic beneficiation; hire 4-6 skilled workers trained in mineral processing
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam as mineral trading/processing unit; obtain IEC code for export; get IMEI registration for mineral exports; obtain mining lease or supplier agreements as proof; incorporate as Partnership/Pvt Ltd for B2B credibility; align with Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) for quality certification
5
First Sale
List on IndiaMART and TradeKey with bulk quantity offers; contact 50+ cement/steel mills directly via LinkedIn and email with product samples; join Buldhana District Industries Association for B2B connect events; attend National Buyers-Sellers Meet organized by FISME; approach regional distributors of industrial minerals
6
Scale
Establish own processing unit with modern crushing/screening equipment (₹40-60L); secure long-term supply contracts with 5-10 major industrial buyers; expand product line to include value-added minerals (calcined kaolin, processed feldspar); set up export documentation cell; build distribution partnerships in East Asia and Middle East markets
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy (35% of project cost) for mineral processing unit setup in Tier 2/3 cities
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
cluster development grants up to ₹5 crore for Buldhana minerals cluster strengthening
🏚
Maharashtra ISUPP (Integrated Single Window System for Permissions and Approvals)
fast-track mining lease and export authorization for startups in Vidarbha region
🏚
Maharashtra Export Promotion Scheme
5% subsidy on FOB value of mineral exports for first 3 years; applicants must export minimum ₹1 Cr annually
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
