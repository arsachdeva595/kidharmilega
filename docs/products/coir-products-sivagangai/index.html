Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sivagangai ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sivagangai Coir Cluster (Sivagangai town center) to study production methods; connect with DC Handicraft office; attend trade fairs at Chennai Export Complex; study competitor products on Amazon and Global Sources.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source coconut husks from local coconut farmers in Sivagangai (₹50-80 per kg), jute from Tamil Nadu jute suppliers (₹80-120 per kg), natural dyes from Madurai suppliers. Initial 500 kg batch ≈ ₹40,000-60,000.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3-5 registered artisans/SHG groups in Sivagangai through DC Handicraft office; start with product sampling (doormats, baskets, planters); invest in simple tools (beating mallets, looms) ≈ ₹50,000; quality check every batch.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, same-day) for MSE status; apply for IEC code at nearest APEDA office in Madurai (₹0, takes 7 days); create brand identity with eco-certifications (Indian organic, handmade labels); register on Udyam Assist for exports.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Handmade and Flipkart Artisan with 10 bestseller SKUs (doormats, plant pots, baskets); email 50 home décor retailers with samples; pitch to 5 corporate gifting agencies; create Instagram account with behind-the-scenes artisan content; list on Global Sources for export inquiries.
6
Scale
After ₹5L revenue, expand to 10-15 artisan partners; launch custom product line for B2B; set up export logistics with freight forwarders in Chennai; open presence at Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore retail markets; apply for SFURTI cluster support grant.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy for MSE startups in handicraft sector with 15-35% subsidy for loans.
🏛
PM Vishwakarma
free training + ₹15,000 advance + low-interest ₹1 lakh credit for traditional artisans; registration on portal essential.
🏚
Tamil Nadu Craftsmen Loan Scheme
soft loans up to ₹10 lakh at 4% interest for handicraft entrepreneurs; apply at nearest SIDCO office.
🏚
Artisan Marketing Services (AMS) by Ministry of Handicraft
free exhibition stalls and buyer-seller meets at trade fairs for registered DSOs and cooperatives.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
