Direct road access via NH-44 and NH-48 links to Chennai port (240 km) and Thoothukudi port (140 km), enabling cost-effective export of 13,42,843 MT coir products annually to 121 countries.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sivagangai ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sivagangai cluster (Manamadurai coir unit area) — call 2-3 established manufacturers, ask about current prices, production capacity, raw material sourcing. Register free Udyam account on udyamregistration.gov.in. Attend District Industries Centre (DIC) monthly entrepreneur meet in Sivagangai town — zero cost, 2 hours, get handbooks on SFURTI cluster support. Do this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw coconut husk from local traders near Manamadurai or directly from Kerala (Kochi wholesale market, 200 km away). First batch: 5 MT (₹15K-25K). Fiber extraction + twisting: partner with 3-5 experienced artisans in the cluster (they provide labor, you supply raw material). Or lease a small 1000 sqft unit in Sivagangai MSME cluster, install basic machinery (coir defibrator ₹1-2L, twisting machine ₹50K).
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (recommended, lower risk): Partner with established artisans in Sivagangai/Manamadurai — you source husk, they extract fiber and twist (₹20-30 per kg labor). Output: 100-150 kg fiber/day. Option B: Rent 1000-1500 sqft unit (₹8K-15K/month) near cluster, hire 2-3 workers (₹10K/month each), invest ₹2-4L in basic machinery. Start with yarn/fiber production, move to finished mats/geotextiles after 3-4 months.
4
Brand & Register
Day 1: Udyam registration (free, 30 min online). Month 1: Get PAN + TAN for business. Month 2: Apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) at nearest Directorate General of Foreign Trade office if planning exports — costs ₹0, processing 2-3 weeks. Coir is not GI-tagged in Sivagangai, so skip GI registration. File MSME certification immediately for govt scheme access. ISO 9001 optional (₹50K-1L, 8-12 weeks) if targeting premium export buyers.
5
First Sale
First sale: Contact 5 local furniture retailers in Madurai/Sivagangai, offer sample mats at wholesale price (₹300-500/mat, you make ₹100-150 margin). Simultaneously, list 3-5 product SKUs on Amazon Seller Central or Flipkart — first order likely ₹5K-15K. Target first month revenue: ₹50K-1.5L (mix of wholesale + D2C). Join cluster WhatsApp groups, attend monthly trader meets — relationships drive bulk orders.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: Invest ₹3-5L in semi-automated machinery (power loom mat machine ₹2-3L, defibrator upgrade). Hire 5-8 dedicated workers (₹12K-15K/month each). Rent dedicated 2000-3000 sqft unit. Launch direct B2B export — hire export agent or consolidator (commission 4-6%). Apply for SFURTI cluster subsidy (up to ₹25L capex support) to scale. Exhibit at India Habitat Centre (Delhi), Buyer-Seller Meet organized by ministry. Build Instagram/YouTube channel (production process, product durability).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
Up to ₹25 lakhs capex subsidy for cluster-based micro-units, 2-year validity, apply via District Industries Centre
🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
Up to ₹3 lakh collateral-free loans for traditional artisans and their dependents engaged in coir crafts, 0% interest for first 2 years
🏚
Tamil Nadu Handicraft Development Scheme
₹5 lakh subsidy for purchase of artisan tools and machinery, apply via DIC Sivagangai
🏚
MSME Export Credit Line
Dedicated low-interest credit (7-8% vs 10-12% market rate) for coir exporters to finance inventory and working capital, apply via nearest SIDBI branch
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Haan, bilkul. Coir products go to 121 countries — USA, UK, Germany, Australia top buyers. Process: Register IEC (free, 2 weeks). Find consolidator or small export agent (takes 4-6% commission). Package as per buyer specs. Ship via Thoothukudi Port (140 km away). Margins: ₹40-200/kg depending on product (fiber vs finished mat). Overall downside loss: ₹2-3L max if you quit in year 1. Not catastrophic.
