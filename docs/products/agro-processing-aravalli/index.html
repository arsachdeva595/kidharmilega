Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Aravalli ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Aravalli agricultural mandi (Modasa or Dhansura) on a market day, talk to 3-5 mango/tomato traders on pricing and seasonal supply patterns. Register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in). Call 2 existing food processing units in the district to understand licensing and cold storage rental costs. Cost: ₹0, time: 3-4 days.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source mangoes and tomatoes from Aravalli mandi or directly from farmer collectives in Dhansura and Modasa tehsils — negotiate ₹8-12 per kg for mangoes (bulk), ₹4-6 per kg for tomatoes. First batch: ₹40,000-60,000 for 5-6 tons. Aromatic rice from local farmers or through Agricultural Cooperative Societies (ACS) at ₹30-40 per kg.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 1,500-2,500 sq ft food processing unit with FSSAI license in a nearby industrial estate (start at ₹15,000-25,000/month). OR partner with 1-2 existing agro-processing artisans to co-produce your brand (revenue share 60:40). Install basic equipment: pulper, juice pasteurizer, filling machine (₹3-5L). FSSAI registration: ₹15,000-25,000, 30-45 days.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 15 mins online). Apply for PMDDKY subsidy (up to 20% capex subsidy, ₹1-1.5L approx) through district MSME office — requires project report (hire consultant ₹10,000-15,000). If exporting: Apply for IEC (Importer Exporter Code) via DGFT portal (₹0, 7 days). GI registration is optional but not critical for starting (₹5,000-10,000 if pursued later).
5
First Sale
First sale: List on Amazon Business or Swiggy Instamart with 1 product (mango juice or jam) — requires ₹50,000-100,000 inventory. Alternatively, contact 1 food service distributor in your city for wholesale first order (100-200 units at ₹25-35 per unit). First month target: 300-500 units, revenue ₹75,000-1,25,000.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: invest in branded packaging (₹50,000-100,000), hire 2-3 skilled packers and 1 sales rep. Apply for SFURTI cluster grant (up to ₹30L for group units) through District Industries Centre. Exhibit at Food Hotel India (Delhi, Mumbai) or IITF to connect with retail chains. Transition to co-packing partners for volume if D2C grows beyond 2,000 units/month.
