Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bajali ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bajali district rice clusters in Pathsala and Samagaun; connect with farming communities and existing ODOP rice producers; attend Assam Agricultural University workshops on rice variety selection and sustainable farming.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified rice seeds from State Seed Certification Agency (₹50,000-100,000 per quintal); secure farmland (leased or owned) or partner with farmer collectives; first-batch cost for 5 acres: ₹3-5 lakhs including seeds, labor, fertilizer.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established farmer groups in Bajali or operate your own 5-10 acre farm; invest in modern drying (solar dryers ₹50,000-200,000) and storage facilities; engage with local agricultural extension officers for best practices and crop monitoring.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam as agricultural producer; obtain IEC code from nearest Regional Export Promotion Council (REPC) office for export eligibility; secure FSSAI registration for processed/packaged rice; trademark your brand name with IP office; get organic certification if positioning premium.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh, Flipkart Supermart, BigBasket with minimum 50kg orders; create Instagram/Facebook business pages showcasing farm-to-table narrative; pitch to Assamese restaurants and cafes in Guwahati and metropolitan cities; attend food expos in Delhi, Mumbai; connect with export traders for bulk orders.
6
Scale
Expand to 20-50 acres or contract farming model with 50+ farmers; diversify into parboiled and organic variants; establish direct wholesale relationships with regional retail chains; build export partnerships with Singapore, UK, Canada; invest in processing unit for value-added products; target institutional buyers (schools, hospitals, hotels).
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy for agricultural processing and marketing units in Bajali district
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
support for rice processing clusters with machinery subsidies up to 35-50%
🏚
Assam Chief Minister's Agricultural Development Scheme
incentives for modern farming, certification, and export of premium rice varieties
🏚
Assam ODOP Promotion Scheme
direct subsidies for packaging, branding, and digital marketing of registered ODOP rice producers
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
