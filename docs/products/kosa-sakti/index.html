opportunity, Sakti mein.

Sakti district (160,088 hectares, 6.47 lakh population, 73.07% literacy) is located on NH-49 in central Chhattisgarh, 180 km from Nagpur and 280 km from Bhopal. The Sakti block (headquarters) and Malkharoud block house approximately 400-500 registered and informal Kosa weavers using traditional pit looms, with access to raw silk suppliers in neighboring Durg and Rajnandgaon districts. Buyer proximity includes high-volume wedding markets in Nagpur, Indore, and Bhopal, plus direct e-commerce channels reaching Delhi and metros within 3-5 days.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sakti ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call Sakti Kosa Weavers Cooperative (contact via DC Handloom office), visit Sakti weekly haat on Tuesday-Saturday mornings, speak directly to 3-4 master weavers about their production capacity and pricing. Register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) using Aadhaar and PAN — zero cost, 10 minutes online.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw silk from registered suppliers in Durg district (30 km away) at ₹1,200-1,800 per kg. Visit Durg silk market (near Rajnandgaon Road) and negotiate bulk rates. First batch of 10 sarees: ₹60,000-90,000 (silk + artisan labor). Connect via DC Handloom office for supplier introductions — they maintain vendor list.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Partnership model — provide raw silk to 4-5 master weavers in Sakti block on consignment, pay ₹3,500-5,500 per saree labor (14-18 days per saree). Setup cost: ₹0 (use their looms). Option B: Rent a 400-500 sqft unit near Sakti bus stand (₹8,000-12,000/month) with 2-3 rented pit looms (₹40,000-60,000), hire 2-3 weavers at ₹12,000-15,000 monthly. Total Option B cost: ₹140,000-200,000 for 3 months.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 10 min). Legally register as Proprietorship under Shop & Establishment Act (₹2,000, 5 days at Sakti municipal office). Apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) if targeting export — free via DGFT portal, 15 days. GI registration not applicable (Kosa not GI-tagged in Sakti yet, but registration support available via Chhattisgarh Handloom Board — ₹15,000 cost, 6 months). Implement production quality control: ISO 9001 consultancy (optional, ₹25,000).
5
First Sale
Launch on Meesho Supplier app — upload 5 Kosa saree listings with professional photos (₹3,000 for shoot) and 1-2 sample orders. First order: 2-3 sarees, ₹22,000-35,000 invoice. Meesho commission 15-25%. Alternatively, approach Delhi Chandni Chowk wholesaler (Bhagirath Palace area) — first order minimum 5 sarees, ₹75,000-120,000. Expected first-month revenue: ₹45,000-80,000 (2-3 Meesho orders) or single wholesale order of ₹75,000+.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire a dedicated e-commerce manager (₹18,000/month) and quality-checker (₹12,000/month). Expand weaver partnerships to 8-10 artisans across Malkharoud and Jaijapur blocks. Participate in Pravah Craft Fair (Bhopal), India Handloom Expo (Delhi), Fabindia's annual seller conclave. Invest ₹60,000 in studio photography for seasonal collections. Set up wholesale showroom in Nagpur (₹50,000/month rent negotiable) to capture walk-in bridal market.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
₹15,000 loan at 0% interest for first 2 years, plus ₹500/month tooling allowance, plus free PMKVY training for artisans (if hiring). Apply at nearest bank in Sakti.
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25% subsidy on machinery up to ₹25 lakh (₹6.25 lakh max subsidy), applicable if registering as partnership/proprietary after business registration.
🏚
Chhattisgarh Handloom Development Scheme
₹50,000 assistance for new handloom units (looms, tools, initial raw material). Apply via DC Handloom office, Sakti.
🏚
Chhattisgarh Chief Minister's Viswakarma Samarth Yojana
₹100,000 subsidy + ₹500/month pocket allowance for registered craftspeople and their descendants starting new units. Processing: 30-45 days via block administration.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
