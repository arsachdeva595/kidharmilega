Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sonipat ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sonipat Food Processing Cluster (SFPC) near Kundli, meet 15-20 existing processors, understand licensing (FSSAI, BIS), conduct market research at ITC Agrotech Park and study competitor pricing on Amazon Fresh and BigBasket.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from Nuh mandis (vegetables), Jind grain markets (cereals), and Baddi spice suppliers; first batch procurement cost ₹2-4 lakhs for pilot production of 1000-2000 units.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with SFPC-based contract manufacturers (50% cheaper than setting up own unit initially) or lease 1000-2000 sqft unit in Kundli FSSAI-approved zones; hire food technologist for formulation.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, free), apply FSSAI license (₹5000-10000, 21 days), obtain IEC code from nearby DGFT office (Delhi); design packaging with QR code linking to farmer stories or nutritional info.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh, BigBasket, Grofers via vendor registration (28% commission); simultaneously approach modern retail chains with samples; email 50 corporate canteen managers in Gurgaon-Noida with B2B pricing.
6
Scale
After ₹50L cumulative sales, shift to owned 5000 sqft facility, expand SKU portfolio by 3-4 products, hire sales team for HoReCa channel, invest in Google Shopping ads and Instagram influencer partnerships targeting health-conscious mothers.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakhs grant (35% subsidy for SC/ST, 25% for general) for food processing unit setup
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹10 lakhs per unit for common facility centers in food processing clusters
🏚
Haryana Agro-Processing and Food Tech Development Scheme
25% capital subsidy for food processing units with FSSAI license in identified zones
🏚
Haryana Food Processing Skill Development Program
free training and placement assistance for production staff, equipment leasing at 50% discount rate
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
