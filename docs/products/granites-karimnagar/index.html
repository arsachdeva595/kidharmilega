Karimnagar sits on one of India's richest granite deposits with over 200+ active quarries producing high-quality black, pink, and multi-coloured granite. The district has 50+ years of mining expertise, established supply chains, skilled craftsmen, and proximity to Hyderabad's tile/construction hubs. Natural geological advantages combined with competitive labour costs make it ideal for value-added granite processing.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Karimnagar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Karimnagar's main granite cluster around Jangaon & Wadiepalli areas; meet 5-10 quarry owners and processing unit operators; study Hyderabad's granite distribution hubs (Abids, Madhapur); attend construction expos like ISM (International Stone & Marble) held in Hyderabad annually.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw granite blocks from local Karimnagar quarries (₹3-5 Cr for 6-month supply agreement); negotiate 30-60 day payment terms; visit reputed sawing equipment suppliers in Bangalore or Chennai for diamond-wire machinery (₹40-80 L per unit).
3
Produce / Partner
Lease 5,000-10,000 sq ft processing unit in Karimnagar industrial zone (₹50-100 L annually); hire 8-12 skilled craftsmen (₹15,000-25,000/month); partner with local artisans under revenue-share model or hire equipment operator for saw-cutting, polishing, and edge-finishing workflows.
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam (MSME portal) immediately; apply for IEC code (DGFT) for export eligibility; create brand identity — design logo, get GST registration (18% on granite products); secure ISO 9001 certification for quality assurance (₹2-5 L); register DIN/PAN for company formation.
5
First Sale
List on IndiaMART & Exim Intra for B2B buyers; join Telangana MSME e-marketplace; contact 50+ architects via LinkedIn with sample portfolios; approach real estate companies directly (Prestige, DLF, Sobha, Lodha); partner with 3-5 regional distributors in metro cities; showcase at India Build Hardware Expo.
6
Scale
After ₹1 Cr quarterly revenue, invest in advanced CNC profiling machines; expand to 2-3 processing units; build export logistics via freight forwarders in Port cities (JNPT Mumbai, Mundra, Chennai); hire sales team for institutional clients; develop premium finishes (anti-slip, textured, thermaled) for differentiation.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 L subsidy (15-35% of project cost) for manufacturing setup; covers machinery, working capital, and building rent for 5 years.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹10-50 L cluster development support for granite clusters; includes common facility centers, branding, and export promotion.
🏚
Telangana Udyam Scheme
₹10 L concessional loan at 5% interest for MSME startups in manufacturing sector with subsidy on interest for 3 years.
🏚
Telangana T-FIBER (Technology Focused Infrastructure for Business Enhancement)
₹5 Cr allocation for stone processing clusters; grants for modernizing equipment and export logistics infrastructure.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
