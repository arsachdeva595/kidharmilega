Bidari ware ki
opportunity, Bidar mein.

Bidar has been the historical seat of Bidriware craft for over 500 years, with established clusters of master artisans and hereditary knowledge of the specialized alloy composition and oxidation techniques. The district's proximity to raw material suppliers and existing ecosystem of skilled craftsmen makes it the authentic and most viable production center for this art form.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bidar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bidar Handicraft Cluster in old city, spend 2-3 weeks learning from established master artisans like those in Azad Nagar and Takht Bazaar; study GI certification requirements through DC Handicraft office in Bidar
2
Source Raw Materials
Source zinc alloy ingots from Bangalore metal suppliers (₹30-40K per 50kg batch); silver foil from Jaipur bullion dealers (₹5-8K per 100gm); engraving tools from Chennai handicraft tool suppliers; first batch setup cost ₹75-100K
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3-5 established artisan families in Bidar old city on contract basis (₹8-15K per artisan per month for skilled labor); alternatively, lease small workshop in existing handicraft cluster for ₹5-8K monthly and train semi-skilled workers
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam as micro-enterprise (online at udyamregistration.gov.in); apply for IEC from nearest DGFT office in Bangalore or local customs; register trademark for brand name; apply for GI sub-licensing as authorized producer
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy (20-30% commission, high tourist/international traffic), Flipkart Handmade (8% commission), own Shopify store; initiate outreach to luxury gift aggregators like FNP, corporate bulk buyers; approach 5-star hotels in Bangalore and Mumbai with curated collections
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand artisan network to 10-15 workers; establish institutional buyers (corporate gifting for 50+ companies); launch curated collections on Ajio and Amazon Artisans; explore B2B export via TradeKey and Global Sources; establish workshop tourism (₹500-1000 per visitor experience)
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma
up to ₹15,000 toolkit grant for artisans + interest-free loans up to ₹1 lakh for traditional craft workers
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh loan at subsidized rates for micro-enterprises in handicraft sector
🏚
Karnataka Handicraft Development Scheme
subsidized workshop space in identified clusters + export promotion grants up to ₹5 lakh for GI-tagged products
🏚
Karnataka State Artisan Welfare Board
skill upgrade training grants + health insurance for traditional craftspeople + design innovation support
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
