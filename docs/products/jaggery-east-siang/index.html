Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — East Siang ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Pasighat (district HQ) and connect with existing jaggery producers in East Siang Jaggery clusters. Meet 5-7 established manufacturers, study their production yield (800-1200 kg per 100 quintals sugarcane), cost structure, and distribution. Visit Dibrugarh sugarcane market in Assam to understand wholesale pricing trends (₹35-50 per kg retail, ₹20-28 per kg wholesale).
2
Source Raw Materials
Source sugarcane from local farmers in Pasighat taluk during October-March season. Negotiate bulk rates (₹4,500-6,500 per quintal). First batch: 50 quintals sugarcane yields ~500-600 kg jaggery, requires ₹2.5-3.5 lakhs. Connect with IFFCO for lime and packaging suppliers in Guwahati (250 km away) for bulk jute bags and labels.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing jaggery makers in Pasighat initially (contract manufacturing model) to avoid high capex. Alternatively, set up small unit in Pasighat with 2-3 open pan boilers, requiring ₹8-12 lakhs. Learn traditional open-pan jaggery-making; recruit 2-4 skilled workers from local jaggery cooperatives. Aim for 500-800 kg monthly production in year 1.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) within 10 days online. Apply for IEC code via InDEXTER portal for export readiness. Design packaging with FPO certification readiness. Register brand with State Food Authority (Arunachal Pradesh) for food safety compliance. Get FSSAI registration (₹2,000-5,000). Consider GI certification filing later if quality benchmarks met.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon, Flipkart, and JioMart marketplace with ₹500-800 per kg pricing. Approach regional health food retailers in Guwahati, Assam and nearby states. Partner with 3-5 cafes in Guwahati and Itanagar for bulk supply. Create LinkedIn and Instagram content showcasing traditional production, mineral benefits, and farm-to-table story. Target organic product communities via Facebook groups.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to 1,500-2,000 kg monthly production. Build direct relationship with 10-15 B2B buyers (bakeries, food processors). Launch value-added product line (jaggery powder with turmeric, ginger variants). Pursue export certifications (Organic, ISO 22000) to enter Nepal, Bangladesh, UAE markets. Establish co-packing partnerships in Guwahati to reach Assam wholesale markets efficiently.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Program)
up to ₹25 lakhs non-refundable subsidy (35% in plain areas) for food processing micro-enterprises
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹5 lakhs cluster-based production support and market linkage in East Siang jaggery sector
🏚
Arunachal Pradesh State Agricultural Promotion Scheme
50% subsidy on processing equipment and packaging for food processing MSME startups
🏚
Invest Arunachal Initiative
land and workspace incentives plus registration support for manufacturing enterprises in identified food processing clusters
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

  • Launch WhatsApp Business catalog with 2-3 bulk packs (500g, 1kg, 5kg). Build distributor network in Assam/Meghalaya by cold-calling wholesale buyers on WhatsApp with village-origin storytelling and price consistency guarantees.
