Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kasaragod ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kasaragod Cashew Research Station and KCMF (Kasaragod Cooperative Marketing Federation) clusters in Payyanur and Kasaragod town. Spend 5-7 days learning from 5-6 established processors; budget ₹10,000-15,000. Join KCCI (Kasaragod Chamber of Commerce) for market intelligence.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw kernels from KCMF auctions (₹400-500/kg) or direct farmers in Kasaragod taluk. Order first 100kg batch (₹50,000-60,000 cost) plus packaging at ₹15,000. Contact suppliers: KCMF Payyanur (0498-2922222) or local traders near Kasaragod railway station.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with SFURTI cluster processors (reduces setup cost by 70%) or rent co-processing space at ₹25,000-40,000/month in Payyanur cashew hub. Alternatively, invest ₹15-20L in a small 500kg/day roasting and packaging unit with IQF technology.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam on udyamregistration.gov.in (free). Apply IEC code at customs office in Kasaragod port area (₹1,000). Register brand name with IP office. Obtain FSSAI registration (₹5,000-10,000) and state food license from Kasaragod health dept. Get BIS certification for packaged foods.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Pantry, Flipkart Supermart, and BigBasket (2-week onboarding). Simultaneously target regional chains like Reliance Fresh and Kirana partners. Send samples to 20 gift shops and hotel chains in Kerala. List on 1mg, Blink, and regional marketplaces. Budget ₹50,000 for initial listing and logistics setup.
6
Scale
After 6 months, launch 3-4 SKU variants (honey-roasted, dark chocolate, spiced blends). Establish presence in 50+ retail outlets. Export to 2-3 countries via APEDA (Agricultural Products Export Development Authority). Build D2C email list of 10,000+ customers. Introduce subscription boxes. Partner with corporates for bulk gifting (20-30% higher margins).
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy (25% for general, 35% for SC/ST) for food processing startups in tier 2/3 districts
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
free cluster support including common processing, certification, market linkage for cashew clusters in Kasaragod
🏚
Kerala Startup Mission (KSUM)
₹5-10 lakh grant and mentoring for food-tech and agri-processing startups plus incubation support
🏚
Cashew Industry Promotion Scheme (Kerala)
subsidized interest rates (2-3% reduction) for working capital and machinery investment up to ₹50 lakh for MSME cashew processors
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
