Anand district has a rich geological heritage with naturally occurring agate deposits in specific zones around Khambhat and surrounding areas. The region's skilled artisan community, combined with access to high-quality raw agate stones and established trade routes to gem markets, makes it uniquely positioned for agate cutting, polishing, and value-added products.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Anand ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Khambhat agate cluster (30km from Anand) and connect with 200+ existing agate artisans; attend Gem & Jewellery Council meetings in Ahmedabad; study GI certification standards from DC Handicraft ministry office in Gandhinagar.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw agate blocks from licensed mines around Khambhat (₹10,000-25,000 per kg for premium grade); establish relationships with 2-3 suppliers; first batch of 50kg costs ₹50,000-75,000; also source polishing compounds and lapidary tools from Jaipur gem markets.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3-5 registered agate artisans in Khambhat cluster (₹15,000-30,000/month per artisan); or set up small lapidary workshop with polishing wheel, cutting tools, and curing station (capital ₹3-5 lakhs); begin with 20-30 pieces/month production.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME portal), apply for IEC code via DGFT portal, register trademark with IP India, apply for GI certification use (existing tag valid through 2025), obtain Handicraft Certificate from DC Handicraft, open GST account (5% applicable).
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy, Amazon Handmade, and Flipkart Artisan with 50+ product photos; approach 10-15 gift retailers in Mumbai/Bangalore; list on iShoppy (e-commerce for ODOP); partner with wellness/spiritual stores; create Instagram content showing polishing process and raw-to-finished journey.
6
Scale
After ₹10L revenue (6 months), hire 2 more artisans, invest in electric polishing equipment (₹2L), expand D2C presence to Shopify + Pinterest, approach luxury hotel chains for bulk corporate gifting, apply for SFURTI cluster development grant, establish wholesale partnerships with 5-10 metro jewelry/décor chains.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy for manufacturing unit setup with 5-10 jobs creation.
🏛
PM Vishwakarma
direct artisan support up to ₹15,000 per beneficiary for skill development and ₹1 lakh interest-free loan.
🏚
Gujarat ODOP Export Promotion Scheme
20% subsidy on packaging, branding, and export logistics for GI-tagged products.
🏚
Gujarat Handicraft Development Fund
concessional credit up to ₹50 lakhs at 3% interest for handicraft MSMEs in registered clusters.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
