Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Thiruvallur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Thiruvallur SIDCO Industrial Estate (free entry) and walk the cluster — call 5 existing component makers, ask about their certification path, customer base, and machinery costs. Download ODOP Thiruvallur handbook from tn.gov.in. Register free on Udyam portal. Cost: ₹0, time: 2 days.
2
Source Raw Materials
Machinery sourcing from GIDC Gandhinagar (Gujarat) or used auction markets — typical CNC, lathe, surface grinder setup ₹25-50L. Raw steel sourcing from Tata Steel Jamshedpur or JSW Steel via Chennai distributors (₹300-400/kg). First batch ₹15-20L. Supplier area: Thiruvallur town, Oragadam SIPCOT cluster.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 2000-3000 sqft unit in SIDCO Industrial Estate Thiruvallur or Oragadam for ₹30-50K/month. Machinery setup ₹25-50L (CNC, lathe, quality tools). Electrical connection ₹2-3L, water/waste setup ₹5L. Total first-year: ₹80-120L for equipment, ₹2-3L annual rent. Hire 8-12 skilled operators (₹12-18K per month each).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 10 min). Apply for ISO9001 and TS16949 certification (₹3-5L, 3-4 months through TCS or BIS). Apply for IEC code from DGFT (₹5K, 1 week). File for brand trademark at IPO (₹4.5K-10K). Total: ₹4-6L, 4-5 months for full certification.
5
First Sale
First customer: approach Tier-1 suppliers (Sundram Fasteners, Caparo, Motherson Sumi) via LinkedIn or trade shows — typical first PO ₹2-5L. Use platforms: Tradeindia (paid listing ₹3K/month), IndiaMART (premium ₹5K/month), Global Sources (export trade magazine). First month realistic revenue ₹5-8L after 3-4 months of sourcing.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire quality engineer and sales person (₹25K each). Apply for PMEGP subsidy (₹25L for SC/ST or 25% for general, up to ₹10L equipment cost). Exhibit at Auto Component Makers Association (ACMA) trade shows, seek TS16949 audits for OEM readiness. Target export at ₹20L+/month by year 2.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25-35% subsidy on fixed asset investment, max ₹25L loan with ₹5-10L subsidy for general category
🏛
MUDRA Yojana
₹10L collateral-free working capital at 8-9% interest via SBI, ICICI, or regional banks
🏚
Tamil Nadu MSMEs Cluster Development Scheme
₹1-2Cr support for common facility centers, skill training, and marketing in engineering clusters
🏚
Tamil Nadu Udyam Sakti Fund
0% interest working capital and machinery finance up to ₹50L for manufacturing units in notified clusters
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
