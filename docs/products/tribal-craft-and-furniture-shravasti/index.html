Tribal craft and Furniture ki
opportunity, Shravasti mein.

Shravasti is a Tier 3 district in eastern Uttar Pradesh, 180 km from Lucknow, with a forest area of 34,238 hectares supplying locally-grown teakwood, rosewood, acacia (babool), and neem wood. The Tharu tribe community has established craft production across the district with handmade items including cloth sheets, embroidered covers, and tablecloths alongside furniture. Locally-made furniture is used in every ceremony organized in the city and has been displayed at national-level exhibitions, indicating both domestic and export validation.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Shravasti ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Shravasti town weekly haat and Tharu craft clusters (ask District DC office for location). Call 3 local furniture makers directly — ask them about their suppliers, pricing, and production timelines. Register free on Udyam MSME portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) using your Aadhar. Zero cost, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source wood from local suppliers in Shravasti town (teakwood ₹40-60/kg, acacia ₹18-25/kg, neem ₹20-30/kg). First batch: order 500 kg mixed wood = ₹12-15K. Identify 2-3 artisans in craft clusters who can work on commission (₹200-400 per piece depending on complexity). Transport via local trucking = ₹2-3K per batch.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent a 400-500 sq ft workshop in Shravasti town at ₹3-4K/month, buy basic tools (chisels, saws, sanders) for ₹30-40K. Option B: Partner with 2-3 established artisans on commission basis (₹8-12K/month retainer). Start with 10-15 pieces/month. Total first-month production cost: ₹35-50K.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (free, 5 min). Apply for Ambedkar Hastshilp Vikas Yojana subsidy (₹3-5L for working capital/tools) via DC Handicraft office. If exporting: get IEC (Importer Exporter Code) from DGFT = ₹0 online, 7 days. Trademark brand name = ₹4.5K, 6-8 months.
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy (6% fee) and Instagram Shop (free tier). First customer: 2-3 pieces to friends/local event planners at ₹8-12K per piece. Target first-month revenue ₹20-25K from 2-3 sales. Use photos in WhatsApp status and local Facebook groups targeting wedding planners.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 2 skilled artisans (₹8K/month each), rent larger workshop (600 sq ft, ₹5K/month), invest ₹50K in photography/content. Exhibit at Delhi Handicraft Expo and National Craft Fair. Add 3-4 online channels. Partner with wedding planners in major cities via direct calls.
