Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Gurugram ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Udyog Vihar or Manesar cluster on a Tuesday/Wednesday, spend 2 hours talking to 3-4 unit owners (no appointment needed), call Modelama Export Pvt Ltd's public line to ask about market trends, attend Gurugram Chamber of Commerce monthly meet (free) — do this within 7 days.
2
Source Raw Materials
Non-woven fabric suppliers in Manesar industrial area (₹80-120 per kg), elastic/nose clip vendors in Udyog Vihar (₹200-400 per 1000 units), sealing machines from authorized importers in Gurgaon (₹8-15L per machine). First batch of 5,000 units will cost ₹2.5-4L for raw materials at this volume.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 1500-2000 sqft unit in Udyog Vihar or Manesar at ₹8-12 per sqft (₹1.2-2.4L/month) or partner with existing units for job work (10-15% margin). Two sealing machines + tables + cutters = ₹15-25L capex. With 5 workers, produce 500-800 kits/day starting month 2.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (free, 5 mins), apply for MSME recognition (₹0, 15 days), obtain IEC from DGFT Gurugram office (₹0, 10 days if export-bound), get BIS certification for PPE kits (₹3-5L, 4-6 weeks), register GST (free, online). Total formal setup cost: ₹3-5L and 6-8 weeks.
5
First Sale
First order: Contact 5 diagnostic chains directly (Apollo, Fortis, SRL labs), propose ₹50 per kit wholesale for 1000-2000 unit monthly orders. Use LinkedIn to pitch hospitals' procurement heads. First month realistic revenue: ₹4-8L (100-200 kits/day × ₹40-50). Second order from government tenders (use MSME portal) can yield ₹10-25L per order.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue, hire dedicated export/compliance officer (₹25-40k/month), upgrade to automated sealing (₹20L more), add a packaging/quality unit (₹5L), apply for AEPC export credit support (5-7% subsidy), target 20,000+ units/month by month 12. Exhibit at AAMI India Medical Device Expo (May), Texfair Delhi (Sept).
