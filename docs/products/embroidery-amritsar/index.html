Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Amritsar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Hall Bazaar embroidery cluster (30 min from city center, free). Spend 2 hours observing artisan workshops. Call 5 suppliers: Bhagirath Palace thread dealers (Ph: ask at local haat). Attend Amritsar Craft Haat on Saturday mornings (free entry). Register on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in, 15 min, free). Message 3 embroidery makers on Instagram asking their sourcing costs. Zero cost, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from Hall Bazaar thread merchants and Bhagirath Palace fabric wholesalers (Amritsar walled city). First batch cost: ₹15,000-25,000 for 20-30 pieces of mixed embroidery stock (threads, fabrics, needles). Get quotes from at least 3 suppliers. Supplier names: Raja Silk House, Sharma Threads, Gupta Embroidery Supplies (all in Hall Bazaar). Lead time: 3-7 days. Ask about 10% bulk discount at ₹20K+ first order.
3
Produce / Partner
Two models: (A) Rent a 250 sq ft workspace in Bhagat Singh Nagar MSME cluster at ₹6,000-10,000/month + hire 3-4 part-time artisans at ₹300-500 per piece output. (B) Partner with a master embroiderer in the walled city on revenue-share (you handle design + sales, they handle 60-80% of stitching). Model A setup: ₹40,000-60,000 initial (rent deposit, basic tools, first material stock). Model B: ₹15,000-20,000 (design software, sample materials, marketing). Expect 20-40 pieces/month from Model A, 10-20 from Model B in month 1.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (udyam.gov.in, free, 20 min). Apply for IEC code at Amritsar DGC office (Ravi Nagar) if planning export — ₹0 cost, 5-day processing. Register trademark with IP India (₹4,500 for Class 25 apparel — 6 weeks processing). Skip GI registration initially (costs ₹40,000+ and takes 3+ years; Amritsar embroidery has no GI tag yet). Get ISI mark optional (₹10,000-15,000 for textiles, if selling to corporates). Open HDFC/ICICI business account (₹0). Total registrations by month 1: ₹4,500.
5
First Sale
List 15-20 SKUs on Etsy (free listing for first 40 items, then $0.20/listing). Set first pieces at ₹1,200-2,500 (cushion covers, dupattas). Post 5 reels/week on Instagram (product photography, artisan at work, design process). Join 'Craft & Design' Facebook groups and WhatsApp seller collectives (free). Send cold emails to 20 corporate gifting companies in Delhi/Mumbai with wholesale price list and Instagram link (free tool: mailchimp). First sale expected week 3-4 at ₹1,500-3,500 gross. Target: ₹25,000-40,000 in month 1 revenue (first 15-25 pieces sold).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire a full-time operations lead (₹12,000/month) to manage supplier coordination. Expand to 8-10 artisans. Invest in 2-3 semi-automatic embroidery machines (₹1.2L each, used market in Ludhiana) for faster bulk orders. List on Amazon India (3% commission) and Amazon US (15% + shipping). Start monthly Instagram Lives with artisans. Attend India Craft Fair (December, New Delhi) and Handcraft Exporters Fair. Apply for SFURTI subsidy (₹10L cluster development grant) to formalize your workspace. Launch bulk order template for B2B. Export 2-3 containers/month to UK/US distributors via DHL or sea freight.

Government Schemes

PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
₹10-25 lakh subsidy (25% for general, 35% for SC/ST) for setting up embroidery unit, processing via KVIC/KVIB, 6-month disbursement
🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹15,000 one-time skilling grant + ₹5L collateral-free loan at 5% interest for traditional artisans, enrollment via pmvishwakarma.gov.in
🏚
Punjab Udyam Protshan Scheme
₹2 lakh subsidy for new MSME in handicraft sector, 20% of capex, applied via Punjab Industries department
🏚
Punjab Handloom Export Promotion Scheme
15% subsidy on FOB value for textile/embroidery exports, processed via DC Handloom

Frequently Asked Questions

Seedhe sawaal, seedhe jawab

Minimum ₹1.5 lakh se shuru kar sakte ho (artisan partnership model). ₹3-5 lakh mein proper workspace + 4-5 artisans ke saath better setup hota hai. ₹5-7.5 lakh se semi-automated machines bhi le sakte ho. Pehle ₹50,000 se sample batch banao, dekho kya baik ho raha hai, phir expand karo.
Stitching khaad experience zaruri nahi. Lekin ₹2 lakh ke andar shuru karo toh experienced artisan ke saath partnership better rahega. Agar ₹5+ lakh invest kar rahe ho toh aap operations handle kar sakte ho aur artisans ko manage kar sakte ho. YouTube + 30 din ka practice aur basic designs seekh sakte ho. Hall Bazaar wale masters ko 1-2 lakh deke ek din ka workshop de sakte hain.
PMEGP se ₹10-25 lakh subsidy milti hai (25-35% of capex). SFURTI se cluster development ke liye ₹10 lakh grant. PM Vishwakarma se ₹15,000 skilling grant + ₹5 lakh loan. Punjab Udyam Protshan: ₹2 lakh subsidy. Export karo toh 15% FOB subsidy Punjab Handloom se. Udyam registration free hai. Trademark ₹4,500. Subsidy ke liye KVIC office Amritsar ya DC Handloom se contact karo.
Etsy + Instagram. Instagram par crafty audience bahut active hai. Pehla order Etsy se expect karo (3-4 weeks) ya cold email corporate gifting companies ko (2-3 weeks). Hall Bazaar mein retailers bhi laga sakte ho (wholesale at 40% discount). Meesho bhi acha channel hai. Expect month 1-2 mein ₹25-40K revenue agar seriously Instagram pe post karo.
Bilkul ho sakta hai. USA, UK, Canada, Germany, Australia, UAE — sab mein demand hai. IEC code free se mil jaata hai (5 days). Etsy pe direct ship kar sakte ho USA/UK ko. Bulk orders ke liye airfreight (DHL/FedEx) ya seafreight (Kandla port via Mumbai). FOB price ₹1,500-3,000 per piece, buyer ka profit 40-55%. Pehla export shipment 1-2 months lagta hai organize karne mein.
Honest baat: embroidery artisans ki demand Amritsar mein high hai. Agar D2C nahi chale toh wholesale retailers ko supply kar sakte ho. Agar artisan partnership liya tha toh wo artisan kisi aur ko supply kar sakte hain — aapka loss sirf initial inventory ka hoga (₹30-50K). Equipment resell kar sakte ho 60-70% mein Ludhiana trade mein. Lekin agar quality control theek rakhega aur marketing karoge toh 70% chance profitable setup ka hai. Phir bhi 1-2 saal commitment rakho, zyada kuch hope mat karo pehle 6 months mein.

Marketing

Ye marketing ideas try karna mat bhulna

  • Instagram Reels: 60-second videos of embroidery process (needle close-ups, color progression, artisan hands). Post 4x/week with hashtags #embroideryart #handmadeinindia #slowfashion. Hook: 'Watch 2 hours of stitching in 60 seconds.' Aim for 50-100 saves/week.
  • Etsy shop page: High-res photography (white background, flat lay, lifestyle shot with product in use). Write detailed descriptions naming thread colors and regional design inspiration. Run Etsy Ads at ₹2,000/month targeting 'embroidery decor' and 'Indian handicraft' keywords in USA/UK.
  • WhatsApp bulk messaging: Collect B2B buyer emails (corporate gifting, interior designers, fashion boutiques). Send monthly catalog with 5 new designs and bulk pricing. Use WhatsApp Business API (₹500-1,000/month) to automate order confirmations and shipping updates.
