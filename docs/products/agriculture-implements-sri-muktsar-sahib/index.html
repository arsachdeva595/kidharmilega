Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sri Muktsar Sahib ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Malout and Muktsar town (2-day trip, ₹500 fuel cost) — walk through Gill Agricultural Implements facility, meet 2-3 competitor unit owners, attend the bi-weekly agricultural implement market at Muktsar Mandi, call 5 farmers asking why they bought their last thresher. Zero cost: call Sri Muktsar Sahib MSME office and request list of registered units. This week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Steel suppliers in Ludhiana (80km away) and Mohali; ball bearing suppliers in Chandigarh; cast iron foundries in Mandi Gobindgarh. First-batch cost for 5 units of one implement type: ₹2-3.5L depending on model. Source from established suppliers already serving Gill Agricultural Implements — they know the spec.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 2000-3500 sq ft workshop shed in Malout or Muktsar (₹4-8K/month). Partner with 2-3 skilled welders and assemblers (monthly cost ₹15-25K each) or hire 5-8 semi-skilled workers at ₹10-12K/month plus provident fund. Turnaround: 15-25 days per unit. Estimated monthly overhead: ₹50-70K. Alternatively, subcontract 40-60% fabrication to existing units and manage assembly in-house.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online at udyamregistration.gov.in, 15 mins). Apply for One District One Product (ODOP) scheme subsidy via Sri Muktsar Sahib District Industries Centre (₹50K-2L depending on category). GI registration not applicable but pursue ISO 9001:2015 certification (₹40-60K, 3 months) to open export doors. Apply for IEC code via DGFT Chandigarh office (free, 7 days) if exporting.
5
First Sale
First sale channel: register on Agribusiness platform (AgroStar, DeHaat, or local Khet.com), list 1-2 thresher models with 5-7 photos. Post 1 demo video on YouTube (free, 3 mins, show threshing speed and durability). Expected first order: 2-4 units from neighbouring district dealer within 2-3 weeks of launch. First-month revenue target: ₹1.5-2.5L (2-3 units sold at ₹80-120K each).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (8-10 units/month): hire production supervisor (₹20K), accountant part-time (₹8K), and 2 additional assemblers. Attend 2-3 state-level agricultural machinery exhibitions (Chandigarh, Ambala, Hissar) per year (₹80-150K per booth). Join Punjab MSME exporters' association and participate in government-organised export missions to Canada, Australia (₹2-4L yearly investment). Build dealer network in Haryana, Himachal by offering 10-15% margin to resellers.
