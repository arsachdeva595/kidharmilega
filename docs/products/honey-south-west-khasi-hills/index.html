Honey ki
opportunity, South West Khasi Hills mein.

South West Khasi Hills district, elevation 800-1500m in Northeast India, borders Bangladesh; forest cover spans 65% of area with continuous wildflower blooms April-October and secondary blooms November-February. Mawkyrwat and Nongstoin are 35-45km from Shillong (₹200-300 daily buyer demand for honey), and 120km from Guwahati (₹2000+ daily institutional demand). 15-20 active beekeepers currently supply fragmented local markets; no organized export cluster exists but Apiculture Mission 2.0 subsidizes 50% of equipment for clusters of 5+ beekeepers.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — South West Khasi Hills ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Mawkyrwat haat on Wednesday mornings to speak with existing beekeepers (ask for Smti. Neirya Shira, Nodal Officer — contact 6396119745); attend one Apiculture Mission 2.0 information session at District Agriculture Office (contact Smti. G.R Khongwir, 9402134301); call 2-3 honey suppliers in Shillong to understand wholesale pricing and order volumes. Zero cost, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source 20-25 Apis mellifera colonies from beekeepers in Mawkyrwat, Nongstoin, or Khliehriat blocks at ₹3,000-4,500 per hive. First-batch cost ₹60-90K for colonies. Wooden hives (10 units) from local carpenters near Nongstoin ₹8-12K per unit (total ₹80-120K). Honey extractor (manual/motorized) ₹15-30K. Total raw material procurement: ₹1.55-2.40L.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (recommended for first-time): Partner with 2-3 existing beekeepers in Mawkyrwat cluster who own land and colonies; you provide processing, packaging, branding, and marketing. Share revenue 40% beekeeper / 60% you. Rent honey processing unit (licensed FSSAI) in Nongstoin industrial area, ₹5-8K/month. Option B (higher control): Lease 1-2 acres near Mawkyrwat or Khliehriat at ₹2-4K/month, set up apiaries + processing shed (₹3-5L one-time), hire 1 apiary technician at ₹8K/month. Estimated production cost per kg: ₹80-120 (including labor, power, processing).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) free online at udyamregistration.gov.in within 1 week (enables GST, bank loans, subsidies). Apply for PM FMPE Scheme (₹6L subsidy on equipment, 20% of ₹30L cap if you plan processing + packaging setup) through District Commerce (Shri. M. War, 7005564339). Register for FoodLicense from Mawkyrwat Municipal Office (₹3-5K, 15 days). Apply for GI registration under Meghalaya Geographical Indications Act (pending status — coordinate with Ministry via District SPOC). If exporting: obtain IEC code from DGFT Guwahati (₹1-2K, 7 days), APEDA registration (free). Total regulatory cost: ₹10-15K, 4-6 weeks.
5
First Sale
First sale: List on Amazon Fresh (takes 2 weeks approval) at ₹550/500g jar, first order typically 10-20 units. Simultaneously approach 5-7 Shillong cafes (Himalayan Tea House, Kynata, Sunnybrook Cafe) with free 200g samples; close ₹8-12K wholesale orders. First month realistic revenue: ₹40-60K (e-commerce 20%, wholesale 70%, local direct 10%). Expect 30-40% of first-month orders to repeat by month 2.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 1 branding/social media person (₹10K/month) to run Instagram content (bee-keeping behind-the-scenes, customer testimonials, forest shots). Add 2-3 retail distribution outlets in Shillong (Mother's Organic, local juice bars). Exhibit at All India Food Processing Buyers Meet (APFB, Delhi, Nov-Dec) via Apiculture Mission 2.0 sponsorship. Upgrade to motorized extractor, add bottling line (₹5-8L capex). Target ₹20L/month by month 18 through Guwahati HoReCa channel expansion and 2-3 export containers/month.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
Apiculture Mission 2.0
50% subsidy on bee colonies, equipment, and processing machinery for individuals, master beekeepers, and bee clusters (up to ₹5-8L subsidy per beneficiary group)
🏛
PM Formalisation of Micro food processing Enterprises Scheme
₹6L subsidy (max 20% of ₹30L capex limit) on processing equipment, packaging, cold storage for honey units with turnover <₹5 Cr
🏚
State Plan Schemes under DOFP Meghalaya
₹2-3L interest-free term loan for apiary infrastructure and 50% cost-share on hive installation in new clusters
🏚
Prime Ministers Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)
₹25L loan (15% subsidy for general category) for 2-3 beekeepers setting up a collective honey processing unit with 8-10 employees
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
