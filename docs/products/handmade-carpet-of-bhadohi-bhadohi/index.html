The cluster sources wool, silk, and cotton locally and regionally, with vegetable dye production embedded in the supply chain. International buyers are concentrated in USA, Europe, Middle East, and Australia, with established export infrastructure and ₹25 billion in annual regional turnover.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bhadohi ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Madhosingh village near Khamaria or Maidagin cluster in Bhadohi on a Friday haat; observe loom operations, speak with 3-5 established weavers/suppliers; call Bhadohi Chamber of Commerce (dial 05425-234XXX) for cluster directory; attend one IICT (Indian Institute of Carpet Technology) open workshop (free entry most months)—zero cost, 2-day trip
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from Mirzapur Bhadohi Weavers Cooperative or private middlemen in Maidagin/Madhosingh areas; first batch of 5-10 carpets (₹1.5-3L depending on size and material); negotiate 30-45 day payment terms; expect 4-6 week production lead time
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-5 established artisan looms (rent-share model at ₹15-25K per loom per month) OR lease a small workshop unit (800-1200 sqft) in Bhadohi MSME cluster at ₹8-12K monthly; total production setup ₹1.5-2.5L includes loom hire, dye station, quality check area; hire 1 production supervisor (₹12-18K/month) and 2-3 finishing workers (₹8-12K each/month)
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online); file GI registration application via Bhadohi Chamber or IICT (₹5-15K legal fee, 6-12 month approval); apply for IEC code via DGFT if exporting (free, online); register on ONDC and MSME e-marketplace (free); total timeline 3-4 months for full registration
5
First Sale
List first 10 carpets on Etsy (listing cost ₹300/carpet); simultaneously pitch to 5-10 interior designers in Delhi-Mumbai via WhatsApp with product images; target first wholesale order from local furniture retailer (₹80-200K for 5-10 pieces); expected first-month revenue ₹1.5-3L gross (3-5 sales at wholesale)
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire a full-time sales/export manager (₹25-35K); allocate ₹3-5L monthly for inventory build; apply for PMEGP or SFURTI expansion grant (₹25L subsidy); exhibit at Delhi Handmade Fair, Mumbai Furniture Fair, or Heimtextil (trade shows); build 10-15 loom partnerships; establish direct relationships with 3-5 export houses or wholesalers in USA/Europe

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
₹15,000 initial grant + ₹1.5L soft loan at 5% for artisans and micro-producers in handicraft
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
₹25-30L subsidy for manufacturing unit setup (25% for general category) + government-backed MUDRA loan up to ₹10L
🏚
UP Carpet Export Promotion Scheme
5% subsidy on export FOB value for registered exporters + free GI certification support
🏚
UP MSME Cluster Development Scheme
₹50L grant for common facility centre in Bhadohi cluster for artisans (shared dyeing, quality testing lab)
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
