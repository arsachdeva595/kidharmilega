Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bhadohi ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bhadohi carpet clusters in Gyanpur, Madiyahun, and Heerapur; spend 2-3 weeks understanding weaving techniques, meet master weavers at Bhadohi Carpet Manufacturers Association, study export practices at CFTL Bhadohi.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source premium wool from wool markets in Bikaner or Ludhiana (₹400-600/kg), silk from Varanasi silk traders (₹800-1200/kg), natural dyes from Jodhpur dye suppliers. First batch raw material cost: ₹2-3 Lakhs for 3-4 medium carpets.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established master weavers in Bhadohi via referrals from Handicraft Development Board; negotiate piece rates (₹80-150/sqft depending on complexity); secure contracts with 3-5 artisan groups to start production of 50-80 sqft/month.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (micro/small enterprise) online, obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Varanasi, register brand with CIPAM for design copyrights, apply for GI tag usage license from DC Handicraft Varanasi, open MSME-linked bank account for credit access.
5
First Sale
Launch D2C Shopify store with high-quality photography; list on Amazon Global, Etsy, and Wayfair with 'Bhadohi Made' certification; target interior designers via Pinterest and Instagram with influencer collaborations; approach luxury hotel chains directly with custom proposals.
6
Scale
Expand artisan network to 10-15 weavers, introduce new design lines quarterly, invest in Instagram/YouTube content (weaving process videos), establish wholesale partnerships with 5-10 premium home decor chains, explore corporate gifting contracts, consider design innovation with sustainable dyes.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma
up to ₹15,000 for upskilling + ₹1 Lakh interest-free loan for traditional craftspeople and their families
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹40-50 Cr cluster development grants for Bhadohi carpet cluster infrastructure and artisan support
🏚
Uttar Pradesh Handcraft Development Corporation (UPHDC)
interest-free or concessional financing for registered handicraft exporters up to ₹25 Lakhs
🏚
UP ODOP Scheme
export subsidy of 5-10% on FOB value for registered One District One Product enterprises in Bhadohi
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
