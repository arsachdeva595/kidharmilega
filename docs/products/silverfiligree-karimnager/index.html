opportunity, Karimnager mein.

Karimnagar has a 400-year-old tradition of silver filigree work with skilled hereditary artisans concentrated in clusters around the old city. The district's access to quality silver suppliers, established export networks, and UNESCO recognition make it the epicenter of this craft in India. Government support through DC Handicraft ministry ensures continued skill transmission and market linkages.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Karimnager ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Karimnagar's Filigree Cluster (Old City area near Collectorate) and spend 2 weeks apprenticing with master artisans like those in Shadnagar lane. Study GI certification guidelines at DC Handicraft office, Karimnagar. Connect with Karimnagar Handicrafts Association for market insights and supplier contacts.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source 99.9% pure silver wire from Delhi's Chandni Chowk wholesalers (₹25,000-35,000 per kg) or Hyderabad's Begum Bazaar (₹24,000-32,000/kg). Order first batch: 2-3 kg silver wire, 500g solder, gemstones from local Karimnagar suppliers. Expected first-batch cost: ₹60,000-80,000.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 trained artisans from Karimnagar clusters (₹8,000-12,000/month per artisan) or establish workshop near old city. Invest in basic tools (filigree pens, tweezers, soldering equipment) costing ₹30,000-50,000. Begin with 15-20 pieces/month production, scale gradually.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSE) under Ministry of MSME. Apply for IEC code from DGFT for export capability. Register GI-certified product tag through DC Handicraft. Create brand name emphasizing heritage (e.g., 'Karimnagar Heritage' or artisan name). File trademark with IP office.
5
First Sale
Launch D2C via Etsy (20% commission, high international reach), Amazon Handmade India, Flipkart Artisan program. Partner with luxury marketplaces (Jaypore, Craftsvilla). Approach wedding boutiques and luxury jewelry retailers in metros. Attend Hyderabad's Artisan Mela and Delhi Handicraft Fair for direct B2B contacts.
6
Scale
After 6 months, establish presence in 10-15 retail boutiques across metros. Invest ₹2-5L in digital marketing targeting luxury segment on Instagram/Pinterest. Hire 2-3 additional artisans. Develop 3-4 signature collections. Explore corporate gifting orders. Establish export partnerships with UK/US distributors via trade shows.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
up to ₹3L interest-free loan for artisans in SC/ST/minority categories, plus free toolkit and skill training
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (25-35%) for manufacturing, applicable to registered handicraft units
🏚
Telangana Handicrafts Development Scheme
export incentive of 5-10% on FOB value, plus market linkage support through THDCL
🏚
Telangana MSME Growth Scheme
5% CGTMIS interest subsidy + working capital loans up to ₹50L for registered handicraft exporters
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
