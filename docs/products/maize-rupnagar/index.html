Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Rupnagar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Rupnagar agricultural market (Bhakra APMC) to understand pricing cycles. Connect with 5-10 established maize farmers in Nurpur, Chamkaur Sahib blocks. Attend Punjab Agricultural University (PAU) Ludhiana training on maize hybrids and best practices. Study competition from Haryana and Himachal maize producers.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source hybrid maize seeds (Monsanto, Syngenta, Pioneer) from PAU seed center or agro-dealers in Rupnagar city (budget ₹15,000-25,000 per acre). Purchase NPK fertilizers (DAP, urea) from cooperative societies in Nangal/Tanda (₹8-12 per kg). First-batch cost for 10 acres: ₹2.5-3.5 lakhs including seeds, inputs, and labor.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 5-10 small farmers in Nurpur/Chamkaur blocks on contract farming model (offer 8-10% premium above MSP). Establish a small collection center (5,000 sq ft warehouse, ₹50,000 rental) in Rupnagar city. Hire 2-3 staff for drying, grading, and packaging. Use Rupnagar's proximity to Nangal for maize drying and storage facilities.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Micro/Small Enterprise) as agricultural trader. Obtain IEC code from Rupnagar Chamber of Commerce for export eligibility. Register with APEDA for organic certification (if applicable). Create brand identity emphasizing 'Rupnagar Premium Maize' with local heritage angle. Register trademark with IP office.
5
First Sale
Launch on AgroStar, Farmizen, and Krishak platforms for direct farmer connections. Approach feed mills (Rasi Seeds, Godrej Agrovet) in Punjab for bulk supply contracts. List on BigBasket, Jio Mart for packaged maize products. Connect with agricultural exporters at APEDA-listed companies. Sell graded maize at Rupnagar APMC on Tuesdays/Fridays.
6
Scale
Develop value-added product line (maize flour, cornstarch) with small processing unit (₹25-40L capex). Expand farmer network to 50+ acres under contract. Build direct B2B relationships with 3-5 poultry/feed companies (₹20L+ annual orders). Launch packaged maize for retail (Bornvita competitor positioning) on D2C channels. Explore organic maize certification for premium pricing (20-30% markup).
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM-KISAN Samman Nidhi
₹6,000 annual income support for farmers; structure business as cooperative to help farmer partners benefit
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy for establishing agro-processing unit with 25-35% margin improvement
🏚
Punjab Crop Insurance Scheme
covers maize farmers against yield losses; use to negotiate better prices with farmers
🏚
Punjab Agriculture & Processed Food Export Policy
5% subsidy on export of maize/maize products; support for IEC registration and APEDA listing
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
