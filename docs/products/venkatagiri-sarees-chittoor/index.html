Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Chittoor ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Venkatagiri cluster market on a Tuesday or Wednesday morning. Talk to 3-4 saree traders at the market (Patur haat timing: 7am-12pm). Call one supplier — ask for saree sourcing cost, lead time, quality standards. Register free on UDYAM portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) using your Aadhaar. Zero rupees, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Contact saree suppliers in Patur or Venkatagiri directly (via cluster traders you meet in step 1). First batch: 10-15 cotton sarees + 5 silk sarees. Cost: Rs 80-120K depending on thread quality and design complexity. Payment: 50% upfront, 50% on delivery. Lead time: 15-20 days.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Partner with 2-3 artisans in Patur or Venkatagiri — give them yarn, pay per saree (₹500-1200 per piece), rent a small storage/display room in the cluster (₹5-8K/month). Option B: Rent a 400-500 sq ft space near the cluster, hire 1-2 artisans, maintain inventory. Cost range for Option A: ₹50K setup + ₹80-120K per batch. Cost for Option B: ₹150-300K setup + ₹5-8K/month rent.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 15 mins). Apply for GI-tagged product certification (₹5-10K, 4-6 weeks, via DC Handloom office in Chittoor or Nellore). Register trademark for brand name (₹10-15K, 4-6 months, online via IP India). If exporting: Get IEC (Importer Exporter Code) from Customs (free, online, 2-3 days). Total: ₹25-40K, 6-8 weeks.
5
First Sale
First sale: Post 5-10 high-quality photos on Instagram, Etsy, and Shopify Store (₹299/month). Target keywords: "Venkatagiri saree", "Patur cotton saree", "GI-tagged saree". First order likely via Instagram DM or Etsy (₹8-15K saree price, ₹4-8K profit). Expected first-month revenue: ₹20-40K (2-4 sarees). Process: COD or bank transfer.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: Hire a social media manager (₹10-15K/month), expand to Facebook ads (₹500/day budget), list on Amazon and Flipkart (15-20% commission), attend regional wedding exhibitions in Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad. Partner with 5-8 artisans instead of 1-3. Hire a customer service person. Cost to scale: ₹50-100K/month additional.
