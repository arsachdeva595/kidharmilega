Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — North ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Wazirpur or Bawana industrial area—walk into 3-5 existing light engineering units and ask owners about their first setup, supplier contacts, and buyer channels. Call Delhi Biz Chamber or Federation of Delhi Trade Associations (FDTA) for free cluster networking events. Register free on Udyam portal. Zero cost, doable in 1 week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Steel and casting suppliers are concentrated in Wazirpur Extension (G.T. Road) and Mundka area—expect ₹2-5L for first batch of raw materials depending on product type. Meet local supplier associations to negotiate credit terms. Electrical components: Lajpat Rai Market, Delhi (wholesale area, 30 km away). Budget 2-4 weeks for first orders.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 1,500-2,500 sq.ft. space in Wazirpur, Bawana, or Narela industrial area (₹8-15k/month), install 1-2 CNC lathe/milling machines (₹2.5-6L second-hand), and hire 1-2 skilled operators (₹12-18k/month each). Alternatively, partner with 2-3 artisan job-workers in the cluster on piecework basis (no upfront capex, 15-25% higher per-piece cost). Total 3-6 month setup.
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam (free, 5 minutes online). Apply for IEC code from DGFT Delhi office (₹0, 1-2 weeks via ICEGATE portal). GI registration is optional—consult cluster association on feasibility (costs ₹5-10k, takes 3-4 months if pursued). Get BIS certification if product falls under mandatory categories (₹15-50k depending on product type).
5
First Sale
Post product catalog on IndiaBIZ (free), TradeKey, and Global Trade Platform. Email 50+ verified OEM buyers (Maruti, Bajaj parts suppliers, pump makers) with samples. First order typically ₹25k-2L. Expect 2-3 months lead time to first revenue, then ₹3-8L monthly if you land 2-3 repeat buyers.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 1 sales person (₹15-25k salary), invest ₹5-8L in 1 additional CNC machine, formalize quality control process, target export shipments via trading houses. Exhibit at India ITME Society shows (annual), and Auto Component Makers Association expos in Delhi. Join export promotion schemes like APEDA or ECEPL for subsidy-backed trade missions.
