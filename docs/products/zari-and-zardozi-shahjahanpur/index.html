Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Shahjahanpur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Shahjahanpur's established Zari-Zardozi clusters in Chakbandi and Raza Nagar areas; connect with senior craftspeople and view production units; attend ODOP exhibitions and trade fairs organized by DC Handicraft; study competitor pricing and designs on Flipkart, Amazon, and specialized sites like TheArtHouz.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source Zari threads from wholesale suppliers in Shahjahanpur (e.g., Chakbandi market) or Delhi's Chandni Chowk; procure beads, sequins, and stones from Surat or Jaipur suppliers; first batch (100 units) will cost ₹80,000–120,000; negotiate bulk discounts after establishing supply relationships.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3–5 established artisans/workshops in Shahjahanpur offering piece-rate agreements; formalize contracts with quality benchmarks; visit workshops weekly to oversee work; alternative: start with 1–2 master craftspeople as consultants and gradually build in-house team as volumes scale.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSE registration) within 1 week; apply for IEC code from nearest DGFT office (Lucknow) for export readiness; create brand identity (logo, tagline, packaging design); register trademark with IP office; obtain GST registration; consider Geographical Indication (GI) application once volume stabilizes.
5
First Sale
Launch on Flipkart, Amazon, and Meesho with high-quality product photography and detailed embroidery descriptions; create Instagram shop showcasing craftsmanship videos; reach out to 20–30 bridal boutiques and high-street retailers for wholesale partnerships; participate in ODOP government trade fairs; set up WhatsApp Business for direct B2B inquiry handling.
6
Scale
Expand artisan network to 10+ partners; invest in branded packaging and logistics; launch own website with SEO; target corporate gifting and international wholesale buyers; apply for SFURTI or PM Vishwakarma support for cluster development; explore B2B2C partnerships with wedding planners and event companies.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma
up to ₹5L collateral-free credit + 1% interest subsidy + ₹15,000 toolkit support for artisans; applicable for Zari-Zardozi craftspeople.
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (15% for general) for new units in manufacturing; ideal for scaling small Zari workshops.
🏚
UP Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises Scheme
capital subsidy up to 20% and power subsidy up to 5% for registered MSEs in handicraft sector.
🏚
UP Kaushal Vikas Mission
training grants for artisan upskilling and product innovation in traditional crafts including Zari-Zardozi.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
