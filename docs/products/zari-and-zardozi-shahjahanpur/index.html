Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Shahjahanpur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bareilly zari bazaar (100 km away) on a Wednesday or Thursday to see live embroidery workshops and supplier yards; call 3-4 raw material suppliers listed with Lucknow Zari Association; attend the monthly craft haat at Lucknow or Bareilly if active. Cost: ₹0. Time: 2 days.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source golden and silver threads, beads, sequins from wholesale suppliers in Lucknow's Chowk area (80 km south) or Bareilly's Zari Market. First batch cost: ₹30,000-₹50,000 for 50 sarees' worth of materials. Supplier names: approach through District Commerce Office or DC Handicraft contacts.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 200-300 sqft production unit near Lucknow/Bareilly for ₹3,000-₹5,000/month OR partner with 5-10 artisans on per-piece commission (₹80-₹150 per saree face embroidery). Master artisan coordination critical. Total initial labor cost: ₹40,000-₹80,000/month for 20-30 pieces.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 20 mins online). Apply for GI registration support under DC Handicraft scheme (Zari-Zardozi already has GI 2009 — you get free use). If exporting: file IEC application (₹free, 7 days). Open business bank account (₹0, same day). Cost: ₹2,000 for GST registration + ₹1,500 for trademark search. Timeline: 30 days total.
5
First Sale
List on Instagram shop (free) with 10-15 high-res photos of finished pieces. Reach out to 50 ethnic fashion retailers via WhatsApp with price list. Sell 3-5 pieces in month 1 at ₹8,000-₹15,000 per saree wholesale (₹12,000-₹25,000 D2C). Expected first-month revenue: ₹40,000-₹80,000 (wholesale) or ₹50,000-₹125,000 (D2C mix).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire a logistics partner (handle WhatsApp orders + shipping via Delhivery/DTDC). Add 2-3 part-time designers for pattern innovation. Apply for SFURTI cluster scheme grant (₹10-25L subsidy for 10+ artisans). Exhibit at Delhi Craft Bazaar, Jaipur Heritage Art Fair, Mumbai Numaish. Build export partnership with Israel buyers through Ministry ODOP export matchmaking.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
₹500 per artisan enrolled; interest-free credit up to ₹5 lakh for tool purchases and working capital.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹10-25 lakh cluster grants for 10+ artisans, common production center, marketing support.
🏚
Uttar Pradesh Craft Cluster Scheme
subsidized workspace (50% rent reduction) for 3 years if registered with MSME Facilitation Centre.
🏚
UP One District One Product (ODOP) Scheme
export finance at 4% interest, free training, GI branding support for registered Udyam artisans.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
