Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bankura ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Panchmura or Bishnupur village; observe production directly at 3-4 artisan family units (no cost, free access). Call the Bankura District Industries Centre (+91-3222-272340) to understand GI regulations. Attend Bishnupur Handicrafts Fair in Feb/March. Register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) using Aadhaar.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw clay from local suppliers in Panchmura (₹400-600 per 50 kg bag). Establish relationship with 2-3 semi-finished product artisans who can deliver hand-molded body parts (Bankura Horse body costs ₹200-400 per set of 7 pieces). First batch: order 50 sets = ₹12,000-20,000 for raw materials + artisan labor.
3
Produce / Partner
Two options: (A) Rent a 400-600 sq ft pottery shed in Panchmura cluster (₹3,000-5,000/month) and assemble + detail pieces yourself; or (B) Formalize partnership with 2-3 established artisan families as contract producers (10-15% markup on their piece cost). Production: 30-50 finished pieces/week per person. Setup cost: ₹2-4L including kiln rental or artisan advance payments.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 2 hours online). Verify GI logo usage with Bankura District Industries Centre — you can legally use 'GI certified Bankura Horse' in branding. File for IEC (Importer Exporter Code) with DGFT if exporting (₹0 cost, 4-5 days online). Create simple product branding kit: product tags + packaging + Instagram handle. Total cost: ₹8,000-15,000.
5
First Sale
First sales: list 15-20 Bankura Horse pieces on Etsy (₹50 listing fee/month in INR equivalent). Target keywords: 'Bankura Horse terracotta', 'Indian folk art horse', 'handmade clay statue'. First 3 months: aim for 2-3 orders/week at ₹3,000-8,000 per piece. Expected month-1 revenue: ₹6,000-12,000.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 1 full-time quality checker (₹12,000/month), formalise 5+ artisan relationships on retainer. Set up dedicated Instagram and Pinterest with process videos. Apply for PM Vishwakarma (₹3L interest-free credit). Exhibit at Nukkad Market, Kala Bazaar (Kolkata), or Small Business Day at crafts expos. Approach 5-10 corporate gift consultants per quarter.
