Zari Silk Sarees ki
opportunity, Lalitpur mein.

Lalitpur district in Uttar Pradesh, positioned on National Highway 44, houses 250-275 skilled craftsmen producing handwoven sarees using traditional looms without electrical machinery. Raw materials (silk threads and metallic zari) flow from established suppliers in Varanasi (120 km) and Surat (280 km), while primary buyers concentrate in Delhi-NCR, Agra, and Lucknow wedding markets. The district has produced only 2 commercial units so far, leaving significant room for new entrants to capture the 25,000-30,000 annual production demand across Zari Silk, Chanderi, and Brocade varieties.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Lalitpur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit the Lalitpur ODOP cluster near National Highway 44 to observe production in real time. Call 5 suppliers of zari and silk threads listed on the district ODOP portal — ask for raw material costs, lead times, and minimum order quantities. Register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) using your Aadhaar. Attend the monthly haat at Lalitpur town market to speak with existing artisans and small vendors about current pricing and buyer demand. Cost: ₹0 (travel only).
2
Source Raw Materials
Source silk threads from established suppliers in Varanasi (Silk Weavers Cooperative or Harish Silk House) at ₹400-600/kg. Procure metallic zari from Surat merchants (Zari Bazaar near Veer Textile Market) at ₹150-250/kg. For first batch of 10-15 sarees, expect raw material cost of ₹35,000-50,000 based on design complexity. Sourcing happens via direct factory visits or Udyog Aadhaar-verified dealer networks.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 5-8 registered artisans from the Lalitpur cluster (contact DC Handloom office for verified list) on a piece-rate model: ₹1,500-2,500 per saree depending on complexity and turnaround time. Alternatively, rent a 400-600 sqft production unit in the district industrial area for ₹8,000-12,000/month and hire 2-3 full-time weavers at ₹8,000-10,000/month each. Total monthly fixed cost via rental route: ₹25,000-35,000. Most founders start with artisan partnerships to avoid fixed overhead.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 15 minutes). Apply for GI registration support under the One District One Product scheme via the district DC Handloom office — process takes 4-6 months, govt covers 80% of filing costs (₹8,000-12,000). If exporting, obtain IEC (Importer Exporter Code) from DGFT portal for free. Create trademark for brand name via IPR cell (₹4,500-8,000, 6-month approval). Total timeline: 5-7 months; cost: ₹15,000-25,000 out-of-pocket.
5
First Sale
Launch first sales on Craftsvilla or Flyrobe (zero upfront fee, 15-20% commission). Upload 8-10 product images with detailed weave descriptions and care instructions. Target first sales through direct WhatsApp outreach to 500 wedding planners and boutique owners in Agra-Lucknow belt using a simple product list. Expected first month: 3-5 sarees at ₹6,000-8,000 each = ₹18,000-40,000 gross revenue. Offer 10% bulk discounts to wedding planners for repeat orders of 10+ sarees.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (12-15 sarees/month), hire a full-time WhatsApp/email coordinator (₹6,000/month) and a photographer to produce 20 new product images quarterly. Transition from artisan partnerships to 1 rented production unit with 4-5 weavers. Exhibit at major craft fairs: Vibrant Gujarat Handloom Expo (January), Surajkund International Craft Mela near Delhi (February), and IITF Delhi (November). Negotiate wholesale accounts with 15-20 boutiques in tier-2 cities at 5-saree minimum orders. Launch a basic website (₹15,000-25,000 annual hosting) with SEO for keywords like 'Zari Silk Saree Lalitpur' and 'Traditional Mughal Weave Saree'.

Government-designated handloom cluster housing 2 commercial production units and 250+ home-based artisans. Shared infrastructure includes raw material storage (500 sqft), exhibition space (200 sqft), and access to common dye vats. Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
One District One Product (ODOP) Scheme
Loan assistance up to ₹10 lakh at reduced interest (4-6% after subsidy). Provides raw material subsidies and market linkage support. Application via district ODOP nodal agency.
🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
₹15,000 credit support + ₹1 lakh credit in year 2 for craftsmen; artisan partners are eligible. Covers working capital for materials.
🏚
UP Handloom Policy 2023
25% subsidy on purchase of raw materials and 30% subsidy on machines/looms. Application via Uttar Pradesh Handloom and Textile Department.
🏚
UP MSME Udyam Vikas Nidhi Yojana
Collateral-free loan up to ₹50 lakh at 4% interest for 5 years. Eligible for registered artisan collectives and handloom businesses.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Seedhe sawaal, seedhe jawab

Shuru karne ke liye ₹2-3 lakh lagega: ₹50,000 pehle batch raw material ke liye, ₹30,000-50,000 business registration aur branding (Udyam, trademark, ghar ka office setup), ₹20,000 website aur social media setup, aur ₹1-1.5 lakh working capital 2-3 mahine ke liye. Agar production unit rent karoge toh ₹25,000 monthly aur extra ₹40,000 artisans ke liye advance. ODOP loan ₹10 lakh tak ka available hai 4-6% interest pe.
Textile experience helpful hai par lazmi nahi. Handloom craftsmen already skilled hain — tumhe sirf artisan partnerships manage karni padegi aur customers tak pahunchana padega. Agar wholesale retail mein background ho toh saree business karna aasan ho jaayega kyunki marjin margins aur buyer psychology same hote hain. Agar zero experience ho toh pehle 3 mahine cluster mein time spend karo aur 5-10 artisans ke saath kaam dekho.
ODOP scheme se ₹10 lakh tak loan milega 4-6% interest pe. PM Vishwakarma se ₹15,000 direct credit aur artisan partners ₹1 lakh additional le sakte hain. UP state se 25-30% raw material subsidy available hai aur handloom machinery subsidies bhi hain. GI registration ka 80% cost cover hota hai — ₹8,000-12,000 lagega tumhara. DC Handloom office mein jao — sabhi forms aur contact numbers wahan milenge.
Craftsvilla aur Flyrobe pe account banao (commission-based, koi upfront cost nahi). Instagram aur Pinterest pe wedding hashtags target karo. WhatsApp se direct 500 wedding planners aur boutique owners ko contact karo Agra-Lucknow belt mein — template message likho aur bulk discount offer de. Monthly haats aur craft exhibitions mein products le jao — Surajkund Mela (Feb), IITF Delhi (Nov) mein decent sales aate hain. First 10-20 orders apne network se hi aayenge.
Haan, export hota hai. USA, UK, UAE, Germany, Australia mein saree demand strong hai. Process: IEC code lo DGFT se (free), bank mein Export Current Account kholo (5 din), nFusion online par BSF approval le lo, phir direct shipment karo DHL/FedEx ya sea freight se. Margins 38-50% FOB basis se milte hain lekin payment 30-45 days late aata hai. First export order ₹50,000+ ka hona chahiye. Flipkart Global, Amazon Global, aur iShopIndian platforms pe list karo.
Honestly, saree ka demand guaranteed hai kyunki wedding industry growing hai aur 70% women traditional sarees prefer karti hain. Risk yeh hai ki agar sarees bi-monthly bikne lage toh ₹1.5 lakh monthly cash flow problem ho skta hai. Sarees 6-20 din mein tayyar hote hain so inventory risk bhi hai. Exit option: artisan partners ko apne sarees banane aur khud sell karne de do (tum commission lo). Aur agar saree business flop ho toh same expertise se dupatta, kurta aur custom embroidery service start kar skta hai — market overlap 60% hai. Apne ODOP loan repay karne ke liye 18-24 mahine runway rakho.

Marketing

Ye marketing ideas try karna mat bhulna

  • Instagram Reels: 15-30 second clips showing a weaver's hands at the loom completing a single motif, with text overlay 'One saree = 80-120 hours of hand-weaving.' Post 3x weekly. Hook: Use trending audio and use hashtags #SareeDesign #HandwoveSaree #WeddingSeason.
  • YouTube Long-form: 5-8 minute 'Behind the Weave' documentary-style video showing raw silk arriving at the cluster, dye process, loom setup, and final inspection. Upload monthly. Drive traffic via Pinterest pins linking to these videos with SEO keywords 'how handwoven sarees are made.'
  • WhatsApp Broadcast List: Build a 2,000+ person list of past customers and wedding planners. Send 2 messages weekly: new stock alerts, care tips, and limited-time bulk discounts (e.g., 'Buy 5 sarees, get 1 free'). Use Broadcast to avoid seeming spammy.
