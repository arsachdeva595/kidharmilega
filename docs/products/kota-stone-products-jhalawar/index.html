Kota Stone Products ki
opportunity, Jhalawar mein.

Jhalawar district in Southeast Rajasthan contains high-quality Kota Stone deposits with natural stratification ideal for thin, durable flagstones. Ricco Industrial Area houses 80-100 registered stone units with shared access to diamond-cutting tools, polishing machinery, and logistics networks. Proximity to Kota city (25 km), Indore (95 km), and Delhi trade routes (400 km) enables rapid movement of finished stock to Northern India's construction boom and direct export via Mumbai/Chennai ports.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Jhalawar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Ricco Industrial Area (Jhalawar town, 5 km north) on any weekday — walk into 3-4 stone units without appointment, ask quarry owners about stone grades and pricing, call Kota Stone Traders Association (contact via Jhalawar District Industries office), join WhatsApp groups of local exporters — all free, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw Kota Stone blocks directly from quarries in Ricco area or 25 km toward Kota (₹30-50 per kg, ₹12-18k per ton). First-batch cost: ₹2-3L for 2-3 tons (100-120 sq ft finished product). Supplier area: Ricco Industrial Area or Kota city stone markets (Nayapura, Subhash Nagar).
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 800-1200 sq ft workshop unit in Ricco Industrial Area (₹4-6k/month) or partner with existing stone artisans on revenue-share (40-50% of gross). Capital needed: ₹4-6L for diamond saw, polishing machine, water pump, hand tools. Alternatively, contract 60-70% of production to established units (cost: ₹50-80 per sq ft) and handle finishing + QC in-house.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, 15 min, free) → Apply for Kota Stone ODOP certification (Jhalawar District Industries, ₹0-2k admin, 10 days) → Apply for ₹50k ODOP exhibition subsidy or 75% stall rent support for trade shows → If exporting, register IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) via online portal (₹0-3k, 10 days) → Consider GI registration application (₹15-25k, 6-12 months, through District Industries office).
5
First Sale
First sale: contact 5-10 local contractors/builders directly (phone calls, WhatsApp with sample photos) → Offer ₹100-120/sq ft bulk pricing for 5-10 ton trial orders (₹5-10L revenue). Simultaneously list on Amazon Home, Flipkart, IndiaMART at premium pricing (₹200-280/sq ft) for D2C orders. Expected first-month revenue: ₹3-7L from 1-2 wholesale orders or 10-15 D2C orders.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 1-2 full-time artisans (₹12-15k/month each), upgrade to semi-automatic polishing (₹3L additional capex), move to 1500+ sq ft workspace (₹8-10k/month). Target: attend IITF Delhi or International Stone Congress (uses ODOP exhibition subsidy, max 3 events/FY). Build export sales via LinkedIn outreach to USA architects and German tile distributors. Set up WhatsApp-based B2B order management, implement 20-30 day payment terms with big builders.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
One District One Product (ODOP) Scheme
ODOP-registered MSMEs from Jhalawar can receive financial assistance up to ₹50,000 or 75% of stall rent for participating in exhibitions; maximum three events per financial year eligible
🏛
MUDRA Loan Scheme
up to ₹10L collateral-free for MSME registration; floating interest 8-10%; can cover workshop setup and initial inventory.
🏚
Rajasthan Stone Industry Subsidy
25% capital subsidy on machinery purchases up to ₹25L (verify current status with Rajasthan State Industries Directorate)
🏚
Rajasthan Export Promotion Scheme
5% cash assistance on FOB export value for registered exporters; valid for MSME units in Jhalawar ODOP sector
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
