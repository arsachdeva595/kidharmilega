opportunity, Rajsamand mein.

Rajsamand district in southeastern Rajasthan overlays limestone and calcium carbonate reserves that feed 4,000+ mining and processing units clustered in Makrana (world-famous white marble), Rajsamand (grey-white varieties), Kishangarh (commercial marble hub), Udaipur, and Banswara. Proximity to raw material, established artisan base, and 50+ years of processing expertise drive ₹30,000–35,000 crore annual production. Buyers are architects and developers across India (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad) and 50+ countries in Asia, Europe, and Middle East via established exporter networks.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Rajsamand ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Makrana or Kishangarh cluster this week (free bus travel from Udaipur 80km away). Walk the 2-3 main marble markets: Makrana Marble Market, Kishangarh Stone Market. Call 3-4 quarry owners directly (numbers in local haat bulletin or from district MSME office). Ask about raw material prices, production cycles, supplier credit terms, export buyer contacts. Attend a Friday wholesale haat in Kishangarh or Makrana to see live pricing. Register free on Udyam portal same week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from quarry suppliers in Makrana or Kishangarh clusters. First batch: ₹2-4L for 50-100 marble/granite slabs or 10,000 tiles (4-6 week payment terms common). Negotiate 15-20% deposit upfront, remainder on 30-45 day settlement. Supplier contacts available via district MSME office or local marble association secretary.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 2,000-3,000 sq ft unit (₹8,000–15,000/month) in Makrana or Kishangarh industrial area with cutting/polishing machinery (buy used for ₹3–8L or share via cooperative arrangement). Hire 4-6 semi-skilled stone cutters and polishers (₹8,000–12,000/month salary). Produce 200-400 tiles/slabs per week. Total setup: ₹8-15L (machinery + deposit + 2-month wages buffer).
4
Brand & Register
Register free Udyam (online, 1 week). Get GST Registration (online, ₹0, 1 week processing). If exporting: apply for IEC code via DGFT portal (₹0, 2-3 weeks). Explore GI registration for specific granite/marble variety via Ministry of Commerce (can add premium 10-15%, apply through state MSME Directorate). Brand name registration under Designs Act optional (₹1,000-5,000).
5
First Sale
First sale: approach 3-5 architects or contractors via direct outreach + WhatsApp/LinkedIn portfolio. Typical first order ₹50,000–2L. Post samples on Amazon, OLX, Instagram. Expect first month revenue ₹1–3L from 5-10 mixed orders (wholesale + D2C). Payment terms: 50% advance, 50% on delivery for new buyers.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire a sales person (₹15,000/month) to hunt B2B contractor and interior designer deals in Delhi-NCR and South. Export via Shimla or Mumbai freight forwarders (₹2-5L per shipment, 8-12 week cycles). Rent second production unit. Invest in wet-polishing machines for marble (₹5-8L, multiplies throughput 3x). Display at Stone and Tile Expo (Mumbai, Bangalore) annually. Join local export association for buyer leads.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Program)
₹25 lakh loan at 5% subsidy for eligible candidates, 10-year tenure, for manufacturing units.
🏛
MUDRA Loans
up to ₹10 lakh at 7-8% interest, unsecured, 3-5 year repayment, zero subsidy but fast disbursal.
🏚
Rajasthan ODOP Scheme
priority subsidies, Udyam registration fee waiver, export support, raw material subsidy up to ₹50,000 for registered units.
🏚
Rajasthan Stone Cluster Development Scheme
₹5-10L capital subsidy for buying machinery if located in notified clusters (Makrana, Kishangarh, Rajsamand qualify).
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
