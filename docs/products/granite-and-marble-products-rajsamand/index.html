Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Rajsamand ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Rajsamand quarry clusters around Khimsar, Nathdwara, and Bhim to understand mining operations, stone grades, and market dynamics. Connect with established processors at Rajsamand Industrial Area and attend trade shows at Jaipur Stone & Tiles Expo to identify buyer networks.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw granite and marble blocks from local quarry owners in Rajsamand district (₹8-15 lakh per truck load for premium varieties). Establish relationships with 2-3 primary quarries and negotiate 6-month supply contracts with 15-20% upfront payment terms.
3
Produce / Partner
Set up a processing unit with stone cutting machines, CNC polishing units, and quality control equipment (₹25-50L for mini setup). Partner with established processing workshops in Rajsamand Industrial Area or Khimsar to start production without full capex, sharing revenue 60-40 until scaling.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free online), obtain IEC code from local customs authority for exports, apply for mining transport permits (e-pass) from Rajasthan Mines Department. Register brand name with IPAB and obtain GST-18% registration for B2B and B2C sales.
5
First Sale
Launch B2B sales via industry platforms (Tradeindia, Indianminerals.com), approach top 20 builders in NCR/Mumbai via direct sales, list premium tiles on Houzz and design marketplaces. Target architects via LinkedIn outreach and WhatsApp catalogs to generate first ₹20-30L in orders.
6
Scale
After ₹50L revenue, invest in full-fledged processing unit with CNC technology, hire sales team for national retail expansion, establish showrooms in Delhi/Mumbai/Bangalore, and begin direct exports via freight forwarders in Jaipur/Ahmedabad.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (35% for SC/ST) for setting up stone processing and manufacturing unit
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
cluster development support including common facility centers and skill training for stone artisans in Rajsamand
🏚
Rajasthan Stone & Mineral Processing Incentive Scheme
5% capital subsidy on plant and machinery for mineral processing units with ₹1Cr+ investment
🏚
Rajasthan Export Promotion Policy
2% export subsidy and freight reimbursement (up to 50%) for registered stone product exporters from ODOP clusters
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
