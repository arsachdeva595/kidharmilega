Wooden Crafts ki
opportunity, Bijnor mein.

Bijnor has a centuries-old tradition of wood carving and inlay work passed down through generations of skilled artisans. The district's proximity to forest regions and established trade networks with Delhi and Lucknow create ideal conditions for sourcing quality wood and accessing metropolitan markets. The concentration of master craftspeople and established GI recognition make it the epicenter of India's decorative wooden crafts industry.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bijnor ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bijnor's main craft clusters in Seohara, Noorpur, and Chandpur localities. Connect with Master Artisans Association of Bijnor (MAAB). Spend 2 weeks observing production at workshops like Aslam Arts and Sharma Handicrafts. Study GI certification details from DC Handicraft office. Analyze competitors on Etsy, Amazon Handmade, and websites like Anasa Gharelu.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source Sheesham and Mango wood from timber merchants in Seohara market (₹80-120 per kg in bulk). Order bone inlay materials and brass fittings from Chandpur wholesalers. First batch of 50 small items requires ₹40,000-60,000. Build relationships with 2-3 trusted suppliers for consistent quality and 15-day payment terms.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 1-2 registered artisans in Noorpur (contracts for ₹800-1,200 per piece depending on complexity). Alternatively, lease a 200 sq ft workshop space (₹3,000-5,000/month) and hire 2-3 trained carvers. Establish production workflow for 30-50 pieces monthly. Implement quality checks at carving, inlay, and finishing stages.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSE classification as manufacturing). Apply for IEC code from local DGFT office for export readiness. Obtain GI Certification (leverage existing Bijnor GI tag by showing artisan partnership). Register brand name with IP office. Create brand story emphasizing heirloom quality, sustainability, and artisan heritage. Get ISO 9001 certification to enhance export credibility (₹15,000-25,000).
5
First Sale
Launch D2C via Shopify or WooCommerce website (₹8,000-15,000 setup). List on Etsy (2.4% + $0.20 fee per listing), Amazon Handmade, and Flipkart Artisan. Establish presence on Instagram and Pinterest with process videos and artisan stories. Start with 5-10 hero products. Send to 20 interior design studios and hospitality businesses for bulk orders. Participate in India Handmade Festival and NIF Bazaar.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand product range to include wall art, boxes, and multipurpose furniture. Hire 2 more artisans and establish inventory of ₹5-8 Lakh for seasonal demand peaks (Diwali, wedding season). Invest ₹30,000 in Facebook/Instagram ads targeting affluent metros. Approach high-end hotel chains and corporate offices for bulk customization orders. Launch B2B portal for retailers. Target 3-5 exports per month via DHL/FedEx partnerships.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
up to ₹15,000 toolkits assistance and ₹125,000 credit at 5% for artisan partnerships
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 Lakh loan at 25-35% subsidy for SC/OBC entrepreneurs in handicrafts
🏚
UP Handicraft Development Policy
export incentives of 5-10% on FOB value for GI-tagged products shipped from Uttar Pradesh
🏚
UP MSME Innovation Scheme
₹2-5 Lakh grant for product design modernization and certification (ISO/GI compliance)
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Marketing

Ye marketing ideas try karna mat bhulna

  • Instagram Reels & TikTok: Show 15-30 second artisan process videos from Seohara workshops—carving, inlay application, finishing. Use hashtags #BijnorWoodCraft #ArtisanMade #SustainableHome. Target pins to affluent metro audiences.
  • Pinterest Strategy: Create 20+ high-quality pins linking to blog posts on 'Best Sustainable Home Décor', 'Heirloom Furniture Guide', 'Eco-Friendly Interior Trends'. Drive traffic to Shopify store. Budget ₹5,000/month for Ads Manager to reach 25-45 year old home decorators.
  • B2B Email Campaign: Identify 200 interior design studios, hotels, and corporate offices in Delhi, Bangalore, Mumbai. Send quarterly catalogs with new collections and bulk pricing. Attend India Design Week (Delhi) and Décor India (Mumbai) shows with 3-4 display pieces. Offer 5% referral commission to designers.
