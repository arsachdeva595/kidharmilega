Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Jamui ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Jamui Handicraft Association cluster near Jamui city center; interview 3-5 master artisans; attend ICCW (Indian Chamber of Commerce & Weaving) events in Patna; research crochet trends on Etsy bestsellers and Pinterest; benchmark competitors on Myntra, Amazon Handmade.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source premium acrylic yarn from Punjab yarn mills (₹80-150/kg) or import from Tiruppur textile hub; buy crochet hooks from Nagpur artisan suppliers (₹2-5 per hook); negotiate first batch of 500kg yarn + hooks for ₹40,000-60,000; establish credit terms with local artisans for raw material float.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 5-8 skilled crochet artisans in Jamui through DC Handicraft referrals; sign 6-month production agreements with ₹500-1500 per week per artisan; conduct quality standardization workshop; set up WhatsApp group for design dissemination and deadline tracking; arrange materials weekly pickup from artisan homes.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) within 1 week online; apply for IEC code from DGFT portal for export readiness; register trademark for brand name at IP India; get Biological Diversity Act certification from Bihar State Biodiversity Board; GST registration (18% applicable).
5
First Sale
Launch Instagram with product photography and artisan stories (post 4x/week); list on Amazon Handmade with 25 initial SKUs; create Etsy shop with free shipping offers; reach out to 15 home decor boutiques in Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore with samples; cold-email 20 corporate gifting agencies; run Facebook/Instagram ads targeting 'home decor' + 'handmade' audiences (₹500/day budget).
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to 15-20 artisans; launch seasonal collections (festival, wedding, monsoon themes); partner with 2-3 design influencers for product placement; open pop-up stores in metro malls during festive season; explore bulk B2B orders with hospitality chains; consider artisan cooperative formation for bulk export.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
up to ₹2L interest-free loan + toolkit grant for artisan-entrepreneurs + 8% collateral-free credit facility
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (25-35%) for new MSME ventures in handcraft sector
🏚
Bihar Handloom & Handicraft Promotion Scheme
15% subsidy on raw material costs for registered handicraft units + free design training
🏚
Bihar Rozgar Srijan Yojana
₹1-2L soft loans for handicraft women entrepreneurs + 2% interest subsidy for first 3 years
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
