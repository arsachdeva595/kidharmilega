Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Satara ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Satara strawberry clusters in Panchgani and Mahabaleshwar villages (50km from Satara town). Meet farmer groups at Satara Agricultural Produce Market Committee (APMC) and connect with 5-10 GI-registered strawberry farmers. Understand seasonal calendar (Oct-May peak), cost structure (₹40-60/kg cultivation cost), and local cooperative structures.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source disease-free strawberry runners from IIHR Bangalore or local Satara nurseries (Panchgani area has certified suppliers). First-batch cost: 1 acre setup requires ₹1.2-1.5L (runners ₹50k, soil prep ₹40k, drip irrigation ₹60k). Local suppliers: Satara Horticulture Department, Agri-Export Zone partners.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established Satara strawberry farmers through revenue-sharing model or lease 0.5-1 acre of existing farm. Alternatively, contract farming with cooperatives like Satara Farmers Producers Organization. Engage local agricultural extension officers for training. Invest in basic cold-chain infrastructure (walk-in cooler ₹2-3L).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online at udyamregistration.gov.in) as agricultural startup. Obtain IEC code from DGFT for export eligibility. Get GI product authentication certificate from state agriculture ministry. Register brand name under Trademark Registry. Obtain FSSAI license for any value-added products. Set up basic farm documentation system.
5
First Sale
First 3 months: Sell to Satara APMC wholesale market and local Pune retailers (high demand). Months 4-6: Launch on BigBasket, JioMart (₹5-7k monthly commission). Simultaneously approach premium restaurants in Pune, Mumbai (direct B2B sales). Establish WhatsApp bulk ordering group for urban professionals. Ship via cold-chain logistics partners (Geek Cold Chain, TFC).
6
Scale
After breakeven, expand from 1 acre to 2-3 acres. Develop own cold storage facility (₹5-8L). Launch private label products (frozen berries, jams) for modern retail. Build export shipment schedule to UAE (2-3 containers/season). Join Satara Strawberry Growers Association for collective branding and government support. Launch farm stay/agritourism to diversify revenue by month 12.
