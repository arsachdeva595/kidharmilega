Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Shahdara ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Shahdara's wholesale garment clusters (Nand Nagri, Farsh Road, Guru Nanak Colony) and Chandni Chowk textile markets. Attend National Garment Summit. Study competitor pricing on Meesho, Flipkart, and Instagram. Shadow 2-3 established garment shop owners for 2 weeks.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fabric from Chandni Chowk or Jama Masjid wholesale markets (₹50-150 per meter depending on quality). For first batch (500 pieces), budget ₹8-12 lakhs including accessories. Establish relationships with 2-3 reliable fabric suppliers for consistent quality.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established tailoring units in Nand Nagri or Farsh Road clusters (50-100 tailors available). Start with contract manufacturing model at ₹100-300 per piece depending on design complexity. Alternatively, set up in-house unit with 5-8 sewing machines and skilled workers.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME online (free, <2 days). Apply for IEC code from DGFT office in New Delhi for export readiness. File trademark with IP India. Get GST registration (4% for readymade garments). Obtain FSSAI if adding organic/cotton certification.
5
First Sale
Launch Instagram account with 10-15 product photos per design; run Instagram Ads targeting 18-35F in metros (budget ₹500-1000/day). List on Meesho and Flipkart Mall within 30 days. Approach 5-10 local retail stores in Delhi for wholesale placement at 35-40% margin. Send samples to fashion bloggers for review.
6
Scale
After first 6 months, expand product range to 15-20 styles. Hire a digital marketer for content and Facebook/YouTube campaigns. Approach regional e-commerce platforms (Biba, W, Nykaa Fashion). Open flagship store or pop-up retail in Delhi's shopping areas. Invest in influencer collaborations and seasonal runway events.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh loan at 6% interest with subsidy of 15-25% for garment manufacturing units.
🏛
MUDRA Yojana
unsecured loans up to ₹10 lakh with minimal documentation for readymade garment business startups.
🏚
Delhi Enterprise Support Scheme
subsidy on plant and machinery (20-40%) and working capital support for textile and apparel manufacturing.
🏚
Delhi Employment Exchange Bonus Scheme
wage subsidy for recruiting skilled and unskilled workers in apparel manufacturing sector.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
