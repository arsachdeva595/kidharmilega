Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Shahdara ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Shahdara Industrial Area (Patparganj, East Delhi) — meet 5-6 garment factory owners, ask about production timelines and unit costs. Call fabric suppliers in Nai Sadak market directly (free calls to WhatsApp Business accounts). Attend Delhi Garment Cluster trade fairs (check Ministry of Textiles event calendar). Costs: zero.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fabrics from Nai Sadak (Delhi) or Surat via agents — 100 pcs trial batch costs ₹1.2-2.5L depending on GSM and design. Partner directly with 2-3 manufacturers in Patparganj Industrial Area for first 500-1000 pcs at ₹180-350 per piece (landed cost depending on style). Lock lead times: 15-20 days for cut-and-sew, 25-30 days for print/embroidery.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (Lower risk): Partner with 2-3 garment manufacturers in Shahdara (Patparganj, East Delhi clusters) on piece-rate (no capex). Option B (Higher control): Rent 1000-1500 sq ft unit in Shahdara Industrial Area at ₹15-25/sq ft/month (₹15-40K monthly), hire 4-5 sewing operators (₹12-18K each), setup cutting table, pressing station — total capex ₹8-12L + 3-6 months working capital (₹6-10L).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 15 min via udyamregistration.gov.in). Apply for Udyam-registered GST (₹0, online). If exporting: file IEC application at DGFT portal (₹0, takes 5 days). Optional: GI registration support via Ministry of Textiles (₹1-2L govt subsidy if product qualifies). FSSAI if selling to institutional buyers (₹5-10K). Total registration cost: ₹5-15K, timeline 30-45 days.
5
First Sale
List on Flipkart Fashion and Myntra (free listing, 15% commission). Target first order: ₹2-5L from 1-2 e-commerce platforms. Post first 200-500 units selling in 30 days at ₹400-900 retail price, expect ₹4-8L first-month revenue. Parallel: cold-email Westside, Max, Shoppers Stop wholesale teams (200-300 units per order at ₹200-400 wholesale price).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 operations manager (₹25-35K) and 1 digital marketer (₹18-25K). Rent second production unit or increase first unit to 2000 sq ft (₹25-30K monthly). Add 6-8 sewing operators. Build Instagram presence with 3-5 daily reels (in-house shoots). Apply for PMEGP subsidy (₹10-25L) at this stage. Target ₹25-35L monthly by month 18. Exhibit at India Fashion Week, National Handloom Day events, TradeFed expos.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy (35% of project cost) for manufacturing units with Udyam registration
🏛
Cotton Mission
₹50 lakh to ₹1 crore credit-linked subsidy for RMG manufacturers using 100% cotton; covers capex for spinning, weaving, dyeing, stitching
🏚
Delhi Enterprise Support Scheme
₹1 crore interest-free loan for textile MSMEs with Udyam registration, repayment starts after 24 months
🏚
Delhi Startup Initiative
₹25 lakh grant (non-dilutive) for women founders or first-gen founders in manufacturing; covers rent, capex, inventory
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
