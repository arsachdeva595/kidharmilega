Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Morena ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit mustard cultivation clusters around Sabalgarh and Joura tehsils in Morena. Connect with Morena District Cooperative Society and local oil millers to understand seed quality, pressing seasons (Oct-Dec peak), and pricing cycles. Attend agricultural extension training at Morena KVK (Krishi Vigyan Kendra).
2
Source Raw Materials
Source mustard seeds directly from farmer cooperative societies in Morena at ₹4,500-5,200/quintal (March-April contracts offer 10-15% discount). For first 1,000 liters production, allocate ₹80,000-₹1,20,000 for seeds (20-25 quintals) plus ₹15,000 for packaging and ₹10,000 for processing/bottling at local oil mills.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established oil mills in Morena town (like those near Banasthali area) for cold-pressing using traditional wooden ghani method (higher quality) or modern expeller units. Negotiate per-liter processing fee of ₹8-12. Ensure mill has ISO 22000 or FSSAI certification. Alternatively, start with 50-liter batch using semi-mechanized portable units (₹1,50,000 capital).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) at udyamregistration.gov.in within 2 days (free, online). Obtain FSSAI license (₹5,000-₹8,000, 7-10 days) specifying oil extraction and bottling. Apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) at icegate.gov.in for export (₹0 cost, 2 days). Register trademark with IP India for brand name (₹5,000-₹9,000, 8-12 months).
5
First Sale
Launch on JioMart, Blinkit, and Amazon Fresh with 500ml and 1L bottles priced ₹250-₹350 per liter (D2C markup). Simultaneously approach retail chains like Big Bazaar, Nature's Basket with wholesale pricing of ₹180-₹200/liter. Partner with regional distributors in Delhi, Jaipur, Indore covering 20-50 retail points. Offer free samples to 30-40 restaurants in Indore/Bhopal.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to 2-3 new cold-pressing units (₹5-8L each) or contract with 5-6 mills. Launch organic mustard oil certification (₹30,000-₹50,000/year) to command 30-40% premium. Develop infused variants (garlic, chili, fenugreek) within 8 months. Build export contracts with 2-3 UK/US distributors via B2B platforms (Global Sources, TradeKey) targeting ₹40-60L annual export revenue by Month 12.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (35% for SC/ST/women, 25% general) for new food processing units with 4+ jobs
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹40-₹50L cluster development grants for food processing with infrastructure support & skill training
🏚
Madhya Pradesh Food Processing Policy 2021
15% capital subsidy + 10% interest subvention for food processing units with minimum ₹10L investment
🏚
Madhya Pradesh Krishi Karman Scheme
support for agriculture-linked enterprises including oil seed processing with priority for ODOP products
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
