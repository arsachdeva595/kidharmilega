Rohtas district in Bihar sits in India's primary menthol production region with favorable subtropical climate for mint cultivation and established mint oil refineries. The district feeds into a supply chain connecting to pharma clusters in Ahmedabad, Indore, and Baddi (Himachal). Global menthol demand is driven by pharmaceutical applications (28% of revenue), with Asia-Pacific accounting for 72% of global output — positioning Rohtas producers as critical suppliers to multinational firms.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Rohtas ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Zero cost: Visit Rohtas mint farming clusters in Jehanabad or Ramnagar areas this week. Call 3-4 established mint oil suppliers (contact via local agriculture office). Attend the weekly Dalmandi haat in Sasaram on Thursday to see fresh mint trading and meet farmers. Register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) before week end.
2
Source Raw Materials
Contact mint oil suppliers in Rohtas's established extraction belt near Ramnagar and Jehanabad. First batch cost: ₹2-3L for 100 kg menthol oil (60-70% purity) or ₹4-6L for pharmaceutical-grade (95%+ purity). Suppliers typically require 7-10 day lead time. Establish relationship with 2-3 growers to ensure consistent ₹1.5-2L/month supply.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent 500-800 sqft semi-finished unit in Rohtas MSME cluster (₹8-12K/month). Partner with existing artisan extraction units on revenue-share (40-50% of margin). Option B: Self-operate small distillery (₹15-25L investment for steam distillation setup + land). Start with 50 kg/month capacity; scale to 200 kg/month within 6 months.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (₹0, 5 mins online). Apply for IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Patna (₹0, 15 days). Pursue GI registration for Rohtas Menthol with State GI cell (₹15-25K, 18-24 months). Obtain pharmaceutical license from Bihar State Drugs Authority if manufacturing formulations (₹20-30K, 30-45 days). Get ISO 22000/GMP certification (₹50-75K, 3-4 months) — required for export.
5
First Sale
First sale: List on IndiaMart (₹5K/month for Gold seller) targeting pharmaceutical distributors. First order likely 25-50 kg from Gujarat pharma company via Udyam-registered invoice. Expected first-month revenue: ₹3-5L at wholesale rates (₹6,000-8,000/kg). Alternatively, pitch 10 kg samples to 5 FMCG formulation houses in Mumbai/Gurgaon within week 1.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: Hire production manager (₹25K/month), export compliance officer (₹20K/month), sales rep (₹15K + commission). Apply for PMEGP subsidy (25-35% capital subsidy) to upgrade distillery. Exhibit at Pharmexcil trade shows (September in Mumbai) and Asia Pack expo. Build direct relationships with top 10 Indian pharma companies. Establish QA lab (₹2-3L investment) for in-house testing to accelerate orders.
