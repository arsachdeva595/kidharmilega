Unnao has a 300+ year legacy of Zari Zardozi craftsmanship, with thousands of skilled artisans concentrated in clusters around Unnao city and surrounding villages. The district's proximity to major silk and thread markets in Lucknow and Kanpur, combined with established supply chains and a trained workforce, makes it the natural hub for this luxury embroidery craft.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Unnao ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Unnao city's Zari Bazaar and Shahganj textile cluster; spend 2-3 days meeting master artisans (Ustads) and traders. Connect with DC Handicraft office in Lucknow. Research online: Unnao ODOP website, Instagram hashtags #ZariZardozi #UnnaoBrocade, and attend Surajkund Craft Mela or India Craft Week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source silk/satin from Kanpur's textile market (₹200-400 per meter); Zari thread from Lucknow's wholesale markets or Mumbai's Dharavi (₹80-150 per roll). First batch for sampling: ₹50,000-₹100,000 for 5-10 pieces. Build relationships with 2-3 trusted thread suppliers for bulk orders.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner directly with 3-5 established Zari artisans in Unnao city (contact via DC Handicraft). Negotiate per-piece rates (₹2,000-₹10,000 depending on complexity). Start with smaller designs, batch orders of 10-15 pieces. Consider hiring 1 master artisan as in-house quality controller (salary ₹15,000-₹25,000/month).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) online at msme.gov.in as handicraft artisan or manufacturer. Apply for IEC code at nearest DGFT office. Create brand identity: trademark name, logo, website. Open GST registration (exempt under handicraft). Open dedicated business bank account. Document artisan partnerships formally.
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy with 8-10 hero pieces and detailed craft story (3-5% seller fees). Post daily on Instagram Reels showing hand-embroidery process (1-2 min videos). Create WhatsApp Business profile for bulk inquiries. Reach out to 20-30 luxury ethnic wear retailers with bulk order offers. Pitch to wedding planners and luxury hotels in Delhi-Mumbai.
6
Scale
After ₹15-20L revenue, hire 2 artisan supervisors and expand to 15-20 artisans. Launch own website with blog on heritage and craft stories. Apply for SFURTI cluster development grant if targeting 50+ artisans. Explore B2B exports via APEDA-registered trading houses. Collaborate with luxury fashion brands for limited collections. Open showroom in Delhi or Mumbai.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
₹15,000 stipend + ₹3L credit at 5% interest for registered artisans; registration at pmsvanidhi.biscraft.gov.in
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy for manufacturing unit; apply at pmegp.gov.in with Udyam registration
🏚
Uttar Pradesh Micro, Small & Medium Enterprise Scheme
15% subsidy on machinery and equipment for handicraft clusters; apply via State MSME directorate
🏚
UP Craft Industry Development Scheme
₹1L-₹5L support for artisan training and cluster development; apply via UP SIDBI or DC Handicraft office
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
