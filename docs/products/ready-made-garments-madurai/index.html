Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Madurai ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Madurai's garment clusters in Avaniyapuram and North Madurai zones. Attend Tamil Nadu Textiles Ministry trade fairs. Interview 10-15 existing garment manufacturers. Study competitors on Flipkart, Amazon India, and export data from DGFT.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fabrics from Coimbatore textile mills (bulk orders: ₹2-5 lakhs for 500-1000 units). Source accessories from Chennai's Purasawalkam market. First batch cost: ₹3-8 lakhs for 500-1000 pieces depending on design complexity.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with Madurai-based tailoring units in Avaniyapuram industrial area or Sellur zone. Start with job work orders to avoid capital investment. Alternatively, lease 500-1000 sq ft production space and hire 5-10 tailors. Cost: ₹15,000-30,000/month plus labor.
4
Brand & Register
Register as Udyam (MSME) online within 5 minutes. Apply for IEC from Madurai DGFT office (₹0, online). File trademark with IP India for brand name (₹4,500). Set up GST registration (18% applicable). Create brand identity via local designer (₹5,000-15,000).
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon, Flipkart, and Meesho with 50-100 SKUs. Run Instagram and TikTok campaigns with micro-influencers (budget: ₹50,000/month). Approach retail chains in Madurai, Chennai, Bangalore. Register on B2B platforms like Global Trade Point and IndiaTradeKey for export leads.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand SKU portfolio by 200+ designs. Establish own retail outlet in Madurai. Scale export by attending trade shows (TFIT Chennai, IGATEX). Hire a dedicated export/logistics manager. Aim for ₹50 lakh revenue by month 12.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh loan at 5% interest for garment micro-enterprises with 25% subsidy.
🏛
MUDRA Yojana
unsecured loans up to ₹10 lakh for garment businesses registered as MSME.
🏚
Tamil Nadu Textile Policy 2020
7% capital subsidy on plant/machinery for garment units with export component.
🏚
TANHEDCO Scheme
raw material supply at subsidized rates and working capital support for MSME garment manufacturers.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
