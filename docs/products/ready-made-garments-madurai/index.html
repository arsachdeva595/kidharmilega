Raw materials flow from Tiruppur's 5000+ textile mills 150km north and Coimbatore dye houses. The district exports Rs 1,39,349.6 crore annually to UAE, UK, Germany, Japan via established shipping corridors at Cochin (250km) and Chennai ports (140km).

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Madurai ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Madurai's Avinashi Road garment cluster (free), walk 5-6 units, call 3 fabric suppliers in Tiruppur (phone costs only), attend Madurai District MSME monthly meet (free, check on madurai.nic.in). Talk to 2 existing RMG makers about supplier costs and lead times. Zero investment, 3-5 days.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source cotton-poly blends from Tiruppur (Sooriya Mills, Sri Gajapati Textiles) at Rs 80-140/meter for bulk. First batch: 500 meters × Rs 110 = Rs 55,000. Accessories from Madurai's Ramakrishnapuram market (buttons Rs 0.50-2, zippers Rs 8-15/piece). Lead time: 7-10 days from order to delivery.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent 1200 sq.ft unit in Avinashi Road cluster (Rs 12-18K/month), hire 4-6 tailors (Rs 8-12K/month each), buy 2 sewing machines (Rs 35K each) + pressing equipment (Rs 15K). Total: Rs 3.5-4.5L first 2 months. Option B: Partner with existing 50-100 person tailoring units (Srinivasan Garments, Nayagam Apparel) for outsourced stitching, pay Rs 80-120/garment + profit sharing. Option B is safer, needs Rs 1.5-2L only.
4
Brand & Register
Day 1: Register Udyam (free, 30 mins online, udyam.gov.in). Cost zero. Month 1: Get GST registration (1-2 weeks, free for MSME). Month 2: Apply for ODOP branding support via Ministry of Textiles (free tag usage, no cost). If exporting: Obtain IEC code (Rs 0, online via DGFT portal, 3 days). GI registration not applicable (district has no GI for RMG — competitive advantage for you). Total compliance cost: Rs 500-1000 (filing, certification copies).
5
First Sale
First 2 months: Sell via own WhatsApp catalog + Instagram (20-30 orders/month, Rs 25-35K revenue). Month 3: List on Amazon Handmade (free, 15% commission), Flipkart (free listing, 8-10% commission). Target first real order: bulk institutional (school, 100 units uniforms = Rs 40-60K). First month realistic: Rs 60-90K revenue from 30-40 orders across channels. Breakeven hits month 10-12 at Rs 5-7L cumulative revenue.
6
Scale
At Rs 10L/month: Hire production manager (Rs 25-30K), expand to 2 workshop units, partner with 2-3 tailoring clusters for capacity. Join PM MITRA park (approved in Tamil Nadu, offers subsidized land + shared facilities, check details on textiles.gov.in). Apply for SFURTI cluster subsidy (Rs 25L grant for 8-10 artisans per unit, payable over 2 years). Exhibit at Madurai Trade Fair (Feb-March), Chennai Fabric Show (Jan), Gandhinagar Garment Expo (Delhi). Build export team, hire bilingual person for UAE/UK buyer relations (Rs 20-25K/month).

Where to Set Up

Infrastructure ready hai — plot lo aur shuru karo

Avinashi Road Garment Cluster
Madurai's primary RMG manufacturing zone with 150+ registered units, 1000-3000 sq.ft units available on rent (Rs 12-20K/month), shared facilities: pressing units, fabric testing lab, common effluent treatment plant, buyer meet hall, 80% occupancy, established supplier network.
MSME Cluster
Madurai SIDCO Industrial Estate
General purpose industrial park with 2-5 acre plots available for lease (Rs 25-40L), power-backed, water supply, basic infrastructure, 20-30 textile units present, farther from established garment supply chain vs Avinashi Road.
Industrial Estate
Madurai Micro & Small Enterprise Cluster (MSEC)
Government-promoted cluster with subsidized rent (50% subsidy for first 2 years) for new registrants, 500-1500 sq.ft units, incubation support, mentoring from SIPCOT, slower turnover approval but lower cost.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM MITRA (Amended Technology for Infrastructure for Textile Manufacturing), free land in cluster + shared stitching facility + export support
applicable to RMG in Tamil Nadu, check textiles.gov.in for Madurai enrollment.
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
Rs 10-25L bank loan with 35% subsidy (MSME), 10% own contribution, Rs 55K processing, applicable if you create 8+ jobs in first 2 years.
🏚
Tamil Nadu MSME Competitiveness Scheme
Rs 50L subsidy (50% of capex) for garment units >Rs 1Cr investment, 2-year tax exemption on VAT/GST.
🏚
Tamil Nadu Textile Promotion Scheme
Rs 15L subsidy for export-oriented units, subsidy on energy costs, reimbursement on export logistics (15% of freight cost up to Rs 2.5L/year).
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
