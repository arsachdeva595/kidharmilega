Granite Cutting and Polishing ki
opportunity, Bapatla mein.

Bapatla district (1,618 km² area, 1.27 million population) is located in northern Andhra Pradesh with quarries in Gurijepalli (Black Granite) and Konidena (Colour Granite) providing raw blocks 270cm x 150cm within 30-50 km radius. Four established clusters (Martur, Ballikurava, Gurijepalli, Santhamaguluru) operate 566 MDL cutting units with standardized wire-saw and polishing infrastructure. Export-oriented units in Maddipadu, Annangi, and Gundlapalli villages (Prakasam district, adjacent) ship to Middle East, Europe, and USA with 321,326 sq.ft dispatched annually.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bapatla ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call 3-4 granite cutters in Martur cluster (contact Bapatla MSME office for numbers, phone 08657-248999), ask their per-sq.ft cost, block size prices, export process. Visit Ballikurava quarry area to see raw block prices directly. Zero cost — doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Negotiate direct with quarry suppliers in Gurijepalli or Konidena (20-40 km away) for raw blocks at ₹120-180 per sq.ft depending on grade. First batch: 50-100 raw blocks (₹180,000-400,000). Clarify delivery, breakage policy, payment terms (typically 15-30 days).
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 3,000-5,000 sq.ft unit in Martur, Ballikurava, or Gurijepalli cluster (₹8,000-15,000/month) with existing wire saws + polishing machines, OR partner with an idle artisan unit owner in exchange for 20-25% revenue share. Wire saw cutting costs ₹8-12 per sq.ft; polishing ₹6-10 per sq.ft. Total unit setup (electricity deposit, working capital) ₹850,000-1,500,000 if buying used equipment.
4
Brand & Register
Register on Udyam (udyamregistration.gov.in, free, 15 min). Get Bapatla Collectorate Udyam certificate print. Apply for IEC code (Importer Exporter Code) via icegate.indiaport.gov.in (₹0, online, 3 days). If targeting premium D2C, file GI certification application with Andhra Pradesh State Handlooms office for 'Bapatla Black Granite' — ₹50,000 legal cost, 1-2 year approval timeline.
5
First Sale
First sale: post 5-10 slab photos on Instagram/Facebook targeting 'Bangalore home interior' + 'countertop granite' keywords. Price wholesale at ₹280-350 per sq.ft (30% margin). Target tile retailers: cold-call 20 shops in Koramangala (Bangalore) with 5 sq.ft free samples + catalog. Expected first order: 30-50 sq.ft from a local fabricator (₹8,000-18,000). List on CommonFloor.com + Magicbricks.com under 'materials suppliers' (free).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month (≈40 sq.ft/day), hire 1 full-time sales person for retail outreach + 1 supervisor for production QC (₹15,000+₹12,000/month). Exhibit at STONA (Stone & Tiles Expo, biannual in Mumbai) and Jaipur Expo to reach builder + architect networks. Upgrade wire technology from 7mm to 1mm precision wires (₹300,000 capex) for 15% waste reduction and premium pricing. Launch B2B portal or WhatsApp catalog for wholesale buyers.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy (25% of project cost) for manufacturing units in MSME category
🏛
MUDRA Loan Scheme
up to ₹10 lakh unsecured loan for equipment purchase, 5-7 year tenor, 8-9% interest rate
🏚
Andhra Pradesh MSME Development Policy 2020
20% subsidy on plant & machinery, ₹2 lakh max per unit, applied retrospectively post-Udyam registration
🏚
AP One District One Product (ODOP) scheme
free branding/packaging support + market linkage through state trade missions, free booth at AP exports fairs
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
