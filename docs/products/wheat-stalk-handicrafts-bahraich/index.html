Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bahraich ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bahraich town weekly haat or gram panchayat cluster area (ask DC office for artisan directory); call 3-5 existing wheat-stalk makers directly (ask local cooperatives for contact); register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in); attend any ODOP or handicraft training session run by District Administration — all zero cost, doable this week
2
Source Raw Materials
Source wheat stalks directly from farmers in Bahraich tehsil (Oct-Nov post-harvest, bulk cost ₹8-12 per kg, need 20-30 kg for first batch); cloth frames from local jute/cotton suppliers in Bahraich town market (₹200-400 per frame). First-batch material cost: ₹4,000-8,000 for 10-15 finished pieces. Supplier contact: approach Bahraich Cooperative Handicraft Union or District Handicraft Board.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A — Rent shared artisan space/workshop in Bahraich town: ₹3,000-6,000/month, 200-400 sq ft. Option B — Partner with 2-3 established artisans on revenue-share (you design, they produce, you handle sales): zero rent, 30-40% of profit to artisan. Start with 20-30 pieces/month in month 1-2. Full production setup (tools, frames, storage): ₹25,000-40,000 one-time.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 15 min) → Apply for ODOP Margin Money Scheme (₹50K-2L subsidy, application via DC office) → Check GI registration status via Controller of Designs office (may take 6-12 months, ₹5,000-10,000 cost); if exporting, get IEC from DGFT (₹0, online, 2-3 weeks). Timeline: Udyam + schemes by month 2, IEC by month 3 if exporting.
5
First Sale
Month 1-2: Launch 5-7 listings on Etsy (free account, $0.20 per listing) with 8-12 product photos + detailed descriptions. First order target: 2-3 pieces from wedding gift/home décor search. Expected first-month revenue: ₹5,000-12,000 from D2C. Simultaneously email 10 gift shops in Lucknow/Delhi with wholesale price sheet (28-35% discount off retail). First wholesale order realistically by month 2-3.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 full-time designer (₹15K-20K/month) to create seasonal collections; partner with 5-8 artisans formally (revenue-share contracts); apply for ODOP Training & Toolkit Scheme (free training, subsidised tools). Exhibit at India International Trade Fair (Sept-Oct), Diwali Home Décor expos in Delhi/Mumbai. Launch corporate B2B vertical with dedicated account manager (₹12K/month). Set up small showroom in Lucknow (₹8,000-12,000/month). Target: ₹20-30L/month by month 18.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
ODOP Margin Money Scheme
subsidy up to ₹2,00,000 for working capital and machinery; interest subvention on bank loans (apply via District Industries Centre)
🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
₹15,000 initial grant + ₹5,000 annual toolkit support + interest-free loan up to ₹1L for registered artisans (eligibility: self-employed traditional craftsperson)
🏚
Uttar Pradesh Handicraft Development Scheme
subsidy on tools, equipment, and training up to 40% of cost (max ₹1,50,000); apply via State Handicraft Board, Lucknow
🏚
ODOP Training and Toolkit Scheme (UP specific)
free skill training for new entrants + subsidised toolkit + certification — covers 6-8 week training programs
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
