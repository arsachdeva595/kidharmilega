Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Rewa ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Rewa Bamboo Cluster (Rewa city center) and speak with 3-4 established bamboo product makers about their supply chains, margins, and export connections — zero cost, do this week. Call the Rewa District Industries Centre on +91-7662-404xxx to get artisan contact list. Attend the weekly Rewa Haat on Saturday to see product varieties and buyer behavior.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw bamboo culms from Rewa Forest Cooperative or registered suppliers in Rewa city — first batch of 500 culms (~₹8,000-12,000) takes 2-3 weeks lead time. Or partner directly with artisans in Rewa Bamboo Cluster who already have supply relationships. Negotiate credit terms (15-30 days) to preserve working capital.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent a 400-500 sqft unit in Rewa Industrial Estate (₹6,000-10,000/month) and hire 2-3 trained artisans at ₹8,000-12,000/month each. Option B: Establish artisan partnerships where they produce against order (zero fixed rent). Option C: Hybrid — rent shared workshop space at ₹3,000-5,000/month and co-produce. Expect ₹40,000-80,000/month operational costs for small-scale production.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online at udyamregistration.gov.in) within 1 week. Apply for GI registration through District Industries Centre if entering premium segment (₹15,000-25,000 cost, 6-8 month timeline) — GI status not required but strengthens export positioning. If exporting, apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) at nearest DGFT office (₹0 cost, 1 week turnaround). Open current account at cooperative bank (DCCB) offering MSME rates.
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy and Amazon Handmade within 2 weeks of production setup — list 10-15 unique pieces with professional photos. Target first wholesale order through Instagram DM outreach to interior designers in Tier 1 cities (expected first order: ₹15,000-30,000). First month realistic revenue: ₹25,000-50,000 if D2C only, ₹60,000-100,000 if one wholesale order lands.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire a dedicated export sales person (₹20,000/month), formalize artisan contracts, invest in consistent product photography/videography setup (₹30,000 one-time), list on 2-3 export marketplaces (Alibaba, Global Sources), apply for VKGUY subsidy. Exhibit at Sambhav Delhi International Handicraft Fair (Jan-Feb) and GiftLand Mumbai to build bulk buyer relationships.
