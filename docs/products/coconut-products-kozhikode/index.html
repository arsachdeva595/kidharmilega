Coconut Products ki
opportunity, Kozhikode mein.

Kozhikode district (population 3.08M, literacy 93.91%) sits in central Kerala's coconut belt with year-round coconut availability. Beypore Port specializes in cargo handling for Malabar spices, coir, and processed foods, with direct shipping routes to UAE, USA, UK, Germany, Singapore, and Saudi Arabia. Wooden chekku clusters are concentrated in Nadapuram, Mukkam, and Thalassery taluks, with 200+ traditional pressing units operating within 30km radius; cold-pressed coconut oil exports from this region exceed ₹2,000 Cr annually.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kozhikode ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Nadapuram coconut market on a Thursday or Friday; call 3-4 wooden chekku operators in Mukkam (find via local cooperative); attend a free MSME cluster demo at Kozhikode DIC office. Zero cost, take photos of pressing setups and note operator names.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fresh coconut from Nadapuram wholesale market or Thalassery coconut cooperative — first batch 1000 coconuts (~₹20,000-25,000). OR partner with existing chekku operator for toll processing at ₹15-20 per liter. Copra (dried kernel) sourcing via Kozhikode Spice Market or Kerala Coconut Development Board approved suppliers.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent 500 sq ft unit in Edakala or Calicut Special Economic Zone (₹8,000-12,000/month); buy 1-2 semi-automated cold-press machines (₹4-6L), labor, packaging. Option B: Contract 80% production to Mukkam chekku operators at toll rate, handle branding + bottling in-house (₹80,000-1.5L setup). Cold-pressing setup takes 6-8 weeks; hot-pressing (traditional) takes 4 weeks.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) online free; apply for GI certification if using 'Kozhikode Wooden Chekku Oil' (₹15,000-25,000, 4-6 months turnaround via Kerala Coconut Board). For export: obtain IEC from DGFT online (₹free, 3 days); get APEDA license if processing (₹5,000, 5 days). Register FPO brand with Food Safety (FSSAI) at ₹500-2,000.
5
First Sale
First sale: contact 5-10 wellness e-commerce brands via LinkedIn or cold email; offer trial 20L order at 10% discount. First platform: Amazon Seller Central (₹6,000 annual); upload 250ml + 500ml SKUs with Kerala Coconut Board certification photos. Target first month ₹40,000-80,000 revenue via 30-50 online orders + 1-2 bulk B2B orders (10-20L).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 1 operations person (₹15,000/month), 1 export coordinator (₹18,000/month). Apply for Kerala Industrial and Export Promotion Policy (2023) incentives — up to 20% subsidy on capital + 5% interest subvention on loans. Exhibit at Beypore Port Trade Fair, Kerala State Food Processing Convention, and international food expos in Dubai (via Niryat Bandhu Scheme logistics support). Expand to coconut flour, desiccated coconut using chekku byproducts.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh loan for food processing units; 25% subsidy (₹6.25L max) for general category
🏛
Ministry of Food Processing SFURTI Scheme
up to ₹18 lakh subsidy for cluster-based processing units; reimbursement of 85% capex for food processing
🏚
Kerala Industrial and Export Promotion Policy (2023)
20% capital subsidy on machinery for food processing + 5% interest subvention on SIDBI loans
🏚
Niryat Bandhu Scheme
logistics support, port handling fee reductions (15-20%), export documentation help for first-time exporters
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
