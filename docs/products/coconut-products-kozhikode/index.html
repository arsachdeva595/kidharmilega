Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kozhikode ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kozhikode's Chelari coconut processing cluster and Kodiyeri artisan chekku workshops. Interview 5+ existing producers at Kozhikode Municipality's ODOP center. Study market at Calicut Spice Market and connect with 2-3 existing exporters. Allocate 3-4 weeks for hands-on learning.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source coconut kernels directly from coconut farmers in Thalassery and Vadakara taluks (₹50-60/kg, first batch ₹1.5-2L for 3,000kg). Establish contracts with farmer cooperatives like Kozhikode District Coconut Producers Co-operative. Negotiate 3-month payment terms.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established chekku operators in Chelari cluster on revenue-sharing (typically 8-12% of gross margin). Alternatively, lease a 2,000 sq ft facility (₹15-20K/month) and procure 1-2 wooden chekku machines (₹4-6L each) or modern screw presses (₹8-12L). Hire 4-6 trained operators.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSE) with FSSAI license (₹10K, 15 days processing). Apply for IEC code via Directorate General of Foreign Trade. Register trademark at IP India. Obtain ISO 22000 certification (₹50-80K) for export readiness. Design packaging meeting FSSAI label requirements.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh and BigBasket within weeks 1-4 (target 200+ units/month). Build D2C store on Shopify (₹500/month). Contact 10-15 organic retailers in Bangalore, Delhi, Mumbai via LinkedIn. Attend Natural & Organic Products Expo in Delhi. Approach 3-5 FMCG distributors in Kerala.
6
Scale
After 6 months, if doing ₹15L+ monthly: (1) Launch 2-3 new products (coconut flour, milk) using same supply chain. (2) Expand to 3-4 distribution clusters across metros. (3) Establish export partnership with 1-2 trading houses for 40% volume growth. (4) Build B2B channel targeting pharma and cosmetic companies.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh government subsidy for food processing units in ODOP districts, with margin money assistance of 5-15%.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹20 lakh for cluster development, artisan skill training, and common facility centers in Kozhikode coconut cluster.
🏚
Kerala SIDBI Finance Scheme
concessional loans up to ₹50 lakh at 2% lower interest rate for food processing ODOP entrepreneurs, with 5-year repayment window.
🏚
KSCB (Kerala State Co-operative Bank) Agricultural Term Loan
up to ₹10 lakh for raw material procurement and equipment at 1-2% concessional rates for registered producer groups.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Marketing

Ye marketing ideas try karna mat bhulna

  • Instagram Reels series (3-4 per week) showcasing wooden chekku extraction process, farmer stories, and nutritional benefits. Tag wellness influencers (10K-100K followers) for organic reach. Budget: ₹5K/month for micro-influencer partnerships.
