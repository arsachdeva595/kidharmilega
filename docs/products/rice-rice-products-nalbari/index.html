Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Nalbari ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Zero-cost: Visit Tihu cluster haat (weekly market) to talk to 5 rice traders; call 2-3 rice mill owners in Nalbari district (Udyam directory online); attend Assam Agri-Tech meet if running. Spend 1 week mapping farmer co-ops, input costs, and buyer networks — all free.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source paddy directly from Tihu and Borigog-Banbhag village clusters at harvest (Sept-Nov for Sali, June-Aug for summer); first-batch cost ₹40,000-80,000 for 10-20 bags (500-1000 kg); negotiate with farmer co-ops for volume discounts and direct harvest contracts.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing rice mills in Tihu or Borigog-Banbhag for milling and processing on revenue-share basis (40-50% of finished product to you); or lease 500-1000 sq ft in Nalbari industrial area, install a mini rice mill (₹1.5-3L), hire 2-3 workers (₹8,000/month each). Start with one rice variety (scented or Sali) for focus.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free online, 30 min); apply for ODOP support from Nalbari district collector's office (₹50,000-2L subsidy, 30-60 days); get FSSAI license (₹5,000-15,000, 45 days); consider GI registration if sourcing from heritage Nalbari terroirs (₹2-5L legal cost, 2-3 years); if exporting, get IEC from Directorate General of Foreign Trade (₹5,000, 15 days).
5
First Sale
First sale: List on Amazon Fresh / Jiomart (free listing, 30-day approval); price rice flour at ₹120-150/kg DTC (vs ₹80/kg wholesale); post first 50 kg order via social media WhatsApp groups targeting urban Assamese families; expect first month ₹20,000-40,000 revenue from 15-20 retail orders + 1 institutional bulk order.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 1 supply chain manager to source 500+ kg paddy monthly; contract 2 more retail channels (Blink, local kirana chains); expand to 3-4 rice varieties and rice-based products (flakes, puffed rice, rice mix); exhibit at Assam Food & Agri Expo; build direct B2B relationships with 10+ food manufacturers; invest ₹1.5L in small cold storage to capture off-season margins.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
₹25L subsidy (35% for SC/ST, 25% others) for setting up rice mill and processing unit
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹3L-10L cluster support for group of rice processors; Nalbari ODOP clusters may be eligible
🏚
Assam ODOP Scheme
direct cash subsidy of ₹50,000-2,00,000 for branded product development, packaging, certification, branding for one-district-one-product entries
🏚
Assam State Agricultural Loan Scheme
concessional credit up to ₹10L at 4% interest for agricultural processing units; processing must be linked to Nalbari paddy sourcing
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
