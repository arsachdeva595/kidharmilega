The district's 1208 villages contain documented makers of temple carvings, decorative beading, and mouldings with 20-30 year family experience. Buyer proximity includes metros within 24-48 hour shipping and direct access to institutional buyers (hotels, restaurants, architects) across eastern India.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Dhenkanal ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kamakhyanagar woodcarving cluster (25km from Dhenkanal town) and Parjang brass works area — call 3-4 established makers, ask their monthly output and current buyer list. Register free Udyam account online (takes 15 min). Attend any local Haat Bazaar in Dhenkanal town to see retail pricing. Zero cost, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from Kamakhyanagar (wooden carvings, temples, panels) or Parjang (brass works). First batch sourcing: ₹1.5-3L buys 15-20 finished pieces or raw carved components. Negotiate 45-day payment terms. Visit supplier workshops directly — no middleman markup.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Artisan partnership (₹0 upfront, 30% commission on sales) with 2-3 makers in Kamakhyanagar. Option B: Rent 800-1200 sqft unit in Dhenkanal town (₹3000-5000/month) + hire 1-2 apprentices (₹8000-12000/month each) + buy finishing tools (₹40000). Production cycle: 14-21 days for custom pieces.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 15 min). Apply for PM Vishwakarma subsidy (₹15000 direct, if eligible). Consider GI registration for Dhenkanal Wooden Handicrafts (₹8000-15000 one-time, gives export edge). If exporting: Register IEC with DGFT (₹0, online). Total registration cost: ₹8000-15000, timeline: 30-45 days.
5
First Sale
First sale: List 10-15 products on Etsy or Amazon Handmade (free listing). Target first order from interior design studios in Bangalore/Mumbai (₹25K-50K order size) or export buyer inquiry via TradeKey. Expected first-month revenue: ₹40K-80K (3-5 orders). Use Shopify (₹99/month) for custom website once you have 5+ SKUs.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: Hire 1 dedicated sales/marketing person (₹20000/month), rent larger workspace (1500-2000 sqft, ₹6000-8000/month), establish supply contracts with 3-4 artisans, exhibit at India Handicrafts and Handlooms Expo (Delhi, Mumbai), and register for export credit line via EXIM bank. Transition 40% revenue to wholesale and 30% to export by month 12.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Yojana
₹15000 direct subsidy + ₹125000 collateral-free loan at 5% for artisans and artisan-led businesses (Handicraft category)
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25-35% subsidy on equipment cost (max ₹10L) if registered as manufacturing unit, reimbursement after proof
🏚
Odisha Handicraft Development Scheme
20% export promotion subsidy for GI-registered products and 10% for non-GI handicrafts on FOB value (min ₹10L annual export)
🏚
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹25-50L cluster development grant if you operate as producer group (3+ makers), covers workspace, tools, marketing
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
