Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sukma ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sukma artisan clusters in Konta and Bijapur blocks; interact with master craftsmen; study Bastar wooden craft legacy at regional handicraft centers; connect with DC Handicraft office in Sukma town.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw wood from licensed timber suppliers in Raipur (₹2-5L per ton) or directly negotiate with forest department approved vendors; initial batch of mixed wood samples ₹50-75K; establish relationships with local artisan cooperatives.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established artisan groups in Sukma (20-50 craftspeople available); visit registered craft societies; negotiate per-piece rates (₹500-₹5000 depending on complexity); consider opening small workshop in Dantewada or Sukma town.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online at udyamregistration.gov.in); obtain IEC code from DGFT for export readiness; register brand name with IPAB; get Handicraft Artisan Certificate from DC office; apply for SC/ST/Tribal certifications if applicable.
5
First Sale
Launch Instagram/Pinterest accounts showcasing artisan stories; list on Amazon Handmade, Etsy, and Flipkart Artisanal; approach interior design studios in Mumbai, Bangalore, Delhi; pitch to premium hotel chains; use WhatsApp Business for direct B2B orders.
6
Scale
Expand artisan network to 100+ craftspeople; open retail experience centers in metro cities; establish export partnerships with international home décor distributors; create tiered product lines (budget to luxury); invest in professional photography and brand storytelling.
