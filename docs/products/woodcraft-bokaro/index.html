The district connects via NH-2 to Delhi (1,050 km), Kolkata (450 km), and Bangalore (1,800 km), enabling both domestic B2B sales to design studios and institutional buyers, and direct exports. Ranchi (80 km) and Jamshedpur (120 km) are adjacent high-income consumption hubs with active interior design and corporate sectors.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bokaro ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bokaro woodcraft artisan cluster in Jagjivan Ram Nagar area or Chas block this week — attend free open studio hours or call DC Handicraft office (05546-222222) for cluster introduction. Register on Udyam (udyamregistration.gov.in) for free MSE registration — takes 15 mins online. Zero cost, zero obligation.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source timber from Bokaro Forest Department approved suppliers in Chas block (sal and teak cost ₹8-12K per cubic meter) or private timber traders near Tenughat. First batch of 20-30 pieces (mix of boxes, coasters, utensils) costs ₹40-60K raw materials. Identify 2-3 artisan workshops in Jagjivan Ram Nagar or Ghatshila willing to do piece-rate work (₹500-2K per piece depending on complexity).
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 400-600 sq.ft workshop space near Bokaro town (₹3-5K/month) with basic hand tools (chisels, saws, sanders, finish supplies) — ₹80-120K one-time investment. OR partner with 3-4 existing artisans on commission basis (save ₹400K rent). Invest ₹60-80K in finishing supplies (wood stain, lacquer, polish), storage racks, and packing material. Total production model: 30-50 pieces per month at ₹200-500 net cost per piece.
4
Brand & Register
File Udyam registration (free, online) within week 1. Apply for PM Vishwakarma scheme (₹10K direct cash subsidy + 5% interest subvention on ₹3L credit). File GI registration application with Ministry of Commerce (₹6K filing fee + ₹20K professional charges) — processing takes 18-24 months but can proceed in parallel. Get GST registration (₹0, online) and IEC code from nearest DGFT office for export eligibility.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Handmade with 8-10 product listings (₹0 seller setup, 15% commission). Post 3 pieces/week on Instagram with studio process reels. First sale target: direct orders from Delhi-NCR interior studios via LinkedIn outreach — expect ₹25-40K first order within 4 weeks. Month 1 revenue target: ₹40-70K gross from 2-3 orders.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month (months 8-10): hire 1 design assistant (₹12-15K/month), partner with 6-8 artisans instead of 3, invest ₹1.5-2L in branded packaging and photography. Apply for SFURTI cluster scheme support (₹15L subsidy for cluster development). Exhibit at Delhi Design Week, Bangalore Home Show (₹80-120K each). Establish B2B accounts with 3-5 corporate interior firms and 1-2 hotel chains.

Carpentry ya furniture ka background bohot madad karega, lekin zaroori nahi. Agar tu hand-skill hai toh 4-6 hafte mein skilled artisans ke saath seekh sakta hai. Quality checking aur design sense zaroori hai. Ek apprenticeship karo Bokaro cluster mein 2-3 mahine pehle.
PM Vishwakarma mein ₹10K cash + ₹3L credit at 5% interest. PMEGP mein 25-35% subsidy. Jharkhand ODOP Export scheme mein 5% subsidy on exports. GI registration support available from DC Handicraft office. Total ₹15-25L free money sambhav hai agar sab schemes use kare.
Amazon Handmade, Etsy, aur LinkedIn outreach. Ranchi aur Delhi ke interior designers ko directly call kar. Corporate gifting platforms jaise Fabelle Experiences aur Giftopolis. First 5-10 orders apne network aur local haats se hi aayenge. Online mein ₹5-15K orders monthly expected hain.
Bilkul. USA, UK, Germany, Australia, Singapore mein strong demand hai. IEC code + GST registration ho toh Etsy aur eBay par directly sell kar. Logistics cost ₹8-12K per shipment (1-2 kg boxes). Profit margin 45-60% hota hai FOB mein. Lekin bulk minimum orders ₹2L+ hote hain wholesale buyers se.
