Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bathinda ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bathinda's Kinnow belt (Mahendra Garh, Rampura Phul clusters) and connect with established growers through Punjab Agricultural University. Attend monthly produce auctions at Bathinda APMC market to understand pricing and quality standards.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified Kinnow saplings from ICAR-IISR Lucknow or Punjab Agricultural Department nurseries (₹15-25 per sapling). First-batch cost for 1 hectare: ₹2-3 lakhs including saplings, irrigation, fertilizers. Bulk sourcing from Abohar region suppliers reduces costs by 15-20%.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established orchardists in Bathinda through revenue-sharing agreements (40-60 split) or lease agricultural land at ₹30,000-50,000 per hectare annually. Hire local farm managers familiar with Kinnow cycles. Set up minimal processing unit (washing, grading, packing) at estimated ₹50-75 lakhs.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam as agricultural producer enterprise. Obtain IEC code from DGFT for exports. Get APEDA recognition for export operations. Register brand name with trademark office. Obtain FSSAI license for any processing. Cold storage certification from state horticulture board.
5
First Sale
Launch on JioMart, BigBasket, and ONDC platform within 3 months. Establish relationships with APMC merchants at Bathinda market for wholesale distribution. Contact modern retail chains' procurement teams (Croma Fresh, Big Basket Direct). Explore export through APEDA-registered trading partners. Start with Instagram/Facebook farm visibility and WhatsApp group sales.
6
Scale
After 6 months, invest in cold chain infrastructure (₹80-120 lakhs) for year-round supply. Develop branded packaged juice line. Establish direct export partnerships with UK and Middle Eastern distributors. Partner with 2-3 modern retail chains for dedicated SKUs. Launch B2B platform for restaurant and juice manufacturer supply.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM AMIRBAD (Agro Micro Refinance for Agricultural Business Development)
up to ₹5 lakh collateral-free loan for agri-entrepreneurs
🏛
Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)
direct income support of ₹6000/year to farmers; also PMEGP for agri-processing units up to ₹25 lakh subsidy
🏚
Punjab Agricultural Loans Scheme
subsidized term loans up to ₹25 lakhs at 4% interest for citrus farming and value-added agro-processing
🏚
Punjab State Horticulture Mission
50% subsidy on cold storage, pack-houses, and processing infrastructure for registered producers
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
