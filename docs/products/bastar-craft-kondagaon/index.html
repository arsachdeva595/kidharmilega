opportunity, Kondagaon mein.

Kondagaon district (3,423 km²) in central Chhattisgarh sits 260km south of Raipur via NH55, with direct access to termite mound soil (locally called 'chikti mitti') and beeswax suppliers within 40km radius. The Kondagaon craft cluster concentrates over 150 artisans specializing in bell metal craft (Dhokra), wrought iron, terracotta, and bamboo work, producing both domestic retail and B2B export volumes. Buyers span premium home-décor retailers in Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, US/UK craft platforms (Etsy, Uncommon Goods), and corporate gifters seeking authenticated tribal art.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kondagaon ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kondagaon cluster (town center, 2-3km radius) on a Tuesday or Thursday; meet 3-4 established artisans (ask Kondagaon municipality office for contacts); attend the weekly Kondagaon haat bazaar to observe buyer behavior and wholesale pricing. Call 2-3 raw material suppliers (termite mound soil vendors) in the cluster to understand sourcing logistics — zero cost, 4-6 hours total.
2
Source Raw Materials
Identify 2-3 artisans within Kondagaon cluster willing to accept custom orders at ₹500-2,000 per piece (figurines, jewelry, utilitarian items). Source beeswax from local beekeepers (Kondagaon taluk) at ₹300-400/kg; termite mound soil from local excavators at ₹50-100 per 50kg bag. First batch of 20-30 mixed pieces costs ₹15,000-25,000 depending on complexity. Supplier area: Kondagaon town and surrounding villages (Kusum Nagar, Bacheli road).
3
Produce / Partner
Start with artisan-partnership model: no factory needed initially. Provide designs (2D sketches or WhatsApp images), advance artisans 50% payment, inspect weekly, collect finished pieces. Rent a 200-300 sq.ft. retail/storage space in Kondagaon town center or Gole Bazar for ₹2,000-4,000/month for display and packing. Total setup cost for 6-month runway: ₹80,000-1,20,000 (space, initial inventory, packing materials, basic photography setup).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) online at udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minutes. Apply for GI registration benefit under Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region (already GI-certified, but claim as registered producer on Ministry ODOP portal). If planning export, apply for IEC (Importer Exporter Code) from DGFT via icegate.indiaport.gov.in — ₹500 and 5-7 days processing. Total cost: ₹500, timeline: 2 weeks.
5
First Sale
First sale: list 15-20 pieces on Etsy (3% commission + ₹8/item); simultaneously approach 2-3 wholesale buyers in Raipur and Mumbai via email + WhatsApp catalog. Typical first online order: ₹3,000-8,000; first wholesale inquiry: ₹25,000-50,000. Expect first sale within 3-4 weeks of listing. Target first-month revenue: ₹40,000-80,000 (2-3 D2C orders + 1 small wholesale deal).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: move to dedicated 500-800 sq.ft. production/showroom space in Kondagaon (₹6,000-8,000/month); hire 2-3 full-time artisans to expand output; invest ₹50,000 in photography/videography for Reels and Instagram Ads; apply for SFURTI scheme (₹25L grant) for cluster development; exhibit at Delhi Haat, Mumbai Craft Bazaar, and major trade shows; build export supply chain with freight forwarder in Raipur for weekly shipments to USA/UK buyers.

Government Schemes

Prime Minister Vishwakarma Scheme
₹15,000 toolkit grant + 5% collateral-free credit up to ₹1L; beneficiary must be registered artisan (register via online portal, free).

PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (35% for general category, 55% for SC/ST/women) for setting up production unit; processing via SIDBI, 3-4 month timeline.
