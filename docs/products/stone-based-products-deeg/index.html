Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Deeg ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit active quarries in Deeg town (contact Deeg Quarry Owners Association) and Basni area. Study pricing at nearby aggregates markets (Bayana, Bhangarh). Analyze competitor costs on Indiamart and Construction Aggregates Council. Attend All India Stones & Aggregates Convention to understand supply chain.
2
Source Raw Materials
Secure mining lease from Rajasthan Mineral Development Corporation (RMDC) or acquire operational quarry (₹2-5 Cr investment). First batch: order 500-1000 MT blasting materials from local suppliers in Deeg (₹15-20 L). Engage contract crushers for initial processing.
3
Produce / Partner
Either operate own quarry (requires mining engineer, environmental clearance, GST registration) or partner with licensed quarry operators in Deeg for toll crushing. Start with mobile crushing unit (₹80-150 L) and invest in screening equipment. Employ 15-25 skilled workers; partner with local contractors for blasting and extraction.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam with MSME (apply at msme.udyamregistration.gov.in). Obtain IEC code from DGFT for exports. Get environmental clearance from Rajasthan State Pollution Control Board. Register business entity (Pvt Ltd or Partnership). Obtain mining lease certification and ISO 9001 for quality assurance. Secure GST and trade license from Deeg Municipal Corporation.
5
First Sale
List on Indiamart, Tradeindia under 'Stone Aggregates' category with bulk pricing tiers. Direct B2B outreach: email quarry procurement teams at major cement plants (ACC, Dalmia) and construction firms. Bid on government tenders (NHAI, PWD) via eTender portals. Partner with aggregates distributors in Delhi, Bangalore, and Mumbai. Attend Construction Aggregates Expo for client acquisition.
6
Scale
After 6 months, invest ₹1.5-2 Cr in second crushing unit and screening plant to increase capacity to 2000 MT/month. Expand to adjacent districts (Alwar, Mathura) for raw material sourcing. Launch B2C arm via construction material platforms (BuildSupply, Indiamart) for smaller orders. Explore value addition: produce concrete-ready admix, decorative aggregates. Build long-term contracts with top 5 customers for revenue stability.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 L loan (35-50% subsidy) for quarrying/aggregates businesses with 10% beneficiary contribution
🏛
MUDRA Loan Scheme
up to ₹10 L unsecured loan via banks for small aggregates businesses, zero collateral
🏚
Rajasthan MSME Promotion Scheme
5% GSTR refund for registered manufacturers; interest subsidy up to 3% on bank loans
🏚
Rajasthan Mineral Development & Mining Promotion Scheme
10-year mining lease support, concessional royalty rates for MSME aggregates producers
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
