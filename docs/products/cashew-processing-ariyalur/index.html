Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Ariyalur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Ariyalur cashew clusters in Andimadam and Jayamkondam blocks; meet 5-10 established processors; study quality grading at Tamil Nadu Cashew Processing Association; attend food processing expos in Chennai and Bangalore.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw cashew nuts from Kerala and Andhra Pradesh suppliers (contact Cashew Exporters Association); expect ₹8-12 per kg for quality raw nuts; first batch of 5000 kg will cost ₹40-60 lakhs including transport and initial inventory.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing micro-processors in Ariyalur for initial contract manufacturing; or invest in basic roasting unit (₹15-25 lakhs for small setup); hire 8-12 skilled workers from local artisan communities; obtain Food Safety license (FSSAI) before any production starts.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (micro-enterprise classification); apply for IEC from Directorate General of Foreign Trade; obtain FSSAI food safety certification; register trademark at IP office; get ISO 22000 certification for export-ready products.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon, Flipkart, BigBasket with 10-15 kg initial inventory; reach out to Spencer's, Nature's Basket, Reliance Fresh for retail placement; contact corporate gifting platforms like Giftxoxo; use Instagram/Facebook targeting premium audience with lifestyle content.
6
Scale
After 6 months and ₹1+ Cr revenue, invest in automated roasting unit; expand to 20+ retail partner chains; hire dedicated export manager; apply for APEDA registration; open branded retail counter in 3-5 malls; launch 4-5 flavored variants based on consumer feedback.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh loan at subsidized interest for micro-enterprises in food processing sector
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
cluster-level support for artisan groups, machinery subsidies up to ₹40 lakhs for cashew processing units
🏚
Tamil Nadu Food Processing Promotion Scheme
25% subsidy on fixed capital investment for food processing units, applicable for Ariyalur district
🏚
Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) Tamil Nadu
free skill training for workers in cashew processing; certification recognized for wage enhancement
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
