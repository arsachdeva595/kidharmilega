Mustard Oil ki
opportunity, Mahendragarh mein.

Mahendragarh sits at the edge of Haryana's mustard belt, 140 km southwest of Delhi, with direct access to A1 grade mustard seed suppliers in Hisar, Jind, and Punjab's Sangrur district. The district has a concentrated artisan base of 150+ small extraction units using traditional Kacchi Ghani methods, most operating from Mahendragarh town and Satnali tehsil. Buyer markets include Delhi-NCR FMCG retail, national e-commerce platforms (Blinkit, BigBasket), and South Indian Ayurvedic medicine hubs in Bangalore and Hyderabad.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Mahendragarh ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call 3 mustard oil makers in Mahendragarh town (contact via Mahendragarh Chamber of Commerce), visit Satnali haat on Friday morning to see raw mustard seed prices, attend Udyam registration orientation at District MSME Centre (free, weekly sessions). Register free on Udyam portal this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source A1 grade mustard seeds from Hisar wholesale mandi (APMC Hisar, 120 km away) or direct farmers in Jind district — first batch of 1 quintal costs ₹5,500-6,500. Contact Jind Beejopachar Sangh for supplier introductions. Lock in 3-month forward contracts to stabilize pricing.
3
Produce / Partner
Lease a 500 sqft unit in Mahendragarh industrial area (₹4,000-6,000/month) with power connection and water supply, OR partner with existing Kacchi Ghani artisans (3-4 units in Satnali) who will extract for 15-18% of output. Kacchi Ghani unit cost is ₹3-5L (used machines available); outsourcing eliminates this capex.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (₹0, 30 min online), apply for Haryana MSME subsidy under Industrial Development Policy 2015 (₹5L support available). Get FSSAI food license (₹2,000, 15 days). For export: apply for IEC at nearest DGFT office (₹0, online). Apply for GI registration support through DC office if branding around Mahendragarh origin (₹15K-25K cost, ₹50K govt subsidy available).
5
First Sale
List on BigBasket, Blinkit, and Amazon fresh (first SKU listing free). Target first order: 200-300 bottles to a regional FMCG distributor in Chandigarh (expected order size ₹25,000-35,000). First month realistic revenue: ₹60K-90K from mix of D2C (20 orders/month at ₹500 avg) and 1-2 bulk orders.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: move to 1,200 sqft facility, hire 2 processing staff + 1 packaging/QC person. Apply for PMEGP subsidy (₹25L loan, ₹10L subsidy if eligible). Exhibit at India Food Forum (Delhi, Feb-March), GulfFood (Dubai, Feb) for export leads. Build B2B channel via food brokers in Mumbai and Bangalore.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP
Pradhan Mantri Employment Generation Programme: ₹25 lakh term loan + ₹10 lakh subsidy (women/SC/ST/minority get up to 35% subsidy)
🏛
SFURTI
Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries: ₹10-15 lakh cluster-level support for artisan networks, common facility centers, tool kits
🏚
Haryana Industrial Development Policy 2015: ₹5 lakh MSME subsidy + capital investment subsidy up to 20% for new units
🏚
Haryana Food Processing Cluster Development: ₹50 lakh support for setting up common processing centers in eligible clusters (Mahendragarh eligible)
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
