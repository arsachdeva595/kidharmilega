Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Nayagarh ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Nayagarh town's metalworking clusters near Sadar Bazaar and Baripada areas; connect with DC Handicraft office for artisan registrations; study 3-4 established family-run units producing bells, urns, and figurines.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw brass ingots and bell metal from suppliers in Cuttack (40km away) or Rourkela industrial area; first batch of 100kg mixed alloy costs ₹1.5-2L; establish supplier relationships for recurring monthly purchases of ₹80-120K.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3-5 registered artisans from Nayagarh Artisan Association; start with shared workshop space (₹15-20K/month rental) or home-based production; train workforce on design specifications and quality standards; target 80-120 units monthly in first year.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME registration) online within 3 days at udyamregistration.gov.in; apply for IEC code via DGFT portal for export readiness; register brand name with IP India; obtain Hallmark certification for brass products from BIS.
5
First Sale
Launch Instagram shop with product photography and artisan backstories (₹0 platform cost); list on Etsy and Amazon Handmade (8-12% commission); directly approach 20-30 interior design studios and home décor retailers in tier-2 Indian cities; attend Vibrant Gujarat/India International Trade Fair.
6
Scale
After 6 months, hire a marketing specialist; establish wholesale distribution in 3-4 metro cities; apply for SFURTI cluster development support; launch B2B portal for hospitality buyers; explore US/EU market participation through export agencies.
