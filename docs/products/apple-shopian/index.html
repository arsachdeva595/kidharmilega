Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Shopian ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Shopian town and Samba orchard cluster (15km from Srinagar); meet farmers at Shopian Fruit Mandi; study cold chain infrastructure at Shopian Cold Storage Association; analyze market trends at Kashmir Chamber of Commerce.
2
Source Raw Materials
Procure saplings from Kashmir University's Department of Horticulture or registered nurseries in Shopian; source from local farmers' cooperatives; first-batch setup (1-2 hectares) costs ₹8-12 lakhs including land lease, saplings, and basic tools.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established orchardists in Shopian for first 2-3 years via revenue-sharing model; alternatively, lease 1-2 hectares directly and hire local skilled farm labor; establish informal arrangement with Shopian Fruit Growers Union for technical guidance.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (micro-enterprise) under Ministry of MSME; obtain IEC code from DGFT for exports; register brand name with trademark office; get FSSAI license for value-added products; obtain organic certification from IFOAM-recognized body in India.
5
First Sale
Launch on BigBasket, Blinkit, Grofers for metro cities; list on Amazon Fresh and Flipkart's fresh section; create WhatsApp catalog for direct B2C in Tier 2 cities; contact Reliance Fresh and Nature's Basket for retail placement; approach FedEx/DTDC for export logistics.
6
Scale
After 6 months, establish own cold storage unit (₹20-30L investment); develop 2-3 value-added SKUs (juice, jam); build brand presence via Instagram Reels showing orchard-to-table journey; partner with 2-3 export houses for international reach; establish corporate gifting channel with hotels and premium restaurants.
