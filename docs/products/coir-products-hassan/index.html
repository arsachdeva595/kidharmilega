Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Hassan ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Hassan's established coir clusters in Belur and Arsikere taluks; connect with artisan groups at Hassan District Handicraft Association; study competitors on Etsy and Indian handicraft exporters; attend IITF Delhi or IHGF Delhi exhibitions.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw coir fiber from Mangalore or Cochin coir suppliers (₹40-60/kg for quality fiber); negotiate bulk pricing for 500kg initial batch (₹25,000-35,000); visit local artisan cooperatives for semi-finished products at better margins.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing artisan groups in Hassan (contact through District Handicraft Association) on job-work basis (20-25% commission) or hire 2-3 trained artisans directly; set up small 500 sq ft workspace with basic equipment (looms, cutting tools, drying racks); production capacity: 50-80 units/month initially.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) classification; obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Bengaluru for export readiness; trademark brand name with IP Office; apply for Handicraft Mark certification; open GST registration (5% applicable).
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy (20% commission) with 50+ high-quality product photos; simultaneously register on Amazon Artisan; create Instagram business account with behind-the-scenes artisan content; approach 5-10 local boutique hotels and homestays in Hassan/Coorg for initial B2B orders; contact Indian handicraft exporters for wholesale partnerships.
6
Scale
After 6 months, hire 2 more artisans and establish design studio; apply for SFURTI cluster development grant; expand product line with seasonal collections; establish direct export relationships with 2-3 European retailers; build corporate gifting vertical with customization capabilities.
