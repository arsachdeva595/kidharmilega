Aipan Products ki
opportunity, Nainital mein.

Nainital district in Uttarakhand's foothills produces hand-painted Aipan art — a folk design tradition unique to the Kumaon region, using locally sourced red and white pigments applied to canvas, ceramics, and fabric. The 15-artisan cluster is concentrated in villages around Nainital town, with raw materials available within 20-50 km of the district center. Demand comes primarily from Delhi NCR and metro cities where returned migrants and affluent families seek authentic cultural home decor; the success story of Minakshi Khati shows annual order volumes of 1000+ are achievable at ₹30-35K monthly revenue per artisan lead.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Nainital ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call Minakshi Khati's Minakrti: The Aipan Project (contact via Nainital DC Handicraft office or Instagram); visit Nainital town bazaar to see Aipan products in shop displays; attend the Uttarakhand Tourism Festival (if scheduled) where Aipan is exhibited; register free on Udyam portal to check existing Aipan MSMEs in district.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source red and white pigments from Ranikhet or Almora mineral suppliers (30 km away, ₹200-400/kg). Buy cotton canvas and ceramic blanks from Haldwani wholesale markets (25 km, bulk ₹2-5L for first 500 pieces). Negotiate with 2-3 village artisans in Nainital villages for per-piece work at ₹100-300/piece depending on complexity.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 300 sq ft unit in Haldwani MSME cluster (₹8-12K/month) or work from home + partner with artisan women in Nainital villages on commission basis (saves ₹1-2L setup). Artisan partnership model: you provide materials (₹50-100 cost), they paint (₹100-200 labor), you handle design/QC/sales. Start with 200-300 pieces/month across 3-4 product lines (wall paintings, keychains, tea cups, nameplates). Initial raw material + tooling: ₹60-100K.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 1 day online). File GI registration application through DC Handicraft Nainital office under PM Vishwakarma scheme (free, covers ₹500 filing fee). Get IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Lucknow (₹0, online only) if planning exports. FSSAI registration if selling food-contact ceramics (₹5K, optional first year). Timeline: Udyam in 1 day, GI filing in 2-4 weeks, IEC in 1 week.
5
First Sale
First channel: Launch Instagram Shop (free) with 20-30 product photos; target Delhi-based Aipan enthusiasts and NRI gifting groups. First order: expect ₹1500-5000 per customer from Instagram; use Razorpay for payments. Second channel: List 10-15 SKUs on Etsy (₹500 setup) targeting overseas buyers. Expected first month revenue: ₹15-30K from 5-10 D2C orders + 2-3 wholesale enquiries. Ship via India Post Speed Post (₹50-200 domestic, ₹500-1500 international).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 1 full-time operations manager (₹15K/month) + expand artisan network to 8-10 women. Target Tier 1 wholesalers (Urban Ladder, Fab India, boutique chains) — bulk orders of 100-500 pieces. Apply for SFURTI subsidy (₹20L one-time for cluster development, 1-2 months to process) to set up dedicated Aipan workshop. Exhibit at India Art Fair (Delhi, Feb), Handmade Fair (multiple metros), Uttarakhand Tourism Festival (yearly). Launch WhatsApp catalog selling to metro NRIs. Target 3000-5000 units/year by month 18.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
₹25L loan at 5% interest with 35% subsidy for SC/ST/women entrepreneurs; can cover setup + working capital
🏛
PM Vishwakarma Yojana
₹15K direct transfer + free tooling + 5% concessional credit; available for all handcraft artisans, no income cap
🏚
Uttarakhand Handicraft Promotion Scheme
₹50K-2L grant for artisans to purchase modern tools and setup; apply via DC office
🏚
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹20L cluster subsidy for 6-10 member groups; 3-month processing time
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
