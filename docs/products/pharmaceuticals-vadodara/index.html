Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Vadodara ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Industrial Area Phase 1 Vadodara on a Monday morning — meet 2-3 contract pharma manufacturers, call Alembic's supplier relations desk, attend Gujarat Pharmaceutical Association monthly meeting (held in Vadodara, free entry). No cost; doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source APIs from Torrent Chemicals or Dishman Pharma (both in Gujarat); excipients from Signet Chemical Corp (Vadodara); gelatin capsules from Hindustan Capsule Pvt Ltd. First batch (10,000 capsules/tablets) costs ₹2-4L depending on formulation. Samples available within 48 hours.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 2,000-4,000 sqft Grade C/D facility in Industrial Area Phase 1 at ₹8-12/sqft/month (₹16-48K/month). Budget ₹20-30L for tablet/capsule press, blister packing, label printer, QC setup. OR partner with an existing contract manufacturer at ₹40-60/unit for capsules (no capex required, slower margins).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 30 mins). Apply for GMP certification from Gujarat FDA (₹3-5L, 3-4 months). File IEC with DGFT for export (free, 2 weeks). Trademark brand name at IPO (₹5-10K). All-in regulatory cost: ₹5-8L over 4 months.
5
First Sale
Register on 1mg seller portal (free) or approach C&F agents directly in Ahmedabad. First order typically ₹5-15L from a distributor. Expected first-month revenue if manufacturing own product: ₹15-25L (at 20-30% wholesale margin). If using contract manufacturer: ₹8-12L.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire a pharmacist (QA head, ₹30-40K/month), one production supervisor (₹20-25K/month). Add 2-3 formulations in same facility (cost ₹3-5L each). Exhibit at pharma trade shows in Mumbai (Pharmarack, July) and Delhi (Pharma India, Feb). Approach hospital procurement teams directly. Build export compliance: hire a customs consultant (₹15K/month).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
Up to ₹25L loan at 5% interest (40% subsidy on borrowing), 5-year repayment for manufacturing units
🏛
MUDRA Udyam Loan
Up to ₹10L collateral-free loan for registered Udyam enterprises at 8-9% interest
🏚
Gujarat Pharmaceutical Policy 2023
GMP certification subsidy of 50% of audit cost (up to ₹1.5L), patent filing support
🏚
Gujarat MSME Capacity Building Scheme
Free training in pharma quality management, regulatory compliance for first 100 beneficiaries per year
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
