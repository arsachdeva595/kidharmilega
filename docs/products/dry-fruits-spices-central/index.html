Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Central ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Khari Baoli spice market on weekday morning (6-9am), walk the main lanes, call 3 active traders listed on district chamber website (Delhi Vyapari Mahasangh), ask them: current price of 1kg almonds, 1kg turmeric, processing cost per kg, storage options. Register Udyam (udyamregistration.gov.in) free — takes 15 mins. Zero cost this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
First batch: order 50 kg mixed almonds + 30 kg turmeric + 20 kg cumin from established Khari Baoli traders like Chhote Lal & Co or Ramesh Spices (ask for referral from step 1 contact). Cost: ₹40-60K depending on season. Source directly from trader warehouses in Khari Baoli (5-min walk from Chandni Chowk metro). Negotiate 7-10% discount for cash payment.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent 500-1000 sqft processing unit in Naraina or Pusa Road (₹8-15K/month) with basic cleaning, roasting, grinding, packaging equipment (₹80-120K one-time). Option B: Partner with existing processor in Khari Baoli for custom packing (take 20% commission, zero fixed cost). Start with option B to test, switch to option A after first ₹10L in sales.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 15 mins). Get FSSAI license (₹2000-5000, 20 days). If exporting: Get IEC code (₹0, free from DGFT portal). Apply for GI registration support (₹30K max govt subsidy) through Ministry of Food Processing. Trademark your brand name (₹4500, optional). Total: ₹40K, 45 days.
5
First Sale
Week 1: List on Amazon Fresh, Blinkit, JioMart (free listings, 48-hour approval). Week 2: Approach 5 modern retail chains' category managers via WhatsApp (sample + price list). Week 3: First order likely from D2C platform (₹5-10K) or retail chain (₹15-30K). Expected first-month revenue: ₹30-60K from D2C + ₹20-40K from 1-2 retail orders.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: Hire 1 operations person (₹15K/month), upgrade to 1500 sqft warehouse with cold room (₹20K/month). Hire 1 sales/export person (₹18K/month) to manage wholesale + export channels. Participate in Food Processing Ministry exhibitions (₹30-50K per fair). Launch own e-commerce site using Shopify (₹10K/month). Approach restaurants, caterers, food manufacturers via direct sales team. Apply for SFURTI cluster support (₹50L max for 10-person cluster, NABARD-distributed).
