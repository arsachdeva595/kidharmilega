Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Pakur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Baghel Agro Industries cluster in Pakur town (free visit — call Pakur District Industries office at DC Handicraft desk). Attend weekly jute traders meet at Pakur haat on Friday mornings. Download ODOP Jharkhand report and call 2-3 active suppliers listed. Zero cost, do this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Raw jute plants sourced from local farmers in Pakur and Sahebganj blocks — establish direct farm relationships (₹8-12 per kg fresh jute, 500 kg minimum). Process retting locally or source semi-processed jute fiber from Baghel cluster units at ₹25-35/kg. First batch cost: ₹30-50K for 1000 kg.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 600 sqft unit in Pakur MSME cluster or partner with established artisans at Baghel Agro (revenue-share: 60-40 split). Setup includes retting tanks, drying racks, spinning/weaving loom — rent ₹3-5K monthly or buy second-hand equipment ₹80-120K. Initial production: 100-150 meters jute fabric weekly with 2-3 artisans.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (free, 10 mins at pakur.nic.in). File Jharkhand ODOP scheme application with DC Handicraft for 25% capex subsidy (₹50-100K depending on unit size). Apply for IEC code from nearest Export Facilitation Centre (APEDA, Ranchi) at ₹0 — needed for international sales. Trademark brand name at ₹4500. Timeline: 30-45 days total.
5
First Sale
First sale: contact Kolkata jute merchants via Balusters Lane phone directory or LinkedIn outreach — offer 200m sample consignment at ₹48/meter (wholesale). List 5 key jute products on Amazon Handmade (takes 2 weeks approval) — target first 5 orders in month 2. Expected month 1 revenue: ₹15-25K from pre-sales + samples.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 2-3 full-time artisans, upgrade to motorized looms (₹2-3L capex via MUDRA), establish export documentation with customs broker (₹500/shipment). Exhibit at India Trade Promotion Organisation (ITPO) jute fairs and Bangalore Craft Bazaar. Build export clientele in UK, Germany via TechSagar or direct B2B platforms.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
₹15,000 toolkit allowance + 5% interest subvention for artisans registered under ODOP.
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25% subsidy on machinery for SC/ST, 15% for general category, max ₹25L loan.
🏚
Jharkhand ODOP Scheme
25-30% capex subsidy (₹50-150K), zero-interest working capital loan via Mudra.
🏚
Jharkhand Artisan Cluster Development Scheme
subsidy for common facilities like retting ponds, drying yards, training — apply via DC Handicraft.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
