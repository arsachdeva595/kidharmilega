Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kaimur (Bhabua) ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bhabua town's weaving clusters (Amar Nagar, Ram Nagar areas) on a Tuesday; speak to 3-4 established carpet producers for production costs, loom rental rates (typically ₹500-1500/loom/month), and artisan wage rates (₹200-400/day); attend Kaimur district ODOP facilitation center (free) to request supplier lists and artisan cooperative contacts. Call the District DC Handicraft office and ask for current member directory of carpet weavers' associations.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from suppliers in Jaipur (wool, natural fibres) or Surat (nylon, polyester) — first batch (5-10 carpets) costs ₹40,000-80,000 for materials. Partner with 2-3 established Bhabua weavers or rent loom space directly; negotiate designs and timelines. First-batch turnaround: 30-45 days for hand-knotted, 15-25 days for tufted.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (Asset-light): Partner with 2-5 local artisans on contract basis, supply raw materials, pay per finished carpet (₹800-2500/sqm depending on complexity); no fixed overhead. Option B (Owned capacity): Rent 1,000-1,500 sqft workspace in Bhabua (₹3,000-6,000/month), install 5-10 looms (₹20,000-50,000 per loom used/refurbished), hire 2-3 weavers (₹8,000-12,000/month each). Option A requires ₹1-3L working capital; Option B requires ₹8-15L total setup.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 10 minutes online at udyamregistration.gov.in); apply for MSME Udyam support within 30 days of registration. For export, obtain IEC code from DGFT (₹0 online via ecircle.indiacore.gov.in, 3-5 working days). If targeting GI status in future, file GI registration at IP India office (₹4,000-10,000 filing fee, 3-6 month timeline); currently Bhabua carpets have no GI tag. Register on GST portal (free).
5
First Sale
First B2C sale: list 2-3 signature designs on Amazon Handmade or Etsy (₹0 listing, 15% commission); target ₹15,000-40,000 per carpet. First wholesale sale: contact 5-10 interior design studios in Delhi/Lucknow via email with 3-4 photos of samples; expect ₹20,000-100,000 orders with 30-day payment terms. Expected first-month revenue: ₹30,000-80,000 (1-2 sales, post-working capital drain).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: expand to 3-5 looms, hire 1 full-time production manager (₹15,000-20,000/month), add 1 designer/pattern specialist (₹12,000-18,000/month). Exhibit at IHGF Delhi Fair (Feb, Aug) and India Expo Centre trade shows. Open B2B selling to corporate gifting platforms (The Better India, Artisan Cellar). Launch Instagram content (loom-to-carpet reels, artisan stories) with budget ₹2,000-5,000/month. Target hospitality sector (hotel chains, resorts) via LinkedIn outreach. Apply for Production Linked Incentives (PLI) scheme when export order book reaches ₹50L+ (4-6% subsidy on export value).
