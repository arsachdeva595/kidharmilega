Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Karaikal ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Karaikal MSME cluster office (contact Tamil Nadu Industries Dept), call BALA MURUGAN CHEMICALS or SUN CHEM PRODUCTS to ask about raw material suppliers and buyer networks — zero cost, take notes on pricing tiers. Register free on Udyam portal this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Bulk raw materials from Chennai chemical suppliers (Ranipet cluster, 150 km) or direct from GAIL/IOCL depots via trade license. First batch ₹8-12L for pilot quantities. Smaller starters can source from local wholesalers in Pondicherry (30 km) at ₹12-15L per 2-3 month supply.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 1,500-3,000 sq ft unit in Karaikal MSME zone (₹4,000-6,000/month) or lease chemist workshop. Start with basic mixing, pH adjustment, packaging equipment (₹25-35L). Alternative: hire experienced contract chemist + apprentice (₹60K-80K/month combined) to run operations while you handle sales.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (1 day, free). Apply for MSME subsidy under PM Vishwakarma or PMEGP (25-35% subsidy cap ₹10L). Obtain Factory License from Puducherry Pollution Control Board (₹5,000, 15 days). Get IEC code from nearest DGFT office (₹500, online). Apply for chemical handling ISO/ISI certification (₹40-60K, 6 weeks).
5
First Sale
First sale via B2B platform IndiaMART (list 3-5 chemical grades, ₹2-5L monthly order typical). Direct outreach to 10-15 pharma/textile firms in Tamil Nadu (get contact lists from Chamber of Commerce). Target first order ₹3-5L, expect 30-45 day payment terms. Month 1 realistic revenue ₹4-8L.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: add second production shift (hire 2 more workers). Invest in automated mixing tanks (₹15-20L). Get product certifications (USP/BP grades if pharma-focused). Hire sales executive for regional travels. Exhibit at India International Trade Fair, Pragati Maidan or regional chemical trade shows. Target ₹25-30L/month by month 18.
