opportunity, Dhule mein.

Dhule district in northern Maharashtra borders Nandurbar and sits 240 km from the port of Thoothukudi in Tamil Nadu, with direct rail connectivity via South East Central Railway to Nagpur division and beyond. The district's agricultural belt produces six major oilseed varieties year-round, with existing oil extraction units requiring steady supplies of quality de-oiled cake. Established export corridor to Raj-Nandagaon and other bulk buyers reduces entry barriers for new processors.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Dhule ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Dhule's cotton seed oil cluster in Sakri tehsil (free); call 2-3 existing de-oiling units (names available via District Industries Centre); attend the monthly NCDEX commodity haat in Dhule city to hear trader language and pricing
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw soya bean and castor seed from Dhule district's agricultural mandis in Sakri and Nardana (₹35-45 per kg, ₹8-12L for first 200 tonnes); arrange 60-90 day payment terms with suppliers via mandi brokers
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 2,000-3,000 sq ft unit in Dhule MIDC or Sakri industrial area (₹8-15K/month); partner with an existing de-oiler on toll processing basis (5% processing fee) or buy a small screw press (₹25-35L, requires electricity 50kW min, water access); hire 3-4 skilled workers at ₹12-18K/month each
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online); apply for IEC code (₹0, online via ICEGATE) for export capability; file for Spice Board or organic certification if targeting premium buyers (₹15-25K, 2-3 months); GST registration ₹0
5
First Sale
First order: approach large animal feed manufacturers like Godrej, Trident, or local Gujarat-based mills with 50-tonne trial batches; use IndiaMART, TradeKey, or commodity brokers for pipeline orders; first-month revenue potential ₹12-18L if 100 tonnes sold
6
Scale
At ₹10L monthly revenue, hire a sales person (₹20K/month) to manage repeat orders; upgrade to full in-house processing unit (₹80-120L capex); obtain export agency registrations; attend IIFC or commodity trade fairs; target 500+ tonnes/month capacity by month 18
