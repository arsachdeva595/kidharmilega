YSR Kadapa district in central Andhra Pradesh hosts the world-class Barytes mineral belt, with extraction zones concentrated in Pulivendula, Kodur, and surrounding taluks. The district has 4 Revenue divisions connecting to 36 Mandals, with established mining and primary processing infrastructure. Visakhapatnam Port (350km north) and Chennai Port (450km south) provide direct export access; domestic buyers are concentrated in Hyderabad, Bengaluru, Gujarat, and Maharashtra industrial zones.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — YSR Kadapa ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Pulivendula Mining Cluster (60km from Kadapa city) — call 3 Barytes processing units and ask about their input costs, buyer types, and export logistics (₹0 cost, doable in 2 days). Attend the monthly Kadapa District Industries haat in Kadapa town. Register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) using your Aadhaar and PAN.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source Barytes ore from licensed miners in Pulivendula or Kodur taluks — typical first batch: 5 MT at ₹2,500–3,500/MT = ₹12.5–17.5L. Contact the District Minerals Foundation or Kadapa Mining Association for vetted supplier lists. Alternatively, buy processed Barium Sulphate powder from Hyderabad chemical distributors at ₹35–45/kg for smaller-scale startup (1 MT = ₹35–45L).
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 2,000–3,000 sq.ft. unit in Kadapa MSME Cluster or Pulivendula industrial area (₹3–6L/year). Setup includes: 1 rotary kiln or ball mill (₹25–40L), drying + grinding equipment (₹8–15L), bagging & labeling station (₹2–3L), testing lab (₹5–8L). Total capex: ₹40–70L. Alternatively, partner with 2–3 artisan processors on ₹2–3L per unit margin-sharing model (lower upfront, 60-month payback).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 15 min online). Apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) via DGFT portal (₹0, 7 days). If planning GI certification eventually, budget ₹3–5L + 18–24 months — but not mandatory to start. Get lab certification for Barium compound purity (Bureau of Indian Standards, ₹8–12K per test). File for Udyog Aadhar + GTIN barcode (₹500 combined).
5
First Sale
First sales: target regional paint & adhesive makers via direct sales calls + IndiaMART listing (free + 5% commission). Post 3 product samples + quality certificates on IndiaMART; typical first order: 500kg–2MT at ₹40–50/kg wholesale = ₹20–100K month 1. Secondary: attend BuildTech Expo 2024 or Chemical & Pharma Expo in Hyderabad (stall: ₹1.5L); secure 3–5 sample trials by month 2.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 quality supervisor (₹25K/month), 1 sales executive (₹20K + commission), 1 export logistics coordinator (₹20K). Invest ₹15–20L more capex in second kiln and lab upgrade. Export via FIEO members or chartered agents (commission 2–4%). Apply for SFURTI cluster development subsidy (₹10–25L grant) or PMEGP loan (₹25L at 5% interest, 3-year tenure) to accelerate capacity.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma
up to ₹15L concessional loan at 5% for mineral processing artisans, 18-month repayment holiday
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Program)
25% subsidy (max ₹25L) for general category, 35% for SC/ST, processing unit capital cost
🏚
Andhra Pradesh Udyam Support Scheme
₹2L subsidy for MSME registration + 3% interest subvention for 5 years on working capital loans
🏚
AP One-District-One-Product (ODOP) Scheme
priority export promotion, buyer linkage via District Industries Centre, booth support at state expos
