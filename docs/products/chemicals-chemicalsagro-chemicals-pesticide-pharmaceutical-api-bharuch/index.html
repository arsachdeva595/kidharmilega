Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bharuch ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bharuch Chemical Park (BCPL) and Dahej Industrial Complex to understand supply chain, meet 10+ existing manufacturers, study competitor pricing at FICCI Chemical Council meetings, review Gujarat Industrial Development Corporation (GIDC) cluster data
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from Dahej port suppliers and IOCL refinery outlets; establish credit lines with chemical distributors in Ankleshwar; first batch sourcing: ₹15-30L depending on product category
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with BCPL or GIDC for land allocation (₹50-80L/acre for 1-2 acre plots), install production equipment from German/Japanese suppliers (Thyssenkrupp, Siemens), hire experienced plant managers from existing Bharuch chemical firms
4
Brand & Register
Register as Micro/Small Enterprise under Udyam, obtain DPIIT recognition, apply for IEC code from nearest DGFT office (Ahmedabad), get ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 certifications, register with Directorate of Industries; brand name + website setup: ₹5-8L
5
First Sale
Launch on IndiaMART and TradeKey with 50+ product listings, attend ChemExpo India (Mumbai) and India Chem annual conference (Delhi), build WhatsApp/email outreach to top 200 pharma/agro companies, offer bulk trial quantities at 10-15% discount
6
Scale
After ₹5Cr revenue, expand to 3-4 new product lines, hire dedicated export/sales team, apply for Production-Linked Incentive (PLI) for chemicals, establish warehouses in Mumbai and Bangalore for faster delivery
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy for SSE/MSE units in manufacturing
🏛
PLI Scheme for Chemicals
up to 10% incentive on sales value for qualifying pharmaceutical and specialty chemicals
🏚
Gujarat Industrial Development Corporation (GIDC) Industrial Park Scheme
land lease at subsidized rates and infrastructure support in Bharuch clusters
🏚
Gujarat Enterprise Development Scheme (GEDS)
subsidy on capex and interest on working capital for chemical manufacturing MSMEs
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
