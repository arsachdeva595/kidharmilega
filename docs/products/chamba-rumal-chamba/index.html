Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Chamba ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Chamba town artisan clusters in Bhattiyat and Chamba Bazaar; spend 2-3 weeks learning from master embroiderers; document techniques at Chamba Museum; connect with 5-10 established artisan families to understand production timelines and quality grades
2
Source Raw Materials
Source premium cotton muslin from Banaras textile wholesalers (₹800-1200/meter); procure silk threads from Aurangabad silk merchants (₹200-400/50gm skeins); natural dyes from Indigo suppliers in Rajasthan; first batch of 20-30 rumals requires ₹80,000-120,000 raw material investment
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 8-12 registered Chamba artisan families through local SHGs (Self Help Groups) like Chamba Weavers Cooperative; establish production center with 15-20 wooden frames; implement quality control system with master artisan oversight; production lead time is 3-6 weeks per custom piece
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Micro/Small Enterprise classification); apply for IEC code through DGFT Shimla office; obtain Chamba Rumal GI certification authenticity stamp from Ministry of Commerce; register trademark with artisan collective name; get ISO 9001 certification for handloom quality
5
First Sale
Launch Etsy store and Flipkart Handloom section (30% commission); build Instagram with artisan storytelling content (2-3 posts/week); partner with luxury home décor platforms like Artifact Heritage and FabIndia; reach out to 50 interior designers in metro cities; launch email campaign to heritage tourism operators
6
Scale
After 6 months with ₹50-75L revenue, expand to 25-30 artisans; establish Chamba Rumal showroom in Delhi NCR or Bangalore; create designer collaboration line; develop corporate gifting program; build B2B relationships with 5-star hotels and embassies; apply for VKGUY scheme for artisan skill upgrades
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹15,000 loan at 0% interest + ₹5,000 toolkit grant for registered artisans in your production group
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 Lakhs loan with 35% subsidy (general) or 50% subsidy (ST/SC categories)
🏚
Himachal Pradesh Handicraft Promotion Scheme
30% subsidy on capital expenditure for handloom units; support for GI-certified product branding and export promotion
🏚
Khaadi Gram Udyog Yojana (Himachal variant)
soft loans and training subsidies for traditional craft entrepreneurs; priority clearance for Udyam registration
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
