Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Baghpat ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Baghpat City embroidery clusters (Mohalla Mandi area) and Barhapur weaving village; attend Craft Mela events at Pragati Maidan Delhi; connect with ODOP nodal agency office in Baghpat; interview 5-10 master artisans to understand production capacity, costs, and design trends.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source 60% raw materials from local Baghpat suppliers (Baghpat fabric market, Govardhan textile wholesalers); 40% from Delhi textile hubs (Shahpur Jat, Nai Sarak). Budget ₹60,000-₹1,20,000 for first 500-unit batch including fabric, threads, dyes, and trim. Negotiate 45-60 day payment terms with suppliers.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3-5 artisan collectives in Baghpat City (minimum 10-15 artisans per partner); sign MoUs specifying designs, quality standards, delivery timelines. Start with 200-300 units/month; provide advance materials worth ₹30,000 per artisan group. Conduct quality audits weekly.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (udyamregistration.gov.in) within 2 weeks; apply for IEC code via DGFT portal; register business under MSME; file trademark for brand name with IP India (₹9,000-₹15,000); obtain GI certification acknowledgment letter from Baghpat ODOP office for marketing claims.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon, Flipkart, Etsy with 50-100 SKUs in first month; create Instagram/Facebook with weekly artisan backstory content; partner with 5-10 interior design studios in Delhi-NCR for wholesale; pitch to OYO, ITC boutique properties for bulk orders; attend Handloom Expo India and Craft Summit (New Delhi) for B2B meetings.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand artisan base to 20-30 partners; launch own D2C website with blog on Mughal textile heritage; enter 2-3 international e-commerce platforms (Wayfair, Not on the High Street); hire export manager for FTA certifications (GOTS, Fair Trade); open Delhi showroom/pop-up by month 12; target ₹2-3 Cr revenue by Year 2.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (25-35% of project cost) for manufacturing units in Baghpat with employment generation of 2+ persons.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
cluster-level support up to ₹2Cr for artisan collective infrastructure, design centers, and market linkage in Baghpat textile clusters.
🏚
Uttar Pradesh ODOP Promotion Scheme
50% subsidy on brand registration, packaging design, and e-commerce platform setup; direct market linkage through government portals.
🏚
UP Employment Exchange & Skill Development Scheme
free workforce training in design, quality control, and digital marketing for artisan groups in notified ODOP clusters.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
