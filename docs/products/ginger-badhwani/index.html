The district is already home to 50+ registered ginger exporters and processing units with active supply chains to Peru, Vietnam, and Asia. Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Badhwani ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Badhwani Agricultural Produce Market (Mandi) on Tuesday or Friday morning to speak 3-5 ginger exporters already operating there. Call Badhwani District Agricultural Department (free, ask for current farmer supplier lists and seasonal pricing). Register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) with your Aadhar and phone number — takes 30 mins.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw ginger from Badhwani Mandi during October-December harvest (₹25-35/kg). Contract 50-100 farmers directly in Badhwani village clusters or use Mandi middlemen. First batch: 2-5 tons (₹50K-₹1.75L raw cost). Supplier area: Badhwani tehsil, Pandsemal block.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent 800-1200 sqft processing shed (₹8-12K/month) in Badhwani MSME cluster with basic cleaning, slicing, drying equipment (₹2-4L capex). Option B: Contract 3-4 artisan drying units at ₹5-8/kg processing charge. Timeline: raw to dried ginger = 10-15 days. Batch size: start with 500 kg/month.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME (free, 1 day online). Apply for APEDA IEC code for export (₹500 fee, 5-7 days processing at nearest office, Indore). Get FSSAI license for D2C (₹3K one-time for basic category, 7 days online). Optional: GI registration study at Ministry office (Badhwani district office) — costs ₹3-5L but Badhwani ginger has no existing GI so high approval chance with ODOP backing.
5
First Sale
First sale via direct Mandi buyer (500 kg at ₹75/kg = ₹37.5K, payment in 5-7 days). Simultaneously list on Amazon Fresh/BigBasket (₹200/200g pack, 8-15 units/day = ₹1.6-3K/month). Export first container (5-8 tons) via APEDA importer in Indore to Peru contact (₹13-18L FOB value, 60-90 day payment terms). Target: ₹3-5L month 1.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 export manager + 2 QC staff (₹40K/month), move to 2000 sqft unit with mechanical dryer (₹8-12L investment). Exhibit at India Global Trade Fair (Delhi), Ginger Exporter Summit (if held), Indore B2B food expos. Build 2-3 more farmer supplier networks. Add ginger oil & candied ginger lines for margin uplift. Target Vietnam market expansion (8x growth potential noted in 2024 data).

Government Schemes

PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh loan with 35% subsidy for manufacturing (max ₹8.75L subsidy), interest 5-10% per annum
🏛
MUDRA Shishu Loan
up to ₹1 lakh unsecured loan for food processing startups at 6-8% interest, zero collateral

provides free branding, logo design, packaging, market linkage support, and export readiness training (no monetary limit, open to registered exporters)

Madhya Pradesh Laghu Udyog Vikas Yojana
6% subsidy on plant & machinery for food processing units up to ₹5 crore, plus 8% interest subsidy on term loans
