Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Haridwar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Haridwar's Khanpur and Bahadarabad sugarcane processing clusters; connect with 5-10 existing jaggery makers; study production at Haridwar Food Processing Park; attend workshops at district MSME office on food safety and quality standards.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source sugarcane directly from Haridwar and Shamli (UP) farmers at ₹2,000-2,500 per quintal; negotiate bulk contracts for 10+ quintals; establish credit terms with 2-3 suppliers; first batch of 5 quintals will cost ₹12,000-15,000 plus ₹5,000 for processing inputs.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing jaggery units in Haridwar under co-production agreements (20-30% commission) for first 3-4 months; alternatively, lease a 500 sq ft unit with basic boiling equipment (pan, strainer, mold) for ₹3,000-5,000/month in Khanpur cluster; hire 1-2 skilled workers at ₹10,000-15,000/month.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) online; obtain IEC code from DGFT for export; register FSSAI license as Small Food Business Operator (₹500, online); trademark brand name at IP office (₹5,000-8,000); design packaging with nutritional/GI-like claims emphasizing purity and traditional methods.
5
First Sale
List on Blinkit, BigBasket, and local sweets/bakery WhatsApp groups within first 30 days; reach out to 20-30 confectioneries via cold calls offering free samples; negotiate supply contracts with mid-range hotels in Haridwar and Dehradun; launch Instagram posts targeting health-conscious audiences with recipe content; first month target: ₹50,000-75,000 revenue.
6
Scale
Secure ₹15-25L PMEGP/MUDRA loan to scale production capacity 3x by month 8-10; add vacuum pan technology for consistency; develop 2-3 value-added SKUs (jaggery powder, cubes); hire dedicated sales person for B2B; launch export shipments to UAE distributors; expand to 3-4 retail partners per city in North India.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh collateral-free loan at 5-6% interest for food processing MSMEs; eligible for manufacturing or rural enterprises
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
grants up to ₹40 lakh for cluster development; Haridwar jaggery cluster may qualify for infrastructure support
🏚
Uttarakhand MSME Policy 2022
5% interest subsidy on bank loans, capital subsidy up to ₹10 lakh for food processing units, tax exemptions for 5 years
🏚
Uttarakhand Agriculture & Food Processing Subsidy
25% capital cost subsidy for food processing equipment; applicable to jaggery units meeting FSSAI/ISO standards
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
