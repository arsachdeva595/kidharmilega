Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Narayanpet ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Narayanpet town handloom cluster this week (free): spend 2 days meeting 10-15 weavers in their workshops, understand costing and lead times. Call 3-4 suppliers (names from NIC office or local weavers association). Attend the weekly haat in Narayanpet bazaar (Thursdays/Saturdays) to see retail pricing and customer behavior. Join Narayanpet Weavers Cooperative WhatsApp group.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from registered Narayanpet weavers in town cluster (within 5 km) or Narayanpet Weavers Cooperative. First batch: order 20-30 mixed sarees (15 cotton, 15 silk) from 3-5 weavers to test quality and speed. Cost: ₹4.5-₹6L for ₹5,500-₹6,500/piece landed cost. Lead time: 20-25 days for standard designs, 30-35 days for custom colors.
3
Produce / Partner
Start with artisan partnership model (no factory): reserve capacity with 3-4 trusted weavers via written agreement. Invest ₹2-₹3L in working capital, quality-checking setup (simple tables, measuring tools, color samples), and packaging (₹15-20 per saree). As you scale past ₹15L monthly revenue, consider renting a 1,500 sq ft finishing/QC unit in Narayanpet MSME cluster at ₹8K-₹12K/month and hiring 2-3 finishers.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (free, 5 min): Ministry of MSME portal. Apply for GI usage certificate from Ministry of Textiles via official GI registry — submit through Narayanpet DC office, ₹2,000-₹5,000 cost, 30-45 days approval. If targeting export, apply for IEC code via DGFT portal (₹500, 7-10 days). Get ISI/AAMI optional certification for premium positioning (₹15K-₹25K, 2-3 months). Create GST registration (free online, required for wholesale).
5
First Sale
First sale: list on Saree.com (commission 12-15%, fast approval) or Instagram Shop (commission 0%, but you drive traffic). Target first order: 5-8 sarees at ₹8,000-₹10,000 retail via Instagram DM → WhatsApp → COD/prepaid. Expected first month (Month 3): ₹60K-₹100K revenue (6-10 sarees), gross profit ₹30K-₹55K. Focus on wedding season (Feb-May, Sept-Nov) for 40-50% of annual revenue.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (Month 8-10): hire 1 dedicated customer service person + 1 packaging/QC person (₹12K-₹15K/month each). Expand weaver network to 8-10 trusted artisans to handle 40-50 sarees/week. Launch on Nykaa and Amazon Handmade (commission 10-12%). Exhibit at Handloom Expo Hyderabad (Jan) and Bangalore Craft Fair (Mar) to land bulk corporate orders. Invest ₹50K in professional photography/videography for social proof. Scale packaging to branded boxes with story cards (₹30-50 per saree).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Program)
up to ₹25L subsidy (25% of project cost for general category), covers machinery, working capital, training; apply via District MSME office
🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹15,000 cash incentive + interest subsidy on ₹100K MUDRA loan at 0% for first 2 years for registered artisans and their family businesses; apply via Common Service Centers
🏚
Telangana Integrated Handloom Development Scheme
₹10-50L subsidy for handloom enterprises, subsidized shed space in handloom clusters at 50% discount; apply via Telangana Handloom Department
🏚
Telangana MSME Support Scheme
5% interest subsidy on working capital loans up to ₹10L for ODOP-tagged products, registration free; apply via State MSME office Narayanpet
