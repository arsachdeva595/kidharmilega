Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Dholpur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sarmathura cluster (35 km from Dholpur town, accessible via NH44). Meet 3-4 established stone merchants in the main bazaar, ask about stone varieties, current pricing per tonne, buyer sources. Call Dholpur District MSME office (0 cost) and request latest ODOP cluster data. Free — complete within 1 week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Purchase raw sandstone blocks from Sarmathura quarries at ₹3000-5000 per tonne (weight-dependent). First batch: 5-10 tonnes costs ₹15K-50K. Supplier contacts via district Artisan Association or referral from cluster visit. Processing (cutting, finishing) adds ₹2000-4000 per tonne — outsource initially to existing processors in Sarmathura or Dholpur town.
3
Produce / Partner
Start with artisan partnership model: rent 800-1200 sqft workshop in Sarmathura or Dholpur town bazaar (₹8K-15K/month) or partner with 2-3 existing processors on commission (15-20% of selling price). Buy basic hand tools (₹30K-50K), rope, safety gear. Capital: ₹60K-150K for workshop + tools + first raw material batch. Alternatively, hire 2-3 skilled stone cutters on day wages (₹500-800/day) and grow from there.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online at udyamregistration.gov.in — 15 mins). Apply for GI tag via Rajasthan Handicrafts Ministry if positioning as Dholpur-origin product (₹5000-8000, 4-6 month process). File IEC with DGFT for export (₹1500-3000, 10 days). Open business bank account (₹0 with Udyam). Obtain State GST (free, 7 days online).
5
First Sale
First sale route: Post 5-8 product photos on Houzz, Justdial, Instagram with WhatsApp contact. Target: 1-2 local architect inquiries in month 1 for ₹20K-50K orders (garden tiles, wall panels). Parallel: Email 20 builders in Delhi/Gurgaon NCR directly with pricing list. Expected month 1 revenue: ₹25K-80K. By month 3, aim for ₹2-4L monthly from local + regional builders.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: Hire 1 full-time sales/export person (₹25K/month), upgrade to 2000-3000 sqft workshop (₹20K-30K/month), invest in semi-automated cutting tools (₹3-5L). Start export outreach via APEDA, Sarmathura Artisan Association, or direct emails to US/UAE importers. Exhibit at India Stone & Tile Fair (ISTF, Jaipur) or International Stone Fair. Build branded packaging (₹2L for molds/printing). Hire 1 export consultant to handle shipping, LC, documentation (₹15K-25K/month).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
Up to ₹25L subsidy (35% for General, 45% for SC/ST/OBC in SC/ST/OBC areas) for manufacturing units, 5-year moratorium on EMI, eligibility up to ₹25L
🏛
MUDRA Loan (Shishu/Kishor/Tarun)
₹50K-10L unsecured loans for manufacturing at 8-12% interest, no collateral, 5-year tenure via banks/NBFCs
🏚
Rajasthan ODOP Promotion Scheme
₹2-5L subsidy for processing/value-addition equipment, brand building grant ₹50K, free MSME cluster training, eligibility via district ODOP identification
🏚
Rajasthan Export Promotion Policy
3-5% export subsidy on FOB value, ₹1L for IEC + APEDA registration, subsidy valid for ₹50L+ annual export
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
