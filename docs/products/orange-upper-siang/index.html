Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Upper Siang ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Yingkiong (district HQ) and Dambuk orange clusters; interview 5-10 established farmers and processors; study GI certification rules from Arunachal Pradesh Agricultural Department and CSR-AAPID office.
2
Source Raw Materials
Connect with farmer cooperatives like Upper Siang Farmers Producer Company (approx. ₹2-3L for 5-10 MT first harvest procurement); establish cold chain partnership with local cold storage units in Pasighat or Tezu.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established juice/candy makers in Yingkiong or Dambuk OR invest ₹15-25L in small processing unit (3000L/day capacity); obtain FSSAI food license and hire 2-3 trained processors.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME online; obtain IEC from APEDA (Arunachal Pradesh); file GI product registration with state; design packaging emphasizing GI tag and hill-origin story; get FSSAI certification within 60 days.
5
First Sale
Launch on Jio Mart Fresh, BigBasket, FreshToHome with ₹3-5L marketing spend; approach 20-30 premium cafes/restaurants in Guwahati and Dibrugarh; start WhatsApp/Instagram pre-orders with local delivery in Pasighat/Tezu.
6
Scale
Expand to 2-3 processing centers across district; build B2B relationships with 5+ food companies; launch exports via APEDA-registered exporters; invest in cold chain expansion; hire operations manager by month 8.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Pradhan Mantri Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy for setting up food processing unit in Arunachal Pradesh.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹20L grant for cluster development and collective processing in Upper Siang.
🏚
Arunachal Pradesh MAAHE Scheme (Multi-Purpose Agricultural & Horticultural Enterprises)
40% subsidy on machinery and processing equipment.
🏚
Arunachal Pradesh Agricultural Marketing Board Support
concessional storage and transportation subsidies for GI-certified products.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
