Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Nanded ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Nanded wholesale markets (Nanded Mandi Complex) and connect with 8-10 existing soya processors; join Maharashtra Soybean Association; attend APEDA export training at Aurangabad; study cost structures at facilities near Nanded city center
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw soya beans from farmer cooperatives in Nanded taluka (₹40-45 per kg, minimum 5 tonnes = ₹2-2.25L); connect with seed suppliers via Nanded Agricultural Produce Market Committee; first batch sourcing takes 15-20 days
3
Produce / Partner
Either: (A) Lease existing processing unit in Nanded's industrial area (₹15-25K monthly) with oil expeller & dal mill machines, or (B) Partner with contract manufacturers in Nanded who process at ₹3-5 per kg; setup time 30-45 days including equipment calibration
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) online in 15 minutes; obtain IEC code from DGFT Mumbai office for export; get FSSAI food license (₹7,500-15,000, 30 days); trademark brand at IP office; set up packaging with nutrition labels & allergen warnings
5
First Sale
Launch on BigBasket, Amazon Fresh, Flipkart (onboard via vendor portal); simultaneously approach organic retailers in Pune/Mumbai; reach out to 50 food brands for B2B contracts; use Instagram/Facebook with nutritionist testimonials and 'farm-to-table' storytelling; list on IndiaTradeHub for bulk exports
6
Scale
After ₹10L monthly revenue: expand product line (soya milk powder, protein bars); establish own small processing unit (₹40-50L capex); hire sales team to penetrate tier-2 cities; apply for APEDA export promotion subsidy; develop 3-4 institutional buyer relationships in hospitality/food service
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Formalisation of Micro food Processing Enterprises (PMFME)
₹10L grant for equipment, infrastructure and working capital for food processing units
🏛
APEDA Export Promotion
up to 15% subsidy on air freight for organic/processed soya products to international markets
🏚
Maharashtra State Food Processing Scheme
25% capital subsidy for soya processing units with FSSAI license
🏚
Marathwada Drought Relief & Development
special 35% subsidy for agri-processing units in Nanded district
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
