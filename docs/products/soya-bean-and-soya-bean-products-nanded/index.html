Raw soybean is sourced directly from 50,000+ farming families within a 30 km radius of district towns like Nanded city and Himayat Nagar. Indore (420 km) and Mumbai (600 km) are the primary end-market hubs; Indore spot rates for soy oil range ₹1410-1490 per 10 kg (as of April 2026).

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Nanded ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call 3 soybean wholesalers in Nanded city (ask via local haat or Nanded agriculture office for contacts). Visit a soybean dal or oil unit in Himayat Nagar area. Attend Nanded wholesale market (Mon/Wed/Fri mornings) to track live rates. Cost: ₹0, time: 4-6 hours this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Buy first 5-10 tonnes of raw soybean from Nanded Mandi (wholesale market) at ₹4800-5100 per tonne (direct farmer contact via mandi brokers). Cost: ₹2.5-5.2L. Alternative: contract 20 tonnes with farmer cooperatives in Umri or Hadgaon tehsils at ₹4700-4950 per tonne with 30-day payment terms.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (rent): lease 1200-1500 sq ft unit in Nanded MIDC (₹8000-12000/month), install crushing/refining equipment (₹40-60L for press + refinery). Option B (outsource): contract process 50 tonnes/month with existing dal/oil units for ₹1200-1500 per tonne processing fee. Recommended: start with outsourcing, then scale to owned unit at ₹1.5-2L monthly volume.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 10 mins). Apply for food safety license via FSSAI (₹5000-8000, 15-20 days). If exporting: obtain IEC code (₹0, 7-10 days online via DGFT) and phytosanitary certificate from agriculture dept (₹500, 5 days). Total: ₹6-9L for licenses + initial testing + HACCP documentation.
5
First Sale
First wholesale order: approach 5-10 local FMCG units (snack makers, bakeries) with 2-3 tonne samples at ₹108-115 per kg. Expected first month: 8-12 tonnes = ₹8.6-13.8L revenue. Concurrent: list on Amazon B2B for institutional buyers (min 50 kg orders).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 2 quality checkers, 1 logistics person. Move to 2500 sq ft unit with dual processing lines (₹80-100L CAPEX). Partner with 3-4 export trading houses in Indore for 30-50 tonne/month export contracts. Exhibit at Global Food Manufacturing Expo (Indore) and agricultural trade shows.
