The Ghadawa artisan cluster is concentrated in Jagdalpur town and surrounding villages; raw materials (riverbed clay, bronze scrap, wax) sourced locally within 20km radius. Buyer markets are Indore furniture hub (200km), Nagpur traders, and direct export to 6+ countries via Nagpur port/rail.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bastar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Jagdalpur Dhokra cluster (Bastar Handloom & Handicrafts Centre) — free visit, meet 3-4 master artisans, observe a full casting cycle (4-6 hours), ask about Ghadawa community contacts. Call Bastar District Industries Centre (0788-2253636) to get current supplier list. Attend Navratri Mela in Jagdalpur (Sep/Oct) to see live demonstrations and buyer networks. Zero cost, doable this month.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from Jagdalpur local clay merchants and Raipur brass scrap dealers (₹8-15k for initial batch of wax, riverbed clay, bronze scrap). Partner with 2-3 established Ghadawa artisans in Jagdalpur who will do piece-work casting at ₹120-400/unit depending on complexity. First batch cost: ₹60-80k for materials + labor to produce 30-40 sample pieces.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent 200-300 sq ft workshop space in Jagdalpur (₹3000-5000/month) with basic furnace, wax pots, clay pits. Option B: Hire 2 experienced Ghadawa artisans on piece-rate (₹120-400/piece) and operate from home with small setup (₹1.5-2.5L initial investment). Option C: Co-production model — buy finished pieces from established artisans, do finishing/polishing/branding yourself (lowest risk, ₹1-1.5L startup). Breakeven point is 40-60 pieces/month at wholesale prices.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME (free, online, 15 min). Apply for GI tag certification via Nagpur Geographical Indications office (₹5-8k, 3-4 months, required for export premium). Get IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) from Nagpur DGFT office (free, 2-3 weeks) if planning export. Register business under Shop & Establishment Act in Jagdalpur (₹1-2k). Total compliance cost: ₹8-12k, timeline 4-6 months.
5
First Sale
Day 1-30: List 12-15 SKUs on Etsy (free shop, 5% fee). Target first 10 orders (owls, key hangers, small idols) at ₹1200-2000 each, expect ₹12-20k first-month revenue. Day 31-60: Approach 5 local home decor retailers in Raipur, offer wholesale at ₹600-1000/piece, MOQ 20 units. Simultaneous: Contact 2-3 corporate gifting companies with sample kit (cost ₹5k). First institutional order expected by month 4-5.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: Hire 1 full-time workshop manager (₹12-15k/month), 2 dedicated artisans (₹250-350/piece production cost). Invest ₹2-3L in second furnace, expand product range to 40+ SKUs. Approach Mumbai/Delhi interior designer stores directly. Join Handicrafts Export Promotion Council (HEPC) membership (₹2-3k/year) to access export shows in Frankfurt, New York. Exhibit at India Crafts Week (annual, Delhi) and Maison & Objet (Paris biennial) — budget ₹1.5-2.5L for stall + samples.
