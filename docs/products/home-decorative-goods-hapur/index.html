Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Hapur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Spend 5-7 days in Hapur's Bajaj Road and Sarai Hussan Khan areas — visit 10-15 established units (contact via Hapur Chamber of Commerce), interview master craftspersons on production timelines and quality standards, visit Jaipur's home furnishing markets to understand design trends and pricing
2
Source Raw Materials
Source cotton (₹80-120/kg) and polyester blends (₹60-100/kg) from wholesale suppliers on Hapur's Bhoot Road or Ludhiana; embroidery threads from Lucknow's Chowk area; cushion fillings from Kolkata suppliers. First batch cost (500 units mixed products): ₹75,000-120,000
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 established artisan clusters in Hapur (e.g., Bajaj Road cluster) on piece-rate contracts for cushion-stitching and embroidery; invest in basic finishing equipment (cutting table, steam press) if doing in-house assembly. Production timeline: 15-20 days for 500 units
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSE account (takes 2-3 days online at udyamregistration.gov.in); obtain IEC from DGFT for export eligibility; trademark brand name via IP India; get testing certificate from AIFTA (All India Federation of Textile Associations) for export quality compliance
5
First Sale
List 50-100 SKUs on Amazon Seller Central and Flipkart Seller Hub within 30 days; pitch to 5-10 interior design studios in Delhi/NCR with sample collections; contact B2B export portals (Global Trade Point, TradeKey) for international bulk inquiries; launch Instagram store with lifestyle photography of products in real homes
6
Scale
After first 6 months and ₹25-50L revenue, hire 1-2 in-house designers for custom collections; expand artisan network to 5+ units; launch private label partnerships with 2-3 large e-commerce brands; establish export agreements with 2 distributors in USA/EU; add premium silk collection targeting luxury segment
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidized loan at 5% interest for textile-based micro-enterprises with 25% subsidy
🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
direct support and branding for artisan-linked businesses including interest-free loans up to ₹3 lakh
🏚
UP Udyam Vikas Yojana
subsidized power tariffs (30% reduction) and land lease benefits for textile manufacturing units in identified clusters
🏚
UP Export Promotion Scheme
cash incentives of 2-5% on FOB value of exports to SAARC and non-SAARC countries
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
