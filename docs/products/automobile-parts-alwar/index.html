Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Alwar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bhiwadi MSME cluster office (12 km from Alwar city) this week — meet 3-5 gear manufacturers, ask about raw material suppliers, production cycles, and buyer contracts. Call Alwar District Industries Centre (free consultation). Attend next Rajasthan Electronics and Automobile Parts Haat — listen to pricing, spec demands, lead times. Zero cost.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source pig iron and steel billets from Ghatol (25 km south) and Mundawar (35 km southeast) mining belt directly, or buy from Alwar-based stockists like Jindal Steel Supplies (established 15 years) at ₹48-52/kg. First batch of 500 kg costs ₹24,000-26,000. Forging oils from Castrol/Shell dealers in Alwar city.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 5,000 sq ft unit in Bhiwadi Industrial Park (₹15-20/sq ft/month = ₹75,000-1,00,000/month) or enter artisan partnership with an existing gear shop taking 40-50% of revenue for use of their CNC/gear-hobbing machine. Alternatively, job-work arrangement: buy semi-finished blanks, outsource cutting/shaping to local foundries, do finishing in-house. Initial CapEx: ₹25-35L for lathe, milling machine, inspection gauges, hydraulic press.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (free, 15 min) with land lease or NOC. Apply for IEC code via e-BIZ portal (₹0, 3-4 weeks). Approach Rajasthan Industries Dept for ODOP branding certificate (free, 1 week). Optional: pursue GI registration through District Industries Centre for ₹50,000-1,00,000 (2-3 years process, credibility boost for exports).
5
First Sale
First sale: Contact Maruti/Hyundai Tier-1 suppliers via LinkedIn, SIAM directory, or DIC referral — submit samples with COA. Expect first order of 100-200 units at 23-25% margin = ₹1.5-3L. Upload catalog to Alibaba, Global Sources (listings free/paid). Parallel: join AutoKey, Amazon Business for retail. Target first-month revenue: ₹2-4L if B2B order lands, ₹50K-1.5L if only retail.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 CNC operator (₹15,000/month), 1 QA inspector (₹12,000/month), 1 sales/export coordinator (₹18,000/month). Invest ₹15-20L in second lathe/gear-shaper. Exhibit at Auto Cluster Trade Fairs (Jaipur, Noida), SIAM Auto Expo, Germany's Hanover Messe (if exporting). Achieve ₹30L+/month by adding stamping/forging capability or expanding to axles.
