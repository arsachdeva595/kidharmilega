Oil - Agar ki
opportunity, North Tripura mein.

North Tripura sits in northeast India with 50 lakh mature Aquilaria trees naturally grown across residential and forest areas, accessible within 5-15 km of Kadamtala, Kalacherra, and Panisagar blocks where 27 licensed distilleries operate. Raw material sourcing requires visiting local wood suppliers (Agartala markets have 15-20 active traders) and negotiating with farmers who harvest resinous wood from injured trees at ₹500-2000 per kg depending on grade. Extraction happens via traditional water-boiling and steam distillation over 3-4 months; finished oil ships to Middle East buyers through Kolkata port (400 km, 8-10 hours by road) or Silchar airport (200 km).

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — North Tripura ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kadamtala block's Agartala wood market on a Wednesday; meet 3-4 agar wood suppliers and ask: (1) current wood prices per kg for Boya, Boha, Khara grades, (2) which trees yield resin fastest, (3) typical extraction yield (litre output from 50 kg wood). Cost: ₹0. Register free on Udyam website. Call one of the 7 licensed processing facilities in Panisagar block to observe a full extraction cycle (offer to visit for 2 hours, no cost).
2
Source Raw Materials
Source chipped agar wood from Kadamtala and Kalacherra block suppliers at ₹800-1500/kg (Boya grade), ₹1500-2500/kg (Boha), ₹3000-5000/kg (Khara). Minimum first batch: 50 kg mixed grades = ₹75,000-150,000. Build relationships with 3-4 suppliers to ensure year-round stock. Negotiate 30-60 day credit if possible.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 800-1200 sqft distillery unit in Panisagar block or partner with an existing 7 licensed facilities as a co-operator (₹30,000-50,000/month rent or 20-30% revenue share). Setup: boiling vats, cooling chambers, glass collection bottles, storage drums. Total equipment: ₹200,000-400,000. Process: soak chipped wood in water 3-4 months (requires storage space), then boil/steam to separate oil from water. Yield: ~5-8% by weight (5 kg wood produces ~250-400 ml oil). Timeline: first batch ready in 16 weeks.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 15 mins). File GI registration application at Tripura Handicrafts Board for 'North Tripura Agar Oil' (₹15,000-25,000, 6-8 months, secures premium pricing). Get Food Safety License from FSSAI if selling incense/cosmetics (₹3,000-8,000). For export: obtain IEC from DGFT Kolkata (₹500, 2 weeks). Export license needed under Wildlife Protection Act since Aquilaria is listed (file at Ministry of Environment, Delhi; process: 1-2 months, ₹5000).
5
First Sale
First sales: list pure agar oil (10 ml bottles) on Nykaa and Amazon India Luxury (2-week listing approval), starting with 10 bottles at ₹10,000 each. Simultaneously approach 2-3 Ayurvedic brands via email (Patanjali, Dabur, Forest Essentials buyer contacts) with 50 ml sample, pitch ₹4.5L/litre wholesale price. First month realistic revenue: ₹1-3L (5-10 D2C sales + 1 wholesale inquiry moving forward).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 1-2 distillery technicians (₹15,000-20,000/month each), 1 marketing/sales person (₹12,000-15,000/month), 1 packer (₹8,000/month). Upgrade to 2000+ sqft production unit and increase raw material sourcing from current 50 kg/month to 300+ kg/month. Exhibit at Aroma Expo India (Delhi, Feb) and Middle East Beauty Summit (UAE, May) to land bulk export orders. Launch premium branded line (Aquilaria Pure, Oud House North Tripura) on luxury fragrance e-commerce and approach Ajmal, Rasasi distributors in Middle East directly.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25% subsidy on equipment up to ₹25 lakh if you're first-generation entrepreneur; total loan eligible: ₹50 lakhs at 6% interest
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹50 lakh for food processing clusters; applicable if you set up within Kadamtala/Panisagar designated cluster areas; covers distillery setup and certification costs
🏚
Tripura Agar Wood Policy 2021
provides 50% subsidy on planting Aquilaria trees and processing facility establishment; covers distillery equipment procurement and raw material storage infrastructure
🏚
Agarwood Export Promotion Scheme (Tripura, 2021)
15% export subsidy on FOB value for first 3 years; applicable once IEC obtained; direct credit to business bank account
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
