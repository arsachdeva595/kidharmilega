Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Moradabad ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Mandi Bazaar metal craft cluster (Central Moradabad) and walk the stalls — talk to 5 unit owners about their production capacity, costs, and buyer channels. Call 2-3 raw material suppliers in the same area (brass, copper dealers) to understand pricing. Register free on Udyam MSME portal to check existing unit structures. All zero cost, doable in one weekend.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw brass from scrap dealers and primary metal suppliers in Moradabad's Mandi area (₹280-320/kg for brass sheets) or from Delhi-based refineries (₹300-330/kg, bulk discount at 100+ kg). First batch trial: ₹30-50K for 100-150 kg mixed metals. Establish relationship with 2-3 suppliers to negotiate credit terms of 15-30 days.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent a 500-800 sq ft unit in Mandi Bazaar cluster (₹8-12K/month rent, electricity included) with basic equipment (hand tools ₹40-60K, buffing wheel ₹15-20K, soldering iron ₹5K, workbenches ₹20K). Option B: Partner with 5-8 established artisans on piece-rate basis (₹100-200 per unit for casting/shaping/finishing) — preferred for first 6 months. Total setup: ₹1.5-2.5L for Option A, ₹50-80K for Option B.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME (free, online, 15 mins). Apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) from DGFT portal if targeting exports — free, 10-15 days processing. Skip GI registration initially — it's for district-level collective use, not individual units. Register brand name at IP India if unique. Obtain FSSAI if selling food-contact items (not typical for metal craft). Total cost: ₹0-5K, timeline 3-4 weeks.
5
First Sale
Upload 10-15 product photos to Etsy or Pepperfry with detailed dimensions and material info — first platform typically gives lead-free results within 2-3 weeks. Simultaneously email 5-10 interior designers in Bangalore/Mumbai with catalogs and samples (₹2-3K shipping). First order typically ₹8-15K from D2C, ₹50-100K from wholesale. Expected first-month revenue: ₹30-50K after first sales begin (weeks 3-4).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 quality controller (₹12-15K/month), 1 logistics executive (₹10-12K/month), dedicate yourself to exports + bulk B2B sales. Exhibit at Gift India Expo (Mumbai, August), India Trend Expo (Delhi), and international trade shows (Handmade in India pavilion at EU fairs). Build export pipeline by joining Moradabad Brass Industry Association and attending monthly buyer visits. Target ₹50-80L/year by month 18-20.
