Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Jangaon ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Jangaon's brass artisan clusters in Ranipur and Bazarpet areas; spend 1-2 weeks documenting production methods, meeting 15+ master craftsmen, visiting DC Handicrafts office for cluster data; cost ₹8,000-12,000
2
Source Raw Materials
Source brass ingots from JMTC (Jangaon Metal Trading Co.) or Hyderabad's metal wholesale markets; establish relationships with 2-3 material suppliers; first batch of 50kg mixed materials costs ₹1,20,000-1,80,000; negotiate 15-20% credit terms
3
Produce / Partner
Partner directly with 2-3 established artisan families in Jangaon through revenue-sharing model (60% artisan, 40% business) rather than wage employment; provide design briefs and quality specs; start with 20-30 pieces/month; invest ₹3,00,000 in shared workshop space and basic casting equipment
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (micro/small enterprise) through udyamregistration.gov.in within 2 weeks; apply for IEC code via ICEGATE for export readiness; trademark brand name at IP India; obtain NSIC recognition; file for ISO 9001 quality certification
5
First Sale
Launch curated Instagram shop with 50+ high-quality lifestyle images of artifacts in home settings; list 30-40 SKUs on Etsy with detailed descriptions and heritage storytelling; approach 5-8 premium interior design studios in Hyderabad/Bangalore with B2B catalogs; email pitch to 20 luxury hotels for corporate gifting programs; aim for first 10 orders within 8 weeks
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand artisan network to 5-6 partners; launch own website with storytelling content; pursue export certification and register on TradeKey/Global Sources for international bulk orders; establish collaboration with 2-3 gift aggregators (FabIndia-style); explore wholesale placement in 15-20 premium home decor boutiques across metros; apply for SFURTI cluster development grant for collective marketing
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
up to ₹15,000 toolkit subsidy and 5% interest rate concession on MUDRA loans up to ₹1 Lakh for artisan partnerships
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 Lakh subsidized loan (35% subsidy for general category) for enterprise setup with margin money assistance
🏚
Telangana Handicrafts Development Corporation (THDC) Export Promotion Grant
10% subsidy on export certificates and international logistics for registered artisans
🏚
Telangana MSME Support Scheme
₹1 Lakh working capital grant and 50% subsidy on quality certification (ISO) for handicraft enterprises in notified clusters
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
