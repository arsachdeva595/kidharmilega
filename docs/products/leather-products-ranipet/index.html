Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Ranipet ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Mega Leather Footwear Accessories Cluster (MLFAC) at Panapakkam, Ranipet on a weekday. Walk the cluster boundaries, identify 3-5 small manufacturers. Call any listed tannery asking about artisan partnerships (₹0 cost). Attend Ranipet leather haat/trader meet on first Saturday of month at Ranipet Chamber of Commerce. Register free Udyam account online this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw leather from MLFAC cluster tanneries directly — Belagavi Group, Kannan Leather Works, or Midas Tannery. First batch cost: ₹80K-₹150K for 500 units footwear uppers or 1000 leather accessories pieces. Lead time 10-14 days. Supplier area: entire MLFAC cluster; negotiate 5-8% discount on bulk orders.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 800-1000 sqft unit in MLFAC cluster (₹8K-₹12K/month); stock basic hand tools and cutting equipment (₹50K). Partner with 2-3 experienced artisans on revenue-share (35-45% of margin) instead of hiring at ₹10K-₹15K/month salary — faster startup, lower risk. Small batch production: 50-100 units/day possible. Total 3-month production cost: ₹3-5L for first batch inventory.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam immediately (free, online, 10 minutes). Apply for GI certification use authorization under official Ranipet Leather Products GI (already awarded 2009) — fills form at Ranipet Collector's office, ₹2000 processing fee, 30 days. Get IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) from DGFT online if exporting — free, 24 hours processing. Trademark brand name (₹4500) with IP attorney in Chennai. Cost timeline: ₹10K total, 6-8 weeks.
5
First Sale
First sale via Flipkart Seller Central or Amazon Seller — upload 10-15 product photos, SKUs for footwear, belts, wallets. First order size realistically ₹40K-₹100K from B2B wholesalers on Alibaba (post 5-star seller profile with factory photos). First month revenue target: ₹1.5-₹3L if split 60% D2C, 40% B2B. Use MLFAC cluster network to get first 2-3 buyer referrals.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 1 full-time production supervisor (₹12K/month), 2 quality checkers (₹8K each). Move to 1500 sqft unit (₹15K/month). Target 300-400 units/day. Invest ₹50K in batch photography for catalog. Apply for working capital under MUDRA Lean scheme (₹25L limit, 4-5% interest). Exhibit at IIFI (India International Footwear Industry) Delhi trade show (₹1.5L booth cost). Join IFPC (Indian Footwear Planers Clubs) for buyer network.

Seedhe sawaal, seedhe jawab

₹5-10L minimum. Breakdown: raw leather stock ₹1.5L, unit rent 3 months ₹36K, tools ₹50K, artisan training ₹25K, working capital ₹2.5L, branding + registration ₹25K. If renting in MLFAC cluster, utilities and raw material sourcing easier — saves ₹1.5-2L vs setting up alone.
Leather background not mandatory if you partner with experienced artisans from MLFAC cluster. But 6+ months hands-on time in tannery or footwear unit essential — you'll understand quality, margins, buyer expectations. Many successful founders started as traders (buying from artisans, selling wholesale) before manufacturing.
IFLDP 2021-26 gives ₹50L subsidy for tech upgrade. MAI Scheme covers 50% trade fair costs. MUDRA Lean: ₹25L at 4-5% interest. Tamil Nadu schemes add ₹5L subsidy if GI-certified. Udyam registration is free. Total possible subsidy support: ₹80L+, but requires paperwork and compliance.
MLFAC cluster network — walk the cluster, exchange phone numbers with 20 wholesalers. They'll buy minimum ₹50K orders if quality matches. Alibaba/Global Sources for international buyers (takes 3-6 weeks to first order). Flipkart/Amazon Seller Central for D2C launch (first order within 1 week usually). Mumbai Dharavi wholesalers also buy direct.
Bilkul. Ranipet exports 46% of production to Europe, USA, UK. IEC registration (free, online), GI certification authorization (₹2K), quality compliance (ISO, FDA if needed — ₹30K-50K one-time), and you're ready. First export order typically ₹2-5L FOB. Lead time 4-6 weeks. Logistics via NVOCC freight forwarders in Chennai (cost 12-18% of FOB).
Honest: leather market is competitive, margins are 25-40% wholesale (tight). If you don't hit ₹3L monthly revenue by month 6, unit rental and artisan costs will burn cash. Exit options: (1) Shift to wholesale-only model (lower capex, customer-dependent); (2) Sell unit lease to another manufacturer (MLFAC has waiting list); (3) Become raw material supplier or agent (lower risk, 10-15% commission). Many founders pivot to sourcing/trading rather than manufacturing.
