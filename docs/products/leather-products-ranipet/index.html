The district's favorable climate, established tanning infrastructure, skilled labor force trained over generations, and proximity to major markets make it the ideal location for leather product manufacturing and export.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Ranipet ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit the Ranipet Leather Cluster in Walajapet and Ambur areas; spend 2-3 days shadowing established leather artisans and tanneries. Connect with Ranipet District Industries Centre and Tamil Nadu Leather Development Centre for market insights. Budget ₹5,000 for travel and learning.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw hides from certified suppliers in Ambur leather market (minimum first batch ₹1.5-2L for 50-100 hides). Purchase vegetable tanning agents from Srimaan Chemical Suppliers (Ranipet) or Chennai-based distributors. Establish credit terms with 2-3 suppliers; initial inventory investment: ₹2.5-3.5L.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 skilled leather artisans in Ranipet (through District Industries Centre referrals) on revenue-sharing or wage basis. Alternatively, lease small workshop space in Ambur leather cluster (₹8,000-15,000/month). Start with 100-150 units monthly; invest in basic tools (cutting, edge beveling, stamping) costing ₹75,000-1.2L.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Micro) via udyamregistration.gov.in. Apply for IEC code through DGFT online portal (₹0 cost). Trademark brand name at IP India (₹4,500-9,000). Get GI-authorized certification from Ranipet Leather Manufacturers Association. GST registration mandatory; obtain leather industry-specific BIS certification if applicable.
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy (10% commission, global reach) and Amazon Handmade India (15% commission). Build Instagram presence with artisan storytelling content. Approach sustainable fashion marketplaces (Jaypore, TheEthical.co). Attend India International Leather Fair (Mumbai) to connect B2B buyers. Launch email campaign to luxury hotels and corporate buyers.
6
Scale
After 6 months, hire 2-3 additional artisans based on order pipeline. Invest ₹5-8L in branded packaging and D2C website on Shopify or WooCommerce. Target export markets via Amazon Global or Alibaba. Pursue SFURTI subsidy (₹25-40L) for cluster-wide infrastructure. Develop 4-5 signature SKUs and license designs to larger manufacturers for recurring revenue.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy (35% for general, 25% if setup in aspirational districts); applicable for micro-unit leather product manufacturing
🏛
PM Vishwakarma
up to ₹15,000 toolkit subsidy + 5% loans for eligible artisans in leather sector; skill upgradation certifications included
🏚
Tamil Nadu Leather Cluster Development Scheme
infrastructure development grants up to 50% of capital cost for units in Ranipet cluster
🏚
TN Handicrafts Marketing Assistance Scheme
50% subsidy on export promotion, market linkage events, and branding for certified artisan units
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
