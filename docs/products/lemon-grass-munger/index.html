Farmers cultivate across 800-1,200 hectares with established seedling nurseries and 100-200 gram seed yields per plant ready for January-February harvest. Export markets span North America, Europe, Southeast Asia, and developed Commonwealth nations with Lemongrass House's presence validating consistent international buyer demand.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Munger ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call Lemongrass House Munger office (verify via munger.nic.in) and ask production/export process. Visit Munger Central Market on Saturday morning to meet 3-4 lemongrass leaf suppliers and farmers. Register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) — takes 15 mins. Zero cost, this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source dried lemongrass leaves from local farmers in Munger town and surrounding villages (Raniganj, Langa areas confirmed supplier zones). First batch cost: ₹25,000-40,000 for 500 kg leaves (yields 2-3 kg essential oil after steam distillation). Supplier area: Munger Haat, Telephone Bazaar — negotiate with 2-3 suppliers for rates ₹18-22 per kg.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent 500 sq ft unit in Munger Industrial Area (₹8,000-12,000/month) with steam distillation setup (₹1.8-2.5L capital). Option B: Partner with 2-3 farmer-producer groups (via SFURTI network) to process on their land (₹0 rent, 15% processing fee). Expect 12-18 month supply stability if farmer-partnered.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 15 mins). Apply for FSSAI license if selling food products (tea blends) — ₹2,500-5,000, 30 days. Apply for GI registration under One District One Product scheme via state agriculture ministry — ₹15,000-25,000, 6-8 months, provides 'Munger Lemongrass' protective status. Register IEC code (Import-Export Code) via DGFT portal if exporting — ₹0, 3-5 days online.
5
First Sale
First sale: Contact 5 spa chains via WhatsApp/email with product sample (costs ₹500). Expect first wholesale order ₹15,000-25,000 (15-20 units at ₹1,000-1,500 per liter). Simultaneously list on Meesho (free, 2-5 sales in month 1). Expected first-month blended revenue: ₹35,000-50,000.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: Hire 1 production assistant (₹12,000/month), 1 order fulfillment person (₹10,000/month). Lease 1,000 sq ft unit in industrial area. Apply for PMEGP or MUDRA loan up to ₹10L for equipment upgrade. Attend International Trade Fair Pragati Maidan (Delhi, Nov) and Asian Spa & Wellness Expo (Mumbai, Sept). Partner with 2-3 export distributors through TradeKey, Global Sources platforms.
