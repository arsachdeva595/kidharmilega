Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Tinsukia ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call 5 farmers in Tinsukia town (Assam Agriculture Department has farmer lists) and ask about yield per hectare, water needs, and buyer contacts. Visit the weekly haat in Tinsukia bazaar or call APEDA (Delhi) to understand export logistics for citrus. Register on Udyam portal (free, 15 minutes online) using your PAN and Aadhar — this is compulsory before any govt subsidy.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fresh Kaji nemu from farmers in Tinsukia tehsil (primary cultivation zone) at ₹10-18 per kg during season (June-Oct). Visit Tinsukia fruit wholesale market near the town center — negotiate direct farmer contracts or commission agent rates. First batch: ₹20-30k for 2 tonnes. Secondary supply from across Assam available year-round at ₹12-22/kg through agricultural produce traders.
3
Produce / Partner
Two options: (1) Partner with 2-3 local farmers on revenue-share model — you handle sales, they handle farming (₹0 upfront, 40% profit split). (2) Lease 2-5 hectares of cultivated land in Tinsukia/Duliajan for ₹3-6L per year + hire 2 farm laborers (₹8k/month each) for maintenance and harvest. Establish a small sorting, grading, and packing unit (500 sqft rented space at ₹5-8k/month in a MSME cluster near Tinsukia town). Initial investment: ₹1.5-4L for land lease + labor + packing setup.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online via https://udyam.gov.in) as a sole proprietor or MSME. Apply for GI registration at Office of Registrar of GI (Chennai) via Assam Agriculture Department (₹5-10k processing cost, 18-24 month timeline, but GI-tagged lemon already exists — apply for your brand under the collective mark). Obtain IEC code from DGFT online (free, 1 hour processing) if exporting. Get FSSAI license for value-added products (₹5k, valid 5 years). Apply for Udyam subsidy under PMEGP or state schemes (₹2-5L subsidy for equipment).
5
First Sale
List on JioMart, Blink, Amazon Fresh as a fresh produce seller within 2 weeks (free listing, ₹50-100 commission per order). Contact 10 restaurant chains (Truffles, Barbeque Nation, local QSRs) with WhatsApp catalog and offer ₹2/kg better rates than wholesale markets. Join Udaan as a seller (free, 3 days approval). First order expected: 100-200 kg mix within 2 weeks via online platforms. First month revenue: ₹30-50k at D2C margins (50-65%), ₹20-35k at wholesale channels (20-30% margin).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 sales executive (₹25k/month) to manage bulk buyer relationships (beverages, hotels, food processors). Secure cold storage (500 sqft, ₹3-5k/month) to extend shelf life from 14 days to 45 days. Invest ₹2-4L in a small juice concentrate or dried lemon unit to capture 70%+ margins. Apply for SFURTI cluster subsidy (₹10L grant) if forming a farmer producer group. Exhibit at Agrotech India (Delhi), organic food expos in Mumbai, and Annapoorna food shows. Build relationships with APEDA-registered exporters for UK/UAE shipments (they handle documentation, you supply product).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)
₹5-25L subsidy (25% of project cost, max ₹25L) for registered MSME in agro-processing, 5-year subsidy-free loan component available
🏛
Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY)
80-90% subsidy for drip irrigation installation on lemon farms to improve yield and water efficiency
🏚
Assam Horticulture Mission
₹1-2L subsidy per hectare for new lemon plantation, ₹50k for farm mechanization, valid for 3-year establishment period
🏚
Assam Food Processing Cluster Scheme
₹2-3L subsidy for setting up juice concentrate or value-added processing unit within 50 km of district MSME clusters
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
