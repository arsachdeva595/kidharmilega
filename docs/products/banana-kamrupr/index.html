Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kamrup(R) ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Baghel Agro Industries facility in Kamrup or call nearby banana growers in Nagaon tehsil (free). Attend Assam Horticulture Department's weekly session at District Agriculture Office to understand banana cultivation cost structure. Register free on e-NAM (electronic National Agriculture Market) platform to see live banana prices in Northeast.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source banana saplings from Assam Horticulture Department nurseries in Guwahati (₹8-12 per sapling, bulk: ₹50K for 5,000 saplings). Organic fertilizer from local compost makers in Kamrup (₹400-600/quintal). Land: lease 1-2 acres in banana-growing villages near Khanapara or Azara for ₹15K-25K/acre/year.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Lease 1.5 acres, hire 2-3 farm workers (₹300/day), start with 5,000 plants = ₹4.5L first-year cost. Option B: Partnership with established organic farmers (Baghel model) — provide capital, they manage cultivation, split harvest 40-60. First harvest in 14-16 months; maintenance cost ₹60K-80K/month for 1.5 acres.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free online, 10 mins). Apply for organic certification through NPOP (₹20K-40K, 6-8 months). Get FSSAI license (₹10K, 15 days if value-adding into chips/powder). If exporting: obtain IEC code from DGFT (free, online). Brand registration ₹5K-10K.
5
First Sale
First sale via BigBasket B2B seller dashboard: list at ₹35/kg (organic premium). Target 50 kg/week in Month 1 = ₹1,750 revenue. Approach Modern Trade buyers in Guwahati with 100 kg sample. Negotiate ₹25-28/kg for 500 kg/week commitment. First institutional order likely from banana chip maker Assam Foods or JV groups (₹1.2L+ per month).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire farm manager (₹20K/month), quality checker (₹12K). Expand to 5 acres. Invest in cooling storage (₹1.5L) for longer shelf life. Partner with logistics provider for supply to Bhubaneswar, Kolkata (3-4 days delivery). Exhibit at North-East Food Industry Expo (NEFIE) annually. Build Instacart integration for B2C.
