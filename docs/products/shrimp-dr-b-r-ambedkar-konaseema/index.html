Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Dr. B. R. Ambedkar Konaseema ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Ainavalli & Ranasthalam shrimp clusters in Konaseema (top 10 farming hubs). Connect with Konaseema Shrimp Farmers Association, shadow 2-3 existing farms for 30 days, understand hatchery operations at Kakinada Fisheries Department.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source shrimp seeds from accredited hatcheries in Kakinada (₹50-80/1000 seeds). Bulk feed suppliers: East Coast Feed Ltd, CP Feedmill. First batch for 2-3 acre pond: ₹4-6 lakhs (seeds, feed, infrastructure).
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established shrimp farmers as co-producers initially (revenue share model). Or: Lease 2-3 acre brackish water pond in Ainavalli/Pedapalli for ₹15-25K/month. Hire 4-6 farm workers at ₹12-15K/month each. 90-120 day crop cycle.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME classification as food processing). Apply for IEC code via Customs at Kakinada port. Get FSSAI license for frozen/processed units. Register trademark on IPAB. Get BIS certification for packaged shrimp products.
5
First Sale
Day 1-30: Bulk sales to Kakinada seafood exporters (₹200-300/kg wholesale). Day 30-60: List on B2B platforms (TradeKey, Alibaba) targeting overseas buyers. Day 60+: Launch D2C via own website + Amazon Fresh/BigBasket for retail (₹800-1200/kg retail).
6
Scale
Month 6-12: Expand to 5-acre farming operation. Month 12+: Build cold chain infrastructure (cold storage, transport fleet). Year 2: Develop branded value-added products (vacuum-packed, spiced varieties). Year 2-3: Establish export partnerships with 3-5 countries, target ₹5-10 Cr revenue.

PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy for shrimp farm setup as agri-enterprise
🏛
PM Matsya Sampada Yojana
up to ₹20L assistance for aquaculture infrastructure, processing units
🏚
Andhra Pradesh Aquaculture Development Policy 2023
zero interest loans up to ₹50L for shrimp farming with 5-year repayment
🏚
Andhra Pradesh Marine Products Export Promotion Scheme
10% subsidy on export value + port fee waiver for registered exporters
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
