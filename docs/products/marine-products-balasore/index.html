Balasore district (3,634 km²) occupies Odisha's north coastal belt with 44 km of Arabian Sea frontage, direct fish landing at Chandipur and Balasore port, and integrated supply of shrimp and fish from allied ponds and trawlers within 30 km radius. The district produced 1,245,417 MT of marine exports in 2023-24 alone valued at ₹50,837.32 Cr combined across all frozen seafood categories. Paradip port (120 km south) handles refrigerated containers; Kolkata (250 km) and Chattogram (180 km) are primary buyer markets with 85+ seafood importers within 200 km.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Balasore ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Balasore fishing harbor or Chandipur landing center on a Tuesday morning (5-7 am) to watch live auction and talk to 5 trawler captains about catch volumes and seasonality. Call 3 existing processors in Balasore town (search 'frozen shrimp exporter Balasore' on Google Maps — free) and ask their first-year volumes and challenges. Register Udyam free on udyam.gov.in (15 mins). Zero cost, do this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Raw fish/shrimp sourcing: Direct from Balasore harbor (morning auction, ₹80-120/kg live shrimp) or contract with 2-3 trawler owners for weekly supply agreements (₹18-25L upfront for season-long commitment). Alternative: buy frozen blocks from Paradip-based wholesalers at ₹110-140/kg (bulk 10 MT minimum). First batch: ₹8-12L for 100 MT mixed stock.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 2,000 sq ft cold storage + processing unit in Balasore industrial zone or near harbor (₹25,000-40,000/month). OR partner with 1-2 existing processors as a co-packing partner (you supply branded packaging + design, they handle freezing/sorting, you take 40-50% margin). IQF tunnel freezer + grading table + vacuum packer = ₹12-18L capex if buying new; used equipment ₹6-10L from Paradip sellers. Hire 8-12 contract workers (₹8,000-12,000/month each) for sorting/packing.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online). Apply for FSSAI license (₹5,000-8,000, 30 days). Get IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) from Customs free if targeting export (10 days online). HACCP certification (₹15,000-25,000, required for EU/USA). GI registration for 'Balasore Seafood' under geographical indication (₹20,000-40,000, 6-9 months, optional but strengthens brand). Total non-recurring: ₹40,000-73,000 over 3 months.
5
First Sale
First order target: Contact 3 HORECA chains (ITC, Marriott, Hyatt Delhi/Mumbai) with sample pack + price list; aim ₹3-5L first order. Simultaneously list on BigBasket/Licious seller portal (2-week approval); first month revenue ₹50,000-150,000 from D2C. For export: Email 5 USA seafood importers (find on TradeKey.com free) with compliance docs + price; 5 MT order takes 4-6 weeks to close = ₹45-60L revenue first international shipment.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: Add second cold storage unit (₹40,000/month rent), hire 1 sales manager (₹40,000/month), invest ₹5L in branded packaging design + e-commerce integration, list on 10+ platforms (Amazon, Swiggy, Blinkit, local apps). Attend IIFTEX (Indian International Seafood Trade Fair, Kochi, annual) to network with EU importers. Join Balasore Seafood Exporters Association for bulk export deals and subsidy intelligence.

Government Schemes

PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25% subsidy up to ₹25L for manufacturing unit, requires 10% owner contribution

SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹10-50L cluster-level grant for fishing communities and food processing units in coastal districts
