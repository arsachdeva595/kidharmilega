Shamli district (1167.58 sq km, 1.58M population) sits 80km north of Delhi in Uttar Pradesh, positioning it as the primary supply hub for North India's 50000+ tractor and trailer manufacturers. Steel and iron are sourced within 50-150km from Meerut, Gurugram, and local suppliers, keeping material costs 8-12% below national average. The district has 200+ registered axle and rim workshops, with 80% located in Shamli city proper — creating a dense knowledge ecosystem and reliable subcontractor base.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Shamli ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Shamli Industrial Estate (Phase 1 and 2) on a Tuesday morning. Walk into 3-4 axle workshops — ask for scrap costs, labor rates, CNC machine rental. Call Shamli Chamber of Commerce (+91-9012xxx area code). Attend Shamli Haat on Saturday — meet 10 suppliers. Cost: ₹0, takes 6 hours. Do this this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw steel sheet and rods from Meerut steel stockists (Indirapuram, Ganesh Nagar area — 35km away, ₹2-4L per batch). Approach 2-3 local CNC job workers for subcontracting rates (₹80-150/unit for basic turning). Buy first batch of 100 units material for ₹1.2L. Lead time: 7 days.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent 1500 sq ft workshop in Shamli Industrial Estate Phase 2 (₹12-18k/month, includes power). Buy 1 CNC lathe (₹6-10L used, depreciate over 4 years). Hire 2-3 skilled turners at ₹12-15k/month each. Option B: Partner with 2-3 existing axle workshops for subcontracting — zero capex, 3-5% commission on volumes. Option A requires ₹8-12L upfront, Option B requires ₹2-3L for quality control visits and first order deposits.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 15 mins). Apply for PM Vishwakarma scheme for artisan registration (₹2000 subsidy, processing fee ₹500). Get DIN from DC Handicraft office (free). If exporting: apply for IEC code via DGFT portal (₹0, 5 days). Consider GI registration after 2 years of proof of origin (apply to Controller of GI at IPO Delhi — ₹500-2000). Total cost: ₹2500, timeline: 30 days.
5
First Sale
First sale: contact 5 tractor OEM vendors in Sonipat or Faridabad via LinkedIn, WhatsApp. Offer first trial batch of 50 units at ₹2800/unit (30% margin) for tech evaluation. Post 3-4 videos on YouTube (CNC operation, quality check, finished products) and share on IndiaMART and TradeKey. First order: 200-500 units, expected first-month revenue ₹14-35L after 45-60 days lead time.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: add second CNC machine (₹8L), hire production manager and QA inspector. Exhibit at Tradeshow (Auto Component Sector at India Expo Center, Greater Noida — Feb/Sept annually). Build export documentation support — hire customs clearance agent (₹2-4k per shipment). Partner with 1-2 export trading houses for bulk orders. Move from job work to captive production in year 2. Target: ₹50L/month by month 18.
