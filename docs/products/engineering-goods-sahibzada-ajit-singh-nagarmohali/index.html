Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sahibzada Ajit Singh Nagar/Mohali ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sector 67-75 industrial cluster in Mohali on any weekday (free), talk to 3-5 shop owners about their sourcing, order size, and margin patterns. Call 2 steel suppliers in Mandi-Gobindgarh (Govt India Yellow Pages) to understand raw material pricing and bulk discounts. Register on Udyam Portal (free, 10 minutes) and download the ODOP district report from Punjab MSME website.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from steel suppliers in Mandi-Gobindgarh (40km) or Ludhiana — contact Federation of Industrial & Commercial Organization (FICO) for verified supplier list. First batch of precision components (100-500 units) costs ₹30K-₹1.5L depending on complexity (nuts/bolts vs. machined parts). Supplier area: Mandi-Gobindgarh Industrial Area — established suppliers offer credit terms of 15-30 days for repeat orders.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 500-1000 sqft unit in Sector 67-75 Mohali MSME park at ₹15K-₹25K/month with pre-installed 3-phase power. Setup: 2-3 CNC machines (₹2-₹4L each), manual tooling station (₹50K), quality checking tools (₹30K), or partner with existing precision fabricators as a co-manufacturer for zero capex. Hire 2-4 skilled machinists at ₹12K-₹18K/month each. Total capex: ₹5L-₹20L depending on machine choice.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online); obtain GST under MSME (free, online); apply for IEC code via Customs (₹0, online on DGFT portal) for export eligibility. Get basic ISO 9001 or ISO 9002 certification from TUV India or similar (₹80K-₹1.5L, 3-4 months) — non-negotiable for major buyers. Total administrative cost: ₹1L-₹2L over 4-5 months. No GI registration required for this category.
5
First Sale
First sales: list on TradeKey Industrial Marketplace or contact 5-10 Tier 2 auto suppliers in Gurgaon/Manesar directly via phone/email (get numbers from auto industry associations). First order likely ₹50K-₹3L for 500-2000 units. Expected first month revenue: ₹1.5L-₹5L if you secure 1-2 institutional buyers; 4-8 weeks payment cycle is standard.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: invest in a third CNC machine, hire 2 quality inspectors and 1 sales coordinator, apply for PMEGP subsidy (₹10L to ₹25L at 35% subsidy) for expansion. Participate in India Exim Summit (Delhi) and Engineering Goods Export Fair (Gurgaon) twice yearly. Build buyer relationships via direct site visits to auto clusters in Haryana/UP. Setup a basic B2B website optimized for export keywords (₹50K). At ₹25L+/month, hire a dedicated export manager, register with APEDA or FIEO export consortium, apply for bank credit lines under MUDRA/SFURTI.
