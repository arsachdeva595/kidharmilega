Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Rajouri ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Rajouri walnut orchards in Nowshera and Darhal tehsils; meet with 3-5 established walnut farmers through Rajouri Agriculture Department. Study walnut processing units in Samba district. Research export requirements via APEDA office in Srinagar. Attend walnut farming workshops at SKUAST-K.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source walnut saplings from SKUAST-K nurseries (₹50-100 per sapling). For immediate resale, contact established Rajouri walnut farmers/aggregators for 500-1000 kg first batch (₹4-6 lakh). Establish relationships with 2-3 primary orchardists for consistent supply agreements.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3-5 walnut orchardists in Rajouri on contract farming model or co-ownership. Set up small processing unit (₹15-25 lakh) for cleaning, grading, and packaging near Rajouri town. Alternatively, work with existing cooperative societies like Rajouri Walnut Growers' Cooperative.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, ₹0 cost). Obtain IEC code from Customs for export from Srinagar. Apply for FSSAI registration for packaged products (₹3000-5000). Trademark brand name at Indian Patent Office. Set up GI certification process if planning premium positioning (not currently GI-tagged).
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh, Blinkit, and Jiomart with 500 kg inventory. Create Instagram/YouTube content showcasing Rajouri orchards. List on export platforms (Global Sources, TradeKey). Email 50 health & wellness retailers and confectioneries in metro cities. Partner with 1-2 online organic retailers (Nature Basket, OrganicJustly). Offer bulk discounts to bakeries and hotel chains.
6
Scale
After ₹10L revenue, expand to 5-7 new e-commerce platforms. Launch value-added products (walnut oil, halves/pieces). Build direct B2B relationships with 20+ food companies. Apply for PMEGP for 2nd processing unit expansion. Achieve 50% export mix by month 12. Build supply chain with 10-15 orchardists for 5000 kg/month capacity.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy for agri-processing units in rural areas with 25-35% margin support
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
support for walnut clusters with ₹20-50 lakh grants for infrastructure and brand building
🏚
J&K Agricultural Subsidy Scheme
40-50% subsidy on walnut orchard establishment and modernization of processing equipment for local farmers
🏚
J&K Export Promotion Scheme
5-10% subsidy on export logistics and certification costs for agricultural products shipped via air/rail from Kashmir Valley
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
