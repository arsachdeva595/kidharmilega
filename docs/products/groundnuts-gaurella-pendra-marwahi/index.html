Groundnuts ki
opportunity, Gaurella Pendra Marwahi mein.

Gaurella Pendra Marwahi district, carved from Bilaspur in 2020, spans 2,307 km² with three operational tehsils directly connected to groundnut belts. Raw material is abundant within 20-40 km radius—166 gram panchayats across the district have active groundnut cultivation; nearest buyers are in Bilaspur mandi (35 km), Raipur wholesale markets (90 km), and Chhattisgarh's export hubs. The tribal community base (Baiga, Gond, Korwa) has 400+ years of groundnut farming expertise, reducing quality-control risk.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Gaurella Pendra Marwahi ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call 3 suppliers in Marwahi mandi (best groundnut hub in GPM); visit a farmer collective in Gaurela village; attend Bilaspur APMC groundnut auction on Thursday morning — free. Register name and mobile on Ministry ODOP website's GPM page to get list of certified suppliers.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw groundnuts from Marwahi mandi (largest in district) or directly from Gaurela farmer cooperative unions. First batch: 2-3 quintals (₹9,000-15,600 at ₹4,500-5,200/quintal). Marwahi mandi operates year-round; peak harvest Oct-Dec offers lowest prices.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 500 sq ft unit in Gaurela town (₹3,000-5,000/month) or Bilaspur MSME cluster (₹4,000-6,500/month). Essential: roasting drum (₹25,000-35,000), oil press (₹40,000-60,000 if extracting oil), drying rack, salt mixer. Total setup: ₹140,000-300,000. OR partner with existing groundnut processor in Pendra (saves 60% capex, gives 12-15% commission).
4
Brand & Register
Register on Udyam (free, 30 mins online). Trademark brand name with IP office (₹9,000-12,000, 8 weeks). For export: apply for IEC from DGFT (free online, 3 days). Consider GI registration support under One District One Product scheme (Rs 5,00,000 subsidy available via Chhattisgarh Industries Dept if filing jointly).
5
First Sale
First sale: post on Amazon Seller Central (5% fees + ₹500/month), list on Meesho (15% commission). Minimum first order: 50 packs (400g). Expected first month revenue: ₹35,000-60,000. OR approach 5 retail shops in Bilaspur directly with samples; target 50 kg/week initial supply.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 2 quality checkers (₹12,000/month each), 1 delivery coordinator. Set up cold storage unit (₹80,000-120,000). Apply for SFURTI cluster subsidy (₹30 lakh max for infra expansion). Exhibit at Chhattisgarh Rajya Khanij Udyog Mela (annual, free stall) and All India Food Expo (Delhi, Feb).
