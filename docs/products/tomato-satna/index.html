Tomato ki
opportunity, Satna mein.

Satna district, in northeastern Madhya Pradesh, sits on alluvial plains with annual rainfall of 1,200–1,400 mm and irrigation from Tons and Betwa rivers, supporting continuous tomato cultivation across 5 development blocks. The district has organized 2,125 villages into 703 panchayats with established transportation links via NH-12 and NH-39, connecting to major processing hubs in Indore (420 km) and Delhi (420 km). Annual production of 45,000–55,000 MT feeds domestic wholesale markets, food processors (Britannia, Nestlé regional units), and export corridors to South Asia and West Asia via Nagpur and Delhi ports.

Suitable For

First-gen founder with ₹2-5L savings, 6-12 months runway, and willingness to learn cold chain and export documentation. Ideal if you have family land (1-2 acre) or can lease it cheaply. Part-time initially, then full-time after first harvest.

Not for: Founders with zero agricultural exposure who expect quick returns without seasonal patience. Avoid if you have no access to water supply or cannot commit 4-5 months per cycle. Skip if allergic to manual labor and soil contact.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Satna ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Satna grain mandi and Satna vegetable subzi mandi on a Tuesday or Saturday morning. Call 3 tomato farmers from Vidisha block or Amarpura village (ask mandi brokers for contacts). Chat for 30 mins — cost, labor, water, yield, buyers. Attend a free farmer seminar at Satna ICAR Krishi Vigyan Kendra (no registration needed). Total time: 2-3 days, zero cost.
2
Source Raw Materials
Lease 1-2 acres in Amarpura, Vidisha, or Patera village (near Satna city, within 20 km). Nursery seedlings from Satna Agriculture Department (₹40-60 per 1000 seedlings) or buy hybrid seeds from Syngenta/Mahyco dealer in Satna city (₹3,000-5,000 per packet covers 1 acre). First-batch land prep + seeds + labor ≈ ₹35,000-50,000. Sourcing time: 1-2 weeks. Cycle: 55-60 days to first harvest.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (Own land): invest ₹80,000-150,000 for drip irrigation, raised beds, and organic inputs. Option B (Farmer partnership): contract with 1-2 established tomato farmers, supply capital (₹60,000-100,000), take 40-50% of harvest. Rent a 500-sq-ft collection/packing shed near mandi for ₹2,500-4,000/month. Total startup ≈ ₹140,000-200,000 if partnering; ₹200,000-280,000 if own land.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (free, 10 mins) on udyamregistration.gov.in. File IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) for export — ₹500-1,000, 3-5 days via Directorate of Foreign Trade (online). GI registration for Satna Tomato not yet filed — you can pioneer it (₹25,000-50,000 legal cost, 2-3 years). FSSAI registration for packaged tomato products ₹4,000-7,000, 2-3 weeks. Total brand setup: ₹5,000-8,000 + 3-4 weeks.
5
First Sale
First sale: approach Satna Subzi Mandi brokers (0-cost intro), commit 50-100 crates (5-10 MT) at wholesale ₹22-25 per kg. Expected first-month revenue ₹55,000-80,000 (10-15 days selling). Parallel: list on BigBasket Vendor Hub and Jio Mart within 2 weeks (margin 50-60% but lower volume initially, ₹10,000-15,000/week). Build small D2C base via WhatsApp orders to 20-30 local hotels/caterers by week 3.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: expand to 4-5 acres contract farming, hire 1-2 farm managers (₹12,000-15,000/month each), invest in cold storage van (₹300,000-400,000 EMI ₹8,000-10,000/month). Export 200-300 crates/month via Nagpur cold chain operators (Mogul Logistics, Cold Express). Apply for PMEGP subsidy (₹1.5L-2.5L) to fund cold chain. Attend Agritech expos (Krishi Unnati Mela, Indore), partner with restaurant chains for direct supply contracts.

Where to Set Up

Infrastructure ready hai — plot lo aur shuru karo

Satna MSME Cluster (Vegetable Processing Zone)
Established 2012 near Satna city bypass. 35 operational units producing tomato paste, puree, sauce, pickle. Provides pre-fabricated sheds (2,000-5,000 sq ft) at ₹25-40 per sq ft annually. Shared cold storage (200 MT capacity), common testing lab, waste disposal facility.
MSME Cluster
Satna Agricultural Export Zone (SAEZ)
Managed by Satna Chamber of Commerce, supports cold chain and export logistics. 12 export-certified units. Offers ₹3,000-5,000 sq ft warehousing with temperature control. Single-window clearance for IEC and customs documentation. 8 km from Satna city.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Kisan Samman Nidhi
₹6,000/year direct cash to farmer; you or contracted farmers can claim this.
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
₹25 Lakhs capital subsidy for agri-business units; up to 35% subsidy if you are a first-generation entrepreneur in AP/general category.
🏚
Mukhyamantri Kisan Kalyan Yojana (MP)
₹2,000-4,000 cash assistance per acre per season for organic farmers; requires Udyam registration.
🏚
MP Organic Farming Mission
free organic certification for 100 acres in a cluster; ₹20,000-30,000 per-farm subsidy for drip irrigation setup.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
