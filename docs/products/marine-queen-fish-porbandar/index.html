Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Porbandar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call 5 active fishermen in Porbandar port area or Veraval (next port, 50 km away) and ask: catch volume per day, price fluctuation, how buyers are contacted. Visit Porbandar fish market (Kharva mohalla) on a 5 AM landing day to see unloaded catch and speak to 3 existing processors. Register free Udyam account online (udyamregistration.gov.in) this week — zero cost.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source first 2 MT from Porbandar fish market directly at landing (₹1.5-1.8L for 2 MT Queen Fish or mixed catch). Negotiate with 2-3 fishermen for recurring weekly supply. First batch arrives same day as payment; arrange ice and cold van immediately (hire ₹800-1200/trip). Supplier cluster: Porbandar port, Veraval fish market.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 600-800 sq ft cold room (₹8-12K/month) + 400 sq ft processing space (₹3-5K/month) near port or in Porbandar MSME cluster (if exists). Partner with 2-3 contract processors on revenue-share (30-40% of sale value to them, you handle sales) OR hire 2 permanent workers (₹8-10K each/month) and 3 contract labor (₹300-400/day). Setup: ice machine (₹60K), vacuum packer (₹25K), refrigerator unit (₹40K), trays/boxes (₹10K). Total: ₹4.5-7L with rent deposit and 2 months float.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam within 3 days (free, 10 min online). Apply for FSSAI license (food processing license) via state FSSAI office in Porbandar — cost ₹5-8K, 10-15 days. If exporting: apply for IEC (Importer Exporter Code) via DGFT portal (free, 7 days online). GI registration for 'Porbandar Marine Queen Fish' optional (₹10-15K, 6 months process) but add credibility. No GI status currently — you can be first.
5
First Sale
First sale: contact 2 premium restaurant chains in Ahmedabad (2 hrs away) with 20 kg sample @ ₹200/kg (wholesale); expect ₹4K first order. Post product on Udaan B2B marketplace and JioMart B2B (free listing) targeting hotel procurement teams. First month realistic: ₹1.5-2.5L revenue if you ship 15-20 kg/day at ₹170-200/kg.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month (40-50 kg/day): hire dedicated sales manager (₹20K/month), register as GST (small supplier, 5% GST), approach frozen food distributors in metro cities, exhibit at Annapoorna International Food Exhibition (Mumbai, Oct). Scale to 2-3 cold units, negotiate 5-10 MT weekly contracts with organized players. Export by month 8-10 via Mundra port (140 km).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (25% for general category) for fish processing unit setup if registered as SSI/MSME.
🏛
MUDRA Loan (Business or Shishu scheme)
₹50L unsecured loan at 10-12% p.a. for working capital and equipment; approval in 7-10 days if Udyam-registered.
🏚
One District One Product (ODOP) Scheme
Gujarat fish processors eligible for export promotion grant (₹5-10L per unit) and brand support, apply via state ODOP nodal agency.
🏚
Gujarat Fisheries Development Scheme
₹2-5L subsidy for cold storage setup and processing units; apply to Department of Fisheries, Gujarat, Gandhinagar.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
