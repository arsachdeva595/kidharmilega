Chandrapur district sits on Maharashtra's eastern plateau with deep irrigation networks supporting 1.38 lakh hectares of rice cultivation. The Mul Road cluster near Chandrapur city houses 40+ operational rice mills with direct access to paddy suppliers in Bramhapuri and Nagbhir tehsils, each producing 200,000+ tonnes annually. Proximity to NH-6 (Mumbai-Nagpur corridor) and rail connections enables same-day delivery to Nagpur, Vidarbha wholesalers, and APMC markets in Malegaon and Nashik.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Chandrapur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Mul Road rice mill cluster (2km from Chandrapur city center) on a Tuesday to see 5-6 operational mills; call 2-3 mill owners asking about paddy sourcing costs, current wholesale price, processing turnaround; register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) and download the ODOP district profile from chandrapur.gov.in. Total time: 1 week, ₹0 cost.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source paddy from Bramhapuri APMC mandi (15km away) or direct farmers in Nagbhir (25km) at ₹1,800-2,000/quintal during harvest (Oct-Dec). First batch: 100 quintals = ₹1.8-2L. Establish supplier relationships through mill owners on Mul Road who will introduce local aggregators.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 500 sq ft unit on Mul Road near existing mills (₹5,000-8,000/month, ready with 15-20 tonne/day processing capacity) OR partnership with an existing mill for toll processing (₹200-300/tonne service fee, no capital needed). Equipment cost if buying your own: ₹18-25L (huller, polisher, grader, packaging unit). Lease option: ₹80,000-1.2L/month for 10 tonne/day capacity.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME (free, 1 day online). Apply for Phytosanitary Cert through Maharashtra Animal Husbandry Dept (₹2,000, 15 days). If exporting: obtain IEC code from DGFT (free, 1 day online). Consider Fair Trade Certification (₹25,000-40,000, 60 days) for premium D2C sales. Total regulatory cost: ₹27-42K, 75 days.
5
First Sale
First sale: Contact 3-4 neighborhood retail stores or online platforms (Jio Mart Business, BigBasket B2B). Offer 5-10kg trial order at ₹45-50/kg wholesale. Expected first month: 50-100kg sales = ₹2.5-5K. Parallel: Register on Udyam helpline (pib.gov.in) for PMEGP subsidy pre-approval. First revenue ramp: 2-3 months to reach ₹50K/month from local B2B.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (~20 tonnes/month): Hire 1 full-time manager + 2 packaging staff. Exhibit at APEDA trade shows (Ahmedabad, Delhi) to access export buyers. Upgrade to 30-tonne/day processing via equipment investment (₹5-8L more) or partnership expansion. Launch D2C brand on Udaan, Blinkit for ₹2-3L/month revenue by month 10-12.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to 25% subsidy on equipment (max ₹50L loan, ₹12.5L subsidy for SC/ST/women), 5-7 year tenure
🏛
PM MUDRA Loan
₹10L unsecured business loan at 8-12% interest for MSME rice mills, no collateral needed post-Udyam registration
🏚
Maharashtra Agricultural Produce Market Committee (APMC) Subsidy
₹1-2L one-time for cold storage/processing units in rural areas, apply via District APMC office
🏚
Maharashtra ODOP Cluster Development Scheme
₹50K-2L grants for equipment upgrades and common facility centers on Mul Road, apply via District Industries Centre
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
