Raw materials—stainless steel billets, brass rods, and plastic pellets—are sourced from Jamnagar, Belgaum, and local suppliers within 200-400km radius, reducing lead times to 7-10 days. Buyers include HDFC, L&T, Kohler distributor networks, 150+ plumbing wholesalers in Delhi-NCR, and 80+ export agents who consolidate containers to US, Germany, and Middle East monthly.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Mathura ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Mathura MSME Sanitary Fittings Cluster office (near Mathura City railway station); call 3-4 established manufacturers (Ashirwad, Finolex dealer networks available); attend Mathura Chamber of Commerce monthly haat; register free on Udyam portal — all doable in one week, ₹0 cost
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from Mathura Local Suppliers Association (MLSA) or direct from Jamnagar brass mills (15-20% cheaper). First 500-unit batch costs ₹1.2-1.8L for waste couplings/flush cocks mix. Brass rods: ₹450-550/kg; stainless steel: ₹120-140/kg; plastic pellets: ₹80-100/kg. Lead time 10-14 days.
3
Produce / Partner
Start via artisan partnership (no upfront capex): Employ 2-3 experienced machinists in Mathura cluster who own equipment, pay ₹30-40/piece; rent a 400 sq.ft. assembly-QC space (₹6-8k/month) to house storage, testing rig, and packaging. OR invest ₹6-8L in own 2nd-hand CNC machines + testing equipment (lathe, drilling, threading); hire 4 workers; rent 800 sq.ft. unit in Mathura MSME cluster at ₹10-12k/month. Payback in 14-18 months at full capacity.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME (free, 30 mins online). Apply for IEC code (Import Export Code) from DGFT office in Agra (₹0, 7-10 days). Get ISO 9001:2015 certification from TUV/BSI (₹40-60k, 4-6 weeks) to access B2B tenders. GI registration not applicable; focus on BIS ISI mark (optional, ₹25-35k, used for retail premium).
5
First Sale
List on IndiaMART as a verified seller (14-day approval); create 20-30 product variants with images. First customer likely from plumbing wholesalers via direct cold calls (collect 5-10 numbers from MLSA directory). Target ₹25-35k first order (50-100 units). Expected first-month revenue ₹40-60k (10-15 orders at ₹3-6k/order average). Alternatively, approach 1-2 plumbing distributors in Agra/Kanpur with samples and 15% trade margin.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: Hire 2 full-time sales staff (IndiaMART + B2B outreach); invest ₹3-5L in brand website + SEO (boosts export inquiries); join Mathura Exporters Association to access buyer databases and trade shows. Exhibit at IPLUMBEX (New Delhi, Feb-Mar annually). Hire a QA tech (₹20-25k/month) to pass stricter B2B audits. Move to 2000 sq.ft. unit by month 18-20 when margin improves from OEM contracts.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
₹10L subsidy (25% of project cost) for units in rural/semi-urban Mathura; 35% subsidy for SC/ST
🏛
MUDRA Shishu Loan
₹50k-1L unsecured loan at 8.5-9% for working capital; repay in 5 years; no collateral
🏚
UP MSME Cluster Development Scheme
₹50k-2L subsidy for machinery, testing, warehouse in notified clusters; apply via District Industries Centre Mathura
🏚
UP One District One Product (ODOP) Export Promotion Scheme
50% subsidy on trade fair participation, up to ₹3L/year; apply directly to Mathura ODOP nodal officer
