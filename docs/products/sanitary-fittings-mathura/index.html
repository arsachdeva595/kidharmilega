Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Mathura ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Hathras sanitary fittings cluster (30 km from Mathura city) — interact with 10+ established manufacturers, tour Radha Enterprises and Spectrum Sanitaryware units. Study competitor pricing on IndiaMART and TradeKey. Attend IIFHE trade show (Delhi) or International Plumbing Exhibition. Cost: ₹5,000-10,000.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source brass rods from Jamnagar suppliers (₹180-220/kg), stainless steel blanks from Pune vendors (₹250-300/kg), and chemicals from local Mathura suppliers. First batch (500 units of mixed fittings): ₹1.5-2.2 L. Build relationships with 3-4 vendors to ensure backup supply and 15-20% discounts on bulk orders.
3
Produce / Partner
Either lease a 2,000 sq ft unit in Hathras MSME industrial estate (₹8,000-12,000/month) or partner with existing job-work units for initial 6-12 months production. Invest in basic CNC machines (₹40-60 L), chromium plating setup (₹15-20 L), and quality testing equipment (₹5-8 L). Hire 5-8 skilled workers trained in fitting assembly and finishing.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam on udyamregistration.gov.in (free, online). Obtain IEC code from DGFT (₹0, online). Get quality certifications: ISI mark for fittings (₹2-5 L, 3-4 months), BIS license, and ISO 9001:2015 (₹80,000-1.5 L). Register trademark with IPO (₹4,500-9,000). Business registration and PAN/GST: ₹5,000-10,000.
5
First Sale
Launch on IndiaMART and TradeKey with 50+ product images and detailed specs. Approach top 20 hardware retail chains (Kajaria, Prince Plumbing, etc.) with samples and price quotes. Join industry associations (AIMA — All India Manufacturers Association). Attend monthly trade fairs at Pragati Maidan or Delhi trade complex. First month target: 20-30 wholesale orders minimum order size 100 units.
6
Scale
After 6 months and ₹30-50 L revenue, invest in export documentation (Customs broker: ₹1-2 L annually). Target Middle East buyers through B2B portals (Global Trade Atlas, Alibaba.com). Expand product range to premium designer fittings. Consider setting up second production line for export-grade products with enhanced QC. Build brand presence at PLUMBEX India and international trade shows.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 L loan with 25% subsidy (general) or 35% (SC/ST) for manufacturing units with ₹5 L margin money
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹10 L grant for cluster development, capacity building, and working capital in Mathura sanitary fittings cluster
🏚
UP ODOP Scheme
subsidy on certification, export promotion, and market linkage for registered ODOP entrepreneurs in sanitary fittings
🏚
UP Start-up Subsidy Scheme
₹10 L interest-free loan for manufacturing in Mathura district with 5-year repayment, zero collateral for first ₹5 L
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
