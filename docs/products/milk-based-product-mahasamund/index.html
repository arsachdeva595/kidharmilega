Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Mahasamund ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Mahasamund Dairy Cooperative Federation and Raipur Milk Union to understand supply chain. Study existing producers in Durg and Rajnandgaon clusters. Attend dairy exhibitions at Chhattisgarh Agricultural Department. Research cold-chain requirements and food safety certifications (FSSAI).
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fresh milk directly from registered dairy farmers in Mahasamund taluk at ₹30-35/liter (negotiate bulk contracts). Purchase starter cultures from Nirma or Chr. Hansen through Delhi food supply distributors (₹50,000 for 3-month batch). First production batch (500L milk capacity): ₹200,000-300,000 including packaging materials.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing micro-dairy units in Mahasamund or lease a dairy facility near Dalli-Rajhara with cold storage. Hire 2-3 trained dairy technicians from Chhattisgarh Dairy Training Institute. Start with yogurt/flavored milk production, then scale to desserts. Consider SFURTI-supported clusters for shared equipment access.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME within 15 days on udyamregistration.gov.in. Apply for IEC code via ecgc.in for export readiness. Obtain FSSAI license (₹5,000-10,000, 15-20 days). Register trademark on ipindia.gov.in. Get ISI/BIS certification for milk products (₹30,000-50,000).
5
First Sale
Launch on Blinkit and Dunzo with 200-500 unit initial inventory for Raipur city (focus Amanaka, Pandri areas). Create Instagram/Facebook brand with farmer sourcing stories. Approach 15-20 premium cafes (Raipur: The Grail, Café Coffee Day outlets) with 10-day trial offers. List on local Swiggy Instamart. Partner with 3-4 organic retail chains.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to Bilaspur and Durg districts via wholesale distributors. Launch 2-3 new SKUs (flavored lassi, paneer-based desserts, premium ghee). Open DTC website with WhatsApp ordering. Apply for PMEGP subsidy for production capacity upgrade. Build B2B relationships with 30+ restaurants. Target ₹30L revenue by month 12.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L loan with 25-35% subsidy for milk processing units, applies to MSME applicants.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
cluster-based support for dairy units with equipment subsidy up to 60% and shared facility development.
🏚
Chhattisgarh Rajiv Gandhi Grameen Bhoomiheen Kranti Yojana
support for dairy entrepreneurs with ₹50,000-100,000 assistance and training for milk-based products.
🏚
Chhattisgarh State Agricultural Department Dairy Subsidy Scheme
40% subsidy on dairy equipment (chillers, pasteurizers) for registered cooperative members and individual producers.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
