Allagadda Stone Carving ki
opportunity, Nandyal mein.

Nandyal district, particularly Allagadda village, has a 200+ year heritage of stone carving mastery passed through generations. The region's geological proximity to quarries of high-quality granite and sandstone, combined with skilled artisan communities, makes it the natural hub for this craft. Local stone carvers have perfected techniques for intricate religious and decorative sculptures.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Nandyal ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Allagadda village (15 km from Nandyal town) to study stone carving clusters. Connect with master carvers, visit 3-4 active workshops, understand stone types and pricing. Contact Nandyal ODOP office and DC Handicraft regional office for artisan networks and GI guidelines.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source granite blocks from Nandyal quarries (₹8,000-15,000 per ton) and Belgaum/Chitradurga suppliers for marble. For first batch of 5-10 pieces, budget ₹1,50,000-3,00,000. Establish relationship with local stone suppliers in Allagadda market for regular supply.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 established artisans in Allagadda on commission basis (₹800-2,000 per piece depending on complexity) before scaling. Alternatively, lease small workshop space in Allagadda (₹5,000-10,000/month) and hire trained apprentices. Plan 60-90 days production cycle for custom orders.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME classification), obtain IEC code for exports via DGFT, register GI certification use with DC Handicraft. Create brand identity emphasizing 'Authentic Allagadda GI Certified' heritage. Register business on ONDC platform and Udyam MSME Sambandh portal.
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy (global reach, ₹0 setup), IndiaCart and Craftizen (B2B connections), Instagram/Facebook with WhatsApp catalog. Approach 10-15 high-end interior design studios in Hyderabad, Bangalore with portfolio. Contact 5-10 luxury hotels and temple committee members directly with custom proposals.
6
Scale
After first 6 months, expand to 5-7 artisan partnerships, hire dedicated designer for custom commissions, build 3-4 showroom partnerships in metro cities (Hyderabad, Bangalore, Chennai). Launch B2B2C model with interior designers. Explore participation in IITF Delhi, Surajkund Mela for brand visibility.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma
up to ₹15,000 annual toolkits + 5% interest subvention on MUDRA loans for artisans and their coordinators
🏛
PMEGP
up to ₹25 lakhs non-refundable subsidy (35% for general, 25% for manufacturing) for enterprise setup in identified sectors
🏚
Andhra Pradesh MSME Development Scheme
5% interest subsidy on term loans + one-time infrastructure subsidy up to ₹10 lakhs for handicraft enterprises
🏚
AP Artisans Welfare Scheme
health insurance, skill development grants, and marketing support for registered handicraft workers and clusters
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
