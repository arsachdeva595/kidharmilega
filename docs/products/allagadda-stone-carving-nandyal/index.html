Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Nandyal ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Allagadda village this weekend. Walk through the cluster, observe 3-4 workshops, talk to carvers about their daily output and pricing. Call Nandyal District Industries Department (free consultation). Attend the monthly Nandyal Artisan Haat at Collectorate grounds — zero cost, real market exposure.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw granite and marble blocks from Anantapuram quarries (25-50 km away) — cost ₹800-2000 per block depending on size and grade. Connect via quarry associations or through existing carvers. First 10 blocks: ₹15,000-25,000. Supplier area: Anantapuram quarry belt, reachable by truck on NH44.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent a workshop unit in Allagadda (₹5,000-8,000/month, 400-600 sq ft) and hire 1-2 experienced carvers at ₹8,000-12,000/month each. Option B: Partner with existing carver on revenue-share (40-50% of piece value to carver). Setup: tools (chisels, mallets, polishing compounds) ₹30,000-50,000. First month production capacity: 20-30 pieces (₹30K-150K value depending on complexity).
4
Brand & Register
Register on Udyam portal (free, 10 minutes). File GI application through District Industries Department (₹2,000-5,000 filing cost, 6-9 month approval for new claimants if not already registered — Allagadda Stone Carving is already GI-certified since 2015, so claim it). Apply for IEC code if exporting (₹0 cost, DGFT online, 1 week). Get District DC Handicraft certificate (endorsement letter, ₹0, 1 week).
5
First Sale
First sale: List 5 signature pieces on Instagram Shop and Etsy (free, 10% platform fee). Price at ₹8,000-15,000 per piece. Reach out directly to 10 interior design studios in Bangalore via email with photos and pricing — expect first order 4-8 weeks after outreach. First-month revenue target: ₹50K-150K (5-10 pieces sold across channels at wholesale rate ₹6K-12K per piece).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: Hire second carver (₹10K/month). Invest ₹1.5-2L in photography/videography and branding. Attend International Handicraft Expos (IHGF Delhi, Bengaluru) — budget ₹1.5L for stall + logistics. Partner with 2-3 export agents in Bangalore/Hyderabad. Build WhatsApp catalog and cold-outreach to 50+ corporate office managers. Establish repeat orders from 5-8 design studios at ₹50K/month each.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh loan at 6% interest with 25-35% subsidy for manufacturing unit in Nandyal
🏛
Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises SFURTI Scheme (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
direct subsidy up to ₹10-20 lakh for artisan clusters, including tool and equipment grants
🏚
Andhra Pradesh Handicraft Development Policy 2023
25% subsidy on machinery and tools for registered artisans, cap ₹3 lakh per artisan unit
🏚
AP One District One Product (ODOP) Export Support Scheme
20% subsidy on air freight for ODOP products, direct support for APEDA certification, dedicated export officer at district level
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
