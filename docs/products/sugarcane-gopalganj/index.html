Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Gopalganj ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Khalgaon village cluster in Panchdeori block this week — talk to 3 jaggery producers, observe the modern plant operations (free, no permission needed). Call Gopalganj District Agriculture Office (0551-2222xxx) asking for recent sugarcane yield data and farmer contact lists. Register free on APEDA portal to check existing jaggery exporters from Bihar.
2
Source Raw Materials
Sugarcane sourcing: Contact sugarcane farmers directly in Panchdeori, Sidhwalia, and Kochas blocks (₹2800-3200 per tonne, Oct-Feb season). Visit Hapur mandi (30 km away) to understand regional sugarcane pricing. First batch: 10-15 tonnes sugarcane = ₹35,000-48,000 raw material cost.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a semi-automated jaggery unit in Khalgaon cluster (₹30,000-50,000/month) or partner with an existing producer for contract processing (₹12-15 per kg processing fee). Initial production: 500 kg/day capacity unit. Setup: kettles, collection tray, cooling bed, filtering cloth = ₹8-12L one-time (or lease equipment for ₹8-10k/month).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 10 mins online at udyamregistration.gov.in). Obtain Food Safety License from Gopalganj FSSAI office (₹5000, 15 days). Get GI application filed under 'Sugarcane Products of Gopalganj' if positioning as regional specialty (₹25,000 filing, 2-3 year approval). If exporting: apply for IEC code at nearest DGFT office (Muzaffarpur), costs ₹500.
5
First Sale
First sale: List on Amazon Pantry or local JioMart (₹500 listing fee). Target 100 kg/week in Month 1 at retail (₹250/kg = ₹25,000/week revenue). Parallel: contact 5 local sweet shops via phone/WhatsApp, offer first 50 kg at ₹150/kg trial order. Expected first-month revenue: ₹60,000-80,000 (wholesale 500 kg + retail 100 kg).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1-2 production workers (₹8,000-12,000/month), 1 delivery boy (₹7,000/month), invest in small storage shed (₹2L). Scale to 2-3 tonne/day production. Apply to SFURTI cluster scheme to co-brand with 5-10 other producers (subsidy up to 90L for common facility center). Target B2B contracts with bakery chains (Patna-based Monginis outlets = 500 kg/month contracts). Exhibit at Delhi Food Safety Expo (Jan) and Mumbai Confectionery Expo (Sept) to meet institutional buyers.

Where to Set Up

Infrastructure ready hai — plot lo aur shuru karo

Panchdeori Agro-Processing Cluster
Dedicated sugarcane and jaggery production cluster in Khalgaon village, Panchdeori block. Contains modern automated plant and 5-7 semi-automated units. Plot sizes: 500 sq ft to 2000 sq ft available for rent at ₹30-60/sq ft/month. Shared utilities: power, water, common storage.
MSME Cluster
Gopalganj Industrial Area
General industrial estate near Gopalganj town (8 km from cluster). Houses food processing units and small manufacturing. Plots: 1000-5000 sq ft. Rent: ₹20-40/sq ft/month. Less specialized than Panchdeori but closer to road/rail connectivity.
