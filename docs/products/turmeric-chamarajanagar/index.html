Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Chamarajanagar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Chamarajanagar's turmeric clusters in Gundlupet and Kollegal taluks; connect with farmer producer organizations like Chamarajanagar Turmeric Farmers' Association; study processing units in Kollegal; analyze competition on Spice Board website.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw turmeric rhizomes directly from farmer cooperatives in Gundlupet (Oct-Dec harvest) at ₹4,500-5,500/quintal; first batch: 10 quintals = ₹50,000-55,000; establish direct farmer relationships to ensure supply continuity.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing FMCG-certified processing units in Kollegal for drying, grinding, and packaging (contract manufacturing at ₹800-1,200/quintal); or establish in-house unit with dryer, grinder, and packaging machinery (₹20-30 lakhs investment).
4
Brand & Register
Register as Udyam MSE; obtain IEC code from DGFT; apply for FSSAI license; register trademark on Ipindiaonline.gov.in; obtain Spice Board's spice exporter certification if pursuing exports.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon, Flipkart, Jiomart via Udyam seller access; partner with Modern Trade chains (Nilgiris, Reliance Fresh) in Bangalore; approach ayurveda retailers in metro cities; create B2B relationships via Alibaba/Global Sources for export inquiries.
6
Scale
Achieve ₹50L monthly revenue by month 16; expand to 3-5 value-added products; build direct export relationships with 2-3 European/Asian distributors; establish brand presence via influencer partnerships in health/wellness niche; scale to 20+ quintal/month processing capacity.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP
up to ₹25L term loan with 25% subsidy for processing unit setup and working capital.
🏛
MUDRA Yojana
collateral-free loans up to ₹10L for inventory, marketing, and machinery for spice businesses.
🏚
Karnataka State Agricultural Export Policy
5-10% subsidy on export certification, cold chain, and logistics for agricultural products.
🏚
Karnataka Agri-Business Cluster Scheme
infrastructure support and common facility centers in Chamarajanagar for processing and quality testing.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
