Hoshangabad is uniquely positioned for sightseeing tourism due to its diverse attractions spanning natural wonders (Bhedaghat marble rocks, Satpura National Park), spiritual sites (Salkanpur Temple), and hill station charm (Pachmarhi). The district's strategic location in central India and well-developed road connectivity via NH44 make it an accessible destination for both domestic and international tourists seeking multi-faceted experiences.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Hoshangabad ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Hoshangabad district and study existing tourism clusters: Bhedaghat marble rocks zone, Pachmarhi Hill Station infrastructure, and Satpura National Park operations. Connect with district tourism office in Hoshangabad city and interview 10+ established tour operators. Study competitor pricing on MakeMyTrip, Airbnb, and local booking platforms.
2
Source Raw Materials
Identify and partner with local accommodation providers, vehicle owners, and trained guides in Bhedaghat and Pachmarhi areas. Negotiate commissions (15-25% for partners) and establish agreements. First batch operational costs: ₹15-25L for vehicle lease (6 months), insurance, and initial marketing materials.
3
Produce / Partner
Register with Hoshangabad District Tourism Board and Madhya Pradesh Tourism. Partner with 5-8 local guesthouses, homestays, and jeep operators rather than owning assets initially. Train 3-4 multilingual guides through NTTF-certified courses. Create standardized itineraries for 2-3 day packages covering Bhedaghat, Pachmarhi, Satpura.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (udyamregistration.gov.in) under tourism services category. Apply for IEC code via customs portal for potential foreign tour bookings. Register brand name with MCA. Obtain GST registration. Partner with Department of Tourism for ODOP promotion and certification.
5
First Sale
Launch on MakeMyTrip, Airbnb Experiences, and ToursByLocals. Create Instagram and Facebook pages with carousel posts of Bhedaghat marble rocks, Pachmarhi waterfalls. Offer first 50 bookings at 20% discount. Partner with 3-4 OTAs (One Travel Agency) in nearby metros. Build WhatsApp booking group. Target travel bloggers for reviews.
6
Scale
After 6 months, add premium packages (₹8K-15K per person for 3-day tours) and corporate team-building experiences. Expand to Indore and Jabalpur markets. Recruit 2 additional guides and tie up with 5 more homestays. Launch paid YouTube channel with destination guides. Partner with hotels in nearby cities for package referrals.

₹10L unsecured loans for tourism and hospitality startups via SIDBI partner banks
🏚
Madhya Pradesh Tourist Circuit Development Scheme
financial support for tourism infrastructure development and promotion in ODOP clusters
🏚
Vichar Manthan Yojana (MP)
skill development and training for tourism guides and hospitality workers in identified tourist destinations
