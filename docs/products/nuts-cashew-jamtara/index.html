Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Jamtara ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Jamtara Agricultural Produce Market and Madhupur cashew farming clusters; interview 10+ established cashew farmers; attend Ministry of Agriculture workshops on cashew cultivation in Jharkhand; study pest management practices specific to the region.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source seedlings from National Cashew Research Centre (ICAR) certified nurseries or local Jamtara growers; first-batch procurement of 5-10 acres seedlings costs ₹150,000-300,000; approach Jamtara District Horticulture Office for supplier networks.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 15-20 smallholder farmers in Jamtara through contract farming agreements; establish a small processing unit (roasting, grading, packaging) near Jamtara town; invest in basic machinery (₹400,000-600,000) for primary processing; hire local labor for manual sorting and quality control.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Micro/Small enterprise) with District MSME office Jamtara; obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office (Ranchi or online); register trademark for brand name with IP office; obtain FSSAI license for processing unit; get organic certification if targeting premium segment.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh, BigBasket, Blinkit within 4 months targeting Tier 1 cities; approach premium snack retailers (Modern Bazaar, Nature's Basket); engage B2B platforms (IndiaMART, TradeKey) for bulk exports; build direct WhatsApp/email customer base from day 1; participate in Jamtara District ODOP promotion events.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to 30-40 farmer partnerships; set up a branded packaging facility with seasonal cold storage; launch 3-4 value-added products (flavored cashews, cashew butter); build export relationships with Vietnam processors and UAE distributors; establish retail presence in 10 Tier 2 cities.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy for processing unit establishment in Tier 2/3 towns
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
grants for processing cluster development and farmer training
🏚
Jharkhand State Agricultural Department ODOP Support Scheme
market linkage, subsidy on machinery, and brand promotion assistance
🏚
Jharkhand Farmer Friendly Solar Scheme
50% subsidy on solar equipment for agricultural processing units
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
