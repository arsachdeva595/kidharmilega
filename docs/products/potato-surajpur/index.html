Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Surajpur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit APMC Rajnandgaon and Surajpur vegetable markets to understand pricing, varieties (Jyoti, Kufri Lalima, Kufri Pukhraj), and buyer networks. Connect with Surajpur District Agriculture Office and local Potato Cooperative Societies. Study seasonal demand patterns (Oct-Mar peak season).
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified seed potato from ICAR-CIPHET Shimla or National Seed Corporation at ₹10-12 per kg. Purchase organic manure from local producers (₹2,000-3,000 per tonne), and setup initial nursery on 0.5-1 acre at estimated cost of ₹80,000-150,000 for first batch.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 20-30 farmers in Surajpur through contract farming agreements providing seed, training, and procurement guarantees. Establish collection centers in Ramanujganj and Surajpur blocks. Set up simple sorting and grading facility (₹200,000-400,000) for quality standardization.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (agriculture processing) and obtain FPO (Farmer Producer Organization) certificate for farmer aggregation. Apply for IEC code from nearest customs office in Raipur. Get certification from Bureau of Indian Standards for processed variants. Brand ideas: 'Surajpur Gold Potato' or 'Chhattisgarh Premium Potato'.
5
First Sale
List on BigBasket, Blinkit, and LocalOkali for D2C sales targeting Raipur, Durg, and Bilaspur metros. Approach APMC wholesale traders directly with sampling at Rajnandgaon APMC. Partner with Ama Ghare app for Chhattisgarh. Pitch to potato chips manufacturers (lay's suppliers) with bulk supply contracts.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to cold storage (2,000-5,000 MT capacity, ₹25-40L investment) to extend off-season sales. Develop own potato chips or dehydrated product line. Establish supply partnerships with 100+ farmers. Target institutional contracts with school mid-day meal schemes and defense canteens.
Government Schemes

PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy for setting up processing/cold storage unit
🏛
Kisan Credit Card Scheme
easy access to ₹3L unsecured credit for agricultural input costs and working capital
🏚
Chhattisgarh Agricultural Export Policy
10% subsidy on cold chain infrastructure and export logistics for agricultural products
🏚
Chhattisgarh Farmer Producer Organization (FPO) Promotion Scheme
grants up to ₹15L for FPO registration and training

Marketing

Ye marketing ideas try karna mat bhulna

  • Instagram and Facebook reel campaign showing farm-to-table journey, targeting homemakers and chefs in tier-2 cities. Partner with micro-influencers in Raipur and Durg (500K+ followers) for organic farm visits.
  • Participate in AgriExpo Chhattisgarh and APEDA trade shows to connect with export buyers and institutional procurement officers. Distribute product samples with educational content on nutritional benefits.
  • WhatsApp and SMS campaigns to B2B buyers (restaurants, QSRs, hospitals) with weekly fresh stock alerts and bulk discount offers. Leverage APMC trader networks for word-of-mouth referrals.
