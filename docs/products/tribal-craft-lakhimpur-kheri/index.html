Lakhimpur Kheri is a 4,620 sq km district in the Terai region straddling Uttar Pradesh and Nepal, with dense sal and bamboo forests surrounding Dudhwa National Park. Tribal populations (Tharus, Korwas) concentrated in Bijua, Nighasan, and Kheri blocks have 200-300 active craft artisans producing wooden items, bamboo baskets, and woven textiles using centuries-old techniques. Proximity to Delhi-NCR (300km via NH-119) provides access to high-value gift and home décor retail; local raw materials are harvested sustainably from forest reserves.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Lakhimpur Kheri ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bijua Block development office (contact SDM Bijua, 05676-220XXX) this month. Call at least 3 artisans directly via Jan Suchna Portal ODOP list. Attend the Lucknow Haat in Lakhimpur Kheri (held monthly, free entry). Register free on Udyam portal — takes 20 minutes online. Zero cost, one week to complete.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from forest cooperatives in Bijua and Nighasan blocks. First batch (50-100 items): ₹40,000-80,000 direct from artisan collectives. Bamboo sourced from Dudhwa Forest Division; wood from licensed timber merchants in Lakhimpur city. Supplier area: Bijua Haat and Nighasan forest zone. Build relationships with 2-3 primary artisans before placing orders.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent 500-800 sq ft workspace in Lakhimpur city (₹3,000-6,000/month) as a collection + finishing unit. Option B: Direct partnership with Bijua artisan clusters — you provide design, they produce, you handle finishing/branding (₹0 rent, 35-40% of item cost as artisan fee). Start with Option B, scale to Option A at ₹10L revenue. Initial team: yourself + 1-2 artisan liaisons on contract.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 15 min). Open a current account with ICICI/HDFC (₹0, tie to Udyam). Apply for GI registration support via ODOP Scheme (₹3-5L subsidy for collective GI filing, 6-8 month timeline). If exporting, apply for IEC (Importer Exporter Code) via DGFT portal (₹0, 7 days). Get ISO 9001 (₹25,000-50,000 if required by international buyers). Timeline: Udyam done in week 1, GI filing in month 2, IEC in month 3.
5
First Sale
First sale: Etsy shop launch (list 20-30 items, average price ₹2,000). Simultaneously contact 5 boutiques in Delhi Craft Village and Good Earth outlets via WhatsApp with catalog + video. Minimum first order: Etsy average order ₹1,500-2,500 (2-3 orders/week), wholesale first order ₹8,000-15,000 from boutiques. Expected first month revenue: ₹25,000-40,000 if you move fast on Etsy + one boutique order in week 2.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire a full-time artisan coordinator (₹15,000/month), a D2C manager for social media (₹12,000/month), open own 1,200 sq ft studio in Lakhimpur city (₹8,000/month). Exhibit at Government ODOP pavilions in Delhi Craft Mela (Feb-March), Amar Jyoti Mela (Jan), Art Street Jaipur (Dec). Build WhatsApp catalog sales channel in parallel — target corporate procurement teams with monthly offerings.

Where to Set Up

Infrastructure ready hai — plot lo aur shuru karo

Lakhimpur Kheri MSME Cluster (Craft Village)
State-designated craft cluster in Lakhimpur city near Railway Station. 40-50 small artisan units, shared workspace available, ₹3,000-8,000/month rent for 300-800 sq ft units. Common workshops, tool-sharing facility, soft loans via SIDBI.
MSME Craft Cluster
Dudhwa Forest-Based Industries Zone (Proposed)
Planned cluster near Dudhwa National Park for bamboo and forest-product artisans. Currently informal. Expected formalization by 2025 with subsidy. Contact Nighasan block office for land allocation.
Forest-Based Industry Zone (Development Stage)
Bareilly MSME Super Cluster (nearby)
80km away in Bareilly district. 1,000+ units. Leather goods, textiles, machinery. Useful for sourcing complementary inputs and marketing exposure.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
up to ₹3L collateral-free MUDRA loan + ₹15,000 toolkits for registered artisans in your supply chain
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
35% subsidy on capital investment for SC/ST founders, 25% for others, up to ₹25L loan
🏚
UP One District One Product (ODOP) Scheme
design support, subsidy for cluster GI registration, up to ₹5L development fund, export facilitation at no cost
🏚
UP Craft Sector Development Scheme
50% subsidy on machinery/tools, ₹5,000-10,000 per artisan/year for training and material
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Frequently Asked Questions

Seedhe sawaal, seedhe jawab

₹1.5-3 lakh se start kar sakte ho — ₹40-50k raw materials ka first batch, ₹30-40k Etsy + branding, ₹50k emergency fund. Zyada paisa agar apna workshop khol rahe ho (₹3-8L). Government se PMEGP/ODOP se ₹3-5L loan subsidy mil jayega 2-3 mahine mein.
Craft experience helpful hai, lekin zaroori nahin agar tu artisans ke saath seedha work karega. 2-3 mahine lagega sahi supplier + design sikh ke. Etsy/branding knowledge best hai — agar nahin hai toh YouTube se 2 hafta mein seekh ja.
UP ODOP Scheme — free design, export market linkage, ₹3-5L cluster GI registration fund. PM Vishwakarma — artisans ko ₹3L loan + ₹15k toolkit. PMEGP — tujhe 35% subsidy on ₹25L capital. Plus Udyam registration se tax benefits + bank credit easy ho jate hain.
Etsy par list kar de, 1-2 hafta mein orders ayenge (gift season best). Saath mein WhatsApp ke zariye 10-15 boutiques ko reach kar — Good Earth, Mother Earth, Delhi Craft Village. Corporate gifting teams ko email kar — HUL, ITC, Nestlé ke CSR teams. First order typically ₹5-15k wholesale order hota hai 2-3 hafta mein.
Bilkul. USA, UK, Germany, France ko easily export kar sakte ho. Fair Trade certification lene se better price milta hai (+20-30%). IEC register kar (free, 1 week), Etsy International kar, aur direct importers ko email kar. ₹1-2L order aana typical hai 2-3 mahine baad export mein. Shipping cost: ₹3-8k per order depending on weight.
Tribal craft wala specific risk nahin hai — problem agar design wrong ho ya sourcing consistent na rahe. Exit option: Etsy band kar, existing stock local boutiques ko clearance sale karke sell kar do. Artisans ko chhod sakte ho anytime. Worst case: ₹50-75k loss, nahin ₹5L+ ki disaster.
