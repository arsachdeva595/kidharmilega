Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kanpur Nagar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kanpur Leather Complex near Jajmau industrial area; connect with Leather Export Promotion Council (LEPC) office; spend 2-4 weeks apprenticing with established leather units like Birla and Thapar family tanneries; attend workshops at Footwear Design and Development Institute (FDDI), Agra
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw hides from Kanpur's regulated hide markets or directly from CFTI (Central Footwear Training Institute) approved suppliers; expect ₹3-5L for first batch of quality vegetable-tanned leather (200-300 sq ft); negotiate bulk rates from local tanneries like Ram Prasad & Co., Kanpur
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with freelance artisans in Jajmau cluster (average cost ₹150-300 per piece for labor); or establish small production unit (2-3 skilled workers) in shared workspace in Kanpur for ₹50K-1L monthly rent; source machinery (cutting, pressing, stitching) for ₹5-8L initial investment
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Micro/Small Enterprise) via udyamregistration.gov.in; apply for IEC (Importer Exporter Code) from DGFT; get GI certification from Kanpur Leather GI board; obtain leather product testing certification from SLTC (Sino-Indian Leather Testing Centre); register trademark with IP India
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy and Flipkart Sellers with 10-15 hero products; contact leather importers on Alibaba.com and Global Sources; reach out to fashion boutiques in Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore via direct outreach; attend Leather Fair (April-May) at Delhi Trade Fair for B2B connections; run Instagram/YouTube content showing craftsmanship
6
Scale
After ₹10L revenue, invest in design innovation and collection expansion; hire 3-5 additional artisans; pursue ISO certifications; build wholesale dealer network across metros; establish own retail point or partner with multi-brand outlets; explore EU/US market through trade bodies like LEPC; implement quality control systems
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma
up to ₹15,000 annual support and collateral-free MUDRA loan up to ₹5L for artisans and craftspeople
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (25-35% of project cost) for setting up leather manufacturing units
🏚
UP Handicraft Development Scheme
assistance for design development, quality certification, and marketing for registered handicraft artisans
🏚
UP ODOP Export Promotion Scheme
incentives for packaging, certification, and international trade fair participation for Kanpur leather products
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Marketing

Ye marketing ideas try karna mat bhulna

  • Create Instagram Reels showing tanning process, hand-stitching techniques, and product transformations; tag fashion influencers (10K-100K followers) in sustainable fashion niche; use hashtags #KanpurLeather #SustainableLeather; target 30-day campaigns with 5-10% discount codes
