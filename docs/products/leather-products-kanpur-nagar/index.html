Kanpur Nagar sits in the Ganges plains of central Uttar Pradesh, 250 km south of Delhi with rail and highway access via NH-19 and NH-116. The Jajmau cluster concentrates 2000+ micro-units, tanneries, and artisan workshops within 8 sq km, sourcing raw hides from cattle markets in Lucknow, Mathura, and Agra (within 150 km). Finished leather goods ship to Delhi ports in 24-48 hours and to Nhava Sheva (Mumbai) in 3-4 days for export to 45+ countries.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kanpur Nagar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Jajmau cluster (Kanpur) on a Monday-Friday; walk through workshops near Leather Complex, Jajmau. Call 3-4 established unit owners (ask ODOP office for contacts). Attend monthly leather traders meet at Kanpur Chamber of Commerce (free). Register on Udyam portal (₹0, takes 30 mins).
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw hides from tannery suppliers in Jajmau (₹150-300 per hide, bulk 100+ hides = ₹15-30K for starter batch). Hardware, dyes, and chemicals from Prem Nagar market suppliers or online (IndiaMART, TradeKey). First batch 50-100 units costs ₹30-60K materials. Connect with 2-3 tanneries for regular supply agreements.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 500-1000 sq ft unit in Jajmau cluster or Leather Industrial Estate (₹8-15K/month) with basic tools (cutting table, hand press, stitching kits, finish stands = ₹50-100K one-time). Hire 2-3 skilled artisans (₹8-12K/month each) or partner with 5-8 home-based artisans on per-unit payment (₹50-150/piece). Setup timeline: 3-4 weeks.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME (free, online, 1 week). Apply for GI recognition support via SFURTI or Ministry scheme (₹1-2L subsidy, 6-8 week process). Register IEC code if exporting (DGFT, ₹500, 2 weeks). File brand name with IP office (₹5-10K, 8-12 weeks). Total formal cost: ₹15-30K.
5
First Sale
First sale: List on Amazon/Flipkart (3-5 wallets, belts at ₹500-2000 each). Target 5-10 sales in month 1 (₹5-20K revenue). Simultaneously cold-email 20 B2B retail buyers (travel brands, lifestyle stores) with product photos and bulk pricing (MOQ 50-100 units). Expected first month blended revenue: ₹10-40K from test sales.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: Hire 2 full-time quality checkers and 1 operations person (₹15-20K/month). Rent larger 2000 sq ft unit (₹25-35K/month). Exhibit at Mumbai Gift Fair, Delhi Gift Fair (₹1.5-3L booth cost but 10-15 bulk orders possible). Build social proof via Instagram Reels (unboxing, artisan stories, design process). Target 50-70% growth MoM via export partnerships and B2B contract manufacturing.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹10L subsidy for cluster development, 30% subsidy on setup cost
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
₹25L loan at 5% interest, 25% subsidy for SC/ST, 10% for general category (max ₹10L subsidy)
🏚
UP One District One Product (ODOP)
₹1L working capital loan at 0% interest for first year, free GI registration support, export promotion subsidy up to ₹2L
🏚
UP MSME Udyam Helpline
free business mentoring, subsidy on ISO certification (₹20K), participation in state-level exhibitions (₹50K reimbursement)
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Honest risk: leather prices volatile, tariffs on exports can spike, artisan dependency means inconsistent quality. Exit options: (1) Sell equipment + inventory to another unit (recover 40-50% cost).
