Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Jind ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Jind's industrial clusters near Safidon and Narwana; meet 5-10 existing engineering goods manufacturers; study SIAM (Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers) data; attend Jind Agricultural Expo; analyze tractor part specifications from John Deere India, Mahindra & Mahindra, TAFE supply chains
2
Source Raw Materials
Source steel from Jind's local metal suppliers (Amrit Steel, Bhagwan Steel) or SAIL distribution centers; initial raw material batch: ₹15-25 lakhs; connect with Jind's fastener suppliers in Safidon industrial estate for components; negotiate 30-45 day payment terms
3
Produce / Partner
Establish small unit (500-1000 sq.ft.) in Jind Industrial Estate; invest in CNC lathe, drilling machine, welding setup; partner with 2-3 existing job-work centers for overflow production; hire 5-8 skilled technicians from Jind's metalworking community; target initial capacity of 500-800 units/month
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam on udyamregistration.gov.in; apply for IEC from nearest DGFT office in Haryana; get ISO 9001:2015 certification (₹1-2 lakhs); register company name with ROC Haryana; create brand identity emphasizing 'Made in Jind' and quality certifications; obtain GST registration
5
First Sale
Contact top 20 tractor OEMs directly with samples; list on IndiaMART and TradeKey with ₹2-3 lakh annual investment; attend Jind Chamber of Commerce meetings; leverage FIEO (Federation of Indian Export Organisations) for export networking; approach 10-15 agricultural equipment dealers in Punjab-Haryana belt with price quotations and delivery terms
6
Scale
After 6 months, add CNC machining center (₹25-35 lakhs); develop 3-4 new component lines based on market demand; hire export manager; establish quality control lab; target ₹100+ crore annual turnover by month 24; build buyer relationships with 50+ distributors; pursue ISO/TS 16949 for automotive-grade products
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakhs subsidy for manufacturing units with 25% capital subsidy for general category entrepreneurs
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
cluster development fund for Jind engineering goods cluster up to ₹10 crores for infrastructure and skill development
🏚
Haryana Start-up Policy
5% interest subsidy on bank loans up to ₹1 crore, land at 50% discount in industrial estates
🏚
Haryana Enterprise Promotion Scheme
capital investment assistance of 25% for manufacturing units in Tier-2 and Tier-3 towns, GST reimbursement of 50% for 5 years
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
