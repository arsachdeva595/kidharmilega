Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Beawar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Beawar's established mineral clusters around MG Road and Rani Bagh industrial areas; meet 3-4 existing powder manufacturers; study processing techniques at plants like Shree Minerals and Beawar Minerals Private Limited; understand mining lease procurement from Rajasthan Mines Department.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw quartz and feldspar ore from licensed quarries near Beawar (15-20 km radius) at ₹800-1200/ton; first batch of 50 tons will cost ₹40-60L; verify Grade-A purity certificates; establish contracts with 2-3 suppliers for stable supply.
3
Produce / Partner
Start with toll processing partnerships with existing mills (cost ₹200-400/ton) before capex investment; identify 2-3 contractors offering ball mill grinding, drying, and bagging services; negotiate long-term rates; target 10-15 ton daily capacity initially.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME with Production Capacity Declaration; obtain IEC code from Jaipur DGFT office; get ISO 9001:2015 and mineral purity certification (Rajasthan Mines Department); register company under Companies Act; create brand identity with packaging and quality seals.
5
First Sale
Launch on Tradeindia and Global Sources for B2B; pitch directly to tile manufacturers in Gujarat and Morbi; contact glass makers in North India; use B2B email marketing to 200+ ceramic units; offer sample batches at cost; target first order of 5-10 tons within 3 months.
6
Scale
After first 6 months and ₹50L+ revenue, invest in own 5-10 ton/day ball mill unit (₹1.5-2.5 Cr); hire experienced plant manager and quality officer; develop 3-4 specialized grades (ultra-fine, high-purity, mesh sizes); expand export via trading houses; target ₹5 Cr revenue by year 2.
