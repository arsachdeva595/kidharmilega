Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Beawar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Beawar Mineral Market (Wednesday, Friday haats) and speak with 3-4 existing powder suppliers about raw material costs and buyer demand. Call Garg Mineral & Chemicals at 76 Gandhi Nagar to ask about their sourcing and production model. Register free on Udyam portal to see active mineral powder units in your area. Zero cost, doable in 2 days.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw quartz and feldspar from local quarries in Ajmer Road, Beawar (Khanpur, Raipur quarry clusters). Expect ₹600-800/tonne for quartz and ₹1200-1500/tonne for feldspar. First batch minimum 5 tonnes (mixed) costs ₹8,000-10,000. Supplier area: Beawar Mineral Suppliers Association handles 80% of local sourcing.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 1000-1500 sqft unit with 3-phase power in Ajmer Road industrial area (₹8,000-12,000/month). Buy a jaw crusher (₹3-5L) and ball mill (₹2.5-4L) as core equipment. Hire 2-3 skilled operators (₹8,000-12,000/month each) who can manage processing, sieving, and bagging. Total production cost per tonne: ₹2,500-3,500 (including labor, power, packaging). Alternative: Partner with existing 3-4 small units for toll processing at ₹400-600/tonne commission.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (free, takes 30 min). File for IEC code at Beawar DGFT office for export readiness (₹500, 7 days). Apply for ODOP brand building subsidy under MSME scheme (up to ₹1L for packaging/branding). GI registration not applicable as product lacks geographical distinction. Trademark your brand name at IPO (₹4,500-9,000, 18-month process).
5
First Sale
Post on IndiaMART and TradeKey with photos of your powder samples and certifications. Target first orders: bulk supplies to 2-3 ceramic manufacturers in Kota/Morbi (minimum 500-1000 kg orders). Expected first order: ₹15,000-30,000 gross. First month target: ₹80,000-120,000 revenue from 3-4 orders at 50kg-1000kg per buyer.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue, hire a dedicated sales person (₹15,000/month) and lab technician for quality testing (₹12,000/month). Invest ₹5-10L in additional processing capacity (second ball mill, sieve shaker). Exhibit at CeramicSExpo (Morbi, annual), GlassTech India (Delhi), and IIJS (Mumbai) to access large-scale buyers. Target 25-30% export revenue by Month 15-18.
