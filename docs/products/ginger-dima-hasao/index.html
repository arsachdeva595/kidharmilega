Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Dima Hasao ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit ginger farming clusters in Dima Hasao (Haflong, Harangajao areas); connect with local farmer cooperatives and the District Agricultural Office; study organic certification requirements (NPOP/APEDA); attend spice trader networks in Assam (Guwahati Spice Mandi).
2
Source Raw Materials
Source ginger rhizomes from certified local farmers or Assam Agricultural University nurseries; negotiate bulk pricing for 5-10 quintals first batch (₹60,000-₹1,00,000); establish contract with at least 3-4 farmers for supply consistency.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing farmer collectives in Dima Hasao (e.g., local SHGs) for processing and drying; set up small drying unit on-farm or hire existing drying infrastructure; ensure APEDA compliance for organic ginger; engage with state agriculture department for extension support.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (agricultural enterprise) with state; apply for IEC code from APEDA for export; obtain organic certification (NPOP) from agencies like INDOCERT or ECOCERT; register brand name with trademark office; get GST registration (5% for agricultural products).
5
First Sale
Launch on BigBasket, Organic Brands, and Biome as primary D2C channels; partner with 2-3 regional spice traders for bulk wholesale; approach ayurvedic companies (Himalaya, Patanjali) for supply agreements; list on Rajasthan Organic Shop and local Assam e-commerce platforms.
6
Scale
Expand farmer network to 15-20 contracted growers; develop ginger powder and value-added products; establish own processing unit (₹25-40L investment); target national wholesale markets (Delhi Spice Market, Mumbai); apply for SFURTI cluster support if reaching ₹50L+ turnover.
