Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sheopur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call 3 guava farming families in Sheopur tehsil this week (ask Sheopur NIC for contact list); visit Pune Mandi on a trading day to observe guava pricing, buyer behavior, quality grading; attend nearest horticultural society meeting or kisan sammelan. Zero cost. Talk to at least one exporter. This takes 1 week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified guava saplings from Rajendra Prasad Central Agricultural University, Samastipur (Bihar) or MP state nurseries in Indore — ₹15-25 per sapling. Bulk fertilizer from local Krishi Vigyan Kendra suppliers or Krishak Mandi in Sheopur town. First 500 saplings cost ₹10,000-12,000. Existing guava clusters in Sheopur city suburbs and Pohri tehsil can be visited to identify land rental options.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Lease 1-2 acres (₹8,000-12,000/year) from local farmers in Sheopur, install drip irrigation (₹40,000-60,000). Option B: Partner with 5-8 existing guava farmers as supplier-aggregator, provide seedlings and training, purchase their output. Hybrid approach costs ₹80,000-150,000 for year 1. Guava trees fruit in 2-3 years; interim revenue from intercropping vegetables possible. Register on Udyam immediately (free, 10 min online).
4
Brand & Register
Register business under MSME/Udyam within week 1 (free, online at msme.gov.in). Apply for GI registration with Madhya Pradesh Handicraft Cluster Council (₹5,000-8,000 fee, 6-month process). If exporting, obtain IEC code from DGFT (free online at dgft.gov.in). Get Agmark certification from Agricultural & Processed Food Products Export Development Authority for export-grade guava (₹3,000-5,000, 2-3 months). Get state trading license from district administration (₹500-1,000).
5
First Sale
Week 4: Create JioMart Fresh seller account (upload 10 kg sample); contact Pune Mandi commission agents (find 3-4 agents at mandi.org.in Maharashtra list). First order likely 50-100 kg to a local restaurant group or juice chain via IndiaMART. Expected first-month sales: ₹40,000-80,000 if wholesale route; ₹80,000-120,000 if D2C with slower ramp. Prioritize wholesale to Pune mandi first 2-3 months for cash flow, then add D2C.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: Hire 2 part-time packers (₹8,000/month each) and 1 quality manager (₹12,000/month). Expand to 3-4 acres leased. Open direct-to-retailer route via Modern Retail, Reliance Fresh buyers (requires GST-registered, FSSAI food license ₹10,000). Exhibit at Madhya Pradesh Kisan Mela (annual), national horticulture expos. Set up cold storage partnership with ITC or local cold chain operator (₹2,000-3,000/month). Target ₹30-40L annual by month 18.

Government Schemes

25% subsidy on machinery/equipment up to ₹25 lakhs for agri-startups
🏛
Agricultural Infrastructure Development Fund (AIDF)
concessional loans up to ₹2 crore for cold storage, pack houses, irrigation infrastructure at 3% interest subsidy

MP Krishi Karman Yojana
₹50,000-₹2,00,000 subsidy on farm equipment, drip irrigation installation, organic certification

30% subsidy on horticulture saplings + inputs for first 2 years (max ₹1,25,000)
