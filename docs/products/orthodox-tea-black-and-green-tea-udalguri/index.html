Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Udalguri ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit local tea estates in Udalguri town (Assam Tea Board can provide list); call 3-4 estate managers to understand leaf pricing, seasonal availability, and processing equipment suppliers. Attend the Assam Tea Auction held monthly in Guwahati (free to observe). Register on Udyam portal (free, online, 10 minutes). Zero cost, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fresh tea leaves directly from Udalguri tea estates or licensed leaf suppliers at ₹40-80 per kg (green leaf). First batch: 500 kg costs ₹20,000-40,000. Supplier clusters are Udalguri town and adjacent Kurikhola area. Negotiate multi-month contracts for consistent supply.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent 500-800 sq. ft. unit (₹8,000-12,000/month) with withering trays, rolling machines, drying drums, sorting tables (initial ₹3-6L equipment cost). Option B: Partner with 2-3 existing micro-tea processors in Udalguri to white-label your brand (₹30-50/kg processing fee, zero fixed cost). Option C: Hybrid — rent ₹1.5L space, buy withering and drying setup (₹2-3L), outsource rolling/sorting (₹20-30/kg). Production capacity: 20-50 kg per day from home setup.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 2 days). Apply for GI registration under Ministry of Agriculture's GI scheme (₹8,000-12,000 filing cost, 4-6 month timeline, if product meets Orthodox tea standards). Apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) via DGFT portal (free, online, 5 days) — mandatory for export. Get APEDA certification (Agricultural Products Export Development Authority) for tea exports (₹5,000, 2 weeks). Get FSSAI license for packaged tea (₹5,000-10,000, 2-4 weeks).
5
First Sale
First sale channel: list on Amazon FBA or Meesho with 100g trial packs at ₹299-399. First order target: 50-100 units via friends/community WhatsApp and local Facebook groups (₹15,000-40,000 revenue). Parallel: cold-email 10 premium cafes/hotels in Guwahati and Dibrugarh with samples; aim for first 10kg B2B order at ₹280/kg. Expected first-month revenue: ₹30,000-60,000 (50% from D2C, 50% from B2B).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 production manager (₹12,000/month), 1 packer/QC staff (₹8,000/month), 1 logistics coordinator (₹10,000/month). Upgrade to 1,500 sq. ft. production unit with semi-automated rolling/drying (₹8-12L capex). Target exports: attend trade shows (CoffeeTea Expo, World Tea Expo), apply for APEDA export promotion subsidy, secure orders from UK/German importers via TradeKey/Global Sources. Build brand presence: Instagram reel series on processing, YouTube channel on tea health benefits, partnership with influencers in health/wellness space.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme)
35% subsidy (max ₹10.5L) for manufacturing units with ₹25L project cost in rural areas
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹5L cluster development grant + 50% equipment subsidy for MSME clusters in tea processing
🏚
Assam MSME Business Assistance Scheme
25% subsidy on machinery + 5% interest subsidy on loans up to ₹50L, government guaranteed
🏚
Assam Tea Board Export Promotion Scheme
50% subsidy on certifications (APEDA, ISO, organic), trade show participation, and shipping costs for first 5 containers
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
