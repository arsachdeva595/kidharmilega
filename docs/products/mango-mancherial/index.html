Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Mancherial ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Mancherial's primary mango growing areas around Luxettipet and Pegadapalli; connect with Mancherial Agriculture Department and local farmer cooperative societies; study processing techniques at food science institutes in Hyderabad like IFMAI or local MANAGE centers
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fresh mangoes directly from Mancherial farmers during June-September harvest season at ₹8-15 per kg for first batch of 5-10 tonnes; establish contracts with 2-3 farmer groups to ensure consistent supply; visit wholesale markets in Hyderabad (Kacheguda) for supplier benchmarking
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing food processing units in Mancherial or Hyderabad with FSSAI license; alternatively, set up small unit (₹25-40L) with basic equipment (pulping machine, pasteurization unit, filling line); employ food technician for recipe development and quality control
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam under Food Processing category; obtain FSSAI license (Food Safety Registration for <5 crore); register IEC code with APEDA for export eligibility; file trademark for brand name with IP office; get GMP certification if targeting exports
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh, Flipkart, Jiomart with focus on premium mango product category; approach specialty health food retailers and organic chains (Nature's Basket, Naturals); contact hotel chains and QSR suppliers in Hyderabad; run Instagram and Facebook campaigns targeting health-conscious demographics
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand product line with mango-based beverages and ready-to-eat products; establish 3-4 state-wide distribution partnerships; pursue organic certification for premium positioning; apply for SFURTI cluster funding; initiate exports to 2-3 target countries through APEDA-registered export houses
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP - Pradhan Mantri Employment Generation Programme provides up to ₹25L subsidy (25-35% of project cost) for food processing entrepreneurs in Mancherial
🏛
SFURTI - Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries offers cluster support up to ₹1.5Cr for mango processing clusters in Telangana
🏚
Telangana Agriculture Mission offers subsidies on food processing equipment (25-50% subsidy) and credit guarantee for farmers and agripreneurs in districts with ODOP products
🏚
Telangana Startup India Seed Fund Scheme provides initial seed funding up to ₹50L for registered startups in food processing and agricultural value addition sectors
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
