Mango ki
opportunity, Mancherial mein.

Mancherial district in Telangana spans 3,840 sq km with deep black soil suited for mango cultivation; six established clusters (Nennal, Jaipur, Bheemaram, Bellampalli, Thandur, Mandamarri) produce 13 mango varieties with 50,000 tonnes annual capacity from 17,538 acres and 1,000 farming units. The district sits 230 km south of Hyderabad (institutional buyer hub) and 400 km west of Nagpur (Maharashtra food processing corridor), with NH44 passing through, enabling direct supply to processors and export ports via Vishakhapatnam (480 km).

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Mancherial ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call 3-4 mango farmers in Nennal or Jaipur cluster via Mancherial agriculture office (0724-2256111) and visit their farms to understand yield, variety, harvest timings. Attend the weekly Saturday produce mandi at Mancherial town to observe buyer behavior and price discovery. Register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) using Aadhaar. Zero cost, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Contact farmer groups in Nennal (largest cluster) or approach Mancherial Agriculture Produce Marketing Committee (APMC) at Mancherial mandi. First-batch sourcing: 2-5 tonnes of mixed varieties costs ₹70k-₹2.5L depending on season and variety selected. June-July peak season offers lowest prices (₹25-35/kg). Negotiate payment terms with 2-3 suppliers to test reliability.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (low cost): Partner with 2-3 existing mango processing micro-units in Jaipur or Bellampalli clusters; provide raw fruit, they process for ₹5-8/kg fee (startup cost ₹0). Option B (medium): Rent a 1,000 sq ft shop with water + electricity in Mancherial town (₹8-15k/month), install basic pulping machine (₹2-4L), hire 2 workers (₹12k/month each). Option C (high control): Lease 2,000 sq ft covered space near APMC with cold storage access (₹20-30k/month), invest ₹8-12L in pulper + packager + labeller + small freezer unit. Total setup: ₹2.5-12L depending on path chosen.
4
Brand & Register
File Udyam (free, 15 min online). Apply for FSSAI-SC license (₹5k, 10 days) via fssai.gov.in—mandatory for any food product. If exporting: Obtain IEC from nearest DGFT office in Hyderabad (₹0, 5 days). Trademark your brand name (₹4.5k, 18 months approval) via ipindiaonline.gov.in. Consider GI registration later if production scales to ₹5Cr+. Total compliance cost: ₹9.5k + 25 days.
5
First Sale
Register on JioMart vendor portal (jiomartb2b.jio.com)—request for Mancherial coverage; upload 50 kg sample with FSSAI cert. First order typically 500-2,000 units (dried mango packs). Expected first-month revenue from digital: ₹1.2-2.5L (if approved). Parallel: Email 10 HORECA procurement officers (ITC Korutla, Taj Gateway Hyderabad) with product samples; target first bulk order of 1-2 tonnes at ₹45/kg = ₹45-90k revenue. Cold email food processors via LinkedIn.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: Hire 1 full-time operations lead (₹18k/month), 2 packers, 1 delivery coordinator. Invest ₹3-5L in walk-in cold storage (-4°C) and small labeller. Apply for PMEGP subsidy (₹10L loan, 35% subsidy for SC/ST/women) for equipment expansion. Exhibit at Hyderabad Food & Hospitality Expo (Nov-Dec) and Bangalore Food Expo to generate B2B leads. Partner with 2-3 logistics providers (Ecom, DTDC) for bulk exports. Formalize farmer supply contracts at fixed prices for 6-12 months.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
₹10 lakh loan with 35% subsidy for SC/ST/women, 25% for general category; covers equipment, working capital, cold storage
🏛
MUDRA Yojana
₹10 lakh unsecured loan for food processing businesses at 8-10% interest; no collateral required, apply at nearest bank
🏚
Telangana Agricultural Infrastructure Scheme
₹10-25 lakh subsidy for food processing unit setup; cold storage, labeller, packaging equipment eligible; apply via district agricultural office
🏚
Telangana MSME Growth Fund
5% interest subsidy on bank loans for food processing MSMEs; upto ₹5 lakh per unit; apply at District MSME office, Mancherial
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
