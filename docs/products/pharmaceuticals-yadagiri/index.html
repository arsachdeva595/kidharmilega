Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Yadagiri ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Yadagiri pharmaceutical clusters and manufacturing facilities in Belgaum-Yadagiri corridor; connect with Karnataka Udyog Association and district MSME office; study FDA/WHO GMP requirements and Indian Pharmacopoeia standards.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source APIs from recognized manufacturers in Hyderabad (Pharmatech Park), Goa, and Mumbai; first batch cost ₹5-15 Lakhs for 100,000 unit production; contact IDMA (Indian Drug Manufacturers Association) for vetted suppliers.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing GMP-certified contract manufacturers in Yadagiri or invest in own facility with WHO GMP certification; require ₹75-150 Cr for brownfield facility, 10-15Cr for contract manufacturing agreement.
4
Brand & Register
Register as Udyam (MSMEs), obtain IEC from DGFT, register with DCGI for drug manufacturing license; file FSSAI certificate for food-related pharma; trademark brand name with IPO; secure ISO 9001:2015 and WHO GMP certification.
5
First Sale
List on 1mg, Pharmeasy, Netmeds as supplier; directly approach Fortis, Apollo, KIMS hospital chains; apply for government tenders via GeM portal; join pharmaceutical e-B2B platforms like Bizongo and PharmaTrace.
6
Scale
Expand product portfolio to specialty drugs; build backward integration for API production; establish distribution network in Tier 2 cities; invest in R&D for WHO-prequalified formulations; pursue SEZ status for export growth.
