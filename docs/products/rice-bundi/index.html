Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bundi ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bundi district's rice clusters around Hindoli and Nainwa villages; meet with local farmers and traders at Bundi Mandi; study soil composition at nearby agricultural extension office; attend ICAR rice variety workshops
2
Source Raw Materials
Contract with 5-10 local farmers in Bundi for paddy supply (₹2-3 lakhs for 50 MT first batch); source packaging from Jaipur packaging clusters (₹50K); establish relationships with rice mills in Bundi town for processing
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with certified rice mills in Bundi (Hindoli or Nainwa) for processing; negotiate milling rate ₹3-5 per kg; establish storage facility (rented warehouse ₹15-20K/month); implement quality testing through NABL-certified labs
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam for agricultural processing; obtain IEC code from Jaipur Export Promotion Council; register brand with Bundi District Industries Centre; apply for organic certification if pursuing premium segment (₹1-1.5L for certification)
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh and Blinkit with premium positioning (₹80-120/kg); sell to Metro, BigBasket via distributor network; activate WhatsApp groups in Tier-1 cities for D2C; engage Rajasthan Cooperative Marketing Federation (RCMF) for institutional sales
6
Scale
Expand farmer contracts to 25+ farmers by month 9; add value-added products (rice flour, basmati blend); establish own small processing unit post-breakeven; target GI certification pursuit if quality metrics stabilize; expand export to 3-4 countries via APEDA assistance
