Yellow Stone Products ki
opportunity, Jaisalmer mein.

Jaisalmer district sits atop naturally abundant yellow limestone and sandstone deposits that have been quarried for centuries. The local geological formation uniquely produces the distinctive golden hue that has built the Jaisalmer Fort and surrounding heritage architecture. Government support for ODOP designation and existing quarrying infrastructure make this district ideal for organized stone product manufacturing and export.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Jaisalmer ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Jaisalmer Fort quarry clusters near Dhorimana and Khuri villages (40km from Jaisalmer town). Connect with district MSME office and existing stone exporters. Study geology at Rajasthan Geological Department office, Jaipur. Attend India Stone & Tiles Exhibition (ISTONE) for market trends.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw blocks from licensed quarries in Khuri (₹500-800/ton for rough blocks). Partner with 2-3 established quarries for captive supply agreements. First batch cost: ₹15-20L for 20-25 tons of quality stone blocks including transport to your processing unit.
3
Produce / Partner
Set up stone cutting and polishing workshop in Jaisalmer town industrial area (₹3-5L rent for 2000 sqft). Hire 5-8 skilled stone craftsmen from local artisan clusters. Alternatively, outsource finishing to established stone cutters on profit-sharing basis. Purchase basic machinery: stone cutter (₹8L), polisher (₹5L), grinding tools.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME classification), obtain IEC code from customs. File brand trademark at IP Office Delhi (₹4500-9000). Get ISO 9001 certification for quality assurance (₹40-60K). Obtain APEDA registration for export eligibility. Create GST registration as manufacturer.
5
First Sale
List on Indiamart and Global Trade Platform for B2B inquiries. Approach 20-30 luxury architects via LinkedIn and direct outreach with sample boxes (₹5K investment). Partner with 3-4 heritage hotel chains for pilot orders. Join Rajasthan ODOP seller portal. Attend IMARC (India Mart) for direct buyer meetings.
6
Scale
After first 6 months, expand to 2 processing units. Hire export-focused sales manager (₹30-40K/month). Launch branded D2C website with Instagram/Pinterest marketing. Approach international distributors in UAE and UK. Apply for PLI or EPCG scheme benefits. Establish processing clusters with 15-20 artisan partnerships.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP
Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme: up to ₹25L subsidy (25-35% of project cost) for manufacturing setup
🏛
Production Linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme for Building and Construction Materials: up to 4-6% cash incentive on incremental sales for 5 years
🏚
Rajasthan MSME Promotion Policy 2022
Capital subsidy up to ₹10L for manufacturing units in heritage sectors
🏚
Rajasthan Export Promotion Policy
Rebates on logistics, certification, and trade fair participation for stone product exporters
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
