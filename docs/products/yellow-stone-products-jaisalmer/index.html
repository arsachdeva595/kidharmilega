Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Jaisalmer ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Jaisalmer stone cluster on Fort Road — spend 2-3 hours with 3-4 existing processors (AB Impex, local cutters). Call Rajasthan MSME helpline (toll-free) to confirm current scheme benefits. Register free on Udyam portal with Aadhar. Zero cost, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Buy first batch of limestone slabs directly from quarry operators in Pokaran (45 km south) or Jaisalmer town outskirts. First batch cost: ₹1.5-2.5L for 40-50 sq meters of raw material. Contact Jaisalmer Quarry Association via district MSME office for vendor list.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 2000-3000 sq ft unit in Jaisalmer MSME cluster (₹8000-12000/month) with water access and 3-phase power. Partner with 2-3 skilled stonemasons (₹400-600/day each) to start cutting/finishing. Invest ₹5-8L in manual/semi-automatic cutting machines (Chinese suppliers on Alibaba or local Jaipur vendors). Production can begin in 30-45 days.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 10 min online). File IEC with DGFT (₹0, online). Apply for MSME loan subsidy under PMEGP (₹25L eligible, 35% subsidy). Consider GI registration filing at Ministry ODOP (currently no GI tag — first applicant gets advantage). Cost: ₹15000-25000 for GI legal filing. Timeline: 4-6 weeks for Udyam + IEC, 6-12 months for GI.
5
First Sale
First sale: List on Architizer or ArchiPro (free listing, 8-10% commission). Target: ₹50-70K first order (10-15 sq meters custom cut tiles). Post 10-15 before-after photos of installations to Facebook and Instagram (DIY). Expected first-month revenue post-listing: ₹80K-150K if you get 2-3 small architect projects.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire a full-time export coordinator (₹25000/month). Attend trade fairs: CBEC (Singapore), Stone+Tile (Las Vegas) or APH Mumbai to directly pitch UK/USA architects. Invest ₹3-5L in a 500-piece showroom display in Jaisalmer city. Automate finishing with CNC or Waterjet (₹25-40L equipment, lease option available). Grow to 5-6 full-time masons.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakhs loan, 35% subsidy for SC/ST/women, 25% for general category; applies to manufacturing setup
🏛
MUDRA Scheme
up to ₹10 lakhs unsecured loan at 10-12% interest; fast approval (15 days), no collateral
🏚
Rajasthan Subsidy Policy 2023
25% capital subsidy on machinery for MSME manufacturers up to ₹50L investment
🏚
One District One Product (ODOP) scheme
branding support, trade fair participation grants, export promotion allowance up to ₹2L annually
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
