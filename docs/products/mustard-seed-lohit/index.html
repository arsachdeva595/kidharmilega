Mustard Seed ki
opportunity, Lohit mein.

Lohit district sits in eastern Arunachal Pradesh along the India-China-Myanmar tri-border region at 1000-2200m elevation, where monsoon climate and alluvial Lohit river soil support 800-1200 tonnes of mustard seed annually. The district's mustard production area covers approximately 3200-4100 hectares spread across Tezu, Namsai, and Chowkham blocks, with farm-gate pricing at ₹45-65/kg depending on variety. Buyer markets are 380km west in Assam (Guwahati spice wholesale at Machkhowa, Sakrapur), 650km east via Moreh to Myanmar, and 480km north via Tawang to Tibet/Nepal; industrial buyers in Europe and Canada account for 45.9% of global market demand.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Lohit ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Tezu Monday haat and Namsai weekly market (₹0 cost); call 3-5 mustard seed farmers around Tezu block via local agricultural office (Lohit District Agriculture Department Ph: 0368-2222606); register free Udyam profile on udyamregistration.gov.in; attend Lohit District ODOP monthly trader meet (ask Lohit NIC office for schedule).
2
Source Raw Materials
Source directly from Tezu, Namsai, Chowkham blocks' primary mustard growers post-harvest (October-November); negotiate ₹50-65/kg for white/yellow, ₹68-85/kg for black mustard depending on grade; first batch 2-5 tonnes = ₹1.2-4.25L outlay; establish relationship with 2-3 farmer cooperatives like Tezu Agricultural Cooperative Union or ask District Agricultural Officer for farmer group contacts.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent 800-1200 sqft unit in Tezu or Namsai industrial area at ₹8-15K/month with basic cleaning, sorting, grading machinery (used equipment ₹1.8-3.2L); hire 2-3 part-time workers ₹6-8K/month. Option B: Partner with 4-6 registered farmer groups for pre-cleaned bulk supply, you handle packaging/branding only (lower capex ₹2.5-4L). Processing cost per kg: ₹8-12 (labor + machine + utilities).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME free on udyamregistration.gov.in (1 day); apply for GI registration for 'Lohit Mustard Seeds' via Ministry of Agriculture via your State Agriculture Department (₹5-8K fee, 8-12 month process, ₹80-1.2L approval grant if approved); obtain IEC code from nearest APEDA-registered export office in Guwahati if targeting exports (₹2K fee, 7 days); fssai Food Licence Category III ₹6-12K; state MSME subsidy for plant/machinery (check Arunachal Pradesh Startup Policy 2022 for ₹10-25L reimbursement).
5
First Sale
Day 1 platform: list on Amazon FBA Pantry, Grofers wholesale, Blink — target first order 200-500kg at ₹280/500g = expected ₹28-56K first month. Day 7: contact 8-10 spice wholesalers in Machkhowa Guwahati via phone + samples, target 1-2 bulk orders 5-10 tonne at ₹98/kg = ₹49-98K. Day 15: reach out to restaurants (Guwahati, Dibrugarh Assam) via direct mail + samples for ₹250-400/kg monthly supply contracts. First month target: ₹80-140K combined across channels.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: move to 2500 sqft facility (₹20K/month rent), add semi-automated grader + mill (₹6-8L), hire quality officer + 4 packers (₹12-18K payroll). Hire export operations manager (₹18-25K) dedicated to Nepal/Bangladesh shipments. Attend India International Spice Trade Fair (annual, Feb-Mar in Cochin or Delhi) for buyer connections. Launch B2B Udaan platform account for bulk food traders. Partner with logistics (Allcargo, Gati) for export container management.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)
up to ₹25L loan at 5% subsidy for manufacturing units; apply via KVIC/bank, 6-month processing
🏛
Ministry of Agriculture's One District One Product (ODOP) scheme
₹5-15L interest-free term loan + ₹80-1.2L grant for GI registration and branding; direct application via District Industries Centre, Lohit
🏚
Arunachal Pradesh Startup Policy 2022
₹10-25L reimbursement for plant & machinery, ₹5L subsidy for MSME registration; apply through Industries Department, Itanagar
🏚
Arunachal Pradesh Agricultural Promotion Scheme
₹1.5L-3L subsidy for agri-processing units in hilly districts; apply via District Agriculture Office, Tezu
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
