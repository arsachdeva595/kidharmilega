Leather Products ki
opportunity, Vellore mein.

Vellore has been a leather manufacturing hub for over 400 years with a well-established ecosystem of skilled artisans, tanneries, and raw material suppliers. The district's geographical proximity to cattle-rearing regions ensures steady supply of high-quality hides. The cluster benefits from generational knowledge of traditional tanning and finishing techniques that are difficult to replicate elsewhere.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Vellore ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Vellore's leather clusters in Pondy Bazaar and Katpadi areas; shadow 2-3 established leather artisans for 2 weeks; study tanning processes at local tanneries; join Vellore Leather Industries Association to access cluster data and market insights
2
Source Raw Materials
Source hides from Vellore's central hides market (₹1,500-2,500 per hide); procure vegetable tanning agents from certified suppliers in Kanpur or local Vellore suppliers (₹15,000-25,000 per quintal); budget ₹3-5 lakhs for 500-unit first production batch including materials and labor
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 skilled artisans in Vellore's Lakshmi Nagar leather cluster (₹8,000-12,000 per artisan monthly); establish a small workspace (1,000 sq ft minimum) for quality control and finishing; implement inventory management for hides and finished goods; source hardware fittings from local suppliers in Pondy Bazaar
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam as micro-enterprise (handicraft); apply for IEC code from Directorate General of Foreign Trade; register brand name and trademark with IP office; obtain GI certification mark usage permission from Ministry of Handicrafts; get health and safety certification for tanning practices
5
First Sale
Launch on Flipkart and Amazon Handmade with focus on GI-tagged positioning; build Instagram presence showcasing artisan stories and process videos; start with 50-100 units on D2C Shopify store; approach luxury lifestyle boutiques in Bangalore, Chennai, Mumbai; pitch B2B orders to corporate gifting platforms like Giftxoxo and CorporateGifts.com
6
Scale
Expand product line (boots, belts, accessories) after validating 2-3 bestsellers; hire 2-3 additional skilled artisans; establish distribution in tier-2 cities through lifestyle retailers; scale exports with direct outreach to buyers in target countries via Alibaba and Global Sources; consider semi-automated finishing processes while maintaining handcraft quality
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakhs loan at subsidized rate for micro-enterprises in handicraft sector
🏛
PM Vishwakarma Yojana
up to ₹3 lakhs collateral-free loan and 5% interest subsidy for skilled artisans and leather craftspeople
🏚
Tamil Nadu Handicraft Development Scheme
subsidy on raw materials and tools for registered handicraft units, up to ₹50,000 per unit
🏚
SMILE (Support for Marginal Individual in Livelihood and Enterprise)
interest-free loans and skill training for artisans in heritage crafts like leather tanning
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
