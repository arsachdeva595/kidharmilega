Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Vellore ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Ranipet leather cluster (20 km from Vellore city) on a Tuesday morning — walk to Ambur Road tannery zone, call 3-4 established unit owners (names on VEPPCB registered list), ask about their raw material sources and daily production. Register free Udyam on https://udyamregistration.gov.in same week. Cost: ₹0, 6 hours.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw hides from Ranipet suppliers (Ramnath Hides, VR Leathers, Vishal Traders) at ₹80-120/kg. First batch: 50 kg hides = ₹5000-6000. For tanning chemicals, contact Sree Chemicals Vellore or Chennai suppliers (vegetable tanning ₹200-300/kg, chrome ₹150/kg). First-batch chemicals + dyes ₹8000-12000. Total raw material for 100 finished handbags: ₹18000-22000.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 500 sq ft workshop in Ranipet leather zone at ₹5000-7000/month (shared workspace available). Partnership option: hire 2-3 local artisans (₹300-400/day each) to handle tanning, dyeing, cutting, stitching. Setup: basic leather cutting tools ₹8000, hand-stitching station ₹12000, drying racks ₹5000. Monthly production capacity: 300-400 units. OR outsource tanning/dyeing to existing tanneries (₹40-60 per hide processed) and do finishing in-house. Total monthly operating cost: ₹25000-35000 (rent + labor + materials for 300 units).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, ₹0, 3 days). GI-registered product — add 'Made under GI Vellore Leather' on labels. File IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) at nearest DGFT office (₹0, 5 days) if exporting. Trademark brand name at IPO (₹9000, 8-12 weeks). Certifications: ISI/FSSAI optional but helpful for premium positioning (₹15000-25000). Total first-year compliance: ₹30000-40000.
5
First Sale
Launch 10-SKU collection on Amazon Seller Central (30% commission) and Flipkart (25-30% commission) with professional photos (hire local studio ₹3000-5000 for 50 images). First-month target: 40-60 units across platforms at ₹3000-5000 ASP = ₹1.2-3L gross revenue, ₹35000-75000 net after commissions and material cost. First B2B wholesale order: approach Reliance Brands, Westside buyers directly with 5-unit sample kit (₹12000 cost). Expected: 1 order of 100-200 units at 40% margin within 6-8 weeks.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 2nd shift (4-6 artisans, ₹1.2L/month labor), upgrade to 1000 sq ft dedicated unit (₹12000/month), add 4-5 new SKU categories (shoes, belts, jackets). Exhibit at trade shows: IITF Delhi (₹60000 booth cost + materials), Craft Mela Bangalore (₹30000), GTSE Chennai (₹40000). Hire 1 logistics/export manager (₹30000/month). Aim for export orders: target 1-2 containers (15-20K units/year) to US/EU at ₹2-3L/month revenue stream by month 16.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
up to ₹15000 toolkit subsidy + ₹5L collateral-free MUDRA loan for registered artisans, 0% interest for first 6 months
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme)
35% subsidy on capital investment (max ₹25L for manufacturing unit), loan through banks at concessional rates
🏚
Tamil Nadu Artisan Welfare Board
₹50000 raw material subsidy + training subsidy up to ₹20000 for registered craftspeople, apply at District Industries Centre
🏚
TN MSME Growth Scheme
5% interest subsidy on SIDBI loans + 5% machinery subsidy (max ₹10L), applicable for units with ₹25-100L turnover
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Seedhe sawaal, seedhe jawab

Minimum ₹2.5-3L: raw materials ₹20K, workshop rental 3 months advance ₹20K, basic tools ₹15K, IEC + Udyam ₹5K, contingency ₹1.5L. Maximum ₹12-15L if buying tanning equipment + modern machinery. Most founders start at ₹4-6L with outsourced tanning (lower capex, higher outsourcing cost).
You don't need 10 years experience, but 4-6 weeks hands-on training with Ranipet artisans is non-negotiable. Learn hide grading, tanning chemistry basics, dyeing consistency, finishing quality. Many successful founders here started as first-time entrepreneurs but invested 2-3 months learning on-cluster before launching full production.
PM Vishwakarma: ₹15K toolkit + ₹5L zero-interest loan. PMEGP: 35% subsidy (max ₹25L investment). TN Artisan Welfare: ₹50K raw material subsidy + ₹20K training. TN MSME Growth: 5% interest subsidy on loans. GI tag (already awarded 2009) = no new registration cost, just add to branding. Total available support: ₹90K-₹25L depending on scheme eligibility.
3 routes: (1) Direct D2C via Amazon/Flipkart seller setup (7-10 days, first sale within 3-4 weeks if inventory ₹2-3L); (2) B2B wholesale — call Westside, Reliance Brands, local fashion retailers with sample kit (₹12K cost, 50% close rate within 8 weeks); (3) Export — email 20-30 USA/EU importers on Alibaba/TradeKey with catalog (₹0 cost, 30-40 day lead time for first order).
Haan, high export potential. Register IEC (₹0, 5 days), get APEDA membership (₹500), prepare product certifications (ISI optional, costs ₹15-25K). USA, Germany, UK, France, Italy buy Indian leather goods. FOB margin: 38-52%. Minimum export order: 500-1000 units. Lead time: 30-45 days for container shipment via Chennai port. Logistics cost: ₹8-12/unit for sea freight on 5000-unit containers.
Honest downside: if you can't hit 200 units/month within 6 months OR wholesale orders dry up, monthly losses are ₹15-25K (rent + labor). Exit options: (1) Pivot to contract manufacturing for larger brands (lower brand risk, steady 10-15% margin); (2) Sell equipment + inventory at 40-60% loss (recover ₹1-2L on ₹4-6L investment); (3) Join another leather unit as operations manager (salary ₹25-35K/month). Biggest risk: supply chain disruption or quality issues = brand damage.
