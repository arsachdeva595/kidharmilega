Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Darrang ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Darrang vegetable clusters in Dalgaon, Mangaldai, and Sorbhog areas; meet 5-10 vegetable farmers at Guwahati's Lokhra vegetable market; study seasonal patterns and local production cycles over 4-6 weeks.
2
Source Raw Materials
Partner with local farming cooperatives or individual farmers in Darrang; source 2-5 acres of farmland (₹5-15L lease/purchase) or negotiate contract farming arrangements; initial seed and fertilizer cost ₹2-5L for first season.
3
Produce / Partner
Register with NABARD for farmer producer organization (FPO) support; adopt integrated pest management practices; partner with 20-50 local farmers through contract farming at fixed rates (₹3-7L monthly supply); invest in cold storage unit (₹30-50L) for post-harvest value addition.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME with vegetables category; apply for IEC code through nearest customs office in Guwahati; obtain Assam Agricultural Produce Market Committee (APMC) license; conduct organic certification through NPOP if targeting premium segment (₹1-2L).
5
First Sale
Launch on BigBasket and Blinkit with direct supply agreements; approach restaurant chains (Barbeque Nation, The Dhaba, local Assamese chains) with samples; sell bulk to Guwahati's Lokhra and Fancy Bazaar wholesale markets; create WhatsApp community group for 100-200 regular household customers.
6
Scale
Establish 3-5 collection centers across Darrang; integrate with FPO network for certified organic positioning; expand to 10+ cities across Northeast through cold chain partnerships; launch private label for retail chains; introduce value-added products (dried herbs, vegetable powders, pickled vegetables).
