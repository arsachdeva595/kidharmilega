Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sangli ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sangli turmeric market clusters (Miraj, Vita, Sangli town) and connect with 5-10 established traders like Turmeric Export Association members; attend Sangli Spice Festival (Feb-Mar) for market insights and farmer contacts
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw turmeric rhizomes directly from Sangli farmers through mandis or cooperative societies at ₹4,500-5,500/quintal; first batch of 50 quintals costs ₹2.25-2.75L; establish relationships with 3-4 farmer collectives for consistent supply
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established turmeric processors in Sangli (processing cost ₹800-1,200/quintal for drying + grinding) or invest in small-scale processing unit (₹15-25L for 5-ton/day capacity); obtain Food Safety License from Sangli Municipal Corporation
4
Brand & Register
Register as Udyam (MSE portal) for ₹0, apply for IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Pune/Bangalore (₹500), register brand name with Indian IP office, obtain FPO certification from APEDA for export eligibility, and get organic certification from accredited bodies (₹1-2L for first year)
5
First Sale
List on IndiaMART, TradeKey, and Global Sources for B2B; launch D2C on Amazon, Flipkart, and Blinkit with ₹50K monthly ad spend; approach regional spice traders in Delhi APMC, Chennai, and Mumbai wholesale markets with sample offerings
6
Scale
Expand product range (turmeric supplements, masks, teas) after 6 months; target 100+ retail partnerships via distribution networks; scale exports by 40% YoY through partnerships with APEDA-registered exporters and cold chain logistics
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Program)
up to ₹25L subsidy for agro-processing unit in rural Sangli with 25-35% margin
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
cluster development and common processing facility support up to ₹50L per cluster in Sangli
🏚
Maharashtra Agro Export Promotion Scheme
5% subsidy on export value for agricultural products with GI tag to international markets
🏚
MSTC Krushi Udyog Scheme
₹5-10L concessional loans for agro-based industries with 7% interest subsidy for first 3 years
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
