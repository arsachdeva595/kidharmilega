Muzaffarnagar's sugarcane cultivation spans over 300,000 hectares with ideal soil composition and water availability from the Yamuna-Ganges belt. The district has a 200+ year tradition of jaggery production with established artisan networks and cold-storage infrastructure. Local farmers produce high-sucrose cane varieties that yield premium-grade jaggery with superior color and taste consistency.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Muzaffarnagar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Muzaffarnagar Jaggery Cluster (Mandi Road, Khatauli block) to shadow 3-5 established producers; attend UP Jaggery Association quarterly meetings; study GI certification requirements via NCBI Pune's database.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source sugarcane directly from Muzaffarnagar farmers cooperative (Kisan Seva Kendra, Jansath); negotiate rates at ₹350-450/quintal (seasonal); budget ₹3-4 lakhs for first 100 quintal batch; partner with established jaggery units initially for processing.
3
Produce / Partner
Collaborate with SFURTI clusters in Muzaffarnagar (FSSAI-registered co-operative units) for first-year production; alternatively, lease processing units in Khatauli or Sarai Nagar; invest ₹20-30 lakhs for own 5-ton/day capacity boiler and filtration setup.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Ministry portal); apply for IEC code via DGFT; obtain FSSAI license (₹10k); file GI usage application with Muzaffarnagar Jaggery Association; register brand at district office; design packaging with nutritional/GI mark compliance.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh, Blinkit, BigBasket with ₹500-750/kg premium positioning; approach Natural Produce (Noida-based bulk buyer), Patanjali supply chain; pitch to ayurvedic e-commerce (Jiva, Organic Tatva); attend IITF Delhi food expo (Nov) for B2B buyer connections.
6
Scale
Achieve ₹1.5Cr turnover by month 12-14, then open exclusive brand retail via franchise model in Delhi-NCR metros; expand export via trade body certifications (APEDA); develop value-added products (jaggery powder, jaggery blocks); build direct-to-consumer subscription boxes.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy for manufacturing units; 25-35% capital subsidy eligibility for food processing in Muzaffarnagar.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹50+ lakhs cluster development grants; common facility center support for jaggery production in Muzaffarnagar cluster.
🏚
UP Agroprocessing Cluster Scheme
50% subsidy on machinery and equipment for food processing ODOP units; priority processing for GI-tagged products.
🏚
UP Laghu Udyog Vikas Nidhi
Interest subsidy (4-5%) on loans up to ₹10 lakhs for registered jaggery producers in ODOP districts.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
