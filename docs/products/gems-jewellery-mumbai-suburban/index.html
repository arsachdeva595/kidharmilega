Mumbai Suburban sits within India's only Tier-1 gems and jewellery manufacturing corridor, home to 3000+ organized retailers and wholesale dealers concentrated in Vile Parle, Fort, and Bandra-Kurla Complex. Raw material flows directly through Mumbai port — loose diamonds, gemstones, and lab-grown diamonds imported daily. The district feeds both the ₹85 billion domestic market and global export pipelines to US, UAE, Italy, and European countries with zero historical tariffs on polished diamonds to the US.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Mumbai Suburban ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Vile Parle Jewellery Market (free walk, 3 hours) and Bandra Fort jewelry district on a weekday; call 5 loose diamond suppliers in Fort area for pricing on 0.5-2 carat stones; register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in). Do this this week—zero cost.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source loose diamonds from Fort or Bandra wholesale dealers (₹50K-₹5L minimum order); source gold from bullion dealers in Fort (spot rate + 1-2% markup). First sample batch: ₹2-3L for 5-10 pieces. Establish relationship with hallmarking center in Vile Parle or Fort for BIS certification.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 300-500 sqft jewellery workshop in Vile Parle or Fort (₹25-40K/month shared, ₹50-70K private) OR partner with 2-3 independent master craftsmen on profit-share (30-40% of piece margin). Buy basic tools (soldering, polishing, stone setting): ₹80-150K. Design software training (2-3 weeks, online): ₹5-10K. First-month operational cost: ₹150-200K.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 15 mins online). Apply for BIS Hallmarking (₹2-5K for initial certification, 3-4 weeks). Get IEC code from DGFT for export (₹0, online, 5 days). Open MSME Udyam-linked bank account (free). Optional: file GI registration support application with MSME Ministry (process takes 4-6 months, supports branding but not mandatory).
5
First Sale
Post 10-15 pieces on Instagram with WhatsApp CTA; list 3 pieces on Amazon Handmade and Etsy. Target first order: bulk custom order from wedding planner or corporate client (₹50-100K order value, ₹150-250K profit potential). First-month realistic revenue: ₹100-150K if 1-2 custom orders close.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 designer + 1 salesperson; lease dedicated 600 sqft unit; establish direct supply contracts with 15+ retail chains; explore export to Thailand/UAE via direct B2B leads. Exhibit at IIJW (Indian International Jewellery Week, annual, Mumbai) and local trade fairs. Build brand via Instagram Reels on custom designs and client transformations.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh loan with 25% subsidy (₹6.25L) for manufacturing; 5-year tenure.
🏛
MUDRA Loan
up to ₹10 lakh at 8-9% interest, no collateral, 5-year repayment for registered MSME.
🏚
Maharashtra MSME Support Scheme
5% interest subsidy on loans above ₹1 crore; applicable to registered units.
🏚
Maharashtra ODOP Scheme
Support for One District One Product certification and branding; eligibility-based subsidy for GI registration and export preparation.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
