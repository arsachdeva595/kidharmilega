Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Srinagar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Zadibal/Rainawari carpet clusters in Srinagar (free, 2-3 hours). Call 5 artisan cooperatives directly (get numbers from Srinagar DC Handicraft office or District ODOP nodal officer). Attend Srinagar Haat (weekly craft fair at Hazratbal) to see active sellers and customer demand live. Zero cost, doable this month.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw silk from Kashmir Sericulture Development Board farms in Srinagar/Budgam district (₹400-600/kg pure mulberry silk; 100kg min first batch = ₹40-60K). Cotton warp/weft from registered suppliers in Srinagar textile markets (Lal Chowk area; ₹80-120/kg; 50kg = ₹4-6K). Total raw material for 5 sq.meter carpets (200 knots/sq.inch density) = ₹70-90K. Negotiate artisan labor separately (see step 3).
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (Artisan Partnership): Contract 2-3 registered carpet weavers from Zadibal cluster to produce on commission (₹500-1,200 per sq.meter labor cost depending on knot density; 5 sq.meters = ₹2.5-6K labor). Provide raw materials; they deliver finished carpet in 40-60 days. Total first batch: ₹80-100K (materials + labor). Option B (Rent Loom Unit): Rent a 400 sq.ft carpentry/loom space in Srinagar industrial area (₹8-12K/month). Hire 2-3 part-time weavers (₹12-15K/month each). First production batch (3-5 carpets) takes 60-90 days. Total monthly overhead: ₹25-35K. Recommend Option A for first 3-6 months (lower risk, faster learning).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 15 mins, udyamregistration.gov.in). Apply for GI-protected label certification through Srinagar DC Handicraft office (₹2-5K fee, 30-day processing, certifies you as authorized Kashmir Handknotted Carpet maker). Obtain IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) from DGFT if planning export (free registration, 7 days, dgft.gov.in). Total cost: ₹3-5K. Timeline: 6 weeks. This unlocks access to export subsidies and GI-authenticated pricing.
5
First Sale
First sales via Instagram (post carpet-making process videos + finished product photos; tag luxury interior accounts; target 500-1K followers in 30 days). Simultaneously upload to Etsy/eBay (higher international reach, 3.5% commission). First order expectation: 1-2 sq.meter custom carpet from interior designer or overseas client via DM/Etsy inquiry within 60 days. Order value: ₹15-30K. Expected first-month revenue: ₹15-40K (if 1-2 orders land).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (6-8 active artisans producing 15-20 sq.meters/month): hire 1 full-time designer (₹25-30K/month) to handle custom orders and client consultations. Establish wholesale relationships with 2-3 interior design studios in Delhi/Mumbai (visit in person, offer 35-40% trade discount, target ₹50K+ bulk orders). Apply for PMEGP subsidy for ₹10-15L expansion (25-35% grant if sanctioned). Exhibit at Dilli Haat (Delhi), Sambhavi (Mumbai), or Craft Biennale (biennial, ₹5-10K booth cost) to secure direct HNI and institutional buyers. Move to rented 800 sq.ft workshop (₹15-20K/month) by month 8-10 to increase capacity.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy (25% for general, 35% for SC/ST/women); covers machinery, raw materials, working capital.
🏛
Pradhan Mantri Vishwakarma Scheme
₹15,000 credit limit + ₹500 annual cash gift + free digital marketing training; fast-track for traditional artisans and carpet makers.
🏚
Jammu & Kashmir Handicraft Development Scheme
₹2-5 lakh subsidy for artisan clusters; GI product makers get priority. Apply via DC Handicraft office.
🏚
J&K Export Promotion Scheme
5-10% reimbursement on export freight and certification costs for GI-protected products; valid 12 months from registration.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
