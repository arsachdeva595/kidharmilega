Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — South ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Apollo Hospitals and Fortis Healthcare patient coordination departments this week (free, walk-in); call 3 medical tourism agents already operating from South Delhi (Justdial search 'medical tourism coordinator Delhi'); sit with 2-3 religious tour guides at Lotus Temple visitor center to understand current tourist flow and gaps
2
Source Raw Materials
Network with hospital patient relations teams (Apollo, Fortis, Narayana) to understand their patient sourcing gaps; source logistics partner near Aerocity hotel cluster; contact 5-8 accredited recovery homes in Chhatarpur/Greater Kailash for partnership framework; first-batch coordination cost ₹1-2L (website, visa assistance, accommodation booking systems)
3
Produce / Partner
Start as a B2B coordinator role partnering with 2-3 hospitals rather than building medical facility yourself (capital-light model); rent small office in Aerocity or Connaught Place (₹40-80K/month) as patient reception/coordination hub; hire 1 multilingual coordinator (₹20-30K/month) and 1 logistics manager; establish partnerships with 3-5 recovery homes and transport providers; cost range ₹8-15L for first year operations (office, staff, tech infrastructure)
4
Brand & Register
Register as Udyam (MSME) — free online, process takes 24 hours; apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) via DGFT portal (₹0, 2-week processing) to legally export medical tourism packages; obtain GST registration (₹0, online); create ISO 9001 quality certification for medical tourism coordination (₹30-50K, 2-3 months); build NABH-aligned documentation for hospital credibility
5
First Sale
First sale via direct outreach to medical travel agencies in Dubai, Doha, Lagos (LinkedIn + email campaigns); launch on Healthgrades, Zocdoc international listings (free + paid tiers); secure first patient via hospital referral (typical first-month revenue ₹3-8L from 2-3 coordinated cases at ₹1-3L per package); build WhatsApp community of past patients for referrals
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire dedicated country managers for Africa (₹30K), Middle East (₹30K), Southeast Asia (₹25K); partner with 8-10 hospitals instead of 3; establish 24/7 patient support center; invest in CRM software (₹20K/month) and multilingual chatbot (₹10K/month); exhibit at IHMEC (International Healthcare Management & Events Conference) and IMTJ Medical Tourism Summit; build ancillary revenue via corporate wellness contracts (₹50L+ annual potential)

Government Schemes

PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L term loan at 5-6% interest for healthcare services, 15-35% subsidy on equipment
🏛
MUDRA Yojana
unsecured loans up to ₹50L for service businesses at 8-10% interest, zero collateral
🏚
Delhi Healthcare Services Scheme
priority registration for medical tourism operators, preferential hospital tie-up rates, ₹5-10L interest subsidy for first 3 years
🏚
Delhi Tourism Development Scheme
grants up to ₹20L for tourism infrastructure, waived VAT on services for first 5 years if registered as approved medical tourism operator
