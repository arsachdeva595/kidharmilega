Suitable For

First-gen founder with ₹8.5-45L capital, supply chain background or dairy farm family, committed to 12-18 month runway. Ideal if you can source milk directly from farmer collectives or partner with existing producer organisations.

Not for: Founders without access to reliable milk supply, or those seeking quick (sub-6 month) returns. Capital-light models don't work in dairy — you need processing infrastructure. Skip if you can't handle perishable inventory and daily operations.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bhiwani ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call 2-3 of the 23 milk producer organisations (list available at Bhiwani Dairy Cooperative) and ask their sourcing model, milk price, quality checks — zero cost. Visit a Mother Dairy collection center in Bhiwani town to observe the pickup-to-processing-to-distribution cycle. Attend Bhiwani Haat (weekly market) to see what paneer/ghee units sell at retail.
2
Source Raw Materials
Partner with one milk producer organisation for steady supply — typically ₹24-28/litre depending on season. Minimum first-month commitment: 10,000 litres (~₹2.5-2.8L). Organisations are concentrated around Bhiwani city and Tosham. Negotiate payment terms (usually 3-5 days post-delivery). Alternative: source packaged skimmed milk powder (₹180-220/kg) and oils from wholesalers in Delhi's Azadpur Market for powder-based products.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (₹15-25L): Rent 2000-3000 sq ft unit in Bhiwani MSME cluster, install 100-150L batch pasteurizer (₹8-12L), packaging line, cold storage (₹12-18L). Option B (₹3-5L): Partner with local paneer/ghee artisans on revenue-share (40-50% you handle sales, they handle production). Option A requires FSSAI license (₹20k, 30 days); Option B piggybacks on their existing license (10-day setup).
4
Brand & Register
Register on Udyam (free, 15 min online). Apply for FSSAI registration (₹20,000, 30 days processing). If exporting, file IEC through NEFT (₹2000, 7-10 days). Skip GI registration (Bhiwani milk has no GI status, and process is ₹3-5L + 2-3 years). Hire a CA for FSS compliance documentation (₹3-5k/month).
5
First Sale
Day 1-7: Register on Blinkit/Zepto merchant portal (free, 5 days approval). Upload 3-5 SKUs (milk 500ml, paneer 250g, ghee 100ml). Day 7-21: Contact 10-15 kiryana stores in your delivery radius, offer ₹1-2 margin per unit. First month target: 50L milk/day D2C + 200L/day wholesale = ₹35-40k/month gross. Focus on morning milk slots (5-8am) where conversion is highest.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 2 daily-route managers for wholesale collections, 1 QC person for batch testing. Expand to 2-3 additional producer organisation partnerships. At ₹25L/month: add a secondary SKU (paneer or ghee), apply for PMEGP subsidy (₹25L max) to fund second processing unit in adjacent tehsil. At ₹50L+: build own collection points in 3-4 tehsils, explore Udyam SFURTI cluster support (₹10L infrastructure grant), target export licensing. Exhibit at Delhi Food Expo (Oct), Pragati Maidan dairy trade shows.

Government Schemes

up to ₹25,00,000 subsidy (25% of project cost for general category, 35% for SC/ST/OBC)
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹10,00,000 for dairy cluster development, includes working capital support

priority allocation of government procurement tenders, GSCL (Ghee & Spice Commission Ltd) buy-back guarantee for ghee at MSP+5%

5% interest subsidy on bank loans up to ₹50 lakhs for processing units registered in dairy cluster zones
