Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Lunglei ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Lunglei fruit market (nearest to district headquarters) on a Saturday and speak directly with 3-5 banana traders. Call Baghel Agro Industries at the ODOP helpline 8787329670 to understand their sourcing, pricing, and supplier network. Attend the local agricultural extension office at Lunglei to get a list of farmer groups and cooperatives. All zero-cost, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from registered farmer groups in Lunglei district (ask extension office for contact list) or approach existing suppliers via ODOP network. First batch cost: ₹80,000-150,000 for 5-10 tons. Supplier concentration: Lunglei town and surrounding agricultural zones within 30 km radius. Negotiate on volume pricing (₹30-45/kg at farm gate) and establish cold-chain logistics.
3
Produce / Partner
Start as an aggregator-processor rather than grower: rent a small 500-800 sq ft unit (₹3,000-5,000/month) in Lunglei town or nearby industrial area for washing, grading, packaging, dried banana production. Alternatively, partner with 5-8 local farmers for direct procurement under an informal supply agreement (no rent needed, but require 2-3L working capital). Processing setup cost: ₹3-6L (grading tables, dryers, packaging equipment, cold storage).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (free, 10 minutes) via udyamregistration.gov.in. Apply for organic certification via IFOAM or India Organic certification body (₹15,000-30,000, 3-6 month timeline). If exporting, file IEC application at Customs (₹0, online) via icegate.indiagateway.org. Get FSSAI food safety license (₹500-2,000, 2 weeks). District ODOP helpline 8787329670 can guide on all registrations.
5
First Sale
First sale: register on BigBasket seller dashboard or Blinkit vendor portal (free). Create product listings for fresh bananas (₹120/kg) and dried banana chips (₹180/250g). Launch with 500 kg fresh banana stock and 100 kg dried products. Expected first-month revenue: ₹40,000-70,000 (low volume, high margin). Parallel: contact 2-3 supermarket chains' procurement teams directly with samples and pricing.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1-2 operations staff, upgrade to 1,500 sq ft production unit, invest ₹2-3L in additional cold storage and drying capacity. Attend food trade fairs (AAHAR Delhi, National Agricultural Fair) for B2B connections. Set up export module: get health certificate from district agricultural office, partner with an export consolidator in Assam (nearby logistics hub). Reach out to regional supermarket chains for bulk supply agreements.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25% subsidy for manufacturing units up to ₹25L investment; eligible for banana processing and dried fruit production
🏛
MUDRA Scheme
₹50,000-10,000,000 loan at 5-6% interest rate with no collateral for small food production businesses; processing units qualify
🏚
Mizoram Agriculture & Horticulture Policy 2022
30% subsidy on farm machinery, cold storage equipment; applicable to registered agricultural processors
🏚
Mizoram ODOP Scheme
dedicated helpline support, brand development assistance, export facilitation for registered ODOP entrepreneurs (contact 8787329670)
