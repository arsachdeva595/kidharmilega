Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Fatehabad ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Fatehabad cotton mandi (main APMC yard) and connect with traders at Satnali, Tohana wholesale markets. Study Haryana State Agricultural Department reports; interview 10+ cotton farmers in villages like Patiala, Kanpur Block. Understand ginning process at local cotton gin mills.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from 50-100 farmer groups in Fatehabad district or contract directly with farmers during harvest (Sept-Dec). First batch cost: ₹15-25 Lakhs for 50 quintals raw cotton; verify quality at ICAR testing labs. Use APMC license for mandi purchases.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing cotton ginning mills in Fatehabad (e.g., units in Satnali, Bhalsmand areas) or set up small-scale cotton gin unit (₹20-30L capex). For value-added: arrange tie-ups with seed extraction units or oilmills. Start with 20-30 quintals monthly.
4
Brand & Register
Register as Udyam (MSE) through https://udyamregistration.gov.in. Obtain IEC code from ICCW Hisar for export eligibility. Get FCI/APEDA registration for commodity trading. Register under Cotton Ginning Act; obtain state GST registration.
5
First Sale
List on Udaan (B2B agri-platform) and IndiaCore (commodity exchange). Approach textile mills in Tiruppur, Coimbatore directly via email with certificates. Join FIEO (Federation of Indian Export Organisations) for export leads. Sell via local APMC regulated channel; attend AgriExpo events in Delhi/Mumbai.
6
Scale
Expand farmer base to 500+ by month 6; add value-added processing (cottonseed oil, cotton meal). Export via APEDA to 2-3 countries minimum by month 9. Build brand on DirectB2B and JoinFarmer; secure a 5-10 acre cluster nearby for contract farming model by month 12.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 Lakhs for cotton gin setup with 35% subsidy for general entrepreneurs
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme for Promotion of Innovation & Entrepreneurship in Textile & Clothing)
grants up to ₹1 Cr for agri-textile value-add clusters in Haryana
🏚
Haryana Agri-Export Promotion Scheme
reimbursement of 50% export promotion costs, certification, and transport subsidy for agricultural commodities
🏚
Haryana Farmer Aggregator Scheme
support for FPOs and contract farming networks with bank credit linkage and input subsidy up to 25%
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
