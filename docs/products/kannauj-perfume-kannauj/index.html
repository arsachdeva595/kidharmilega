Kannauj Perfume ki
opportunity, Kannauj mein.

Kannauj has been India's perfume hub for over 500 years, with a legacy of skilled artisans, established supply chains for rare botanical ingredients, and deeply rooted knowledge systems. The region's climate, water quality, and proximity to flower-growing regions make it uniquely suited for distillation and attar crafting. Government infrastructure, training centers, and existing export networks provide immediate market access.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kannauj ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kannauj's Perfume Cluster (Jajmau area) and connect with existing attar makers; spend 2-4 weeks learning distillation from established workshops; study GI tag requirements and export certification norms from Kannauj ODOP office.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fresh flowers from local Kannauj growers (Rose: ₹40-60/kg, Jasmine: ₹80-120/kg); buy essential oils and base materials from Bahadurganj wholesalers in Kannauj or Mumbai's Girgaum Road; first batch production cost ₹2-5L for 500ml-1L premium attar.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing master artisans in Jajmau for co-production initially (revenue-sharing model); or set up small copper still equipment (₹1-2L) and learn distillation from local mentors; scale to own production after 6-12 months of partnership.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam through udyamregistration.gov.in (MSME status); apply for IEC code via ECIC online portal; register brand name with IP office; obtain FBO license from local Kannauj Municipal Corporation; get fragrance certification from SCCPL or similar testing lab.
5
First Sale
Launch on Nykaa Indie, Amazon Handmade, Etsy targeting international buyers; reach out to 20-30 luxury boutiques and wellness stores via email + WhatsApp samples; approach corporate gifting platforms (Giftawala, CorporateGifting); list on Kannauj ODOP marketplace and government e-commerce portals.
6
Scale
Hire and train 2-3 additional artisans from Jajmau cluster; expand product range with seasonal fragrances and blends; establish distributor network in 3-4 major metros (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore); secure export orders through trade shows (IITF, PhandanExpo) and international B2B platforms; invest in branded packaging and storytelling marketing.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy + bank loan @ 5% interest for MSME manufacturing units in Kannauj.
🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹15K credit limit and skills training for artisans involved in traditional attar production.
🏚
UP ODOP Scheme
technology upgrades, packaging support, export promotion grants, and cluster development for GI-tagged Kannauj Perfume.
🏚
UP Rojgar Sangathan Niyam
subsidized loans up to ₹10L for small manufacturing units and skilled artisans in heritage clusters.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
