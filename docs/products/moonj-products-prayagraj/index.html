Moonj products ki
opportunity, Prayagraj mein.

Prayagraj district (5,105 km² population) sits on Indo-Gangetic plains where sarpat grass grows wildly, especially in Naini area, providing free raw material year-round. Two established clusters (Naini and Mahewa) house hundreds of trained artisans, mostly women, with 60-70 years of craft heritage. Export-ready infrastructure exists: Amazon/Alibaba sellers already active, national buyers (home décor chains, hospitality) established, and planned Moonj Bank in Naini will centralise supply chain.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Prayagraj ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Naini cluster (10 km from Prayagraj city centre) on any weekday — meet 3-4 moonj artisans, observe production process. Call Prayagraj ODOP office (publicly listed) to get list of registered women self-help groups. Check Amazon.in for existing moonj sellers and reverse-engineer their pricing, volume, reviews. Attend Prayagraj Haat or local artisan mela if running — cost zero, insights invaluable.
2
Source Raw Materials
Raw material: Contact Naini artisans or Moonj Bank (under setup) for bulk sarpat grass, kaasa, dyed grass at ₹200-400/kg. Plastic strips + tinsel from Prayagraj city wholesale markets (Hanuman Mandir area). First batch (50 baskets + 20 mats): ₹15,000-25,000. Supplier area: Naini (moonj), Prayagraj main bazaar (accessories).
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (Partnership): Contract 5-8 artisans from Naini/Mahewa self-help groups, give them raw material + ₹150-300/piece, handle branding/selling. Initial artisan training: ₹5K-10K. Option B (In-house): Rent small 300-400 sq ft unit in Naini cluster (₹3-5K/month) or near Prayagraj city, learn craft basics (2-4 weeks), hire 2-3 trained artisans at ₹8-12K/month. Setup cost range: ₹30K-150K (tools, workspace, initial raw material).
4
Brand & Register
Register UDYAM (MSME) online in 15 minutes, free. Apply for GI certification recognition support (ODOP office provides guidance, eligibility already met since GI awarded 2009). If exporting: Get IEC code from nearest DGFT office (Prayagraj has one), cost ₹100, same-day issuance. Register on Udyam, apply for PMEGP subsidy (15-35% subsidy for women entrepreneurs). Timeline: 6-8 weeks for Udyam + IEC; GI support is recognition only (no new cost).
5
First Sale
Week 1-2: Photograph 20-30 products on white background (phone camera OK). Upload 15 baskets/mats to Amazon Handmade (free listing), price ₹400-800 per basket (benchmark existing sellers). Week 2-3: Post on Instagram 5x/week (WhatsApp videos of artisans working, close-ups of details) — tag #EcoHomeDecor #MoonjCraft #PrayagrajArtisan. First order likely from Amazon or direct Instagram DM. Expected first-month revenue: ₹8K-20K (conservative; 10-25 units @ ₹800-1200).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: Hire 1 person for admin/shipping. Partner with 15-20 artisans (contract basis). Attend GiftMa Trade Fair (Mumbai, Feb), Heimtextil (Frankfurt, Jan) or Craft India exhibitions to reach bulk B2B buyers. Invest ₹50K in product photography/catalog. Apply for SFURTI subsidy (up to ₹5L for cluster development) if planning to formalise artisan cooperative. Stock Alibaba + direct buyer platform (TradeKey). Consider hiring export consultant (₹20K-30K one-time) to manage FOB orders.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
15-35% subsidy on setup cost (women get higher %) for MSME registration, applies to moonj craft
🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹15,000 credit + skill training + toolkit for registered artisans, up to 50 artisans can be enrolled under founder's enterprise
🏚
ODOP (One District One Product) Scheme
Non-dilutable support: raw material subsidy, bulk purchase guarantees from govt, export facilitation, free participation in state trade fairs
🏚
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹5 Lakhs for cluster-level infrastructure, cooperative setup, common facility centre establishment in Prayagraj
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
