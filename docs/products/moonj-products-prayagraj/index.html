Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Prayagraj ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Prayagraj's moonj clusters in Chhatnag and Koraon blocks; meet 3-5 established moonj cooperatives (Prayagraj Moonj Shilp Vikas Samiti); attend one trade fair in Delhi or Mumbai to understand market demand and pricing benchmarks.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source moonj grass directly from farmer collectives in Prayagraj districts (first batch ₹50,000-75,000 for 500kg); purchase dyes from Allahabad chemical markets; establish direct relationships with 2-3 weaving units for sample production.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 5-10 registered moonj artisans in Prayagraj via formal MOU; invest ₹300,000-500,000 in shared workspace, basic tools, and quality control setup; begin with limited SKU (6-8 core products) before expanding.
4
Brand & Register
Register for Udyam MSME classification; obtain IEC code from Allahabad customs; register Prayagraj Moonj GI certification in brand name; create packaging with GI mark; file trademark with IP office.
5
First Sale
Launch on Flipkart, Amazon Handmade, and Etsy (international reach); approach 5-10 premium home décor retailers in Delhi and Mumbai; execute Instagram/Pinterest content strategy with artisan storytelling; secure placement in 1-2 eco-luxury hotel chains.
6
Scale
Hire marketing and operations manager; expand to 15-20 artisan partners; develop sub-categories (lampshades, wall art); establish direct export relationships with 3+ international distributors; launch corporate gifting vertical; consider pop-up stores in Tier 1 cities.

PM Vishwakarma
up to ₹15,000 toolkit + ₹5L credit at 0% interest for registered artisans and MSMEs in traditional handicrafts
🏛
PMEGP
up to 35% subsidy (₹50-70 lakhs) for manufacturing enterprises in notified backward districts including Prayagraj
🏚
Uttar Pradesh Khadi & Village Industries Promotion Scheme
subsidy up to 25% for artisan clusters and cooperative societies engaged in traditional handicrafts
🏚
UP One District One Product (ODOP) Scheme
dedicated marketing support, trade fair participation funds, and GST exemption for registered ODOP entrepreneurs
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
