Local demand from schools, offices, and hospitality sectors provides a stable market foundation.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Mandi ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Mandi's industrial clusters around Thunag and Baijnath areas; interview 5-7 existing steel furniture manufacturers; study demand patterns at local schools and government tenders via GeM portal; analyze competitor pricing on Indiamart and 1mg seller networks.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source mild steel sheets and tubes from Ludhiana or Jalandhar steel suppliers (bulk order: ₹1.5-2.5L for first batch); source welding rods from local hardware shops in Mandi town; negotiate payment terms at 15-30 days; establish relationship with a paint supplier for coating materials.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 established steel furniture artisans in Mandi for initial 50-unit batch on job-work basis; or rent 1500-2000 sq ft workshop space in industrial area of Mandi town (₹8-12K/month); hire 2-3 skilled welders and 1 finishing worker; invest in basic welding machines, cutting tools, and spray booth.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME in Mandi district; obtain IEC code from Customs for export readiness; register trademark on IP India portal; create GST registration; obtain Pollution Control Board NOC; design packaging with brand name and product certifications (ISO 9001 or equivalent).
5
First Sale
List products on Amazon Business, IndiaMART, and Flipkart B2B; reach out to 20-30 schools via GeM portal tenders in Himachal Pradesh; approach corporate bulk buyers through LinkedIn; launch Instagram storefront with product videos; contact interior designers in Shimla and Kangra for referrals.
6
Scale
After 6 months, if achieving 30-40 units/month: hire dedicated sales team; expand product range to modular office furniture; open second production line; explore exports via TradeKey or Global Sources; establish dealership network across North India.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy for registered MSMEs in manufacturing sector
🏛
MUDRA Loan
up to ₹10 lakh collateral-free loan for business expansion and working capital
🏚
Himachal Pradesh Industrial Policy 2022
offers investment subsidy of 15-25% on plant and machinery for manufacturing units in notified districts including Mandi
🏚
HP MSME Support Scheme
provides marketing support and participation subsidies for trade fairs and exhibitions at state and national levels

Marketing — Zero Budget Se Shuru Karo
  • Create product demo videos on YouTube and Instagram showing durability tests (weight-bearing, drop tests); target school management and corporate procurement officers through LinkedIn ads.
  • Partner with 3-5 interior designers and architects in Shimla, Kangra, and Solan to offer 10-15% wholesale margins in exchange for referrals and brand visibility.
  • Participate in trade shows like India International Trade Fair (Delhi) and North India Industrial Expo (Punjab); focus on B2B buyer engagement with product catalogs and bulk pricing.
