Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Mandi ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit English Road and Nababpur manufacturing clusters in Mandi town this week. Spend 2 hours walking shops, talk to 3 owners about raw material costs, production timelines, and buyer types. Call one local supplier (steel plate seller) for free quote. Zero cost — do this before investing.
2
Source Raw Materials
Buy raw materials from steel suppliers in Mandi and nearby Solan/Nalagarh within 80 km radius. First trial batch: ₹40,000-60,000 for steel sheets, rods, consumables (enough for 3-4 pieces). Nababpur cluster has 5+ established suppliers; request introductions from cluster shopkeepers.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 600-800 sq ft workshop space in Mandi manufacturing area for ₹8,000-12,000/month (negotiate 3-month advance). Invest ₹80,000-1,20,000 in basic tools (welding machine, cutting tools, drill, grinding machine). Start with 1-2 semi-skilled welders (₹15,000/month each). Alternatively, outsource fabrication to nearby artisans and do finishing/painting in-house.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam on udyamregistration.gov.in (free, 10 minutes online). File sole proprietorship at local SMO (₹500). Get PAN if not filed (free online at incometaxindiaefiling.gov.in). Since GI tag not applicable for Mandi steel furniture, focus on product certification: ISO 1161 welding or metal furniture standards (₹15,000-25,000, 4-6 weeks via NABL labs). If exporting, apply for IEC from local DGFT office (₹0, online process).
5
First Sale
First sale: post 10-12 product photos with dimensions and pricing on IndiaMART and Udaan. Target small retailers, offices, schools. First-month realistic target: ₹1,00,000-1,50,000 revenue (8-10 units at ₹12,000-18,000 each). Use WhatsApp to follow up leads from existing cluster networks; ask existing shopkeepers for referrals.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 2nd full-time welder (₹18,000/month), add powder coating facility (₹50,000 investment), reduce outsourcing. Exhibit at India Expo Centre (Delhi), India International Trade Fair, and regional furniture fairs to meet bulk buyers. Build relationships with procurement officers at government offices, schools, hospitals. Launch on Flipkart B2B. Consider opening a second location in adjacent Himachal towns.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25% subsidy on setup cost up to ₹10 lakh (max ₹2.5 lakh subsidy), requires ITI training or matched savings
🏛
MUDRA Shishu Loan
up to ₹50,000 collateral-free from banks for micro enterprises, 0% processing fee for first 2 years via PM Jan Dhan accounts
🏚
Himachal Pradesh MSME Loan Subsidy
3% interest subsidy for loans up to ₹25 lakh if unit established in notified industrial areas
🏚
Himachal Pradesh Skill Development Scheme
free welding and metal fabrication training via HIMACHAL SKILL DEVELOPMENT CORPORATION (HSDC) for young founders
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
