The district's 200+ registered Ayurvedic manufacturers operate with established quality testing labs, cold storage, and AYUSH-certified facilities creating a ready supply chain for new entrants.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Karnal ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Karnal's Kunjpura and Nilokheri industrial areas (free, 2 hours) to meet 5-6 Ayurvedic manufacturers — call ahead using Chamber of Commerce numbers. Attend the Haryana State Ayurveda Board monthly meet (free) in Chandigarh (45km away). Register free on Udyam portal before month-end.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from: (1) Himachal Herbs Cooperative, Kangra district — ashwagandha, brahmi, tulsi at ₹800-1200/kg; (2) Indore herbal market — turmeric, ginger at ₹200-400/kg; (3) Local Haryana farmers via Jind/Hisar agricultural markets — seasonal fresh botanicals. First batch of 100kg mixed herbs costs ₹50-80K. Carrier oils from Kolkata refined oil suppliers at ₹180-220/liter (100L order = ₹18-22K).
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 2000-3000 sqft unit in Kunjpura Industrial Area (₹15-25K/month) or Nilokheri MSME cluster (₹12-18K/month) with water/power connection. Minimum setup: cold extraction equipment (₹2-3L), mixing/grinding setup (₹1-1.5L), bottling machine (₹1.5-2L), quality testing kit (₹50K). OR partner with existing manufacturers in Karnal for contract manufacturing at ₹15-25 per unit (no capital). Total setup: ₹5-8L for independent unit or ₹2-3L for contract route. Hire 4-5 staff (₹1-1.5L/month salary).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME (free, 10 days online). Apply for GI registration if formulation is proprietary (₹15K fee, 6-8 months); not mandatory but adds premium pricing. Get AYUSH product license from Haryana AYUSH Board (₹5-10K, 30-45 days) — required for manufacturing. If exporting, apply for IEC code via DGFT online (₹0, 5-7 days). Get ISO 22000 or ISO 9001 certification (₹40-60K, 3 months) — customers demand this.
5
First Sale
Create Amazon Seller account (free), list 5-10 SKUs with 8+ photos each. First month: target 50-100 units through organic search + sponsored ads (₹10K/month marketing budget). Contact 20 local Ayurvedic clinics via WhatsApp with bulk pricing (25-50 units at ₹180-220 per bottle). Join WhatsApp groups for wellness entrepreneurs and corporate buyers. Expected first-month revenue: ₹3-5L (50 units D2C at ₹400 = ₹2L + 50 units wholesale at ₹200 = ₹1L).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: (1) Hire sales manager (₹30-40K) to handle clinic/corporate relationships. (2) Start Amazon Advertising account with ₹50K/month budget. (3) Apply for PMEGP subsidy (₹25-50L depending on category) to expand unit. (4) Launch WhatsApp Business with catalog and automated responses. (5) Exhibit at Natural Products Expo India (Delhi, 3x/year) — stall cost ₹1-2L but 50+ B2B leads per event. (6) Build export relationships: hire export consultant (₹15-20K/month retainer) and submit samples to EU/US distributors. At ₹20L+/month, hire 15-20 staff and lease 5000 sqft unit.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)
25-35% subsidy (up to ₹50L) for manufacturing units in non-metro areas; Haryana has higher subsidy rate for women entrepreneurs
🏛
Ministry of AYUSH Ayurveda Promotion Scheme
50% grant for GI registration, AYUSH certification, and export quality testing; max ₹2-5L per applicant
🏚
Haryana MSME Promotion Scheme
₹5L interest-free loan for first-time manufacturers under Udyam registration; 3-year repayment with 1-year moratorium
🏚
Haryana State Ayurveda Board Export Incentive
10-15% rebate on export documentation, customs clearance, and port charges for products originating in Haryana
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
