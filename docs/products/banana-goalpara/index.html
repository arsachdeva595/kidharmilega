Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Goalpara ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Goalpara fruit wholesale market (Goalpara town) and Bothaliganj banana farm clusters to meet 3-5 vendors and farmers — ask current buying prices, seasonal output, and export contacts. Call Ministry ODOP helpline (1800-258-0039) for free advisory. Attend Goalpara Chamber of Commerce monthly meet (free entry). Zero cost, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source directly from Bothaliganj and Goalpara town farm clusters; expect ₹8-12 per kg farm-gate price for 5-10 metric tonnes first batch (₹40K-₹1.2L). Negotiate 15-30 day payment terms with farmers; connect via Goalpara Farmers Association or contact 3-5 farms directly.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 2,000-3,000 sqft ripening unit + cold storage in Goalpara town (₹15K-₹25K/month, 12-month lease = ₹1.8-₹3L). Partner with 5-10 local farmers on offtake agreement (minimum 2 metric tonnes/month guarantee) rather than owning farms. Equipment: cardboard boxes, ripening chambers (₹50K-₹1.2L depending on capacity), weighing scales, cold storage rental. Total first 6 months: ₹3.5-₹6.5L.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 5 min online at udyamregistration.gov.in). Apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) at nearest DGFT office in Guwahati (₹0 cost, 3-5 days processing) if targeting exports. Approach Assam Ministry of ODOP for GI registration support (currently No GI status — process takes 6-8 months, covers ₹1-2L application cost via subsidy). Obtain FSSAI license for food handling (₹500-₹2,000, 15 days) if planning D2C. Total: ₹3,500-₹5,000 and 4 weeks.
5
First Sale
First sale: Connect with 2-3 wholesale fruit vendors at Guwahati Fancy Bazaar (1 metric tonne order, ₹18-₹22/kg wholesale = ₹18K-₹22K revenue, 10-15% margin = ₹1,800-₹3,300 profit). Simultaneously upload to Jio Mart seller portal (free listing); target 50-100 kg first month via D2C at ₹35-₹45/kg = ₹1,750-₹4,500 revenue. Expected first-month combined revenue: ₹20K-₹27K (1.1-1.3 metric tonnes moved).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (40-50 metric tonnes/month output): hire 1 supply chain manager (₹20K-₹25K/month), 2-3 ripening unit staff (₹8K-₹10K each), 1 export documentation manager. Invest in second cold storage unit (₹3-₹5L). Apply for PMEGP subsidy (up to ₹10L loan + 25% subsidy) to fund expansion. Exhibit at Assam Agro Food Expo (annual, Guwahati) and India Fruit Summit. Target 3-5 export containers/month to Iraq and UAE via freight forwarders in Kolkata.
