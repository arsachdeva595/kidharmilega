Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Balrampur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Balrampur Agricultural Department office and Gram Vikas Mandals in Daman and Ramanujganj blocks. Connect with Balrampur Rice Millers Association. Spend 2-3 weeks observing harvest cycles (Sept-Nov) and understanding GI certification requirements from CGRHADA (Chhattisgarh Rice and Horticulture Research and Development Authority).
2
Source Raw Materials
Source paddy from registered farmers in Balrampur cooperative societies (Daman block has ~450 Jeeraphool cultivators). First batch: 5-10 quintal paddy @ ₹2,800-3,200/quintal = ₹1.4-3.2L. Contact District Agricultural Officer for certified seed supplier list. Arrange storage facility locally.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing rice mills in Balrampur town (5+ operational mills) for milling and grading services. Start with toll milling model (₹80-120 per quintal processing fee) before investing in own machinery. Connect with Balrampur Laghu Udyog Samiti for artisan networks and subsidized equipment loans.
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam Aadhar (online, free). Obtain IEC from Raipur DGFT office. Get GI label certification validation from Agricultural Ministry. Trademark your brand name with IP office. Register with FSSAI (food processing license) — ₹2,000-5,000 processing fee. Create packaging with GI logo and origin certification.
5
First Sale
Launch on Udaan (B2B procurement platform) and IndiaFresh for bulk orders. Create Instagram/Facebook with farm-to-table content and farmer testimonials. Partner with Organic stores in Raipur/Bilaspur. Approach 10-15 premium restaurants in Chhattisgarh with branded samples. List on your own Shopify store with ₹500/month plan. Target NRI platforms like IndianGroceryStore and Diaspora Spice.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to 50-100 quintal monthly production. Establish direct-to-consumer subscriptions (₹1,500/month curated rice boxes). Launch on Amazon/Flipkart with FBA logistics. Hire a regional sales rep for Madhya Pradesh. Invest in food processing facility for value-added products (rice flour, roasted rice). Apply for APEDA export subsidy for first 10 containers shipped. Aim for ₹1.5Cr annual revenue by month 18.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Program)
up to ₹25L government subsidy (35% for general category) for rice processing unit setup with 6-8% subsidized loans
🏛
APEDA Export Promotion Scheme
20% subsidy on freight for first 5 containers of GI products exported, plus market development assistance for premium rice exports
🏚
Chhattisgarh ODOP Fund
₹15L subsidy + ₹25L loan at 4% interest for registered ODOP units with GI certification, valid for 3 years
🏚
Chhattisgarh Agricultural Cooperative Marketing Federation (CCMF) Support
free market linkage, bulk procurement guarantee, and storage facility access for registered rice producers at ₹50/quintal
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
