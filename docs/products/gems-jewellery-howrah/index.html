Howrah district (1,467 sq km, 4.8M people) sits adjacent to Kolkata metro and houses two dedicated jewelry clusters — Ananda Niketan and Jewellery Park — with direct access to gold, diamond, and precious stone suppliers. West Bengal ranks in India's top 5 states for organized retail jewelry stores, and the district's 83.31% literacy rate supports design and quality consciousness. Howrah's jewelry makers export to USA, UAE, UK, and Singapore, with government-mandated hallmarking now ensuring quality assurance across all retail.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Howrah ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Ananda Niketan cluster and Jewellery Park in Howrah city, meet 3-5 master craftspeople, ask their cost structure and typical order sizes. Call 2-3 bullion suppliers (gold dealers near Howrah station area) and note pricing. Attend a local jewelry market fair or Puja season fair (free entry). Register on Udyam (free, online in 10 mins at udyamregistration.gov.in).
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from gold dealers near Howrah railway station or in the Jewellery Park complex (₹40-60L for 100gm working stock). Buy diamonds and precious stones from certified wholesalers in Kolkata's Diamond Street (₹20-40L first purchase). Join a WhatsApp group of local jewelers (ask in the clusters) to track price movements.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent a 250-400 sq ft shop in Ananda Niketan or Jewellery Park (₹8-15K/month) with basic jewelry tools (₹80-150K one-time). Option B: Outsource production to 1-2 master craftspeople from the cluster on commission (3-8% of piece cost). Most founders do hybrid — own design and finishing, outsource heavy casting.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online). Get hallmarking certificate from nearest BIS-approved assay office in Kolkata (₹500-1000 per batch). Apply for GST (free). If exporting, get IEC from customs (₹0 cost, 5 days online). For GI registration of designs, file at Controller of Patents (₹10-20K, 2-3 year process, optional but gives brand moat).
5
First Sale
First sales: list 5-10 signature pieces on Amazon and Etsy (free listing), price at ₹12-25K per piece. Approach 5 bridal boutiques in Kolkata with 2-3 sample pieces on consignment. Target first ₹80-120K revenue in month 1-2 (8-12 pieces sold). Use Instagram Reels showing design process and close-ups to build social proof.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 1-2 junior designers and a social media manager. Exhibit at All India Handicraft Expo (Delhi) and Mumbai jewelry shows to get bulk buyers. Create a catalog for corporate gifting companies. Expand D2C to your own website and Pinterest. Consider hiring 1-2 artisans full-time (₹12-18K/month) for capacity. Move to a 600-800 sq ft workshop (₹15-25K/month) with dedicated polishing and quality check.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹15,000 stipend + 5% interest subvention on loans up to ₹1L for individual artisans, and ₹20-25L for cooperative workshop setups
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
subsidy up to ₹25L and loan guarantee for setting up a jewelry workshop, 15-25% subsidy depending on location
🏚
West Bengal Handicraft Development Council scheme
up to 25% subsidy on purchase of modern jewelry tools and machines, max ₹5L
🏚
West Bengal ODOP scheme
marketing support, free stall at state exhibitions, reduced GST on artisan sales in specific craft clusters
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
