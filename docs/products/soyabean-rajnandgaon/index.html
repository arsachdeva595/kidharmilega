Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Rajnandgaon ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Rajnandgaon mandi (wholesale grain market) on Tuesday or Friday morning. Call 3-4 soybean traders at the mandi — ask current rates, seasonal patterns, buyer list. Attend a Rajnandgaon District Agricultural Office haat (free entry) to understand MSP policy. Zero cost, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source directly from farmers in Manpur, Chandrapur, or Ghat tehsils (peak supply Oct-Nov). First-batch cost: ₹3,800-4,200 per tonne × 10 tonnes = ₹38,000-42,000 for trial run. Supplier area: Manpur APMC mandi, 22 km from Rajnandgaon city.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Partner with existing oil mills (Rajnandgaon has 4-5 licensed mills) for contract crushing — pay ₹800-1,200 per tonne, no fixed cost. Option B: Rent 1,200 sq ft unit near Rajnandgaon industrial area (₹12,000-18,000/month) and buy basic cleaning equipment (₹80,000-120,000). Total entry: ₹100,000-180,000 for processing setup.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online). Apply for IEC code (₹0 fee, 2 weeks) if exporting. File GI registration support with Chhattisgarh Laghu Utta Udyog Board if pursuing GI (₹15,000-25,000, 6 months). Get FSSAI license (₹5,000-8,000 for small enterprise, 30 days) if selling edible products.
5
First Sale
First customer: Register on IndiaTradeNet or connect with an APEDA registered export trading house (zero cost, referral-based). Typical first order: 20 tonnes from mill buyer at ₹4,100/tonne = ₹82,000 gross. Expected first month: ₹1-1.5L if securing 3-4 mill buyers simultaneously.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month (25-30 tonnes/month): hire 2-3 quality checkers, join Rajnandgaon Agri Export Association (₹5,000/year membership), apply for SFURTI cluster development grant (₹5L-20L subsidy if part of group). Exhibit at India International Trade Fair (IITF) annually and regional agri-expos in Delhi, Mumbai. Negotiate storage with FCI or cold chain operator for ₹2,000-3,000/tonne/month.
