Decades of tradition have created reliable supplier networks and quality benchmarks.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Hamirpur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Hamirpur's shoe clusters in Bijnor Road area and Salempur industrial zone. Spend 2 weeks meeting 10+ established manufacturers to understand production costs (₹150-400 per pair), timelines (10-15 days per batch), and current export contacts. Join Hamirpur Shoe Manufacturers Association (if exists) or connect via local chamber of commerce.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source leather from tanneries in Kanpur (quality hides at ₹80-150/kg) or local Hamirpur suppliers. Buy rubber soles from Bahadurgarh (Haryana) or local suppliers at ₹30-60/pair. First batch of 500 pairs: budget ₹75K-125K depending on shoe type. Negotiate 30-45 day payment terms with suppliers.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 established artisan workshops in Hamirpur (production capacity 200-500 pairs/day each). Negotiate ₹120-200 per pair production cost. Start with simple sandals/casual shoes to test demand, then expand to formal/semi-formal. Visit workshops weekly to ensure quality control and on-time delivery.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (one-day online process on udyamregistration.gov.in). Obtain IEC code from DGFT portal (₹500-1000). Register brand name on ipindia.gov.in (trademark, ₹9000-15000). Get GST registration (online, 3-5 days). Apply for ISO 9001 certification (optional, ₹50K-150K, adds export credibility).
5
First Sale
Launch on Meesho, Myntra, Flipkart (via aggregators like Shiprocket) and Amazon within first 60 days with 50 SKUs. Create Instagram/Facebook pages with shoe care content and artisan stories. Send samples to 20 B2B bulk buyers (corporate offices, schools) offering ₹15-25% discounts for minimum 100-pair orders. Reach out to 10 regional distributors in Delhi/NCR and Punjab offering 20-25% wholesale margins.
6
Scale
After hitting ₹20L revenue (6 months), expand artisan partnerships to 5-6 workshops. Launch second line (formal/casual hybrid range). Secure export orders via directories like Alibaba and ThomasNet. Hire a full-time operations manager. Invest in basic machinery (skiving, sole-pressing machines, ₹30-50L) to reduce per-unit production cost. Explore SFURTI or PMEGP subsidy reimbursement for equipment.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Yojana
up to ₹3L credit at 5% interest for registered artisans and small manufacturers
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Program)
up to ₹25L subsidy + ₹25L bank loan for new MSMEs in shoe manufacturing
🏚
UPSRLM (UP Self Reliance Loan)
up to ₹50L at subsidized rates (2-3% interest subsidy) for SHG and MSME in manufacturing sector
🏚
UP ODOP Branding and Marketing Assistance
grants up to ₹15L for packaging, branding, and digital marketing of notified ODOP products

Marketing

Ye marketing ideas try karna mat bhulna

  • Partner with 20 micro-influencers (50K-500K followers) in Delhi/NCR to create 'Day in the Life of a Hamirpur Shoemaker' reels on Instagram. Offer commission-based sales (8-12%) to incentivize promotion. Cost: ₹30-50K for 4-week campaign.
  • Run Google Shopping Ads targeting 'comfortable shoes for office/casual wear' within 200km of major metros. Budget ₹50K/month, target CPA ₹150-200. Retarget website visitors with 15% discount codes on Facebook/Instagram.
  • Launch referral program: existing customers get ₹500 credit for every successful referral. Seed with 100 free pairs to micro-communities (office groups, WhatsApp circles, alumni networks). Target 10% referral conversion.
