opportunity, Kotputli-Behror mein.

Kotputli-Behror lies on National Highway 48, 60km from Delhi's automotive OEM hubs and 150km from Jaipur, placing founders within 2-3 hours of all major Indian auto buyers and 4-6 hours of ports. The district contains 8 tehsils (Kotputli, Behror, Neemrana, Bansur, Mandhan, Paota, Viratnagar, Narayanpur) with 1200+ small-scale auto parts units already producing components for India's top 5 automakers. Raw material suppliers are concentrated in Delhi NCR; semi-finished steel, aluminum, and fasteners arrive daily through established logistics corridors, reducing lead times to 3-5 days.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kotputli-Behror ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kotputli MSME cluster (Govindgarh Industrial Area) and Behror auto parts zone this week — no cost. Call 5 suppliers: ask about material costs, lead times, buyer names. Attend Rajasthan MSME meet or local Behror chamber event. Register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) using your Aadhaar and PAN.
2
Source Raw Materials
Contact material suppliers in Okhla Industrial Area (Delhi, 70km away) or Behror local suppliers. First batch cost: ₹3-5L for 100-200 units (engine mounts, brake components, or electrical connectors). Lead time: 2-3 weeks. Supplier clusters: Okhla Phase 1 & 3 (Delhi) or Behror main market.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 2000-3000 sqft unit in Behror or Kotputli MSME estate (₹12-18K/month). Partner with experienced tooling/machining units already present in the cluster for first 6 months (sub-contract model, 25-30% of parts cost), or invest ₹50-80L in CNC machines + manual press + assembly area if you have ₹1.5L+ capital. Hire 3-5 semi-skilled workers (₹12-15K/month each).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (free, 1 day). Apply for ISO 9001:2015 certification (₹20-30K, 45 days) — mandatory for OEM supply. Register IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) with DGFT (free, 3-5 days online) only if you plan exports. BIS certification if producing brake or electrical parts (₹50-75K, 60 days). Skip GI registration — no GI status for this product.
5
First Sale
First sales: Contact Tier-2 suppliers via B2B platforms (Udaan, TradeKey, Global Sources). Target first order: 200-500 units at ₹800-2000/unit wholesale. First-month revenue potential: ₹2-4L if you land 1-2 small orders. Use WhatsApp and phone for cold outreach — call 20 Tier-2 suppliers daily, visit 2-3 per week.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: Hire quality control officer (₹18-22K), upgrade to semi-automatic machines (₹30-50L capex), formalize logistics (tie up with Allcargo or local carriers for bulk shipment to Delhi/Gurgaon). Exhibit at Auto Component Makers Association (ACMA) shows (Pune, Delhi, Bangalore annually). Target export: attend Germany Auto parts fairs (Automechanika) or approach German importers via Alibaba.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
₹25L subsidy (35% of project cost) for manufacturing; apply via KVIC/KVIB district office.
🏛
MUDRA Loan (Standup India variant for auto manufacturing)
₹50L unsecured loan at 8-10% interest, 7-year tenure; apply via any scheduled bank.
🏚
Rajasthan MSME Capital Subsidy Scheme
25% subsidy on plant & machinery up to ₹50L; apply via MSME department.
🏚
Rajasthan Export Promotion Scheme
₹5-10L support for first-time exporters; apply via Rajasthan State Export Promotion Council.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
