Handicrafts (Stone Carving) ki
opportunity, Khurda mein.

Khurda district is home to skilled stone carvers trained through generations in temple art traditions. The proximity to Bhubaneswar and availability of high-quality soapstone and granite deposits make it an ideal hub for stone carving crafts. The district's historical connection to Jagannath Temple architecture sustains demand and expertise.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Khurda ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Khurda stone carving clusters in Bhubaneswar (Sachivalaya Marg area) and meet master artisans; research Jagannath Temple stone architecture; study competitor pricing on Etsy and Amazon Handmade; attend ODOP exhibitions in Odisha
2
Source Raw Materials
Source soapstone from Khurda local quarries (₹2,000-3,000 per ton) or Rajasthan sandstone suppliers; order basic carving tools from Kolkata tool markets or online (₹15,000-25,000 for starter set); budget ₹50,000-80,000 for first 50-piece batch
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established master carvers in Bhubaneswar's artisan colonies (30-40% revenue share typical); begin with 3-5 apprentices; establish small workshop (200-300 sq ft, ₹5,000-8,000/month rent); implement quality control process for consistency
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, free); apply for IEC code through DGFT portal (₹500-1,000); create brand identity reflecting Odisha heritage; obtain CSR certifications for social enterprise angle; consider trademark registration for unique designs
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy (2.8% + $0.20 per listing) targeting Western home décor buyers; list on Amazon Handmade with high-quality 360° product photography; approach interior design studios in Mumbai and Delhi; partner with Indian heritage e-commerce platforms (Craftsvilla, Jaypore); attend National Handicraft Fair
6
Scale
Expand artisan network to 8-10 carvers by month 7; develop signature product lines (religious sculptures, abstract art); establish B2B relationships with 5+ hotel chains or corporate offices; launch Instagram-driven influencer collaborations with home décor creators; explore international markets through trade shows (Handcraft 2024 in Mumbai)

PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakhs subsidy for manufacturing enterprises with 15% capex support
🏚
Odisha Handicraft Development Scheme
raw material subsidy up to 20% + marketing support for ODOP clusters
🏚
Odisha Artisan Pension Scheme
₹500/month stipend for registered traditional artisans aged 60+ years
