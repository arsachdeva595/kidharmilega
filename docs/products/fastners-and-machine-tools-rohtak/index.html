Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Rohtak ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Rohtak MSME Cluster in Industrial Area Phase 1 (free, 2 hours) — walk 5-6 active fastener units, ask about raw material suppliers, speak with 2-3 owners about startup costs. Call Rohtak District Small Industries Association (Google) — free guidance. Register free Udyam account on udyamregistration.gov.in.
2
Source Raw Materials
Ferrous material: Dada Nagar, Delhi (45 km) or Chikli MIDC suppliers via mobile—order 2-3 ton test batch at ₹35-42 per kg. Non-ferrous: contact Jatia or Ballarpur dealers (60 km radius). First batch cost ₹1.8-2.2L for 500 kg mixed fastener raw stock. Local supplier list available from Rohtak District Industries Centre.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 1,500-2,000 sq ft unit in Rohtak Industrial Area Phase 1 at ₹8-12K/month. Purchase CNC lathe (₹18-25L, or hire existing unit at ₹6-8K/day). Partner with 2-3 experienced turners as piece-rate workers (₹150-250/day each). Total initial production setup: ₹28-38L for owned machinery, ₹12-15L for rented + working capital.
4
Brand & Register
Udyam registration: 10 min online, free, use ITR or bank statement. IEC code (for export): ₹5K fee, 5 days, apply via DGFT portal if export revenue >₹1L/year. GI registration: Not applicable (no regional/artisan uniqueness). Pan + GST: ₹0 + 30 mins online. Total formal cost: ₹5K, timeline 7-10 days.
5
First Sale
First sale: contact 5-6 automotive aftermarket distributors in Rohtak/Jind via phone (₹0 cost). First order size: 50-100 kg mixed fastener batch. Use IndiaMART to post product listings (free tier), target maintenance departments in Delhi NCR. Expected first month revenue: ₹45-75K from 3-4 small local orders.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 junior operator + 1 sales coordinator (₹12-15K salary each), invest ₹15-20L in second CNC lathe, move to 3,500 sq ft unit. Apply for PMEGP subsidy (₹25L at 35% subsidy) for expansion. Exhibit at Auto Component Clusters trade fairs in Jind, Hisar (₹40K booth). Contact 2-3 OEM buyers directly via industry directories.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
35% subsidy on capital investment up to ₹25L, interest-free term loan ₹10L for registered MSMEs
🏛
MUDRA Shishu Loan
up to ₹50,000 collateral-free working capital from bank on Udyam registration, 8-10% interest
🏚
Haryana MSME Promotion Scheme
25% subsidy on plant/machinery for new manufacturing units registered within district, claim within 6 months of purchase
🏚
Haryana Export Promotion Scheme
5% cash incentive on FOB value for exports >₹25L/year, valid for 5 years post-registration
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
