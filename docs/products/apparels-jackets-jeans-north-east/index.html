Apparels (Jackets & Jeans) ki
opportunity, North-East mein.

North-East Delhi (92 sq km, 1.95M population) is India's largest concentrated apparel manufacturing zone with 2,000+ registered units producing jeans, jackets, and formal wear. Raw materials arrive daily from Panipat textile mills (40 km away), Tiruppur denim suppliers (1,400 km), and local button/zip vendors in Chandni Chowk and wholesale fabric markets in Shahpur Jat. Major buyers include Reliance Brands, Aditya Birla Fashion, export consolidators, and D2C platforms; the district exports to USA, UK, Germany, and Australia with established logistics via Delhi and Mumbai ports.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — North-East ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Okhla Industrial Area Phase 1 & 2 garment clusters (free walk, talk to 5 unit owners), call 3 fabric suppliers in Panipat via phone, register free on Udyam.gov.in, attend monthly CEDC/SEDO buyer-seller meet in Shahdara — zero cost, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source denim from Panipat mills (Ravi Fabrics, Gokul Textiles, ₹80-120/meter for bulk), cotton blends from Tiruppur (₹60-100/meter), buttons/zips from Chandni Chowk or Shahpur Jat (₹2-8 per unit). First batch of 200 jackets + 300 jeans = ₹1.5-2L material cost; suppliers offer 30-45 day payment terms.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 1,000-2,000 sq ft unit in Okhla or Shahdara for ₹15-25K/month with stitching machines (4-6 industrial units @ ₹40-60K each = ₹2-3.5L one-time). Or partner with 2-3 established tailoring units in cluster (zero rent, pay per piece ₹60-120 per jacket). Initial production = 500 pieces/month; scaling to 2,000/month needs additional ₹3-5L for machines and 2-3 skilled staff @ ₹12-18K/month.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 10 min online, no rejection); file IEC with DGFT (₹0, 15 days turnaround for apparel export); obtain GST registration (₹0, 10 days). Skip GI registration (North-East Delhi has no GI tag). Trademark brand name with IP India (₹3,000-5,000 per class, 12-16 months). Total: ₹3-5K + 4-6 weeks.
5
First Sale
Launch on Myntra or Amazon Seller Central (first upload = free; commission 15-20%, first order: ₹50-100K, payment in 15 days). Parallel: cold-call 15 retail boutiques in Saket, Punjabi Bagh, GK1 (target ₹80-120K bulk orders). First month revenue target: ₹1.5-2.5L across both channels.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire a dedicated pattern-maker (₹20K/month), QA inspector (₹15K), 2 stitchers (₹12K each). Expand unit to 3,000 sq ft (₹25-30K/month). Launch on Ajio, Snapdeal. Exhibit at Pret-a-Porter Fashion Week (Delhi), GIF Trade Fair (New Delhi). Negotiate with export consolidators like Perry Ellis India, Brandix. Set up WhatsApp B2B catalog, attend monthly CEDC business summits.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy on loan amount, 35% subsidy for SC/ST/women founders, subject to bank loan eligibility.
🏛
MUDRA Loan Scheme
up to ₹10L unsecured loans for small manufacturing units; no collateral required, processed via SBI/HDFC Bank branches.
🏚
Delhi Enterprise Support Scheme
10% capital subsidy up to ₹10L for apparel manufacturing units registered as MSME, applied post-establishment.
🏚
Delhi Textile Policy (2020)
5-year property tax exemption for registered garment units, support for skill training, and export credit guarantee.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
