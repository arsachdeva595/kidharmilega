North-East Delhi hosts over 40,000 garment units and is home to Chandni Chowk, one of Asia's largest wholesale markets for apparel. The region has established supply chains, skilled labor pools, and proximity to raw material suppliers in Tiruppur, Tamil Nadu. Integrated logistics to pan-India retail and e-commerce platforms make it the fastest production-to-market destination.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — North-East ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Chandni Chowk apparel market and meet 5-10 established jacket/jeans manufacturers. Attend IITF Textile Pavilion in November. Join NIFT alumni network or connect with Apparel Export Promotion Council (AEPC) Delhi office for market intelligence.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source denim fabric from SITRA-certified mills in Tiruppur or local Chandni Chowk suppliers. First batch: ₹2-3L for 500 pieces (mix of sizes). Connect with thread/button suppliers on Alibaba or local Nai Sarak market. Negotiate 30-45 days credit terms.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with contract manufacturers in North-East Delhi (Karawal Nagar, Kalyan Vihar) who have cutting, stitching, and finishing capacity. Visit 3-5 units, negotiate per-unit production cost (₹250-400 per jacket/jeans). Alternatively, lease small 1000-1500 sqft unit and hire 8-10 tailors from local vocational centers.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) certificate online (Free). Apply for IEC code at DGFT portal (₹0). Register brand name at Ministry of Commerce IP cell. Open business bank account with GST registration. Design logo via Fiverr or local designer (₹5-10K). Obtain Clothing Quality Standard (CQS) and Azo dye compliance certificates.
5
First Sale
Launch Instagram shop and TikTok with user-generated content from micro-influencers (100K-500K followers) at ₹10-30K per post. Approach 20-30 retail stores in Delhi NCR for wholesale placement. List on Flipkart Seller Central with ₹20K initial investment. Target back-to-college season (May-July) and festive campaigns.
6
Scale
After 10K monthly units sold: Expand to 2-3 contract manufacturers, add 3-5 new SKUs (cargo jeans, leather jackets). Hire social media manager. Approach Amazon, Myntra, Nykaa. Explore bulk B2B orders from companies for workwear. Build WhatsApp bulk-order channel for retail partners.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L loan at subsidized interest for apparel manufacturing MSMEs.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹25L grant for cluster development in textiles with 5+ units.
🏚
Delhi Enterprise Promotion Scheme
5% SGST reimbursement for new apparel units for 3 years + subsidy on factory rent up to ₹5L annually.
🏚
Delhi Startup Policy
Registration subsidy of ₹25K + TDS exemption for 5 years if registered as StartUp India entity.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
