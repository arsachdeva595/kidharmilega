Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Papum Pare ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Papum Pare district agricultural office (Itanagar) and connect with ICAR-NEC (Indian Council of Agricultural Research, North East) to study local turmeric varieties. Meet farmers in Arunachal Pradesh Agricultural Produce Marketing Committee (APAPMC). Research curcumin testing labs in Delhi and Bangalore.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified turmeric rhizome seeds from ICAR stations or local Papum Pare farmer collectives at ₹15,000-25,000 per quintal for initial 5 quintal batch. Identify organic compost suppliers in Itanagar. Estimated first-batch cost: ₹80,000-120,000 including land prep.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 8-10 smallholder farmers in Papum Pare via contract farming (3-5 acres). Set up small processing unit (drying, grinding, packing) near Itanagar with 500 kg/month capacity. Train farmers on organic certification standards (NPOP/APEDA). Cost: ₹200,000-400,000 for processing setup.
4
Brand & Register
Register as micro-enterprise under Udyam portal. Obtain IEC code from APEDA for export. Apply for organic certification from APEDA-accredited agencies (₹15,000-25,000). Trademark brand name with IPAB. Setup GST registration (18% applicable on processed turmeric).
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Seller Central and Flipkart for D2C at ₹450-550/100g. Approach bulk buyers via LinkedIn and B2B platforms like Global Sources. Cold-email to Ayurvedic companies and nutraceutical manufacturers. Partner with organic retailers like Naturals and Health & Glow. Target 10-15 kg/week initial sales via 3-4 channels.
6
Scale
After 6 months, scale farmer network to 25-30 acres. Launch proprietary turmeric extracts and capsules (₹800-1,200/bottle retail). Pursue ISO 22000 and GMP certifications for exports. Target Japanese and EU markets through export-quality documentation. Establish B2B relationships with 5-7 major spice exporters for bulk contracts (50+ kg monthly).
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy for micro-enterprises in agricultural processing.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
support for farmer clusters with value-addition infrastructure up to ₹15 lakh.
🏚
Arunachal Pradesh APCB (Agriculture Credit Based Subsidy)
40% subsidy on agricultural inputs and equipment for registered farmers.
🏚
AP-AGRO (Arunachal Pradesh Agriculture Export Promotion Scheme)
10% subsidy on export-related certifications and logistics for registered exporters.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
