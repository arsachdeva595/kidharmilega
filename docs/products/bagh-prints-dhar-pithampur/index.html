Bagh Prints ki
opportunity, Dhar (Pithampur) mein.

Bagh village in Dhar district (coordinates: ~22.6°N, 74.8°E) sits 35km from Indore and 80km from Pithampur industrial zone, with ready access to white cotton and silk base fabrics from regional suppliers. The Bagh River is core to production—its mineral composition is believed to optimize natural dye fixing, making location non-negotiable. The cluster comprises 80+ active Khatri artisans producing 40-60 units/week each; buyers range from Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore premium boutiques to international export houses in Frankfurt, Paris, and New York.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Dhar (Pithampur) ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bagh village cluster (20km from Dhar town) and spend 3-4 days observing Ismail Khatri's workshop and neighboring artisans' units. Call 3-4 established printers (ask DC Handloom office for contact list). Attend Pithampur Commerce Association monthly meet (free). Register on Udyam portal (₹0, takes 30 mins). Zero cost, doable this month.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source white cotton base fabric (20x20, 40x40 count) from textile suppliers in Rajkot, Gujarat (₹40-80 per meter). Source silk from Bangalore silk merchants (₹150-300 per meter). Order dye chemistry from established suppliers in Dhar or Indore. First batch cost: ₹25,000-35,000 for 100 meters of cotton + ₹40,000-60,000 for 50 meters of silk. Supplier area: Rajkot (cotton), Bangalore (silk), Dhar town (local dyes).
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent a 300-400 sq ft workspace near Bagh cluster (₹3,000-5,000/month) and hire 1-2 trained Khatri artisans (₹12,000-18,000/month each) to do printing while you manage operations and sales. Option B: Partner with an established artisan as a buyer-exporter (no rent, split revenue 50-50 on finished goods). Option C: Apprentice under a master for 2-3 months (free/low-cost) to learn block carving and printing yourself. Production cost range: ₹150-400 per unit including labor, dyes, fabric.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 48 hours). File GI-protected use application with Department of Commerce, Ministry via Dhar DC office (₹0-5,000, 2-3 weeks approval). If exporting: apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) from DGFT office in Indore (₹0, 2-3 weeks), open current account with bank for export credits. Total: ₹2,000-8,000, 4-6 weeks.
5
First Sale
First sale via Instagram/Etsy: 2-3 sarees or cushion covers (₹4,000-6,000 per piece = ₹8-18k order). Facebook marketplace for local Indore/Pune buyers. Direct email to 50 boutiques in Delhi, Mumbai. First month realistic revenue: ₹20-40k (5-8 units). Expected first order size: ₹8,000-25,000.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire operations manager (₹20-25k/month), add 2-3 more artisans, invest in export documentation and shipping setup, apply for Merchandise Exports from India Scheme (MEIS) benefits. Exhibit at Heimtextil (Frankfurt), Maison & Objet (Paris), India Design ID (Delhi). Build B2B relationships with 5-10 boutique retailers via monthly order contracts. Add product line: wall hangings, table linens, home décor range.
