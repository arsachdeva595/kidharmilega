Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bharatpur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bharatpur's Agro-Market near Railway Station; meet farmers and merchants at Chhoti Sarai mandis; connect with Bharatpur Chamber of Commerce; attend NAFED trade meets in Delhi for commodity pricing insights.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source directly from Bharatpur's farming cooperatives (IFFCO, Jito) or Chhoti Sarai mandi; expect ₹75,000–₹2,00,000 for first 10-20 MT batch of mustard/pulses at seasonal rates; negotiate bulk discounts.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing mini mills in Bharatpur for cleaning, grading, and packaging (₹5-8/kg processing); hire local silo operators; or set up a small 1-2 MT/day cleaning unit with ₹12-15 lakh investment; employ 3-5 workers initially.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) online within 48 hours; obtain IEC code from DGFT portal for export eligibility; get FSSAI license (₹5,000–₹10,000); brand design on Canva; register trademark with IP India (₹4,500–₹9,000).
5
First Sale
Launch on Udaan, Jumbotail, Amazon Business with ₹50,000 initial inventory; approach Bhagirath Palace wholesale market in Delhi; contact Fair Trade and organic export agents; sell 500 kg/week to local institutional buyers before scaling online.
6
Scale
After 6 months, secure ₹15–₹30L credit line from NABARD; add value-added products (mustard oil, sprouted dal); expand distribution to 3-4 new cities via regional distributors; obtain organic certification; hire export logistics partner.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP
Up to ₹25 lakh subsidy for food processing unit with 25% founder contribution; processing micro-enterprises eligible.
🏛
SFURTI
Support for setting up food processing clusters; Bharatpur agro-processing zone eligible for ₹50L+ support per cluster.
🏚
Rajasthan Laghu Khanij Jan Samman Yojana
₹10L subsidy for food processing startups registered under MSME
🏚
Rajasthan Krishi Udyogyogi Yojana
5% interest subsidy on NABARD loans for agro-based businesses up to ₹20L
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
