Bharatpur sits on 507,073 hectares of cultivable land in eastern Rajasthan (latitude 26.22–27.50°N, longitude 76.53–78.17°E), producing mustard, wheat, bajra, and pulses for India's primary food basket. The district has 10 tehsils and 1,472 villages with over 10,000 micro/small agro-processing units concentrated in Bharatpur city and surrounding tehsils like Kaman and Deeg. Major buyers include Delhi/Noida wholesale markets (170 km), Jaipur food processors (240 km), and export nodes via Ludhiana and Delhi-ICD ports.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bharatpur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bharatpur city mandi (Monday/Wednesday early morning, free entry) and observe mustard seed and pulse traders. Call 3 existing agro-processor owners (ask local district office for contact list). Attend next Rajasthan ODOP scheme awareness session at Bharatpur Collectorate (dates on rajasthan.gov.in). Zero cost, do this within 7 days.
2
Source Raw Materials
Contact farmer cooperative societies in Kaman, Deeg, and Weir tehsils for direct mustard and pulse sourcing at ₹45-55/kg (mustard), ₹60-75/kg (pulses). First 500 kg batch will cost ₹30-35K. Alternatively, approach wholesale trader clusters in Bharatpur city mandi for year-round supply at ₹2-5% markup — reduces logistics but ties capital in receivables.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 1,000-1,500 sq ft food-processing unit (₹5-8K/month) in one of Bharatpur's 6 industrial areas (Bharatpur Industrial Area, Kaman MSME cluster). Install 3-5 MT/day capacity machinery: destoner (₹80K), pulverizer (₹120K), packing line (₹150K). Total production unit setup: ₹4-8L. Alternatively, start with contract processing at existing units (50% margin, zero capex) for first 6 months to validate demand.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam within 7 days (udyam.gov.in, free, takes 1 hour). Apply for FSSAI license (₹5-8K, 10-15 days). File GI application if doing specialty product like Bharatpur mustard oil (₹15-20K, 2-3 years approval). Get IEC code from DGFT for export (₹500, 2 days). Total regulatory cost: ₹20-30K, timeline 4-8 weeks.
5
First Sale
Day 1: Create seller account on Blinkit/JioMart (free). Upload 3-5 SKUs (mustard oil, bajra flour, pulse mix). Target first order: ₹15-25K per platform. Expected first-month revenue: ₹1-1.5L from D2C + ₹40-60K from 1-2 mandi wholesale orders. Alternatively, cold-call 5 BigBasket/Modern Bazaar store managers in NCR — first supply order typically ₹30-50K.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 operations lead (₹18-22K/month) and 1 marketing/sales coordinator (₹15-18K/month). Invest in SSL e-commerce website (₹60K). Exhibit at Rajasthan Food Safety Show (Jaipur, Feb-Mar) and AAHAR Delhi (Nov). Target institutional buyers via tenders on GeM portal. Start Instagram food education content (3 posts/week on bajra nutrition, mustard benefits). Scale production to 15-20 MT/month by partnering with 2 co-packers.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
₹10L subsidy (25% of project cost) for food processing micro-enterprises, up to ₹25L project cost. Eligible if annual turnover below ₹5L. Apply via nearby District Industries Centre, 60-90 days approval.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹2-3L cluster development grant if joining registered artisan/processor cluster. Covers common facility centre (drying, processing, cold chain). Apply via Ministry of Food Processing, 45-60 days.
🏚
Rajasthan ODOP Fund Support
Up to ₹10L credit at 4% interest for producers under ODOP products. Apply through State ODOP mission office (Jaipur) or district office. Disbursement 15-30 days post-verification.
🏚
Rajasthan Food Processing Policy 2022
5-year tax holiday for new food units + subsidy on cold chain infra (30-40% of cost). Apply within 12 months of UDYAM registration at RMEG (Rajasthan Mineral and Energy Company).
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
