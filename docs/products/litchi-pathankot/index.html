✓ Verified Success Story
Litchi Estate Sujanpur (Government Initiative)

Punjab government established Litchi Estate at Sujanpur in Pathankot district to centralize production and export. First commercial litchi consignment (10 quintals) was exported to London via Amritsar airport in June 2024 — a proof-of-concept that Pathankot litchi can reach premium international markets. Horticulture Minister contributed ₹1 lakh from personal salary to facilitate the export.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Pathankot ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Zero-cost: Visit Litchi Zone Sujanpur (5 km from Pathankot city) and walk through mature orchards to understand growing cycles, speak to 2-3 established growers about yields and costs, call Pathankot District Horticulture Office (+91-186-2201234) to get cluster map and registered producer list — all free, doable this week
2
Source Raw Materials
Source litchi saplings from Sujanpur nurseries (cost ₹30-50 per sapling); buy cardboard boxes + plastic crates from local packaging suppliers in Pathankot city (₹2-5 per box) — first-batch cost for 1-acre setup: ₹8,000-15,000 raw materials. Supplier area: Sujanpur block.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Lease 0.5-2 acres of litchi orchard in Sujanpur at ₹30,000-50,000/year, manage trees yourself or hire local orchard labor at ₹300-400/day; Option B: Partner with 2-3 existing orchardists as contract buyer and build processing unit (400-500 sq ft) for sorting, packaging, cold storage — capital range ₹5-15L for cold chain setup
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) online free at udyamregistration.gov.in (1 hour); apply for GI registration support through Ministry of Agriculture's Geographical Indication Cell (non-urgent given no current GI but differentiator if approved); obtain Food Licence (FSSAI registration, ₹5,000-10,000, 15 days) if processing; apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) via DGFT portal at ₹0 within 7 days for export eligibility
5
First Sale
First sale via BigBasket or Blinkit supply network — approach their Punjab produce partner, sell 20-50 kg trial lot at ₹100/kg fresh litchi (₹2,000-5,000 revenue); simultaneously list on local mandis (Pathankot Mandi, Amritsar Mandi) and contact 3-5 juice makers in nearby food processing units — expected first-month revenue ₹15,000-30,000
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 pack-house manager + 2 sorting staff (₹12,000/month total), invest in upgraded cold storage and ripening chambers (₹30-50L), set up second supply agreement with modern retail chain (BigBasket, Blinkit expanded placement), exhibit at Punjab Horticulture Trade Fair (June-July), attend PJTSME Export Summit in Chandigarh, directly approach London/EU importers via Federation of Fruit and Vegetable Associations India

Frequently Asked Questions

Seedhe sawaal, seedhe jawab

Agar seechi orchard lease lo aur manual processing karo, toh ₹5-8L ka setup cost (lease + labor + packaging). Agar professional pack-house aur cold storage bana rahe ho, ₹15-25L lagega. Punjab govt scheme se 50% subsidy mill sakti hai packing materials par, toh actual out-of-pocket ₹7.5-20L.
Helpful hai agricultural background, lekin zaruri nahi. Sujanpur ke orchardists already established hain; tu partnership karke shuru kar sakta hai ya contract farming kar sakta hai. Pehli saal challenging hoga (tree health, pest management seekna padega), lekin training available hai horticulture department se.
Punjab scheme se: 50% subsidy cardboard boxes par, 50% plastic crates par, 50% poly sheet replacement par, plus ₹10,000/acre incentive for new litchi gardens. Central schemes: PMEGP loan up to ₹25L at 5% interest, AIDF loan for cold storage. Free training at Horticulture Department Pathankot.
Amritsar mandi ya Pathankot mandi se direct wholesale buyers contact kar — typically 5-10 traders regularly buy fresh litchi. Digital: BigBasket, Blinkit, JioMart ke procurement teams ko call kar — trial order 20-50 kg le sakte ho. Export: Federation of Fruit and Vegetable Associations India ke member importers UK/Europe mein.
Haan, proven. June 2024 mein first 10-quintal consignment London gaya Amritsar airport se. Process: FSSAI certification + Udyam registration + IEC code + phytosanitary certificate (Plant Quarantine office Amritsar, ₹5,000-10,000). Pack properly in insulated containers, ship via air cargo or reefer containers. FOB price ₹90,000-110,000/quintal, UK importer margain 45-55%.
Realistically: litchi farming risk hai — 1-2 bad seasons mein 50-60% loss possible due to frost, pest, weather. Agar orchard lease model chalao, toh loss limited; agar owned land par lao, tab long-term play. Exit option: processing/value-add model switch karo (juice, jam maker ban ja), ya local mandi ka regular supplier ban ja aur small steady margin rakh (₹500-1000/week). Franchise model explore kar ya post-harvest logistics ka partner ban ja.
