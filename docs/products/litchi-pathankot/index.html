Pathankot's elevation (300-400m), moderate rainfall, and alluvial soil create ideal conditions for litchi cultivation. The region's unique microclimate and temperature fluctuations during flowering enhance fruit sweetness and aroma, differentiating Pathankot litchi from other Indian litchi-growing areas. Historical orchards spanning 3,000+ hectares provide established supply chains and farmer expertise.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Pathankot ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Pathankot Litchi Growers Association (contact via Punjab Agricultural Department), study 5-10 established orchards in Pathankot & Nurpur blocks. Tour Pathankot Mandi to understand pricing, seasonality (May-June peak), and buyer networks. Connect with ICAR Research Station, Baijnath for soil & variety guidance.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source premium litchi saplings (Bombay, Shahi, Mozac varieties) from Punjab State Agricultural Department nurseries or Himachal Pradesh horticultural centres (₹30-50 per sapling). For fresh fruit, establish direct ties with 2-3 established growers in Pathankot producing 5-10 tons first season. First batch procurement cost: ₹2-3L for 500kg fruit quality control & logistics.
3
Produce / Partner
For own orchard: lease/purchase 1-2 hectares, install drip irrigation (₹1.5L per hectare), plant 100-120 saplings/hectare, await 3-year yield cycle. Faster path: contract with 5-10 small growers via Farmer Producer Organisation (FPO) model, provide training & inputs, secure supply. Parallel: establish micro food processing unit (juice/pulp) with 1-2 staff, basic equipment (₹25-40L).
4
Brand & Register
Register as MSME under Udyam (online, 10 mins). Obtain IEC code from DGFT portal for export eligibility. Secure FSSAI license for processed products (₹500-1000). Create brand identity: Pathankot-specific tagline, GI story (petition for GI tag separately). Trademark brand name with IP India.
5
First Sale
Launch on JioMart & BigBasket with fresh litchi boxes (500g, 1kg premium packs, ₹200-300) targeting tier 1 cities. Simultaneously pitch to HoReCa (Oberoi, Taj, ITC chains in Delhi/Mumbai) with bulk supply contracts (2-5 tons/month). Engage Instagram/YouTube with orchard farm diaries, harvest videos. Partner with Pathankot Municipal Corporation for 'Litchi Festival' promotions (June harvest season) attracting tourists & influencers.
6
Scale
After 6 months: expand to 3-5 value-added SKUs (dried litchi, litchi jam, ready-to-drink juice). Secure bulk B2B contracts with juice companies, ice cream brands. Open 2-3 agro-tourism homestays in orchards (tie-up with ATDC). Establish export partnerships via APEDA for UAE/Bangladesh markets. Upscale processing unit, hire 5-8 staff, target ₹2-3 Cr revenue by year 2.
Government Schemes

up to ₹25L subsidy for food processing unit with litchi pulp/juice focus
🏛
APEDA Export Promotion Scheme
50% subsidy on logistics, certification for fresh litchi export to specified markets
🏚
Punjab State Horticulture Mission
₹5L per hectare subsidy for litchi orchard expansion, drip irrigation support
🏚
Punjab Agricultural Department FPO Support
grant assistance for forming Farmer Producer Organisation to aggregate litchi supply
