Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sangareddy ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit the Sangareddy industrial cluster near Medchal-Malkajgiri, study 3-4 established precision engineering firms (Bharat Engineering, Prema Precision), understand ISO 9001/AS9100 standards required for aerospace, visit ITC-STRIVE centre in Hyderabad for quality benchmarks.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from steel suppliers in Secunderabad (₹15-25L for first batch), establish relationships with local CNC tool distributors, negotiate payment terms with JSW Steel or Tata Steel branches in Hyderabad; first batch raw material cost ₹20-30L depending on product specifications.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 existing CNC manufacturing units in Sangareddy under co-production agreements, invest in quality testing equipment (CMM machines), hire experienced production manager from local cluster, or set up in-house facility in Sangareddy Industrial Estate with 2000-3000 sqft space.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME with district office in Sangareddy, obtain IEC code from DGFT Hyderabad office, register brand with IP India, get ISO 9001:2015 certification (₹3-5L), apply for Star House or Trading House status if export-focused.
5
First Sale
Target B2B via LinkedIn outreach to OEM procurement teams, list on Global Sources and Alibaba as verified supplier, attend aerospace/manufacturing expos (Aero India, EEPC India events), directly contact tier-1 automotive suppliers like Bosch, ZF, Mahindra & Mahindra procurement teams, establish payment terms with L/C options.
6
Scale
After 6 months, invest in ISO AS9100 and NADCAP certification for aerospace premium pricing (+15-20% margins), expand CNC capacity by leasing additional machines, hire export manager, establish distribution in 2-3 key export markets (Germany, Singapore), develop proprietary processes for higher-complexity components.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L grant for manufacturing setup in MSME category.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
cluster-level support for engineering hubs, up to ₹2Cr for Sangareddy cluster infrastructure.
🏚
Telangana MSME Development Scheme
15% subsidy on plant & machinery for manufacturing sector up to ₹50L.
🏚
Telangana Export Promotion Policy
5-10% reimbursement on export value for registered units, custom duty exemptions on imported raw materials for export-oriented manufacturing.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
