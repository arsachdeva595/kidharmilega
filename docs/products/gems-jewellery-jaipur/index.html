Gems & Jewellery ki
opportunity, Jaipur mein.

Jaipur has been a gemstone and jewellery trading and manufacturing center for over 300 years with an established ecosystem of skilled artisans, cutters, polishers, and designers. The district hosts the largest concentration of gems and jewellery units in India, with integrated supply chains for sourcing, cutting, designing, and exporting. Government infrastructure support and clusters like Jaipur Gems and Jewellery SEZ provide ready access to skilled labor and market networks.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Jaipur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Jaipur Gems and Jewellery SEZ and the main gem markets in C-Scheme, M.I. Road, and Gopal Pura Bypass. Spend 2-3 weeks understanding pricing, supplier networks, and artisan rates. Connect with 5-10 established jewellers to learn production timelines, quality standards, and export requirements. Attend GJF (Gems and Jewellery Federation) events in Jaipur.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source rough stones from Jaipur's wholesale gem dealers in M.I. Road market (₹2-5L first batch for trial designs). Identify 2-3 trusted stone cutters and polishers in Jaipur (rates: ₹50-200 per carat depending on stone). Establish accounts with precious metal suppliers in Jaipur for gold/silver (₹50-100K first purchase). Allocate ₹3-8L for initial gemstone and metal inventory.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established artisan workshops in Jaipur's Govind Nagar and Brahmpuri localities rather than setting up your own initially. Negotiate production agreements with 2-3 master craftspeople on piece-rate basis (₹500-2000 per piece depending on complexity). Ensure artisans have GIA/IGL certification access for gemstone authentication. Start with 50-100 designs; let artisans guide feasibility.
4
Brand & Register
Register as Udyam (Micro/Small enterprise) via udyamregistration.gov.in. Obtain IEC code from DGFT for export eligibility. Get GST registration (18% applicable on jewellery). Register trademark for brand name with IP India (₹10K-15K). Get ISO 9001 certification from reputable body for quality assurance (₹50K-100K, optional but strengthens B2B sales).
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy and Amazon India with 20-30 hero products in first month. Build WhatsApp Business catalog for bulk order inquiries from local retailers. Attend Jaipur Gem and Jewellery Fair (annual event) to showcase designs to domestic and international buyers. Email outreach to 500+ jewelry retailers in metros with catalog and wholesale pricing. Launch Instagram with 3 posts weekly showing design-making process and finished pieces.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand product range based on customer feedback (add semi-precious lines, costume jewellery). Launch second revenue stream: corporate gifting for 100+ corporate clients via LinkedIn. Hire 1-2 full-time designers and open small showroom in Jaipur's main gem market or metro city. Apply for SFURTI cluster support for upgrading production capacity. Target export markets by registering with GemsValley or similar B2B platforms; aim for ₹50L+ monthly export orders by month 18.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Yojana
₹3L interest-free loan + ₹1.5L toolkit grant for skilled artisans and jewelers engaged in traditional crafts
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L loan with 35% subsidy for manufacturing of gems and jewellery with Udyam registration
🏚
Rajasthan Small Industries Facilitation Council (RSIFC) subsidy
20% capital subsidy on plant and machinery for small enterprises in gems and jewellery sector, max ₹10L
🏚
Rajasthan Investment Promotion Scheme
GST refund on exports and concessional power tariffs for registered manufacturing units in Jaipur district
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
