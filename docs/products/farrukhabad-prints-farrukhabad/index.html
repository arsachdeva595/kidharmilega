Farrukhabad Prints ki
opportunity, Farrukhabad mein.

Farrukhabad district (2181 sq km, 1.88M people) sits 285km southeast of Delhi on the Ganga plains, making it the nerve center of India's hand block printing. Cotton and silk arrive directly from Punjab/UP textile zones within 200km. The old city cluster alone has 800+ active block-printing units and 3500+ trained block carvers and printers. Buyers from Delhi fashion district, Mumbai exporters, and London/NYC retailers source directly from Farrukhabad workshops.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Farrukhabad ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Farrukhabad old city main printing cluster (Shahi Bazar, Nai Basti area) — walk through 5-6 working units, talk to block printers for 2-3 hours, understand hand-stamping flow and block costs. Call Farrukhabad District Industries Centre (DIČ office) for names of 3-4 suppliers. Register free on Udyam portal using Aadhaar and GST (if existing business). Zero cost, 1 day on ground.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source 50 pre-carved wood blocks from Patel Block Works or Sharma Block Carving (both in Nai Basti, Farrukhabad) at ₹800-1500/block = ₹40K-75K. Source 100kg cotton fabric from Chandni Fabrics or textile wholesalers on MI Road, Farrukhabad at ₹150-180/meter. 50 meters minimum first batch = ₹7500-9000. Natural dyes from Bhagwat Dyes (local, ₹2K-5K for 5kg setup). Total material: ₹50K-90K.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 500-800 sqft printing unit in Farrukhabad cluster (Shahi Bazar area) at ₹8K-12K/month with water access and open floor. Hire 2 experienced block printers (₹12K-15K/month each). Buy drying racks, stamping tables, washing area basics (₹30K-50K one-time). Partner with 1-2 freelance block carvers for custom design blocks at ₹5K-10K per block. Output: 50-100 printed meters/day at 60% gross margin after materials.
4
Brand & Register
File Udyam (free, online, 5 minutes). Register GST (₹0, online). Apply for GI mark usage certificate from DIČ (₹2000-5000, 15 days). Get IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) from DGFT Lucknow office for export (₹0, 3-5 days online). Get FSSAI if selling food packaging prints (₹2000-5000, optional). Cost: ₹4K-10K, timeline: 30 days.
5
First Sale
Upload 10-15 product photos to Etsy shop (free listing, 3% + ₹20 per sale commission) and Meesho B2C (commission 20-30%). First order target: 5 salwar suit sets or 20 meters fabric = ₹8K-15K revenue. Post 5-10 Instagram reels of block printing process weekly. Reach out to 50 boutique owners in Delhi NCR via WhatsApp with catalog. Expected first month: ₹30K-50K revenue at current margins.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (250+ orders), hire 2 more printers, invest ₹2L in washing/drying setup. Partner with 5-10 regional wholesalers. List on Amazon Artisan or Flipkart Handmade (20-25% commission). Attend textile trade fairs (India Handicraft, Handloom Expo in Delhi) quarterly. Build custom design capability for B2B clients. Consider 2-3 apprentices for block carving to create in-house block bank.

Where to Set Up

Infrastructure ready hai — plot lo aur shuru karo

Farrukhabad MSME Cluster (Shahi Bazar, Nai Basti)
800+ printing and block-carving units concentrated in 2 sq km of old city. Water from Ganga canal, electricity via state grid, zero waiting period for small rentals. Typical 500 sqft unit ₹8K-12K/month. Supplier ecosystem for blocks, dyes, fabric fully embedded.
MSME Cluster
Farrukhabad Industrial Area (Bypass Road)
Newer industrial zone with 20-30 larger printing units and dye houses, plots available 1000-5000 sqft, power and water assured, ₹150-250/sqft lease. Better for scaled production (500+ meters/day) but far from traditional artisan cluster.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
₹15,000 cash + ₹1,20,000 collateral-free loan + subsidy for tools and training for traditional artisans
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25% subsidy for women entrepreneurs, 15% for general, up to ₹25L loan amount, interest subvention 5-6%
🏚
UP Handloom Sector Incentive Scheme
10% subsidy on machinery purchase up to ₹10L for registered units
🏚
ODOP Fund
dedicated working capital and marketing support for GI-tagged products, access to UP government exhibitions
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Frequently Asked Questions

Seedhe sawaal, seedhe jawab

₹2 lakh se ₹8 lakh — rent, initial blocks, fabric stock, dyes. ₹3-4 lakh par ho jayega entry-level (500 sqft space, 50 blocks, 2 printers)। Loan ₹1-2 lakh ke liye apply kar sakte ho PMEGP ya PM Vishwakarma se।
Bilkul zaroori nahi। Farrukhabad mein 800+ units hain — agar tu 3 mahine block printers ke paas baithega toh learn ho jayega। Lekin printer hire karte waqt experience wala chahiye। Apne likhne ke liye designer experience na ho toh bhi chalega — existing designs se start kar।
PM Vishwakarma se ₹15K cash + ₹1.2L loan। PMEGP se 15-25% subsidy on capital। ODOP fund access for GI products — marketing exhibitions free। UP Handloom Scheme se 10% machinery subsidy। Kul mein ₹50K-1L tak directly mil sakta hai direct subsidy।
Etsy aur Amazon Handmade pe 2-3 mahine mein orders aane lagenge। Delhi-Noida ke boutiques ko WhatsApp catalog bhej — response rate 3-5%। Sadar Bazaar wholesalers mein walk-in करke ₹5K-10K ka first order mil jayega। Instagram par 500 followers tak pehle 1-2 mahine lage, phir orders badhne lagte hain।
Bilkul, GI tag hai isliye premium price milega। IEC lelo (free, 3 days), GST lelo (free, online), aur direct ship kar India Post or DHL se USA-UK-Germany mein। Unit cost ₹200-300 export karte waqt profit ₹100-150 per piece, jo domestic se 2x zyada hai। Lekin pehle domestic order 2-3 mahine lelo, tab export start kar।
Block printing mein risk kam hai kyunki artisans hamesha kaam layenge aur blocks ki value baaki rahega। Worst case tu printer ko hire kar aur dusre trader ko supply kar — margin 10-15% hoga lekin loss nahi। Fabric paisa barabar chla jayega। Real risk ye hai order na aaye — issliye Etsy + WhatsApp dono channels zaroori hain।
