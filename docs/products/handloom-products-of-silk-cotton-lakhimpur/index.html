Handloom Products of Silk & Cotton ki
opportunity, Lakhimpur mein.

Lakhimpur lies in upper Assam's sericulture belt where Eri and Muga silk cocoons grow wild and are cultivated by local farmers, reducing raw material costs by 25-35% vs importing. The district has an organized Handloom Department and approximately 400-500 active artisans concentrated in Dhemaji, North Lakhimpur, and Lakhimpur town clusters. Buyer access spans direct-to-consumer via Etsy, Flipkart Handloom, Amazon Handmade, and wholesale to premium Indian retailers (Fabindia, Nalli, regional boutiques) plus European wholesalers via Alibaba and ThomasNet.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Lakhimpur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Dhemaji weaving cluster (25 km from Lakhimpur town) to meet 3-5 master weavers; call the Lakhimpur District Handloom Department (+91-9435245xxx range) to understand current subsidy schemes; register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in); attend the weekly Thursday market in North Lakhimpur to see wholesale pricing in action — all doable this week, zero cost.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw Eri and Muga silk from Dhemaji cocoon farmers (₹400-600/kg raw silk) or contact Assam State Silk Board agents in Lakhimpur; establish relationship with 2-3 dyers in North Lakhimpur (natural dyes cost ₹80-150/kg fabric); minimum first batch of 50 meters (₹8000-12000 at wholesale rates) to test product-market fit before scaling.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 1-2 established weavers in Dhemaji or North Lakhimpur cluster on revenue-sharing (50-60% of selling price to weaver) rather than renting a loom initially; this preserves ₹60K-120K capex; weavers will handloom your designs at ₹200-350/meter labor cost; alternatively rent a 2-loom unit in North Lakhimpur MSME cluster for ₹8000-12000/month if targeting higher volume (100+ meters/month).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 10 minutes online); apply for PM Vishwakarma scheme within 6 months (₹15K tooling support for artisans you partner with); file GI registration application through Ministry of Textiles GI cell (₹3500 filing fee, 18-24 month approval, positions brand as authentic); obtain IEC (Importer Exporter Code, free via dgft.gov.in) within 2 weeks if planning export; get ISI cotton/silk certification (₹5000-8000, optional but increases wholesale appeal).
5
First Sale
First sale: post 5-8 high-quality lifestyle photos on Instagram, tag Indian fashion micro-influencers (50K-200K followers), link to Etsy store; expect first order 2-3 weeks after launch, typical size ₹2000-4000 (1-2 sarees or 3-4 dupattas); simultaneously pitch 10-15 premium online boutiques (email + samples) — 2-3 will order ₹15K-40K bulk; first month realistic revenue ₹25K-50K if executing both channels.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 dedicated customer service person (₹8000/month); engage 5-8 weavers full-time instead of freelance; invest ₹40K in professional photography and video content; apply for SFURTI cluster subsidy (50% capex support up to ₹25L) if scaling production; exhibit at India Handloom Expo (Delhi, biannual) and Sonar Sansar (Mumbai, annual); start WhatsApp bulk-order fulfillment system; consider opening small showroom in Lakhimpur town (₹15K-25K/month rent) as confidence grows.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹15,000 tooling/equipment support per artisan, 5% interest subvention on MUDRA loans up to ₹2 lakh; covers weavers you partner with
🏛
SFURTI (Amended)
50% subsidy on capex (machinery, infrastructure) up to ₹25 lakh for clusters with 5+ artisans; Dhemaji and North Lakhimpur clusters eligible
🏚
Assam Handloom Development and Marketing Society (AHDMS) subsidy
₹5000-8000 per loom modernization, ₹10000 to weavers for raw material pre-financing; apply via District Handloom Department
🏚
Assam Enterprise Assistance Scheme
5% subsidy on plant and machinery for MSME entrepreneurs in handicrafts, up to ₹5 lakh per enterprise; apply via District Industries Center, Lakhimpur
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
