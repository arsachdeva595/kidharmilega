Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Umaria ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Umaria's sal forest clusters in Bandhya, Ghat, and Naigarh blocks. Connect with NTPC-supported mahua cooperatives and local tribal groups. Attend the Umaria Mahua Fair (seasonal) and interact with established processors in Umaria city to understand grading, pricing, and market linkages.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from tribal mahua collectors in Umaria district during May-June flowering season at ₹40-60 per kg fresh flowers. Partner with TRIFED (Tribal Cooperative Marketing Development Federation) for verified supply. Budget ₹1-2 lakhs for first 500kg batch from local harvesters in Bandhya or Ghat blocks.
3
Produce / Partner
Start with micro-processing units (legal min. ₹3-5L investment for oil extraction equipment). Partner with existing mahua processors in Umaria to outsource initial production under SFURTI schemes. Alternatively, work with registered tribal cooperatives like Umaria Mahua Sangh who have food safety certifications and cold-chain facilities.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam via udyamregistration.gov.in as Food Processing MSE. Obtain IEC from nearest APEDA office (Indore, ~150km away). Get FSSAI license for oil/food products (₹5,000-15,000). Register trade mark with IP India. Partner with certification bodies for Organic/Fair Trade tags.
5
First Sale
List on Amazon Pantry, Bigbasket, and Blinkit as premium organic oil. Create D2C via Shopify targeting health & cosmetics communities. Supply to local ayurveda brands and organic cosmetics startups. Approach exporters in Bangalore/Mumbai for international market entry. Use LinkedIn for B2B cosmetics sourcing. Target GCCs and naturopathy institutes.
6
Scale
Achieve ₹50L revenue by Month 12 via B2B supply scaling. Develop branded cosmetics line (soap, balm) for D2C expansion. Establish forward contracts with tribal collectors to secure 2-3 tons annual supply. Add mahua wine production under excise licensing. Explore export partnerships with EU/US importers; pursue organic certification by Month 14. Hire 3-5 staff by Month 16.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (35% for General, 45% for SC/ST) for food processing units in Umaria
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
cluster-level support up to ₹10L per unit for mahua processing clusters in Umaria
🏚
Mukhya Mantri Kanya Vivah Yojana (MP State)
tax incentives for food processing MSEs in Tier 2/3 districts like Umaria
🏚
MP ODOP Promotion Scheme
direct subsidy up to ₹5L and market linkage support for registered ODOP producers in Umaria district
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
