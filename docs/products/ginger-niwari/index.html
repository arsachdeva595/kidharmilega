Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Niwari ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit ginger cultivation clusters in Niwari district near Niwari town and Sanchi region; connect with local farmer cooperatives and APEDA; study export standards from AgriExport India database; attend spice trade shows in Kochi and Delhi
2
Source Raw Materials
Source quality ginger rhizomes from certified seed suppliers in Niwari district (₹8,000-12,000/quintal); negotiate direct farmer contracts for 5-10 quintal first batch (₹40,000-60,000); secure organic certification seeds if pursuing premium segment
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 established organic ginger farmers in Niwari for contract farming; invest in small-scale drying and powder-making unit (₹3-5L); use traditional methods; ensure organic certification through APEDA-accredited agencies
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Micro enterprise) online; obtain IEC code from DGFT for export eligibility; apply for organic certification from APEDA within 6 months; register brand name with IP office; design packaging aligned with organic/premium positioning
5
First Sale
Launch on BigBasket, Blinkit, Natureloc, and other organic marketplaces (₹5-10K onboarding); reach out to Ayurveda retailers and organic stores in metros; join AgriExport India trade portal; attend regional spice expos; build WhatsApp B2B sales for bulk orders
6
Scale
Expand product line to ginger powder, pickled ginger, ginger candy; target bulk B2B orders from food companies; secure export contracts through APEDA; establish cold storage facility; scale to 20-50 quintal annual production within 18 months
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L loan at 0-5% interest for agricultural enterprises
🏛
APEDA Export Promotion
market access support, quality certification subsidies, and trade show participation grants for agricultural exports
🏚
Madhya Pradesh Organic Farming Mission
₹8,000-10,000/hectare subsidy and certification cost reimbursement
🏚
Mukhyamantri Kisan Kalyan Yojana
income support and crop insurance for registered farmers producing organic produce
