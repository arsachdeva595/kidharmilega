opportunity, Amroha mein.

Amroha (population 692k) lies 100km northeast of Delhi NCR in Uttar Pradesh's Jat-dominant region, with raw material access to goat skin from Meerut-Bareilly slaughterhouses (40km radius) and sheesham wood from Uttarakhand timber merchants. The Dholak cluster is geographically concentrated in central Amroha city with 150+ registered artisan households producing 40-60 units daily. Primary buyers include Delhi-NCR wedding musicians (Oct-Mar peak season), North Indian classical music institutes, and international buyers from USA, UK, Canada, Germany accessing the cluster via Udyog Bhawan platforms and GI certification credibility.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Amroha ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Amroha city's main bazaar (near Clock Tower) this week — no cost. Spend 2 hours meeting 3-4 established dholak makers (ask local tea vendors for names), ask them: raw material costs, daily production, buyer contacts, training timeline. Call Meerut leather market suppliers (8km away) to confirm goat skin pricing. Join Amroha ODOP WhatsApp group (ask district MSME office). Zero spend, maximum intel.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from Meerut leather merchants (40km): goat skin ₹80-150/sqft, buffalo leather ₹60-120/sqft, sheesham wood blocks ₹200-400/kg from Uttarakhand timber dealers in Delhi NCR. First batch of 10 dholaks needs ₹25,000-35,000 raw material cost. Find suppliers via Meerut Leather Market Association (contact: Meerut MSME cluster office) or ask your artisan mentor.
3
Produce / Partner
Apprentice under an established master artisan in Amroha for 6-8 weeks (free/₹5,000 honorarium) to learn wood shaping, skin stretching, tuning. After that, either: (A) Rent a 150-sqft workshop in Amroha city center (₹3,000-5,000/month) and produce solo, or (B) Partner with artisan for revenue share (30-40% of retail price). Solo setup: ₹150,000 (3-month rent, tools, initial raw materials). Partnership setup: ₹50,000-80,000.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Ministry MSME portal, free, 5 mins online). File for GI protection use under your brand name (₹5,000-8,000 legal fee, 30 days). Apply for IEC code from DGFT if exporting (₹0, 15 days online). Register business name at district magistrate office (₹500, 5 days). Total: ₹6,000-8,500, 45 days.
5
First Sale
Week 1: List 3 samples on Etsy, Flipkart, Amazon Handmade (free listings). Week 2: Contact 5 music retailers in Delhi NCR via WhatsApp with price list + photos. Week 3: Reach out to wedding drummer networks in Delhi via community groups. Expected first month: 2-4 retail orders (₹10,000-20,000 revenue) + 1 wholesale inquiry (₹15,000-25,000). Use Udyog Bhawan.gov.in free buyer-seller portal to register.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 2 artisan partners (revenue share model), expand workshop to 400-500 sqft (₹8,000-12,000/month). Invest ₹50,000 in professional photography/videography for YouTube demos. Exhibit at IITF Delhi (Nov), Handicrafts Expos in Mumbai (Feb). Register for SFURTI cluster development scheme to access ₹50-100L subsidy for workshop setup. Build 3-person team: 1 lead artisan, 1 sales + export, 1 admin.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
₹15,000 advance to artisans + 5% interest subsidy on MUDRA loans up to ₹5L, processing time 30 days
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (25% for general, 35% for SC/ST) for manufacturing setup, processing time 45-60 days
🏚
Uttar Pradesh One District One Product (ODOP) Scheme
50% subsidy on raw materials for GI-registered products, up to ₹2L/artisan, quarterly disbursement
🏚
UP MSME Cluster Development Scheme (SFURTI variant)
₹50-100L for cluster infrastructure, common facility center for 10-15 artisans, application via district MSME office
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
