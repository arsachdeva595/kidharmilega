Musical Instruments (Dholak) ki
opportunity, Amroha mein.

Amroha has an unbroken 500-year legacy of dholak craftsmanship with family-run workshops scattered across the old city. The region's access to premium goatskin from Rajasthan and locally sourced mango and sheesham wood, combined with generations of collective knowledge, makes it the epicenter of authentic dholak production in India.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Amroha ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit the Amroha old city dholak clusters (Naya Bazar, Jami Masjid area) and spend 2-3 weeks learning from master artisans like those in the Bansal family workshops. Document production techniques, quality grading, and pricing from 5-7 established makers. Connect with Amroha Chamber of Commerce for market intelligence.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source premium goat skin from leather suppliers in Kanpur (₹8,000-12,000 per hide for 5 hides). Procure aged mango/sheesham wood from timber merchants in Saharanpur (₹25,000-35,000 for first 50 kg batch). Budget ₹50,000-70,000 for initial raw material stock for 20-25 trial pieces.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 established Amroha dholak artisans as production partners on commission basis (8-12% per piece), or lease a 200 sq ft workshop space near Jami Masjid cluster (₹3,000-5,000/month) and train under a master craftsman. Aim to produce 15-20 quality pieces per month in year 1.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) online for ₹0, apply for IEC code via DGFT portal (₹200). Register GI-certified brand name (e.g., 'Amroha Heritage Dholak') at IPAB. Get FSSAI clearance if selling food-related products; obtain Geological Indication certificate photocopy for marketing.
5
First Sale
Launch Etsy shop (2.5% commission + 3% payment fee) with 50+ high-quality photos and 10-minute demo videos. Simultaneously pitch to Baluji Shrivastav (world music distributor, NY), SoundMax (India's largest music retailer), and music colleges in Delhi/Mumbai. Run Instagram content campaign around artisan stories and musician testimonials.
6
Scale
After 6 months, hire 1-2 additional trained artisans to increase monthly output to 40-50 pieces. Expand to Amazon Global and Amazon India with sponsored listings. Approach 20+ music schools in metro cities with bulk pricing (₹4,500-6,500/piece). Launch YouTube channel with musician collaborations. Participate in World Music Expo (WOMEX) in Berlin for B2B export visibility.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma
up to ₹15,000 grant + 5% interest subsidy on loans up to ₹1 Lakh for artisans and craftspeople
🏛
PMEGP
up to ₹25 Lakh loan (80% subsidy for SC/ST, 35% for general) for manufacturing MSMEs with minimum 25% margin contribution
🏚
Uttar Pradesh ODOP Scheme
100% subsidy for registration, certification, and branding of GI-tagged products; free participation in 5 state trade fairs annually
🏚
UP MSE Cluster Development Scheme
up to ₹50 Lakh for common facility centers, tool rooms, and training centers in Amroha dholak cluster
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
