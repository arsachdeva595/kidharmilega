Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Daman ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Daman's Madhupark Industrial Estate and Nani Daman manufacturing clusters; interview 5-7 existing plastic manufacturers to understand production processes, raw material sourcing (Ahmedabad, Vadodara, Surat), machinery suppliers, and market pricing; attend plastic industry trade shows at Gandhinagar or VAPI.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source virgin plastic pellets (PP/HDPE) from suppliers in Ahmedabad (Urvashi Polymers, Arora Polymers) and Surat; order injection molding machines (₹8-15L used, ₹20-30L new) from machinery dealers in Rajkot or Ahmedabad; first batch raw material cost: ₹3-5L for production setup.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing Daman plastic units for contract manufacturing initially (avoid heavy capex); or lease 2,000-3,000 sq ft factory space in Madhupark Estate (₹1-2L/month); hire experienced mold designers and machine operators from local labor pool; invest in 2-3 injection molding machines and extrusion units.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME (online at udyamregistration.gov.in); obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office (Daman or Gandhinagar); register business name with Registrar of Companies (Gujarat); apply for GST registration; create brand identity with simple, industrial design; register trademark for brand name.
5
First Sale
Approach automotive suppliers in Gujarat (Vadodara, Ahmedabad clusters) with product samples; list on IndiaMART and TradeKey for B2B orders; contact logistics and e-commerce warehousing companies (Amazon, Flipkart distribution centers); attend Plastindia or Plast India trade fairs; cold-call food processing units in nearby states.
6
Scale
After 6 months: add injection molding capacity or extrusion lines; develop 3-5 proprietary designs; establish contracts with 2-3 large anchor buyers (automotive Tier-1/2 suppliers); launch D2C channel via Shopify/Amazon for smaller consumer products; explore export markets through trade bodies like FICCI or PLEXCONCIL; invest in quality certifications (ISO 9001, BIS if applicable).
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy for manufacturing units with 25-35% promoter contribution; especially favorable for MSME in plastic manufacturing.
🏛
MUDRA Loan (under PM SVANidhi/PMMY)
up to ₹10L unsecured loan for MSME with minimal documentation; fast-track approval for existing manufacturers.
🏚
Dadra & Nagar Haveli-Daman & Diu Industrial Policy 2022
capital subsidy of 10-15% for new manufacturing units, infrastructure support, and utility cost concessions in notified zones.
🏚
Daman & Diu MSME Promotion Scheme
interest subsidy on bank loans up to 4% and machinery subsidy for plastic processing units in priority sectors.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
