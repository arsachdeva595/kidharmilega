Rampur district (2,367 sq km, 23.36L population) is located in central-western Uttar Pradesh on NH-119, 120 km from Delhi and 140 km from Lucknow — this positioning enables artisan clusters to access metro buyer markets within 4-6 hours. The region has a concentrated 5,000+ artisan base trained in Mughal-era applique and zari techniques, with mentha cultivation in adjacent Lucknow and Barabanki districts providing complementary raw material access. Current production volumes reach 300-400 embellished units daily, with 25-30% already flowing to export channels (US, UK, Germany, Australia).

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Rampur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit the applique artisan cluster in Rampur City (near Jama Masjid area) — spend a day talking to 5-6 established makers, attend the weekly Thursday market at Subhash Nagar to see finished goods pricing. Call the DC Handicraft office (0591-2320212) and ask for referrals to 3 cluster leaders. Download the ODOP official product sheet from rampur.nic.in. Zero cost, doable in 3 days.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source colored fabrics from Sadar Bazar (Rampur City center) — wholesale markets like Lal Ganj sell cotton/silk in bulk at ₹120-300 per meter depending on quality. Zari thread sourced from Delhi's Chandni Chowk (1-day trip, ₹8-15 per roll for metallic). Patch materials from local waste fabric dealers in Mandi area. First batch (500 units' materials): ₹25-40K for applique pieces, ₹15-20K for zari stock.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent a 600-800 sq ft workshop in Subhash Nagar cluster (₹8-12K/month) and hire 3-4 direct artisans at ₹400-600/day piecework. Option B: Partner with 8-10 artisans working from homes (consignment model), supply materials, pay ₹300-500 per finished piece. Setup cost for Option A: ₹1.5-2L (deposit + cutting table + storage); Option B: ₹50-80K (material buffer only). Production capacity: 40-60 pieces/day in Option A, 80-120/day in Option B.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, 15 mins, free) at udyamregistration.gov.in. Apply for GI mark usage via DC Handicraft office (already certified 2009, ₹0 usage cost). If exporting: obtain IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) from DGFT regional office in Lucknow (₹0, takes 5-7 days). Apply for UP ODOP Scheme: get ₹25,000 subsidy over 5 years via state govt (application at rampur.nic.in, needs Udyam + bank details, 60-90 days approval).
5
First Sale
First sales: Start on Instagram Reels (post 3-4 process videos showing hand-cutting, stitching, final product) + Etsy listing (20-30 SKUs). Target first order from local wedding decorators in Rampur/Delhi NCR via WhatsApp/direct outreach — ₹15-30K order typical. Expected first month: 3-5 orders = ₹30-75K revenue gross (after artisan payout: ₹18-45K net). Second month push to corporate gifting buyers in Delhi via email outreach + LinkedIn.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire a full-time quality controller (₹15-20K/month), a designs/sourcing person (₹12-18K/month), and move to 2-unit workshop (1,500 sq ft) in Subhash Nagar or nearby cluster. Expand artisan network to 25-30 makers. Exhibit at IITF Delhi (November, ₹40-60K stall cost), Craft Bazaar Mumbai (quarterly). Launch Instagram Stories + TikTok for faster reach. Approach Fab India, SJVN corporate buyers directly. Open a small showroom in Delhi Saket or Gurgaon (₹40-60K/month).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25% subsidy on equipment cost for manufacturing units up to ₹25L capital investment.
🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹15,000 tooling grant + 5% interest subsidy on loans up to ₹5L for artisans and craft entrepreneurs.
🏚
One District One Product Scheme (UP)
₹25,000 subsidy to local craftsmen and entrepreneurs over 5 years, launched 24 January 2018, expected to raise state GDP by 2%.
🏚
UP Handicraft Development Scheme
30% subsidy on workshop setup costs (rent/equipment) up to ₹3L for registered artisans, coordinated by DC Handicraft office.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
