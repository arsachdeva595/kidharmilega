Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — North Garo Hills ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Tura main vegetable market (3km from district HQ) on Wednesday and Saturday haats — talk to 3-5 banana wholesalers about their sourcing, margins, buyer list. Call Meghalaya Horticulture Development Society office in Shillong (0364-2223000) — ask about organic farmer clusters. Attend the next All-India Horticulture Congress or State Agriculture Produce Marketing Committee meeting (free to sit in). Zero cost.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from verified organic farmers in Garo Hill villages (Rongjeng, Gasuapara, Selsella areas) — contact via Meghalaya Organic Farming Society. First batch: 500kg minimum at ₹45-55/kg = ₹22.5-27.5K cost. Supplier names: Village-level producer groups (Gasuapara Banana Growers Cooperative established 2022). Verify organic cert via APEDA database or state horticulture dept.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a small 1,000 sq.ft. collection-and-packing unit in Tura town (₹8-12K/month) OR partner with 2-3 farmer producer groups on contract basis (zero rent, commission model 8-12%). Setup: banana ripening chamber (₹60K), washing-sorting table (₹15K), packaging (₹10K), small generator if power unstable (₹25K). Total setup: ₹110-150K for full unit, ₹30-40K for partnership model.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME online (free, 10 mins) — generates UDYAM number needed for all govt schemes. Apply for Organic Certification via APEDA-accredited certifier (Jaivik Haat, Northeast region office) — cost ₹8-12K, 3-month process. Register IEC code if exporting (DGFT portal, ₹0, 5 days). Register under One District One Product (ODOP) scheme for state-level promotion support. Get FPO registration if working with farmer groups (₹5-10K through district agricultural department).
5
First Sale
First sale: BigBasket Supplier onboarding (list products on their sourcing dashboard, first order typically 100-200kg at ₹95-110/kg retail cost, your payout ₹65-75/kg). OR Guwahati fruit wholesalers direct — 300kg trial at ₹60/kg = ₹18K first week revenue. OR PMSSY school procurement — submit tender to district education office (minimum 200kg/week supply). Expected first-month revenue: ₹45-70K gross from 600-800kg sales.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (est. 800-1000kg/week): hire a supply chain coordinator (₹20K/month), add 2-3 new farmer partnerships, lease a 2,000 sq.ft. climate-controlled storage (₹20K/month), apply for SFURTI cluster grant to upgrade equipment (₹25-40L subsidy for food processing if adding value-added products). Exhibit at Organic Produce Expos in Northeast region (Guwahati Food Fair, Shillong Agri Expo), target export agent relationships, consider certification for Fair Trade or Rainforest Alliance (add ₹15-20K premium per shipment).
