Banana and Banana Products ki
opportunity, Wokha mein.

Wokha district in Nagaland's Kohima zone sits at 1,400m elevation with subtropical conditions ideal for naturally sweet banana varieties; the 864-hectare cultivation base is densely clustered around Wokha town and Chümoukedima block, directly supplying 6,000 farmers to processing centers. Buyer markets are 90km to Dimapur (wholesale), 200km to Assam, and 300km to Kolkata via NH-39; ICAR-NRCB partnership at Wokha ensures quality tech transfer and certification pathways for export.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Wokha ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call ICAR-KVK Wokha (0369-2244092) to understand current banana varieties and processing specs. Visit Wokha town haat on Saturday to walk farmer supply chain and speak with 3-4 active banana traders. Attend the Wokha Banana Festival (Oct 22-24) to observe market, meet competitors, and see consumer demand live. Zero cost, do this within 2 weeks.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fresh bananas directly from Wokha and Chümoukedima block farmers via ICAR-KVK's farmer contact list or local farmers cooperative. First batch: 500kg fresh bananas @ ₹20-25/kg = ₹10-12.5K. Alternatively, engage Wokha Banana cooperative (if registered) for bulk at ₹18/kg. Supplier area: Wokha town radius 8km.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 400-500 sqft space in Wokha town (₹4-6K/month) with basic utilities and water connection. Set up a small banana chips frying unit (manual or semi-automatic fryer ₹60-80K), drying racks (₹8-10K), packaging station (₹5K). Partner with local SHGs or hire 2-3 women processors at ₹200-250/day. Total production capex: ₹180-250K for manual operation or ₹400-600K for semi-automated fryer.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online within 15 min). Obtain FSSAI license (₹5-8K, 20-30 days). Design packaging with 'Wokha Banana' reference per ODOP branding guidelines (design cost ₹5-10K). If exporting: obtain IEC from DGFT (₹0, online), register with APEDA (₹2K). Apply for GI certification support via One District One Product scheme (no direct cost, subsidy-backed process, 3-6 months). Total: ₹15-25K + 45 days.
5
First Sale
List on Amazon Karigar within 2 weeks of FSSAI approval. Prepare 50-100 units of banana chips (₹8-12 per unit cost, sell at ₹250-300). First order target: 10 units via marketplace + 5kg wholesale to local Wokha retailer = ₹4-5K first-month revenue. Simultaneously, approach ICAR-KVK to connect with institutional buyers (hotels in Dimapur).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: Upgrade to semi-automated fryer and drying tunnels (₹250-350K additional). Hire supervisor + 5-8 processors (₹15-20K/month wages). Add banana flour and stem-fiber products (new processing lines ₹80-150K). Apply for PMEGP subsidy (25-35% for SC/ST, 15% for general) for next phase capex. Exhibit at Wokha Banana Festival annually. Approach bulk buyers: Assam food chains, Kolkata exporters, ONDC wholesale partners.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
One District One Product (ODOP) scheme
25-35% subsidy on capex for SC/ST founders, certification and branding support included, ₹0-200K additional grant for processing unit setup.
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25-35% subsidy (SC/ST) or 15% (general), max ₹25L loan at 0% interest for first 12 months, processing unit eligibility confirmed.
🏚
Nagaland Agriculture Department Processing Subsidy
20-30% capex support for food processing units, eligibility ₹10-50L project cost, apply via district agriculture office.
🏚
Nagaland Startup Policy
Tax exemption and cheap land lease for MSME/startup registered within district, 5-year tax holiday if registered before December 2025.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

₹1.8-2.5L minimum for a small manual banana chips unit (fryer, drying racks, packaging, FSSAI). ₹4-6L if you want semi-automated setup with better quality. Loans available via PMEGP at 0% interest for 12 months; subsidies cover 15-35% cost depending on caste and ODOP scheme.
No formal experience needed, but you need willingness to learn. Attend a 2-3 day ICAR-KVK training (free or ₹500), observe existing banana chip makers in Wokha town (1-2 days), and partner with experienced SHG women if you're new. 14 farmers and 5 officials have already been trained at NRCB Tamil Nadu, so local knowledge exists.
ODOP scheme gives 25-35% capex subsidy for SC/ST, certification backing, and 'Wokha Banana' branding license. PMEGP gives 15-35% subsidy + 0% interest loan up to ₹25L for first year. ICAR-KVK provides free tech training and farmer contact lists. Nagaland Agriculture Dept adds 20-30% more capex subsidy on top. Total govt support can cover ₹5-8L of your ₹10-15L project cost.
Amazon Karigar and Flipkart Samarth are easiest—list in 2 weeks post-FSSAI, expect 5-10 orders in first month. Simultaneously, contact Dimapur retailers directly via WhatsApp (50-100kg bulk orders at wholesale rates). ICAR-KVK can introduce you to hotel chefs and ice cream makers in Assam. Local Wokha haat gives immediate cash sales (₹2-3K/week possible). Biggest quick win: approach Nagaland Tourism Authority for hotel supply contracts.
Haan, banana chips and flour export to Bangladesh and Bhutan is realistic within 6-12 months. You'll need: IEC (free, online), APEDA registration (₹2K), FSSAI export certification, and PhytoSanitary clearance (₹2-5K). Shipping to Dhaka costs ₹40-60/kg by road, FOB margin is 38-50%. ONDC is developing a cross-border pilot starting 2025. Start with 50-100kg test shipments to Dhaka traders; profit is ₹30-40/kg after all costs.
Honest downside: banana supply seasonality (May-Sept is low supply, Oct-Apr is peak) means revenue will fluctuate 40-50% month-to-month. If processing unit doesn't sell, you can pivot to fresh banana wholesale (lower margin 28-35% but faster turnover). Worst case: rent out your equipment to other processors (₹2-3K/month per item) while rebuilding. Exit option: sell customer list and brand name to an existing larger processor (₹5-10L value if you've built a 50+ buyer base). Total downside loss unlikely to exceed ₹3-4L if you exit in first 12 months.
