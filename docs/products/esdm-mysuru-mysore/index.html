Mysuru lies 140km south of Bangalore on NH44, offering access to Silicon Valley-grade talent at 40% lower costs while maintaining supply chain links to OEM automotive hubs in Tamil Nadu and Karnataka. The dedicated ESDM cluster in Mysuru hosts electronics system design, embedded automotive solutions, IoT/IIoT, 5G testing, and space tech manufacturing across 50+ registered players. Raw material sourcing (connectors, PCBs, semiconductors, sensors) is available from Bangalore suppliers (4-hour lead time) and Surat electronics component traders, with finished product buyers concentrated in automotive (Maruti Suzuki, Hyundai facilities within 200km), telecom (5G base stations), and defense procurement nodes.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Mysuru (Mysore) ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Mysuru ESDM cluster (address: request from Mysuru district MSME office, Irwin Road); call 3 existing players listed on IESA directory (iesa.org.in); attend next Karnataka ESDM meet (check KDEM calendar); register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) — all doable Week 1 at ₹0
2
Source Raw Materials
Source PCB substrates and connectors from Bangalore electronics suppliers (Girdhar Electronics, Sensormatic, local distributors in Whitefield — ₹1-2L first batch); embedded processors from Arrow Electronics or ScanSource via IEC account; sensors and wireless modules from Heilind Electronics or local authorized distributors. Lead time: 2-4 weeks for first batch depending on volume.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 2,000-5,000 sqft light industrial unit in Mysuru ESDM cluster (₹8-15/sqft/month = ₹1.6-7.5L annually) or partner with existing contract manufacturers in cluster for first 1,000 units (outsource at 15-20% markup, no capex); buy soldering, testing, and assembly equipment (₹8-20L for 50-unit/day capacity). Total initial production setup: ₹15-30L.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 15 mins); apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) from nearest DGFT office Bangalore (₹0, 2-3 weeks) if planning export; obtain ISO 9001:2015 certification (₹3-5L, 4-6 weeks) for B2B automotive contracts; explore GI registration for 'Mysuru ESDM' cluster products (₹50K-2L govt support via DPIIT scheme); file GST registration (free, online). Total compliance cost: ₹4-8L, timeline 3 months.
5
First Sale
Cold-email Tier-1 automotive Tier-2 suppliers (Bosch, Motherson Sumi, Lear, Flex) with technical specs and sample units; list IoT products on Amazon Launchpad (₹50K annual fee, 2-week approval); reach out to telecom integrators via LinkedIn (5G testing modules). First order target: ₹5-25L from single automotive OEM or 50-200 units of IoT product on Flipkart. Expected first-month revenue: ₹10-50L depending on segment.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 2-3 design engineers, 1 supply chain manager, 1 quality lead; move to dedicated 5,000-10,000 sqft manufacturing unit (₹25-40L annual rent + ₹10-20L equipment capex); apply for MSME subsidy (PMEGP 25% subsidy up to ₹25L); exhibit at Electronics and IT Hardware Show (EITHW), InnoSpace conferences; build direct relationships with OEM procurement teams; consider contract manufacturing for Fortune 500 tech companies entering India (Apple, Intel, Qualcomm supplier networks).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25% subsidy up to ₹25 lakh for SC/ST/OBC founders, 15% for general; funds for machinery, working capital, and infrastructure
🏛
Udyam Registration + MSME benefits
80% credit guarantee under CGTMSE, preferential procurement policy, exemption from tender deposits, collateral-free loans up to ₹1 crore via MUDRA/Priority Sector
🏚
Karnataka Electronics Development Fund (KEDF)
₹2-5L grants for startups in semiconductor design, IoT hardware, and EV component manufacturing; apply via KDEM
🏚
Karnataka IT/BT/ST Department
Capital subsidy up to 25% for electronics manufacturing units; eligible for SFURTI cluster development funds; check with Mysuru District Industries Centre
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
