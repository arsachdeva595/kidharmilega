Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Mysuru (Mysore) ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Mysuru's IT parks (Infosys Mysuru Campus, TCS facility) and electronics clusters in Belagavi region. Network with NASSCOM members, attend ESDM seminars at Mysuru Chamber of Commerce, study DPIIT reports on electronics manufacturing policy.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source electronic components from distributors in Bangalore's Electronics City (2-3 hrs away) like Sensormatic, Premier Farnell; software libraries from AWS/Azure Marketplace; allocate ₹20-50L for initial infrastructure including servers, development tools, and compliance certifications.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing electronics manufacturers in Mysuru or establish a small development center. Register with MeitY for electronics manufacturing benefits. Hire 5-8 technical professionals (₹30-40L annually) and establish quality control protocols aligned with ISO 27001 and IEC standards.
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam (MSE portal), obtain IEC code from DGFT for export readiness, secure ISO 27001 for cybersecurity, register trademark on Ipindia.gov.in, open GSTIN with 18% HSN codes for software and electronics.
5
First Sale
Launch on B2B platforms (IndiaStack, TechVendor, Nasscom portal), pitch to government agencies through e-procurement portals (GeM), create LinkedIn B2B marketing campaign, establish partnerships with system integrators in Bangalore and Mysuru.
6
Scale
Expand product portfolio to adjacent areas (IoT, edge computing), establish regional offices in Bangalore and Pune, apply for DSIR/MeitY grants, pursue exports through FIEO or VCCI networks, scale team to 30-50 employees within 18 months.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L loan at subsidized rates for ESDM startups in manufacturing or software.
🏛
MeitY Startups Scheme
funding, mentorship, and market access for electronics and software startups with technology innovation focus.
🏚
Karnataka Startup Policy
grants up to ₹1Cr for tech startups; tax incentives and lab access through Bangalore Bioinnovation Centre.
🏚
Electronics Manufacturing Promotion Scheme
subsidies for setting up electronics manufacturing units; support from KCCI and industrial corridors.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
