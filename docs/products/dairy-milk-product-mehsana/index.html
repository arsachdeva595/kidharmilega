Dairy/ Milk Product ki
opportunity, Mehsana mein.

Mehsana district produces milk at scale with 304 million bovines (199.1 million cattle, 105.3 million buffaloes) across a 4,470 km² area, creating unmatched raw material proximity for dairy processors. The district's 96,000 registered dairy cooperatives and 170 milk producers' unions form a buyer-supplier ecosystem with pre-built logistics into major Indian cities. National dairy sector contributes 15% of GNI and grows 5% annually, making bulk buyers (FMCG platforms, export agents, institutional chains) active in Mehsana year-round.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Mehsana ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Mehsana cooperative milk union office (Mehsana City, near collectorate), call 3-5 local dairy suppliers to understand milk procurement cost (₹18-25/liter in Mehsana vs ₹28-35/liter in metros), attend Anand cooperative haat. Register free Udyam profile on udyamregistration.gov.in. Zero cost, do this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw milk from Mehsana district dairy cooperatives (avg. ₹20/liter bulk, min. 100L/day = ₹2,000/day). Suppliers cluster in Mehsana City, Kadi, Unjha tehsils. Rennet and starter cultures from Bangalore biotech suppliers (₹800-1,200/kg, 10-15 day lead time). First month raw material cost: ₹60,000-₹120,000.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 1,200-2,000 sq ft FSSAI-approved dairy processing unit in Mehsana MSME cluster or Dairy Park Kadi (₹12,000-₹25,000/month). Buy pasteurizer (₹2.5-5L), paneer/ghee making equipment (₹1.5-3L), cold storage (₹1-2L). Partner with local artisan dairy units on profit-sharing if capital is constrained (30-40% revenue split). Total first-batch setup: ₹8-25L over 60 days.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 24 hours online). Apply for FSSAI Food License (₹5,000-₹10,000, 20-30 days). If exporting: IEC registration via DGFT (₹1,000, 5 days), ISO 22000 certification (₹15,000-₹30,000, 30 days). GI registration not applicable to Mehsana dairy per ODOP data. Total compliance cost: ₹21,000-₹50,000 + 45 days.
5
First Sale
First sale via Blinkit Seller Hub or BigBasket — list 3 SKUs (ghee 500ml, paneer 250g, buttermilk 1L). Minimum first order likely 50-100 units. Expected first-month revenue: ₹80,000-₹150,000 (if 2-3 repeat buyers across platforms). Use WhatsApp to contact 5-10 wholesale distributors in Ahmedabad simultaneously for faster first wholesale order (₹2-5L order typical).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 1 procurement manager (₹15,000/month) and 1 sales executive (₹12,000/month). Apply for PMEGP subsidy (₹10L loan, ₹2.5L subsidy) to upgrade equipment. Exhibit at Ahmedabad Food & Beverage Expo (Jan-Feb) and Vibrant Gujarat (biennial). Expand to 2-3 regional distributors. Target ₹25L/month in 12 months via multi-platform D2C + wholesale hybrid.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
₹10 lakh collateral-free loan, ₹2.5 lakh subsidy (25% for general category), payback 84 months at 5-8% interest via SIDBI
🏛
MUDRA Loan (Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana)
₹10 lakh unsecured working capital loan at 8-12% interest, zero subsidy, 5-year tenure via bank
🏚
Gujarat One District One Product Scheme
exemption from state GST on exported dairy (15-18% savings), priority land allotment in dairy parks at ₹25-50/sq ft vs ₹80-120/sq ft market rate
🏚
Gujarat MSME Subsidy for Food Processing
₹5 lakh capital subsidy (25% of equipment cost) for dairy units, application via GSIDC (Gujarat State Industrial Development Corporation)
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
