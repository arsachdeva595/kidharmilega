Bomkai Saree & Fabrics ki
opportunity, Sonepur (Subarnapur) mein.

Sonepur district in western Odisha, 270km from Bhubaneswar, sits where cotton grows in local fields and imported silk threads arrive via Raipur road. The three named clusters (Sonepur, Subarnapur, Kantapalli) employ 800-1200 active weavers producing 250-350 sarees daily using 600-year-old Jala hand-design technique. Sarees retail for ₹8,000-₹40,000 in metros; export FOB ₹4,500-₹18,000 to North America, Europe, and Japan.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sonepur (Subarnapur) ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call Sonepur and Subarnapur haat organizers (search Google: 'Sonepur weekly saree haat' or ask district textile office). Visit Kantapalli cluster on a Thursday — walk the weavers' lanes, see 3-4 looms, ask pricing on 5-yard sample bolts. Zero cost, meet 5-6 artisans face-to-face, understand their current buyer channels. Do this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from Sonepur cluster artisans directly or middlemen in Subarnapur town (ask cluster shopkeepers). First batch: 10-15 sarees at ₹3,500-₹6,500/piece depending on silk content (cotton ₹3.5K, silk-cotton blend ₹5K, pure silk ₹8K+). Total first batch cost: ₹35,000-₹100,000. Payment: cash, 50% advance to artisan.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Commission artisans (fastest, zero capex) — pay per saree, ₹0 setup. Option B: Rent 800sqft workshop in Sonepur or Subarnapur town (₹3,000-₹8,000/month), hire 2-3 weavers + 1 dye-hand, ₹180K-₹300K initial (loom rental ₹500-₹1,500/loom/month, weaver wage ₹8,000-₹12,000/month). Advice: Start as commission agent, scale to owned unit after ₹25L revenue.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) at udyamregistration.gov.in (free, 10 mins). Apply for GI benefit certificate via District Textile Office, Sonepur (check eligibility, free registration post-approval). If exporting, get IEC from DGFT portal (₹0, 5 days online). Trademark your label name at ipindia.gov.in (₹5,000-₹10,000). Total cost: ₹5K-₹15K, 3-4 week timeline.
5
First Sale
Post 15-20 saree photos on Etsy (beginner category 'Traditional Sarees'), price ₹12,000-₹28,000 each. First sale: expect within 2-4 weeks. First month target: 3-5 orders = ₹40,000-₹150,000 gross revenue. Parallel: Contact 5-10 ethnic boutiques in Delhi (search Google: 'ethnic wear boutiques Karol Bagh' or 'South Delhi'), send WhatsApp with 8-10 product photos, offer ₹5,000-₹8,000 per saree. First wholesale order likely ₹30,000-₹50,000.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: Hire full-time Instagram content creator (₹15,000/month) to shoot reels of artisan at loom. Hire logistics partner for MetaCare or Shiprocket (automated). Apply for SFURTI subsidy (up to ₹45L cluster development grant — check state eligibility). Exhibit at India Handloom Expo (Delhi, Feb-March) and Surajkund Mela (Feb). Hire 1 export sales person (₹25,000/month) to pitch overseas. Target: 60-80 sarees/month by month 18.
