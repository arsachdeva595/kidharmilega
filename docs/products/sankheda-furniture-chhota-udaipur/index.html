Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Chhota Udaipur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sankheda village artisan clusters in Chhota Udaipur (30 km from Kawant) and spend 2-3 weeks with master craftsmen; study GI certification documents from DC Handicraft office; attend ICCW (Indian Chamber of Commerce & Crafts) workshops in Gujarat to understand market pricing
2
Source Raw Materials
Source premium teak and sal wood from licensed timber merchants in Chhota Udaipur or Vadodara (₹8000-12000 per cubic meter); natural dyes from herbal suppliers in Ahmedabad or Indore (₹5000-8000 for starter bulk); brass fittings from Jamnagar suppliers via B2B platforms (₹3000 initial); first 10-piece furniture batch estimated at ₹40000-60000
3
Produce / Partner
Partner directly with 3-5 artisan families in Sankheda through local gram panchayat introductions; establish shared workshop in village or rent 500 sq ft space in Kawant town (₹5000/month); formalize through written agreements ensuring quality standards and delivery timelines; plan 4-6 weeks per furniture piece depending on complexity
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (micro/small enterprise) at udyamregistration.gov.in within 2 hours online; obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Vadodara for export eligibility (₹0 cost); apply for GI certification usage from Ministry of DC Handicraft (brand authenticity); register trademark with crafted brand name at ipindia.gov.in (₹9000-12000); obtain business license from Chhota Udaipur Municipality
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy with 15-20 high-quality product photos (70% of Sankheda exports via this platform); list on Indigo Living, Nappa, Craft Jaipur (Indian premium platforms); build Instagram shop (@YourBrandName) with reels showing carving process (drives 40% D2C traffic); reach out to 50-100 interior designers via email with portfolio and samples; register with export promotion councils like AHIMSA for order linkages
6
Scale
After 6 months and ₹20L+ revenue, hire 2-3 additional artisan partnerships and expand to adjacent villages; launch B2B relationships with 5-star hotels and resorts across India; apply for government export subsidy schemes (MOOWR, RODTEP) to boost margins by 5-8%; develop 3-4 signature design collections per year; establish showroom in Ahmedabad or online presence optimization for luxury segment

up to ₹25L grant (25% for SC/ST/Women) for setting up handcraft production units

Gujarat Artisan Udyam Vikas Yojana

🏚
Gujarat Handicraft Export Promotion Scheme
