Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Chhota Udaipur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sankheda village cluster in Vadodara (12 km from Chhota Udaipur border) — spend a day with existing furniture makers, observe turning and hand-painting. Call 3-4 established suppliers like DMK Sankheda or local artisans directly (find via local block office or through craft association). Free. Doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source teak wood blanks (pre-turned chairs, table legs) from Vadodara timber and turning suppliers — first batch of 8-10 chairs costs ₹28K-₹35K (₹3K-₹4K per piece raw). Source lacquer and paint from Vadodara paint merchants — ₹5K-₹8K for starter colors. Or partner directly with a Sankheda artisan who supplies finished semi-finished pieces at ₹8K-₹12K per chair.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 400-500 sq ft workshop in Sankheda village or nearby Vadodara (₹6K-₹10K/month) with basic hand tools, paintbrushes, turntable for finishing. OR hire 2-3 artisans on piece-rate (₹800-₹1.2K per chair carved and hand-painted). First batch of 15-20 chairs: ₹80K-₹1.2L for materials + labor + rent for 2 months.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free online, 5 mins). Apply for GI registration via DC Handicraft office if branding independent identity — no direct cost but 8-12 week process. Export route: apply for IEC code via DGFT (₹0, 1 week processing). Get APEDA or craft council certification if exporting (₹2K-₹5K, 3-4 weeks).
5
First Sale
First sale: post 5-6 product photos on Etsy, Indiamart, and WhatsApp business profile. Target first order from interior design studio in Bangalore or Mumbai via Indiamart Premium (list 8-10 chairs at ₹42K-₹58K per piece). Expected first-month revenue ₹0-₹45K (depends on orders received). Simultaneously pitch to 2-3 heritage hotels in Rajasthan/Gujarat via direct email with photos.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire a full-time design coordinator to handle client customization and photography (₹15K/month). Partner with 5-8 artisans instead of hiring (cost-effective for scaling). Exhibit at India International Trade Fair (IITF) Delhi, Seagull Design Fair Mumbai, and regional furniture expos (₹1.5L-₹2.5L booth cost, generates 2-4 bulk orders per show). Add export logistics partner (freight forwarder) to handle shipments to UK/USA (₹8K-₹12K per container).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Yojana
up to ₹15,000 credit per artisan plus tool kits for traditional craftspeople, covers lacquer furniture makers; plus ₹500/month stipend for first 6 months (verify latest terms).
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy for manufacturing unit + 15-35% subsidy on plant and machinery; combined with MUDRA loan up to ₹10L at concessional rates.
🏚
Gujarat ODOP Scheme
direct support for product branding, export promotion, and artisan skill training; exhibition subsidies up to ₹50K per year.
🏚
Gujarat Handicraft Artisan Welfare Scheme
₹10K one-time support per registered artisan, health insurance coverage, and pension after 15 years continuous work.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
