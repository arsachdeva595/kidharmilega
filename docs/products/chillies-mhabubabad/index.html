Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Mhabubabad ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Mhabubabad Mandal agricultural office and connect with farmer collectives at Tandur and Siddipet chilli markets; attend 2-3 NCDEX commodity trading workshops to understand pricing dynamics and grade standards.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified seeds from ICRISAT (Hyderabad, 40km away) or local seed banks; initial 10-acre cultivation requires ₹3-4L for seeds, labor, and inputs; connect with 15-20 smallholder farmers in Naskal and Tandur taluks for contracted supply.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing farmer cooperatives like Mhabubabad Farmers Cooperative Society for primary production; establish a small drying and grading unit (₹15-20L capital) in district; hire technical support from ICAR-ICRISAT for quality optimization.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam as micro/small enterprise; obtain IEC code from DGFT for export; get FSSAI license for food processing (₹5000-10000); apply for GI certification to build premium positioning; trademark brand name.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh, BigBasket, and Blinkit with tiered chilli grades; create direct B2B partnerships with 5-10 food processors in Hyderabad and Mumbai via LinkedIn; start Instagram and YouTube channels showcasing farm-to-table narratives; secure 2-3 bulk orders (500kg+) from restaurant chains.
6
Scale
Expand to 50+ acres via farmer networks; set up ISO 22000 certified processing facility (₹50L investment); enter EU-compliant export corridors; build brand through specialty food partnerships; launch subscription boxes for global D2C segments.
