Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kurnool ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bathermcherla Stone polishing cluster (30km from Kurnool city) this week — walk through 15-20 active units, observe carving techniques, ask ₹0 questions: raw stone costs, artisan wages, typical production timeline, buyer sourcing, export challenges. Call the District Industries Centre (DIC) Kurnool office or visit their physical office to understand ODOP support. Attend the Kurnool weekly haat (Thursday/Friday) to see wholesale stone buyers in action.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source stone from registered quarries in Bathermcherla area or Nandyal taluk via local stone suppliers (₹1200-2400/tonne for rough limestone; ₹2000-3500/tonne for polished granite offcuts). First batch (for 50-100 pieces) costs ₹15000-35000. Key supplier clusters: Bathermcherla itself, Nandyal taluk, and Adoni area — all within 60km of Kurnool city. Negotiate bulk rates by joining a supplier cooperative or going direct to quarry owners.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (Recommended): Rent a 600-800 sqft open-air/semi-covered carving unit in Bathermcherla cluster for ₹4000-7000/month + utilities ₹800/month; bring in 2-3 artisans at ₹250-400/day wage (₹5000-8000/month each). Option B: Partner with established artisans (revenue-share model, 40-50% to artisan, 50-60% to you as founder). First 6 months: ₹80000-150000 (rent, tools, first wage cycle, contingency). Production timeline: 4-6 weeks for medium-batch orders (20-30 pieces).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (₹0, online at udyamregistration.gov.in, 5 min). Register IEC code at local DGFT office if exporting (₹0, 15 days processing). Apply for GI registration under One District One Product scheme via State Handicrafts Ministry (covers ₹50000-80000 legal cost, 6-8 month timeline, enhances export credibility). Obtain GST registration (₹0 online, 7 days). Total compliance cost: ₹2000-5000 out-of-pocket.
5
First Sale
First sale: Contact architects/interior designers in Bangalore via LinkedIn, Instagram DMs, or local architecture forums — pitch a 3-5 piece pilot order (₹15000-40000 value). Alternatively, list 5-10 products on Etsy or own Shopify site within 2 weeks of production (₹500 Shopify/month). First-month revenue target: ₹25000-80000 (1-3 wholesale orders or 5-15 D2C units sold). Use word-of-mouth from Bathermcherla cluster contacts to seed first 3-5 customers.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: Hire a full-time design/sales manager (₹15000-20000/month), scale artisan team to 6-8 carvers, rent a 2000 sqft branded studio/showroom in Kurnool city center (₹12000-18000/month). Exhibit at Design Craft (Delhi, Feb-Mar), India Art Festival (major metro cities, ₹40000-80000 booth cost). Build a design team (2-3 designers, ₹10000-15000/month each) to create custom orders for architects. Invest in product photography/videography (₹40000-60000). Launch B2B trade partnerships with home décor chains and hospitality groups.

Government Schemes

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Yojana
free skill training for artisans (₹500K budget allocation), tool kit grants up to ₹15000 per artisan, 8% GST exemption for registered artisans
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy (25% for general, 35% for SC/ST), loan guarantee for ₹10-50 lakh, 10 months processing via KVIC/KVIB
🏚
AP Handlooms, Handicrafts & Sericulture (HHS) Department
One District One Product subsidy covers 50% of design registration, export promotion, exhibition costs (₹2-5 lakh per unit)
🏚
AP Udyam Scheme
startup grants ₹10-25 lakh for first-time entrepreneurs, 5% interest subsidy for working capital loans up to ₹50 lakh, processing via District Industries Centre
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
