Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sidhi ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sihawal block weaving cluster in person — meet 3-4 established carpet units and watch production. Call District Handicraft Officer at Sidhi District office (free). Register on Udyam portal (free, 10 min). Zero cost, doable this month.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source wool from Indore or Ujjain wool markets (350 km away, 2-day sourcing trip). First batch: 2-3 carpets, ₹8-12K in raw material. Negotiate with Sihawal artisans for supply agreements — aim for ₹1.5-2K per carpet production labor cost.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent 300-400 sq ft space in Sihawal block, ₹2-4K/month, set up 2-3 looms, hire 2-3 weavers part-time. Option B: Artisan partnership model — you source materials, they produce on contract basis, ₹0 rent. Setup cost: ₹30-60K for Option A (loom, tools). Timeline: 2-3 weeks.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online). Apply for One District One Product scheme support at DC Handicraft office (₹1-2L in potential subsidy). File GI registration application for 'Sidhi Panja Dari' with Intellectual Property Office (₹10-15K filing fee, 18-24 month process). Register IEC code with DGFT if planning export (₹0, online).
5
First Sale
First sale: list on Etsy or Amazon Handmade (free listings, 15% commission). Approach 5-10 interior designers in Delhi/Mumbai via email with catalog. Join one handicraft fair (₹5-10K booth cost). Target: first order 2-3 pieces within 6 weeks, ₹25-40K revenue in month 1-2.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire dedicated order manager (₹8-12K/month), expand Sihawal artisan network to 8-10 units, open WhatsApp Business account for corporate inquiries, exhibit at India International Trade Fair (IITF) and Surajkund Mela. Partner with 2-3 interior design studios for steady orders. Consider export logistics partner for USA/Germany sales.
