Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Chittorgarh ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kishangarh cluster (45 km from Chittorgarh city). Call 5 existing marble/granite units directly (ask for contact via Chittorgarh MSME office — free). Attend the annual Stone India Expo if timing allows, or visit local Kishangarh stone haat to understand pricing, quality grades, and local supplier networks. Zero cost, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw blocks from Kishangarh quarry owners or Udaipur stone traders (30-50 km away). First batch of 5-10 granite blocks (2x3 meters) costs ₹45-65K depending on color/grade. Partner with an existing cutting unit in Kishangarh to process on job-work basis initially (₹800-1200 per sqm cutting charge) rather than buying equipment.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 1500-2000 sqft cutting/polishing unit in Kishangarh industrial area (₹8-12K monthly). Invest ₹2-3.5L in a diamond saw, polishing pads, and hand tools. Alternatively, run zero-asset model first: buy blocks, pay existing units for cutting/finishing, sell as semi-finished stock. Unit rental + utilities = ₹12-15K monthly.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 10 minutes online). File IEC with DGFT if planning exports (₹500-1000, 5 days). Apply for MSME Credit Guarantee Scheme loan if needed (up to ₹5L). No GI protection exists yet for Chittorgarh granite — focus on brand name and quality certification instead (ISO 14001 for quarries, ₹15-25K). Timeline: 3 weeks total.
5
First Sale
First order: contact 5 real estate projects in Jaipur/Delhi via LinkedIn or building supplier networks. Target ₹1.5L first order (300 sqft of tiles/slabs at ₹500/sqft wholesale). Use Builderscrack or IndiaMart for lead generation. First month: ₹2-5L gross revenue from 2-3 projects + 1-2 individual D2C orders via Instagram.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 sales person to manage builder relationships, 1 logistics coordinator. Exhibit at Stone India Expo (₹2-4L booth cost annually). Invest in ISO certification (₹30-50K) and 3-year export contracts with 2 Middle East importers. Consider adding finished goods (countertops, tiles) manufacturing in-house. Partner with 2-3 architects in each major metro for repeat orders.
