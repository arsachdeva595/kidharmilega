Granite and Marble Products ki
opportunity, Chittorgarh mein.

Chittorgarh sits on some of India's richest granite and marble reserves with centuries of extraction expertise. The district's geological formations produce distinctive colors and patterns (blacks, greys, reds, whites) prized for luxury construction and monuments. Skilled artisans and established quarrying infrastructure make it the natural hub for stone processing in Rajasthan.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Chittorgarh ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Chittorgarh's main quarrying clusters near Bassi and Nimbahera (40km radius); spend 2-3 weeks meeting 10+ quarry owners and 5+ processing unit operators to understand geology, extraction costs (₹500-₹1200/tonne), and market dynamics. Join Rajasthan Stone & Mineral Association.
2
Source Raw Materials
Contract with established quarries in Bassi cluster for first 5-10 tonne blocks (₹50,000-₹100,000 for sample blocks). Negotiate 30-45 day payment terms. First batch cost: ₹3-₹5 lakhs for stone + transportation.
3
Produce / Partner
Lease a 2000 sq ft processing unit in Chittorgarh city (₹15,000-₹25,000/month) or partner with existing processors on margin-share basis. Invest in 1-2 stone cutting machines (₹6-₹12 lakhs), polishing equipment (₹4-₹8 lakhs), and skilled mason team (2-3 artisans at ₹12,000-₹18,000/month each).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Ministry of MSME), apply for IEC (Directorate General of Foreign Trade) within 15 days. Register brand name with IP India. Get ISO 9001 certification (₹40,000-₹60,000) to qualify for premium contracts. Open current+savings account for GST registration under MSME.
5
First Sale
List on Indiamart and Alibaba B2B platforms with high-res photos; target first 3-5 inquiries per week. Cold-email 50+ architectural firms and 20+ real estate developers in metros (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore) with samples and price quotes. Attend Stone India Expo (Jaipur, Jan) and Buildtech India (Delhi) trade shows. Join 2-3 WhatsApp builder groups and post weekly product updates.
6
Scale
After ₹50 Lakh revenue: hire dedicated export manager (₹30,000/month), attend 2-3 international stone trade shows (Dubai Stone, Italy Marmomacc), secure letters of credit from international distributors. Invest in upgraded machinery to produce 500+ units/month. Build second processing unit or acquire smaller competitor's capacity. Create Instagram/YouTube channel showcasing project case studies and before-after installation stories.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 Lakhs loan at 5% interest for manufacturing units, 15-35% subsidy for SC/ST/women entrepreneurs
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹10 Cr cluster development grant for stone processing clusters in Chittorgarh, covers machinery and training
🏚
Rajasthan MSME Promotion Policy 2024
5% interest subsidy for 5 years on loans up to ₹50 Lakhs for manufacturing units, capital subsidy up to 20% for new machinery
🏚
Rajasthan Stone & Mineral Industries Development Scheme
concessional quarry lease rates, GST refund on export of stone products (up to 12% additional benefit)
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
