The region's rich Lepcha-Nepali heritage, thriving cardamom economy, and year-round moderate climate attract both domestic trekkers and international tourists seeking authentic Himalayan experiences.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — West Sikkim (Geyzing) ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Geyzing town and shadow established homestay owners like those in Yuksom and Tashiding for 2 weeks. Study Sikkim Tourism Board reports and connect with Sikkim Tour Operators Association. Research trekking demand on Indiahikes and TravelTriangle platforms. Budget: ₹15,000-20,000.
2
Source Raw Materials
Partner with local Geyzing homestay owners for accommodation, source trekking equipment from Darjeeling suppliers (₹2-3L for initial batch of ropes, tents, cooking gear). Connect with local guides through District Administration. First-batch operational cost: ₹5-7L including permits and initial marketing.
3
Produce / Partner
Formalize partnerships with 5-7 local homestay families in Geyzing, Yuksom, Tashiding villages. Train 3-4 local guides (16-day mountaineering certification via Nehru Institute, Uttarkashi). Design 3-4 signature treks (Yuksom-Khangchendzonga, Geyzing heritage circuit). Establish collaboration with Sikkim Artisan Collective for craft products.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam as Tourism Service Provider under MSME. Apply for IEC code for international tour bookings. Register brand on Instagram/Facebook as 'GeysingAdventures' or similar. Get Sikkim Tourism Board recognition. GST registration at 5% for hospitality services. Adventure tour operator liability insurance mandatory.
5
First Sale
Launch on TravelTriangle and Indiahikes with 3-5 signature treks at ₹8,000-15,000 per person. Create Instagram content showcasing trekkers, monasteries, cardamom fields. Partner with corporate HR platforms (CultureTrips, Offsiteadventures.com). List homestays on Airbnb, BookMyStay. Email marketing to adventure clubs in Bangalore, Delhi, Kolkata. First target: 15-20 trekkers/month by month 3.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to 8-10 signature routes covering 200+ trekkers/month. Add premium eco-lodge partnerships. Launch cardamom farm-stay experiences (₹3,000/day). Introduce international group tours via partnerships with UK/US adventure companies. Hire 2-3 full-time coordinators. Target ₹30-40L annual revenue by month 12.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (35% for general category in hilly areas) for tourism enterprise setup
🏛
MUDRA Yojana
unsecured loans up to ₹10L for tourism operators under Shishu/Kishore schemes
🏚
Sikkim Tourism Policy 2018
Tax exemptions and subsidies for adventure tourism operators; incentives for homestay registration and certification
🏚
Sikkim Rural Enterprise Development Scheme
concessional loans up to ₹15L and capacity building for community-based tourism enterprises
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Marketing — Zero Budget Se Shuru Karo
  • User-generated content campaign on Instagram: incentivize trekkers to share experiences with #GeysingAdventures, repost best content. Partner with 5-10 travel Instagram influencers (50k-200k followers) in India for 1-2 sponsored treks/year at ₹30-50k each.
