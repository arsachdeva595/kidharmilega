Tourism ki
opportunity, West Sikkim (Geyzing) mein.

Geyzing sits at 6,500 feet in West Sikkim, 10 km from Pelling (major entry point) and 2 km from Yangtey (1,828 m altitude village). The district's tourism infrastructure includes Barsey Rhododendron Sanctuary (10,000 ft), Dafeybhir Pass Trek, Sidkeong Tulku Bird Park (established 2015), and 15+ active monasteries. River Rangeet runs through the western region, supporting rafting and water-based activities; cardamom plantations provide agro-tourism angles. Market demand comes from Nepal border region (100 km), Kolkata (500 km road), and international tourists seeking Himalayan experiences.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — West Sikkim (Geyzing) ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call 5 active guesthouses in Geyzing (ask locals for numbers), ask about tourist footfall, pain points, and referral networks. Walk Geyzing town market and Pelling highway for 3 hours noting foot traffic. Register free Udyam account online (no capital). Attend Sunday market at Geyzing town to meet local guide associations.
2
Source Raw Materials
Partner with existing local guides (₹200-300 per day share), monasteries (negotiate 10-15% commission per visitor), and guesthouses (referral deals). First package design costs ₹0 (use existing trails). Trekking gear rental sourced from Pelling adventure shops (₹15,000-30,000 inventory for 10 packages).
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a small office/cafe space in Geyzing central market (₹8,000-12,000/month, 2-3 rooms). Hire 1 local guide coordinator (₹8,000-10,000/month) and 1 customer service person (₹6,000-8,000/month). Partner with 10-15 freelance guides rather than employ (saves ₹2L annually). Total monthly opex: ₹25,000-35,000.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 15 mins online). File sole proprietorship (₹500, local nagar palika). Apply for Sikkim Tourism Trade License (₹2,000-3,000, 2 weeks). Get GST registration (free, 1 week online). If targeting exports, file IEC code through DGFT portal (free). Consider applying for One District One Product (ODOP) scheme and Ministry of Tourism's HRIDAY scheme for branding support.
5
First Sale
Week 1: Create WhatsApp Business account + Instagram page with trail photos. Week 2: Post 2-3 trek packages on Airbnb Experiences (commission 10-15%). Week 3: Cold-email travel agents in Delhi (50 emails) offering 25% commission. Week 4: First booking likely ₹4,000-6,000 (1 couple trek). Target 3-4 bookings first month = ₹15,000-20,000 gross, ₹9,000-12,000 net.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (25-30 bookings/month): hire dedicated trek coordinator (₹12,000/month), launch website with booking system (₹30,000 one-time), advertise on Facebook/Google Ads (₹1,000/day), exhibit at Delhi Travel Mart and Adventure Travel World Summit (₹1-2L/year). Expand to multi-day packages (3-5 days), add homestay network (30-40 homes), launch corporate retreat vertical.
