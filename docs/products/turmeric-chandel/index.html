Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Chandel ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Chandel district turmeric clusters in Tungjoy and Songbijang villages; meet farmer cooperatives like Chandel Farmers Association; study curcumin levels and grading standards at Imphal Agricultural Research Institute; analyze competitor pricing on Spice Board India data.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source turmeric rhizomes from local farmers in Chandel at ₹5000-6500/quintal; arrange first batch of 5 quintals (₹25000-32500); negotiate bulk rates with CHANDEL AGRICULTURAL COOPERATIVE; ensure organic certification documentation from suppliers.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3-5 farmer groups in Chandel for direct supply agreements; set up drying units in Chandel town (₹3-5L for basic setup) or outsource to existing SFURTI-supported drying centers; process turmeric through grinding and quality testing at IMPHAL Spice Processing Hub.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam as micro-enterprise (udyamregistration.gov.in); obtain Spice Board registration and IEC code (apeda.gov.in); get Ayurveda/FSSAI certification; register brand name with DIB Manipur; set up traceability system for organic/GI-eligible products.
5
First Sale
List on Amazon, Flipkart, Blinkit within month 1-2; approach Ayurvedic retailers and pharmacies in Delhi/Bangalore with wholesale pricing; contact spice exporters in Kochi and Chennai; launch Instagram/Facebook content with farmer stories and health benefits; submit samples to food manufacturers in Pune and Hyderabad.
6
Scale
Establish value-added product line (turmeric capsules, blends) by month 7-8; hire 2-3 local staff for packaging and QC; negotiate larger export contracts after first successful shipment; apply for PMEGP/SFURTI subsidy for processing equipment upgrade; target Ayush Ministry's Honey & Turmeric initiatives.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L loan with 35% subsidy for manufacturing; eligible for turmeric processing unit setup in Tier 2/3 districts.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹10L for spice processing infrastructure and farmer cluster development in Chandel.
🏚
Manipur Agricultural Mission
₹2L subsidy per farmer cooperative for quality seed production and organic certification in turmeric cultivation.
🏚
Manipur MSME Innovation Scheme
₹15L grant for value-addition and branding of turmeric products produced in district; emphasis on Ayush sector.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
