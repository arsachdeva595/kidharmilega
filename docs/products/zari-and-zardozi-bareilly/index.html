Bareilly sits 250km northeast of Delhi in central UP, historically the capital of Zari embroidery with artisan families trained over centuries. Raw materials arrive from Chinese suppliers and domestic thread mills in Delhi, with established logistics via NH19 and Bareilly railway junction. Export-ready pieces ship to Israel and Middle Eastern buyers, while domestic sales concentrate in Delhi, Mumbai, and UP wedding markets worth ₹10 crore annually.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bareilly ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bareilly's Zari cluster in Sabzi Mandi and Nai Basti areas on a Wednesday or Saturday to meet active artisans and unit owners; call the Bareilly District Industries Center (0581-2460950) to understand ODOP support available; attend Bareilly Haat (weekly market Tuesdays at Collectorate grounds) to observe demand. Zero cost, doable within 3 days.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source gold and silver threads, beads, sequins from established suppliers in Bareilly's Aliganj wholesale market or Delhi's Chandni Chowk thread market; expect ₹30,000-50,000 for first-batch raw materials covering 200-300 embroidered pieces. Lead time: 5-10 days. Negotiate bulk rates after placing 2-3 orders.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 200-300 sq ft workspace in Sabzi Mandi cluster for ₹2,000-4,000/month or partner with 8-12 existing artisans on per-piece payment (₹50-150 per embroidered item depending on complexity). Artisan partnership requires zero rent; factory rent option allows quality control. Budget ₹1,00,000 for tools, worktables, storage, and first 2 months of artisan wages.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online at udyamregistration.gov.in within 3 days); GI registration is already state-approved for Bareilly Zari — apply for certification via Bareilly Chamber of Commerce (₹2,000 for membership, ₹5,000-10,000 for GI certification support). For export, apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) via Indian Customs portal (₹0 cost, 5-7 days). Total: ₹10,000 over 4 weeks.
5
First Sale
First sale: list 20-30 signature pieces on Meesho or Instagram Shop (free); reach out to 50 wedding designers in Delhi NCR via WhatsApp with portfolio images; target 2-3 first orders of ₹5,000-15,000 each within month 1. Expected first-month revenue: ₹15,000-40,000 if 3-5 orders convert.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 1-2 sales managers for wholesale outreach, standardize 5-6 bestselling designs, exhibit at Delhi Craft Week or Mumbai wedding expos (booth cost ₹50,000-1,00,000), apply for PMEGP subsidy (₹10-25L) to add 15-20 more artisans, set up a showroom in Bareilly or Delhi NCR.
