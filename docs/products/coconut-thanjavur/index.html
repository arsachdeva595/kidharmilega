Buyer markets are 80-120 km away in Coimbatore and Chennai, with Marico having a 20-year supply relationship in the region.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Thanjavur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Pollachi or Thanjavur agri-business cluster this week (40 km from main town). Call the cluster head — ask about farmer groups, raw material contracts, and existing processor partnerships. Free. Then attend a Thursday haat in Thanjavur town to talk to coconut traders and understand price cycles.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fresh coconuts directly from Parachute Kalpavriksha Foundation member farmers (44,000 active farmers in their network) or Thanjavur Agricultural Produce Cooperative. First batch: 5,000-10,000 nuts at ₹25-35 per nut (seasonal variation). Build contract with 2-3 farmer groups for ₹1.5-2.5L initial purchase.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 1,200-1,500 sqft processing unit in Pollachi or Thanjavur agri-business cluster (₹8-12K/month). Invest in basic copra drying racks, oil extraction setup (cold-press or centrifuge ₹3-5L). OR partner with existing agri-business center on revenue-share model (they process, you brand & sell — 40-50% margin to them). Staffing: 2 full-time processors + 1 QC person minimum.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 5 mins). File for food processing license under FSSAI (₹5-8K, 10-15 days). If exporting: apply for IEC from DGFT (₹0-500, 5-10 days online). For future GI advantage, document 'Thanjavur Coconut' origin story for registration application (₹15-20K legal cost, 6-9 months timeline — not mandatory but valuable).
5
First Sale
First sale: Contact 5-6 local FMCG distributors in Coimbatore (80 km away) or list on Udyam portal + BigBasket Seller + Amazon Pantry. First order size: 100-200 liters coconut oil to a local bakery chain (expected ₹25-40K first month). Parallel: onboard to 1XBET or local coconut trader associations for bulk supply agreements.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: Hire sales person (₹18-22K salary) + marketing person (₹15-18K). Exhibit at Thanjavur Agricultural Fair (annual), Agrotech Expo Coimbatore. Partner with 3-4 online platforms (Meesho, Zepto, Blinkit) for distribution. Expand to branded coconut water, coconut flour — each adding ₹2-5L/month.
