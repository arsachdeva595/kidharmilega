Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Thanjavur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Papanasam, Buvaneswar, and Nannilam taluks to study coconut cooperatives like Thanjavur District Coconut Producers Society and Karaikal Coconut Cluster. Attend coconut auctions at Thanjavur Farmers Market every Tuesday and Friday.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source directly from coconut farmers in Thiruvaiyaru and Nannilam (₹8,000–₹12,000 per 1000 nuts). Negotiate 10-ton bulk orders with cooperative unions. First batch: ₹1,20,000 for 1000 mature coconuts with processing setup.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with registered coconut processing units or establish mini cold-press oil units (₹5–₹10L). Connect with artisans in Thanjavur's coir cluster via Tamil Nadu Coir Board. Start with drop-shipment from TCCDB members for first 3 months.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam as Micro or Small Enterprise. Obtain IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) from DGFT. Get FSSAI license for food products (₹5,000–₹15,000). Register brand with IP office. Obtain organic certification if targeting premium segment (₹20,000–₹50,000).
5
First Sale
Launch on Meesho, JioMart, and Amazon with coconut oil SKUs. Approach local HORECA outlets (hotels, cafes). Create WhatsApp Business account for wholesale bulk orders. Contact FabIndia, Nature's Basket, and organic retail chains for B2B partnerships.
6
Scale
Expand product line to VCO, coconut milk, and activated charcoal by month 6. Build direct B2B relationships with 10+ cosmetic and food brands. Aim for ₹5–₹8L monthly revenue by month 12. Explore APEDA export certification and establish direct partnerships with Middle Eastern and Southeast Asian importers.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy for coconut processing units with 25% founder contribution
🏛
MUDRA Yojana
up to ₹10L unsecured loan for coconut-based agribusiness; low interest rates for first 2 years
🏚
Tamil Nadu Chief Minister Integrated Scheme for Farmers Development (CMISFD)
₹1.5L subsidy for coconut processing machinery and farm mechanization
🏚
Tamil Nadu Coconut Mission
direct support for value-added coconut products, market linkage, and branding assistance
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
