Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Pune ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Pimpri-Chinchwad Industrial Area and MIDC Pune to study existing units; interview 10+ manufacturers; join Pune Industrial Association; study ISO 9001, AS9100 (aerospace) standards; benchmark competitor offerings at engineering expos.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from Pune Steel Market (Camp area) and Chinchwad suppliers; establish relationships with bearing/fastener vendors in Aurangabad; first-batch raw material cost ₹15-25L depending on product spec; negotiate credit terms of 30-45 days.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 established job-work units in MIDC Chinchwad or Pimpri for initial production (60-70% of capacity); invest in CNC machines, VMC, lathe, grinding machines (₹20-35L); hire experienced technician (₹8-12L annually); implement quality control SOP.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, free); obtain IEC code from DGFT; apply for ISO 9001:2015 certification (₹1-1.5L, 3-4 months); register trademark for brand name (₹15-25K); obtain GST registration; file for Quality Scheme participation if applicable.
5
First Sale
List on IndiaMART, TradeKey, and Global Trade Hub; cold-outreach to Tier-1 suppliers (Bosch, Motherson) with technical specs and pricing; attend Pune Industrial Expo and ACME Engineering Exhibition; leverage WhatsApp and email for B2B lead generation; offer samples for testing.
6
Scale
Achieve ISO certifications (AS9100, IATF 16949); expand product range based on customer feedback; hire sales engineer for international markets; establish quality partnerships with testing labs; invest in additional machinery; explore contract manufacturing from larger OEMs; apply for export incentives (RODTEP, RoSCTL).
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (PM Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L loan subsidy for manufacturing units with 15-25% government subsidy.
🏛
SIDBI Make in India Loan Scheme
up to ₹10Cr for manufacturing, focus on advanced manufacturing and automation.
🏚
Maharashtra MSME Sector Development Scheme
up to ₹50L subsidy for technology upgradation and ISO certification.
🏚
Maharashtra Udyam Kranti Yojana
provides credit linked capital subsidy up to ₹25L for registered MSME manufacturing units.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
