Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kangpokpi ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kangpokpi agricultural clusters and Imphal Spice Market; speak with existing turmeric farmers and traders; analyze turmeric pricing at Erode Spice Market (Tamil Nadu benchmark)
2
Source Raw Materials
Partner with Kangpokpi farmers or buy from local cooperatives at ₹80-120 per kg fresh rhizomes; for first batch of 5 MT dried turmeric, budget ₹2.5-3.5 Lakh including curing and processing
3
Produce / Partner
Tie up with ICAR-approved turmeric processing units in Manipur or Meghalaya; alternatively, establish small-scale processing with sun-drying and polishing setup (₹4-6 Lakh capital); hire trained processors from agricultural colleges
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam for MSME benefits; obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office (Imphal); get FSSAI food business license (₹5000-12000); register brand name with IP office; obtain phytosanitary certificate for export
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon, Flipkart, Jio Mart with ₹15-25/gram pricing; approach HUL, Dabur, Himalaya spice buying departments; connect with Indore spice traders; list on Agribazar and NutriCargo platforms
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand product line with turmeric capsules and golden milk; target 10+ wholesale distributors across India; establish export compliance for 2-3 Southeast Asian markets; hire 2-3 field staff for farmer relationships
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP
up to ₹25 Lakh subsidy for food processing and agro-business with 15-35% subsidy for SC/ST applicants
🏛
PM AASHA (Agricultural Infrastructure Fund)
concessional loans up to ₹1 Cr for cold storage, warehousing, processing units
🏚
Manipur Agricultural Promotion Scheme
incentives for spice cultivation and value-addition; 20% subsidy on inputs
🏚
Manipur MSME Export Promotion Scheme
reimbursement of 50% export certification and shipping costs for first 2 years
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
