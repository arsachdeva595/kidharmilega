Ports at Mumbai (480 km) and Kandla (620 km) connect directly to USA, UK, Japan, and Gulf buyers via pre-existing export corridors.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Aurangabad ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Marathwada mango cluster in Parbhani or Jalna (1.5-2 hours from Aurangabad city), call 5 exporters listed on Aurangabad ODOP office, attend Aurangabad Agricultural Produce Market Committee (APMC) mandi on a Tuesday to observe wholesale pricing. All free, doable within 3 days.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source directly from farmers in Parbhani, Jalna, Beed, or Latur tehsils during April-June. Expect ₹30-40/kg for Grade A fruit, ₹20-25/kg for Grade B. First batch: 5-10 MT from cooperative clusters like Marathwada Krishi Seva Sahakari or direct farmer groups. Cost: ₹1.5-3.5L for first season trial.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 2,000-5,000 sq ft unit near Parbhani APMC (₹8-15K/month) or partner with existing fruit processor for sorting, grading, packaging on revenue-share (no upfront capex). Manual sorting + labor: ₹2-4/kg. Packaging (cardboard + foam + labels): ₹3-5/kg. Total cost per kg: ₹5-9 before sale.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 15 min online at udyamregistration.gov.in). Apply for GI certification for 'Aurangabad Kesar' brand at IP Office, Pune — ₹15-20K, 90 days. If exporting: IEC from DGFT (₹500, 5 days). FSSAI License: ₹3-5K, 30 days.
5
First Sale
First sale: contract with 2-3 FMCG distributors in Aurangabad and Pune (direct approach via APMC contacts) for 500 kg-1 MT. Expected ₹20-25K revenue on 1 MT at wholesale. Simultaneously list on BigBasket/Jio Mart — expect 20-50 kg orders first month, ₹8-12K. First-month revenue estimate: ₹40-60K if sourcing 1-1.5 MT.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (~2.5-3 MT/month in-season): hire 1 supply chain manager (₹25-30K), 2 packers (₹12K each), 1 export documentation specialist (₹20K). Exhibit at Summer Produce Show, Pune (June) and Food Logistics India, New Delhi (September). Explore co-packing with FSSAI units in Jalna to meet bulk export orders (5-10 MT). Build email list of UK/US importers via TradeKey, ContactUSA.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25% subsidy for general category (₹2.5-5L depending on unit cost), 35% for SC/ST/OBC. Apply via KVIC or bank.
🏛
Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PMKISAN)
if primary farmer: ₹6,000/year direct to account (register at pmkisan.gov.in). Supports working capital.
🏚
Maharashtra Agriculture Infrastructure Development Scheme
cold storage subsidy 40-50% on capex, max ₹60L. Contact State Horticulture Department.
🏚
Maharastra ODOP Scheme
direct marketing incentive + GI branding support + export documentation subsidy 50%, max ₹2L. Apply via District Industries Centre.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
