opportunity, Sehore mein.

Sehore district in Malwa foothills has documented teak wood sourcing from local sawmills and forest areas, supporting 800+ active artisan households producing carved wooden temples, beaded decorations, and toy sets. The 5 development blocks (Ashta, Goharganj, Nasrullaganj, Rehli, Sehore town cluster) house concentrated workshops where multi-generational craftspeople maintain quality standards. Direct rail connectivity via Sehore Junction and proximity to Bhopal buyer market (40 km) plus Delhi (850 km) enables same-week delivery to corporate and retail buyers.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sehore ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit the Sehore town workshop cluster (near District Handicraft Office) and call 2-3 established toy makers; attend Sehore Haat (every Thursday/Saturday) to see finished inventory and pricing; register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) using Aadhar — all zero cost, doable this week
2
Source Raw Materials
Source teak wood from Sehore Wood Sawmill (Ashta or Sehore town main market) at ₹8-12 per kg, brass hardware from Indore wholesalers (₹150-300/kg for fittings); first batch raw material cost ₹30-50K for 100-150 toy units; supplier contacts available through District Handicraft Office or local Karigar Samiti
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 400-600 sqft ground-floor workshop space in Sehore town or Ashta block at ₹4-7K/month, OR partner with 2-3 established artisans on revenue-share (40% to craftsperson, 60% to founder) — this avoids ₹2L+ equipment cost; initial setup with hand tools + painting station: ₹50-80K; 4-8 week lead time per batch from design to finished inventory
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 15 mins online); apply for MP Handicraft Cluster Development grant (₹1-2L subsidy under SFURTI, 3-month processing); if exporting, file IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) via dgft.gov.in (₹500, 5 days); explore GI registration for 'Sehore Wooden Toys' (₹15-25K filing cost, 18-24 month process) to access 5% export subsidy; register on Udyam Product Listing portal for visibility
5
First Sale
First sale via Etsy (10% fee) or Instagram shop by Week 8-12 — list 20-30 SKUs with professional photos (₹3-5K for shoot); target ₹8-15K monthly revenue in Month 3-4; parallel: approach 3-5 local gift shops with 10-15 piece trial orders at wholesale; Week 12-16: secure first corporate bulk order (₹20-40K) via LinkedIn outreach to event managers
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1-2 apprentices (₹8-12K/month each); upgrade to 800 sqft workshop (₹10-12K/month); launch on Amazon Handmade (8% fee, higher traffic); attend gift fairs (Delhi, Mumbai) and B2B trade shows; explore retail placement in 5-star hotel gift shops; set up WhatsApp-based bulk order intake team; launch 2-3 new product lines (carved boxes, decorative arches from ODOP product list) every quarter

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹25-40L cluster grant to support 50+ artisans, machinery subsidy up to 50%, applicable immediately for Sehore Handicraft Cluster
🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
₹3L collateral-free loan at 5% interest for registered artisans/woodworkers, ₹15K skill-training grant, open since 2023
🏚
MP Handicraft Cluster Development Scheme
₹1-2L one-time subsidy for registered new units in notified clusters (Sehore qualifies), 3-month approval
🏚
Mukhya Mantri Laghu Udyog Yojana (MMUY)
5% subsidy on machinery up to ₹2L, interest-free working capital loan up to ₹40L for registered MSMEs in MP
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
