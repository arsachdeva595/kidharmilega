Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Soreng ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Soreng cardamom clusters (Soreng-Chakung area) and connect with Sikkim State Cardamom Board. Attend the annual Soreng Cardamom Festival (typically Feb-Mar). Study auction prices at Siliguri Spice Market and Jorahat markets in Assam.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from registered farmer cooperatives like Soreng Cardamom Growers Federation. First batch of 500kg dried cardamom costs ₹150,000-200,000 (at ₹300-400/kg wholesale). Establish direct relationships with 3-4 plantation owners in Chakung or Upper Soreng.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing farmer collectives for raw supply. Invest in small drying facility (₹300,000-500,000) or use shared community drying units. Contract with local processors for sorting and grading to GI standards. Begin with 1000-2000kg capacity, scale to 5000kg within 12 months.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSE) classification as agricultural products business. Obtain IEC code from Gangtok DGFT office for export. Apply GI certification use rights through state agricultural department. Register brand with trademark registry. Get FSSAI license for packaged products.
5
First Sale
Launch on Spice brands' B2B platforms (IndiaTrading.com, TradeKey), approach premium retailers (Nature's Basket, Spencer's), contact Ayurvedic manufacturers via LinkedIn. Sell 50-100kg/month at wholesale first. Simultaneously build D2C via Shopify + Instagram targeting health-conscious affluent buyers.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to 5-10 farmer partnerships, establish processing facility in Siliguri (closer to transport hubs), launch branded variants (whole pods, powder, organic certified). Target 500kg/month D2C sales. Attend Gulfood, Biofach for export opportunities.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy for setting up agro-processing unit in Soreng district
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹5-20 lakh support for spice cluster development and collective processing infrastructure
🏚
Sikkim Agricultural Mechanisation Scheme
partial subsidy for drying and processing equipment for organic/GI products
🏚
State Export Promotion Scheme
subsidy on air freight and documentation costs for cardamom exports from Sikkim
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
