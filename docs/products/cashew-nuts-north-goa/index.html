Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — North Goa ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Cashew processing clusters in Bicholim, Pernem, and Sattari taluks in North Goa; connect with Goa Cashew Exporters Association; study pricing at Vasco da Gama wholesale market; understand GI compliance requirements
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw cashew nuts from farmer collectives in North Goa (₹200-250/kg wholesale); initial 500 kg batch costs ₹1.2-1.5 Lakh; establish relationships with 2-3 reliable farmer suppliers
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing FSSAI-certified cashew processing units in Bicholim or Pernem (contract processing at ₹20-30/kg); or invest in small roasting unit (₹8-12 Lakh for 50 kg/day capacity); recruit trained kernel graders from local clusters
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam on udyamregistration.gov.in; obtain IEC from nearest customs authority (Panaji); FSSAI registration (₹5000-15000); register GI authorized user status with Goa Cashew Exporters; file brand name at IP office
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon/Flipkart with ₹999-2999 premium pricing; reach out to specialty food retailers in metros; contact export agents registered with APEDA; engage B2B marketplaces like IndiaMART and TradeKey; target gifting platforms
6
Scale
Expand product line with roasted & salted variants; build own processing unit after ₹50L revenue; establish export pipeline to 2-3 countries; develop retail partnerships with premium grocery chains; invest in digital marketing targeting health/fitness audience
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM-KISAN
Direct income support ₹6000/year for farmer partners and cooperative members
🏛
PMEGP
Up to ₹25L subsidy (35% for general, 50% for SC/ST) for agro-processing units with ₹4L to ₹10L investment
🏚
Goa Agricultural Produce Marketing (GAPM) Board
Market linkage support and quality certification for ODOP products
🏚
Goa Integrated Agriculture Development Scheme
Up to ₹5L subsidy for value addition and processing infrastructure in agriculture sector
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
