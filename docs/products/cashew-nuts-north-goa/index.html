North Goa is part of India's southern cashew processing corridor (Kollam Kerala, Mangalore Karnataka, Vettapalam Andhra Pradesh) where raw nuts arrive from African ports. The district has 10+ lakh employed across processing units and ancillary services, with established logistics to Mormugao Port (15 km) and Dabolim Airport (30 km). Buyers range from local spice traders in Panjim markets to global importers, with APEDA-registered exporting units already operational.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — North Goa ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Goa Cashew Exporters Association office in Panjim or Margao, call 3-4 existing processor-exporters (names available via APEDA directory), attend the monthly Panjim spice market haat on Tuesday mornings. Talk to at least one processor about their raw nut sourcing, processing cost per kg, and export logistics. Zero cost, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Buy raw cashew nuts from importer-wholesalers in Mormugao (port area) or Margao market — first batch of 500 kg costs ₹60,000-75,000 (₹120-150/kg imported rate). Build relationships with 2-3 suppliers for price negotiation. Expect 30% weight loss during processing.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 1,500-2,000 sqft unit in Margao, Vasco, or Panjim industrial area (₹8,000-15,000/month) with roasting, grading, and packing equipment. Initial equipment cost: ₹80,000-150,000 (2nd-hand roasting drum, grader, sealer). OR partner with an existing processor as a contract manufacturer (no rent, pay ₹8-12/kg processing fee). Month 1: ₹150,000-250,000 for rent, equipment, and first raw batch.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (free, 10 minutes). Apply for GI certification under Goa ODOP scheme (₹5,000-8,000, 2-3 months, proof: origin, quality test, member of Cashew Exporters Association). If exporting: obtain IEC from DGFT (₹1,000-2,000, 1 week), register with APEDA (₹500-1,000), get FSSAI license (₹500-2,000 for MSME, 2-3 weeks).
5
First Sale
First order: contact 5 local retailers (Panjim supermarkets) for 20 kg trial at ₹200/kg = ₹4,000. Simultaneously upload 100g and 200g packs on Amazon Seller Central (FBA), list at ₹350-400. Expect 2-3 units/day on Amazon by month 2, 50 kg/week to retail by month 3. First-month revenue: ₹15,000-25,000.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (~400 kg/month): hire 1 full-time packer (₹12,000/month), 1 sales/export coordinator (₹15,000/month). Exhibit at ICCOA (Indian Cashew Processors Council) annual fair, Goa Food & Spice Expo (Nov), APEDA export summits. Launch 3-4 flavored variants (chili, honey-roasted, organic). Target export orders via APEDA buyer-seller meets or direct LinkedIn outreach to importers.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
Pradhan Mantri Micro Units Development & Refinance Agency (MUDRA) Loan
up to ₹10 lakh at 4-6% interest, collateral-free, 5-year repayment.
🏛
Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)
₹10-25 lakh subsidy (25-35% of project cost) for manufacturing unit, requires ₹3-5L own capital.
🏚
Goa One District One Product (ODOP) Scheme
₹2-5 lakh export promotion subsidy, 50% subsidy on GI certification, market access support.
🏚
Goa Food Processing Industry Policy
₹5-10 lakh subsidy for cold chain, packaging unit, ISO certification (for units in designated zones).
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
