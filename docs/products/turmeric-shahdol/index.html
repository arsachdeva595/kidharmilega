Shahdol district in Madhya Pradesh sits at the edge of the Deccan plateau with seasonal rainfall supporting turmeric cultivation from April-July. The two production clusters (Jaisinghnagar and Beohari) operate across 500+ farming families, with rhizome seed sourced locally at standardized 2000-2500 kg/ha rates. Buyer access spans Indore spice market (350 km), Ujjain pharmaceutical hubs (280 km), and direct export corridors via Mumbai (750 km) and Delhi NCR markets.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Shahdol ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Jaisinghnagar or Beohari cluster this month — talk to 3-4 farmers directly, attend Shahdol district agricultural office, call turmeric buyers in Indore market (Rajwada area spice traders). Zero cost; takes 2-3 days.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source rhizome seed from local cooperative in Jaisinghnagar (contact Shahdol KRISHI VIGYAN KENDRA) at ₹2000-2500/kg/hectare. First batch for 1 hectare costs ₹2-2.5L seed + ₹3-5L land rental (if no family land). Beohari suppliers also available; compare varieties (Roma, Suroma, Saguna preferred).
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 1-2 hectare agricultural land in cluster area (₹1500-2500/month) or partner with 2-3 established farmers for processing arrangement. Boiling equipment (large vessels, drying yard) costs ₹80-150K if purchased, or use cooperative facilities. 6-8 month crop cycle from April sowing to December harvest. Processing (boiling 45-60 min, drying, polishing) takes additional ₹15-25K labor and utilities per tonne.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, free, 15 minutes on udyamregistration.gov.in). Obtain FSSAI food license (₹4500-7000, 10-15 days). If exporting: apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) at nearest office (₹0, online via aayat.nic.in, 3-5 days). GI registration for Shahdol turmeric is ongoing — monitor Ministry ODOP updates; pre-register brand to lock naming before GI award.
5
First Sale
First customer: Contact 2-3 spice traders in Indore's Rajwada area (INDORE SPICE MARKET) — typical first order 100-200 kg packed turmeric, ₹5-8L revenue. Parallel: List on Amazon Business, BigBasket seller portal (commission 10-15%). Target first D2C order via Instagram + WhatsApp marketing in month 3-4. Expected month 1 revenue ₹0-1L (sourcing stage); month 2-3 revenue ₹2-5L (first wholesale); month 4+ revenue ₹8-15L (combined channels).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: Hire 1 full-time operations manager (₹18-25K/month) and 2 part-time packers. Lease dedicated 2000 sq ft processing + packing unit (₹8-12K/month). Attend SIAL, Indofood expos for B2B export leads. Negotiate backward integration — directly source rhizome from 5-10 contract farmers at harvest, process and store year-round for steady supply. Apply for PMMY (Pradhan Mantri MUDRA Yojana) loan up to ₹10L for working capital expansion.
