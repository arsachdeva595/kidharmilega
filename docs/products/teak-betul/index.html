Betul district is blessed with vast natural teak forests covering over 45% of its geographical area, making it one of India's richest teak reservoirs. The district's ideal climate, soil composition, and forest management practices create optimal conditions for high-quality teak cultivation. Proximity to timber processing hubs and established supply chains to furniture centers in Indore and beyond amplifies its comparative advantage.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Betul ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Betul Forest Department office and Betul Chamber of Commerce; connect with established teak suppliers in Betul city and Amla region; study timber grading and kiln-drying processes at local sawmills; attend timber trade shows in Indore (120 km away)
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw teak logs from Betul Forest Produce Corporation or licensed private timber merchants; first-batch cost (2-5 cubic meters) approximately ₹8-12 lakhs; establish accounts with kiln-drying facilities in Betul or Indore for quality processing
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established sawmills in Betul city for log processing and drying (80% of founders do this initially); invest in CNC machines and finishing equipment if planning value-added products; alternatively, subcontract furniture manufacturing to Indore-based artisan networks
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Micro/Small business) with district MSME office; apply for IEC code from nearest APEDA office in Indore for export eligibility; register brand name with trademark office; obtain Forest Department clearance and timber transport permits
5
First Sale
Launch B2B outreach to furniture manufacturers via LinkedIn and IndiaMART; contact 50-100 interior designers in Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore via email campaigns; list on ExportGenius, TradeKey for international buyers; partner with 2-3 furniture e-commerce platforms (Urban Ladder, Pepperfry) for D2C channel; attend India Wood Summit and International Timber Trade Shows
6
Scale
After 6 months, establish own small showroom in Indore; hire dedicated export manager; scale to own processing unit with advanced drying and grading tech; expand D2C through own website and Shopify; build contracts with 5-10 major furniture OEMs; enter specialty markets (yacht teak, luxury hospitality)
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy (35% for general, 25% for SC/ST) for setting up wood processing unit
🏛
MUDRA Loan Scheme
up to ₹10 lakh collateral-free loan for timber business setup at 8-10% interest
🏚
Madhya Pradesh Forest Produce Promotion Scheme
preference to registered MSME timber processors for forest produce; subsidy on kiln drying equipment
🏚
Madhya Pradesh MSME Export Promotion Scheme
10% subsidy on export shipping costs for first 3 years for wood products exporters
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
