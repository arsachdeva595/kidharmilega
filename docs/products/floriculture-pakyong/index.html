Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Pakyong ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit established flower clusters in Pakyong (Singshore, Karfectak areas); spend 2 weeks shadowing nurseries like Pakyong Orchid Center and local growers; study climate data and soil testing reports from Sikkim Agricultural Department; join Sikkim Horticulture Association
2
Source Raw Materials
Source saplings from Darjeeling nurseries (₹5-15 per seedling) and Bangalore flower exporters; obtain growing media from Kerala suppliers via rail; first batch setup cost ₹1.5-2.5L for 0.5 acre greenhouse including shade nets, drip irrigation, and initial stock
3
Produce / Partner
Lease 0.5-2 acres of land in Pakyong block; construct medium-cost naturally-ventilated greenhouses (₹200-300/sq.ft); partner with Sikkim Agricultural Department for technical training; hire 2-3 local laborers at ₹8,000-12,000/month; start with orchids and gladioli (6-month harvest cycle)
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (₹0); apply for IEC code from Gangtok customs office (₹500, 7 days); create brand identity with registration at Gangtok District Industries Center; obtain organic certification from APEDA if pursuing organic floriculture
5
First Sale
First 3 months: supply 20-30kg weekly to Gangtok flower traders via direct contact and WhatsApp groups; register on FlowerAuction.com and FreshIndiaPro platforms (₹5,000-10,000/month commission); partner with 2-3 event companies in Gangtok; arrange 100-200 stems weekly shipment to Delhi via air-express (₹20-30/stem margin)
6
Scale
Expand to 2-3 acres by month 6-8; invest in cold storage facility (₹2.5L); develop branded packaging and website; establish direct contracts with 5-10 corporate gifting companies; export 5,000+ stems/week via Pakyong Airport to 3-4 international buyers; hire operations manager and account executive
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (35% for general, 45% for SC/ST) for setting up floriculture enterprise
🏛
Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana
₹20k/ha/year for 3 years if certified organic floriculture adopted
🏚
Sikkim State Agriculture Department Horticulture Promotion Scheme
40-50% subsidy on greenhouse construction and drip irrigation systems for floriculture
🏚
Sikkim Horticulture Mission Grant
up to ₹50,000 per beneficiary for certified nursery establishment and cold chain infrastructure
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
