The cluster has 400+ GI-registered weavers concentrated in Berhampur town, sourcing mulberry silk from Karnataka silk markets and local cotton from Odisha plains. Buyer markets are concentrated in North India (Delhi, Lucknow, Varanasi wedding seasons), with emerging export demand in USA and UK heritage markets.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Boudh ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call Boudh DC Handloom office (0765-2220286) or visit Berhampur weavers' haat on Thursday mornings. Spend 2 days with 2-3 master weavers in their homes, observe pit loom setup, costs, timelines. Free. Do this this month.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source silk from Karnataka silk merchants via Chennai or Bangalore wholesale markets (WhatsApp orders possible). First batch: 5 kg mulberry silk ≈ ₹2,500-3,500/kg = ₹12.5K-17.5K. Cotton from local Odisha mills (₹150-200/kg). First batch sourcing cost: ₹25K total. Supplier area: Berhampur town has 8-10 established silk agents.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent a weaving shed in Berhampur cluster (₹3K-5K/month for 400 sqft pit loom space) and hire 2 trained weavers at ₹8K-10K/month each. Option B: Artisan partnership — tie up with 2-3 master weavers on per-saree commission (₹2K-2.5K per completed piece). Initial setup: ₹80K-120K (shed rent 3 months + 2 pit looms ≈ ₹40K + working capital).
4
Brand & Register
Udyam registration (free, online at udyamregistration.gov.in, 10 mins). GI certification already exists for Berhampuri Patta — use this in marketing. File IEC with DGFT (₹0, online at ieconiline.gov.in if exporting). Total official cost: ₹0. Timeline: 1 week for Udyam, 3 days for IEC.
5
First Sale
First sale: Post 5 saree photos on Instagram, tag #BerhampuriSaree + wedding hashtags. List on Meera Pearls (takes 3-5 days approval). Target: 1-2 custom orders in first month at ₹18K-20K each. Expected first-month revenue: ₹25K-35K (1-2 sales). Use WhatsApp + Instagram DM for orders. Shipping via Blue Dart (₹800-1200 per saree in India).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 full-time operations person (₹8K-10K/month), rent showroom space in Delhi or Bangalore (₹15K-20K/month), partner with 8-10 weavers instead of 2-3, attend Wedding Fair season (Nov-Dec, Feb-Mar), launch Myntra/Amazon Artisan seller account. Margins compress from 60% to 35-40% due to platform commissions, but volume increases 3-4x.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Yojana
up to ₹15,000 toolkit assistance + ₹35,000 business loan at 5% interest for handloom artisans and entrepreneurs.
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25% subsidy on plant/machinery for SC/ST founders, 15% for general category, max ₹25L project cost.
🏚
Odisha Handloom Weaver Comprehensive Welfare Scheme
₹2,000/month pension + health insurance for registered weavers, improves staff retention.
🏚
Odisha SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹40 lakh cluster development grant (not direct to individual, but cluster-level support available).
