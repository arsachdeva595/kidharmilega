Bengal Patachitra ki
opportunity, Paschim Medinipur (West Medinipur) mein.

Naya village in Pingla block, Paschim Medinipur, sits 180 km from Kolkata (direct rail/road to major markets). The cluster hosts 150–200 established Potua families with 400+ years of accumulated knowledge, ensuring instant access to trained artisans, natural dye suppliers, and informal credit networks. Raw materials (cloth waste from Bengali mills, turmeric powder, raktachandan seeds) are sourced within 50 km radius; international buyers (EU, USA, Japan) access paintings via Kolkata port (120 km) and Delhi export hubs (1,200 km by road).

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Paschim Medinipur (West Medinipur) ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Naya village, Pingla block (2 hours by road from district HQ Medinipur town). Meet Potua artisans directly—no cost required. Call the DC Handicraft office (0144-222-5050 or check district ODOP portal) to get artisan contact list. Attend Pingla haat (weekly village market) to observe price points and buyer behavior. Register free Udyam account online (udyamregistration.gov.in) this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from Naya village suppliers directly or via Medinipur town's cloth waste dealers (near Medinipur railway station market) and spice merchants. First batch cost: ₹8K–₹15K for 5–10 paintings (cloth/paper: ₹1.5K/piece, natural dyes: ₹500–₹1K, labor: ₹4K–₹6K if artisan-hired). Supplier area: Naya village Pingla block or Medinipur town market; travel time 2–4 hours from any West Bengal city.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Partner with 2–3 Potua artisans in Naya village on piece-rate (₹4K–₹8K per finished painting). You handle sourcing raw materials, QC, and sales. Setup cost: ₹20K–₹50K (space rental in Medinipur or Kolkata for storage/finishing). Option B: Rent 200–300 sq ft studio in Medinipur town (₹5K–₹8K/month) and hire 1 experienced Potua on ₹12K–₹18K/month salary. Total 6-month setup: ₹60K–₹150K (rent, artisan salary, working capital).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME (free, online, 30 min). Apply for GI protection extension/licensing via DC Handicraft office (₹0–₹10K, 4–8 weeks approval). If exporting: apply for IEC code via DGFT (Directorate General of Foreign Trade) online—₹0 cost, 10 days approval. Get BIS/ISOIEC testing done (optional but buyers appreciate): ₹15K–₹25K for natural dye safety certification.
5
First Sale
List 3–5 paintings on Etsy with high-quality photography (phone camera acceptable). Target first D2C sale: ₹15K–₹30K per painting. Simultaneously reach out to 10 Kolkata heritage galleries (Kolkata Gallery Guide on Instagram) with portfolio—expect first wholesale order ₹40K–₹80K (5 paintings at ₹8K–₹16K each). Expected first 30-day revenue: ₹50K–₹150K (1–2 D2C + 1 wholesale order).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (12–15 paintings/month): hire second artisan (₹15K/month), upgrade studio to 500 sq ft (₹12K/month), invest in professional photography/videography (₹30K one-time), run Instagram/Facebook ads (₹5K–₹10K/month), exhibit at 2–3 national craft fairs annually (Surajkund Crafts Mela, New Delhi; National Handicraft Fair, Delhi; Kolkata Literary Festival craft stalls: ₹20K–₹50K per fair). Negotiate direct contracts with 5–7 corporate procurement teams and export agents.

Where to Set Up

Infrastructure ready hai — plot lo aur shuru karo

Medinipur MSME Cluster (Paschim Medinipur Handicraft Complex)
Located in Medinipur town (district HQ), houses 40+ textile, printing, and handicraft units. Plot sizes available: 1,000–5,000 sq ft. Common facility center for dyeing, finishing. Nearby logistics hub for export shipments.
MSME Cluster
Naya Village Craft Hub (de facto artisan cluster)
150–200 household-based Potua units concentrated in Naya village, Pingla block. No formal park infrastructure but strong peer network, raw material suppliers, skilled labor pools. Best for partnership/sub-contracting model.
Craft Village
Kharagpur Industrial Estate (50 km, larger backup option)
Multi-sector industrial estate with plot sizes 2,000–10,000 sq ft, better power/water backup. 1 hour commute to Medinipur. Useful if scaling to factory-like setup with multiple artisans.
