Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Visakhapatnam ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Visakhapatnam Fish Market (Sri Atchyutapuram) and Waltair fishing harbor to understand supply chains, pricing, and seasonal patterns. Connect with VFPDO (Visakhapatnam Fisheries Federation) and interview 10+ existing seafood traders. Study export protocols via APEDA website.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fresh catch directly from trawler owners at Waltair Port or through MATSYA fishermen cooperatives. First batch (500 kg frozen prawns + 300 kg fish) will cost ₹60,000-80,000. Establish relationships with 3-4 fishing boats for consistent supply.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing VCCI-registered processing units (Vizag Seafood Complex) or lease cold storage (₹5,000-8,000/month). No production required if acting as trader; outsource processing to certified facilities with FSSAI approval.
4
Brand & Register
Register as Udyam (online, 10 minutes). Obtain FSSAI license (₹6,000-15,000, 30 days). Apply for IEC code from DGFT office in Visakhapatnam. Brand name, packaging design, and QR code traceability system cost ₹50,000-100,000.
5
First Sale
Launch on Blinkit and Zepto with 5-7 SKUs (frozen prawns, fish varieties, mixed seafood packs). Target WhatsApp-based B2B sales to 50+ restaurants in Vizag, Bangalore, Hyderabad. Email outreach to 20+ hotel chains and catering companies. Minimum order ₹3,000.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to Flipkart, Amazon Fresh, and regional cold chain logistics (Essar, Allcold). Build direct export channel via APEDA-registered exporter license. Launch subscription model for weekly seafood boxes. Hire 2-3 BD executives to target corporate/hotel contracts.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (25% for general category) for food processing and export business setup
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹3-5L cluster development and common facility center support for seafood clusters
🏚
Andhra Pradesh Marine Resources Development Scheme
subsidy on cold storage, processing equipment, and quality testing facilities for registered seafood exporters
🏚
AP Seafood Export Promotion Scheme
5% subsidy on container freight charges and FSSAI/export certification costs for APEDA-registered exporters
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
