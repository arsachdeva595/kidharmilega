Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Ratlam ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Ratlam Namkeen Cluster (Sadar Bazaar area) — meet 3-4 established makers, ask about their production cost per kg, supplier names, recipes. Zero cost, takes 2 days. Call Ratlam District Industries Centre (phone available via MP.gov.in ODOP page) and request cluster profile — free info. Attend the weekly traders' congregation at Ratlam Sadar Market on Tuesday mornings.
2
Source Raw Materials
Raw materials: chickpea flour from Ratlam Milling Association (4-5 mills in Sadar, bulk ₹38-42/kg, MOQ 500kg), groundnut oil from Bansa Oils (Indore, ₹110-130/L), spices from Ratlam Spice Merchants on Ganesh Marg (cloves ₹800-950/100g, black pepper ₹500-650/100g). First batch cost (50kg finished sev): ₹12,000-14,000. Supplier area name: Ratlam Namkeen Cluster, Sadar Bazaar, Ratlam town.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (fast): Partner with 3-4 established local artisans on revenue-share model (they get 8-10% of sale price, you handle branding/sales). Cost: ₹80K-120K for packaging, labeling, initial inventory. Takes 30-45 days. Option B (control): Rent 500 sq ft unit in Ratlam industrial area at ₹6-8K/month, buy 1 semi-automatic sev extruder (₹200-250K secondhand from retiring units), hire 1-2 workers at ₹8-10K/month. Total: ₹350-500K setup, 60-90 days to first batch.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 10 mins). Apply for GI certification co-benefit through District Industries Centre (no cost, they manage filing). Get FSSAi registration (₹2,000-5,000, 15 days). If exporting: apply for IEC code via DirectorySyndicate or IEC portal (₹0, online, 3-5 days). Entire process: ₹2,000-5,000, 30-45 days.
5
First Sale
First sale: register on Amazon Seller Central or Blinkit vendor portal (free). Upload 3 SKU product listings (500g pouch, 250g pouch, 1kg bulk) with GI badge in title. Target first order: 10-15kg minimum (1,500-2,000 pouches) via B2B bulk platform. Expected first-month revenue: ₹40-60K (wholesale 30-40kg to 2-3 kirana chains + 10-15kg D2C). First gross margin: ₹12-18K.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 full-time salesman for wholesale cold-calling (₹12K/month), upgrade to semi-automated extruder (₹150-200K additional), expand to 2,000 sq ft unit (₹12-15K/month rent). Hire 1 packaging supervisor. Exhibit at Delhi Auto Expo regional snacks section (booth ₹80-120K), apply for One District One Product Export scheme subsidy (covers 50% of first export shipment up to ₹50K). Target: ₹20-30L/month by month 18.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)
up to ₹25 lakhs loan at 5% interest subsidy for food processing, eligible if manufacturing in rural/semi-rural Ratlam.
🏛
MUDRA Loan Scheme
unsecured ₹10 lakh loan within 7 days for working capital/machinery, no collateral, 6-8% interest.
🏚
Madhya Pradesh One District One Product Scheme
50% subsidy on first export shipment (up to ₹50K), free participation in 2 state-level exhibitions yearly.
🏚
MP Food Processing Policy 2020
20% capital subsidy on plant & machinery for food processing units, max ₹5 lakh; apply via DIC Ratlam.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
