Namakkal has established itself as the poultry hub of South India with a highly developed infrastructure for feed manufacturing, availability of quality raw materials, and a strong network of poultry farmers. The district's proximity to major agricultural zones in Tamil Nadu and Andhra Pradesh enables reliable sourcing of maize, soybean, and other feed ingredients. A concentrated ecosystem of feed mills, veterinary pharmaceutical companies, and hatcheries creates natural supply chain advantages.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Namakkal ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Namakkal feed mill cluster in Namakkal town and Koilkkarai — spend 1 week shadowing existing feed manufacturers. Interview 10+ farm owners in Erode and Krishnagiri districts. Study feed formulation at TANUVAS (Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences) in Chennai. Cost: ₹5,000-8,000
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from Namakkal Grain Market (maize, soybean), NCCF outlets (agricultural inputs), and pharmaceutical suppliers in Bangalore (vitamin/mineral premixes). First batch cost for 50 MT capacity: ₹8-12 lakhs. Build relationship with 2-3 suppliers to negotiate better margins.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing feed mill operators in Namakkal on contract manufacturing basis initially (30% margin) OR establish your own plant with installed capacity of 20-50 TPD. Hire feed technician from existing mills (₹20,000-25,000/month). Get formulations approved by your state veterinarian.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, 10 minutes, free). Obtain IEC from DGFT if exporting (₹500 + documentation). Register business under GST (18% for poultry feed). Get ISO 22000 certification (food safety) — cost ₹80,000-150,000. Trademark your brand name with IP office.
5
First Sale
Approach 20-30 farms directly with samples and nutrient comparison data. Build WhatsApp groups for 3-4 clusters (Namakkal, Erode, Krishnagiri). List on AgroStar, KisanKonnect. Partner with 2-3 veterinary pharmacies for retail distribution. Attend Poultry India Expo in Chennai. Target ₹30-40 lakh first 6 months.
6
Scale
Expand to 2-3 additional feed formulations based on farm feedback. Open 2-3 distribution hubs in adjoining districts (Erode, Salem). Build B2B platform or WhatsApp channel for direct farm orders. Explore export to Sri Lanka via Namakkal exporters network. Hire sales team of 5-6 people. Target ₹2-3 Cr revenue by 18 months.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy for manufacturing unit setup with 25% margin money required
🏛
MUDRA Loan Scheme
up to ₹10 lakh unsecured loan for working capital and equipment purchase through local banks
🏚
Tamil Nadu Agro-Processing Cluster Scheme
subsidy for setting up manufacturing units in identified clusters, including Namakkal poultry zone
🏚
State Animal Husbandry Department Subsidy
25-33% support for purchase of feed processing equipment and quality certification
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
