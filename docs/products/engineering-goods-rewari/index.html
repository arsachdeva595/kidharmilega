Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Rewari ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Zero cost: Visit Qutubpur industrial area on any weekday, walk into Jangid Industries and 10 other machinery units, ask to see their production floor, talk to owners for 30 mins each on cost, lead time, and buyer types. Join WhatsApp groups 'Rewari Engineering Manufacturers' and 'Dairy Machinery Buyers'. Register free on Udyam portal. This week — doable in one day from Delhi.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from Qutubpur (10km north of Rewari city center): mild steel coils from Sandhya Steel or Shri Ram Steels (₹45-55/kg), stainless steel from Shiv Metals (₹90-110/kg). First batch of 1-2 units costs ₹1.2-1.8L in raw material. Lead time 2-3 weeks. Supplier area concentrated on GT Road near Qutubpur toll plaza.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 1000-1500 sqft unit in Rewari MSME cluster or Qutubpur industrial area (₹8-15k/month). Buy basic welding, lathe, drilling, and assembly tools (₹4-6L one-time). Partner with 1-2 semi-skilled welders and 1 fitter (₹12-18k/month each). First machine takes 20-30 days to produce. Total monthly fixed cost ₹35-50k.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 10 mins). Apply for GI registration through State MSME directorate (₹2500 fee, 6-month process, covers 'Rewari Engineering Goods'). If exporting, get IEC from DGFT (₹5000, online, 2 weeks). Get shop & establishment license from municipality (₹1500). ISO 9001 optional but helps export (₹30-50k, 8-10 weeks).
5
First Sale
First sale: Post 3-5 machine photos + specifications on LinkedIn, WhatsApp to 50 dairy cooperative contacts (get list from your Qutubpur supplier network). Target 1st order from a tier-2 milk chilling plant (order size ₹2-3L, 45-60 day delivery). Use Indiamart as secondary listing (free). First-month revenue realistically ₹0-1.5L (slow first month is normal); expect 2nd-3rd month to hit ₹3-4L.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 1 dedicated sales person (₹18-25k/month), attend 2-3 regional food & dairy expos per year (₹80-120k/year), start targeted Google and Instagram ads (₹15-20k/month), partner with 2-3 equipment dealers in Gujarat and Punjab on commission. Expand workshop to 2500 sqft, hire 2 additional welders. Set up after-sales service franchise in 2-3 key cities.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Program)
up to ₹25L subsidy (25% for General, 35% for SC/ST) for setting up manufacturing unit in non-urban Haryana
🏛
MUDRA Loan
collateral-free ₹10L for working capital via MSME lending; disbursed within 7 days of registration
🏚
Haryana MSME Investment Subsidy Scheme
5% subsidy on plant & machinery cost (up to ₹50L max subsidy) for units in notified clusters; apply via Haryana MSME portal
🏚
Haryana Export Promotion Scheme
5% cash back on FOB export value for 3 years; apply via Export Promotion Board
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
