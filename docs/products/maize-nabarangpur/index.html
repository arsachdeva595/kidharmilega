Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Nabarangpur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Nabarangpur APMC (Agricultural Produce Market Committee) market on a Tuesday or Thursday; call 3 maize mill operators in Nabarangpur town (names available via APMC office); attend the weekly village haat in Nabarangpur block to understand farmer pricing. Cost: ₹0. Time: 4-5 days. Register on Udyam portal (free, online, 15 mins).
2
Source Raw Materials
Source maize directly from farmers or cooperative unions in Nabarangpur block (60,496 ha cultivation) at ₹23-25/kg during Kharif (Oct-Dec). First batch: 5 MT from Nabarangpur APMC or farmer collectives costs ₹1.15-1.25 lakh. Purity and moisture already meet 14% max requirement. Supplier area: Nabarangpur town and surrounding taluks.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 500 sq ft space near APMC for ₹5,000-7,000/month for grading, packaging, and value-add (corn flour/roasting). Partner with existing maize mills for bulk processing (drying, milling) on toll basis at ₹1.50-2/kg, avoiding capex. Manual grading initially: 3-4 workers at ₹300/day each. Total production cost range: ₹25,000-40,000/month for 2-3 MT output.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 10 mins, online). Apply for NFSM assistance in Nabarangpur (2023-24 cycle: up to ₹60 lakh per 1000+ hectare group; individual farmer benefits ₹3,000-5,000 per hectare). Obtain Food Safety & Storage License from District Health Department (₹2,000-3,500, 2 weeks). For export: apply for IEC code via DGFT portal (₹5,000, 5 days) and APEDA registration (free, 3 days). GI registration not applicable as no existing GI on Nabarangpur maize.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Business (B2B) or Udaan.com (wholesale platform) with first product batch: 500 kg packaged roasted/popped corn at ₹100/kg = ₹50,000. Target animal feed manufacturers directly via WhatsApp cold outreach to 15-20 contacts from APMC network; quote ₹24/kg for 1-2 ton monthly supply. First month projected revenue: ₹1.8-2.2L from mix of 3-4 small wholesale orders and D2C online sales.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (≈12-15 MT/month), hire 1 operations manager (₹12,000/month) and 2 graders/packers (₹8,000 each). Rent a 1,200 sq ft processing unit (₹12,000-15,000/month) with dedicated grading line, moisture meter, and packaging machine (capex ₹1.8-2.2L). Exhibit at Odisha Agro Expo (annual, Bhubaneswar) and approach organized feed manufacturers (ICCF, Godrej, Cargill) directly. Register as supplier on Udaan Premium and BigBasket B2B.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
NFSM (National Food Security Mission)
up to ₹60 lakh subsidy per 1000+ hectare farm group or ₹5,000 per hectare for individual farmers in Nabarangpur; covers seeds, fertilizer, drip irrigation
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Program)
25% subsidy on setup cost (max ₹25L for general, ₹30L for SC/ST) for maize processing unit; apply via district KVIB
🏚
Odisha State Agricultural Marketing Board
zero-interest loans up to ₹2L for farm input traders, cold storage, and processing units; administered via district agriculture department
🏚
Odisha MSME Subsidy Scheme
15% capex subsidy on machinery for food/agro processing units up to ₹1L; valid for registered micro-enterprises
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
