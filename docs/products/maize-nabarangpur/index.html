Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Nabarangpur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Nabarangpur Agriculture Department office and connect with farmer cooperative groups in Umerkote and Kharla blocks. Study existing maize mills like Nabarangpur Grain Milling Unit. Attend district agricultural fairs and connect with 5-10 established maize farmers to understand yield, costs, and market timing.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified maize seeds from Odisha State Seeds Corporation (Bhubaneswar) at ₹45,000-60,000 per quintal. Bulk fertilizer from district cooperative societies at ₹12,000-15,000 per tonne. For first 2-hectare cultivation, budget ₹3,50,000 including seeds, fertilizers, labor, and irrigation. Partner with existing mills for processing at ₹2,000-3,000 per tonne.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3-5 established maize farmers in Nabarangpur district under contract farming (80-100 hectares cumulative). Establish agreement on delivery quantity, quality standards, and price floor. Alternatively, lease 5-10 hectares through district land management portal and hire local agricultural laborers. Engage with Nabarangpur Maize Processing Cooperative for milling and packaging at ₹1.50-2 per kg processed product.
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam for MSME classification (online at udyamregistration.gov.in). Apply for IEC code from nearest DGFT office (Bhubaneswar) for export potential. Register brand name and packaging design with trade mark office. Obtain FPO (Farmer Producer Organization) certification or join existing FPOs in district for collective branding and quality compliance.
5
First Sale
Launch on BigBasket, Blinkit, and Flipkart Wholesale with ₹10-15k initial inventory per SKU. Create WhatsApp Business catalog and target 20-30 local wholesalers in Rayagada, Koraput, and Balangir. Approach regional modern retail chains (Spencer's, DMart) with samples and compliance certificates. Partner with 3-5 food processing companies needing maize inputs through B2B platforms like TradeKey and IndiaMART.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to 3-4 value-added maize products (maize flour, popcorn, cornmeal) with ₹15-20L investment in small processing equipment. Build direct-to-consumer presence via Instagram and Facebook with farm-to-table storytelling. Establish cold storage partnership in Nabarangpur town for year-round supply. Approach regional FMCG distributors and explore export to Nepal and Bangladesh via trade facilitation centers.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)
direct income support of ₹6,000 annually for farmer partners
🏛
Agricultural Infrastructure Development Fund (AIDF)
concessional loans up to ₹2 Cr for setting up maize processing and storage units
🏚
Odisha Farmers' Empowerment Scheme
₹25,000 grant per hectare for organic maize cultivation and certification
🏚
Odisha AGRI Export Promotion Scheme
5% subsidy on export value for agricultural products including processed maize to South Asian countries
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
