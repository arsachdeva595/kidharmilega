Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Ahmednagar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Phaltan or Rahuri sugar cooperative cluster (2-3 hour drive from district HQ). Call 3 existing unit owners — ask about current capacity utilization, cane procurement cost, and cooperatives they source from. Free. Register yourself on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) while planning. This week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Sugarcane procurement from Phaltan-Rahuri belt during harvest season (Oct-March) at ₹300-350/quintal. Form ties with 2-3 farmer groups or approach local cooperative (SSCL, Phaltan co-op) for bulk contracts. First batch: 50 tonnes = ₹15-18 lakhs raw material cost.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 5,000-8,000 sq ft industrial shed in Rahuri MSME cluster or Phaltan industrial zone (₹20-30k/month). Install boiler, crusher, pan, centrifuge, packaging unit. Equipment cost: ₹30-50 lakhs. OR partnership model: lease capacity from existing unit + supply your raw cane + pay ₹4-6/kg processing fee (capex drops to ₹8-12 lakhs for packaging + QC lab).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 20 min). Get FSSAI license (₹5,000-10,000, 15 days). ISO 9001 certification optional (₹30-50k, accelerates B2B sales). GI registration not applicable (Ahmednagar cane sugar has no GI). For export: IEC code (₹0-500, online via aatmanirbhar.dcis.gov.in), APEDA registration (₹500, 5 days). Total compliance cost: ₹6,000-12,000.
5
First Sale
Week 1-2: Register on B2B platforms (TradeKey, Global Sources, Alibaba) — list 10-20 MT capacity. Week 2: Pitch to 5 Mumbai/Pune distributors with sample (WhatsApp photo + spec sheet). First bulk order realistically: 10-20 MT from regional distributor at ₹44/kg = ₹4.4-8.8 lakhs revenue. Expected first-month sales: ₹3-5 lakhs (after you hit the right distributor).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 sales rep (₹20k/month) to manage export inquiries, 1 quality tech (₹18k). Exhibit at Agri-Expo Mumbai, Sweets & Spices Fair (Delhi). Invest ₹2-3L in product branding (label design, FMCG packaging trials). Reach out to 20+ confectionery/bakery units via direct email + sample kit. By month 12: aim for ₹25L/month (20 MT/month wholesale + 5 MT export).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Program)
₹25 lakhs collateral-free loan at 5% interest, 15% subsidy for SC/ST, 5% for general (cane processing qualifies as food processing)
🏛
Credit Guarantee Scheme (CGTMSE)
₹1 crore loan guarantee at 0.75% annual premium (de-risks bank lending for setup)
🏚
Maharashtra ODOP Scheme
preferential export promotion, participation in state fairs, ₹50k branding subsidy for registered units
🏚
Maharashtra Sugar Industry Subsidy
cooperative-linked units can access ₹5-10/quintal cane procurement subsidy during lean seasons (check MAHASUGARFED eligibility)
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
