Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Palakkad ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Palakkad Rice Cluster in Chittur and Ottapalam taluk; connect with established Matta Rice Producer Co-operative Societies (MRPCS); attend Palakkad Rice Festival (annual); study GI certification requirements at Ministry of Agriculture office in Palakkad
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified Matta paddy seeds from Kerala Agricultural University (Thrissur campus) or registered seed centers in Palakkad; contract with 10-15 local farmers for ₹8-12 lakhs for first 20-30 acres; bulk raw paddy costs ₹2,500-3,200 per quintal
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with government-registered rice mills in Chittur taluk (Palakkad Rice Mill Cluster has 150+ mills); negotiate milling, drying, and sorting at ₹200-300 per quintal; alternatively start with custom milling for first 6 months before investing in equipment
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, 10 mins) as agricultural producer; apply for IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Kochi; obtain GI-authorized right to use from Ministry of Agriculture (reference GI2008/0066); get organic certification from ISOPB/IORA (₹25,000-40,000); register brand name on IP India website
5
First Sale
Launch D2C via Amazon Seller Central and Flipkart with 5kg premium packs at ₹450-550/kg; create LinkedIn B2B account targeting exporters and bulk buyers; approach Organic Stores (Greenwave, Namastute) in Kochi/Bengaluru; list on 1mg Ayurveda and Health-focused platforms; negotiate first wholesale order with 1-2 regional distributors (Palakkad/Ernakulam) offering 30-day credit
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand B2B exports via direct trade brokers (contact APEDA for export list); invest in vacuum packaging and organic certification for premium D2C segment (margin up to 65%); launch white-label supply to regional organic chains; establish contract farming with 30-50 farmers; explore retail partnerships with premium grocery chains like Nature's Basket
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakhs loan with 35% central subsidy for micro-enterprises in agriculture sector
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
cluster development for rice processing units in Palakkad with up to ₹30 cr cluster grants
🏚
Kerala GKSY (Govt. Kishan Vikas Yojana)
interest-free loans up to ₹2 cr for agricultural ventures and allied activities
🏚
Kerala Agri-Export Promotion Scheme
5% export incentive and brand promotion subsidy for GI-tagged agricultural products
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
