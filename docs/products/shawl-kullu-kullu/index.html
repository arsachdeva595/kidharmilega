Shawl-Kullu ki
opportunity, Kullu mein.

Kullu district (5,503 km²) sits in the central Himalayas at 1,500-2,500m elevation, where local wool sourcing is direct and cheap due to regional sheep herding traditions. The district has sustained handloom weaving for centuries with most inhabitants as working artisans, creating a 3-shift production capacity and established dye houses. Buyer markets span tourists visiting Kullu valley (800,000+ annual visitors), Delhi metro (450 km road), and 50+ export-focused retailers in US/Europe.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kullu ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bhuntar weaving cluster (30km from Kullu town) to observe 2-3 established units and inspect loom capacity; call Kullu Handloom Export Association (office in Kullu bazaar) for supplier list; attend weekly Manali Saturday haat to watch direct tourist transactions and price discovery — all free, doable this week
2
Source Raw Materials
Source wool from Himachal Pradesh cooperative wool centers (wool depots in Bhuntar, Kasol) at ₹180-240/kg for grade A. First 50kg batch costs ₹10-12K. Dyers located in Bhuntar (20-30 units, each can dye 100kg weekly) charge ₹40-60/kg. First colored batch ₹3-4K. Establish supplier relationship with 2-3 master weavers in Bhuntar or Jaipur suburb who do piecework at ₹400-700/shawl.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (Recommended for first-gen): Partner with 3-5 established master weavers on piecework contract (you supply wool, they produce) — no factory rent needed, ₹0 for first 1-2 months. Option B (Scale faster): Rent a 1,200 sq ft unit in Bhuntar MSME cluster at ₹8-12K/month, purchase 4-6 traditional pit looms (₹40-50K each), hire 2-3 weavers at ₹10K/month each. Total setup ₹3-5L. Production timeline: 1 shawl takes 4-7 days on pit loom depending on complexity.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 5 mins, get UID); apply for GI-registered Kullu Shawl manufacturer certificate via Ministry of Handloom Kullu office (₹5K, 15 days, enables 'authentic Kullu shawl' claim). If exporting, file IEC with DGFT (₹0 online filing, activation 7-10 days). Get FSSAI registration if selling online (₹0 for manufacturing, food-agnostic). Seek PM Vishwakarma artisan subsidy (₹500/month for 18 months if registered as master or senior weaver).
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy (list 10 designs, ₹15K setup with photography/listing); contact 5 tourism retailers directly (emails, WhatsApp calls — try Jaipur City Palace, Manali Inn gift shop, Delhi airport retailers). First order target: 10-15 shawls from 1-2 wholesale buyers at ₹900-1200 net. Expected first month (month 2-3): ₹20-30K from Etsy + ₹15-25K from 1 wholesale order = ₹35-55K gross revenue.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month, hire 1 operations person (₹15K/month) and 1 logistics person (₹12K/month). Expand artisan network to 8-10 weavers (contract, not salary). Exhibit at 2-3 trade shows annually (Delhi Crafts Mela Jan, Delhi Haat, Jaipur City Palace Expo). Launch custom design service, partner with 2-3 interior designers in Bangalore and Mumbai. Set up WhatsApp catalog selling (600-800 contacts build, referral-based). Apply for SFURTI cluster grant (₹25L government subsidy for cluster improvement, shared across 3-5 participants).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹500/month stipend for 18 months + interest-free credit up to ₹1L for registered artisans/weavers, online registration, 5-7 days approval
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Program)
25% subsidy on equipment (up to ₹2.5L subsidy capped) for new handloom units, loan eligibility ₹1-10L, 8% interest subvention for 3 years
🏚
Himachal Pradesh Artisan Export Promotion Scheme
5% subsidy on export shipments (FOB value) for registered handloom exporters, reimbursement within 60 days of LCL/FCL shipment
🏚
HP MSME/Handloom Cluster Development Fund
₹15-30L shared subsidy across 5-member clusters for joint kiln/dye facility setup, 40% govt contribution, apply through District Industries Center Kullu
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
