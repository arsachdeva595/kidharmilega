Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Jamnagar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Jamnagar's old city Bandhani clusters (Khambhalida area, Rang Bazar) to study 10-15 master artisans' techniques. Spend 2-4 weeks apprenticing, understanding supply chains, and documenting traditional methods. Visit Jamnagar Chamber of Commerce and Government Handloom Department for market data and artisan networks.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source 100% pure cotton sarees/fabric from Ahmedabad textile mills (₹8,000-15,000 per kg bulk) or Surat markets. Buy natural indigo and mineral dyes from Jamnagar's established dye suppliers like Raj Chemicals, Jamnagar (₹2,000-5,000 per liter). First batch budget: ₹1-2 lakhs for 50-100 pieces quality fabric plus dye materials.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3-5 established Bandhani artisans in Jamnagar's textile clusters through local weaver cooperatives (SEWA, Jamnagar Handloom Board). Negotiate per-piece production rates (₹400-800 per saree depending on complexity). Start with 50 pieces monthly, scale based on orders. Ensure quality checks and GI tag compliance documentation.
4
Brand & Register
Register business under Udyam (Ministry of MSME portal) as micro-enterprise. Apply for IEC code from APEDA for export readiness. Register GI usage license with DC Handloom, Gujarat. Create brand identity emphasizing heritage and sustainability. Register trademark for brand name. Get ISO 9001 certification for quality control.
5
First Sale
Launch e-commerce on Meesho, Amazon Handmade, and own Shopify store with ₹50K initial investment. Post daily on Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest with artisan stories and process videos. Approach fashion boutiques in Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore with wholesale samples (₹5-8K minimum orders). List on Etsy for international customers. Reach out to export houses like SITA, Gitanjali Group.
6
Scale
After 6 months, hire a digital marketing manager for paid ads (Google, Instagram). Expand artisan network to 10-15 craftspeople. Launch corporate gifting division targeting luxury hotels, IT companies. Apply for export credit lines from banks (₹10-50L at 8-10% interest). Attend international trade shows (Paris Fashion Week, New York International Gift Fair) through APEDA export promotion program.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
Up to ₹3 lakh interest-free loan for artisans and their businesses, skill upgradation, and digital marketing support
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
Up to ₹25 lakhs subsidy (25-35% of project cost) for manufacturing and export-focused enterprises
🏚
Gujarat Handloom Policy 2023
10-15% subsidy on raw material procurement and 50% subsidy on brand development and international market participation
🏚
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
Up to ₹20 crores cluster development grant for Bandhani clusters in Jamnagar including artisan training, infrastructure, and marketing
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
