Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Jamnagar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call Vasant Shantilal Dubal or Dharmesh Manohbhai Bhojani (via Jamnagar Handloom Association, phone: District Handloom Office 0288-2550555) and request a 2-hour studio visit. Zero cost — ask about knot-dyeing technique, dye sourcing, timeline. Attend Sunday Bazaar at Kotha Market where local fabric vendors gather. Register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source untreated cotton sarees (60s count, 5.5m length) from Rajkot wholesale market (Hirawala Bazaar) at ₹300-500 per piece in bulk of 50. Source Jacquard silk from Ahmedabad (Girdharnagar) at ₹800-1200 per piece. Pure indigo and reactive dyes from Jamnagar Chemical Belt suppliers (Reliance, ONGC contractor area) at ₹80-150/kg. First batch cost for 20 sarees: ₹12K-18K materials + ₹6K tying labor = ₹18K-24K total.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (Recommended): Partner with 1-2 existing Bandhani artisans in Jamnagar on piece-rate basis. Cost: ₹150-300 per saree labor depending on design complexity, 6-8 week turnaround. Rent a 200 sq ft dye house (₹3-5K/month) near Kotha area for fabric washing and finishing. Option B: Hire 2 trained knot-tiers yourself (₹8-12K/month each) and rent 400 sq ft unit in Jamnagar Handloom Cluster (₹6-8K/month, 12-month lease). Recommend Option A for first 6 months to test demand with ₹0 fixed overhead.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, free, 10 min). File GI protected product claim with District Handloom Office (₹1000, 30 days). If exporting: Apply for IEC code at DGFT (₹0, 3-5 days online via dgftfiis.gov.in). Register brand name at IP office (₹5000, 3 months). Apply for DC Handloom artisan subsidy grant (₹50K-1.5L available under PM Vishwakarma for registered artisans) — coordinate via District Handloom Office.
5
First Sale
Launch on Meesho, Etsy, Instagram Shop simultaneously with 8-10 design variations priced ₹4500-8500. Target wedding season (Jan-March, Oct-Dec). First order size: expect 2-3 sarees in month 1 (₹12K-20K revenue). Build email list via website (Shopify free 3 months). Pitch to 5 ethnic wear boutiques in your nearest city (Mumbai/Bangalore) in month 2 with wholesale pricing (₹4-6K per piece). Expected first-month revenue: ₹15K-25K.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: Hire 1 FT designer (₹20-30K/month) for pattern innovation. Partner with 4-5 artisans instead of 1-2. Establish 1 dedicated dye house (₹500 sq ft, ₹8-10K/month). List on luxury platforms (Pernia's Pop-Up Shop, TheKoop) and export aggregators (India Trade Promotion Organization, GJEPC). Apply for SFURTI cluster development grant (₹5-10L one-time to upgrade artisan workspace). Target 40-50 sarees/month production. Attend India Craft Fair (Delhi, Bangalore) and International Textile Expos (Dubai, Paris) quarterly.
