Pathanamthitta has a centuries-old tradition of food processing rooted in Kerala's spice trade heritage and agricultural abundance. The district's ideal climate, access to coconut, rice, jaggery, and spices, combined with skilled artisans trained in traditional recipes, makes it uniquely positioned for food product manufacturing. The established market for traditional Kerala foods ensures ready demand and export potential.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Pathanamthitta ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Pathanamthitta's food processing clusters in Aranmula, Kozhencherry, and Ranni. Connect with 20+ existing Unniyappam producers. Interview artisans at the Pathanamthitta Food Processing Unit. Attend Kerala Food & Agricultural Products Expo. Study competitor products on Amazon, Flipkart, and specialty websites.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source rice flour from Emakulam rice mills (₹35-40/kg), organic jaggery from Attappadi cooperative (₹55-60/kg), cardamom from Idukki spice traders (₹600-800/100g), and coconut oil from local Pathanamthitta producers (₹150-200/liter). First batch of 50kg will cost approximately ₹8,000-12,000.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established artisan clusters in Kozhencherry (20+ units) or lease a 500-1000 sqft space with basic fryer setup (₹4-6L investment). Alternatively, adopt co-packing model with certified food manufacturers in Kochi Food Park. Ensure FSSAI license and food safety compliance from day one.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Micro/Small Enterprise) online via udyamregistration.gov.in (₹0 cost). Apply for IEC code from DGFT portal for export capability. File brand registration with IP India. Obtain FSSAI Food License (₹8,000-15,000). Get ISI mark for packaged food if scaling beyond ₹50L annual turnover.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh & Flipkart Super (apply via seller central, 10-15% commission). Start Instagram & Facebook shops with daily content on product heritage & recipes. Partner with 3-5 specialty food retailers in Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi metro areas. Create WhatsApp Business account for bulk orders. Offer first 100 customers 20% discount via Google Shopping ads targeted at 'Kerala sweets' searches.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to 5+ metro cities via modern retail partnerships (Spencer, Nature's Basket). Launch premium gifting line with custom packaging. Explore B2B corporate gifting with HR vendors. Apply for export certifications (APEDA registration) and launch on Amazon Global & Noon.com. Scale production to 10,000+ units/month via contract manufacturers.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Program)
up to ₹25L loan with 35% subsidy for SC/ST, 25% for general category, suitable for ₹3L-25L food unit setup
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹40L-1Cr cluster development grant for registered producer groups in Pathanamthitta food clusters
🏚
Kerala Food Processing Policy 2019
Capital subsidy of 25-30% for food processing units, emphasis on traditional products with ODOP status
🏚
KIIFB Export Promotion Scheme
8-15% subsidy on export shipments for food products from registered Kerala food processors
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
