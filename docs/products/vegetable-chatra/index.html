Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Chatra ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Chatra district vegetable markets (Chatra Mandi), connect with progressive farmer groups in Simaria and Tandwa blocks, study pricing at Ranchi vegetable mandi, analyze seasonal patterns and local production data from Agricultural Department Chatra
2
Source Raw Materials
Source seeds from ICAR research centers or certified suppliers in Bihar/Jharkhand; bulk fertilizers from local agricultural cooperatives; first batch estimated ₹150,000-300,000 for 1-2 acre pilot production with inputs and labor
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 50-100 small farmers in Chatra region through contract farming agreements; establish grading and packing unit in Chatra town; invest in basic cold storage (₹500K) for post-harvest management; hire agricultural technician for quality control
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam as FPO (Farmer Producer Organization) or MSME; obtain IEC code from nearest STPI office (Ranchi); register pesticide-free certification if applicable; design packaging with FSSAI license information; trademark brand name at IP office
5
First Sale
List on Jio Mart, BigBasket, and urban e-commerce platforms in Ranchi/Patna; supply to Reliance Fresh and local retail chains; partner with food delivery apps (Swiggy Instamart); establish direct relationships with 10-15 institutional buyers; create WhatsApp business groups for bulk orders
6
Scale
Establish cold chain logistics to Delhi/Mumbai NCR; build relationships with 3-5 organized retailers; launch private label products; develop value-added products (frozen vegetables, pickles); expand to 500+ farmers within 18 months; target ₹2-3 Cr revenue by Year 2
Government Schemes

PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh grant for agricultural processing enterprises with 25% subsidy
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
support for agricultural value-addition clusters with equipment and training grants
🏚
Jharkhand State Agriculture Department
Vegetable Production Promotion Scheme with 50% subsidy on cold storage and farm equipment for certified groups
🏚
Jharkhand AAJEEVIKA Scheme
support for farmer collectives and FPOs including training, certification, and market linkage development for vegetable producers
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
