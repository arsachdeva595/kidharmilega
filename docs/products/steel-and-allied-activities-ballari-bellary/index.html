Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Ballari (Bellary) ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Ballari Steel Cluster (Hospet area) and meet existing mill owners like JSW Steel, RINL facilities; study SAIL-Ballari operations; join Karnataka Steelmakers Association; analyze raw material costs at Ballari Mining Corporation sites; understand export logistics through Ballari Port Authority
2
Source Raw Materials
Source iron ore from Ballari Mining Corporation (₹4,000-5,000/tonne), coking coal from Singareni Collieries (₹8,000-10,000/tonne), limestone from local quarries (₹1,500-2,000/tonne); budget ₹50-100 lakhs for first 100 tonnes of raw material inventory; negotiate contracts with mining suppliers for long-term supply agreements
3
Produce / Partner
Lease operational capacity at existing steel mills or partner with smaller mills in Hospet cluster that offer processing on toll basis; alternatively set up mini-blast furnace with DRI route starting at ₹10 Cr; hire experienced mill managers from JSW/RINL who have retired; source second-hand equipment from Mumbai/Raipur scrap markets to reduce capex
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam MSME scheme immediately for concessional credit; obtain IEC code from DGFT for export eligibility; get ISO 9001 (quality), ISO 14001 (environment) certifications within 3 months; register with Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) for mandatory steel product certification; brand name registration under Indian Patents Office
5
First Sale
Secure first contracts through Ballari Chamber of Commerce and Industry; pitch to NBCC, L&T, Larsen & Toubro procurement teams; list on GeM (Government e-Marketplace) for government construction contracts; approach real estate developers in Bangalore/Hyderabad; use TradeKey, Alibaba for international B2B leads; attend BUILD India Expo and steel industry trade shows
6
Scale
After 6 months, invest in advanced rolling mill equipment to capture value-added segments; establish regional sales offices in Bangalore, Chennai, Pune; hire dedicated export manager; apply for Export Credit Guarantee Corporation (ECGC) coverage; join industry associations like Indian Steel Association; diversify into specialty steels (stainless, tool steel) commanding 40%+ higher margins

MUDRA Yojana
collateral-free loans up to ₹10 lakhs for MSME steel processing units through participating banks
🏚
Karnataka MSME Competitive Cluster Development Scheme
₹5-10 Cr cluster grants for steel processing units in Ballari cluster with infrastructure and training support
🏚
Karnataka Industrial Policy 2020-2025
5% interest subvention on term loans up to ₹50 Cr for manufacturing units; 30% capital subsidy for pollution control equipment
