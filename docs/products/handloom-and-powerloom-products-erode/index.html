Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Erode ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit the Erode Handloom Cluster in Neelambur or Dharampuri area (free, 3 hours). Call 5 established suppliers in Erode Cotton Market. Register free on Udyam MSME portal. Attend the monthly Erode Textile Trade Haat — all free, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from Erode Cotton Market (cotton yarn) and Bangalore's Chickpet market (silk yarn, dyes) — first batch ₹80-120K for 500 kg. Supplier area: Neelambur, Dharampuri, Bhavani — prices 15-20% cheaper than metro wholesalers.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent a small powerloom unit in Neelambur cluster (₹3-5K/month rent, 40-50 looms). Option B: Partner with 2-3 artisan weavers on commission (₹50-200 per piece). Total initial production cost: ₹80-120K for raw material, ₹40-80K for 3-month rent/artisan advances. Start with 200-300 pieces in month 1.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 20 mins). File for GI tag through Tamil Nadu Handloom Directorate (₹8-12K fee, 6-8 month process — optional but credibility booster). If exporting, apply for IEC code from Directorate of Foreign Trade (₹0, online, 48 hours). Get ISI certification for bed sheets if aiming at institutional buyers (₹15-25K, 30-45 days).
5
First Sale
First sales via Instagram direct DM + Meesho Supplier (free listing, 15-20% commission). Post saree reels with designs + price. Target first order: 50-100 units from a Tier 2 online buyer or retail chain contact. Expected first month revenue: ₹1.5-2.5L if you execute visibility.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: (1) Hire 1 operations manager + 1 design/photography person. (2) List on Ajio + Flipkart (₹50-100K onboarding). (3) Attend Heimtextil Frankfurt or India's Textprex Mumbai. (4) Secure 1-2 corporate contracts (₹5-10L per order). (5) Set up WhatsApp B2B catalog for wholesale buyers. (6) Expand to 150-200 looms by month 12.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
₹15,000 as advanced tool kit, 8% interest credit up to ₹1 lakh for handloom weavers; register on pmvishwakarma.gov.in
🏛
PMEGP (Pradhan Mantri Employment Generation Programme)
25% subsidy for General category up to ₹10 lakh project cost; 35% for SC/ST; apply via kvic.org.in
🏚
Free Power Scheme for Handloom and Powerloom Weavers
1,50,000 handloom + 80,000 powerloom weavers benefit with zero power tariff; apply at Erode District Textile Directorate
🏚
Monthly Pension Scheme
₹400/month for handloom weavers above 60 years (doubled from ₹200); benefits 13,000 eligible weavers; apply through local panchayat/collector office
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
