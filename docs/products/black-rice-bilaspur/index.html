Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bilaspur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bilaspur Agricultural Department office and Raigarh district's rice cultivation clusters. Connect with ICAR-IISR (Indian Institute of Rice Research) for black rice variety data. Study Bilaspur's Champa mandi for market pricing and competitor analysis.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified black rice seeds from state seed corporations or ICAR. Establish partnerships with 15-20 farmers in Bilaspur for cultivation. Budget ₹2-4 lakhs for first harvest sourcing covering 10-15 acres.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with rice mills in Bilaspur (Bilaspur Rice Mill Cluster) for milling and processing. Alternatively, establish a small rice processing unit with destoning, drying, and packaging equipment (₹8-12 lakhs capital).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME online. Apply for IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Bilaspur. Get FSSAI license (₹5,000-10,000). File trademark application for brand name. Get organic certification if marketing as organic.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon, BigBasket, Flipkart in 500g and 1kg packs priced ₹180-220 per kg. Create Instagram content showing farm-to-table story. Reach out to 50+ premium grocery stores in Raipur, Nagpur. Attend India Food Forum for B2B connections.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand farmer network to 50+ acres. Launch value-added products (black rice flour, ready-to-cook meals). Enter export markets via food export associations. Target 30-40% monthly growth by month 12.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L loan for rice processing unit setup with 35% subsidy for general category
🏛
Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY)
supports irrigation infrastructure for consistent black rice cultivation across seasons
🏚
Chhattisgarh Agricultural Promotion Policy
provides ₹5-10L subsidy for value-added processing and export of specialty crops including black rice
🏚
Indira Gandhi Parivar Yojana (IGPY)
offers crop insurance and credit support for black rice farming at reduced interest rates in Bilaspur
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
