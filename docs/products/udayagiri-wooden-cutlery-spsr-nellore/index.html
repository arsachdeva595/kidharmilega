opportunity, SPSR Nellore mein.

SPSR Nellore has a centuries-old woodcraft tradition centered in Udayagiri, where artisans possess hereditary skills in turning and finishing wooden utensils. The district's access to quality timber from nearby forests and a stable artisan community makes it ideal for scaling sustainable wooden cutlery production.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — SPSR Nellore ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Udayagiri village cluster (20km from Nellore city) to meet 8-10 established wooden cutlery artisans; study design trends on Etsy and Pinterest; attend annual Crafts Fair at DC Handicraft exhibitions; connect with existing GI-tagged producers to understand supply chain and pricing.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source timber from registered wood merchants in Nellore (Timber Depot Road) at ₹8,000-12,000/quintal for first batch of 500kg mixed wood; negotiate directly with artisans in Udayagiri for finished pieces at ₹40-80 per unit depending on design; first batch investment ₹1-1.5L.
3
Produce / Partner
Form direct partnerships with 5-8 artisan families in Udayagiri through village production cluster; establish quality control checks (moisture content, finish smoothness); provide design specifications via WhatsApp; offer steady monthly orders of 200-300 pieces minimum; consider renting small shared workspace in Udayagiri for packaging and branding.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) classification online at udyamregistration.gov.in for ₹0; obtain IEC code from DGFT Tirupati office (₹500) for export eligibility; trademark brand name at 4000-8000 via ipindia.gov.in; obtain GI certification leverage letter from DC Handicraft Ministry; display 'Handcrafted in India' mark on all products.
5
First Sale
Launch D2C via Shopify store with focus on eco-narrative; list on Etsy, Amazon Handmade (10-15% commission); approach eco-friendly kitchenware distributors (Greenway, FabIndia wholesale partners) with samples; reach out to 50+ eco-resorts in Goa/Kerala with bulk pricing; partner with gift portals like The Good Fill and Artisan Box for curated collections.
6
Scale
After ₹5L revenue, hire dedicated export coordinator; expand to 15-20 artisan partners; develop 3-4 signature design lines; pursue direct B2B contracts with European eco-retailers (via trade shows); set up small packaging unit; target ₹50L+ annual revenue by month 18.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
up to ₹15,000 toolkit assistance + ₹1.5L interest-free loan for traditional artisans + business training for enrolled woodcraft artisans.
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (35% for service sector manufacturing) for setting up production units; 10-year loan at 4% interest through designated banks.
🏚
Andhra Pradesh Handloom, Handicraft & Sericulture Policy 2021
₹5-15L cluster development grants, skill training subsidy (₹10,000 per artisan), and GI promotion fund (₹2-5L for GI product units).
🏚
AP Artisan Pension Scheme
₹2,000/month pension to enrolled traditional craftspeople; health insurance coverage; directly benefits your artisan network in Udayagiri cluster.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
