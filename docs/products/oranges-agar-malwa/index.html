Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Agar Malwa ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Agar Malwa district during harvest season (Nov-Jan); connect with established orange farmers near Agar town and Susnergarh cluster. Study farming practices at Agar Malwa Agricultural Cooperative and visit Indore Fruit Mandi to understand buyer preferences and pricing.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source quality saplings (₹20-30 each) from Indore horticulture nurseries or ICAR-CTCRI centers in Madhya Pradesh. For mature orchards, partner with existing farmers on revenue-sharing basis. First-batch land lease costs: ₹15,000-20,000 per acre annually; initial fertilizer and pesticide costs: ₹40,000-50,000 per acre per season.
3
Produce / Partner
Either lease 2-5 acres of productive orange orchard (8-10 year trees yield 15-20 MT per acre) or form a farmer producer company (FPC) with 5-10 local growers. Set up basic post-harvest facility (cold storage, sorting shed) costing ₹2-3 Lakhs. Partner with agricultural extension officers at District Agriculture Office, Agar Malwa.
4
Brand & Register
Register as Udyam (agricultural business) on udyamregistration.gov.in. Apply for IEC code via DGFT (online in 7 days) if planning exports. Register brand with State Agricultural Department. Obtain FSSAI license (₹2,500-5,000) for value-added products. Trademark brand name with IP office (₹5,000-10,000).
5
First Sale
Launch on Blinkit and JioMart (zero commission for agricultural produce for first 3 months). Register with AgriMarket and e-NAM (National Agriculture Market) for B2B wholesale. Build direct relationships with fruit traders at Indore Fruit Mandi and Azadpur Mandi, Delhi. Use WhatsApp Business to manage bulk orders from restaurants and juice shops.
6
Scale
After 6 months: Establish cold chain logistics partnership with local transport operators; add 2-3 more acres. Develop branded orange juice SKU for retail (FSSAI certification). Explore group membership in National Horticulture Board export subsidy scheme. Launch seasonal export shipments to Bangladesh and UAE via trade houses. Build loyalty with 20-30 HORECA accounts by month 12.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM-KISAN
₹6,000 annual cash support for farmer members; PMEGP — up to ₹25L grant for agribusiness startups with 25% subsidy
🏛
SFURTI (Cluster Development)
up to ₹45L for citrus processing clusters in Agar Malwa district; National Horticulture Board subsidy for export-quality infrastructure
🏚
MP Horticulture Mission
50% subsidy on drip irrigation, mulching, and micro-nutrient management for orange orchards
🏚
MP AgriTech Fund
grants for storage, processing units, and cold chain logistics; MP Farmer Producer Organization Scheme — support for FPC registration and capacity building
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Marketing

Ye marketing ideas try karna mat bhulna

  • Partner with 5-star HORECA chains (Vivanta, ITC hotels) in Indore and Mumbai for direct supply with co-branded packaging. Offer 15% seasonal discount and consistent quality assurance. Create LinkedIn thought leadership on sustainable citrus farming.
  • Run Instagram/Facebook carousel ads (₹10,000-15,000 monthly budget) targeting health-conscious urban audiences with #FarmToTable content; partner with 10 micro-influencers (50k-200k followers) in major cities for unboxing videos and recipe content.
  • Participate in AgriExpo trade shows (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore) organized by APEDA and National Horticulture Board. Sponsor local fruit festivals and melas in Indore and Ujjain. Build WhatsApp communities of 500+ wholesale buyers for direct sales.
