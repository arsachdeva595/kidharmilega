Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Annamayya (Rayachoty) ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Rayachoty textile cluster (government website: annamayyarayachoty.ap.gov.in lists contact points). Call 3-4 denim suppliers in Hyderabad Textile Hub (Charminar area — WhatsApp numbers available from district ODOP office). Attend Rayachoty weekly Thursday haat to meet 8-10 local tailors and understand labor availability. Register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) — takes 2 hours.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source denim from: (1) Tiruppur mills — 40-50 kg minimum order at ₹280-380/kg (door delivery). (2) Hyderabad textile merchants (Charminar area) — 20-30 kg orders at ₹320-420/kg. (3) Local cotton ginners in Annamayya taluk — raw cotton at ₹4200-5200/100kg bale. First batch budget: 100 units = ₹42000-65000 for fabric + ₹8000-12000 for threads/accessories.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent 1000-1200 sq ft unit in Rayachoty MSME cluster at ₹8000-12000/month, install 8-10 sewing machines (₹120000-180000 one-time). Option B: Partner with 5-8 home-based tailors in Rayachoty + hire 1 quality checker (₹15000/month). Production cost per unit: ₹180-260 (fabric ₹120-160 + labor ₹60-100). Minimum setup: ₹280000-450000.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 2 hours online). File IEC code with nearest DGFT office (Hyderabad, 150 km — ₹0 cost, 5 days processing). Apply for ODOP scheme support (₹10-25L subsidy available for registered units under AP state scheme). Trademark your brand name at CGPDTM (₹4500-9000, 8 weeks). ISO 9001 certification optional but adds 12-15% price premium (₹80000-120000, 12 weeks).
5
First Sale
First sale: Register on Flipkart Seller Hub or Myntra Vendor portal (commission 25-35%). List 20-30 SKUs (sizes 28-36 waist, 2-3 wash types). Minimum order: 5-10 units. Expected first month: 15-30 units at ₹1200 avg selling price = ₹18000-36000 gross. Parallel: Cold-email 10 wholesale buyers (Spencer's, Westside) with catalog PDF + factory photos (lead time 14 days).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: (1) Hire 1 operations manager + 1 export coordinator (₹25000/month combined). (2) Add 4-6 sewing machines and expand to 15-20 home tailors. (3) Exhibit at India Expo Centre apparel show (Delhi) or TEXPROCIL events (Mumbai). (4) Launch TikTok/Instagram Reels showing behind-the-scenes production (10-15 posts/month). (5) Apply for PMEGP subsidy for second facility. Transition to full retail brand within 18-24 months.
