Maize ki
opportunity, Sheohar mein.

Sheohar district spans 442.99 sq km with 656,246 population distributed across 5 administrative blocks and 203 villages, creating a consolidated maize-growing region in North Bihar. Farmer clusters are concentrated in blocks like Sheohar proper, Sewapuri, and Supaul-adjacent areas where irrigation from rainfall and tube wells supports December–March harvest cycles. Primary buyer markets lie within 100-200 km: poultry feed plants in Madhubani and Darbhanga, milling clusters in Sitamarhi, and export aggregators in Patna; Nepal and Bangladesh markets are 150-300 km away.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sheohar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sheohar main mandi or Sewapuri farmers market on a Tuesday/Saturday; call 3-4 maize traders at Sheohar mandi (ask for Maize Vyapar Sangathan secretary for list); attend Sheohar district agriculture office farmer group meeting (free, held monthly). Register on Udyam portal (free, takes 20 mins online).
2
Source Raw Materials
Source directly from farmer clusters in Sewapuri and Sheohar blocks during Oct–Jan harvest. First batch: 5-10 tonnes from 4-5 farmer contacts at ₹18-21/kg (wet grain, harvest price). Storage: arrange 500 sq ft drying yard locally (rent ₹2000-4000/month) or tie-up with existing miller who dries for 5% commission. First batch cost: ₹90k-210k + drying/storage ₹5k-8k.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (Low-cost): Partner with existing miller in Sitamarhi cluster (milling cost ₹1.50-2/kg, they do cleaning+grinding). Option B (Medium): Rent 800 sq ft unit in Sheohar town near mandi (₹8k-12k/month), install small huller+grinder (₹150k-280k used equipment from Madhubani suppliers). Start with 10-15 tonnes/month. Hiring: 2 casual workers ₹6k/month each for bagging, labeling, loading.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 10 mins, submit Aadhaar+PAN). Apply for FSSAI food licence (₹5k, 2-3 weeks). Optional: GI registration costs ₹25k-40k and takes 4-5 months (file at Bihar Commerce Dept) — skip unless selling as 'Sheohar Maize' premium brand. Get IEC for export (₹0, filed with customs if/when exporting) — takes 1 week online via ICEGATE.
5
First Sale
First sale: list on Udyam.gov.in marketplace (free) + Indiamart.com B2B portal (₹500/month); cold-call 5 poultry feed plants with 2kg samples (cost ₹300). First order: 50 kg to local wholesaler at ₹22/kg (net ₹1100 revenue). Expected first-month revenue: ₹40k-80k from 2-4 small wholesale orders + 50-100 units D2C via word-of-mouth.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month turnover: upgrade to 2000 sq ft unit with automated bagging (₹200k capex), hire 1 full-time manager (₹12k/month) + 4 workers. Target 100+ tonnes sourcing. Exhibit at Agri Expo North (Patna, Jan–Feb) and Agrotech India (Delhi). Partner with ISAP India Foundation (active in district) for export certifications and buyer linkages. Develop 3-4 SKUs (organic flour, cornmeal, animal feed-grade).

Sheohar MSME Food Processing Cluster (proposed)
District administration planning 15-acre food park with common infrastructure; currently 8-12 small mills operate independently in Sheohar town. Plot sizes 500-1500 sq ft available for lease. Existing tenants: flour mills (5), dal processors (3).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹15,000 interest-free loan + 5% govt subsidy on raw material purchases (capped ₹3 Cr transactions); no collateral required for MSME food processors
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25-35% subsidy on capex (₹25 Lakhs limit for OBC/General, ₹20L limit); loan via SIDBI-partnered banks
🏚
Bihar Agricultural Enterprise Development Fund
₹5-10L concessional loan at 4% interest for food processing units; 2-year moratorium
🏚
Bihar Direct Farmer Marketing
exemption on mandi tax for farmers selling direct to processors; ₹10k rebate on bulk storage infrastructure rental
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Bilkul. Nepal, Bangladesh, and UAE are main buyers. Need: FSSAI cert (already planned), IEC code (free, 1 week online), phytosanitary certificate from agriculture dept (₹200, 3 days). Export price ₹25-32/kg FOB vs domestic ₹20-23/kg. Start small (5-10 tonnes/month) to Nepal via road border trade.
