Dharwad district in north Karnataka sits at the confluence of agricultural zones producing sugarcane, groundnut, dairy, and spices — raw materials for processed foods and packaged goods. The Mummigatti FMCG cluster park, announced in 2019 and inaugurated by CM Basavaraj Bommai in October 2022, provides ready-built shared facilities, testing labs, and knowledge centers. The cluster connects via NH-48 to Bangalore (320km), Goa, and Mumbai, enabling both domestic retail chains and direct exports to Southeast Asia and Gulf markets within 36 hours.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Dharwad ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Dharwad FMCG cluster park at Mummigatti and speak with the cluster authority (contact: Dharwad DPIIT office). Call 3-4 existing tenants to understand real setup timelines and costs. Register on Udyam portal (free) with your Aadhaar and PAN. Attend the quarterly ODOP farmer-entrepreneur meet at Dharwad District Industries Centre.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from two channels: (1) Dharwad agricultural mandis (sugarcane, groundnut, dairy — rates 15-25% below metro markets), (2) Certified suppliers in Belgaum and Belagavi cluster (20km away). First batch costs: ₹3-8L for 500-1000 units of a shelf-stable product. Supplier area: Dharwad Mandi complex (vegetables, grains) and Belgaum APMC.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 1000-1500 sq.ft. production unit in Mummigatti cluster park (₹25-40/sq.ft./month = ₹25-60K monthly). Cluster provides pre-fitted units with water, power, and waste management. Alternatively, partner with 2-3 women's self-help groups (SHGs) in Dharwad taluk (lower capex, risk-sharing). Full production setup (machinery + furnishing): ₹18-35L. Cluster will fast-track FSSAI license within 30 days if you're a tenant.
4
Brand & Register
Register on Udyam (free, online, 10 mins). Apply for FSSAI food business license (₹5000-12000, 30-45 days). If exporting, apply for IEC code via Rajiye Trade (free online, processed in 5-10 days via cluster office). Trademark your brand name at IP India (₹4500-9000, 18-24 months). GI registration not applicable for FMCG products unless it's region-specific spice blend or organic certification (NPOP: ₹8000-20000, 6 months).
5
First Sale
First sale: Contact Amazon Sme (Bengaluru Regional Manager) or Flipkart seller hub (requires GST + FSSAI). Typical first order size from e-commerce: 200-500 units. First-month revenue expectation: ₹1.5-3L (if 400 units × ₹400-600 retail price). Alternative: Approach Modern Trade via foodservice distributors (Emami AgroVet, ITC eChoupal) — MOQ 2000-5000 units, payment cycle 30-45 days.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (typical at 8-10 months), hire: (1) Sales manager to manage e-commerce and wholesale channels, (2) QA-QC technician, (3) Packing operator. Move to larger 3000 sq.ft. unit in cluster (₹60-90K/month). Exhibit at Anutex (Ahmedabad, Feb-Mar), Gulfood (Dubai, Feb) to access bulk export orders. Apply for PMEGP subsidy (₹10L loan, 25% grant) to fund second production line.

Government Schemes

PMEGP
₹10L loan at 4% interest, 25% grant (₹2.5L) for SC/ST/women entrepreneurs, 35% for general; covers machinery, working capital

MUDRA Shishu scheme
₹50K loan, zero collateral, quick approval within 7 days via Nodal Bank
🏚
Karnataka Startup Incentive Scheme
3 years property tax waiver + exemption on stamp duty (₹2-5L savings) if registered as startup in Dharwad cluster
🏚

₹50L cluster-level grant for infrastructure, testing labs, training; Dharwad cluster already approved
