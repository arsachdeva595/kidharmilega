Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Garhwa ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Garhwa carpet clusters in Bagodar, Tikar, and Chandwa blocks; spend 1-2 weeks learning loom operations, natural dye preparation, and design techniques from established weavers; network with DC Handicraft office in Garhwa for artisan directory.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source wool from Jharkhand Handloom Corporation or direct suppliers in Ranchi (₹150-200/kg); natural dyes from local herbal markets in Garhwa town (₹5-15/kg per dye); jute backing from Kolkata suppliers (₹30-40/kg); initial batch of 10 small carpets (4x6 ft) costs ₹80,000-120,000.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established weaving families in Bagodar cluster (nearest major weaving hub, 25km away) or establish your own 2-4 loom unit in Garhwa town; negotiate revenue-sharing (typically 60% artisan, 40% coordinator) or direct hire at ₹400-600/day per skilled weaver.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam as micro-enterprise under Handicraft sector; apply for IEC code via DGFT portal for export eligibility; register brand name with DC Handicraft for 'Garhwa Carpet' authenticity certificate; file GI claim if targeting premium positioning.
5
First Sale
List on Etsy, Amazon Artisan, and Amabasador with 8-10 high-res photos and detailed craftsmanship story; approach 10-15 interior design studios in Ranchi and Dhanbad with sample carpets; email hospitality chains (Airbnb hosts, boutique hotels) in Tier 2 cities; launch Instagram content showing artisan process.
6
Scale
After first 6 months, expand loom capacity by 2-3 units; develop 15-20 signature designs; hire dedicated e-commerce manager; approach trade export houses for wholesale; attend IHGF Delhi Fair and International Craft Fair (Jan, March); build corporate tie-ups with 3-5 hospitality chains.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Yojana
up to ₹3L interest-free loan + ₹15,000 toolkit grant for registered artisans; 5% cashback on transactions
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L loan with 35% subsidy (general) or 45% subsidy (SC/ST/women) for micro-enterprises
🏚
Jharkhand Kaushal Mission
free skill training in carpet design and advanced weaving techniques for 100+ artisans annually
🏚
Jharkhand Artisan Credit Guarantee Scheme
zero-collateral loans up to ₹10L at 5% interest through partner banks for registered handicraft units
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
