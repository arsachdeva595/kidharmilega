Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Aligarh ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Aligarh lock cluster this week (Akbari Gate area, Jama Masjid road — free). Call 5-6 manufacturers directly (numbers available via local chamber of commerce). Attend Aligarh MSME Meet (monthly, check Aligarh MSME Association website). Walk through Brass Bazar for raw material pricing. Call freight forwarders on Shamshabad Road for export cost quotes. Zero cost, 3-4 days of boots-on-ground.
2
Source Raw Materials
Raw materials: brass scrap from Meerut Brass Association (₹380-420/kg), zinc ingots from Hindalco dealers in Aligarh (₹250-290/kg), mild steel from Jindal dealers (₹45-55/kg). First batch cost: ₹80K-150K for 500kg mixed metals. Supplier area: Brass Bazar (Akbari Gate) and Shamshabad Road have 15+ verified dealers. Payment: 50% advance, 50% on delivery (standard).
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent 800-1200 sqft unit in Aligarh MSME Industrial Estate (₹6K-10K/month rent). Option B: Partner with 3-5 artisans in home-based workshops (40% outsource cost, you manage QC). Machinery cost if in-house: ₹4-6L (lathe, drilling, polishing units, molds). Rent + utilities: ₹10K/month. First production: 100-150 units/day in-house or 300-500 via artisans. Cost range: ₹3-6L capital, ₹12-15K monthly overhead.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME (free, 10 min online at msme.gov.in). Apply for GI tag usage license via IP Facilitation Center, Aligarh District Industries Center (₹5K-10K, 30 days). Get ISO 9001:2015 certification via TUV India or BVQI (₹80K-150K, 6-8 weeks). Get IEC code from DGFT (free, 5 days online) if planning exports. TAN and GST registration (free, 5 days).
5
First Sale
First sale platform: Register on GeM (free) and bid for institutional tenders (₹10K-50K orders). Parallel: contact 3-5 hardware distributors in Delhi/Bangalore with samples (WhatsApp + cold calls — target order size ₹50K-100K). Expected first month: ₹1.5-3L revenue from 2-3 wholesale orders + 1 institutional tender.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 full-time QC officer (₹15K/month), 1 export sales coordinator (₹12K/month). Rent separate finishing and polishing workshop (₹15K/month). Exhibit at IITF Delhi (Nov), Canton Fair (spring) — budget ₹3-5L per show. Add 2-3 B2B platforms (Alibaba, EC21). Engage with EEPC India for export market linkages.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
35% subsidy on fixed capital up to ₹25L, max subsidy ₹8.75L for manufacturing.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹40L cluster-level support for artisan clusters; individual benefit ₹10-15L for tooling and infrastructure.
🏚
UP MSME Udyam Vikas Yojana
₹1L-5L concessional loan via NABARD (5% interest subsidy for 3 years).
🏚
UP One District One Product (ODOP) Scheme
₹50K-2L support for brand building, packaging, and export market participation.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Frequently Asked Questions

Seedhe sawaal, seedhe jawab

₹2.5-8L startup cost: ₹1.5-3L raw material + ₹0.8-2L machinery + ₹0.2-1L rent + ₹0.3-0.5L registration/testing. Agar artisan-based model, toh ₹2.5-4L kaafi hai. Agar in-house full setup, toh ₹6-8L.
Hardware retail ya manufacturing experience helpful hai lekin zaroori nahi. Lock-making ka technical knowledge CNC operators se aata hai (hire them). Sabse zaroori: quality control ka discipline aur buyer relationships. Aligarh mein 6 months training workshops hain free/paid — district training institute se join kar lo.
PMEGP: ₹8.75L subsidy tak (35% of capital). SFURTI: ₹10-15L toolkit support. ODOP scheme: ₹50K-2L branding + export help. UP Udyam Vikas: ₹1-5L loan at 5% discount. Udyam registration free hai. GI tag usage: ₹5-10K one-time license fee.
3 raaste: (1) GeM portal par tender lagao — ₹10K-50K orders common hain. (2) Local hardware distributors ko samples + price list bhejo — they'll bulk order. (3) Direct B2B calls to builders in nearby metros. First 30 days mein ₹1.5-3L order expected.
Haan, aasan hai. Middle East (UAE, Saudi, Kuwait), Africa (Kenya, Tanzania), Southeast Asia (Vietnam, Bangladesh) main demand strong hai. Process: Get IEC code (free, 5 days), open LC with bank, ship via EEPC-affiliated agents, FOB margin 35-50%. Min order size ₹3-5L usually. Port nearest: Mundra (800km) ya Nhava Sheva (1000km).
Realistic risk: quality issues (reject orders), market oversupply (price fall), and slow receivables (buyer payment delay). Mitigation: 1000+ small-scale producers exist — market absorbs entrants but unfit ones exit within 18-24 months. Exit option: Machinery bikta hai ₹3-4L mein. Artisans shift to other producers. Raw materials recoverable. Total loss worst case: ₹2-3L (20-40% of investment). Cluster presence + GI tag = recovery possible.
