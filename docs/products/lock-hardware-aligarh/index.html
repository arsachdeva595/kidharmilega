Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Aligarh ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Aligarh lock clusters (Koil Bazar, Naya Bans areas) and meet 3-5 established manufacturers to understand production processes, cost structures, and market dynamics. Attend annual Trade Fair exhibitions in Aligarh to benchmark products and identify potential customers.
2
Source Raw Materials
Contact suppliers in Aligarh Industrial Area for mild steel sheets (₹30-35/kg), springs (₹80-100/kg), brass alloys (₹400-450/kg). Source first batch of raw materials (₹3-5 lakhs) from established vendors like Hindalco Industries or regional Aligarh suppliers. Negotiate 30-day payment terms.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established artisan groups in Aligarh registered under SFURTI schemes (e.g., Aligarh Lock Makers Association) or invest in CNC machines and manual assembly lines. Hire experienced foremen from existing units at ₹25,000-40,000/month and semi-skilled workers at ₹12,000-18,000/month.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME classification), obtain IEC code from DGFT for exports, apply for BIS certification (IS 4873 for padlocks, IS 1695 for mortise locks), register brand name at Aligarh Collectorate. Budget ₹50,000-100,000 for certifications and registrations.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Business, Flipkart B2B, and IndiaMART within 4 months. Approach 20-30 local hardware stores (Modi Nagar, Delhi areas) with samples and 10% introductory discounts. Cold-email 50 construction companies and real estate developers with product specifications and pricing.
6
Scale
Expand product SKU range (digital locks, smart locks alongside traditional locks). Target tier-2 cities through regional distributors (Lucknow, Kanpur, Agra). Launch export-focused business development by month 8—leverage India Trade Promotion Organisation (ITPO) to participate in Frankfurt/Dubai trade fairs.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
₹15,000 non-interest-bearing loan for traditional artisans in lock-making sector with skill training component
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh loan subsidy (25-35% depending on location) for manufacturing units in Tier-2/3 areas
🏚
Uttar Pradesh ODOP Scheme
GST reimbursement up to 5 years, market linkage support, and free participation in state-level trade fairs
🏚
Uttar Pradesh Micro & Small Enterprise Cluster Development Scheme
subsidy for common facility centers, testing labs, and working capital support for ODOP artisans in Aligarh
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
