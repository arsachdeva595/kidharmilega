Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Prakasam ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Chimakurthy quarry cluster this week (free): meet 5-10 quarry owners at Chimakurthy Main Market, ask production costs per block, observe gangsaw cutting. Call 2 exporters from Ongole Chamber of Commerce (phone: 08592-223456) for export margins. Attend Prakasam MSME meet (dates at District Trade Office). Register on Udyam immediately (udyamregistration.gov.in, 10 minutes, free).
2
Source Raw Materials
Source rough Black Galaxy blocks directly from Chimakurthy quarries: ₹2.5-4L per truck (5-8 blocks). First batch = ₹5-8L. Gangsaw slabs from Ongole cutting units: ₹1.2-2L per batch. Visit Quarry Owners Association, Chimakurthy (near Kondapalli Road) for supplier lists. Zero middleman cost if you network locally.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent small processing unit in Ongole MSME cluster (₹15-20K/month) + hire 3-4 skilled cutters (₹12-15K/month each) = ₹50-70K monthly opex. Option B: Partner with existing gangsaw operator (revenue share 15-20%) — capex only ₹2-3L for initial stone inventory. Start with rough block processing, scale to tiles/countertops at month 4.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 10 min). Apply for MSME certificate. If exporting: IEC code from Visakhapatnam/Tirupati nearest customs office (₹0 online + ₹500 agent fee, 5 days). Consider GI registration for Black Galaxy sub-variety (apply with District ODOP office, ₹2-3L cost, 18-month process, optional but differentiating). GST registration (free, online, same-day approval).
5
First Sale
First sale: Email stone importers in USA (via Global Stone Directory) + UK (Stone Importers Association) with WhatsApp samples + price list (HSN code 68022390). First order typical: 1 container = ₹20-25L FOB. Expected first-month revenue if 1 local wholesale deal: ₹4-6L. Use trade marketplace: IndiaTradeZone.com, Tradekey.com, Alibaba B2B (free listings).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: Hire export manager (₹30-40K/month) + account manager (₹20K). Join Prakasam Stone Exporters Association (networking for bulk orders). Exhibit at International Stone Trade shows (Xiamen Stone Fair, USA Stone Expo — ₹3-5L per show, ROI 2-3x). Set up dedicated processing facility (₹50L+ investment). Add value with water-jet cutting, polishing for premium products (margin lift to 60-70%).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Program)
up to ₹25L loan at 5% interest for micro-enterprises, subsidy 15-35% of project cost depending on category
🏛
MUDRA Scheme (MUDRA Loan)
up to ₹10L unsecured working capital, zero collateral, 8.5% interest, quick approval for registered enterprises
🏚
Andhra Pradesh MSME Development & Facilitation Fund
₹50L concessional term loan at 4% interest for registered MSME units in manufacturing
🏚
Andhra Pradesh Export Promotion Scheme
5% subsidy on export value for first 3 years, reimbursement of 50% of overseas trade show participation costs
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
