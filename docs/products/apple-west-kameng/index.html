Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — West Kameng ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Shergaon apple clusters in West Kameng (contact District Horticulture Dept); study 2-3 established orchards; attend State Horticulture Mission training workshops; benchmark yield (15-20 tonnes/acre) and market rates (₹40-80/kg wholesale).
2
Source Raw Materials
Source grafted saplings (Himachal Pradesh nurseries via Kinnaur-Kulu network or state horticulture dept); first-batch cost for 100-200 saplings: ₹1.2-1.8L. Bulk fungicides and fertilizers from regional agricultural input suppliers in Itanagar or Dibrugarh.
3
Produce / Partner
Land requirement: 2-5 acres minimum. Partner with West Kameng District Horticulture Department for technical support; implement drip irrigation system (₹3-4L for 2 acres); join cooperative like Shergaon Apple Growers' Cooperative; hire seasonal labor during pruning/harvest (Aug-Oct). Orchard maturity: 3-4 years.
4
Brand & Register
Register as Udyam (online at udyamregistration.gov.in); obtain IEC code for exports via regional apeda office; register with state agriculture department for PMEGP eligibility; create FPO (Farmer Producer Organization) registration for collective marketing; design packaging with GI-aspiration labeling.
5
First Sale
Launch on BigBasket, Grofers for direct delivery in NE metros (Guwahati, Assam); approach regional supermarket chains (Assam-based); contact NAFED and APEDA for government procurement tenders; create Instagram/Facebook agritourism page showcasing orchard experiences; partner with local homestay platforms (iStay) for farm-visit bookings.
6
Scale
Post 6 months: establish cold storage facility (₹30-50L) in Shergaon for year-round supply; build value-added processing unit (juice, dried apples) for margin expansion; develop B2B contracts with food companies (ITC, Nestlé regional units); expand agritourism infrastructure (farmstay capacity, experience packages); apply for Krishi Infra Fund for cold-chain development.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy for agricultural startups (cold storage, processing units)
🏛
Krishi Infra Development Fund
concessional loans for cold storage and post-harvest infrastructure development
🏚
Arunachal Pradesh Horticulture Mission
subsidy on fruit crop development, plant material, and drip irrigation (up to 50% cost)
🏚
State Farmer Producer Organization Scheme
support for FPO registration and marketing linkage for collective farming
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
