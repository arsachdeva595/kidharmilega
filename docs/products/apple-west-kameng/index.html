Shergaon cluster in West Kameng (7,422 sq km district) sits at 1,800-2,200m elevation on the Indo-China border with year-round cool temps ideal for apple cultivation. State Horticulture Farm at Seinthuk provides certified buds and saplings, eliminating middleman sourcing costs. Nearest markets are Itanagar (90 km), Guwahati (400 km), and Delhi (2,000 km), with poor road connectivity (NH52 partially paved) limiting current off-take to 20% organized channels.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — West Kameng ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call State Horticulture Farm, Seinthuk (Shergaon) — ask nursery manager about apple bud costs, current orchard owners, and yield per hectare. Visit 2-3 existing apple farms in Shergaon during Sept-Nov harvest (free, meet farmers). Register on GeM portal (free). Attend local Kisan Mela if held in Oct-Nov. Zero cost, one week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Orchard land: lease 1-2 hectares in Shergaon for ₹15,000-25,000/month; ask local panchayat for names. Apple buds: buy 500-1,000 certified buds from State Horticulture Farm Seinthuk at ₹15-20/bud (₹10K-15K first batch). Cold storage rental in Shergaon/Itanagar: ₹8,000-12,000/month for 5-10 MT capacity.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Lease existing mature orchard (2-3 hectares, 5+ year old trees) — low cost entry, 12-18 month payback from harvest. Cost ₹50K-100K/year maintenance + ₹2L for cold storage setup. Option B: Plant new saplings on leased land — costs ₹80K-120K for 1 hectare (500 saplings × ₹160 each) but 3-4 year wait for fruit. Partner with 2-3 existing farms for first 2 years, buy their surplus at ₹60-80/kg, re-pack and brand.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 15 min online at udyamregistration.gov.in). Apply for GI registration via Geographical Indication Registry, Chennai (₹4,000 application fee, 18-24 month process; state/dist farm will support). Get FSSAI registration (₹8,000, 10 days if packing/processing). Open IEC code from DGFT (₹500, 7 days) if exporting. Total: ₹12,500, 8-10 weeks for non-GI setup.
5
First Sale
First order: list on Blinkit/BigBasket or contact 1 Delhi fruit wholesaler (Azadpur APMC) — negotiate 5 MT order at ₹100-110/kg. Expected first month revenue: 5 MT × 105/kg = ₹5.25L gross. Sell remaining to local restaurants, schools via WhatsApp orders. Use Paytm, UPI for payments. Target first order within 4 weeks of setup.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (100 MT/month during peak): hire 1 logistics coordinator (₹15K/mo), 1 quality checker (₹12K/mo). Partner with 1-2 cold chains (Snowman, TepidCold) for nationwide D2C shipping. Exhibit at Agrotech Expos (Delhi, Bangalore, Guwahati in Feb-Mar). Apply for PMEGP subsidy (₹10L-25L) for processing unit (juice/concentrate plant) to absorb grade-2 fruit.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
Prime Minister's Micro Units Development and Refinance Agency (MUDRA) Loan
up to ₹10L at 8-9% for working capital and cold storage setup
🏛
Pradhan Mantri Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)
subsidy up to 25% of project cost (max ₹10L) for processing unit; requires state DIC clearance
🏚
Chief Minister's Krishi Rinn Yojana (Arunachal Pradesh)
agricultural loans at subsidized rates; check with West Kameng District Agriculture Office for eligibility
🏚
Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Swavalamban Yojana
promotes rural self-employment; contact West Kameng DRDA for skill training subsidy and micro-loans up to ₹50K
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
