Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kanker ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kanker agriculture department and connect with custard apple farmers in Katghora and Mungeli blocks (60km from Kanker town). Study 3-4 existing small processing units near Rajnandgaon. Research cold chain infrastructure at Kanker wholesale market and Raipur APMC.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fresh custard apples directly from Kanker farmer cooperatives during July-November season at ₹50-80/kg. Negotiate bulk rates (2-5 tonnes) for ₹3-4 lakhs per batch. Identify secondary suppliers in Dhamtari district as backup. Establish contract farming agreements with minimum 10-15 farmers for supply consistency.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with Kanker-based food processing clusters or rent commercial kitchen space (₹15,000-25,000/month) in Kanker town with FSS license. Partner with established jam/preserve makers (available in Rajnandgaon) for initial batches under contract manufacturing model (₹8-12 per unit). Alternatively, lease semi-equipped processing unit with cooler and pasteurization setup (₹50,000-75,000/month).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, free) under food processing category. Obtain FSSAI food license (₹12,000-18,000) and IEC from DGFT Chennai (₹0). Trademark brand name at IP office (₹10,000-15,000). Design packaging compliant with FPO (Food Products Order) with ingredient and nutrition labels. Get BIS certification if targeting premium segment.
5
First Sale
Launch on Swiggy Instamart, Zepto, Blinkit (24-hour groceries) targeting tier-2 metros. List on Amazon Fresh and Big Basket. Partner with 5-8 premium ice cream chains or specialty cafes in Raipur/Nagpur. Start D2C on Instagram and WhatsApp Business with monthly subscription boxes. Approach 20-30 organic retail chains (Big Basket partner stores, Nature's Basket equivalent outlets).
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand product line to custard apple nectar, dried custard apple chips, and RTC (ready-to-cook) mixes. Increase production capacity by 60% via second kitchen/manufacturing partner. Launch in Madhya Pradesh and Telangana markets (similar climate, demand). Build retail presence in 50+ modern trade outlets. Establish export partnerships with Singapore and UAE through food export aggregators.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Program)
up to ₹25 lakhs subsidy (35% for SC/ST, 25% general) with loan up to ₹10 lakhs for food processing startups
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹40 lakhs for food processing clusters with infrastructure and common facility support
🏚
Chhattisgarh Agro-Processing Cluster Development Scheme
capital subsidy up to ₹1.5 crore for food processing units with infrastructure support
🏚
Rajiv Gandhi Kisan Nyay Yojana
procurement support and farmer tie-up incentives for value-added agri-products from registered processing units
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Marketing

Ye marketing ideas try karna mat bhulna

  • Micro-influencer campaign (50-100k followers in food/wellness niche) on Instagram and YouTube featuring custard apple farm visits, processing videos, and tasting reviews. Target 8-10 influencers in tier-2 metros at ₹20,000-40,000 per collaboration.
  • Partner with 10-15 premium cafes and ice cream parlors in Raipur for in-store tastings and co-branded menus. Offer 15-20% margin to retail partners. Run geo-targeted Instagram ads (₹500/day) in 10km radius of partner locations.
