Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Raichur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Deosugur Industrial Area this week — call Shilpa Pharma reception (search online) to understand their USFDA facility layout, then visit 2-3 other pharma units in the cluster; attend Raichur Chamber of Commerce meeting (free) to network with API suppliers; call Maharashtra and Gujarat API distributors to understand current market pricing for Capecitabine, Zoledronic Acid.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source chemical precursors from registered suppliers in Hyderabad (Organics Chemicals Hub), Bangalore (Chemtech Hub), or Mumbai (MIDC). First batch cost for Capecitabine base materials: ₹15–20L for 100kg pilot batch. Approach established API reagent firms like Ami Life Sciences, Laurus Labs (for reference pricing), or Nagarjuna Fertilizers (chemical procurement).
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 5,000–10,000 sqft unit in Deosugur Industrial Area (₹2–4L/month) with USFDA-eligible layout — existing pharmaceutical units available. Alternatively, negotiate toll manufacturing tie-up with Shilpa Pharma or similar USFDA-certified facility for first 12 months (0% upfront cost, 15-20% processing fee on output). Initial production setup (reactors, distillation columns, analytical instruments): ₹50L–₹1.5Cr.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam within 30 mins (udyamregistration.gov.in, free); apply for IEC code via DGFT portal (₹0, 5 days); file for USFDA Establishment Inspection File (EIF) registration (₹5–10L, 90 days); pursue EUGMP or WHO-GMP certification (₹20–30L, 6 months); optionally file patent for novel synthesis method via IP India (₹50,000, 18-24 months). Total regulatory spend: ₹25–50L over 12 months.
5
First Sale
First sale: contact pharmaceutical wholesalers on PharmaBiz.com, Indiastack, or Alibaba with COA (Certificate of Analysis); target contract manufacturers via cold calls to top 20 API importers listed on Invest India; reach out to US-based pharma distributors on TradeKey or Global Sources. Expected first order: 50–100kg at ₹80–120/gram = ₹4–12L revenue in month 6-8 post-production setup.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 15-20 production chemists (₹3–5L per person) and 10-15 QC analysts (₹2–4L) from IIT Bombay, NIT Warangal, or Nirma University; apply for Pradhan Mantri MSME Development Program (loan ₹50L at 5% subsidy); exhibit at Pharmatech Asia (Mumbai, annual), Chemtech (Gujarat), or Analytica India (Delhi) to showcase USFDA compliance and certifications; establish ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 (₹3–5L); negotiate long-term supply contracts with top 5 US distributors for recurring orders.

Bilkul. Pharma APIs require 8-10 years minimum experience in organic chemistry synthesis, quality control, USFDA compliance, and batch documentation. Ministry requirement: B.Sc, M.Sc, B.Pharm, M.Pharm, B.Tech, or M.Tech in chemistry/pharmacy. Without this, hire a Chief Chemist (₹8–15L/year) and Quality Head (₹6–10L/year) from someone's payroll, or partner with an experienced API maker.

Total potential subsidy: ₹20–50L over first 3 years.

Haan, bilkul. Raichur's Shilpa Pharma already exports to USA, Europe, South Asia. Process: get USFDA Establishment Inspection File (EIF) clearance (₹5–10L, 90 days), EUGMP or WHO-GMP certification (₹20–30L), IEC code (free, 5 days), COA (Certificate of Analysis) for each batch, and compliance with destination country pharma import rules. Export margin: 45-60% FOB. Timeline to first export: 12-18 months from setup.
Honest answer: pharma APIs have 2-3 year payback, but failure rates are high without regulatory clearance or existing customer contracts pre-launch. Exit options: (1) sell your IEC code + regulatory approvals to larger pharma firm (₹50–200L recovery), (2) pivot to toll manufacturing mode (low upfront, 15-20% processing fee), (3) merge with an established API house. Risk mitigation: secure 1-2 LOIs (Letters of Intent) from distributors before major capex.
