Amethi (26.15°N, 81.82°E) sits in the Gomti River basin with rich alluvial soil ideal for moonj grass. The district's 13 blocks host 2500+ registered moonj artisans concentrated in Gauriganj, Musafirkhana, and Tiloi tehsils, producing 15000+ units monthly. Buyers include Delhi design studios (350 km via NH-2), Lucknow retailers (100 km via NH-56), and direct export to US/EU via Delhi air cargo.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Amethi ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Gauriganj haat (weekly market, Thursdays) to meet 30-40 moonj artisans directly; call DC Handicraft office (05544-222222) for cluster list; attend ODOP training at Amethi District Industries Centre (free, monthly sessions). Join WhatsApp group 'Amethi Moonj Artisans' — ask any founder's contact from them — zero cost, do this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw moonj grass from Tiloi and Musafirkhana village suppliers (50-80 km from Gauriganj). First batch cost: ₹8-12K for 100 kg grass (makes 50-60 medium baskets). Supplier contact: Call local sarpanch offices or ask at Gauriganj haat. Delivery in 5-7 days.
3
Produce / Partner
Start via artisan partnership model: visit 3-4 clusters, identify 1-2 experienced artisans, commission first 100 pieces at ₹120-180/piece (delivered). Rent a 200 sq.ft. unit in Gauriganj for packing/quality check (₹3-5K/month). Total outlay: ₹25-30K. By month 2, scale to own 600 sq.ft. unit (₹8-12K/month rent) with 3-4 full-time workers at ₹6-8K/month each.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (udyamregistration.gov.in, 15 mins, free). File GI certification application through District Industries Centre (₹0 cost, 3-month process for Amethi moonj products — GI already awarded in 2008, you get automatic coverage). Apply for IEC from DGFT Lucknow branch (₹500, 2 weeks). Get ISI/BIS certification for export (₹5-8K, optional but increases buyer confidence).
5
First Sale
List 10 product SKUs on Amazon Handmade (listing fee ₹0, 15% commission) — first order typically ₹2-5K. Reach out to 5-10 interior design studios in Lucknow/Delhi via email with WhatsApp catalog (₹0 cost). First wholesale order: ₹20-30K (50-100 units). Target ₹80-120K first-month combined revenue (mix of 3-4 online and 1-2 wholesale orders).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 fulltime packing/QC person (₹8K/month), 1 sales coordinator (₹10K/month). Attend 2-3 export trade shows per year (Mumbai Gifting Show, Delhi Craft Bazaar, cost ₹1-2L). Build relationships with 2-3 US/EU importers (target ₹2-5L/month export revenue). Invest ₹3-5L in brand photography and content marketing. Consider opening a small 800 sq.ft. retail showroom in Lucknow by month 9.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹15,000 cash grant + ₹2 lakh collateral-free loan at 5% interest for registered artisans and small producers (apply via pmvishwakarma.gov.in).
🏛
SFURTI (Amended Scheme)
Up to ₹20 lakh non-refundable cluster subsidy for toolkits, training, and common facility centers for handicraft clusters (apply via kvic.org.in).
🏚
UP Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSME) Policy 2023
7% capital subsidy (max ₹25 lakh) on fixed investment for new units in handicraft sector (apply via udyamregistration.gov.in or District MSME office).
🏚
UP One District One Product (ODOP) Scheme
Up to ₹1 lakh for brand development, ₹50K for website, export subsidy of 10% on FOB value (apply via DC office Amethi).
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
