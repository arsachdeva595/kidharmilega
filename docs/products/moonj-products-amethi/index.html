The artisanal community has perfected intricate weaving techniques passed down generations, making it the natural heartland for authentic moonj products that command premium prices in eco-conscious markets.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Amethi ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Amethi's moonj artisan clusters in Sultanpur and Jagdishpur tehsils; spend 2 weeks learning from master craftspeople; attend craft fairs at Delhi Haat and Surajkund to understand market positioning and pricing; connect with existing GI certified moonj exporters.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw moonj grass directly from wetland harvesters in Amethi (₹8,000-12,000 per quintal); establish relationships with 2-3 primary suppliers to ensure quality; first batch of 500 units will cost approximately ₹80,000-120,000 including materials and artisan labor.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established moonj artisan cooperatives in Amethi (e.g., Amethi Handicraft Cooperative); start with 3-5 skilled artisans working from home workshops; establish quality control checkpoints; plan production cycles of 6-8 weeks per batch to ensure handcrafted excellence.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) classification online; apply for IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Lucknow; obtain GI certification use authorization from Department of Commerce; register trademark with IP India; get ISO 9001 or craft certification from DFRL for export compliance.
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy with 50-100 products and invest ₹20,000 in ads; approach ethical brands like AERA, Craftizen for distribution partnerships; pitch to premium hotels like The Oberoi and ITC chains; reach out to sustainable lifestyle influencers for review collaborations; list on Amazon Handmade India with detailed heritage narratives.
6
Scale
After 6 months, hire dedicated sales rep for B2B channel; expand artisan network to 15-20 craftspeople; invest in professional product photography and storytelling content; pursue international trade shows in Frankfurt, London, and Paris; establish export logistics partnerships; develop premium branded packaging.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma
up to ₹15,000 toolkit assistance and ₹3 lakh concessional credit for registered artisans and micro-businesses
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh loan with 25% subsidy for general category entrepreneurs in manufacturing handicrafts
🏚
UP Handicraft Policy 2023
20% subsidy on export promotion, participation in international trade fairs, and packaging support for GI-tagged products
🏚
UP State ODOP Scheme
direct export support, branding assistance, and online marketplace integration for registered ODOP artisans
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
