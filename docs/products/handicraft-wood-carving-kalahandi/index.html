Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kalahandi ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kalahandi district's artisan clusters in Bhawanipatna and Lanjigarh towns. Spend 2-3 weeks shadowing master carvers, documenting techniques. Meet DC Handicraft office in Bhawanipatna for ODOP guidelines. Study export data at APEDA office, Delhi. Cost: ₹8,000-12,000.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source premium teak from Odisha State Forest Development Corporation (OFDC) or authorized timber merchants in Kalahandi. First batch: 50 kg teak wood @ ₹1,200-1,500/kg = ₹60,000-75,000. Carving tools from Bangalore tool suppliers or local Kalahandi blacksmiths: ₹15,000-25,000. Establish direct relationships with 3-4 artisan groups.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established artisan cooperatives like Kalahandi Handicraft Cooperative Society. Start with 2-3 senior carvers on contract basis (₹8,000-15,000/month per artisan). Begin with bestselling items: wall panels (3-6 months lead time), figurines (4-8 weeks), doors (8-12 weeks). Invest in proper drying kilns and workshop space (₹100,000-150,000).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Micro/Small enterprise) at udyamregistration.gov.in — free registration. Apply for IEC code via ICEGATE for export readiness. File brand name trademark via IPO Delhi (₹4,500 for application). Get ISI certification for wood quality. Create GST registration (18% applicable). Certify artisans under PM Vishwakarma scheme.
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy, Amazon Handmade, and Flipkart for initial traction (₹5,000-10,000 setup per platform). Create Instagram/Pinterest accounts showcasing artisan stories (post 3x weekly). Reach out to 50 interior design firms and corporate gifting companies via LinkedIn. Participate in India International Trade Fair (IITF) Delhi in Nov-Dec. Partner with 1GLOBAL or Alibaba for B2B export inquiries.
6
Scale
After 6 months, hire 2 more senior artisans and train 3-4 apprentices. Invest ₹200,000 in e-commerce optimization and paid ads (Facebook/Instagram). Establish showroom partnerships in Delhi/Mumbai/Bangalore premium furniture stores. Apply for SFURTI cluster grant to set up shared workshop infrastructure. Attend international trade fairs (Frankfurt, Maison & Objet Paris). Build B2B export pipeline targeting 40% revenue by month 12.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy (25-35% of project cost) for manufacturing. Eligibility: new unit in tier 2/3 district with turnover up to ₹10 lakh.
🏛
PM Vishwakarma Yojana
₹15,000 direct cash transfer + 5% concessional credit up to ₹5 lakh for registered artisans. Covers skill certification and toolkit purchases.
🏚
Odisha MSME Development Scheme
5% subsidy on term loan up to ₹50 lakh for handicraft units in non-metro areas. Interest subsidy for 3 years.
🏚
Odisha Handicrafts Cluster Development Scheme
provides common facility centers, design support, quality testing lab access, and export promotion grants of ₹2-5 lakh per unit in ODOP clusters.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
