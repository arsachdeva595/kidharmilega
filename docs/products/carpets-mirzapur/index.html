Carpets ki
opportunity, Mirzapur mein.

Mirzapur district (4,521 sq km, 24.97 lakh population) is the production epicenter of the Mega carpet cluster stretching across Mirzapur-Bhadohi-Varanasi, employing 2 million artisans and 10 lakh weavers. Wool sourcing networks feed into the district from Rajasthan; silk and cotton are imported via established suppliers in Varanasi and Indore. The cluster annually produces ₹3,000 crore in carpets, 85% destined for US and European markets, with direct buyer relationships through federation channels and trade associations already in place.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Mirzapur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call Mirzapur Carpet Exporters Association (MCEA) office this week; visit Naya Bazar or Godown area carpet wholesale market on a Saturday; attend the monthly artisan haat at Mirzapur town. Talk to 3-5 established exporters, ask them raw material costs, lead times, and which markets they serve. Zero cost, 1-2 days work.
2
Source Raw Materials
Visit wool merchants in Balupur or Mahmoorganj wholesale area (₹200-400/kg for good quality); identify 2-3 weaver groups through MCEA or Khadi Board office (they have artisan directories). First sample batch: 2-3 small carpets (2x3 ft) cost ₹15,000-₹30,000 including materials + weaver payment; lead time 30-45 days.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (Asset-light): Partner with established weaver families in Mirzapur-Bhadohi; provide wool, design specs, pay ₹8,000-₹15,000 per carpet to weaver. Option B (Own unit): Rent a 500-800 sq ft loom space in Godown cluster (₹3,000-₹8,000/month), hire 2-3 weavers at ₹8,000-₹12,000/month each. Option A needs ₹3-5L for first 15-20 carpets; Option B needs ₹8-15L for setup + 3 months working capital.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (free, 15 mins, instant MSME certificate). Apply for GI authentication through Mirzapur Weavers Cooperative or District Industries Center (DIC) — ₹5,000-₹10,000, 2-3 month process. Get IEC code from FIEO if exporting (free, online application, 1-2 weeks). Hire a local CA to manage GST registration (₹2,000-₹5,000 one-time). Total: ₹10,000-₹20,000, 2-4 months.
5
First Sale
List 3-5 carpets on Etsy (free listing, 6.5% commission + payment fee). Simultaneously pitch 2-3 Instagram DMs to US-based home decor micro-influencers (under 50k followers, high engagement) with samples. First order likely from D2C: ₹25,000-₹60,000 per carpet; expect 2-4 orders/month at launch. First-month revenue target: ₹50,000-₹150,000 gross.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire a logistics coordinator (₹15,000/month), partner with 10-15 weaver families instead of 3-5, hire a part-time export documentation assistant (₹10,000/month). Exhibit at India International Trade Fair (IITF) Delhi or USA-based home decor trade shows (costs ₹2-3L). Build WhatsApp catalog + video content showing weaving process to sell directly to interior designers. Pursue bulk contracts with hotel chains and corporate buyers.

Where to Set Up

Infrastructure ready hai — plot lo aur shuru karo

Mirzapur MSME Cluster (Godown Area)
Main carpet production cluster with 500+ registered loom units, shared infrastructure, raw material markets, artisan groups. Shared dyeing facilities available. Space for new units: 500-2,000 sq ft plots rented at ₹3,000-₹8,000/month.
MSME Craft Cluster
Mirzapur Mega Carpet Cluster (Government initiative)
Recently developed with World Bank support; includes design center, common facility center (CFC), export promotion hub, training institute for young weavers. Units get subsidized lease (₹2,000-₹5,000/month for 500 sq ft), free design consultancy, export documentation support.
Government Mega Cluster (SEZ-like benefits)
Bhadohi Carpet Cluster Extension
Adjacent to main Mirzapur belt in neighboring Bhadohi district (20 km away); 1,000+ units, similar infrastructure, lower rents (₹1,500-₹4,000/month). Direct access to Varanasi exporters and suppliers.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹10 lakh loan at 5% interest subsidy for MSMEs; 25-35% margin credit subsidy.
🏛
PM Vishwakarma Yojana
up to ₹15,000 toolkit grant for artisans; ₹3,00,000 collateral-free loan at 5% interest subsidy for registered handicraft workers.
🏚
UP One District One Product (ODOP) Subsidy
25-30% subsidy on capital investment for registered carpet units; additional subsidy on marketing + export promotion.
🏚
UP Handicraft Artisan & Exporter Scheme
20% subsidy on raw material + transport + export certification costs for units exporting above ₹50 lakh annually.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Frequently Asked Questions

Seedhe sawaal, seedhe jawab

₹2.5-5 lakh se start kar sakte ho. ₹2.5L से छोटे order (5-10 carpets) का first batch, ₹5L se better control setup (rent करके + 2 weavers hire करके). Government से PMEGP या Vishwakarma से ₹10-15L तक loan मिल सकता है 5% interest पर।
Direct weaving experience जरूरी नहीं है, पर textile या export business का कोई base होना अच्छा है। यहां सभी weavers पहले से trained हैं — तुम्हें सिर्फ design, quality, और customer manage करना है। महीने भर में cluster में रहके सीख जाते हो।
UP ODOP scheme से 25-30% subsidy मिलती है capital investment पर। PMEGP से ₹10L तक loan + 25% subsidy। PM Vishwakarma से artisans को ₹15k toolkit grant + ₹3L loan। Export करो तो additional 15-20% subsidy raw material और certification पर।
Etsy पर list करो, Instagram रीलस डालो तो 2-4 हफ्ते में first US/EU order मिलेगा। या फिर Mirzapur Exporters Association के through established buyers को contact करो — वो resellers हैं, bulk order दे सकते हैं। Local buyers (Delhi/Bangalore designers) WhatsApp से जल्दी मिलते हैं।
हां, यह star product है export के लिए। 85% export US और Europe को होती है। तुम्हें चाहिए: IEC code (free, 1 week), Shipping partner (DHL/FedEx के साथ tie-up करो), Invoice + Packing List, Quality Certificate। ₹2-3 lakh की shipment का freight ₹25k-₹40k आएगा। FOB price पर 35-50% margin रहता है।
Carpet business में flop होने का risk कम है क्योंकि demand है। लेकिन अगर हो तो: (1) अपना design खराब हो या quality हो, तो losses होंगे। (2) Export documents में गड़बड़ी से shipment रुक सकता है। (3) Payment default से cash flow खराब हो सकता है। Exit option: अपने weavers को keep करके agent बन जाओ किसी बड़े exporter के लिए — तब fixed commission मिलेगा।
