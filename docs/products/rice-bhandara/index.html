Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bhandara ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bhandara's established rice mills in Sakoli and Pauni clusters. Study 3-4 GI-certified brands (Vidarbha Rice, Bhandara Gold). Spend 2 weeks on-ground with local millers to understand procurement, processing, and quality standards.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw paddy directly from farmer cooperative societies in Bhandara district (₹25-30 per kg). Identify 2-3 reliable milling partners to handle processing. First batch of 10 MT costs ₹3-4 lakhs including milling and packaging.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing APMC-registered mills (Bhandara Rice Mills Association has 40+ members). Start with 10-15 MT monthly contract milling. No need for your own mill initially — negotiate milling fees at ₹800-1200 per tonne.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, 15 mins). Obtain IEC code from DGFT Nagpur office. Secure GI usage certificate from Ministry of Agriculture (₹5000-10000 fee). Design packaging with GI tag, nutritional labels, and batch coding. Register trademark with IP office.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh and Flipkart with ₹2000-3000 per 5kg pack positioning. Create WhatsApp Business catalog for B2B bulk buyers. Approach 20-30 HORECA chains in Mumbai, Pune with samples. List on exporters' platforms (TradeKey, Alibaba) targeting 5 export inquiries monthly.
6
Scale
After 6 months and ₹25L revenue, invest in automated packaging line (₹15-20L). Hire a full-time export coordinator. Expand product range to 5 SKUs (basmati blends, organic, parboiled). Target 500MT monthly production and ₹5Cr annual revenue by month 18.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy (35% for general, 45% for SC/ST) for rice processing and milling units.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹5 lakh per unit for clustering of rice processors with shared infrastructure support.
🏚
Maharashtra Rice Brand Promotion Scheme
₹50,000 subsidy for GI rice producers registering new brands, valid for Bhandara ODOP rice.
🏚
Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Shetkari Karjmukti Yojana
zero-interest loans up to ₹10 lakh for agricultural processing entrepreneurs in Maharashtra.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
