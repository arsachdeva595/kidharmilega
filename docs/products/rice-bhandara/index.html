Bhandara district sits on the Wainganga River in central Maharashtra, with 85,000 hectares under rice cultivation and a 100+ mill processing cluster in Bhandara town. The district's aromatic rice varieties (Chinnor, Dubhraj, Kalikammod) command 35-45% price premium over standard basmati in domestic markets and 40-52% FOB margin in export markets (UAE, UK, Singapore). Raw material sourcing happens directly from farmers April-June; mill capacity ranges from 5-50 MT/day across small, medium, and large units.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bhandara ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bhandara rice mills cluster (Bhandara town main market); call 2-3 mill operators (find via Bhandara District Industries office); attend Pauni weekly haat on Thursday to see farmer activity; register free Udyam account via https://udyamregistration.gov.in — zero cost, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source paddy directly from Pauni, Sakoli, and Lakhani tehsils via farmer cooperatives (avg cost ₹1,800-2,000/quintal April-June). For finished rice, buy from established mills in Bhandara town (Rathi Mills, Jai Hind Mills cluster) at ₹85-95/kg for regular varieties, ₹140-160/kg for Chinnor/Dubhraj. First 5 MT order costs ₹4.25-5.75L.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (zero capital): partner with existing mill for co-packing — they process, you brand, you pay ₹5-8/kg processing fee. Option B (₹25-45L): rent 1,500 sqft space in Bhandara MSME cluster, install 5 MT/day rice mill (dehusker + polisher + sorter). Option C (₹12-18L): rent 800 sqft in Bhandara town, buy 2 MT/day equipment, do grading/packaging of mill output.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 15 min online). Apply for GI certification addendum via Bhandara District Industries office (₹2,000 filing fee, 30 days). Register IEC code with DGFT via online portal (₹0, 7 days) if export intent. Get FSSAI license (₹2,000-5,000, 45 days for food processing unit). Total: ₹5,000-7,000 and 45-60 days.
5
First Sale
First channel: WhatsApp bulk order to 15-20 local restaurants/hotels in Nagpur, Bhandara via referrals from mill owners. Target order: 50kg at ₹95/kg = ₹4,750 first transaction. Or list 500g packs on JioMart Seller (registration free, go live in 5 days) — expect first 20-30 unit sales/month at ₹130-180/kg selling price. First month realistic revenue: ₹40,000-80,000 from 2-3 channels.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (~11,000 kg/month sales): hire 1 packaging supervisor (₹12,000/month), add quality control staff (₹8,000/month), move to 3,000 sqft unit if doing in-house processing. Open second wholesale account with 1-2 premium restaurants/corporate cafeterias for 500kg/month standing order. Apply for APEDA export promotion grant (₹5-10L reimbursement over 2 years) and exhibit at Annapoorna Food Show (Mumbai, March). Set up Amazon/BigBasket seller account (3-5% commission) after 6 months stable supply.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L loan with 35% subsidy for MSMEs, if eligible as new manufacturing unit.
🏛
MUDRA Loan Scheme
up to ₹10L unsecured working capital loan for small businesses via SBI/post office branches in Bhandara.
🏚
One District One Product (ODOP) Scheme
zero registration fee, GI certification support, marketing subsidy up to ₹3L for approved ODOP enterprises in Bhandara.
🏚
Maharashtra Agro-Processing Cluster Development Scheme
up to ₹50L subsidy for food processing units in identified clusters (Bhandara qualifies); apply via District Industries office.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
