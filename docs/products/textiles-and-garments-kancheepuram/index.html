Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kancheepuram ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Spend 2 weeks in Kancheepuram's Avaniapuram and Sripuram weaving clusters; visit 10+ established weavers, attend Tamil Nadu Handlooms department workshops, study competitor brands (Nalli, Kumaran Silks) on Flipkart/their websites.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw silk from Rajapalayam or direct Karnataka suppliers (₹800-1200/kg); metallic zari from Bangalore; first 50-100 saree batch costs ₹4-6L including labor; negotiate directly with cluster weavers at Sripuram market.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 5-10 master weavers in Avaniapuram cluster (visit Handlooms Cooperative Society); provide designs, quality specs, and 50% advance; hire local design assistant to manage quality and timelines; visit weekly to monitor production.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam on udyamregistration.gov.in (free, 1 day); obtain IEC code from DGFT portal (₹0, 2 days); register trademark with TM Registry (₹4500); GST registration (₹0, 3 days); design logo using Canva or hire local designer (₹5K-15K).
5
First Sale
List on Amazon and Flipkart (30% commission); launch Instagram/Facebook with product photos and artisan stories (budget ₹10K/month for content); start 50-unit pre-order campaign with 40% margin; email outreach to premium saree retailers and wedding planners; participate in Chennai saree exhibitions.
6
Scale
After first 6 months, recruit 2-3 designer partners to create seasonal lines; expand to 20-30 weavers; launch B2B wholesale division targeting Tier 2 cities; invest in influencer partnerships with saree bloggers; build corporate gifting vertical with bulk order discounts.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹10L subsidy for entrepreneurs, 25-35% subsidy for manufacturing
🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
up to ₹3L credit assistance and free toolkit for artisans and craft workers
🏚
Tamil Nadu Handloom Weaver Comprehensive Welfare Scheme
direct subsidy support for weavers you partner with, reducing your labor costs by 10-15%
🏚
TN Textile Technology Upgradation Fund
grants up to ₹50L for machinery modernization if you scale production after 12 months
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
