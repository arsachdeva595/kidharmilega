Panchkula (898 sq km, 5,61,293 population) is a planned city 35 km from Delhi and directly adjacent to Chandigarh, placing it within 300 km of India's largest auto OEM cluster (Maruti in Manesar, Hero in Haryana, Bajaj in Pune corridor). The Panchkula MSME cluster houses 400+ registered auto parts units producing everything from engine components to suspension parts, drawing raw materials from Punjab suppliers and Kolkata steel stockyards. Buyer concentration spans 2,000+ dealerships and service centers across NCR, Punjab, and Himachal, plus direct OEM contracts feeding 8+ assembly plants within 500 km.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Panchkula ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call 3 established auto parts suppliers in Panchkula MSME Cluster (near Sector 12-14); visit Sector 25 MSME Common Facility Center; attend Panchkula Chamber of Commerce monthly meetup; zero cost, do this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source steel billets from Kolkata suppliers (Tata Steel dealers in Sector 12) or Punjab Forge suppliers in Ludhiana; first batch sourcing cost ₹8-12 L for 500-1000 units. Request supplier lists from Panchkula District Industries Office (free). Fasteners and bearings from authorized Fastenal/SKF dealers on Sector 26.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 2,500-4,000 sq ft climate-controlled unit in Panchkula Industrial Estate Phase-II (₹30-50 per sq ft/month = ₹75-200K/month); alternately, negotiate job-work tie-up with existing CNC shops in Sector 25 (no fixed rent, 8-12% job work charge). Equipment: 2-3 CNC machines (₹12-18 L), precision measurement tools (₹3-5 L), quality testing rig (₹2-3 L). Partner hiring: BOP supervisor, 4-6 machine operators, 2 quality checkers. Total 3-month ramp cost ₹30-40 L including equipment, rent, and manpower.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME online (free, 15 mins at https://udyamregistration.gov.in); apply for IEC Code via eSanchit portal (₹0, 7 days); obtain BIS/AIS certifications required for your part type (₹50-100K, 4-6 weeks). Seek GI registration if design is unique (₹5-10K application, 18-24 months processing). GST registration at local Commercial Tax office (₹100-500, 2-3 days).
5
First Sale
First order: contact 2-3 regional distributors via Panchkula Chamber referrals, quote 500-1000 unit volumes at 20% margin; target ₹5-8 L first month revenue. Secondary: list on IndiaMART with 7-10 day lead time; aim for 50-100 unit repeat orders. Post IEC, email 20-30 export buyers from AutomosExports database with sample quotation (US$ 8-12 per unit FOB).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 2nd shift supervisor, add 1-2 CNC machines, invest ₹5L in ISO 9001:2015 and IATF 16949 certification (needed for tier-1 OEM contracts). Exhibit at Auto Tech Expo (Delhi, Oct), Components Show (Chennai, Aug). Hire junior accountant + export-import specialist. Negotiate consignment inventory with 2-3 major distributors. Explore contract manufacturing partnerships with Tier-1 suppliers.
