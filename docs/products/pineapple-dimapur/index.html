Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Dimapur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Dimapur Agricultural Produce Market Committee (APMC) and connect with 5+ established pineapple farmers; study export quality standards at Dimapur Export Association; attend training at ICAR-Manipur Research Station on premium cultivation techniques
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified pineapple crowns from Assam Agricultural University nurseries (₹8-12 per crown); secure organic fertilizers from Nagaland Organic Mission retailers; budget ₹2-3 lakhs for 1-acre first batch including land prep, planting, and 6-month maintenance
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3-5 small farmers in Dimapur taluka under contract farming (minimum 5 acres pooled); establish collection center with cold storage (₹15-20L capital); hire agricultural supervisor experienced in pineapple post-harvest handling
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam as 'Micro/Small Food Processing Enterprise'; apply for IEC code from APEDA for export eligibility; obtain FSSAI license for juice/value-added products (₹500-1000 online); trademark brand name at IP India; get organic certification from accredited body (₹50-75K annual)
5
First Sale
Launch fresh fruit sales via Jio Mart, BigBasket with Dimapur supply chain; contact Tropicana, Real, Minute Maid for concentrate supply partnerships; list dried chips on Amazon Fresh and Blinkit; approach Cold Storage chains (Foodhall, Hypercity) in Metro cities; ship samples to Singapore/Malaysia importers via APEDA-registered freight forwarders
6
Scale
After 6 months, double farmer partnerships to 10+ acres; invest ₹25-30L in processing unit for juice/chips production; hire sales team for B2B relations with FMCG brands; apply for PMEGP subsidy (if eligible) for capacity expansion; explore private label partnerships with premium juice brands
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM-KISAN Samman Nidhi
₹6000 annual direct income support for farmer partners
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L loan with 35% subsidy for processing/value-addition unit setup
🏚
Nagaland Horticulture Mission
₹50K per hectare subsidy for pineapple cultivation; free drip irrigation setup support
🏚
Nagaland Organic Farming Scheme
₹10K per hectare for organic certification; market linkage support for certified produce exports
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
