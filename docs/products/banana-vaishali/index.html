Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Vaishali ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Hajipur banana market (near Vaishali bus stand) and meet 3-5 banana farmers on a Tuesday morning; ask about surplus pulp disposal. Call Taruwar Agro (search contact via district ODOP page or local MSME office) and request 30-min founder chat. Register free Udyam account online. Zero cost, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw banana from Hajipur weekly markets or direct farmer partnerships (₹2-4 per kg bulk). First batch: 500 kg banana = ₹1.5-2K. Vermicompost culture sourced from Patna agricultural university or online (₹3-5K for starter culture). Supplier area: Hajipur vegetable mandi, 8 km from district HQ.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 500 sqft production unit in Hajipur MSME cluster (₹4-6K/month) or partner with existing farmers for on-farm fibre extraction. Equipment: banana fibre extraction machine (₹2-3.5L one-time), composting beds, drying racks. Month 1 cost: ₹8-12L (rent, equipment, first batch materials). Alternative: contract fibre extraction from local artisans on per-kg basis to start with ₹2-3L capital.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (free, 10 min). Apply for GI tag under Ministry of Agriculture One District One Product scheme (₹1.5-2L govt subsidy via SFURTI cluster, 6-month process). Obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Patna (₹500-1K, 2 weeks). Get FSSAI registration for vermicompost packaging (₹5-8K, 3 weeks). Total: ₹6-10K + 4-5 months timeline.
5
First Sale
First B2B sale: contact 5 apparel/handicraft brands in Bengaluru via email with samples (LinkedIn, Instagram DMs). Expected first order: 50 kg fibre at ₹250/kg = ₹12.5K. Simultaneously list on Etsy and Instagram Shop (free setup). First month revenue realistic: ₹40-60K (mixed channels). Use vermicompost sales to offset cash burn.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 1 fibre extraction specialist (₹12K/month), 1 vermicompost manager, 1 packaging/labeling person. Increase raw material sourcing via farmer network agreements. Exhibit at Organic Expo India (Delhi, Sept), Textiles India (Bengaluru, Jan) to attract bulk buyers. Launch WhatsApp bulk order catalog. Consider GI tag certification (if eligible by then) as premium positioning. Partner with 2-3 export freight forwarders in Patna for Japan/EU shipments.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25% subsidy on eligible fixed asset cost up to Rs25 lakh for manufacturing unit, plus Rs10 lakh credit at 0% interest for first 3 years.
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
Rs40-100 lakh cluster development fund, common facility centers for fibre extraction and vermicompost, training subsidies up to ₹50K per entrepreneur.
🏚
Bihar Startup Seed Fund Scheme
Rs5-10 lakh grant for eligible agricultural startups within first 2 years; requires Udyam registration and bank account.
🏚
Bihar Krishi Vikas Mitra Scheme
Rs3-5 lakh subsidy for agro-processing units using agricultural waste; 40% grant for equipment if raw material is locally sourced.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
