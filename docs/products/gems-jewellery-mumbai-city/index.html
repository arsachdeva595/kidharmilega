Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Mumbai City ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Zaveri Bazaar, Bhuleshwar, and Kala Ghoda jewellery clusters. Spend 2-3 weeks shadowing established jewellers. Register with Bombay Bullion Association and connect with BIS-approved assay offices to understand certification norms
2
Source Raw Materials
Source gold from Rajesh Masrani & Co or MMTC-PAMP in Mumbai (₹5-10L for initial 100g stock). Procure polished diamonds from Antwerp-certified dealers in Fort/Kala Ghoda (₹10-20L per carat quality). Build vendor relationships with pearls/gemstone suppliers in Zaveri Bazaar
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 experienced artisans from Zaveri Bazaar clusters (₹15,000-30,000/month per craftsman). Set up a 500-800 sq ft workshop in Bhuleshwar or Kala Ghoda with basic tools (furnace, scales, polish wheels). Obtain BIS hallmarking certification or partner with a hallmark center
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, 10 mins). Obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office (Ballard Estate). Get BIS hallmark license (₹50,000-1L). Register GST as 5% for jewellery. Secure a catchy brand name and file trademark with IP India
5
First Sale
Launch on Myntra, Ajio, Nykaa Fashion and create Instagram/Facebook shop. Approach jewellery retailers in Mumbai, Pune, Bangalore with wholesale pricesheets (15-20% margin). Attend Mumbai Jewellery Show (IIJW) for B2B connections. Cold-call wedding planners and corporate gift coordinators
6
Scale
Open 1-2 franchise showrooms in Tier 2 cities (Pune, Bangalore). Scale exports via forwarders at Gateway of India. Hire a sales team to cover North India. Launch a loyalty program and referral scheme. Invest in Instagram influencer collaborations targeting 25-40 age brides
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy for manufacturing units in gems and jewellery sector with 25% margin loan eligibility
🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹15,000 stipend and ₹5 lakh concessional loan for traditional jewellers and artisans
🏚
Maharashtra Startup Policy
5-year property tax waiver for manufacturing units in designated industrial zones; GST refund of 5% for first 3 years
🏚
MAITRI (Integrated Textiles & Apparel Scheme)
20% capital subsidy on plant and machinery for registered artisan clusters in Zaveri Bazaar
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
