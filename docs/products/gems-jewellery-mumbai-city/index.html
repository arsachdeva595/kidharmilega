Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Mumbai City ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Zaveri Bazaar retail showrooms on Muhammad Ali Road (free, 2-3 hours) to see finished goods pricing and quality standards. Call 3-5 diamond/gold suppliers in Bharat Diamond Bourse (contact via bdbindia.com) to understand raw material cost structure. Register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in). Attend the next MSME meet at IJPM, Navi Mumbai (check dates on ijpm.in).
2
Source Raw Materials
Source cut-polished diamonds and lab-grown diamonds from Bharat Diamond Bourse suppliers (₹2-10L first batch depending on grade and quantity); gold/silver from jewellery supply shops in Zaveri Bazaar or SEEPZ area (₹1-5L). Visit supplier showrooms, negotiate payment terms (typically 15-30 days post-supply), verify hallmark certificates (BIS standard).
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 500-1,500 sq ft unit in SEEPZ (₹20-40K/month, 5-year lease typical) for machine-made jewelry or Zaveri Bazaar area (₹25-60K/month) for handcrafted work. Alternatively, partner with 3-5 artisans on piece-rate basis (no rent). Equipment cost: basic designs + CNC/polishing tools ₹1-3L. Initial inventory + working capital ₹3-8L. Total first setup: ₹5-15L.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) free on udyamregistration.gov.in (2-3 days, no cost). Open business bank account (₹0 to ₹5K for MSME packages). File IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) with DGFT if exporting (₹0 cost, 7-10 days via icegate.gov.in). Obtain BIS hallmark certificate for gold/silver (₹2-5K per product type, 10-15 days). GI registration not applicable for Mumbai City gems/jewellery, but BIS certification mandatory.
5
First Sale
Launch first batch on Etsy (2.6% + $0.20 fee per order, 48-hour approval) or Meesho (no commission, seller retains 100% for first 3 months). First product: 10 units of best-selling design (estimated ₹50-100K order value). Target first-month revenue ₹1-2L via D2C. Simultaneously contact 5 retail jewellers in your city for wholesale orders (₹2-5L typical first order). Post catalog on Bharat Diamond Bourse Supplier Portal (free listing).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (roughly ₹1.2Cr annual): hire 2-3 design/production staff (₹15-20K/month each), add 1 sales/export coordinator (₹18K/month). Exhibit at IIJS (International Jewellery Show, February, Mumbai) and IGJF (Jaipur) to meet importers—booth cost ₹2-3L. Apply for export subsidy under Maharashtra Gems Jewellery Policy 2025 (details via msme.gov.in). Scale to 1,500+ sq ft workshop, hire 8-10 artisans, establish 2-3 retail franchises.

Where to Set Up

Infrastructure ready hai — plot lo aur shuru karo

Bharat Diamond Bourse (BDB)
20-acre hub, 2 million sq ft, 2,500 offices for diamond trading, cutting, polishing, design; state-of-the-art security, on-site testing labs, 60,000 employees. Office rental ₹25-80/sq ft/month depending on floor and location. Suited for trading, design studios, export units.
MSME Cluster
SEEPZ (Special Economic Zone)
111-acre machine-made jewelry hub specializing in fashion, costume, machine-cut designs; 30% of India's jewelry exports originate here. Plot sizes 2,000-10,000 sq ft, rental ₹20-50/sq ft/month. SEZ duty exemptions on raw material import and finished goods export; shared CFC (common facility centre) available.
SEZ
India Jewellery Park Mumbai (IJPM)
Upcoming mega facility in Navi Mumbai (adjacent to Mumbai City); ₹50,000 crore infrastructure investment planned; expected 1,00,000+ jobs. Plot allotment expected 2025 onwards. Common facility centre (CFC) for finishing, testing, hallmarking. Subsidized plots for registered units. Contact: ijpm.in

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L loan with 35% subsidy for manufacturing units; processing via SIDBI or MSME banks.
🏛
MUDRA Scheme
up to ₹10L unsecured loan at 8-11% interest for registered MSMEs; no collateral required.
🏚
Maharashtra Gems Jewellery Policy 2025
targets ₹1 lakh crore investment, creation of 5 lakh jobs, export promotion subsidies (details via Maharashtra MSME portal); validity 5 years from approval.

₹50,000 crore investment infrastructure in Navi Mumbai; expect 1,00,000+ new jobs; common facility centre (CFC) rental assistance and subsidized plots for registered units.
