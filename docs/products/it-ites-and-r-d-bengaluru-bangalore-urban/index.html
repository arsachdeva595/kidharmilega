Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bengaluru (Bangalore) Urban ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bengaluru's tech clusters: Whitefield, Koramangala, Indiranagar, and Electronic City. Attend events at IIM-B, NASSCOM headquarters, and co-working spaces like WeWork/The Hive. Connect with accelerators like Blume Ventures, Y Combinator India partners, and TiE Bangalore. Analyze 5-10 successful SaaS/AI startups in the region.
2
Source Raw Materials
Secure cloud infrastructure through AWS/Google Cloud/Azure free tiers (₹0-5L for first 12 months). Access open-source frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch, Kubernetes—free). Partner with hardware vendors in Bangalore for server procurement (₹10-20L for pilot infrastructure). Allocate ₹3-5L for team hiring.
3
Produce / Partner
Build or hire a technical co-founder team (2-4 engineers minimum). Join incubators like IISc Bangalore, StartupxSeed, or Catalyst at NASSCOM. Establish agile development practices using in-house talent or contract engineers. Set up continuous integration/deployment pipelines and quality assurance protocols.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSE online (udyamregistration.gov.in). Obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Bangalore. Secure ISO 27001 (cybersecurity), ISO 9001 (quality), SOC 2 certifications. Register domain, establish corporate entity (Pvt Ltd/LLP), and create company GST within 7 days.
5
First Sale
Launch MVP on Product Hunt and Hacker News. Pitch to early adopters via LinkedIn (target CTOs, tech leads). Secure first 3-5 pilot clients through Y Combinator, AngelList, or personal networks. Run Google Ads/LinkedIn ads targeting enterprise decision-makers (₹2-5L monthly budget). Attend NASSCOM tech expos and Bangalore tech meetups.
6
Scale
Raise seed funding from Bangalore VCs (Accel, Sequoia, Lightspeed, Bessemer) targeting ₹50-200L. Expand team to 20-50 engineers. Build channel partnerships with system integrators and consulting firms. Enter global markets through reseller networks and establish sales offices in US/Europe by month 12-18.
