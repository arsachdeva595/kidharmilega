Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kolkata ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kasba Industrial Estate (Kolkata) on a weekday — meet 2-3 manufacturers at Khadim, Acknit, or Farinni Leather facilities (free, 2 hours). Call the Council for Leather Exports hotline to understand export requirements. Walk through Brisul Haat and Bagree market on Saturday morning to track customer demand, pricing, and bestsellers. Zero cost, do this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw leather from Kasba suppliers or Kanpur tanneries via brokers in Entally (₹150-350/sqft depending on grade). First batch: 100 sqft buffalo leather + hardware (zips, buckles, rivets) from Barabazar suppliers = ₹25-40K. Establish 15-day payment terms. Contact list: Kasba Industrial Estate Association office has verified supplier directory.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 400-600 sqft semi-finished unit in Kasba Industrial Estate (₹4000-7000/month) with cutting tables, stitching machines, edge-finishing tools. Alternatively, partner with artisans in Kolkata who work on subcontract (₹80-150 per piece for bag assembly). Initial machinery cost: ₹1.5-3L (cutting table, 4-5 stitching machines, edge-finishing setup). Labor: hire 2-3 skilled leather artisans at ₹12-18K/month each.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 15 minutes online at udyamregistration.gov.in). Apply for GI registration if developing a heritage-linked collection (₹10-20K filing cost, 18-24 months process) through DC Handicraft office. If exporting, obtain IEC from nearest DGFT office (₹0, online form, 1 week). Get ISO 9001:2008 certification if targeting institutional buyers (₹30-50K, 4-8 weeks).
5
First Sale
First sale: list 10-15 designs on Udaan or Meesho (free listing). Approach 5 retailers in Brisul Haat, Bagree personally with samples. Target first bulk order from a hotel or logistics company via LinkedIn outreach. Expected first month: 50-100 units sold = ₹30-60K revenue. Use to reinvest in inventory.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire a full-time operations manager (₹20K), a designer (₹18K), and 3-5 more artisans. Rent a larger 1200-1500 sqft workspace. Apply for SFURTI cluster development subsidy (up to ₹50L for equipment/infrastructure). Exhibit at IIFF (India International Footwear Fair) annually. Launch export to Bangladesh via Council for Leather Exports (adds 20-30% margin). Develop 5-7 core SKUs, reduce customization overhead.

PM Vishwakarma Yojana
₹15,000 tooling subsidy + 5% interest subvention on ₹1-3L working capital loan for artisans and small leather goods makers
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹50L subsidy for cluster infrastructure, equipment, common facility centers in Kasba Industrial Estate
🏚
West Bengal One District One Product Subsidy
₹2-5L subsidy for leather goods manufacturers registering under ODOP scheme in Kolkata
🏚
West Bengal Artisan Credit Card Scheme
zero-collateral working capital loan up to ₹5L at 4% interest for registered leather artisans
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
