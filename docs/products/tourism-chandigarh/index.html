Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Chandigarh ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Rock Garden, Sukhna Lake Rose Garden, Government Museum Sector 10 on foot; call 2-3 travel agencies listed on Tourism Chandigarh website; attend Ministry of Tourism monthly tourism stakeholder meet in Sector 17 (free, open to entrepreneurs); request copy of ₹100 crore master plan document from Chandigarh Tourism Department website.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source local guides from Chandigarh Tourism Association (Sector 17), hotels in Sector 35, travel agencies in Sector 22; contract first 5 guides at ₹1,500-2,500/day; arrange Sukhna Lake boat rental partnership (₹50,000-100,000 upfront for tie-up); first batch operational cost ₹200,000-300,000 for guide training, insurance, marketing collateral.
3
Produce / Partner
Register as tourism experience operator: Rent 200-300 sq ft office in Sector 22 (₹15,000-25,000/month) or work from home initially; hire 2-3 experienced tour coordinators (₹15,000-20,000/month each); partner with 15-20 freelance guides rather than employ (payment on per-tour basis); initial operational setup ₹500,000-800,000 covering office, guide coordination platform (Viator or custom WhatsApp system), basic insurance.
4
Brand & Register
Register MSME on Udyam portal (free, 15 minutes); apply for Tourism Ministry recognition under Swadesh Darshan 2.0 scheme (free); open IEC code from nearest DGFT office for international tour exports (₹5,000 one-time); register tourism business under Chandigarh UT Tourism Act; get GST registration (18% applicable on tourism services); total compliance cost ₹20,000-30,000, timeline 30-45 days.
5
First Sale
First sale: List 3 signature experiences on Airbnb Experiences (commission 25%, takes 1 week approval); post 10-15 Instagram Reels showcasing Rock Garden hidden spots, Sukhna Lake sunrise, museum guides (free); send 20 cold pitches to wedding planners and corporate event companies (target first contract ₹50,000-100,000); expect first 3-5 bookings within 30 days, ₹150,000-250,000 first-month gross revenue.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: Hire 1 full-time operations manager (₹25,000/month); contract 40-50 guides instead of 20; add 5-6 signature experiences (food tours, heritage walks, photography tours); partner with 10-15 premium hotels for concierge revenue share; apply for Swadesh Darshan 2.0 subsidy for experience infrastructure (up to 60% project cost); exhibit at SATTE Delhi travel trade show (₹150,000 stall cost); target international operator tie-ups with UK/US tour companies.
