Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Chandigarh ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Chandigarh Tourism Bureau at Sector 16, meet experienced tour operators in Sector 17-22, study Rock Garden and Sukhna Lake visitor patterns. Interview 10+ existing tour operators and hotel concierges to understand demand, seasonality, and operational gaps.
2
Source Raw Materials
Partner with 2-3 local transport operators for vehicle hire (₹2-4L initial commitment), establish relationships with 5-10 hotels/homestays in Sectors 9-35 for bulk bookings, hire 2-3 experienced local guides (₹15-20K monthly). First-batch operational cost: ₹3-5L for 3 months.
3
Produce / Partner
Register as tour operator with Chandigarh UT Tourism Department, create 5-6 signature tour packages (half-day/full-day Rock Garden+Museum+Lake routes), develop partnerships with 8-10 hospitality providers, establish vendor network with restaurants for food experiences.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) as Service Provider, obtain IEC for inbound tourism packages, register brand name at IP India, get GST registration, secure Tour Operator License from Chandigarh Tourism, create Instagram/YouTube channels with high-quality tour videos.
5
First Sale
List on Tripadvisor, GetYourGuide, and Airbnb Experiences immediately, create WhatsApp business profile with booking automation, partner with 3-4 hotels for direct bookings, launch targeted Facebook/Instagram ads targeting 'Chandigarh tourism' and 'Rock Garden tours'. First goal: 5 bookings in month 1.
6
Scale
After month 6 profitability, hire 2 additional guides, expand to 12-15 tour variants, establish corporate retreat packages, build email list of 5K+ past guests for repeat bookings, partner with travel agencies in Delhi/Punjab, launch luxury 'experience' packages at 3x margin.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy for tourism service enterprises in tier 2 cities.
🏛
MUDRA Loan Yojana
up to ₹10L collateral-free credit for tourism business expansion.
🏚
Chandigarh Tourism Infrastructure Development Scheme
grants for hospitality-linked businesses and tour operators registering with UT Tourism.
🏚
UT Chandigarh MSME Support Scheme
10% subsidy on equipment and technology purchases for registered tour operators.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
