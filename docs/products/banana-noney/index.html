Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Noney ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Noney agricultural office, study banana farming in Senapati district (adjacent), connect with Manipur Agricultural Department at Imphal. Attend ICAR-ATARI workshops. Visit established banana growers in Moirang area and Loktak region. Study banana market dynamics at Guwahati wholesale markets.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source disease-free banana suckers from Manipur Agricultural University nursery (₹8-12 per sucker, minimum 500 units = ₹4,000-6,000 for 0.5 hectare). Organic fertilizer from local compost makers or ICAR recommendations. Initial batch cost including land preparation: ₹1.5-2.5 lakhs for 0.5-1 hectare.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 5-10 banana farmers in Noney via contract farming agreement guaranteeing ₹15,000-18,000 per ton. Train farmers in GAP (Good Agricultural Practices) certified by Manipur Agricultural Department. Establish small collection center at Noney town. Use cold storage facilities in Imphal (hire) or establish ₹50,000 seasonal storage unit.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online at udyamregistration.gov.in — free), apply for IEC code via iccnet.dicgov.in. Register organic certification with APEDA-recognized certifier (₹15,000-25,000 annually). Brand registration: Noney Bananas Pvt Ltd via MeiteiKhongKhalanaba or similar local-meaning Hindi name. Get FSSAI license (₹5,000-10,000).
5
First Sale
List on BigBasket, Grofers, Amazon Fresh with ₹20,000 listing fee. Create Instagram & WhatsApp Business account with farmer-story content (farmer portraits, plantation videos). Partner with 5-10 premium organic retailers in Delhi-NCR and Bangalore. Launch WhatsApp-based bulk ordering (minimum 20 kg) for restaurants in Guwahati and Assam region. Attend Noney Farmer Melas for direct B2B connections.
6
Scale
After first 6 months (once 50 tons/month validated): expand to 5-10 farmer partnerships covering 5-10 hectares. Invest ₹2-3 lakhs in processing unit (banana chips, dried banana, puree). Apply for PMEGP grant (up to ₹25L) for processing expansion. Launch B2B export via traders to Bangladesh (minimum 500 tons/year). Build cold chain infrastructure (₹50-75 lakhs) with SIDBI/NABARD support. Partner with food processors for contract manufacturing.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh grant for processing unit, 25% subsidy for SC/ST founders
🏛
NABARD's Agricultural Infrastructure Fund
up to ₹2 crore loan for cold chain, storage, processing infrastructure at concessional rates
🏚
Manipur Agricultural Mechanization Scheme
50% subsidy on farm machinery up to ₹2 lakhs for equipment purchase
🏚
Manipur Organic Farming Scheme
₹10,000/hectare annual support + free organic certification for 3 years
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
