Kanpur Dehat sits 45km south of Kanpur city, India's metalworking hub, giving immediate access to zinc, tin, and copper suppliers at wholesale rates. The Raniya-Shivli cluster spans 8 villages with 400+ registered artisans and 600+ unregistered workshops producing daily-use utensils and cookware. Buyers include rural UP (Lucknow, Varanasi, Agra markets), institutional canteens, and exporters to Bangladesh, Nepal, and Gulf states via Kanpur's trading networks.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kanpur Dehat ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Raniya-Shivli zinc cluster on any Tuesday (market day), call 3 existing makers, attend Kanpur Dehat MSME monthly meetup at District Gramodyog Officer Office (free entry) — all doable this week at ₹0
2
Source Raw Materials
Source zinc sheets from Kanpur city metal suppliers (Jajmau industrial area, 40km away) or local wholesalers in Raniya. First 500-unit batch costs ₹25,000-₹35,000 raw material. Supplier names available at District Gramodyog Office.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent a 300-400 sq ft workshop in Raniya MSME cluster for ₹3,000-5,000/month, buy hand tools (₹40,000), hire 1-2 trained artisans (₹8,000-10,000/month). Option B: Outsource production to existing artisan at ₹12-18 per unit. Total setup: ₹1.8-5L for Option A, ₹0.5-1L for Option B.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (₹0, 10 minutes, use Aadhar + PAN). Apply for Chief Minister Matikala Gramodyog Rojgar Yojana: max ₹10L project cost, 25% subsidy, 5% beneficiary contribution. Apply by 15 May 2026 at District Gramodyog Officer Office, Bank of India first floor, Chitikpur Chauraha, Raniya. Obtain BIS certification for cookware (optional but increases D2C price by 8-12%). Get IEC code from DPIIT (₹500, 2 weeks) if exporting.
5
First Sale
First sale: Contact Kanpur-based wholesale traders or list on Udaan (registration free). First order: 50-100 units to a regional retailer, expected revenue ₹15,000-25,000. Aim for 20 units/day direct sales via Instagram+WhatsApp by month 3.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 1 full-time sales person, open WhatsApp catalog, exhibit at UP State MSME Trade Fair (annual), partner with 5-10 regional distributors, move to 800-1000 sq ft workshop. By year 2: apply for Pradhan Mantri Micro Units Development Refinance Agency (MUDRA) for ₹25L expansion loan at 8% interest.
