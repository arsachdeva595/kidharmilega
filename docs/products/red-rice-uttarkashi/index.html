Uttarkashi district spans 8,016 sq km across the Garhwal Himalayas at 1,500-3,500m elevation, where cool climate and mineral-rich soil produce red rice with 40% higher antioxidant content than white rice varieties. The district has 330,090 residents with 600+ farmer collectives cultivating red rice across Purola, Uttarkashi, and Dunda tehsils — over 8,000 hectares under cultivation. Export-ready infrastructure exists: NH44 connects to Delhi (350km), Dehradun airport (210km), and direct sea-freight routes from Mumbai/Kandla ports to Thailand, Italy, Vietnam, USA reach buyers within 30-40 days.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Uttarkashi ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Purola or Uttarkashi town mandi on Monday/Thursday (weekly haat days). Spend 4-5 hours: speak directly with 4-5 farmer collective heads (ask for contact from Uttarkashi Krishi Gram Vikas Kendra), call 2-3 rice millers in Uttarkashi town (names: Himalaya Rice Mills, Garhwal Pride), attend one farmer-to-buyer transaction. Cost: ₹800 travel. Do this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from Purola Agricultural Cooperative Union (1,200 farmers, 3.5MT/month average harvest) or Uttarkashi Krishi Vikas Samiti. First batch: 2-3 MT raw paddy at ₹2,200-2,600/MT (wet), yields 1.6-1.8MT milled rice after 15% moisture loss. Cost: ₹4.4-7.8L for 3MT raw paddy. Supplier contact: Purola tehsil office connects you within 2 days.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Partner with existing rice miller in Uttarkashi (5-6 operational units, ₹25/kg milling fee). Rent space for storage (₹3,000-5,000/month) + packaging. Option B: Buy used huller+polisher from Dehradun (₹1.5-2.5L setup), rent 800 sq ft unit (₹6,000-8,000/month), hire 2-3 seasonal staff (₹12,000-15,000/month each). Total production cost: ₹2.8-4.2L for 3MT output including milling, drying, packing labor.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (5 minutes, online, free). File GI registration as 'Uttarkashi Red Rice' with Ministry of Agriculture (₹5,000 govt fee, 4-6 month process; use ODOP cluster certification). If exporting: Apply for IEC from DGFT Dehradun office (₹0 fee, 7 days online). Hire local CA (₹8,000-12,000) to handle FSSAI registration (₹5,000) and GST (18% compliance).
5
First Sale
First sale: contact BigBasket/Blinkit category managers directly (email: seller@bigbasket.com, vendor-setup@blinkit.com) with GI cert + organic/conventional certification. Offer 25kg trial order at ₹85/kg wholesale. Alternative: Cold-call 20 organic retail chains in nearby Himachal Pradesh towns (Shimla, Mandi) — ₹500/kg delivered, 50kg minimum. Expected first month: 150-200kg at ₹55-75/kg = ₹8,250-15,000 gross revenue.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (≈2MT/month sales): Hire 1 order manager (₹18,000/month), contract with 2 farmer groups for steady supply, register for organic certification (₹15,000/year, helps D2C command 40% premium). Target expansion: build branded packaging (₹80,000 design + ₹2L for 50,000 units), apply for SFURTI subsidy (₹25L capital grant for processing unit), exhibit at Global Organic Expo (Vigyan Bhawan, Delhi, Oct) and Asia Food Trade Show (Bangkok, Apr). Hire export manager (₹25,000/month) to pursue 5-10 Thai/Italian bulk buyers.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹15,000 credit + ₹500 cashback for MSME registration + free toolkits (if you hire artisans), 5% interest subsidy for ₹3L loans
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
₹25L subsidy (25% for general, 35% SC/ST) for milling unit setup, 6-8 week approval if linked to Uttarkashi district ODOP
🏚
Uttarakhand ODOP Subsidy Scheme
25-40% capital subsidy (up to ₹15L) for processing/milling units, 12-month approval cycle via Uttarkashi District Industries Centre
🏚
Uttarakhand Agriculture Export Promotion Scheme
₹2L subsidy for IEC registration, shipping costs (FOB discount up to 15%), APEDA certification reimbursement
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
