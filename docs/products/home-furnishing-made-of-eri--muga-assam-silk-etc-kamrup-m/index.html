Home Furnishing made of Eri , Muga & Assam Silk, etc. ki
opportunity, Kamrup (M) mein.

Kamrup (M) district, anchored by Guwahati city, sits in Assam's eastern valley with Som and Soalu forest zones within 100 km supplying raw cocoons year-round. The Kamrup and Sualkuchi clusters alone house 6,000+ handloom weavers producing 400+ home furnishing pieces daily—cushion covers, bed linens, wall hangings, and upholstery fabric in signature golden-sheen Muga silk (which intensifies with each wash). Buyers are concentrated in Tier 1 metros (40% demand), Southeast Asia export hubs (35%), and institutional hospitality chains (25%).

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kamrup (M) ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sualkuchi or Kamrup cluster haat (Saturday/Wednesday mornings, free entry) — talk to 5 weavers about production timelines and costs. Call 3 established home furnishing makers via Kamrup Chamber of Commerce directory (free). Join Assam Silk Weaver WhatsApp groups to understand current prices. Register free on Udyam portal using Aadhaar.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from Sualkuchi cluster (40 km from Guwahati) or Kamrup town weavers — first batch cost ₹60K-2L depending on order size (50-200 pieces). Payment terms: 50% advance, 50% on delivery (2-4 weeks). Verify supplier's GI certification and past customer references.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Partnership with 3-5 master weavers in Kamrup/Sualkuchi cluster (no capital needed, 30-40% of revenue to them). Option B: Rent 500 sq ft workshop space near cluster (₹5-10K/month) and employ 2-3 junior weavers (salary ₹8-12K/month). Option C: Pre-order model — take customer orders, pay weavers per unit (₹200-600 per piece depending on complexity), keep 50-65% margin.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, free, 15 min). Apply for GI-tagged certification under Assam Silk promotion board (₹500 application fee, 30-45 days). If exporting: Apply for IEC code via ICEGATE portal (₹1,000 processing fee, 10 days). Join Ministry of Textile's ODOP database (free listing).
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy or Amazon Handmade (10% commission, 2-week setup) with 20-30 products. Target first order from interior designer or corporate buyer — approach 10 studios in your city with ₹50K minimum order offer. Expected first month: 15-25 units sold = ₹37,500-2L gross revenue (if D2C) or ₹50K-1L (if B2B).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: Hire a full-time operations manager (₹15K/month). Expand to 8-12 partner weavers. Launch Instagram + Pinterest content (3x/week reels showing weaving process). Apply for North Eastern Region Textile Promotion Scheme (NERTPS) subsidy (up to 30% capex support). Exhibit at International Home and Lifestyle Fair (IHLF) Delhi or Heimtextil Frankfurt. Develop 5-7 seasonal collections.
