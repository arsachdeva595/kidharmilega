Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kandhamal ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Gumamaaha Raikhia Haldi Cluster (15 km from Phulbani town) and speak to 3-4 turmeric farmers and KASAM (Kandhamal Spice and Agriculture Marketing) representatives. Call the Kandhamal District Agriculture Office (0669-2220123). Attend Phulbani weekly haat on Wednesday and Thursday to see turmeric trading firsthand. Zero cost, arrange via local farming contacts.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source dried turmeric rhizomes directly from farmers in Gumamaaha and Raikhia blocks during October-November harvest. First batch: 500 kg at ₹35-40/kg (₹17,500-20,000). These farmers deliver to your collection center or local KASAM facility. Quality check: ensure moisture under 12%, purity 95%+.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 500 sq ft unit in Phulbani town (₹8,000-12,000/month) with drying, grinding, and packing setup. OR partnership with existing turmeric powder makers in the cluster and pay ₹5-8 per kg for processing. Initial investment: ₹1.5-2.5L for rent (deposit + setup) + ₹1.5-2L for grinder, sieve, packing machine, and drying racks. Hire 1-2 local workers at ₹7,000-9,000/month.
4
Brand & Register
Register as Udyam (free, 10 mins online). Apply for GI certification linkage through Kandhamal District ODOP nodal agency — no additional fee, brand can use 'Kandhamal Haladi' GI tag. File for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) if exporting (₹0, digital process). Food License from FSSAI (₹2,000-5,000 for manufacturing license, 15 days).
5
First Sale
First sale: list on Amazon and Flipkart under turmeric powder category (₹499-699 per 250g). First order target: 50 packs/week via D2C by month 2. Parallel: approach 2-3 Ayurveda brands in Bangalore/Mumbai with samples (₹10-15/kg wholesale). Expected first-month revenue: ₹25,000-40,000 from online + ₹15,000-25,000 from B2B initial orders.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: shift to 2,000 sq ft facility (₹18,000-22,000/month), hire 5-6 workers for packing and QC, invest in automated packaging machine (₹2-3L). Add turmeric capsules and blends (200% margins). Export: hire customs agent in Bhubaneswar (₹30,000/shipment), apply for organic certification (₹40-60K one-time) to unlock EU/US buyers. Exhibit at Spice Board trade shows in Delhi and Frankfurt.

Frequently Asked Questions

Seedhe sawaal, seedhe jawab

₹3-5 lakh se start kar sakte ho. Processing unit rent ₹10k/month, turmeric raw material ₹20k for first 500 kg, machinery ₹1.5-2 lakh, license ₹5k. Agar partnership karo farmer ke saath, turmeric khud dete hain, tab sirf ₹2-3 lakh se ho jayega.
Farming experience zero samajhna padta hai, par supply chain samajhna zaroori hai. Phulbani ke farmers ke saath 2-3 mahine close kaam karo, tab turmeric quality aur pricing samajh jayega. Turmeric drying aur grinding ke liye local workers hire kar sakte ho.
PMEGP se ₹10 lakh tak subsidy (25-35%), SFURTI se cluster support, Odisha MSME loan par 20% interest subsidy. Spice Board export grant bhi hai. Total ₹15-20 lakh tak government support possible hai.
Amazon-Flipkart se direct D2C customer milega, 50-100 packs/month se shuru. Parallel mein Ayurveda companies like Patanjali, Himalaya ko samples bhejo, ₹15-25/kg se bulk orders aa sakte hain. Local organic stores Bhubaneswar aur Delhi mein bhi list kar sakte ho.
Bilkul. USA, UK, Germany, Japan ko turmeric bhej sakte ho. Organic certification lelo (₹50-60k), IEC apply karo (free), customs agent Bhubaneswar mein hire karo. Minimum 500 kg shipment profitable hai, FOB price $6-8 per kg, 45-60% margin.
Turmeric har saal harvest hota hai aur fresh stock bante rehte hain, so inventory rot nahi hoga. Agar branded product nahi chalti, raw turmeric aur powder ko wholesale traders ko bech sakte ho (₹30-40/kg). Farmer partnership zyada safe hai kyunki turmeric guaranteed sale ho jayega. Worst case, ₹3-5 lakh investment recovery ho jayega local buyers ke through.
