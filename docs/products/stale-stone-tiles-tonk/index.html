Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Tonk ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit established slate tile clusters in Tonk town and nearby Jaipur stone markets (Kishanpole, Ramganj). Study production at Tonk Stone Association members. Analyze export data from Jaipur Chamber of Commerce. Spend 2 weeks shadowing 2-3 established tile manufacturers.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw slate blocks from quarry operators in Tonk district (₹35,000-50,000 per ton). Negotiate 10-ton bulk orders. Establish relationships with tool suppliers in Jaipur (cutting wheels, polishing pads). Budget ₹4-6 Lakhs for first batch of 50-80 sq.meters of finished tiles.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing artisan collectives in Tonk (Tonk Stone Craftsmen Guild) to lease workshop space with equipment, or invest in semi-automated tile-cutting and polishing units (₹8-12 Lakhs for machinery). Start with 200-300 sq.meters monthly capacity. Hire 4-6 skilled workers at ₹12,000-18,000/month each.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Micro/Small business portal) for MSME status. Apply for IEC code via ICEGATE for export. Register trademark with IP Office. Obtain ISO 9001 certification (₹40,000-60,000) to strengthen B2B credibility. Open GST registration.
5
First Sale
Launch on ArchiPro and Houzz with high-quality product images and 3D tiles visualizations. Contact 50 architects and interior designers in metros via LinkedIn with product samples. Pitch to 10-15 builders in Jaipur, Delhi, Bangalore. Attend Plumbex India and Batimat India (construction material trade shows). Offer 5% early-buyer discount for first 5 bulk orders.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand product line to include honed, brushed, and tumbled finishes. Invest in 1-2 additional polishing units. Hire export coordinator and attend international trade shows (Coverings USA, Marmomacc Italy). Establish partnerships with 2-3 tile distributors in USA and EU. Increase monthly capacity to 1000+ sq.meters.
