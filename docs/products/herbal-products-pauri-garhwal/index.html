Pauri Garhwal's high-altitude terrain (1200-3600m) provides ideal climatic conditions for growing medicinal herbs like Ashwagandha, Brahmi, Kutki, and Jatamansi. The district's rich biodiversity and traditional Ayurvedic knowledge systems make it naturally suited for herbal product manufacturing. Local farmer cooperatives and established supply chains support consistent raw material procurement.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Pauri Garhwal ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Pauri Garhwal herbal clusters in Srikot and Majkhali villages; interview farmer cooperatives like Garhwal Herbals Farmers Group; study competitors on Amazon, Nykaa, and NaturalCare; attend Uttarakhand AYUSH Department workshops on herbal product standards
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from certified organic farmers in Pauri via Uttarakhand Organic Farming Board; expect ₹80,000-₹150,000 for first 500kg mixed herb batch (Ashwagandha ₹400/kg, Brahmi ₹300/kg); establish contracts with 3-4 local suppliers for supply security
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with GMP-certified herbal processing units in Kotdwar (40km from Pauri); alternatively, set up small-batch production with dried herb processing and tea packing machinery (₹200,000-₹400,000); ensure FSSAI registration for food products or Ayurveda license for formulations
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME within 2 days (udyamregistration.gov.in); apply for IEC code at DGFT office in Dehradun; obtain FSSAI license (₹10,000-₹15,000); get Ayurveda certification from state AYUSH board; register trademark with IP office (₹4,000-₹8,000)
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Handmade and Flipkart Seller Central (commission 15-20%); build D2C Shopify store with Instagram/Facebook ads targeting wellness keywords; partner with 10-15 local wellness stores in Delhi-NCR; join Nykaa Beauty as vendor; start with ₹2,00,000 initial inventory across channels
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand product line to herbal formulations and supplements; establish export relationships with APEDA-recognized agents; apply for GI certification post-scale to differentiate; hire dedicated content creator for YouTube educational content; establish B2B sales team for clinic partnerships; explore contract manufacturing for brands
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy for manufacturing units with 15% margin support
🏛
PM Vishwakarma Yojana
₹15,000 initial grant + ₹5,000 annual toolkit support for herbal artisans and small manufacturers
🏚
Uttarakhand Agriculture Promotion Policy 2023
35% subsidy on herbal processing machinery and GMP certification costs for ODOP products
🏚
Uttarakhand AYUSH Development Scheme
free training, ₹3 lakh subsidy for herbal product certifications, and market linkage support through state AYUSH Ministry
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
