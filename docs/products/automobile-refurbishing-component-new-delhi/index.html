Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — New Delhi ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Okhla Industrial Estate Phase 1-3 and Ecotech Extension in New Delhi; network with established component suppliers like Hindustan Motors ancillary units; attend biannual Auto Component Summit New Delhi; join SIAM (Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers) chapters; study competitor catalogs from Lumax, Motherson Sumi, and local players.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source plastic resins from polymers distributors in Okhla (₹40-60/kg); steel from local suppliers in Phase 2 Okhla (₹35-45/kg); rubber from Jaipur-Delhi highway suppliers (₹80-120/kg); fasteners from Chandni Chowk and Karol Bagh wholesale markets. Budget ₹20-30L for first production batch of 500-1000 units across SKUs.
3
Produce / Partner
Establish 5000-8000 sq ft manufacturing unit in Okhla Phase 3 or Ecotech (₹30-50/sq ft/month); acquire injection molding machines (₹10-15L), CNC machining centers (₹8-12L), and assembly fixtures; hire experienced production supervisors from nearby Maruti/Hyundai supplier networks; partner with NASSCOM-affiliated plastic molding units for overflow capacity.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam-MSME on udyamregistration.gov.in; apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) at DGFT portal for export eligibility; register brand name with Indian Patent Office; obtain ISO/TS 16949 certification (mandatory for OEM supply); get BIS certification for components; register with GST and obtain DIN for company structure.
5
First Sale
Establish direct relationships with NADA-affiliated dealerships across Delhi-NCR through targeted outreach; list products on IndiaMART and TradeKey for B2B visibility; attend Automechanika Delhi (biennial trade show); send samples to top 50 authorized service centers with 15-day trial offers; engage a sales team to visit repair workshops in Karol Bagh, Malviya Nagar, and Mehrauli clusters; create WhatsApp catalog for quick inquiries.
6
Scale
After 6 months, establish sub-assembly manufacturing partnerships with 2-3 nearby units to increase capacity 3x; launch export operations through freight forwarders in Inderlok; develop 5-10 new SKUs based on market feedback; hire dedicated export/quality manager; invest in ERP system for inventory and order tracking; secure working capital facility from MUDRA/bank for faster turnover.
