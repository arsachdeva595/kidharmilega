Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Devbhoomi Dwarka ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Khambhaliya weaving cluster in Devbhoomi Dwarka district; connect with Master Weavers Association; spend 2-3 weeks understanding loom mechanics, design patterns, and dye processes at production centers near Khambhaliya town
2
Source Raw Materials
Source premium Merino wool from Bikaner wool market (₹800-1200/kg) and silk from Surat textile suppliers (₹600-900/meter); natural dyes from Rajasthan dye suppliers; first batch of 50 shawls requires ₹100,000-150,000 raw material investment
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 3-5 certified artisans through Devbhoomi Dwarka Handloom Cooperative; establish production agreement with quality benchmarks; plan 10-15 shawls/month initial production; leverage SFURTI clusters if available for shared workspace
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (udyamregistration.gov.in); apply for IEC code via DGFT portal; file trademark for brand name with IP office; create GI documentation for future certification; obtain Handloom Mark from Textile Commissioner
5
First Sale
Launch on Flipkart Handmade, Etsy, and own Shopify store; pitch to luxury brands on Instagram and LinkedIn; contact heritage fashion editors for coverage; offer 15-20 pieces to Nykaa Fashion for initial placement; partner with 2-3 heritage boutiques in Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore
6
Scale
Expand artisan network to 10-15 weavers; launch seasonal collections; establish pop-up stores in metro cities; build corporate gifting vertical; launch export partnerships with luxury retailers in US/UK; create content around artisan stories for social media growth (₹20,000/month content budget)

PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakhs loan with 5-6% government subsidy for handloom startups
🏚
Gujarat Handloom Weaver Support Scheme
direct subsidy of ₹5,000-10,000 per loom per annum for registered weavers
🏚
Gujarat ODOP Promotion Scheme
marketing support, quality certification assistance, and export market linkages for identified ODOP products
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
