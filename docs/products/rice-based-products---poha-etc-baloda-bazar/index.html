Baloda Bazar is in central Chhattisgarh, 85km from Raipur (major buyer market). The district harvests rice across 293,769 hectares with reliable April-October paddy supply. Nearest railhead at Dhamtari (45km) and Raipur (85km) connects to Delhi, Mumbai, and eastern export hubs. Local ODOP cluster has 40-60 small-scale poha and murmura producers concentrated in Baloda Bazar, Bhatapara, and Mandir Hasaud blocks.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Baloda bazar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Baloda Bazar haat on Tuesday or Friday morning; meet 3-5 existing poha producers (names available at district ODOP office); call Baloda Bazar ODOP coordinator (contact via nic.in website); attend free SFURTI orientation session if running. Zero cost, 4 hours total, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source parboiled rice from millers in Mandir Hasaud block (20km away)—first batch ₹40-50k for 500kg. Meet suppliers at Baloda Bazar grain market on Wednesdays. Negotiate 10-15% credit for first order. Salt and packaging from Raipur wholesalers (₹8-12k for cartons, labels).
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent 500 sq ft unit in existing MSME cluster in Bhatapara (₹3k-5k/month). Option B: Partner with 2-3 artisans in Baloda Bazar town (they own equipment, you provide rice + capital, pay ₹15-20/kg commission). Equipment cost if solo: ₹2-4L (rice flattening machine + drying beds). Food license: ₹5-8k, 15 days.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 10 mins online). Apply for FSSAI registration (₹5-8k, 15 days). If exporting: Get IEC code from APEDA in Raipur (free, 5 days + ₹500 documentation). Apply for GI registration support via ODOP scheme (reimbursement up to ₹50k if approved). Total: ₹15-20k, 30-45 days.
5
First Sale
First batch: produce 200kg, list on Blinkit and Amazon Fresh (approval 7-10 days, ₹0 fee first month). Approach 5 local grocery chains (Baloda Bazar, Bhatapara towns) with free sample (30kg). Expected first-month sales: ₹25-40k from online + ₹15-20k from wholesale. First order size: 50-100kg minimum from retailers.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (600-800kg/month), hire 1 production supervisor (₹8-12k/month), 1 delivery coordinator (₹6-8k/month). Move to 1500 sq ft facility (₹8-10k/month). Add murmura line (parallel production). Target 3-4 grocery chains per block. Exhibit at Raipur Food Fair (Jan-Feb) and Chhattisgarh Food Exhibition. Approach ICDS procurement boards in 2-3 blocks.
