Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Baran ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit the garlic farming clusters around Baran town and Chhabra block. Connect with the Baran District Agriculture Department and visit APMC Baran market to understand pricing, seasonality, and trader networks. Study existing garlic product companies in Rajasthan like those in Jodhpur.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source seed garlic directly from certified farmers in Baran district during July-August at ₹40-60 per kg for first 5-10 tonnes. Contact local agricultural cooperatives like Baran District Cooperative Agriculture Society. Arrange farmland lease through local landlords at ₹8,000-15,000 per acre annually.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established garlic farmers in Baran through contract farming model where you provide seeds, technology, and buyback guarantee. Or lease 5-10 acres directly near Chhabra or Baran town and hire local farm labor at ₹300-400 per day. Harvest typically July-August. For processing, start with a 5-10 tonne capacity unit using basic drying and grinding equipment (₹400K-800K investment).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSE) within 10 minutes online. Obtain IEC code from DGFT portal (₹0 cost, 3 days processing). Register for FSSAI license (₹2,000-5,000). Create a brand identity with Hindi and English labeling compliant with Spices Board standards. Trademark your brand name with IP India.
5
First Sale
List on Amazon Fresh, Blinkit, and Jiomart with bulk orders starting at 50 kg packs. Approach BigBasket and local supermarket chains with wholesale pricing. Contact Baran APMC traders for distribution into regional markets. Use WhatsApp Business to connect with restaurant and food service buyers. Create Instagram and YouTube content showing farm-to-table journey.
6
Scale
After 6 months, invest in cold storage facility (₹15-25L) near Baran town for off-season supply. Expand product line to garlic powder, dehydrated cloves, and fermented products. Build B2B relationships with 5-star hotels and food companies. Register for GI mark application (even if pending) for premium positioning. Explore international export through APEDA and freight forwarders in Delhi/Mumbai.
