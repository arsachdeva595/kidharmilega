Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Ferozepur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Ferozepur's key Basmati clusters: Makhu, Jalalabad, and Abohar towns. Meet established rice millers (Dunar, Daawat group have operations here) to understand procurement, grading standards, and export compliance. Connect with Punjab Agricultural University's Basmati research center in Ludhiana (30km away) for quality benchmarking.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified Basmati paddy directly from Ferozepur farmer cooperatives (cost ₹3000-3500/quintal for 100 quintals = ₹30-35L for first harvest). Partner with local APMC markets or direct farmer networks. Budget additional 20% for processing, drying, and milling labor.
3
Produce / Partner
Either lease 5-10 acres in Makhu taluka (₹15000-20000/acre/season for wet lease) and hire local labor, or partner with existing rice millers in Ferozepur who have spare capacity (₹200-300/quintal milling fee). GI certification requires processing in Ferozepur district only — no outsourcing permitted.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam as an MSME (via udyamregistration.gov.in, free). Obtain IEC code from DGFT (icegate.indiaport.gov.in) for exports. File GI-protection affidavit with district administration. Brand registration at Intellectual Property Office (Delhi, ₹4500). Get FSSAI food license (₹500 for manufacturing) and ISO 9001:2015 for export compliance (₹30000-50000).
5
First Sale
Launch D2C on Amazon, Flipkart (apply via seller central, margins 18-25% after fees). Approach BigBasket and Blinkit for bulk orders (minimum 500kg per order). Contact HoReCa distributors via NutriBiz, Indiamart. Export through APEDA-registered exporters or direct tie-ups with UK/US importers via Global Sources platform. Target 50kg initial wholesale orders to test product.
6
Scale
Month 7-12: Achieve ₹40-50L monthly revenue via mix of 60% wholesale, 30% e-commerce, 10% export. Invest in automated bagging machinery (₹15-25L) to reduce per-unit cost by 8-12%. Develop 2-3 premium variants (aged Basmati, organic, single-estate) to capture 35-40% margins. Build brand via Instagram (farm-to-table storytelling), YouTube rice cooking tutorials, and regional food influencer partnerships (₹3-5L annual budget).
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Formalisation of Micro food processing Enterprises (PMFME)
up to ₹10L capital subsidy for rice processing unit setup
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L grant/loan for food processing ventures with 25% subsidy for SC/ST entrepreneurs
🏚
Punjab Agricultural Infrastructure Development Fund (PAID)
₹2-5L subsidy for milling, drying, and storage equipment in agricultural zones
🏚
Punjab Rural Livelihood Mission (PRLM)
interest subvention of 2-3% on loans up to ₹20L for agri-processing MSMEs in Tier 2/3 districts
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
