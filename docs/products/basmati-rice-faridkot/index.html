Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Faridkot ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Faridkot's established rice mills in Arepur and Sadar Bazaar clusters; connect with Punjab Agricultural University's Ludhiana center for Basmati cultivation best practices; study GI certification standards from WIPO database
2
Source Raw Materials
Source Basmati paddy from certified farmers in Faridkot via APMC markets (₹2,500-3,500/quintal); partner with local seed agencies for certified seed stock; first-batch requirement: 50-100 quintals paddy (~₹150K-350K)
3
Produce / Partner
Lease milling capacity from existing Faridkot rice mills (₹15K-25K/month for 5-10 ton daily capacity) or invest in small 2-3 ton/hour rice mill unit; source polishing, grading, and packaging equipment from suppliers in Ludhiana's industrial area
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Ministry MSME portal) as rice miller/processor; obtain IEC code from nearest customs office; apply for GI-certified label permissions via District Agriculture Department; register brand name with Trade Mark Registry (₹4,500-9,000)
5
First Sale
List on Amazon, Blinkit, Bigbasket with ₹350-550/kg premium positioning; approach restaurant chains via B2B platforms (IndiaMART); contact export houses in Delhi/Mumbai for international orders; establish direct sales via Instagram/Facebook targeting NRI diaspora
6
Scale
After 6 months, add rice bran oil and flour products; establish wholesale distribution network across 5-10 major metros; explore Indiamart + Global Trade Platform exports to 8-10 countries; hire regional sales agents for institutional segment

MUDRA Yojana

🏚
Punjab Agri-Export Policy 2023
subsidy on export certification, logistics, and cold storage for agricultural products
🏚
RKVY-RAFTAAR
support for GI product promotion and export marketing infrastructure in Punjab
