Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Ghaziabad ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Loni Industrial Area (Thursday morning 9am when traders are active) — walk into 3-4 existing CNC shops, ask them about their first year, raw material costs, and current margins. Call SIDCUL helpline (toll-free) for cluster data. Register free Udyam account on udyamregistration.gov.in — takes 15 minutes. Zero cost, do this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from Loni Market traders (Shakti Steel, Rajesh Tubes, Modi Steels) — first batch ₹1.5-3L for 500kg mixed metals. Steel bars ₹55-75/kg, aluminium ₹200-240/kg, labour ₹8-12/hour in Mohan Nagar unit rentals.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 500-800 sq ft unit in Mohan Nagar or Dasna (₹3,000-5,000/month), install 2-3 second-hand CNC machines (₹8-12L per machine on resale markets like Quikr), hire 2 skilled operators (₹12-18K/month each). Total setup: ₹18-25L. Alternatively, start with job work arrangement with existing unit — zero rent, 12% of job revenue.
4
Brand & Register
File Udyam registration (₹0, online). Apply for PMEGP subsidy if first-time (₹25L eligible for 35% subsidy = ₹8.75L grant). Get IEC code from DGFT (₹500, 2 days online). For export orders, apply for ISO 9001 certification (₹30-50K, 6 weeks). Ghaziabad MSME office provides free export guidance and customs facilitation.
5
First Sale
Post detailed product photos on IndiaMART (free) with exact specifications. Target first orders from Loni traders and distributors — typical first order: ₹1.5-3L. Expected first month: 2-3 orders = ₹4-8L gross, ₹50-100K net profit after materials and labour.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire production manager (₹18-22K), add quality inspector (₹12-15K), invest in CNC upgrade or 4th machine (₹10-12L), get ISO 9001 and TS 16949 certifications for OEM contracts. Exhibit at India Engineering Trade Fair (IETF), Pragati Maidan. Join Ghaziabad Chamber of Commerce (₹5K/year) for buyer connects.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
35% subsidy up to ₹25L loan, 10% for SC/ST/OBC, zero interest first 36 months for MSME manufacturing.
🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹15K subsidy for hand tools and skill training, applicable for artisan-led production units in engineering goods sector.
🏚
UP MSME Policy 2023
8% interest subsidy on term loans up to ₹1Cr, free patent and trademark registration for first 2 years.
🏚
UP Export Promotion Scheme
5% subsidy on freight charges for exports above ₹10L annual value, free participation in 2 international trade fairs.
