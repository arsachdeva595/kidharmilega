opportunity, Gautam Buddh Nagar mein.

Gautam Buddh Nagar is 30-40 km from Delhi's Chandni Chowk and Sadar Bazaar fabric markets, reducing raw material lead time to same-day pickup. The district hosts 2500 garment factories concentrated in Greater Noida, Ecotech Extn, and Sector 62-63 areas with established supply chains for trims and components. Buyer proximity includes Delhi-NCR retail chains, national e-commerce platforms headquartered in Gurgaon 15 km away, and direct access to international freight forwarders via Delhi airports.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Gautam Buddh Nagar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Ecotech Extension or Sector 62 Greater Noida on Monday morning, walk 3-4 factory gates, ask yard owners about sample costs and MOQs. Call Greater Noida Association of Apparel Manufacturers (contact via District MSME office). Attend Greater Noida Textile Haat (if running). Register free Udyam account same week at udyamregistration.gov.in — zero cost, 10 minutes.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fabric from Chandni Chowk Delhi (Bhagirath Palace area) or Noida City Centre local suppliers — first batch of 500 pieces costs ₹45,000-75,000 depending on quality (cotton vs poly-blends). Buttons, zips, threads from Shahpur Jat Delhi or local Noida suppliers (₹5,000-8,000 per 500 pieces). Payment is spot cash, delivery 2-3 days.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 1500-2000 sq ft unit in Ecotech Extn or Sector 62 Greater Noida (₹25,000-40,000/month) with basic cutting and stitching setup. OR partner with 2-3 local artisans/tailoring units on piece-rate (₹50-120 per garment sewn). First batch production cost (labor + overhead) = ₹35,000-55,000 for 500 pieces. Full setup with 4-6 sewing machines, pressing, packing = ₹4-6L.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online). Apply for Pradhan Mantri Credit Scheme for Powerloom Weavers or ODOP Finance Assistance Scheme via District MSME office for ₹5-10L collateral-free loan. Get IEC code from nearest DGFT office (₹0, online) if planning exports. No GI tag needed for startup. Apparel Training Center (run by District) can provide 1-2 month quality training for designs.
5
First Sale
First sale: List on Meesho or your own Instagram Shop with 10-15 designs, ₹500-1200 retail. Or cold-call 3-5 local retailers in Delhi NCR with ₹20,000 order (50 pieces). Expected first-month revenue ₹1-2L if you push D2C simultaneously (assume 20-30 pieces/week Meesho + 1 small wholesale account).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month, hire 1 operations person (₹15,000/month), 2-3 additional tailors. Exhibit at Delhi Trade Fair, Pragati Maidan (Jan-Feb), or reach out to Flipkart/Myntra seller onboarding. Join apparel export directory via DGFT. Upgrade to ISO 9001 or GOTS if targeting premium export (costs ₹80,000-1.5L but justifies ₹2000+ FOB pricing).
