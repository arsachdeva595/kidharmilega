Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Gautam Buddh Nagar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Gautam Buddh Nagar's apparel clusters in Noida City Centre and Sector 58 to understand production standards. Study competitor offerings on Meesho, Myntra, and Amazon. Attend IITF Fashion Week or India Fashion Forum to network with manufacturers and understand market trends. Spend 2-3 weeks shadowing existing garment units.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fabric from Okhla and NOIDA fabric suppliers (₹40-80/meter for cotton blends). Connect with local dye houses in Noida for bulk orders. Initial raw material batch of 500 units (~₹2-3L) sufficient. Negotiate 30-day payment terms with established suppliers. Join NFIDC (National Fibre Development Corporation) supplier list.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with registered unit in NOIDA SEZ or rent 2000-3000 sqft space in Sector 62 industrial area (₹15-25K/month). Hire 10-15 skilled tailors through NIFT Noida placements or local labor contractors. Invest in 5-8 basic sewing machines, pressing equipment. Alternatively, outsource production to established units while managing design and quality control.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME within 2 days online (Ministry of MSME portal). Apply for IEC code from DGFT (₹0 cost, 3-5 days processing). Register brand with Trademarks office (₹4500-9000). Obtain GST registration (0% cost, GST rate 5% for garments). Design logo and branding guidelines; allocate ₹50K for branding assets.
5
First Sale
Launch on Meesho in Week 1 (0% commission for first 100 orders). Simultaneously create WhatsApp Business catalog and B2B inquiry on IndiaMART. Approach 5-10 local boutiques in Delhi/NCR with product samples and 35-40% wholesale discount. Run Instagram Reels targeting trendy designs; allocate ₹10K/month for initial paid social. Attend GIFBAG Mumbai (garment trade show) or Delhi Fashion Festival for bulk orders.
6
Scale
After ₹5L revenue, diversify into seasonal collections (summer, winter, festive). Invest in digital marketing (₹30K/month). Expand distribution to MBOs and national retail chains through wholesale agents. Launch subscription/capsule wardrobe model on D2C site. Explore B2B corporate contracts with HR procurement platforms. Plan second production facility or increase capacity by 40-50% by Month 12.
