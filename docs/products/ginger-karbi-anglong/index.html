Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Karbi Anglong ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Karbi Anglong ginger clusters in Sadipur, Bokajan, and Diphu areas; meet established farmer cooperatives like Karbi Anglong Spice Growers Association; study GI certification benefits and export protocols at Ministry of Agriculture regional office in Guwahati.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source seed ginger from certified nurseries in Karbi Anglong district (₹80,000-1.2L for first 5 acres); bulk organic manure from local suppliers; connect with 3-5 farmer collectives to secure steady supply at ₹30-45/kg.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established farmers in Sadipur cluster for contract farming on 2-3 acre plots; invest in basic processing equipment (drying units, grinding mill) costing ₹2-4L; establish small processing unit under FSSAI guidelines.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME portal) as agricultural producer; obtain IEC code from DGFT for export eligibility; apply for GI certification usage rights through district agriculture office; register brand name with IP office; get FSSAI registration for value-added products.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon, Flipkart, Jio Mart with GI-certified product listings; approach regional spice traders in Chennai and Kolkata markets; register on ITC e-Choupal platform; contact pharmaceutical bulk buyers through TradeKey and Global Sources; start B2B outreach to food manufacturers.
6
Scale
Expand cultivation area to 10-15 acres through farmer partnerships; develop 3-4 value-added product lines (ginger candies, supplements, organic powder); establish export logistics with APEDA-registered agents; open retail presence in Tier 1 cities; build direct-to-consumer brand through social media and e-commerce advertising.

up to ₹25L subsidy for food processing and agricultural business; 25% subsidy for general category.
🏛
PM Matsya Sampada Yojana Agriculture Component
₹10-20L for value addition infrastructure in agricultural products.
🏚
Assam Agriculture Directorate Subsidy Scheme
50% cost subsidy for agricultural processing units in ODOP clusters.
🏚
Assam Agro-Business Development Scheme
₹5-10L loan at 2% interest subsidy for export-oriented agricultural enterprises.
