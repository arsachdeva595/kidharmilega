Handicrafts - Horn Bone ki
opportunity, Sambhal mein.

Sambhal district in northwest Uttar Pradesh sits in India's cattle-rearing belt with direct supply access to buffalo and cattle horns — no animals are killed specifically for raw material, making it zero-waste. Sarai Tarin cluster concentrates 25,000 artisans producing beer mugs, decorative items, spoons, and jewelry with GI certification awarded July 2022. Products sell wholesale at ₹900-4000 range, with export markets in America, Europe, and Gulf generating ₹200 crore annually.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sambhal ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sarai Tarin cluster this week — call DC Sambhal handicrafts office (0571-2550000) for cluster contacts, walk through 3-4 artisan units, speak to 2 established retailers about their margins and first orders. Cost: ₹0. Time: 1 day.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw horns from local Sambhal suppliers or Sarai Tarin middlemen at ₹150-400/kg depending on grade. First batch: ₹30-50K gets 30-50kg mixed horns. Connect via cluster artisans or Sambhal traders association. Lead time: 2-3 weeks.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Partner with 2-3 Sarai Tarin artisans on 60-70% revenue share (no upfront cost). Option B: Rent 150-200 sq ft workshop in Sarai Tarin or Sambhal town at ₹3-5K/month + ₹1-2L for basic carving tools, polishing equipment. Train 1-2 local craftspeople at ₹8-12K/month. Total 6-month production cost: ₹3-8L.
4
Brand & Register
Day 1: Register free Udyam MSME on udyamregistration.gov.in (15 min). Day 5: File GI registration application with Sambhal DC (cost ₹500, timeline 8-10 weeks already certified for this product). Day 10: Apply for IEC code via DGFT portal or customs broker (₹500-1000, 10 days). Get ISO/handmade certification (₹10-30K optional, adds export credibility). Total: ₹1-3L for all formal registrations.
5
First Sale
First sale platform: Amazon Handmade or Etsy (list 20-30 products, cost ₹0). First order likely ₹8-15K from gift/decor retailer via Instagram DM or WhatsApp. Expected Month 1 revenue: ₹40-80K across 3-5 orders if marketed properly. Use Sarai Tarin contacts to get 2-3 retail inquiries immediately.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: Hire 1 production manager (₹15K/month), expand to 4-6 artisans, invest ₹2-3L in better tools. Start export outreach via trade shows (India International Trade Fair Delhi, International Handicraft Fair Delhi in Feb). Apply for PM Vishwakarma loan scheme (₹0-3L) to scale artisan workforce. Set up B2B sales team for hotel/resort chains. Build email list of international wholesalers (US, EU importers). Consider physical retail presence in Delhi Khan Market or Jaipur by Month 18.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
up to ₹3 lakh interest-free loan for artisans and tool kits, 1% interest if used for training
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25% subsidy on project cost up to ₹10L for manufacturing units, max ₹2.5L subsidy
🏚
UP ODOP Scheme
CM Yogi funded ₹10 crore Common Facility Centre in Sambhal, free access to carving machines, polishing units, design studio for registered artisans
🏚
UP Craft Loans Scheme
subsidized credit up to ₹25L at 4% interest for registered handicraft businesses through SIDBI
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
