Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Nicobars ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Port Blair cooperative societies (free visit — Andaman Coconut Producers Cooperative Society), call 3-4 local exporters listed in Chamber of Commerce to understand pricing and supply chain, register on Udyam portal (free, 15 mins), attend Port Blair haat/market to see local volumes and buyer types
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from village-level copra makers and coconut farmers directly in Nancowry/Katchall areas (60% cheaper than middlemen). First batch of 500kg copra costs ₹18,000-22,000. Or contract with existing coir mills in Port Blair (Andaman Coir Industries zone) for ready-made oil. Farmer networks operate through local cooperative unions.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 1,000 sq ft unit in Port Blair (₹3,000-5,000/month) or partner with licensed artisans/micro-units (zero rent, profit-share model at 15-20%). Cold-pressed virgin coconut oil setup: ₹80,000-2,00,000 (expeller machine + filtration). Or start with copra-to-oil outsourcing at ₹8-12 per kg (no equipment needed).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online at udyamregistration.gov.in — 10 mins). Apply for GI registration under Ministry of Agriculture if targeting premium market (₹15,000 filing + 18-24 months). Apply IEC from DGFT Chennai (₹0 cost, online, 2 weeks) if exporting. Get FSSAI license (₹5,000-12,000, 30 days if food item) or APEDA certification for coconut products.
5
First Sale
List on Amazon/Flipkart via Accelerator program (8-10% commission), target health stores via IndiaMART first-order at ₹15,000-25,000 (bulk oil), or contact local FMCG distributors in Tamil Nadu/Kerala. First wholesale order typically ₹50,000-1,00,000. Expected first-month revenue: ₹40,000-80,000 from 1-2 wholesale clients.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 2-3 production staff (₹8,000/month each), set up a second mini unit, certify for organic/cold-pressed labels (₹30,000 one-time), exhibit at APEDA export fairs in Delhi/Mumbai, hire a logistics partner (3PL) to handle bulk export orders, partner with 5-10 FMCG distributors across India.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25% subsidy for manufacturing (max ₹10L subsidy) if processing coconut into value-added products
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹20L cluster development fund for coir-based artisans and small units
🏚
Andaman and Nicobar Islands MSME Support Scheme
15% interest subsidy on bank loans up to ₹25L for agro-based manufacturing
🏚
A&N Islands Export Promotion Scheme
10% subsidy on freight costs for products shipped via seaport to international destinations
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Yes, high demand. Copra exports to Sri Lanka (₹35-40/kg), Bangladesh (₹38-42/kg), Malaysia (₹45-50/kg). Virgin coconut oil to Middle East, EU at ₹850-1,200/MT FOB. Process: APEDA registration + DGFT IEC + phytosanitary certificate (₹2,000). First export order takes 8-12 weeks.
