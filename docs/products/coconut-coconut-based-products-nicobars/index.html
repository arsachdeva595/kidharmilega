Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Nicobars ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Port Blair and Nancowry markets to study existing coconut oil processors and handicraft clusters. Connect with Nicobar Cooperative Society and local plantation owners. Research competing brands on Amazon, Flipkart, and identify premium positioning gaps. Study export documentation via APEDA portal.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source coconuts directly from local plantation farmers in Nancowry and Car Nicobar at ₹25-35 per nut. Establish bulk purchase agreements for consistent supply. First batch of 500 kg coconuts costs approximately ₹15,000-18,000. Connect with existing copra makers for supplementary supply.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing small-scale coconut oil extraction units in Port Blair or establish micro-processing unit with 5-10 L capacity cold-pressed oil equipment (₹200,000-400,000). Engage local women artisans for handicraft production through cooperative model. Register as FSSAI-certified food business for oil production.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) classification online. Obtain IEC code from DGFT (Director General of Foreign Trade) for export potential. Get FSSAI license for food products (₹4,000-6,000). Register trademark with IP India. Create GMP-certified branding emphasizing 'Authentic Nicobar Island' heritage.
5
First Sale
Launch on Flipkart, Amazon, and Meesho with premium pricing (₹450-550 for 500ml coconut oil). Create Instagram and Facebook brand presence with island production videos. Approach organic retail chains (BigBasket, Blinkit). Register on Indmart B2B platform for bulk orders. Start with 50 units monthly and track reviews aggressively.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand product line to coconut milk powder and coconut flour. Establish direct B2B relationships with 5-7 cosmetics and food companies. Apply for SFURTI cluster development support. Set up second processing unit in Car Nicobar. Target 500+ units monthly and ₹15-20 L monthly revenue by month 12.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma
up to ₹3 L loan for traditional artisans engaged in coconut handicraft production at 5% interest subsidy
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 L subsidy (15-35%) for setting up coconut processing units in rural areas
🏚
Andaman and Nicobar Islands Coconut Development Board Subsidy
40% capital subsidy for coconut processing equipment and training (max ₹10 L)
🏚
Island Agriculture Development Fund
concessional loans at 4% for ODOP product entrepreneurs with ₹50 L available per cluster
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Marketing — Zero Budget Se Shuru Karo
  • Create Instagram Reels showing traditional coconut harvesting and handicraft-making processes on Nicobar beaches. Partner with 10-15 micro-influencers (50K-200K followers) in organic/wellness niche. Use hashtags #NicobarCoconut #IslandOrganic #CoconutCraft. Target 2-3 Reels weekly with UGC-style content.
  • Launch 'Adopt a Coconut Tree' CSR campaign offering customers monthly shipments while funding local farmer education. Create email marketing to organic product enthusiasts emphasizing island origin story, sustainability, and women artisan support. Offer referral incentives (₹200 discount on third purchase).
  • Approach 15-20 premium ayurveda and wellness centers in metros (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore) for bulk retail partnerships. Create WhatsApp group for B2B buyers offering 15-20% wholesale discounts. Participate in India International Trade Fair (IITF) and Organic Food Expo for visibility. Leverage PM Vishwakarma badge in marketing.
