Orange ki
opportunity, Kamle mein.

Kamle district (1,659 km², population 16,892) produces Mandarin Orange — the oldest cultivated major fruit of Arunachal Pradesh — across 9 geographically dispersed growing clusters spanning altitudes of 1,200-1,800m. Nine clusters including Wakro-Lohit, Roing-Dambuk-Lower Dibang Valley, and Pangin supply raw material year-round with peak harvest November-February; smallholder farmers use organic indigenous pest control, yielding 200-300 fruits per tree per year. Buyer markets are 1,200km away (Delhi, Bangalore via air/NH), or export-accessible via land to Bangladesh, Nepal, Bhutan within 400-800km.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kamle ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call 2-3 orange farmers in Wakro-Lohit cluster (contact via Kamle DC office) to understand yield, seasonal gaps, pricing. Visit Pangin or Mebo haat on a Saturday during Nov-Jan to observe buyer volume and price movement. Register free Udyam profile online. Zero cost, this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from farmer cooperatives in Roing-Dambuk (Lower Dibang Valley cluster) or direct farmers in Wakro-Lohit. First batch: 2-3 tons (₹24,000-36,000 at ₹12-15/kg farm gate). Expect 15-20% grade loss for processing/juice. Lock in 3-5 farmers for consistent supply Nov-Feb; negotiate advance payment terms.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent 500 sq.ft. commercial unit in Roing or Pangin (₹8,000-12,000/month), install 2 cold storage units + juice extraction equipment (₹2.5-4L capex). Option B: Partner with existing orchard owner for on-farm processing (lower capex ₹80K-1.5L, share revenue 60-70%). Start with 500kg/day capacity. Hire 3-4 seasonal workers (₹300-400/day).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam within 1 week (free online, 10 mins). Apply for GI certification holder status to Ministry of Agriculture via state DC office (₹5,000-8,000, 8-12 weeks). Register business name with local tehsil. If exporting: apply for IEC code via DGFT portal (₹500, 5 business days). Total compliance cost ₹6,000-10,000, timeline 12 weeks.
5
First Sale
Upload product listings on JioMart/Amazon Fresh with GI tag visual (₹0 seller fee, 5% commission). Target first order: 100kg minimum from institutional buyer (contact hospital bulk procurement). Expected first-month revenue ₹60K-90K gross (assumes 300-500kg sales at ₹120-180/kg depending on channel). First D2C order via Blinkit typically 20-50kg at ₹280/kg.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire supply chain manager (₹25K/month), add second processing unit. Exhibit at Arunachal Pradesh Horticultural Society annual fair (July), Agritech expos in Delhi. Build farmer WhatsApp group (50+ farmers). Launch juice concentrate line (₹30L additional capex). Export via agri-exporter networks (contact Arunachal Chamber of Commerce). Target 50-60% export mix by month 18.
