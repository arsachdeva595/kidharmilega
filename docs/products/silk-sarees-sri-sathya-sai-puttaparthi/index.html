Silk Sarees ki
opportunity, Sri Sathya Sai (Puttaparthi) mein.

Sri Sathya Sai district in Rayalaseema has 56,110 acres of mulberry land with 29,097 sericulture farmers supplying cocoons to two major weaving clusters: Hindupur and Kadiri. Cocoon sourcing happens via regulated markets in Puttaparthi and Hindupur; finished silk sarees travel 400km north to Delhi exporters or 200km east to Chennai ports for international shipment. The district's 26,242.6 MT annual cocoon output (3,619.6 MT raw silk) ensures year-round raw material access for saree production.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sri Sathya Sai (Puttaparthi) ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Hindupur cluster (40km from Puttaparthi) and walk the Silk Market Road on a Saturday morning — talk to 3-4 active saree weavers, ask current cocoon prices and 15-day turnaround feasibility. Call Kadiri Sericulture Co-operative (Ph available at district Collectorate website). Register free Udyam (https://udyamregistration.gov.in) while you're planning. Zero cost.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw silk from Hindupur Cocoon Market (regulated, transparent pricing) or direct from Kadiri cluster cooperative — first batch 30-40kg raw silk @ ₹2,800-3,200/kg = ₹84,000-1,28,000. Dyeing subcontractor in Hindupur charges ₹400-600 per saree for process. Total first-batch cost (materials + dyeing): ₹1,50,000-1,80,000 for 50 sarees.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent 800 sq ft workspace in Hindupur cluster (₹8,000-12,000/month) + hire 2 weavers on contract (₹15,000/month each) — setup = ₹1,50,000 initial + ₹40,000/month opex. Option B: Partner with existing Kadiri weaver group (revenue split 60-40, zero rent) — lower capex, slower control. Recommend Option B for first 500 sarees, then graduate to Option A.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (free, 15 mins). Open bank account under business name (₹0-500). Get IEC code from nearest Commerce Ministry office in Puttaparthi (₹0, online, 5 days). File GST registration (₹0 online, get 18% input credit on silk costs). Optional: Apply for GI registration support via Silk Samagra scheme (₹30,000-50,000 subsidy for collective branding).
5
First Sale
Day 1: Upload 10 SKU (color/design variants) to Meesho and Flipkart Seller Central (free). Target price ₹9,500-12,000 per saree. Expected first month: 5-12 orders = ₹50,000-1,44,000 gross revenue if 55% margins. First wholesale buyer: contact bridal store owners in Hyderabad via Instagram DMs (sample cost ₹2,500 per piece, min order 10 pcs).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1 full-time operations manager (₹18,000/month), scale Hindupur workspace to 1,200 sq ft (₹15,000/month), add 2 more weavers. Launch export via Shopify (₹29/month). Exhibit at IITF Delhi (Dec, ₹45,000 booth fee), Handloom Expo Hyderabad (free for ODOP), wedding expos in metros. Apply for PMEGP term loan (₹25L max at 5% subsidy) if ready to scale factory model.
