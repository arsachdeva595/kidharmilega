Sohrai Painting ki
opportunity, Hazaribagh mein.

Hazaribagh district in south-central Jharkhand sits 220km south of Patna and 400km northwest of Kolkata, making it accessible to metro buyers via NH-2 and rail. The Isko village cluster in Barkagaon block concentrates 60-80 active Sohrai artisan households with direct access to foraged natural pigments (red, black, yellow, white earth) from local forests and merchants. Buyer markets include Jaipur, Delhi, Bengaluru, and Goa (via courier/wholesalers), plus exports to USA and Germany through ethnic craft portals.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Hazaribagh ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Isko village in Barkagaon block (nearest railhead: Hazaribagh Junction, 45km away) and meet 3-4 Sohrai painter households. Call 2-3 local pigment merchants in Hazaribagh town to understand sourcing costs (₹50-200/kg). Attend Sohrai festival celebration in October-November or January to observe production and sales (free entry). Register on Udyam (free, online at udyamregistration.gov.in) to get your 12-digit UDYAM ID.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source natural pigments directly from Hazaribagh district merchants or forest foragers; first batch of 20 units of hand-painted products costs ₹8000-15000 (raw materials ₹3000-5000, artisan labor ₹5000-10000 for 10-15 days). Supplier area: Isko village Barkagaon block and Hazaribagh town weekly market (Thursdays). Build a pre-agreed purchase agreement with 2-3 artisan households at ₹300-600/unit wholesale price.
3
Produce / Partner
Start with artisan partnership model (no factory overhead): offer artisans ₹500-1000/unit for finished hand-painted products; you handle design input, materials supply, packaging, sales. Alternatively, rent a 500 sq ft workshop in Hazaribagh town at ₹3000-5000/month if you plan in-house production. For 50 units/month output, artisan partnership is more capital-efficient (₹0-50K). For 200+ units/month, workshop + 3-4 trained painters needed (₹2-3L capex + ₹30K monthly wages).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 10 mins online). Apply for GI-tagged Sohrai Painting certification via Ministry of Handicraft (DC Handicraft) — verify authenticity with Isko cluster, cost ₹5-10K, 3-6 months processing. Register IEC code (Importer-Exporter Code) online at ices.gov.in if exporting (₹0-5K, 2 weeks). Register business name at Registrar of Companies (Ranchi office, ₹2-5K). Get trademark for your brand name at ipindiaonline.gov.in (₹4500 + agent fee ₹3-5K if using agent). Total timeline: 4-6 months, ₹15-30K.
5
First Sale
First sale: post 5-8 product photos on Instagram, create Etsy shop free, list products at ₹600-2000/unit. Send 10-15 cold emails to boutique hotel chains (Jaipur, Udaipur, Goa) and home decor retailers (Fab India, TheArtisanBox) with wholesale pricing (₹250-800/unit). Expected first-month revenue: ₹20-40K from 10-20 units sold. First bulk order likely from D2C (Etsy/Instagram) or a local retailer in Jaipur within 4-8 weeks.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (120-150 units/month): hire a full-time operations person (₹15K/month) to manage artisan payments and quality; move to a 1000 sq ft workshop (₹5-8K/month) and train 3-4 additional painters; expand product range to include wall murals, custom commissions, and seasonal limited editions; launch paid Instagram Ads (₹500-1000/day) and attend 2-3 trade shows/year (Delhi Craft, Jaipur Fine Art, Bengaluru Home Decor). Build distributor relationships in metros; target B2B institutional sales (hotels, offices) which yield ₹50-100K per project.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
up to ₹15000 interest-free loan for artisan-linked businesses, 18% subsidy on first loan tranche if registered as artisan cooperative
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25% capital subsidy (max ₹10L) for manufacturing/handloom enterprises, 35% for SC/ST founders; loan up to ₹25L
🏚
Jharkhand MSME Support Scheme
5% interest subsidy on bank loans up to ₹10L for 3 years, free skill training for workers (60 hours)
🏚
Jharkhand Handicraft Development Scheme
up to ₹1L subsidy for artisan collectives, access to state craft fairs (Jamshedpur Craft Fair, Ranchi Haat Bazaar) with stall fee waived
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
