opportunity, Bulandshahar mein.

Bulandshahar's Khurja cluster, 60km from Delhi on NH-119, contains 350 pottery units producing earthenware, stoneware, and porcelain from local clay deposits of the Yamuna belt. The cluster supplies Delhi-NCR's ₹1000+ Cr home decor and institutional buyer market, plus 40-50% of India's ceramic tableware exports. Raw material sourcing is 2-3km away, labor cost is 25-30% below national average, and buyer footfall to Khurja showrooms happens daily from Metro cities.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bulandshahar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Khurja pottery cluster (60km from Delhi, 2 hrs by road on NH-119) — walk through 5-10 showrooms on Main Bazar and Industrial Road, call 3-4 raw material suppliers (clay, feldspar), attend a weekend pottery haat at Khurja Nagar marketplace. Cost: ₹500 travel + tea. Do this this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Raw clay sourced from Yamuna belt suppliers near Khurja (list available at District Industries Center) — first batch 100kg @ ₹150-200/kg = ₹15-20k. Glaze and feldspar from registered suppliers in Khurja Industrial Road at ₹80-120/kg. Quality check: buy 5kg sample first, test in a kiln before bulk order.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (₹2-3L): Rent a 200-300 sqft pottery shed in Khurja cluster (₹8-12k/month), buy a used electric kiln (₹80-120k), purchase basic pottery wheels and tools (₹40-50k). Option B (₹50-150k): Partner with 2-3 artisans in Khurja cluster on revenue-share model (40% artisan, 60% your brand), they produce, you handle design + sales + branding. Option B is lower-risk.
4
Brand & Register
Register as Udyam (free, 15 mins online at udyamregistration.gov.in). File GI certification application with District Industries Center (₹5-8k legal cost, 4-6 weeks, Bulandshahar already has GI approval at district level — you inherit this). Open IEC code at DGFT (₹2k agent fee, 5-7 days) if exporting. Register trademark with IPO (₹4-6k, 8-10 months) — not urgent first year.
5
First Sale
First sale: upload 20-30 product photos to Meesho seller portal (free, 48 hrs setup), set prices 40-50% above cost. First order likely from Meesho after 2-3 weeks (expect ₹5-15k). Simultaneously, cold-DM 10 cafes/boutiques in Delhi offering 10% wholesale discount on ₹25k+ bulk orders. Parallel: email 20 overseas importers on Alibaba with MOQ 100 units. First-month realistic revenue: ₹30-50k if all channels combined.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month (150-200 units/month): hire 1 full-time designer (₹20-25k/month) for custom orders, add 1 logistics/packing person (₹12-15k/month), move to 800+ sqft shared kiln facility in Khurja. Exhibit at India Geospatial Forum (Delhi, Feb), Summer Trends Show (Delhi, April), and Gift & Homeware Fair (Delhi, June) — each costs ₹15-25k booth fee. Launch wholesale program: appoint 2-3 Tier-1 city distributors. At ₹25L/month: own small kiln, hire 2-3 artisans directly, build your own Shopify store.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
MUDRA Loan (Margin Money Scheme for Artisans)
up to ₹10L unsecured, 0% margin money first 2 years if registered, approx ₹1-2L subsidy component for craft sector
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Program)
25% subsidy on ₹25-50L projects (up to ₹6.25L), plus ₹10k credit guarantee coverage — requires DIC nomination
🏚
Ambedkar Hastshilp Vikas Yojana (UP)
₹50k-2L subsidy for artisan skill upgrades, tool purchase, design training — apply via District Industries Center Bulandshahar
🏚
Rajiv Gandhi Shilpi Swasthya Bima Yojana (UP)
free health insurance for registered artisans + 5 dependents, ₹30k annual premium covered by govt
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Frequently Asked Questions

Seedhe sawaal, seedhe jawab

Minimum ₹1.5-2L agar artisan partnership model chalao (tum design + sales handle karo, artisan production). Standalone pottery shed + kiln + tools ke liye ₹2.5-3.5L. MUDRA loan se ₹10L tak zero-margin financing mil sakta hai agar DIC se nomination le lo.
Experience zaroori nahi, but design eye zaroori hai. Agar khud design nahi kar sakte, ₹15-25k/month ka designer hire karo. Production samajhne ke liye 2-3 din Khurja cluster ke artisans ke paas baithke seekh lo — sab openly sikhate hain.
MUDRA Loan ₹10L tak, Ambedkar Hastshilp Vikas Yojana se ₹50k-2L tool/training subsidy, PMEGP se 25% subsidy on ₹25-50L projects, Rajiv Gandhi health bima. Sabke liye District Industries Center Bulandshahar se registration zaroori. GI certification Bulandshahar ke naam pe already approved hai — tum use kar sakte ho apne products par.
Meesho + Instagram Shop upload karo, 2-3 hafte mein first orders aayenge. Parallel: Delhi/NCR ke 10-15 cafes, homeware boutiques ko direct email/visit. Export? Alibaba pe account banaio, importers khud aate hain. First 2 mahine mein ₹30-50k revenue realistic hai agar sabhi channels chalao.
Bilkul. Khurja ke 350 units mein se 150+ export karte hain. IEC code lelo (₹2k, 5 days), Alibaba/TradeKey pe list karo, USA, Germany, UK, Australia importers ready hain. GST exemption bhi hai EPCG scheme ke under. First export order 500-1000 pcs ke around hoga, ₹2-5L revenue ke around. 4-6 weeks production time samajh lo.
Honest: artisan partnership model risk low hai kyunki equipment aur stock zyada nahi hota. Standalone pottery unit mein kiln, shed rental, inventory mein ₹2L+ stuck ho sakta hai agar sales flop ho. Lekin Khurja mein artisan + wholesale + export ye 3 channels hain — sabka flop hona unlikely. Worst case: raw materials aur finished goods ko Khurja ke 50+ existing traders ko sell kar sakte ho at 20-30% loss.
