Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Zunheboto ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Nagaland Agricultural Department in Kohima, connect with existing soybean farmers in Zunheboto block, study ICRISAT soybean varieties suited for Northeast India, attend agricultural fairs at Dimapur market
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified soybean seeds from ICRISAT or State Seed Corporation (₹40-50K for 100kg quality seeds), organic compost from local suppliers in Kohima (₹8-10K per ton), first batch cultivation cost ₹1.5-2 lakhs for 5 acres
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 10-15 farming families in Zunheboto through contract farming model, establish collection center for aggregation, secure mini processing unit license from district administration for value-added products (soy milk, flour)
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam on udyamregistration.gov.in as agricultural producer, apply for IEC code from nearest DGFT office in Nagaland for export, register brand name with state government, obtain organic certification from APEDA if targeting premium segment
5
First Sale
List on IndiaFarming.com and AgriMarket B2B platforms, contact soy product manufacturers in Guwahati and Bangalore, supply to organic retail chains (Naturals, BigBasket Organic), launch WhatsApp catalog for institutional canteen buyers in metro cities
6
Scale
Build processing facility for value-added products (₹20-30L investment), establish supply contracts with 5+ regional food companies, expand cultivation to 50+ farmer network, develop branded packaged soy products for D2C Amazon/Flipkart, explore government support for FPO formation
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

PM Formalisation of Micro food Processing Enterprises (PMFME)
up to ₹10L subsidy for soybean processing unit setup
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L loan with 35% subsidy for agricultural business
🏚
Nagaland Agricultural Credit Linked Capital Subsidy Scheme
25-33% subsidy on farm machinery and processing equipment
🏚
State Horticulture & Agriculture Department Value-Addition Support
subsidy for cold storage and processing infrastructure for pulses/soybean
