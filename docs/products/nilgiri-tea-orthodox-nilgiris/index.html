The region's unique terroir produces teas with a characteristic muscatel flavor that global buyers seek. Multi-generational tea-growing expertise and established tea estates provide ready infrastructure and knowledge base.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Nilgiris ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Coonoor and Ooty tea clusters; meet estate managers at Nilgiri Tea Association (NTA); attend tea auctions at Coonoor Tea Auction Centre; study processing at partners like McLeod Russel or Kanan Devan Hills.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fresh tea leaves from contract farmers or lease estate land in Nilgiri hills (₹8-12L for 5-10 acres); establish supply agreements with licensed tea gardens; first batch of 500kg processed tea costs ₹4-6L.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with licensed tea processing units in Coonoor (40+ units available) or set up own unit with FSAI license; hire trained workers from local tea workforce; implement standard orthodox processing (withering 16-20 hours, rolling, fermentation, drying).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Micro/Small Enterprise) with ₹0 cost; apply for IEC code from DGFT (₹500); obtain FSSAI license (₹2000); trademark brand name (₹4500-9000); GI certification already exists — use on packaging.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon.in (FBA), Flipkart, BigBasket Premium; create WhatsApp Business catalog; partner with 5-10 specialty tea retailers in metros; reach out to corporate gifting platforms (GiftNilgiris, Artisan Box); start Instagram/LinkedIn with estate stories and tasting notes.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to international B2B (UK, Germany buyers); launch 3-4 flavor blends; establish subscription model (monthly tea box ₹999-1499); open pop-up tea bars in Delhi/Bangalore; apply for PMEGP grant for scaling production.

PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy for tea processing unit setup in hilly regions
🏛
PM Matsya Sampada Yojana (extension for tea/spices)
infrastructure grants for quality improvement and certification
🏚
Tamil Nadu MSME Promotion Scheme
5% interest subsidy on loans up to ₹50L for food/beverage processing
🏚
Tamil Nadu Agricultural Processing Cluster Development Scheme
infrastructure support for processing units in Nilgiri cluster
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
