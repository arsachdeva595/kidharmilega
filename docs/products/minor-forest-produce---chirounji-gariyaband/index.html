Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Gariyaband ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Gariyaband district headquarters and meet NTPC's Forest Produce Cooperative at Chhindgarh cluster; attend chirounji collection workshops during Oct-Nov harvest season; study pricing at Raipur wholesale market (Rajnandgaon APMC).
2
Source Raw Materials
Connect with forest-dependent tribes and Gariyaband Van Samiti (forest committee) for raw chirounji collection; negotiate ₹80-120 per kg for raw seeds; plan first batch of 500 kg (cost: ₹50,000-60,000); arrange drying yard space (rent ₹3,000-5,000/month).
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing chirounji processors in Gariyaband town or Raipur (Dhobighat area); use semi-automated cleaning and grading machines (₹2-4 Lakhs capital); or establish small processing unit with 2-3 workers; standardize size and quality per buyer specs.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSE certificate online (₹0 cost); apply for IEC code via ICEGATE portal for export readiness; register trademark at IP Office (₹4,500); get FSSAI food license (₹5,000-10,000); obtain state forest clearance from Gariyaband Divisional Forest Officer.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh & Flipkart Supermart with premium packaging (₹8-12/unit packaging cost); target food blogger reviews; approach Mumbai-based confectionery wholesalers (Prabhat Market); register with NaturoPathic stores and health food chains; email 50 premium dessert manufacturers in NCR with samples.
6
Scale
After 6 months, hire a supply-chain manager; negotiate direct supplier agreements with 3-4 major confectionery brands; establish export partnerships through APEDA-registered trading houses; expand to roasted/flavored variants; invest in cold-storage facility (₹10-15 Lakhs) for year-round supply.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 Lakhs grant for micro-enterprise in food processing sector with 25% beneficiary contribution.
🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
support for food processing artisans and tribal forest-product workers with upskilling and working capital up to ₹15,000.
🏚
Chhattisgarh Van Dhan Vikas Yojana
supports forest-produce aggregation with equipment grants and skill training for tribals; applicable for chirounji collection & processing.
🏚
Chhattisgarh ODOP Development Scheme
direct fund allocation for packaging, branding, export promotion, and skill development specific to ODOP products.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
