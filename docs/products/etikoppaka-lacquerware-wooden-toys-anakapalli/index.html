Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Anakapalli ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Etikoppaka village cluster (30 min from Anakapalli town). Meet 3–4 artisans in their workshops along the main bazaar. Call Vishakhapatnam Chamber of Commerce or the District Industries Centre (+91-891-2770055) for artisan contact list. Attend the weekly Sunday craft haat at Anakapalli town square. Zero cost, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw cashew wood offcuts directly from Etikoppaka village artisans or cashew processing units near Paderu. First batch: 50–100 units unfinished wood costs ₹8,000–15,000. Natural organic dyes (indigo, turmeric, henna-based) available at Anakapalli spice market or online from Ahmedabad suppliers at ₹2,000–5,000 for starter palette. Total first-batch material cost: ₹15,000–25,000.
3
Produce / Partner
Start with artisan partnership model: engage 2–3 Etikoppaka artisans on ₹500–1,500 per unit commission (10–20 unit batches). Alternatively, rent a 300–500 sq ft workshop in Etikoppaka village at ₹3,000–6,000/month and learn basics from resident artisans over 4–6 weeks. Setup cost: ₹30,000–80,000 (rent deposit + basic tools + dye vats).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 10 min). Apply for GI-tagged product certification via District Industries Centre (₹0–5,000 facilitation, 8–12 weeks). If exporting: obtain IEC from DGFT (₹0, takes 3–5 business days online). Get trademark for brand name filed at IP office (₹9,000–15,000). Total compliance cost: ₹15,000–25,000, timeline 10–14 weeks.
5
First Sale
First sale: list 20–30 SKUs on Amazon Handmade or Etsy (free listing, 15% commission). Target first order: corporate gifting buyer or gift shop chain ordering 50–100 mixed units. Expected first-month revenue: ₹60,000–120,000 (wholesale) or ₹80,000–150,000 (D2C at higher margin). First export shipment: connect with Vishakhapatnam-based STPC or export council; MOQ typically 200 units, 30-day production, ₹120,000–200,000 FOB value.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (wholesale + D2C mix): hire 1 full-time operations manager and engage 5–8 artisans on fixed commission. Exhibit at Delhi Craft Bazaar (biannual), Bangalore India International Centre, and Frankfurt Ambiente Fair (annual, Sept). Build export network: hire 1 export-logistics freelancer (₹15,000–25,000/month) and register with 2–3 European/US gift distributors. Upgrade to own 1,000 sq ft unit near Etikoppaka for quality control and packaging.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma
Up to ₹15,000 tooling subsidy + ₹3,000 credit support for registered artisans; 5% interest subsidy on MUDRA loans up to ₹10L
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25–35% capital subsidy for manufacturing units, max ₹25L; MUDRA loan up to ₹10L at concessional rates
🏚
Andhra Pradesh HANDICRAFT SUBSIDY SCHEME
20% subsidy on machinery, raw materials, and working capital for registered artisans; GI-tagged product promotion fund up to ₹2L per unit
🏚
AP Artisan Credit Linkage Scheme
6% interest subvention on term loans up to ₹5L from banks; collateral-free loans for ODOP artisans
