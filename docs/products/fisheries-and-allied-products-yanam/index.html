opportunity, Yanam mein.

Yanam occupies 30 sq km on the Coringa River delta in Puducherry UT, directly adjacent to Andhra Pradesh's East Godavari district, one of India's highest-yield fishing zones. The district operates 26 landing centres across 38 marine fishing villages, ensuring year-round raw material supply at wholesale fish market rates (₹40-120/kg for fresh catch). Nearest bulk buyers are seafood exporters in Kakinada (30 km), Chennai processing units (150 km), and direct access to Puducherry port for containerized exports to Southeast Asia and EU.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Yanam ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Yanam fishing harbour (Coringa jetty) on a Tuesday morning, observe the daily fish auction, call 2-3 fishing cooperative leaders (ask Yanam fisheries office for names). Attend the bi-weekly fish market at Yanam town market. Register free Udyam account. Zero cost, 1 day.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source daily catch from Coringa landing centre or Yanam fisheries cooperative — ₹500-2000/day for 50-100 kg mixed fresh fish. First month raw material cost ₹35-40K. Build relationship with 1-2 fish traders at Yanam harbour who supply to export houses.
3
Produce / Partner
Start with hired processing labour in rented 400 sq ft unit (₹6-8K/month) near a landing centre, OR partner with existing 3-4 artisan women's SHGs in Yanam fishing villages who already do smoking/drying (split revenue 60-40). Equipment cost for smoking kiln + drying racks: ₹80-120K. Cold storage rental: ₹3-5K/month shared facility.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online). Apply for FSSAI food business licence (₹5-7K, 20 days). If exporting: get IEC code from DGFT (₹0, online). For GI registration — Yanam fisheries products not yet GI-tagged; apply through Puducherry commercial ministry if doing distinctive regional product (₹30-50K, 18-24 months). Get quality certifications: ISO 22000 (₹40-60K).
5
First Sale
First customers: contact Pondicherry hotel chains (Arya Nivas, Surguru) and order 20-30 kg frozen/smoked fish at ₹180/kg (wholesale). Parallel: list 5-10 product SKUs on Amazon/Flipkart as private label (₹300-400/unit). First-month revenue target: ₹40-60K (mix of 30% wholesale + 70% e-commerce). Use Meesho for tier-2/3 town distribution.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 2 full-time processing supervisors + 3 seasonal workers. Invest ₹3-5L in dedicated cold storage unit (not rented). Apply for Pradhan Mantri Matasya Sampada Yojana subsidy (30-50% capex for processing unit). Exhibit at Aqua India (Chennai, annual) and INDIAFRESH (Delhi). Approach institutional buyers in Mumbai, Delhi (hotels, restaurants, airline catering).
