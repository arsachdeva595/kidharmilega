Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Yanam ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Yanam fish markets (Yanam Fishing Harbor, Pulicat Lake area) and meet established exporters; study cold chain infrastructure at Puducherry Port and Ennore Port; connect with Puducherry Fisheries Department and MPEDA officials for data on processing volumes and export licenses.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source initial catch from registered fishermen cooperatives in Yanam or through fisheries auctions at the harbor (₹2-4L for first month of mixed species); establish relationships with 3-4 primary suppliers; negotiate 10-15% payment discount for bulk orders.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing FSSAI-certified processing units in Yanam or Puducherry town (many operate on co-processing model at ₹8-15/kg processing fee); or invest in own 500-1000 sqft unit with freezing, canning, and smoking equipment (₹25-40L capex); hire trained processing staff from local fishing communities.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, free); obtain FSSAI food processing license (₹5-10K, 15-20 days); apply for IEC from nearby Customs (DPE office in Puducherry, ₹500); register trademark with IP office; setup GSTNo and open dedicated business bank account.
5
First Sale
Launch on Udaan, TraceLink, and FreshToHome for B2B wholesale; create WhatsApp catalog and email outreach to 50+ HoReCa clients in Chennai/Bangalore; list on Flipkart, Amazon Fresh for processed products; attend Seafood Expos (VDFEX in Chennai, India Seafood Expo); approach regional exporters for supply contracts.
6
Scale
After first 6 months, expand to 2-3 more processing modalities (smoking, canning, vacuum-packed fresh); build direct export relationships with 5-10 importers in Southeast Asia; invest in cold storage facility (₹50-80L); hire dedicated compliance and export logistics manager; scale online D2C presence via Shopify + influencer partnerships.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L loan with 35% subsidy for manufacturing units in food processing sector
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹50L cluster-level investment for fisheries processing units with training and brand support
🏚
Puducherry NEDP (Non-Edible Fishery Development Programme)
subsidized cold storage, processing equipment, and export certification support
🏚
Puducherry Marine Fisheries Modernization Scheme
50% subsidy on acquisition of quality aquaculture and processing equipment, max ₹15L per unit
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
