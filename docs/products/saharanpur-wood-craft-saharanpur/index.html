Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Saharanpur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Saharanpur wood craft clusters in Kotwali and Mandi areas; tour established workshops like those in Nai Basti; attend Northern India Handicraft Expo; network with Saharanpur Woodcraft Association members to understand design trends, pricing, and export channels
2
Source Raw Materials
Source premium Sheesham wood from licensed timber merchants in Saharanpur (₹40-60k for quality logs). Alternatively, purchase pre-cut wood blocks from local suppliers. First batch of 20-25 finished pieces (~₹1.5-2L including raw materials and labor); establish relationships with artisans in Saharanpur for ongoing supply
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-3 skilled master craftsmen in Saharanpur's traditional clusters (Kotwali/Mandi areas) on piece-rate basis (₹2-8k per finished item depending on complexity). Alternatively, lease a small workshop (1000-2000 sqft) in artisan-dense areas for ₹8-12k/month and hire trained carvers
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) within 48 hours at udyamregistration.gov.in; apply for IEC code via Directorate General of Foreign Trade portal for export eligibility; register brand name and basic logo with IP office; obtain GI authentication certificate from Saharanpur District Administration for Geographical Indication compliance
5
First Sale
List on Etsy (15% commission), Jungle (12% commission), and Amazon Global within 2 months; create Instagram and Pinterest accounts showcasing craftsmanship process (reels driving 40% of initial traffic); attend international virtual trade fairs (Global Sources, Trade Key); contact interior design studios and luxury furniture retailers for B2B partnerships
6
Scale
After ₹30L revenue, hire dedicated export manager; establish showroom in Delhi NCR (tier-1 proximity for retail); partner with 5-8 artisans to scale production to 100+ pieces/month; apply for SFURTI cluster development benefits; target hospitality sector (Marriott, Taj, ITC) for bulk orders; explore wholesale to international distributors in UK/Germany
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma Scheme
₹15k initial grant + ₹5L collateral-free loan + GST exemption + brand building support for artisans and micro-enterprises
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Program)
up to ₹25L loan (₹10L for manufacturing) with 25% government subsidy for traditional craft entrepreneurs
🏚
Uttar Pradesh Handloom & Handicraft Development Society (UPHDS)
export promotion subsidies up to 15% and participation in international trade fairs
🏚
UP ODOP Scheme specific incentive
artisan training grants, cluster marketing funds, and quality certification support through District Administration
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
