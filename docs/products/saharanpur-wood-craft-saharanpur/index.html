Raw materials are available locally at wholesale rates, and established export corridors connect directly to 9 countries (USA, UK, UAE, Germany, France, Netherlands, Saudi Arabia, Canada, Australia) with ₹200 crore already flowing outbound annually, meaning buyer networks and shipping infrastructure are already mature.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Saharanpur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Zero-cost this week: (1) Visit Saharanpur town on any Tuesday-Thursday and walk through the main wood market near Nai Basti — call artisans directly (names from local chamber of commerce website), ask to tour their workshop for 30 mins, learn which designs sell fastest. (2) Attend the monthly artisan collective meet at DC Handicraft office, Saharanpur (check NIC website for dates) and listen to experienced exporters talk about margins and buyer demands. (3) Register free on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) using your Aadhar. Cost: ₹0, travel only.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from artisans in Saharanpur town (concentration in Nai Basti, Deoband, Behat tehsils — 20-30 min drive from city center). For first 2-3 months, order samples at ₹200-500 per piece (carved boxes, trays, frames) from 5-8 different artisans to test designs. Bulk first order: ₹2-5L gets you 50-100 mixed pieces (carved chairs, jaali panels, mirror frames). Negotiate 15-20% discount for bulk + repeat commitment. Supplier area: Saharanpur town, Deoband (famous for intricate jaali work).
3
Produce / Partner
Two options: (1) Rent a 1,000-1,500 sq.ft workshop in Saharanpur town (₹8,000-15,000/month) with basic hand tools and 1-2 in-house carvers + partner with 10-15 artisan units for overflow; ₹4-8L first-year investment. (2) Skip workshop, work as a pure aggregator-exporter — place orders with trusted artisans, pay per piece, handle only design, QC, packaging, and export. Requires ₹1.5-3L for initial inventory and QC setup. Start with option 2 if capital is limited.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 10 mins). Apply for Saharanpur Wood Carving GI certification co-branding via DC Handicraft office (₹0 for ODOP products, 2-3 week approval, gives you official GI label rights). If exporting, file IEC (Importer Exporter Code) at nearest DGFT office (Saharanpur or Meerut, ₹0 online, 2-3 days). File GST if turnover >₹40L (₹500-1,000 one-time setup, 1 week). Total: ₹1,500-2,000, 3-4 weeks.
5
First Sale
First sale: List 10-15 top designs on Etsy within 15 days of branding setup (free listing, 6.5% commission). Expect first order within 2-3 weeks, average order ₹4,000-8,000 (small tray or carved box). Simultaneously, email 20 interior design studios in Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore offering custom panels and furniture at wholesale rates (bulk order: 5-10 pieces, ₹50K-2L). First month realistic revenue: ₹80K-150K if you get 5-10 D2C orders + 1 bulk B2B inquiry.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month (12-18 months in): (1) Hire a designer (₹25K/month) to develop 3-4 new collections per quarter based on Instagram comments and export buyer feedback. (2) Allocate 2-3 artisan units as dedicated partner units (give them 60-70% higher advance for exclusive slot in their production schedule). (3) Hire a QC + packaging person (₹15K/month) stationed in Saharanpur. (4) Exhibit at India International Handicraft Fair (IIHF, Delhi, Feb) and International Gift Show (Mumbai, Jan) — ₹2-3L booth + travel, generates 5-10 institutional bulk orders. (5) Approach USA and UK importers directly via LinkedIn and TradeFair leads — target 2-3 container exports per year.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹40 lakh for cluster infrastructure, CFC equipment, design training; applied via DC Handicraft. Processing time: 4-6 months. Eligibility: registered unit in notified craft cluster.
🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹15,000 upfront credit + ₹1 lakh collateral-free PMEGP loan at 0% interest for first 3 years if you employ 1+ artisans. Processed via Jan Suvidha Kendra or MSME-DI office, 2-3 weeks.
🏚
UP State Handicraft Development Board
Exhibition subsidy of 50% cost (max ₹1 lakh) for exhibiting at national-level fairs (IIHF, Suraj Kund, etc.). Application: 2 months before event.
🏚
UP MSME Loan subsidy
3% interest subsidy on MUDRA and bank loans up to ₹5 lakh for first 5 years; process through nearest MSME-DI office (Saharanpur branch).
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
