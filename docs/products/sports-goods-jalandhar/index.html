Sports Goods ki
opportunity, Jalandhar mein.

Jalandhar district, located 148 km northwest of Chandigarh, 83.5 km southeast of Amritsar, and 61 km north of Ludhiana, hosts 300 distinct sports goods manufacturing centers producing cricket bats, footballs, hockey sticks, badminton rackets, and shuttlecocks. Raw materials (willow, rubber, leather, nylon) are sourced from established suppliers within the cluster, with over 4.5 million workers creating dense sub-supplier networks. Direct export channels to UK, USA, Germany, France, and Australia exist at factory level, allowing founders to skip distributor margins and reach institutional buyers.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Jalandhar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Jalandhar Sports Goods Cluster (Basti Ram Nagar or Ram Nagar industrial area) to see 10-15 manufacturing units in operation—free. Call 3-4 established suppliers to understand raw material costs and supplier lead times. Attend Punjab ODOP meet or local Chamber of Commerce event to meet existing manufacturers. Zero cost, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from established Jalandhar suppliers (willow from Himachal, rubber/cork from Chennai, leather from Kanpur). First-batch cost: ₹80K-2L depending on product type (cricket bats ₹80K-1.2L minimum per batch of 100 units; badminton rackets ₹60K-80K). Basti Ram Nagar and Ram Nagar are primary supplier concentration areas.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 500-800 sq ft unit in Jalandhar's industrial estate (₹5K-12K/month) or partner with 2-3 local artisans on piece-rate basis (no upfront rent). Equipment for assembly and finishing: ₹50K-3L depending on automation level. Full-service production start: ₹1.5L-2.5L.
4
Brand & Register
Register on Udyam (₹0, online in 1 day). Apply for MSME loan under PM Vishwakarma (₹1-10L at 5-7% interest). If exporting: register IEC code with DGFT (₹0-2K, 5-7 days). Consider GI registration filing for competitive advantage (₹3K-5K filing fee, 2-3 year timeline if approved).
5
First Sale
First sale: contact regional sports dealers via LinkedIn or local sports procurement WhatsApp groups. Post product on Amazon Seller Central and Flipkart. Target: first order ₹30K-1L within 60 days. Expected first-month revenue (post-launch): ₹40K-80K if 2-3 wholesale orders land.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 3-5 artisans, rent 1500 sq ft unit, invest ₹5L in semi-automated machinery, hire 1 export coordinator. Exhibit at Kashmir Sports Expo (Srinagar), IITF Delhi, and UK/US trade shows via APEDA subsidy. Build email list of 500+ institutional buyers; target repeat orders.
