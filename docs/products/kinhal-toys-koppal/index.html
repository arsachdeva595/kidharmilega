opportunity, Koppal mein.

Kinhal village in Koppal district, North Karnataka, sits 120 km south of Hampi heritage zone and 40 km from Belgaum-Gulbarga trunk road. Ponki Marra softwood sourced within 5-10 km radius of Kinhal. The 25-family artisan cluster produces 1200-1500 painted idols monthly; nearest buyer markets are Hubli toy traders (80 km), Pune gift exporters (450 km), and direct-to-consumer via craft platforms serving pan-India heritage segment.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Koppal ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kinhal village (20 km from Koppal town via NH 52) and meet 2-3 families directly — zero cost, call Koppal district handicraft office (0802-2325200) to confirm cluster timing. Attend Koppal Haat or Belgaum craft market. Register free Udyam portal account and search ODOP Koppal.
2
Source Raw Materials
Buy first raw materials (Ponki Marra wood + indigenous gum) directly from Kinhal village — ₹15,000-25,000 for 100 units worth. Alternatively, contact Koppal District Handicrafts Association. First batch takes 3-4 weeks with 2 artisans working part-time.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent 300-400 sqft workshop in Kinhal/Koppal town (₹3,000-5,000/month) for storage, packing, finishing. Option B: Partner with existing 2-3 artisan families on consignment (they handle carving/painting, you handle materials + sales). Total production setup: ₹80,000-150,000. Lead time: 20-30 days for 200 units.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (free, 48 hours). File GI-tag usage application with Koppal DC Handicraft office (₹5,000-10,000, 30 days). Get FSSAI if selling to institutions (₹5,000-15,000). If exporting: apply for IEC code at Belgaum DGFT office (₹0, online, 5 days). Obtain GST registration (₹0 online).
5
First Sale
Launch on Etsy, Amazon Handmade, Melalab simultaneously (free listings) — target hero product: Lakshmi/Krishna idol 6-inch size. First month goal: 10-15 orders averaging ₹1200 each = ₹12,000-18,000 revenue. Contact 3 Bangalore gift importers directly via WhatsApp with catalog. First wholesale order: 50-100 units at ₹600-750 each.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 1 full-time packer/account manager (₹12,000/month), add 2 more artisan families, invest ₹50,000 in photography/videography, exhibit at Craft Plus Fair (Delhi), Handmade Fair (Bangalore), source Ponki Marra directly from forest dept to reduce costs by 15%, create 5-SKU seasonal line (Diwali/Dussehra themed).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹15,000 toolkit subsidy + 5% collateral-free MUDRA loan up to ₹50L for artisan-led toy units
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹20L grant for cluster infrastructure (common workshop, kiln, drying shed) — apply via Koppal DC
🏚
Karnataka Handicraft Development Scheme
25% subsidy on fixed capital investment (max ₹5L) for registered artisans or artisan-partner entrepreneurs
🏚
Karnataka MSME Export Promotion Scheme
5% subsidy on airfreight costs for GI-tagged products exported outside India
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
