Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Koraput ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Koraput's cashew clusters around Jeypore, Nabarangpur, and Kotpad; connect with farmers at Koraput ATMA and cooperative societies like KAPCOL (Koraput Agricultural Products Cooperative) to understand cultivation cycles, yields (4-6 tons/hectare raw), and processing standards.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw cashew nuts directly from 150+ registered farmers in Koraput or via cooperatives at ₹60-70 per kg (seasonal: March-June). First batch of 5 tons costs approximately ₹3-3.5L; establish relationships with 3-4 suppliers for consistent supply.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing processing units in Jeypore industrial area for shelling, roasting, and grading (₹15-20 per kg processing cost), or lease a small unit (₹30-50K/month) and hire trained labor from local cooperatives; maintain FSSAI compliance and ISO standards.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) online, obtain Spice Board IEC for export, register brand with state IP office, get FSSAI Food License (₹5-10K), and implement GMP-certified packaging with origin labels and nutrition facts.
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon, Flipkart, and Blinkit with premium positioning; approach BigBasket and Nature's Basket for retail distribution; target direct exports via Fiverr B2B platform and trade portals (TradeKey, Alibaba); build WhatsApp bulk order list from corporate gifting and HORECA clients.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand packaging SKUs (200g, 500g, gift boxes), negotiate contracts with national e-grocery chains, establish direct export partnerships with 2-3 overseas distributors, and consider value-added product lines (butter, honey-roasted variants) to increase per-unit margins.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy at 35% for SC/ST entrepreneurs or 15% for general category in cashew processing units.
🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹15K toolkit + ₹1.32L collateral-free loan for small-scale agro-processors and artisans in cashew clusters.
🏚
Odisha Scheme for Agricultural Development (OSAD)
50% subsidy on machinery and equipment for cashew processing units; apply via district agriculture office.
🏚
Odisha Export Promotion Scheme
5% subsidy on export shipments for cashew and processed nuts; claim via state commerce department.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
