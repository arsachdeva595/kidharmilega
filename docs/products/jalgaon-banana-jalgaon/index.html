Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Jalgaon ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Jalgaon's primary banana clusters: Chopda taluka and Amalner taluka. Spend 5-7 days meeting 15-20 farmer cooperatives (Jalgaon Banana Growers Cooperative Society being the largest), participate in auctions at Jalgaon Agricultural Produce Market Committee (APMC), analyze competitor units in Nashik banana processing.
2
Source Raw Materials
Contact Jalgaon APMC directly for wholesale banana procurement; initial 10-ton batch costs ₹80,000-120,000. Identify 2-3 reliable farmer suppliers for consistent weekly supplies. Transport cost to your processing facility: ₹8,000-12,000 per ton via hired tempo/truck.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing banana processing units in Jalgaon (ITC-affiliated units or Banana Research Station contact) for contract manufacturing of chips/pulp, or lease a 500 sq ft facility near Erandol MIDC Industrial Area (₹15,000-25,000/month) and invest ₹2-3 lakhs in basic drying/pulping equipment. Hire 4-6 semi-skilled workers at ₹8,000-12,000/month each.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam via udyamregistration.gov.in (free, online, 5 minutes). Apply for IEC (Importer Exporter Code) through ECIS portal if exporting. Register with FSSAI for food business license (₹5,000-10,000) and get GI certification use approval from Controller of Patents & Designs. Trademark your brand name via iPleaders or local IP attorney (₹20,000-35,000).
5
First Sale
Week 1-2: Create D2C Shopify store and list on Amazon Vendor/Flipkart Seller accounts. Week 3-4: Reach out to 50+ modern trade chains (Big Basket, Nature's Basket, Spencer's) with samples. Simultaneously approach 20 juice/food companies for B2B supply contracts. Use Instagram (15 posts/week with farm-to-table narrative) and LinkedIn (targeting food importers) for initial 500 followers.
6
Scale
At 6-month mark with ₹50L+ revenue: Open 2nd processing facility in Amalner. Apply for APEDA recognition for direct export. Launch 3-4 SKU variants (organic, enzyme-treated, fortified). Hire a distributor in Delhi-NCR and Mumbai for modern trade penetration. Consider PE/VC fundraising for ₹1-2 Cr expansion to 5 states.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakhs subsidy (35% for general, 45% SC/ST) for food processing micro-enterprises
🏛
APEDA Export Promotion Scheme
50-100% reimbursement of quality certification, testing, and packaging costs for agricultural exports
🏚
Maharashtra Agri-Export Cluster Scheme
₹50-100 lakhs assistance for cold storage and processing infrastructure in clusters
🏚
Maharashtra Food Processing Cluster Development Scheme
subsidized land, credit guarantee, and capex assistance for agro-food processors
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
