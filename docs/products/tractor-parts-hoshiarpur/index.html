Hoshiarpur has a 50-year legacy of tractor parts manufacturing with a skilled workforce, established supply chains to major tractor OEMs (Mahindra, TAFE, John Deere), and proximity to Punjab's agricultural heartland. The district benefits from backward linkage with steel suppliers in nearby Himachal Pradesh and forward linkage with tractor assembly units across North India.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Hoshiarpur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Hoshiarpur's main industrial clusters (Tanda Road, Ghumar Mandi, Talwandi industrial area) and meet 5-10 existing tractor parts manufacturers; attend Punjab MSME DIC trade shows; study OEM specifications from Mahindra, TAFE, Sonalika websites; understand ISO/IS certification requirements for automotive suppliers
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from Ludhiana steel suppliers (Jindal Steel, Ratnamani Metals), cast iron vendors in Hoshiarpur itself, and bearing suppliers in Delhi NCR; negotiate bulk orders for first batch (₹20-30 L); establish credit terms with 30-45 day payment cycles typical in the sector
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing CNC machine shops in Hoshiarpur for contract manufacturing initially (avoids ₹50L+ capex on machinery); invest in quality testing equipment; obtain technical drawings from OEMs; hire experienced production supervisor familiar with automotive standards; consider Job work agreements with established manufacturers before scaling
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME classification (free, online); apply for IEC code from DGFT for export; obtain ISO 9001:2015 or TS 16949 certification (automotive quality standard); register brand name with IP India; set up GST registration (18% applicable); obtain BIS mark if manufacturing safety-critical components
5
First Sale
Approach tractor OEM procurement teams with sample parts and ISO certificates; list on industry platforms like IndiaMART, TradeKey; attend Agrimach Expo, Shandong Agricultural Machinery Expo; contact agricultural equipment distributors in Ludhiana, Bathinda, Chandigarh; pitch to farm equipment rental companies (Mahindra Sampo, CAP); build relationships with procurement managers at Sonalika, Farmtrac head offices in Punjab
6
Scale
After 6 months, invest in advanced CNC/VMC machinery; achieve ISO/TS certification; develop 2-3 new product variants; establish dedicated export team; participate in trade missions to USA, Germany, Middle East; expand workforce from 15 to 50+ skilled workers; establish quality control lab; develop proprietary products beyond OEM orders; consider strategic acquisition of smaller job shops

up to ₹25 L subsidy (25-35% for manufacturing units) for machinery and capex
🏛
MSME Technology Development Fund
up to ₹10 L for technology adoption, ISO certification, and quality upgradation
🏚
Punjab MSME Promotion Policy 2022
5-7% interest subvention on loans up to ₹1 Cr for manufacturing enterprises
🏚
Punjab GFSIDC Industrial Growth Fund
subsidized credit for machinery procurement at reduced interest rates
