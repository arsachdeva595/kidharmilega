Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Hnahthial ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Hnahthial district farming clusters around Serchhip and Lunglei; interview 10+ traditional chilli farmers; study GI certification database at Geographical Indications Registry; assess seasonal calendars and yield patterns.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified seeds from State Agriculture Department, Mizoram; establish direct relationships with 3-5 local farmers in Hnahthial; estimate first-batch cost ₹80,000-150,000 for 2-3 acres including labor and inputs.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established Mizo farming cooperatives (e.g., Hnahthial Farmer Producer Organization); hire local labor during harvest; invest in basic drying shed and processing unit; start with 2-3 acres and scale to 5-10 acres by year 2.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME online; apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) from DGFT; obtain GI certification documentation from Ministry of Agriculture; register brand name with trademark office; secure FSSAI license for processed products.
5
First Sale
Launch on Udyam registrar e-marketplace and IndiaTradeSupply; approach Spice Board export helpdesk for buyer connections; list on specialty platforms like The Spice House, Pepper Berry; attend International Spice Trade shows; partner with premium food retailers like Nature's Basket.
6
Scale
Expand cultivation area to 15-20 acres; establish processing and packaging unit with ₹25-40L investment; hire dedicated export manager; build B2B relationships with 20+ international buyers; develop 3-4 value-added product lines by month 18.
