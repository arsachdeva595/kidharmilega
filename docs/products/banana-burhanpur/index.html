opportunity, Burhanpur mein.

Burhanpur district in south Madhya Pradesh sits on the Deccan plateau, 350km east of Mumbai, with a 10-year track record of scaling from 16,000 hectares (1M tonnes, 2014) to 25,000 hectares (1.7M tonnes, 2024) at 70 tonnes/hectare productivity. Khaknar farmer-producer organisation anchors the cluster of 18,660 farmers; raw materials move through daily spot auctions (200 trucks/day peak season) and existing processing units (Iccha Chips 500 qt/yr, Bananify, Mangalam Kalpataru, Banhide). Buyer geography spans domestic (MP, Gujarat, UP, Rajasthan) and 6 export destinations (Iraq, Iran, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Turkey, Kazakhstan) with ~100 containers shipped monthly.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Burhanpur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Khaknar farmer-producer organisation office (free, 30min from Burhanpur city), call 5 fruit traders at Burhanpur mandi (ask about daily pricing, variety demand, contract terms). Register on Udyam portal (15min, free). Attend Thursday haat at Khaknar if active or call Iccha Chips founder (public contact) to understand processing economics.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fresh banana from Burhanpur spot market (200 trucks/day during June-Dec season, ₹8-11K/tonne delivered to your processing site). First batch: 5 tonnes = ₹50K. Pseudo stems free from same farmers post-harvest. Establish direct contact with 3-5 farmers in Khaknar cluster to secure weekly supply at contract rates (5-10% cheaper than mandi).
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (Low risk): Partner with existing processor (Bananify, Iccha Chips, Mangalam Kalpataru) for co-packing banana chips/powder at ₹6-8K per tonne processing cost, you supply fruit + branding. Option B (Medium risk): Rent 1,500-2,000 sq ft unit in Burhanpur industrial area (₹15-25K/month), buy small dehydrator (₹3-8L), hire 2-3 trained workers. First-year opex: ₹40-50L (rent 12m, equipment, labor, utilities, licenses).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 1 day). Apply for FSSAI food license (₹10-15K, 30 days). File IEC with DGFT (₹5K, 15 days) if targeting export. No GI registration needed (Burhanpur banana not GI-tagged). Trademark brand name at IPO (₹5-8K, 18 months). Total: ₹20-30K, 2-3 months.
5
First Sale
First sale: Contact 2-3 Burhanpur fruit wholesalers + Indore Modern Trade buyer (Bhopal road, 1hr distance), target ₹2-5 tonnes trial order in Month 2. Second: List on Amazon Fresh/Blinkit in 'banana chips' category, push 20-30 units/day via WhatsApp to Burhanpur town retailers. Expected first-month revenue: ₹1.5-2.5L (mix of wholesale + online).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: (1) Hire accounts + export compliance officer (₹40-60K/month). (2) Add 2-3 processing units or contract 2 co-packers to hit 30-40 tonne/month. (3) Exhibit at GulfFood (Dubai) or Exim India shows to lock export contracts. (4) Launch 2nd product line (banana powder, banana leather) using pseudo stem waste. (5) Apply for PMEGP/SFURTI subsidy for equipment expansion (₹25-40L subsidy available).
