Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sangrur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sangrur oil mill cluster in Dhuri and Sangrur city; meet 3-4 mill owners at Sangrur Oil Mills Association; understand seed sourcing from Amritsar/Bathinda agricultural markets; attend Punjab Food Processing Society meetings
2
Source Raw Materials
Source mustard seeds from Amritsar mandis (₹45-50/kg), sunflower from Bathinda (₹55-60/kg); establish relationships with 2-3 seed suppliers; budget ₹25-30L for first 100 tons of raw material inventory and processing
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing oil mills in Dhuri for contract manufacturing (10-15% of output cost) OR lease a 5,000 sq ft unit with cold-press machinery (₹18-22L capex); hire certified oil technician and QA personnel; implement FSSAI-compliant food safety protocols
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam-MSME, obtain IEC code for exports, secure FSSAI license (₹5,000-10,000), apply for Sangrur district ODOP support; create brand identity (logo, packaging design in ₹3-5L); register trademark with IP office
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon, Flipkart, Blinkit with ₹1L initial marketing spend; approach regional FMCG distributors (HT, Spencer's, local kirana networks); partner with 5-10 organic/health-food retailers in Delhi/Mumbai NCR; build email list via website
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand product line (infused oils, organic variants); establish own retail presence through kiosks in Tier-2 cities; scale exports through food trade associations; invest ₹50L in new cold-press equipment to increase capacity 3x
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy for manufacturing units with 25% beneficiary contribution
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹10L for cluster development and equipment in Sangrur oil mill zone
🏚
Punjab Startup Support Scheme
₹5-10L subsidy for food processing startups; priority registration under Punjab ODOP framework
🏚
Chhote Kaarigaron Vikas Nidhi Yojana
low-interest loans up to ₹10L for MSME food processors in rural Punjab districts
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
