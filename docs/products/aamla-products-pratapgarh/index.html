Aamla Products ki
opportunity, Pratapgarh mein.

Pratapgarh district in eastern UP spans 3,730 sq km with 32+ lakh population and 15,000+ hectares of aonla cultivation, making it India's aonla capital. Raw material sourcing costs are 30-40% lower here than other regions due to local glut during harvest (May-July), and processing clusters are concentrated in Pratapgarh town, Manpur, and Patti tehsils. Buyer access extends to all-India institutional markets (hotels, Ayurvedic chains, wellness retailers) and export routes via Delhi/Lucknow hubs, with GI certification opening premium positioning.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Pratapgarh ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Pratapgarh town weekly haat on Wednesday/Sunday, talk to 3-4 existing aonla processors about costs, yield, and supply chains. Call Pratapgarh ODOP cell (0519 2250 123) to understand subsidy eligibility. Register free on Udyam MSME portal. Zero cost, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fresh aonla from Manpur and Patti tehsil farmers May-July at ₹8-15/kg (2-3 tonnes for ₹20-45K first batch). Contract 2-3 farmer groups now to lock supply. Dried aonla costs ₹40-60/kg year-round from Pratapgarh spice wholesalers near railway station.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent 1,000 sq ft food-grade unit near Pratapgarh town (₹5-8K/month) or partner with 2-3 existing Home-Based micro-units (HBOs) paying 15-20% commission on output. Minimal capex: stainless steel vessels (₹25K), boiling setup (₹15K), packaging station (₹10K). First batch 200 kg costs ₹60-80K (raw material + labour).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam within 10 minutes online (free). Apply for GI mark benefit via UP MSME directorate (₹5K filing fee, govt covers 80%). Get FSSAI license (₹5-12K, 7-10 days if already-registered unit). If exporting, get IEC via APEDA or directorate (free, online, 5 days).
5
First Sale
First sale via direct B2B: contact 5-10 Ayurvedic retailers in Lucknow (150 km away) with 10kg samples — typical first order ₹15-25K. Parallel: list on JioMart Superfast in your zip code (zero listing fee), target ₹20-30K first month revenue. Use WhatsApp order groups to reach health shops in nearby towns.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 2-3 production staff (₹8-12K/month each), dedicate 1 person to exports paperwork, invest ₹2-3L in commercial kitchen certification. Exhibit at India International Trade Fair (IITF) Delhi Sept-Oct, Natural & Organic Expo (Delhi/Mumbai), and connect with HUL/ITC procurement teams via state ODOP cell.

Government Schemes

PMEGP (Pradhan Mantri Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L loan at 5% interest + 35% subsidy (women: 40%)
🏛
Credit Guarantee Trust Fund Scheme (CGTMSE)
zero-collateral ₹50L loans for food processing units via bank partners

One District One Product (ODOP) Scheme
includes ₹3-5L subsidy for certified units, GI support, and export promotion through UP MSME

Uttar Pradesh Udyam Nidhi
₹1L seed grant for registered MSME, reimbursement for equipment purchase up to 40%
