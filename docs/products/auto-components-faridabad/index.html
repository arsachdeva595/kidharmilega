Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Faridabad ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit EPIP Industrial Estate office (Faridabad, open Mon-Fri 9am-5pm) and request a list of 10 active auto component suppliers; call 3 of them and ask: 'What is your OEM client list?' and 'How did you land your first order?' Simultaneously, register free on Udyam (udyamregistration.gov.in) to see tax and compliance structure. Cost: ₹0.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials (steel, electronic controllers) from Faridabad's EPIP-zone suppliers (e.g., Bhatnir Steel, Advance Metal Works) or from Delhi's Wazirpur industrial area (15km away, 30min drive). First batch of 5,000-10,000 units: ₹12-18L depending on component type. Meet suppliers in person at EPIP Common Facility Centre or via chamber of commerce (Faridabad Industries Association, CII Haryana).
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 2,000-3,000 sqft unit in EPIP or FISE industrial zone (₹25-40k/month) with basic machinery (CNC lathe, milling machine, assembly line) — total machinery capex ₹40-80L if buying used/secondhand from cluster scrappers. Alternatively, partner with an existing component maker as a contract manufacturer (job work agreement, 0 capex, ₹8-12L first-batch cost). Year 1 production capacity: 20,000-50,000 units/month depending on setup scale.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (₹0, online, 10 min). File Business Registration with Faridabad MSME department (₹5,000, 15 days). Apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) with nearest DGFT office in Faridabad if planning exports (₹0, online, 5 days). Consider GI registration only after ₹2Cr+ revenue (cost ₹50-80k, 18-24 month process). Obtain ISO 9001:2015 certification from TÜV India or BSI (₹2-4L, 6-8 weeks) — required by most OEMs.
5
First Sale
First sale: Contact procurement officer at a local Tier-2 two-wheeler component supplier (find via Faridabad Industries Association) with a sample + test report. Target first PO: 2,000-5,000 units at ₹45-65/unit (depending on component). Expected first-month revenue if sold: ₹9-32.5L. Simultaneously, list on Udyam seller portal and apply to Amazon Business seller program (both free, approval 7-10 days).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: Hire a QA engineer (₹30-40k/month) and a sales coordinator (₹20-25k/month). Invest ₹10-15L in a second production line or CNC upgrade. At ₹30L/month: Pursue ISO 14001 (environmental certification, required by large OEMs, ₹3L cost). Exhibit at Auto Tier-1 Supplier Summit (Mumbai, annual) and Automechanika Delhi (biennial). Target Tier-1 OEM contracts via registered supplier databases (Maruti, Hero, Bajaj vendor portals).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
Production-Linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme for Automobile and Auto Components
4-6% incentive on incremental sales of auto components if registered and meeting capacity/sales thresholds. Administered by Department of Heavy Industry.
🏛
MUDRA Loan Scheme
collateral-free loans up to ₹10L for manufacturing MSMEs via nominated banks (HDFC, ICICI, SBI, Axis). 0% interest subsidy for first 12 months if turnover <₹2Cr.
🏚
Haryana Enterprise Promotion Policy 2021
10% capital subsidy on plant and machinery for new manufacturing units, cap ₹50L per unit. Apply via District Industries Centre, Faridabad.
🏚
Haryana MSME Export Promotion Scheme
₹1L subsidy for first international shipment + 50% reimbursement of freight/certification costs for MSME exporters from Haryana.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
