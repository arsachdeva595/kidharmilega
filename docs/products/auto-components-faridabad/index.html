Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Faridabad ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Faridabad's FSIDC industrial area Phase-I and Phase-II to network with existing component manufacturers. Attend AUTO EXPO New Delhi (biennial) to understand buyer demand. Study successful case studies of Kalyani Group, Motherson Sumi, and local SMEs. Join Faridabad Industries Association to access market intelligence and supplier databases.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw materials from established suppliers in Faridabad's steel market (sector-21 area) and Delhi's Okhla industrial estate. First batch cost: ₹25-40 lakhs for steel, fasteners, and consumables. Negotiate 30-45 day credit terms with suppliers after establishing business registration. Build relationships with 2-3 alternate suppliers to ensure supply continuity.
3
Produce / Partner
Acquire or lease 5,000-10,000 sq ft space in FSIDC Phase-I/II (₹15-25 per sq ft). Install CNC lathes, drilling machines, heat treatment furnace, and testing equipment (₹30-50 lakhs). Hire experienced production engineer and skilled machinists. Partner with local job-work centers for non-core processes. Start with contract manufacturing for established OEMs to build production expertise.
4
Brand & Register
Register under Udyam (MSME) portal within 48 hours. Apply for IEC code from DGFT (Directorate General of Foreign Trade) to enable exports. Register company under Companies Act/LLP. Obtain ISO 9001:2015, TS 16949 (automotive quality standard), and relevant IATF certifications. Create LinkedIn brand profile and technical website showcasing capabilities, certifications, and past projects.
5
First Sale
Pitch to OEM procurement teams directly with samples and quotations; attend automotive trade shows (Auto Expo, Components Expo). List on IndiaStack B2B, TradKey, and Global Sources for international inquiries. Cold contact tier-1 suppliers' quality teams with certifications. Leverage FSIDC supplier directory to reach auto manufacturers. Use LinkedIn outreach targeting procurement managers at auto companies.
6
Scale
After ₹1-2 Cr revenue, invest in advanced CNC/4-axis machines and robotic assembly. Pursue quality certifications (ISO 45001, IATF Level 3). Develop proprietary products or high-value niches (e.g., EV-specific components). Build export partnerships via trade bodies and government export promotion programs. Consider backward integration into raw material processing or forward integration into sub-assemblies.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakhs subsidy (25-35% of project cost) for MSMEs with own contribution
🏛
MUDRA Yojana
unsecured loans up to ₹10 lakhs for small manufacturing units with zero collateral
🏚
Haryana Enterprise Promotion Scheme
5% interest subsidy on term loans and capital subsidy up to ₹10 lakhs for new manufacturing units in notified sectors
🏚
Haryana Udyam Vikas Nidhi
grant-in-aid up to ₹5 lakhs for quality certification, patent filing, and technology upgradation in manufacturing
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
