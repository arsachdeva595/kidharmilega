Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kinnaur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kinnaur fruit producer clusters in Reckong Peo and Kalpa; connect with Kinnaur Horticulture Department and local ODOP associations; study GI certification requirements through Ministry of Food Processing office in Shimla
2
Source Raw Materials
Source Chulli fruit seeds directly from Kinnaur cooperative societies and local farmers during harvest (Sept-Oct); establish relationships with 3-4 farmer groups; budget ₹80,000-150,000 for 500kg first batch of seeds yielding ~100L oil
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing cold-press units in Kinnaur (Reckong Peo area) through contract manufacturing initially; alternatively, invest in small Italian-make cold-press equipment (₹600k-800k) and lease processing space in Kinnaur food park; hire trained oil extraction technician from neighboring regions
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME immediately; apply for IEC code via customs portal; get FSSAI license (cost ₹5k-12k) for food processing; obtain GI-certified tag documentation from district authorities; design packaging with heritage/mountain imagery and nutritional certifications (organic, non-GMO, cold-pressed)
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh, Blink, and Meesho with ₹2-3L initial inventory; create Instagram/YouTube content showing farm-to-bottle process and health benefits; approach Whole Foods, Organic shops, and wellness centers in Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore for B2B trials; list on specialized platforms like iShopIndian and Global Organic
6
Scale
After 6 months: expand product line (Chulli oil variants, infused oils); establish direct export partnerships with European distributors; open branded experience center in Kinnaur for agritourism; hire brand ambassador nutritionists; scale production to 500L+ monthly via multiple cooperative partnerships
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy/loan for food processing MSME with 35% subsidy cap
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
cluster development support for food processing units with infrastructure grants up to ₹5Cr per cluster
🏚
Himachal Pradesh ODOP Scheme
marketing support, certification assistance, and export promotion grants for GI-tagged products
🏚
HP Agri-Business Advancement & Modernization Scheme
subsidy for food processing equipment (30-50% of equipment cost up to ₹10L)
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
