Oil-Himachali Chulli ki
opportunity, Kinnaur mein.

Kinnaur district at 1,500–3,500m elevation in the western Himalayas is the exclusive natural habitat for wild apricot trees producing Himachali Chulli Oil, with June–August harvest yielding 15,000–18,000 MT annually across 8,000+ hectares. The Pooh–Kalpa cluster alone has 60+ registered and 100+ unregistered small producers extracting and fermenting chulli oil using 400-year-old phab fermentation techniques passed through families. Buyers span Delhi-NCR premium wellness chains (40km radius, 6-hour road access via NH5), European nutraceutical importers (8–12 week ocean freight), and Ayurveda institution networks across India.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kinnaur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Zero cost: Call 3 registered producers in Pooh (contact via Kinnaur District Industries Centre, 01786-222211) and visit Kalpa haat on market day (Saturdays) to source 5kg wild apricot, inspect phab fermentation, and watch oil extraction. Download ODOP official doc from Ministry of Food Processing website and GI registration details from IP India portal (free). Join WhatsApp group 'Kinnaur Food Processors' (ask at KDI Centre) — active with 180+ members sharing real market rates.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source wild apricots June–August direct from Pooh-Kalpa farmers (wholesale rate ₹80–120/kg for Grade B fruit, ₹180–220/kg for Grade A). First batch: 100kg fruit ≈ ₹12,000–18,000 yields 18–22L oil after cold-pressing (yield 18–22%). Partner with 2–3 registered producers (₹0 upfront, pay per batch) or lease a 400 sq ft unit in Pooh Industrial Area (₹6,000–8,000/month, available through KDI).
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (lean): Partner with 3 registered artisans in Pooh cluster (take 60% margin on their production, zero capex). Option B (own unit): Lease 400–600 sq ft in Pooh (₹8,000/month), install cold-press machine (₹1.2–1.8L), storage drums (₹25,000), labels and bottling (₹40,000), licenses (₹8,000). Total capex ₹1.8–2.3L. Produce 50–80L first month (July–August), scale to 200L/month post-season via apple-based extension oil.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, 15 mins online at udyamregistration.gov.in). Apply for GI authentication certificate from Kinnaur District Industries Centre (₹800, 10 days, required for legal use of 'Himachali Chulli Oil' label). If exporting: obtain IEC from DGFT Delhi (₹0, 7 days) and GST registration (₹0, online). Total cost ₹5,000–8,000, timeline 3–4 weeks.
5
First Sale
First sale: List on Blink (₹0 commission for first 30 days, free shipping promo), post 2–3 bottles at ₹999 retail, expect 8–12 orders in first week (₹8K–12K revenue). Simultaneously, call 5 metro organic retail chains directly with sample (250ml bottle, ₹150 cost). Target ₹25K–35K revenue month 1 (50–55L production basis).
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: Hire 1 production supervisor (₹18,000/month) and 1 logistics/sales coordinator (₹15,000/month). Expand cold-press capacity (add 2nd machine, ₹1.2L). Exhibit at Natural & Organic Products Expo India (Delhi, Feb–March) and Europe's Vitafoods (June) to acquire export orders. Launch apple-blend variant (apple + apricot oil, ₹80/L lower cost) for wholesale price compression. Set up minimum 500L standing orders from 3 institutional buyers by month 12.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25–35% subsidy on machinery and equipment, max ₹25L loan at 0% interest for first 3 years if SC/ST/Woman/Minority applicant, standard 10–25% subsidy for others; processing via state MSME DI
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
cluster-based support, up to ₹5L per unit for common facility centers, technology upgrades, packaging design; Kinnaur Chulli Oil cluster registered, applications via KDI
🏚
Himachal Pradesh GI Registration Support Scheme (2004 onwards)
covers 50% of registration fee (max ₹2,000), supports producers in any GI-registered craft/product to obtain state certification; administered via HP Handicrafts Department
🏚
HP ODOP Marketing Fund Scheme
₹1.5L–3L grant for registered ODOP producers for brand building, digital platform registration (Blink/Amazon), expo participation, packaging design; apply via District ODOP Nodal Officer, Kinnaur
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
