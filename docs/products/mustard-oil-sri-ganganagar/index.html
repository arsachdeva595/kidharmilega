Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sri Ganganagar ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sri Ganganagar's oil mill cluster (Amar Nagar, Jai Nagar areas) and shadow 3-4 producers for 2 weeks; interview farmers in Padampur, Ramnagar; attend Rajasthan Food Processing Association meetings to understand quality standards and export compliance
2
Source Raw Materials
Partner with mustard seed suppliers in Ganganagar's Mandi (wholesale market) or directly with farmers in Anupgarh taluk; source 10-20 MT of premium mustard seeds at ₹4,500-5,500/quintal for first batch (₹450K-550K investment); establish cold-press equipment supplier relationship with mills in Raisinghnagar
3
Produce / Partner
Lease existing oil mill capacity (₹50K-100K/month) in Sri Ganganagar with cold-press setup, OR partner with registered FSSAI-compliant producers on revenue-sharing model (60:40); ensure BIS/AGMARK certification for quality testing; produce first 500L batch for market testing
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam on udyamregistration.gov.in (free, online); apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) via DGFT portal for export readiness; obtain FSSAI license (₹5K-15K), register brand name with IP India; design packaging with nutritional labeling and traceability codes
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh, Flipkart Supermart with ₹50-100 listing promotion; pitch to BigBasket and Grofers directly with samples; approach 20-30 retail chains in Delhi/Chandigarh; contact foodie influencers (healthy eating segment) for product gifting; create Shopify store with focus on bulk B2B orders
6
Scale
After 6 months, establish distribution partnerships with 50+ modern retail outlets; scale D2C to ₹20L+/month through paid ads (Google, Instagram) targeting health keywords; explore organic certification (₹80K-150K) to command 30-40% premium; initiate APEDA export licensing for 3-5 target countries; develop private label contracts with 2-3 established FMCG brands
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (25% of project cost) for food processing micro-enterprises with 10% entrepreneur contribution
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹2.5L per artisan/producer for cluster-based food processing infrastructure and marketing support
🏚
Rajasthan Food Processing Policy 2019
5% interest subsidy on term loans, capital subsidy up to 25% for food processing units in notified clusters including Sri Ganganagar
🏚
Rajasthan ODOP Promotion Scheme
export subsidy of 5% on FOB value for ODOP products, marketing support fund up to ₹10L for registered entrepreneurs
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
