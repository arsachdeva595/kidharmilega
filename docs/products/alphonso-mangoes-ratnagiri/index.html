Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Ratnagiri ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Devgad or Ratnagiri town market on a Saturday morning (free, 2 hours). Call 3-4 mango exporters listed on APEDA directory to ask their April-June sourcing volumes and pricing. Attend the Ratnagiri Krishi Haat or agriculture department office to meet 5-6 direct growers. Zero cost.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source directly from 2-3 growers in Devgad taluke (Jambhuli, Shekhad villages, 15-25 km from Ratnagiri town). First 200-kg batch costs ₹18,000-24,000 (₹90-120 per kg ex-farm). Establish contracts via agriculture department liaison officers. Plan for April-May sourcing window.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 500 sq ft packing unit in Ratnagiri town MSME cluster for ₹8,000-12,000/month (includes basic grading, ripening chamber, cooling). OR partner with 1-2 existing packers who will grade/pack for ₹12-18 per unit commission. Setup includes ripening racks (₹25,000), weighing scales (₹8,000), labels/boxes (₹2,000-4,000 for 500 units).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (free, 15 mins). File GI-certified label application with district collectorate (₹5,000, 30 days). If exporting, apply for IEC from DGFT office in Pune or via eAadhaar (₹500, 7 days). Hire a CA for Udyam + IEC combo: ₹8,000-12,000.
5
First Sale
First sale: Contact BigBasket local supplier portal or register on Amazon Seller Central (₹500 setup). List 50 dozen boxes of A-grade (₹2,299 per dozen retail). Expected first month (April peak): 80-150 dozen sold = ₹1.84-3.45L gross, ₹1.10-2.07L net after commissions/logistics. First export order via APEDA trade directory: 500 kg container to UK importer at ₹3,500 FOB = ₹1.75L gross.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: Hire 1 full-time supply chain manager (₹25,000/month), 2 packers (₹12,000 each). Expand to 2-3 packing units. Secure 5+ grower contracts for exclusive supply. Exhibit at India International Trade Fair (IITF) Delhi in Nov, Bangalore Fruit Fest in June. Build WhatsApp B2B group for institutional buyers. Negotiate with logistics partner (DHL/FedEx) for export cost reduction.

Government Schemes

50% subsidy on agricultural infrastructure (cold storage, ripening chambers) up to ₹50 lakhs for MSME agribusinesses

PMEGP (Pradhan Mantri Employment Generation Programme)
25-35% subsidy on project cost (max ₹25 lakh) for food processing/agri-export startups

Maharashtra Agricultural Subsidy Scheme
₹2-5 lakh subsidy for GI product processing units with certification

Free training, market linkage, and MOU support for Ratnagiri Alphonso exporters registered under state scheme
