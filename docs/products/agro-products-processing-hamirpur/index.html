Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Hamirpur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Hamirpur's agro-processing clusters in Nahan and Balh areas; connect with existing producers like Himachal Organic Products Federation; attend APEDA workshops on food processing exports
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fruits from Hamirpur's fruit mandi (September-November peak season); establish contracts with 3-4 local orchardists; initial batch (1000 bottles) costs ₹25,000-40,000 including packaging
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing SFURTI-supported processing units in Nahan for outsourced production initially; alternatively, set up small kitchen unit (500-1000 bottles/day capacity) with FSSAI license and food safety training
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (Micro/Small), obtain FSSAI license (₹500-5000), apply for IEC code if targeting exports, trademark brand name at IP office, use Himachal GI association for authenticity messaging
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Fresh and Flipkart Supermart with focus on North India initially; create Instagram business account with behind-the-scenes orchard content; approach modern trade chains (Nature's Basket, Bio Basics) with samples
6
Scale
Expand to 5-6 product variants; secure APEDA recognition for exports; build relationships with international distributors; apply for PMEGP expansion grant; scale to 5000+ bottles/month capacity by month 12
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy for food processing units in aspirational districts
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
clustering support, infrastructure, and training for traditional food processors
🏚
HP State Food Processing Policy
10% capital subsidy for food processing units; exemption on VAT for 5 years
🏚
HP Udyam Suvidha Scheme
₹1 lakh startup support for registered MSMEs in agro-processing sector
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
