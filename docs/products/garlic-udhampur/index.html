Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Udhampur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Udhampur sabzi mandi on a Tuesday morning (free entry), speak to 3-4 garlic wholesalers, ask prices, harvest season, and farmer contacts. Call Udhampur NIC office (01996-245555) and ask for ODOP coordinator and local farm cooperative details. Zero cost, doable this week.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source from local farmers in Raipur and Tikri tehsils (contact Udhampur Agriculture Department) or lease 1-2 acres of farmland for ₹15,000-25,000/season. First-batch cost: 100kg seed at ₹100-120/kg = ₹10,000-12,000, plus labor ₹8,000-12,000 for one season.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Partner with 5-6 local farmers (zero land cost, ₹50,000-80,000 for seeds + input supply). Option B: Lease 1-2 acres (₹20,000/season), buy seeds and compost (₹25,000), hire labor (₹30,000 for 6 months). Option C: Buy processed garlic from wholesale mandis and package (₹2-3L working capital). Total range: ₹50,000-₹150,000 for first season.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online 5 min on udyam.gov.in). Open FSSAI license (₹7,500 for non-refrigerated foods, 30-45 days). Open IEC for export on IEC.gov.in if exporting (₹500-1,000, 10 days). Consider GI registration if targeting premium segment (consult Ministry of Agriculture, ₹5,000-15,000 process fee, no guarantee).
5
First Sale
List on JioMart wholesale portal (free listing) — target first order of 20kg at ₹2,000 total margin. Simultaneously approach 2-3 vegetable retailers in nearest Jammu/Pathankot market with samples (transport ₹500). First-month revenue target: ₹8,000-15,000 from mixed channels.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: hire 1-2 pickers and 1 quality checker (₹10,000/month each), rent cold storage unit (₹8,000/month), move to WhatsApp bulk ordering + Google Pay. Exhibit at Agritech and FMCG expos in Delhi (stall ₹25,000-40,000). Partner with food processor units to shift from raw to value-added (garlic powder, paste).
