Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Navsari ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Navsari's Mango Belt around Urvashi, Bilimora, and Chikhli talukas. Meet established mango farmers and join Navsari Agriculture Department workshops. Study market dynamics at Navsari Mandi and connect with Gujarat Agricultural Produce Market Committee (GAPMC). Allocate 3-4 weeks to understand pricing, varieties, and buyer networks.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source grafted Hafus saplings (₹150-250 per sapling) from Navsari's established nurseries like Mehta Nursery or government-certified centers in Bilimora. For initial 1-acre setup, budget ₹30,000-50,000 for 100-120 saplings plus ₹40,000 for soil preparation, organic matter, and micronutrient inputs. Connect with local input suppliers in Navsari City.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established farmers for contract farming (80/20 revenue share typical) or lease agricultural land 2-5 acres in Bilimora/Urvashi clusters. Invest in drip irrigation (₹2-3L for 1 acre) and join local Mango Growers Cooperative. Hire experienced farm managers from local labor pools. Arrange cold storage partnership with nearby cold chain operators (₹500-1000/ton for 3-4 month season).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME (online, free, 5 minutes). Obtain IEC code from nearest DGFT office (Surat — 40km away, ₹5,000-10,000 facilitation). File trademark with IP India for brand name. Get FPO (Farmer Producer Organization) certification for cooperative credibility. Secure FSSAI registration for value-added products if planned.
5
First Sale
Launch on BigBasket and Blinkit with 15-20 kg initial orders. Connect with export traders via Navsari Chamber of Commerce. Pitch to premium hotels and restaurants in Ahmedabad/Mumbai. Use WhatsApp Business API to manage B2B orders from regional wholesalers. Attend Agrotech exhibitions in Gujarat (Ahmedabad, Vadodara). Start email outreach to international fruit importers via LinkedIn.
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to 2-3 acres and add processing unit (pulp/juice) for off-season revenue. Build branded packaging and enter organized retail chains like Reliance Fresh. Pursue organic certification (₹50,000-70,000 one-time cost) to command 25-30% premium. Establish export quality certifications (GlobalGAP, organic) and explore EU/US markets. Invest in cold chain infrastructure to control entire supply chain.

PM-KISAN
₹6,000 annual direct income support for landholding farmers engaged in mango cultivation

PMEGP (Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh subsidy for agricultural value-added processing setup
🏚
Gujarat Krishi Vikas Yojana
₹2-5 lakh subsidy for high-tech mango farming (drip irrigation, micronutrient systems, cold storage linkage)
🏚
Gujarat Agri-Export Promotion Scheme
10% rebate on export logistics for registered FPOs exporting fresh/processed mango
