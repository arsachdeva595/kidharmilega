Kangra Tea ki
opportunity, Kangra mein.

Kangra Valley in Himachal Pradesh spans 1400 hectares of tea cultivation at 900-1400m altitude on Dhauladhar slopes, split between 625 hectares of large estates and 775 hectares of small growers in Kangra Valley and Palampur clusters. The district exports 9-10 lakh kg annually to EU, Australia, Central Asia, and Afghanistan, with GI recognition from European Union. Over 900 hectares lay inactive until recent mechanisation from 2014-15 revived 200-300 hectares back to pluckable state, providing significant expansion opportunity for new processors and exporters.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kangra ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Palampur or Kangra Valley tea clusters (free, 2-hour drive from Dharamshala) and meet 3-5 tea estate owners to understand cultivation cycles, land leasing terms, and current market prices. Call 2-3 existing tea processors (search 'Kangra tea exporter' on IndiaMART) to ask buyer feedback and export margins. Attend the monthly Dharamshala haat or contact Kangra District Agriculture Office for active farmer networks — zero cost.
2
Source Raw Materials
Lease or partner with existing tea estates in Kangra Valley or Palampur (10-50 hectares on share-cropping or lease basis — ₹1-2L/hectare/year typical). First-batch green leaf sourcing: ₹40-60/kg for fresh-plucked leaf (March-October peak season). Visit Palampur or Kangra town's agriculture input dealers for fertiliser, equipment, and processing support contacts. Negotiate long-term estate agreements to ensure consistent supply.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent 800-1200 sqft tea processing unit in Palampur or Kangra town (₹5-8K/month, shared equipment common). Option B: Partner with existing 1-2 hectare small grower who owns basic withering and rolling tables (50/50 revenue split). Setup cost: ₹3-8L for basic withering tables, rolling machines, drying beds, storage (gas dryer optional). Hire 2-4 seasonal workers during pluck season (March-October, ₹200-300/day). Processing: green leaf → withering (12-16h) → rolling (40-60min) → oxidation (2-4h) → drying (20-30min).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online at udyam.gov.in, 15 min). Apply for GI registration support via Ministry of Agriculture ODOP scheme (paperwork + ₹5-15K legal fees, 4-6 weeks). Obtain FSSAI food processing license (₹0-10K, 2-4 weeks). If exporting: Apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code, free, 3-5 days at nearest DGFT office) + Customs enlistment. Get organic or EU certification if targeting premium export (₹1-3L annual audit cost).
5
First Sale
First sale: Register on IndiaMART or Global Tea Auction platforms. Target: 50-100kg first order at ₹200-250/kg wholesale = ₹10-25K revenue. Post 3-4 photos + tasting notes on Instagram/YouTube (5-10 min daily content). Email 20 online tea retailers (Teabox, Chai Point, Indian Tea Company) with sample pack (₹1-2K) + pricing sheet. Expected first month: 200-400kg sales across channels = ₹40-100K revenue.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: Hire 1 full-time operations lead (₹20-30K/month). Invest ₹3-5L in lab equipment for quality testing (moisture, flavour consistency, microbial testing — EU export requirement). Exhibit at Heimexpo (Delhi), International Tea & Coffee Festival (Mumbai), or food expos in Bangalore/Hyderabad (₹2-5L booth cost). Build B2B relationships with HUL, Nestlé sourcing teams. Consider lease farming additional 20-30 hectares by month 12.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
25% subsidy on fixed assets up to ₹25L (₹6.25L subsidy for tea processing unit setup)
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
₹3-10L grant for common processing facilities if joining tea cluster cooperative, administered via MSME-DI
🏚
Himachal Pradesh Horticulture Department
₹50K-1.5L subsidy for tea plantation revival on abandoned land (200-300 hectares currently eligible)
🏚
Himachal Pradesh MSME Promotion Scheme
5% interest subsidy on MUDRA/bank loans up to ₹10L for processing/export enterprises
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
