King Chilli ki
opportunity, Leparada mein.

Leparada district in eastern Arunachal Pradesh sits at 1000-1500m elevation with cool, moist conditions ideal for Capsicum chinense cultivation. While Leparada itself has emerging production, the contiguous Nagaland King Chilli cluster (Dimapur, Kohima, Wokha districts) supplies seed stock, processing knowledge, and established buyer networks for exports and domestic pickle/seasoning companies. Raw chilli volumes: 800-1200 units daily across the region; dried chilli commands ₹200-400/kg wholesale, ₹500-800/kg retail, and export-grade can fetch ₹600-1000/kg FOB.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Leparada ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Call 5 King Chilli growers in Nagaland (Dimapur or Kohima district via local agriculture office) OR visit Leparada District Agriculture Office (free consultation on cultivation, variety availability). Attend a regional agricultural produce haat in Guwahati or Kohima if one is scheduled. Register free Udyam account online. Cost: ₹0, time: 1-2 weeks.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source fresh King Chilli seeds/saplings from Nagaland State Seed and Organic Certification Agency (NGSOCA), Kohima, or directly from farmers in Dimapur taluka (₹5-8/sapling, bulk discounts at 5000+ units). First-batch cost for 1000 saplings: ₹6000-8000. Alternatively, source fresh chillies directly from grower co-operatives at ₹150-180/kg (pre-season booking recommended). Supplier cluster: Dimapur Agricultural Market, Nagaland.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A: Rent a 600 sq ft food processing unit in Nagaland's nearest MSME cluster (₹8000-12000/month, includes drying racks and basic equipment). Option B: Partner with an existing artisan/farmer group doing drying and grinding (revenue share 60-40). For pickling: small kitchen setup with sterilization (stainless steel vessels, glass jars, labels). Cost range: ₹2-5L for rented unit + equipment; ₹30-50K if artisan partnership route. Drying time: 10-14 days. Processing capacity: 50-100 kg dried chilli/day in a small unit.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, instant). Apply for FSSAI Food Business License (₹500-2000, 10-15 days). Get GI usage approval from Nagaland GI registrar (existing since 2006, ₹0 if using on authorized product). Apply for IEC (Importer-Exporter Code) if exporting (₹0, online via DGFT portal, 1 day). Total non-product cost: ₹2500-5000. Timeline: 4-6 weeks.
5
First Sale
First sale: List on Amazon Seller Central or Flipkart Wholesale (free, 7 days to activate). Create 5 SKUs (100g, 250g, 500g dried whole chilli + powder variants). First wholesale order: Target local pickle makers in Guwahati or Dimapur (₹20-30K order size, net 15-30 payment terms). Expected first-month revenue: ₹40-80K (5-10 wholesale orders of 10-20 kg each + 50-100 D2C units). Gross profit first month: ₹12-30K.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue: Hire 2 full-time staff (drying supervisor, packaging) + 1 part-time accountant (₹30-40K/month payroll). Expand to 2000 sq ft production facility (₹25-35K/month rent). Launch co-packing contracts with 2-3 spice companies. Apply for PMEGP subsidy for capacity expansion (₹25-50L loan with 35% subsidy). Exhibit at IIFB (India International Food Bazaar), Natural and Organic Products Expo. Approach modern retail buyers (Azelis, ITC spices division). Explore capsaicin extraction as higher-margin ancillary. Build social proof on Instagram (recipe videos, farm stories) and YouTube (Shorts on heat-level content).

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
₹25-50L loan with 35% subsidy (General category) or 45% (SC/ST/OBC), for unit setup, working capital, equipment. 5% admin charges, 5-year repayment.
🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹15L collateral-free loan at 5% interest for artisans and MSME owners, includes toolkits and upskilling grants.
🏚
Arunachal Pradesh Organic Farming Scheme
₹50-100K subsidy per hectare for organic King Chilli cultivation if integrated with processing unit.
🏚
Arunachal Pradesh MSME Development Fund
₹10-20L concessional loans at 6% interest for food processing units with 2-year grace period.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
