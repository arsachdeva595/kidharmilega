Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Longleng ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Longleng ginger clusters around Phomching and Niyikhed villages; study cultivation at ICAR Regional Research Station, Nagaland; connect with existing ginger farmer collectives through District Agriculture Office, Longleng; analyze 3-4 competitor farms to understand yield (12-15 tons/hectare) and market linkages
2
Source Raw Materials
Source 500kg certified ginger seed rhizomes from ICAR Nagaland or approved nurseries (cost ₹50-60k); procure curing chambers/drying racks locally or from Guwahati suppliers (₹20-30k); first batch production cost approximately ₹80-100k for 0.5 hectare plot
3
Produce / Partner
Begin with 0.25-0.5 hectare cultivation using traditional Longleng methods; partner with 2-3 experienced local farmers for knowledge transfer; set up simple drying facility using bamboo platforms and shade; target harvest in December-January (9-10 months post-planting)
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam under Agriculture/Food Products category; apply for IEC code through DGFT for exports; obtain Food Safety License from FSSAI; brand as 'Longleng Premium Ginger' with GI reference and altitude certificate; trademark at Indian IP Office
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Pantry and Indmart for fresh ginger; sell dried/powdered ginger on Jio Mart Business and Udaan B2B platform targeting spice merchants; approach local Ayurvedic shops and naturopathy centers in Kohima and Dimapur; contact 5-10 pickle/sauce manufacturers for bulk supply agreements
6
Scale
After 6 months, expand to 1-2 hectares; develop 2-3 branded SKUs (ginger powder, ginger candy, dried slices); secure ISO 22000 certification; approach export consolidators in Guwahati for Southeast Asia shipments; partner with wellness e-commerce platforms (Nykaa, WOW, Ayurstate) for premium positioning
