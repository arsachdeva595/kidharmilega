Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Shimla ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Walk Lakkar Bazaar on any weekend — visit 5-10 shops without announcing yourself, ask owners how many years they've been selling, what wood types they use, typical weekly footfall. Call the Lakkar Bazaar shopkeepers association (local contact via Shimla Municipal Office) to attend next monthly meet — free. Register on Udyam portal (udyamregistration.gov.in) — takes 15 minutes, free, get your UDYAM number same day.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw walnut and cedar from timber merchants on Lakkar Bazaar itself or nearby Shimla forest depots (Chakkar Bazaar area, 2km away). Get quotes for 50-100kg walnut blocks at ₹400-600/kg, cedar at ₹300-450/kg. First batch cost: ₹40-60K for wood + ₹5-10K brass inlay materials. Lead time: 2-3 weeks from order.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a small workshop (2-3 room unit) in Lakkar Bazaar or adjacent Chaura Maidan area at ₹8-12K/month, or partner with 1-2 retiring craftsmen as piece-rate artisans (₹200-400/piece depending on complexity). Setup cost for 3-4 hand chisels, carving tools, lathe (second-hand): ₹60-100K. First 3 months: produce 100-150 pieces while learning market feedback.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online). Apply for Himachal Pradesh Micro Enterprise Scheme — ₹5L subsidy available for setup costs (contact DPIIT Shimla, 48-week process). GI registration for Shimla wooden crafts is under cluster name — verify status with Shimla DIC. If exporting, get IEC code from DGFT (₹500 fee, 5-7 days online). Trademark your brand name with IP India (₹4500, 18-month process).
5
First Sale
Launch on Amazon Handmade and Etsy simultaneously — upload 15-20 product photos, write detailed descriptions mentioning Himalayan walnut and deodar cedar. First order likely from Etsy (international tourists); expect ₹1500-2500 per carved figurine. Negotiate with 2-3 Delhi/Bangalore gift retailers for 20-30 piece initial orders at wholesale. Target first month: ₹80-120K revenue across channels.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month, hire 2-3 skilled carvers at ₹12-15K/month salary + piece rate bonus. Move to 400-500 sqft workshop with 2-3 workstations. Create 8-10 core designs (deity figurines, spice boxes, chessboards), batch-produce. Attend 2-3 craft export fairs annually (World Travel Mart London, Frankfurt Gift Fair, Heimtex Stuttgart). Build B2B relationships with bulk buyers — hotel chains, corporate gifting agencies. Launch own Shopify store for D2C margin improvement.
