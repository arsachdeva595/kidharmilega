Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Shimla ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Shimla tourism clusters — Kufri, Mashobra, Naldehra; study 5-10 established players (Wildflower Hall, Oberoi Wildflower, Woodstock School campus conversions); attend Shimla Tourism Forum quarterly meets; analyze 2024 visitor data from HP Tourism Board
2
Source Raw Materials
Identify property in central Shimla (near Mall Road or Ridge) or off-season locations; acquire adventure equipment from Decathlon/Quechua suppliers in Delhi; partner with local Shimla guides through District Tourism Office; budget ₹50-100L for initial property lease/license and ₹20-30L for equipment and staff training
3
Produce / Partner
Register as hospitality business with Municipal Corporation Shimla; obtain tourism license from HP Tourism Board; partner with 5-8 certified adventure guides (contact Adventure Tour Operators Association Himachal); tie-up with local artisans for experiential add-ons (cooking classes, craft workshops); launch soft operations in off-season (Sept-Nov) to test and refine
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSME at udyamregistration.gov.in; obtain IEC from nearest DGFT office (Chandigarh); register brand on Trademark India; get HP Tourism Department recognition; secure GST registration; obtain fire & safety clearance from local authority
5
First Sale
Launch on Airbnb, Booking.com, MakeMyTrip, EaseMyTrip with professional photography; create WhatsApp Business catalog and Facebook page with user testimonials; partner with travel agents on The Ridge (Shimla's main market) for commission-based bookings; launch email list via Mailchimp targeting previous Shimla visitors; offer 15-20% early-bird discount for first 50 bookings
6
Scale
After 6 months, add 2-3 satellite properties or experiential offerings (spa, wellness); build corporate packages and B2B tie-ups with Delhi/Mumbai companies; expand D2C channel via Google Ads targeting 'adventure holidays India' + 'Shimla hotels'; launch loyalty program; scale staff to 15-25 and reach ₹50-80L monthly revenue by month 18
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy for setting up new tourism enterprise with 25-35% grant component
🏛
MUDRA Loan Scheme
collateral-free loans up to ₹50L for hospitality business expansion at 8-10% interest
🏚
Himachal Pradesh Tourism Policy 2019
land lease incentives, GST deferment up to 5 years, property tax exemptions for registered tourism units
🏚
HP Skill Development Mission
free training for guides, hospitality staff, adventure instructors; supports 200+ certifications annually
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
