Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Alappuzha ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Alappuzha coir cluster (town center) this week — walk through 3-4 established units, call Coir Board Kerala office (free inquiry), attend monthly Alappuzha market haat to see product varieties and wholesale pricing. Zero cost, 2-3 hours investment.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source coconut husk from coir pith suppliers in Alappuzha town (₹8-12/kg in bulk). First batch: contact 2-3 artisan groups in Ambalapuzha or Thalikkulam areas (within 15 km); negotiate batch of 50-100 kg processed fiber at ₹1200-1800. Supplier contacts available through Alappuzha Coir Industry Association.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (Low capital): Partner with 2-3 established artisans, provide fiber, pay ₹80-120 per mat/piece, retain branding. Option B (Medium capital): Rent a 1200 sq ft unit in Alappuzha MSME cluster at ₹6000-8000/month, hire 3-4 artisans at ₹12-15K salary each. Initial setup: ₹2-3L covers machinery (beating frame, spinning wheel), first material batch, and 2 months rent.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online); apply for GI tag benefit certificate (₹0 cost, 4-6 weeks via Coir Board); if exporting, apply for IEC from DGFT (₹200, 10 days). Get trademark registered (₹9000-12000, 18 months). Total: ₹10-12K and 2-3 months.
5
First Sale
First sales: list on Amazon Handmade (0% commission for artisans, 10-15% for others) or Flipkart Artisan; target bulk corporate buyers via LinkedIn outreach to hotel procurement teams. First realistic order: 100-200 units (₹40-80K value) within 30 days of listing; first-month revenue ₹80-150K if wholesale, ₹150-250K if D2C.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 2 part-time designers for custom products, build relationships with 2-3 export agents (commission 8-12%), participate in India International Trade Fair and Kerala Crafts exhibitions. Upgrade to 2500 sq ft production space (₹12-15K rent), employ 6-8 artisans full-time, introduce tufted carpets and geo-textiles (higher margins). Invest ₹2-3L in branded packaging and website.
