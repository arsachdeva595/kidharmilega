Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Bongaigaon ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Bongaigaon Agricultural Department, engage with maize farmer collectives in Nalbari taluk, study market rates at Bongaigaon Mandi, attend Assam Agriculture Ministry seminars on maize value-addition.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified hybrid seeds from Assam Agricultural University or Syngenta dealers in Bongaigaon town; procure land (10-50 acres leasehold) from local farmers at ₹40,000-60,000/acre annually; first-batch fertilizer and pesticide cost ₹3-5 lakhs for 20 acres.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established maize farming collectives like Bongaigaon Krishak Samiti; contract farmers for bulk production; or lease farmland and hire local labor at ₹300-400/day; use local hired threshers for processing (cost ₹2,000-3,000 per ton).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam online (5 mins); apply for IEC from DGFT Guwahati office; brand name under FSSAI if adding value-added products; register as agricultural producer company under Indian Companies Act for better market access and subsidies.
5
First Sale
Begin wholesale supply to 5-10 regional mills (contact via Bongaigaon District Agriculture Office); list on AgriMarket platforms (NCDEX, Agribuzz); attend Assam Food & Agro Industries Expo (Guwahati); approach poultry feed manufacturers in adjacent districts via direct B2B outreach.
6
Scale
Invest in mechanized threshing and drying units (₹15-25 lakhs) after 6 months; develop packaged cornflour/snacks under own brand; establish contract farming with 50+ farmers; explore export to Bangladesh/Nepal through APEDA-registered traders; open collection centers in 2-3 villages.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM-KISAN
₹6,000 annual income support for farmer producers; leverages cooperative registration.
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakhs subsidy for agro-processing units with 25-35% margin assistance.
🏚
Assam Agribusiness & Rural Transformation Scheme (AARTS)
₹10-15 lakhs grant for agricultural processing setup in ODOP clusters.
🏚
Assam State Agricultural Marketing Board (SAMB) Subsidy
15-20% cost support for storage infrastructure and mandi registration in maize-producing districts.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
