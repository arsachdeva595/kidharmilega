opportunity, NTR (Vijaywada) mein.

Kondapalli village in NTR district is the historic epicenter of toy-making craft, with artisans possessing 400+ years of inherited knowledge. The region's access to cashew wood and proximity to Vijayawada's commercial networks makes it the natural production hub. The GI certification validates authenticity and commands premium positioning globally.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — NTR (Vijaywada) ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kondapalli village cluster (15km from Vijayawada); meet established artisans at Kondapalli Toy Cluster Association; study design catalogs at NTR District Handicraft Development Office; research 50+ active toy makers to understand production standards and pricing benchmarks.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source raw cashew wood from local suppliers in Vijayawada (approx ₹25,000-35,000 for first batch); procure acrylic paints from Chennai or online (₹5,000-8,000); buy brush sets and finishing materials from Hyderabad craft suppliers; total first-batch RM cost: ₹40,000-50,000.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner directly with 3-5 artisan families in Kondapalli on work-order basis (no employment, commission-based); establish small workshop space (300-500 sqft) in Kondapalli village or Vijayawada for design finalization and quality control; implement QC checklist for GI compliance.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) online within 24 hours; apply for IEC from Vijayawada DGFT office; file GI certification holder agreement with Kondapalli Artisan Collective; register trademark with IP office; create product photography portfolio and sustainability story narrative.
5
First Sale
Launch Flipkart and Amazon storefronts with lifestyle photography and heritage storytelling; approach 20-30 premium gift retailers in metros via email + samples; post 3x weekly on Instagram and Pinterest with behind-the-scenes artisan content; partner with 2-3 corporate gifting platforms (Giftmyntra, Pourri, The Artisan Box).
6
Scale
Expand to 8-10 artisan partnerships; launch subscription gift box (quarterly curated sets); enter European craft fairs (Craft + Design Fair, Homeland Berlin); build B2B wholesale via handicraft trade shows; open pop-up retail in Bangalore, Delhi, Mumbai malls; develop custom design service for high-ticket corporate orders.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM Vishwakarma
up to ₹15,000 toolkit grant + 5% collateral-free loan up to ₹1 Lakh for artisan-entrepreneurs in handicraft
🏛
PMEGP (Pradhan Mantri Employment Generation Program)
up to ₹25 Lakh subsidy (25% for general category) for setting up handicraft units
🏚
AP Handloom and Handicrafts Policy 2022
₹1 Cr cluster development fund for GI-certified products like Kondapalli toys; training and workspace support
🏚
Export Promotion Scheme AP
5-10% export subsidy for handicraft products with GI certification shipped internationally
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
