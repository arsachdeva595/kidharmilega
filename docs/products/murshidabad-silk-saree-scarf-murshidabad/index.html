Bombyx mori cocoons sourced locally and from West Bengal's sericulture belt reduce raw material lead time to 10-15 days. Export-ready infrastructure includes direct links to UK, USA, Singapore, Australia via e-commerce and B2B textile platforms.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Murshidabad ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Murshidabad town silk weaving cluster (Katra area, Google: Murshidabad Silk Weavers Cooperative) — no cost, spend a day watching looms, interview 2-3 weaver groups, call 3 cocoon suppliers using local numbers from District Handloom Office phone list. Attend monthly Silk Traders Meet at Murshidabad Chamber of Commerce (free, register online). Check Udyam portal (free registration) for existing silk saree businesses in district to understand license structure.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source Bombyx mori cocoons from Silk Mark certified local suppliers in Murshidabad or Nadia district — ₹350-450 per kg, first batch 50 kg costs ₹18,000-22,500, 5-7 day lead time. Buy natural dyes (madder, indigo, marigold) from Baharampur dye merchants (₹2,000-3,500 for starter kit, 20-30 color variants). Partner with 2-3 weaver groups in Katra or Basantapur area to outsource production initially — ₹1,500-2,500 per finished saree (45% of your target wholesale cost).
3
Produce / Partner
Start with artisan partnership model (no rent needed): provide pre-dyed silk yarn to 3-5 weavers, they produce pure silk sarees at ₹1,800-2,500 per piece (48-hour turnaround), you handle finishing, blocking/hand-painting. Rent a 300 sq ft workspace in Murshidabad town for ₹4,000-6,000/month if adding block-printing or hand-painting in-house. First batch: 50 sarees = ₹1L outflow (yarn + dyeing + weaving), total cost ₹1.25L with workspace setup, blocking tools, packaging.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME) online free, 10 minutes, no fees. Apply for GI certification for Murshidabad Silk Saree — ₹8,000-12,000 registration cost at DC office, 8-12 weeks processing (GI status not yet awarded, first-mover advantage). Register IEC (Import-Export Code) with DGFT online free if planning exports, 2-week approval. Get Silk Mark certification from Silk Mark Organization India — ₹1,500-2,000 per batch, 10-15 days, required for export credibility. Total branding cost: ₹10,000-15,000.
5
First Sale
First sale: list on Etsy (2-3% fees) and Instagram Shop simultaneously, photograph 10-15 sarees in home studio, write product descriptions citing Murshidabad heritage + Silk Mark, post 3x weekly on Instagram. Expected first month: 1-3 sales via Instagram DM or Etsy = ₹15,000-40,000 revenue. Approach 1 local ethnic wear boutique in your city with 5-piece sample collection, negotiate ₹60% MRP wholesale rate. Contact 1 wedding planner WhatsApp group to pre-launch custom services. Target: ₹50,000-80,000 first-month revenue, breakeven at month 9-10.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month revenue (15-20 sarees/week): hire 1 full-time designer/quality checker (₹18,000/month), negotiate better cocoon rates directly with 2-3 cocoon farms, expand weaver network to 8-10 artisans. Exhibit at India International Silk Fair (Feb-March, Delhi), IITF (Nov, Delhi), Handloom Expo (quarterly nationwide). Launch private-label B2B for 2-3 boutique chains, negotiate ₹3,500-5,500 per saree at 100+ piece orders. Partner with influencers in wedding/festive space (₹8,000-15,000 per collab). Start wholesale account on Amazon Handmade India and Craftsvilla. Total team at this scale: 3-4 people.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25 lakh loan at 5% interest for MSMEs, 25% govt subsidy for General category (Gen/SC/ST categories differ), apply through DIC Murshidabad
🏛
PM Vishwakarma
₹3 lakh collateral-free loan for traditional artisans, 0-15% interest per beneficiary category, free skill training included, launched Sept 2023, active for silk weavers
🏚
West Bengal Handloom Development Scheme
₹50,000-1 lakh subsidy for small looms or DFP (Decentralized Fibre Processing), 60% subsidy available, apply through DC Handloom office
🏚
West Bengal Weavers' Facilitation Fund
interest-free working capital loan up to ₹5 lakh for registered cooperative weavers, process through State Handloom Board, 8-week approval timeline
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
