opportunity, Sawai Madhopur mein.

Sawai Madhopur sits on abundant marble deposits from local quarries, with a centuries-old tradition of stone carving passed through generations of artisans. The proximity to quarries reduces raw material costs and enables high-quality marble sourcing, while the region's artistic heritage ensures superior craftsmanship in sculptures and decorative pieces.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Sawai Madhopur ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Banasthali and Khandar marble clusters in Sawai Madhopur; interview 5-8 established marble artisans and workshop owners; study marble grading and sourcing from local quarries near Mohanpur. Attend gift and home décor trade shows in Delhi and Mumbai.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source marble blocks from local quarries in Mohanpur and Banasthali areas (₹15,000–25,000 per ton for good-quality white/pink marble); establish relationships with 2-3 local suppliers; budget ₹3–5 lakhs for first-batch raw materials (10–15 tons). Purchase tools from Delhi-based stone craft suppliers or local artisan tool vendors.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with 2-4 established marble artisans or microenterprises in Banasthali cluster under revenue-share or job-work model; alternatively, set up small workshop (1,500–2,000 sq ft) with 3-5 skilled carvers. Implement quality checkpoints at roughing, detailing, and finishing stages.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam MSE on udyamregistration.gov.in; apply for IEC (Import-Export Code) via DGFT portal; register trademark with IP India; open GST account. Create brand identity with reference to Ranthambore heritage and artisanal excellence.
5
First Sale
Launch Instagram and Pinterest accounts with high-quality product photography; list on Etsy, Amazon Handmade, and Craftsvilla; approach interior design firms in Delhi-NCR, Mumbai, and Bangalore; join LinkedIn to B2B outreach; participate in Rajasthan ODOP state exhibitions and Delhi Pragati Maidan trade fairs.
6
Scale
Post 6 months, expand product range (wall panels, fountains, inlay work); build relationships with 3-5 interior design agencies for repeat orders; explore export via trade agents or direct DDP shipments to USA and UAE; invest in workshop mechanization (cutting machines) to reduce lead times and increase capacity.

🏚
Rajasthan ODOP Scheme
direct support for branding, packaging, marketing, and export facilitation for Sawai Madhopur marble products
🏚
Rajasthan Kaushal Vikas Mission
subsidized skill training for marble carving, polishing, and finishing; up to ₹10,000 per trainee
