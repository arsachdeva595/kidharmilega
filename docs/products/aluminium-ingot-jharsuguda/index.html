Jharsuguda hosts India's largest aluminium smelter (Vedanta Aluminium) with integrated bauxite mines, captive power generation, and superior logistics via rail and road networks. The district's industrial ecosystem, skilled workforce, and established supply chains make it the natural choice for high-purity aluminium ingot production catering to aerospace, automotive, and construction sectors globally.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Jharsuguda ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Vedanta Aluminium Jharsuguda facility (or arrange B2B tour), study LME aluminium pricing, join NALCO/GECC industry clusters. Spend 2-3 weeks researching quality standards (AS 1117, DIN 226) and competitor products at nearby smelters in Chhattisgarh.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source bauxite from NALCO suppliers (Damanjodi cluster) or Vedanta's approved vendors at ₹1500-2000/tonne. Cryolite from specialty chemical suppliers in Mumbai/Ahmedabad (₹80K-120K/tonne). First 100-tonne batch: ₹2.5-3.5 Cr capex for foundry equipment + working capital.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing Jharsuguda smelters (Vedanta, NALCO) for toll smelting (20-30% margin) OR set up in-house electric arc furnace (₹100-150Cr capex, 12-18 month lead time). Begin with toll smelting to test market viability.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME portal), apply for IEC (Directorate General of Foreign Trade, New Delhi). Obtain ISO 9001:2015 & aerospace-grade certifications (AS 9100D for Boeing/Airbus contracts). File brand with IP India. Get BIS certification for Indian standards compliance.
5
First Sale
Pitch directly to Tier-1 aerospace suppliers (HAL, Bharat Dynamics, Mahindra Aerospace) with metallurgical test reports and price quotes. List on TradeKey, Alibaba, Global Sources for international inquiries. Cold outreach to construction companies via LinkedIn + industry directories. Target ₹50L first order from regional automotive tier-1s.
6
Scale
After 6 months, add value-added products (rolled sheets, rods, wire) via partnerships with rolling mills. Expand export to 8-10 countries via freight forwarders and commodity brokers. Build long-term contracts with 3-5 anchor clients. Invest ₹20-30Cr in second smelter or captive furnace by month 18-24.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
Up to ₹25L grant for manufacturing MSMEs in Jharsuguda district.
🏛
Trade Receivables Discounting System (TReDS)
Access to working capital financing against invoices from large buyers.
🏚
Odisha Industrial Investment Promotion Scheme (OIIPS)
Land subsidy (25-50%), electricity discount (5-10 years), stamp duty waiver for manufacturing clusters.
🏚
Odisha Mineral Processing Policy 2019
Concessional mining lease rates, integration incentives for smelting operations integrated with mining.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
