Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kurukshetra ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Sangaria cluster (26 km from Kurukshetra city) on a Tuesday/Wednesday; meet M/S Bhoor Singh Agri Implements or peer manufacturers — free site visit, ask about production costs, lead times, export process. Call 2-3 dealer contacts in Punjab (get numbers from Sangaria units). Attend Kurukshetra Farmers' Mela (annual, typically Nov-Dec) — free entry, observe demand patterns.
2
Source Raw Materials
Raw materials sourced from Ludhiana steel suppliers (120 km away) and local Haryana fabrication shops; first batch of 10-15 ploughs/cultivators costs ₹6-8L (materials + contract manufacturing). Partner with existing fabrication units in Sangaria as job-work suppliers initially; no capex needed on machining.
3
Produce / Partner
Rent a 2000-3000 sqft warehouse+assembly shed in Sangaria or Kurukshetra industrial area (₹8-12K/month) for assembly and testing. OR partner with existing manufacturer as a sub-brand licensee (pay 5-8% royalty on sales). Assemble kits from sub-suppliers; invest ₹20-30L in welding, testing, and finishing equipment (locally sourced).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free, online, 10 mins); apply for IEC code from DGFT Chandigarh office (₹0, 5-7 days); file GI registration if developing proprietary design (₹5-8K + lawyer fees ₹10-15K, 12-18 months). No GI tag mandatory to start.
5
First Sale
First sale: approach Jivhala or AgroStar (online registration free); list 1-2 SKUs (Cultivator Basic, Plough MB). OR cold-call dealer networks in Punjab (Jalandhar, Ludhiana) — typical first order ₹5-10L for 10-15 units. Expected first-month revenue ₹8-15L if dealer path; ₹2-4L if D2C.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 1-2 full-time field sales reps for Punjab/UP; attend farm machinery exhibitions (Dehradun Jan, Ludhiana Feb); invest ₹10-15L in CNC machinery to reduce fab time. At ₹25L/month: set up export documentation; partner with ECGC for export credit insurance; explore distributorships in Africa via chambers of commerce.

Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Program)
25% subsidy for general category (max ₹25L project cost = ₹6.25L subsidy), 35% for SC/ST/women; 10% own contribution required.
🏛
MUDRA Loan
up to ₹10L collateral-free for MSME; 0-2% interest subvention for first 3 years if routed via priority sector banks.
🏚
Haryana One District One Product (ODOP) scheme
50% subsidy on certification, branding, and export promotion (max ₹5L); eligibility for all registered cluster units.
🏚
Haryana Industries Investment Promotion (HIAP)
20-30% subsidy on machinery + land lease in notified industrial areas; ₹2-5 Cr project-dependent caps.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.

Frequently Asked Questions

Seedhe sawaal, seedhe jawab

Minimum ₹15-18 lakh: shed rent (₹3L/year), machinery (₹8-10L), raw material first batch (₹4-5L). Maximum scale-up: ₹40-45L including CNC, testing lab, 3-month working capital. PMEGP subsidy can cover 25-35% if eligible.
Ideal hain — 3-5 years in agri-equipment manufacturing, dealer networks, or farm machinery selling. NOT mandatory if you hire a production supervisor and visit Sangaria cluster 2-3 times for training. Farming background helps sales, not production.
PMEGP subsidy 25-35% (max ₹6.25L), MUDRA loan ₹10L @ 0-2% interest first 3 years, Haryana ODOP scheme 50% subsidy on branding/certification (max ₹5L), HIAP machinery subsidy 20-30%. Total potential: ₹25-30L subsidy + ₹15-20L loans.
Dealer networks in Punjab (Jalandhar, Ludhiana, Amritsar) — cold-call via farm equipment dealer associations. Online: list on Jivhala, AgroStar, Krishi.com (free). Cooperative societies in Haryana/UP — contact gram panchayats. Attend Kisan Mela Jan-Feb; direct farmer inquiries.
Haan, high-potential. South Africa, Australia, Canada already buying from Sangaria. Process: get IEC code (₹0, free, online), partner with ECGC for export credit insurance (₹50K/year premium), join agriculture export group of FIEO. First export order: 20-30 units, ₹35-50L FOB value. Shipping: sea freight via Mundra Port (2-3 weeks) or air (costly).
Honest: agri-implements market is 10+ year cycle — not overnight. Risk: dealer defaults (negotiate 30% advance), raw material price spikes (lock supplier contracts), farmer payment delays (sell via cooperatives or pre-orders). Exit: sell machinery secondhand at 40-50% loss, lease shed to another MSME. But cluster location + proven demand makes pivoting easier — add spare parts, service center, or tractor accessories.
