Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Kurukshetra ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Kurukshetra's manufacturing clusters in Shahabad, Pipli, and Thanesar; meet 5-10 established implement makers; study Mahindra & John Deere dealer networks in Punjab; attend Agro Tech India exhibition; research competitor pricing on agritech platforms.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source mild steel & cast iron from Ludhiana steel mills (transport cost ₹2-4L per batch); procure bearings & hydraulic systems from Faridabad industrial suppliers; negotiate first-batch raw materials cost of ₹15-25L for 50-100 units; establish credit terms with 30-45 days.
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with established 3-5 machine shops in Kurukshetra industrial area (Sector 7-8) for contract manufacturing; invest in assembly and quality-check facility (₹5-8L); hire 2-3 skilled welders & fabricators (₹15-20K/month each); start with 2-3 implement types (e.g., seed drill, plow, cultivator).
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online at msme.gov.in); obtain IEC code from DGFT portal; register brand name at BRD; get FSSAI/ISO 9001 certification if targeting government contracts; create GST registration; design technical specifications & product manual.
5
First Sale
Launch on AgroStar, Bijnis, Krishify for farmer direct sales; register on GeM portal for government tenders; build dealer network by visiting 20-30 agricultural hardware shops in Punjab/Haryana; offer introductory discounts (10-15%) on first orders; create WhatsApp catalog with pricing.
6
Scale
After 6 months, establish dedicated 3-4 person sales team; open dealership agreements in 3-5 states; invest in digital marketing (Google Ads, YouTube technical demos); launch export trial shipment to Nepal/Bangladesh; add 2-3 new implement variants based on farmer feedback.

Haryana Start-up & Innovation Policy
₹50L seed funding for agriculture-tech manufacturing startups registered in Haryana
🏚
Haryana Export Promotion Scheme
5% export subsidy on FOB value for agricultural machinery exports

Marketing

Ye marketing ideas try karna mat bhulna

  • Create 15-20 second YouTube Shorts demonstrating each implement in live farming; feature testimonials from 5-10 farmers showing productivity gains; target agri-villages with Facebook ads at ₹3-5 CPM.
  • Sponsor farm machinery demos at 2-3 district agricultural fairs (Sept-Nov season); provide free consultations on implement selection; capture leads via SMS/email for follow-up.
  • Build strategic partnerships with 3-5 regional agricultural input dealers; offer 8-10% trade margin; provide marketing collateral and training for their sales teams.
