Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Manpur-Mohla- Ambagad Chowki ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Raipur Agricultural University and connect with soybean research programs; attend weekly farmer markets in Raipur and Durg; join Chhattisgarh Farmers Federation; study pricing at APMC Raipur and e-NAM platform for real-time market rates.
2
Source Raw Materials
Source certified soybean seeds from ICAR-Indian Institute of Soybean Research (Indore) or Chhattisgarh State Seed Corporation; connect with 3-5 local seed dealers in Manpur for competitive pricing; first-batch cost for 5 hectares: ₹1.2-1.5L (seeds, fertilizers, pesticides).
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with agricultural extension officers from Manpur block; organize 2-3 hectare pilot farming with advanced techniques; connect with 50-100 smallholder farmers through cooperative networks for contract procurement; use drip irrigation and soil health cards; establish cold storage facility (₹25-40L) for quality preservation.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (MSME registration) online within 1 week; apply for IEC code from nearest DGFT office (Raipur) for export eligibility; register brand under Chhattisgarh Organic Certification Board; obtain FSSAI license if adding value-added products; trademark brand name at IP India.
5
First Sale
List on e-NAM and AgriMarketplace for B2B sales; contact food processing units in Chhattisgarh Industrial Area (Raipur) directly; register on IndiaStack and TraceNet for export documentation; approach Evergreen Cooperative and NAFED for institutional procurement; launch Instagram/YouTube content showing farm-to-table story for D2C traction.
6
Scale
Establish certified organic farming on 20-50 hectares; build processing unit for soy milk/flour (₹50-80L investment); create farmer producer organization (FPO) to aggregate 500+ farmers; export 50+ tons monthly to Southeast Asian importers; develop premium brand positioning for health-conscious urban consumers; partner with modern retail chains.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PM-KISAN
₹6000 annual support to farming partners; allows input cost reduction and improves farmer profitability by 15%.
🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (35% for general category) for processing/value-addition unit setup in agriculture sector.
🏚
Chhattisgarh Raishree Yojana
₹10000-50000 subsidy per hectare for organic farming certification and soil health improvement.
🏚
Chhattisgarh Bhaiya Duj Subsidy Scheme
40-50% subsidy on farm machinery and drip irrigation for soybean cultivation in identified districts.
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
