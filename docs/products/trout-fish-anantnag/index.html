Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Anantnag ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit established trout farms in Anantnag (Shupiyan and Bijbehara clusters) and connect with Kashmir Fisheries Department. Study production at 2-3 operational units; attend Directorate of Fisheries training in Srinagar; benchmark practices at 1-2 farms in Himachal Pradesh.
2
Source Raw Materials
Purchase certified trout fingerlings from Government Fish Seed Hatchery Shupiyan (₹60-80 per fingerling, minimum 5000 needed ≈ ₹3-4L). Source fish feed from Kashmir Aqua Feed suppliers in Srinagar (₹35-45/kg, ~₹50K for initial 6 months). First-batch setup: ₹5-6L for ponds, aerators, feed storage.
3
Produce / Partner
Lease or purchase 0.5-2 acres of land with spring water access in Anantnag rural areas (₹2-4L annual). Set up concrete/earthen ponds with oxygen systems. Partner with Fisheries Department extension officers for free technical guidance on breeding, feed schedules, disease management, and water quality monitoring.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (online, 10 mins). Apply for IEC code from DGFT Srinagar office for export readiness. Register business name with District Administration. Obtain FSSAI license for food processing (₹5K, 2-3 weeks). Design ODOP-certified packaging with Anantnag origin mark.
5
First Sale
Launch on MeShop and Swiggy's premium seafood section. Directly approach 8-10 five-star hotels in Srinagar, Gulmarg resorts with samples. List on Blinkit and Zepto Fresh in urban metros. Contact seafood exporters in Mumbai and Delhi for bulk offtake agreements. Use WhatsApp B2B groups for Kashmir hospitality sector.
6
Scale
After 6 months of consistent production: expand to 3-5 acres, increase fingerling intake to 20K annually, establish cold-chain infrastructure for export. Build direct export relationships through trade shows in Singapore/Dubai. Launch branded retail presence in premium chains (Spencer's, Reliance Fresh). Develop aqua-tourism add-on with farm visits for high-margin revenue.
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy for aquaculture-based manufacturing units in rural areas
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹30L for artisan clusters; applicable if forming cooperative trout farming cluster
🏚
Jammu & Kashmir Integrated Horticulture and Food Processing Policy
5-10% subsidy on farm infrastructure and cold chain equipment
🏚
J&K Fisheries Promotion Scheme
40% subsidy on pond construction and 25% on fish seed procurement for registered aquaculturists
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
