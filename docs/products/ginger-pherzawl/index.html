Pherzawl district (2,285 sq km, 40,390 population) is nestled in Manipur's northeast with temperate hill terrain perfect for premium ginger. The district has certified organic ginger on 600 hectares under NPOP and 1,500 hectares under MOVCDNER Phase-II, providing guaranteed pesticide-free raw material at farm-gate ₹3,000-4,500/MT. Buyers span Cochin spice markets, Japanese importers (via APEDA), Ayurveda hubs in Bangalore/Kerala, and wellness D2C platforms across metros.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Pherzawl ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Pherzawl ginger cluster (Sairang or Khongjom area) this month. Call 3-4 certified organic ginger farmers listed under APEDA registry—ask about fresh ginger cost, harvest cycle, bulk order minimums. Free: attend any local haat or agri-mandi, ask traders about demand patterns. Register on Udyam (free, online, 15 mins).
2
Source Raw Materials
Source directly from APEDA-certified farms in Pherzawl taluk (Sairang/Khongjom tehsil)—Oct-Jan is harvest season. First batch: 2-3 MT fresh ginger costs ₹7,500-9,000 (₹2,500-3,000/MT farm-gate + transport ₹2,000-3,000). Alternative: buy dried ginger directly from primary processors in Pherzawl town at ₹6,000-7,500 per MT.
3
Produce / Partner
Option A (artisan partnership): Contract with 2-3 existing ginger processors in Pherzawl town (Sairang), provide raw material, they dry/powder/extract for ₹1,500-2,500 per MT processing fee. Option B (own unit): Rent 800-1200 sq ft unit in Pherzawl agri-zone, buy drying trays + grinder + packaging machines (₹3,50,000-6,50,000 capex), produce 50-100 kg/day dried ginger or powder. MOVCDNER or SFURTI may subsidize 30-50% of machine cost.
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam (free online). Apply for APEDA certification if exporting (₹15,000-25,000, 4-6 weeks). Get IEC from customs (₹0, online). Brand registration (₹5,000-8,000). Get FSSAI license (₹5,000-12,000 + food testing ₹3,000-5,000). Total setup: ₹30,000-50,000 for all registrations.
5
First Sale
First sale: Email 10-15 spice importers in Kochi Mattancherry or list on TradeKey/Alibaba with 50 kg sample. Expected first order: 500-1000 kg dried ginger, ₹3,50,000-5,50,000 revenue. Or: List on Amazon/Blink (approval 3-5 days), 20-30 units/day at ₹220/100g = ₹4,400-6,600 per day revenue expected by month 2.
6
Scale
At ₹10L/month: hire 3-4 processors/packers, expand to 300-500 kg/month dried output, tie up with 4-5 certified farms for consistent supply. Exhibit at India International Trade Fair (IITF) Delhi or Spice Board fairs in Kerala. Launch ginger oil/oleoresin (higher margin, ₹35,000-50,000/L). Hire export documentation staff (₹8,000-12,000/month) and pursue direct Japanese/EU contracts.
