Edible Oil ki
opportunity, Tiruvannamalai mein.

Tiruvannamalai has ideal agro-climatic conditions for oilseed cultivation (groundnut, coconut, sesame) with fertile black soil regions. The district has a long-established tradition of cold-pressed oil extraction using manual and semi-mechanical methods, creating a strong artisan base and supply chain. The cool climate and water availability in surrounding areas support consistent oil crop yields throughout the year.

Step-by-Step Roadmap

Zero se pehli sale tak — actual kaam

Har step realistic hai — Tiruvannamalai ke liye, as is.

1
Learn / Research
Visit Tiruvannamalai oil extraction clusters around Polur and Chengam taluks; meet established oil producers and visit mandis in Arani; join Tamil Nadu Food Processing Association; analyze competitor brands like Chettinad Oil and regional co-operatives
2
Source Raw Materials
Source groundnut from Kolar and Tirupati sourcing centers; connect with FPOs in Tiruvannamalai district; negotiate bulk orders from local farmers (₹3-5L for first 500kg batch); establish cold storage agreements with local warehouses
3
Produce / Partner
Partner with existing cold-press units in Polur (20+ operational units); negotiate ₹500-800 per liter processing fees; or purchase used cold-press machinery (₹15-25L) and set up in Arani industrial area; hire 3-5 trained operators from local oil mills
4
Brand & Register
Register Udyam for food processing unit; obtain IEC code from local customs authority; apply for FSSAI license (₹7000-15000); trademark brand name at IP office; get ISO 22000 certification for export readiness (₹50000-80000)
5
First Sale
List on Amazon Fresh, BigBasket, Blinkit with ₹5L minimum guarantee; approach Modern Bazaar, Nature's Basket, Reliance Fresh; direct B2B outreach to 20-30 restaurants via WhatsApp/email with samples; launch Instagram/Facebook with health benefit content; partner with local organic markets
6
Scale
Achieve ₹40L revenue in months 6-8 then scale to 5 SKUs (sesame, coconut, sunflower); build direct-to-consumer via subscription boxes (₹999/month); establish supply to 50+ Ayurvedic clinics; explore State Trading Corporation export contracts; invest in upgraded packaging (glass bottles, sustainable labels)
Government Schemes

Woh paisa jo tujhe pata nahi tha

🏛
PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme)
up to ₹25L subsidy (35% for SC/ST/women, 25% for general) for food processing unit setup
🏛
SFURTI (Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries)
up to ₹5L per unit for cluster development, common facility centers, and training in Tiruvannamalai oil processing cluster
🏚
Tamil Nadu Agro Processing Cluster Development Scheme
grants for food processing units focusing on value-addition with ₹5-10L subsidy component
🏚
Tamil Nadu Health-based Food Processing Grant
specifically for health-focused food products with certification support up to ₹3L
Pehla step: udyamregistration.gov.in — free, 10 minute. Bina iske koi scheme apply nahi hogi.
